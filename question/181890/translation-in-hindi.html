Translation in Hindi

Hi,

I'm currently developping an app that have to be translated into hindi.
I have done everything correctly :
Put all my translation into the i18n > hi
Cleaning the project (or also remove the build folder)
When I debug Ti.Locale.getCurrentLanguage() it returns me "hi" (for hindi)

But IOS doesn't take those files into account.
I canno't test under Android cause it doesn't have Hindi support.

Any idea ?

  • Have putted below thing in your tiapp.xml and clean/build the project.

    
    <ios>
        <plist>
            <dict>
                <key>CFBundleLocalizations</key>
                <array>
                    <string>en</string>
                    <string>hi</string>
                </array>
            </dict>
        </plist>
    </ios>
