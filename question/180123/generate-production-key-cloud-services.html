Generate Production Key Cloud Services

Hello, in my tiapp.xml, I clicked in the button "Enable" Enable Cloud Services, but generated only Development Key, now I can not generate Production Key.
Does anyone know how I can generate the Production Key?

Thanks.

  • Turning off ACS/Cloud and turning it back on will re-use existing keys, not create new ones. So, that would be one way to solve it – turn it off, make sure all the keys are gone from your tiapp.xml file, then turn cloud services back on.

    Or, log onto my.appcelerator.com, click My Apps and find your app in the list. Click Manage ACS. There should be an option on that screen to view your keys. You can then copy them to your tiapp.xml. If you're an enterprise user, keys are also shown on your app's page on your Dashboard site.
