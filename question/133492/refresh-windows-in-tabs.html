    It's default behavior of tab group. A window once loaded in tab will not be reloaded while you navigate through the different tabs. So You have to refresh you window your own.   • Put this in your app.js, changing the 'index' property to whichever tab you're wanting to refresh.

    tabGroup.addEventListener('focus',function(e){
        if(e.index==1)
        {
                Ti.App.fireEvent('reload');

        }

For the reload, you're going to have to have the entire setup of your window that you're trying to reload inside a function, and in the addEvent for the reload, just call that function
