'Console' app on device will only capture crash log. I tried 'iphone configuration utility' on mac to get log from device when it is connected to mac, but still of no use. I could not get log of info statments.

  • If you've plugged it into your Mac, go to Xcode -> Organizer and then choose 'Console' in the left-hand panel it will display not only crash logs but log statements from Titanium (e.g., Ti.API.info, etc.).
