Restricting the users from selecting the past time in timepicker

Hi guys, I have created the time picker as shown in the following listing. 

var timePicker = Ti.UI.createPicker({
        type: Ti.UI.PICKER_TYPE_TIME        
    });
    timePicker.addEventListener("change", function(e){
        var date = new Date(e.value);
        selectedTime = date.getHours()+":"+date.getMinutes();        
        Ti.API.info(selectedTime);
    });

The time picker is working fine.Now my question is, say for example now the present(current) time is 12:00 pm, I don't want the user to choose a time that is earlier than 12:00pm. How can I do that ? and ideas ?

Thanks
