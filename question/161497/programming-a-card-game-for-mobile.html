Programming a card-game for mobile.

I have a client looking for a card game to run on all supported mobile devices. Having developed a simple titanium app based on the RSS Reader example project, I hold a basic understanding of the framework but am having a hard time figuring out the best way to program this. I'm looking for a 2D look but with basic 3D transforms, shadows, etc to give it more life. It also needs to work for different screen sizes.

For example, what would be the best way to deal cards on a table, and flip a card in 3D?

I could easily program this in HTML5/CSS3, but this needs to have a native feel, be available through the app store, and with experience with PhoneGap and Appcelerator I much prefer the development workflow and solidity of Appcelerator.

Thanks!

  • You can do the most of the animations with Alloy.builtins.animation (dealing cards, flipping cards) and use images or views. If you prefer classic Titanium you can use Animator by Raul Riera.

    Make sure you also take a look at Platino: the game engine for Titanium (www.lanica.co)
