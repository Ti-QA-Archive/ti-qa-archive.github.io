    Hi,

    why you don't program your own dot styled pagecontrol for android? I thought you are "born to code" ;)

    No offense. It's really not that complicated. It's just an array of simple views, with borderRadius equal to the views width/2, backgroundColor:white, then just pass the 'scrollEnd' events of the scrollableView to your custom pagecontrol and change opacity of the views, depending on the value of scrollableView.currentPage property.

    Concerning ListView: A new point, which isn't mentioned in your question. Did you tried a ScrollableView within a TableView instead of a ListView? The ListView is not built to host advanced views like a ScrollableView. One tip (for the TableView) don't set a className on the row which hosts the ScrollableView. ScrollableViews cannot be reused properly, because they are itself reusing their subviews.

    Hope this helps you to get started
