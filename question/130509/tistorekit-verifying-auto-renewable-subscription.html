Ti.storekit Verifying Auto Renewable Subscription

Hi,

I am attempting to implement an in-app auto renewable subscription via the ti.storekit module. I am looking for the best way to verify that the user is still subscribed, without forcing them to verify their purchase every time. I looked through the Q&A here and it seems no one has received any help, so I figured I'd ask again now that the entire platform is a bit more mature with 1.8 out.

Right now, I'm thinking of storing the transaction date and then asking the user to verify their purchase once a month. But that still seems sort of hackish and I am wondering if anyone knows of the way other non-titanium apps maintain a subscription without pestering their users?

Thanks a ton.
