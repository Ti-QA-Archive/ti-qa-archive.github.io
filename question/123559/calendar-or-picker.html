Calendar or Picker

How would you guys suggest going about the user selecting a date, however some of the dates could be unavailable. I need to do this for both IOS and Android.

I can't seem to find a cross platform calendar, and although the carousel pickers are nice for picking a date, it is hard to display unavailable dates.

Any thoughts very welcome!

  • In my calendar App I display all of the days as items in a tableView. To navigate from month to month I use buttons at the top of the tableview.

    This approach has worked well since it gives me space to provide visual cues if a day has information associated. For that reason our users like this approach better then the native calendar looking object.

    If you need a full month calendar you might want to check out using a webview and the jQuery FullCalendar plug-in http://arshaw.com/fullcalendar/ That seems to work well on the iPad but was to big for our mobile screens.

    Hope this helps.
