  • The problem is not from Titanium but Apple,
    refer to this link http://community.redfoundry.com/redfoundry/topics/invalid_binary and it's work for me,

    1) In your Provisioning Portal, click App IDs in the left-hand column.

    2) Click "Configure" next to your relevant App ID.

    3) Check the box that says something like "Enable for Apple Push Notification Services".

    4) Click "Configure" next to Production Push SSL Certificate.

    5) Follow the instructions on-screen to create your Cert with the help of Apple's Keychain Access application.

    6) Click "Provisioning" in your left-hand column.

    7) Click "Distribution" tab. Delete your old provisioning for your app and create a new one.
