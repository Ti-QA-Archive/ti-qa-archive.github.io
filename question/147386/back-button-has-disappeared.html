  • I believe this Titanium.UI.currentTab.open may be the culprit, second post this week with problems and currentTab in the latest TiSDK.

    currentTab and currentWindow require that its run in a context where it has value. I personally dumped their use about a year ago, opting to pass a reference to _parentWindow and _parentTab around so that I know for certain I knew 'where I was'.
