    • Open Titanium
    • Click on "Project"
    • In the drop down menu click on "Properties"
    • Click on "Run/Debug Settings"
    • CLick on the "New" button on the right
    • Select "Titanium iOS Simulator"
    • Browse to the projects directory
    • Choose whether this is an iPhone or iPad app
    • Choose an SDK version <- THIS IS WHERE YOU WILL SEE WHAT TITANIUM IS RECOGNIZING, IF ANYTHING
    • Select log level
