  • It needs to be hacked into your SDK installation located in ~/Library/Application Support/Titanium

    When I first started, I noticed that in one of the videos showing an NBC development or something, I noticed that the developer had a forked SDK, renamed for a project.

    I started to do that from there on out. We have a number of different hacks depending on the needs of the client. In the sdk folder on my system, you will find nearly all the 1.7.x releases and the 1.8.x releases. But sitting beside 1.8.2 you will find 1.8.2-ccenter. Its a forked sdk that is used just for that particular client.
