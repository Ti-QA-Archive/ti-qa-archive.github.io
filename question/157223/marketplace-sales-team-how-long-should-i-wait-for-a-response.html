Marketplace Sales Team: how long should I wait for a response?

My company is really interested in purchasing the Encrypted Database Module and the Crypto Module, and would like to know more about them.

They said that they had reached out to the sales team for me and that I should've heard back from them soon. That was 8 days ago. Total waiting: 12 days. No emails, no phone calls.

How long should I wait before someone will reply to my enquire?
