Add image to label or view - iPad

In my ipad app I have a list of actions on the left that is just text. I'm trying to add an image to help make it look a little nicer, but when I do, it stretches across the whole view (and label).

I tried creating an imageview and then adding that to the view, but the simulator crashes on the createImageView for some reason. The image is valid, I changed it to a working one that I'm using for something else to test, and it still crashes.

Is there a way to do something like a leftImage:'myimage.png' in either a label or view?

    Hi Josh,

    Did you try setting the width and height properties of the imageView? I believe that if those are left out, it uses 100%. At least it does that with other views such as buttons.

    Also, if your action items are in a tableView, take a look at the Table View (Layout 2) example in Kitchen Sink.
