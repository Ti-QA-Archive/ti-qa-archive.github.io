Hi

I am planning to make few educational apps for kids. I am interested to know whether Titanium can be used for this purpose because I would need following things for it:

1-Play sound in slow motion(just for kid to understand the accent)

2-Record the voice

3-Storing prerecorded voice.(Will storing .mp3 file names in db suffice to do that?)

Kindly help

  • Accepted Answer

    1: take a look @ Ti.Media.Audioplayer, maybe you can change the bitrate property to make it play slower / faster.

    2: audio recording can be done through Ti.Media.AudioRecorder

    3: you can store files using Ti.Filesystem. Take a look at the documentation.
