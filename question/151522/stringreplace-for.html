  • The only way I could get this to work was to do the following:

    var clean = link.replace(/nbsp/g," ").replace(/\&/g,"").replace(/amp\;/g,"").replace(/\;/g,"");

    For obvious reasons this won't work for everybody since it will wipe out all semicolons and ampersands but it worked for me. I'm perplexed as to why I can't get the entire &nbsp; replaced but for now I'm happy.
