Our first app is now available in the App Store - Eyejot Video Mail

It's a video mail platform. Built with Titanium 1.7.6 and SDK 4. Our review time was five days. It's been sitting waiting for release since November 22 but we decided to wait until today while we buttoned up our backend infrastructure and did more testing.

We have 1.01 in review now (built with 1.8 and SDK5), but we decided to release the current version. The 1.01 is more stable (thanks to 1.8) and offers some extra features. We hope it's available soon.

http://iphone.eyejot.com

Titanium is an awesome platform and it's been a joy to use, despite the many challenges one faces using a new platform that's constantly evolving. I want to also thank the platform http://unbounce.com for helping us create a kick-ass landing page. Awesome platform.
