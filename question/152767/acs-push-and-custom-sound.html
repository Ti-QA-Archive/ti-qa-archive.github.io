ACS Push and custom sound

I'm having trouble playing a custom sound for my android push notifications.

Here's what I send:

{
    "alert":"i am your alert",
    "title":"i am your title",
    "icon":"your_icon",
    "sound":"your_sound.wav"
}

the icon is working, it's located at &#x2F;platform&#x2F;android&#x2F;res&#x2F;drawable-hdpi&#x2F;your_icon.png (for hdpi, my test phone).
the sound is not working, it's located at &#x2F;platform&#x2F;android&#x2F;assets&#x2F;sound&#x2F;your_sound.wav

Where should the sound be, to make it work?

  • Hi Joucke,

    Custom sound must be placed in below directory
    app_root/platform/android/assets/sound/beep.wav

    Here beep.wav is custom sound, specified in the push message with the filename extension. For example

    {
    "alert":"i am your alert",
    "title":"i am your title",
    "icon":"your_icon",
    "sound":"beep.wav"
    }

    check below tutorial for more info,

    http://www.titaniumtutorial.com/2013/10/acs-push-notification-using-gcm.html
