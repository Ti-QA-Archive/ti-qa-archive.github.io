    Do you mean you want orange under the white status bar text?

    Or to change the actual colour of the white text to orange?

    On iOS7, you can use the window.barColor to change the color to orange. Also set the fullscreen:true and translucent:true properties on your window.

    Setting the fullscreen to false will drop the window down 20 pixels showing the black status bar with white text.
