Ti Studio: Change MobileSDK

Is there a simple way to switch the MobileSDK a project is being compiled against in TiStudio? I can't seem to find it in the settings.

    Open the tiapp.xml file in the left frame. The SDK is easily selected from a dropdown in the overview tab. For complete control of all tags, switch to the other tab (labeled tiapp.xml)
