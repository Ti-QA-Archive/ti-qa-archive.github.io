Maps Requirement

I have a scenario where I need an offline map to be preloaded into my mobile and my position has to be tracked on it even when am offline(without internet).

The use case is the map is available on the app download itself with certain pins on certain locations which I wanted to pin down and I dont need internet to access my maps. Any suggestions?

I find google static maps not good for this. Any thoughts and suggestions?

— asked December 3rd 2012 by Arun Kumar K

  • You will need to contact the mapping vendors and pay a large licence fee to achieve this. Google static maps are also not licensed to cache and re-use yourself just so you know.

    These companies pay millions for this information and the licences we are provided do not carry the ability to store cached versions of that mapping information.
