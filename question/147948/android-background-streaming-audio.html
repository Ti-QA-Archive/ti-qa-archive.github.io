Android Background Streaming Audio

I have found two issues with background audio streaming on Android devices. First, when the user receives a phone call – the background audio continues to play. Anyone know a way to disable background streaming while on a phone call?

Also, I noticed that setting "allowBackground" to "false" no longer disables background audio. It still continues to play when the app is in the background.

Any suggestions would be greatly appreciated. Using 3.0.0 SDK. Here is my simple code:

var player = Titanium.Media.createAudioPlayer({
    allowBackground : false, //does not work, audio still continues to play in the background
});

  • Hi, you can try one thing, detect the app is in foreground or background and the manually play and pause the audio.

    to detect the app in foreground or background, you have to get the the window activity and then check whether that activity is paused or resumed.

    another approach could be, use Ti.Android.getCurrentActivity() to get the current activity and check the state
