  • Phones don't have real GPS. They have A-GPS which stands for Assisted GPS and the way it works is, your location and satellites' locations are retrieved from the internet somehow then you are connected directly to the satellites. So in my opinion PROVIDER_NETWORK and GPS should be equally fast. Unless you have real GPS on your phone (some do but not the modern models), in that case PROVIDER_NETWORK should be faster. GPS is used mostly for long term connections not just to get a position.
