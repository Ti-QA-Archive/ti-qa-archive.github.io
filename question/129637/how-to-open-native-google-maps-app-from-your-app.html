How to open native google maps app from your app?

I know how to do a mapview and all of that, but I am trying to open the native google maps app from my app when I click on an address in my application.

Is this possible?

— asked December 20th 2011 by Leonardo Amigoni

  • Titanium.Platform.openURL('http://www.google.com/maps/adddress='+youraddress);

    Look and see what the real URL is, but putting that in your click event will open it in the native maps app on android and ios. It will load a webpage in the simulator, but works fine on the device.

  • var navIntent = Titanium.Android.createIntent({
  action: Titanium.Android.ACTION_MAIN,
  className: 'com.google.android.maps.driveabout.app.DestinationActivity',
  packageName: 'com.google.android.apps.maps'
});
navIntent.addCategory(Titanium.Android.CATEGORY_LAUNCHER);
Titanium.Android.currentActivity.startActivity(navIntent);
