  • It's really down to the system and the screen size. The ActionBar decides wether or not it splits its tabs into a stacked ActionBar below the main ActionBar. It even converts your Tabs into a drop-down spinner if it is necessary. There is, AFAIK, no way to influence this.

    So, the only way I see to avoid this default behavior of the ActionBar is to avoid using the ActionBar Tab-Navigation. Instead you could use a ViewPager with fixed tabs for your tab navigation. It won't merge with the ActionBar.
