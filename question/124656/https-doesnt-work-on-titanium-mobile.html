https doesn't work on Titanium mobile?

Hi,

I have a question about ssl and Titanium.
As specified in a few posts, I use an address like 'https://foo.com' tu enable https in the open method of my httpClient (xhr.open(&#39;POST&#39;, &#39;https:&#x2F;&#x2F;foo.com&#39;);).

When I use Wireshark to snif the network, I see an https request and I can't know

  • the different parameters of this one
  • the url

BUT, if I use another sniffer which can be used as a proxy (Paros), I can see the url and the parameters.

Anyone knows how I can hide these data? If I have done anything wrong?

Just for information, there is the procedure I followed to snif the network with Paros:

1 Download and unzip: http://www.parosproxy.org/download.shtml
2 Plug your mac to the ethernet
3 Activate Internet sharing on your mac
4 Check the IP of your mac: System Preferences > Network (I am not sure of the name, because I have a French translation on my mac)
5 Run paros.jar
6 In Paros: Tools > Option > Local Proxy
7 Fill in with the IP of the step 4
8 With your iPhone, connect to the network created at step 3
9 In the iPhone, click on the right arrow, scroll to the bottom and choose 'Manual'
10 There, enter the IP of step 4 and port 8080

I have also tried with port 443 (ssl one), and in this case, I can't see anything in Paros…

Thx in advance for your help, and code strong!
