Clear browser cache in Titanium Desktop app

I can't figure out how to clear the cache in my app. I have a page with a <video> tag which loads a video, and I need to update that video, but I can't get the app to hit the server again to grab the latest version of the video. I've tried deleting the dist, ~&#x2F;Library&#x2F;Caches&#x2F;[app], ~&#x2F;Library&#x2F;[app], and ~&#x2F;Library&#x2F;Application Support&#x2F;Titanium&#x2F;appdata&#x2F;[app] directories, and nothing seems to work.

— asked December 13th 2011 by Joshua Rieken

  • The problem was the QuickTime cache at ~&#x2F;Library&#x2F;Caches&#x2F;QuickTime&#x2F;downloads. I deleted all content there and was able to reload the video.
