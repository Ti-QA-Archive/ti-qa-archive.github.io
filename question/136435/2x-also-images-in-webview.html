@2x also images in webView?

I am currently developing an app for the new iPad 3 (retina screen). I will include all the images in both plain (standard resolution) and @2x version (x2 resolution). But I don´t know if this rule also applies for local webView images.
So, will the webView local images (from a local url) upscale when played on an iPad3 as the rest of images in the app will?

  • No, they will not. My understanding is the @2x is only for the native side of Titanium. WebView sadly is basically opening a web browser within your app, you'd probably have to make logic on the webview to handle different screen sizes, etc.
