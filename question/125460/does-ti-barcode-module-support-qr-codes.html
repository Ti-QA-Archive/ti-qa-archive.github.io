  • Ti+ barcode module does support QR codes. It has both an iOS and Android versions available. (They are different modules – if you need to support both you must add both to the project.)

    TiBar (the third party module) is iOS only, but does still work with SDK 1.7.2

    In terms of giving a source to confirm Ti+ barcode suports QR and both iOS and android, the documentation links are behind the paid subscriber helpdesk. Since I don't see a subscriber icon beside your name, I assume you are not yet a paid subscriber.

    Once you are (or start a 30-day trial), the documentation will be at Helpdesk > Titanium+ Modules > Barcode module then click the underlined link.

    That leads to a page listing the supported formats for iOS and Android (which differ but both support QR codes) and the download links for each OS.

    Micro QR codes are not listed, but regular QR codes are supported.
