Removing the Titanium Logo On start up for an APP

Hi I am new to titanium and was just wondering how i could remove the powered by titanium logo from my app so my app when run goes into my initial screen rather than the powered by titanium logo first?

  • You need to replace the images called Default.png with your own in /Resources/android/images folder and any of the sub-folders where you need to support the specific device screen dimensions / density
