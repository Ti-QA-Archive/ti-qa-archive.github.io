  • Hi

    If I understand your question correctly, you want to be able to create a framework of contained views, buttons, labels etc as you see fit that together make up a common "something" that you re-use in your app one or more times.

    If that is the case then there is built-in functionality for this called Require, quick example from the docs (modified).

    <Alloy>
    <TabGroup>
        <Tab id="leftTab">
            <Require type="view" src="window" id="rssTab"/>        
        </Tab>
        <Tab id="rightTab">
            <Require type="view" src="window" id="aboutTab"/>        
        </Tab>    
    </TabGroup>
</Alloy>

    And the window.xml file;

    <Alloy>
    <Window>
        <Label>Hello</Label>
    </Window>
</Alloy>

    You can associate a tss file with window.xml to provide your presentation as well. Once used, you can still access all the elements within the required elements.

    You can take this further with widgets but I think the above solves your immediate issues.
