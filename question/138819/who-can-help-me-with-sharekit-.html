    • Well, I figured out how to get my issue resolved, feeding back for anyone else having an issue. The app I'm integrating ShareKit into is fairly old; it's been in the app store since SDK version 1.5.1, so I'm not sure if that plays a part here or not, but thought it might be useful information.

      Turns out that while I think I followed all of the install and configure instruction correctly, I must be missing something, because it turns out my info.plist was not getting updated correctly. I had this entry in my info.plist (editied for privacy):

          <key>CFBundleURLTypes</key>
    <array>
        <dict>
            <key>CFBundleURLName</key>
            <string>app.fqdn.id</string>
            <key>CFBundleURLSchemes</key>
            <array>
                <string>appidentifier</string>

            </array>
        </dict>
    </array>

      and when I added my FB App ID to this CFBundleURLSchemes array, things started working! Here's my info.plist afterwards:

          <key>CFBundleURLTypes</key>
    <array>
        <dict>
            <key>CFBundleURLName</key>
            <string>app.fqdn.id</string>
            <key>CFBundleURLSchemes</key>
            <array>
                <string>appidentifier</string>
                <string>fbMY_ACTUAL_FB_APP_ID</string>
            </array>
        </dict>
    </array>

      I figured this out by lots of searching and trial and error, and ultimately this SO post: http://stackoverflow.com/questions/9647273/facebook-connect-safari-cannot-open-page

      Hope this helps someone else!
