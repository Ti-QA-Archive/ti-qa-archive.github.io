Publish an android table app

Hi everyone!

I would like to know if it's possible to publish an android app but only as a tablet app (as you can do with an Ipad), so that you can't download it with your android phone… And if it's possible, then how can you do it?

Thank You!

    By supporting (or not) the various densities in your TiApp.xml you narrow down the available marketplace for the app automatically. When you go to publish in the Android Marketplace you can see which device types your app will be compatible with.
