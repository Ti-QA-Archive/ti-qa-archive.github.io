  • It's a common misconception that the version of the SDK is aligned with the device family versions. In short, its not.

  • After you build your app and Titanium and are ready to generate an ipa and deploy it to devices, go into the build/iphone directory of your ti project and double click the xproj file, which will launch your project in xcode. Then you can set the deployment target to 4.2 (last version that works with iphone 3g), or whatever version you want.

    — answered January 27th 2012 by Justin Toth
