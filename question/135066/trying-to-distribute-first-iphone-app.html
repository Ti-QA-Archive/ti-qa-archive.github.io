  • Found my issue. Your may be the same. Look at the last bit of your build.log in the iOS directory. Find the last file it was attempting to compile. Then look at the reason it failed.

    In my case it was a regular expression it didn't like:

    var = /inline;filename="([^:"\\/?<>|])"/;

    It saw it as an unterminated string.
