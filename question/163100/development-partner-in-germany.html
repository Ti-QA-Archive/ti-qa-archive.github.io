  • Johannes,

    Take a look at the DevLink. Unfortunately, you cannot sort by region (that be a nice feature).

    You want to look for folks high on the list, who are titans (active in the community), who have current certifications (newer certifications are badge shaped, not squared).

    I'd also make the argument that unless face to face is an absolute must, there are a number of GREAT development shops that you could partner with.

    My short list of folks near you, if I needed help to cover my shop's workload (in no particular order):

    Core 13, Things are Moving, Rborn, Napp, Fokke Zandbergen, Tipsy and Tumbler
