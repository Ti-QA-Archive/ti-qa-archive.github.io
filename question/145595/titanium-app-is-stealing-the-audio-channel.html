Titanium App is stealing the audio channel

Hi,

I just finished my first app (Built w. 2.1.4.GA and deployed on iPhone). Unfortunately it steals the audio-channel directly when I switch to it from another application which has background-sound running (such as Spotify).

Sure, I do have some sounds in my application (on clicks etc.) but I also have the possibility to turn them off in the application. Even then my app is muting the background-sound.

Can I workaround this in any way? I do want to have spotify running in the background while running my app with sounds disabled.

I am very thankful for any insights into this!

Tobias

  • I believe you want to set your audioSessionMode to Ambient.

    Ti.Media.audioSessionMode = Ti.Media.AUDIO_SESSION_MODE_AMBIENT;
