  • Hi Addie,

    You can use it just like you did with traditional Titanium development. Under project directory make platform folder then make android > res > values . Place your 9 patch image under platform/android/res/drawable/image_name.png. Also create a file called theme.xml and modify it according to the docs.

    However, You may not get proper alignment of the 9-patch splash screen. A JIRA ticket has already been raised at :https://jira.appcelerator.org/browse/TIMOB-14508

    Thanks.
