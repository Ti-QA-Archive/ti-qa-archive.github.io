  • This is an architectural problem at its root.

    Who needs the data?

    When do they need the data?

    Do they need all the data at the same time?

    Example:

    • Does a user in the backwoods of West Virginia, without any network connection need to be able to lookup 100% of the records?

    • Does a user on a slow network connection need 100% of the records, or, can he possibly be served a subset of the data?

    These are business use questions that govern how you look at the architecture of your app.
