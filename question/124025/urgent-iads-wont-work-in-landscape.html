Urgent, iAds won't work in landscape??!!

Hello everyone. I've been using admob in my free apps, as I heard iAds had a very low fill rate. After running admob for a few weeks, and doing more research, it seems the best way to generate revenue is to have iAds, and if those don't fill, fall back to admob.

I've spent hours getting all of that setup, and was about to submit my apps, when I noticed that if an iAd is clicked in landscape, it loads in portrait, and then when you close the iAd, the app is hidden, and you only see a blank screen. If the iAd is clicked in portrait, it all works fine. (This happens on both iPhone and Ipad.)

I've tried to catch a click event on the iAd by adding event listeners, and found that "click" events don't seem to have listeners. I can catch an "action" event, but it doesn't fire until after the ad loads. My hope was to switch the app to portrait when an iAd is clicked, which will probably fix the issue.

I've found this thread which also confirms the issue, but not any resolution.
http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/122793/how-to-get-iad-to-display–480x32

I'd love to hear any advise on getting iAds to work in landscape.

Thanks,
