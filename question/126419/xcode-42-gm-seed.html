  • Yes, we know GM is not beta, but we just got our hands on the GA too, so we are doing final testing before releasing fixes.

    The gold master remains under the terms of Apple's NDA except for items announced by Apple at their event and on their website. For that reason, bugs related to iOS5 cant be discussed openly for open source projects. We are working very hard to make sure that everything works correctly on iOS5.

    As I stated, the latest fixes will be in the CI builds for 1.7.3 or 1.8.0. But know that if you update to Xcode 4.2/iOS5 we can't support you, as we can't openly talk about it as we are under NDA.
