  • Hello Rainer,

    These Modules are packaged with the SDK but you have to import them since they are optional. To do so:

    • open your tiapp.xml in overview mode
    • in the modules section, add modules (like facebook)

    Then you can use the module from you code:

    var fb = require('facebook');

    When you get an error after you have done that, try cleaning your project and perform a full re-build.
