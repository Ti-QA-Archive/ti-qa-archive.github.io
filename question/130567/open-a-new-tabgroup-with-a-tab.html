Open a new TabGroup with a Tab

Hi there, appreciate if anyone can help on this.

I have a tabGroup with tab1 and tab2. In tab1, window with a TableView is displayed, and when a TableViewRow is clicked, a modal window will be opened. so far so good.

Then I add a new tabGroup to the modal window, with tabA and tabB.
What I trying to do is when this modal window with tabA and tabB is closed, the original window with tab1 and tab2 will be shown as before.

But it doesn't work.
The new tabGroup doesn't attach to the modal window but seems replace the original tabGroup of tab1 and tab2 in the original window.

Is it possible to do so? or only one tabGroup is allowed at a time?
any suggestion on alternatives to implement something like that?

Thanks in advance.

  • Bowie, you could always try going with something along these lines for your modal window, that way it is not literally a tab group: Custom tabBar

    Or, you could always just create the modal window (which should be fullscreen). Anchor a view to the bottom of the modal window that spans the entire width of the window. Set a height of 49px for this view, which is the standard height of a tabGroup–for iPhone. Specify a backgroundImage if you so desire (could resemble the regular platform tab bars). Then add however many imageViews you need to this bottom view. Add in flexibleSpace, etc. This way your tabA and tabB tabGroup is unique to that modal window and will not replace the app's main tab1 and tab2 tabGroup.

    Might be too much of a unnecessary work-around…probably not the most efficient solution, but if you're looking to only have a tabGroup unique to that modal window, it might be the most practical solution to specify a couple of imageViews, add whatever event listeners you need to each when they're clicked, etc.

  • Hi Kyle, thanks for your suggestion.

    Using Custom tabBar is feasible but as you said, it might be too much to do.
    End up I workaround by adding all tabs (tab1, tab2, tabA and tabB) to the tabGroup. By default, tabA and tabB is hidden. When the modal window is opened, hide tab1 & tab2 and show tabA and tabB. vice versa when modal window is closed.
    I am not sure if it is the most efficient way but the simplest way to me so far.

    Thanks again :)
