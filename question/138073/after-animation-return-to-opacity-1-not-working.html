  • I have found a solution but it´s a little trick, I think it´s not the better way of codding, but works:

    myimage.animate({opacity:0,duration:4000},function(){
    myimage.animate({opacity:1,duration:100});
});

    I´m totally open to another better solution. Maybe It´s possibly an appcelerator error?

})
