  • You need to write it to the HD first and then point the .image property to your imageView

    var f = Titanium.Filesystem.getFile(Titanium.Filesystem.tempDirectory + "/yourImage.png");
f.write(query1.field(BLOBINDEX));    
var photoView = Ti.UI.createImageView();
photoView.image = Titanium.Filesystem.tempDirectory + '/yourImage.png';
