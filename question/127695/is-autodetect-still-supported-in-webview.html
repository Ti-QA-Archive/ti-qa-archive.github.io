Is autoDetect still supported in WebView?

I have downloaded an old library that creates a web view and passes it an autoDetect parameter as follows:

webView = Ti.UI.createWebView({
          url: pUrl,
          autoDetect:[Ti.UI.AUTODETECT_NONE]
});

However I couldn't find autoDetect in the docs. Is it still supported? And what's it for?

  • Behrang

    Note that the AUTODETECT constants are described in the APIDocs for Titanium.UI rather than webview, although also be aware that they are iOS-specific, which is why they are moving to Titanium.UI.iOS for Ti 1.9 (see TIMOB-5166).

    I've only actually seen them used with text areas, as in the textarea_autodetect.js example in the KitchenSink.
