  • You have a reference to ti.urbanAirship (notice uppercase A) as the module name. You need to locate all instances of it in your .js files as well as your tiapp.xml and make sure it is all lowercase.

    Also ensure that the module is installed in the proper location. For OSX it should be: /Library/Application Support/Titanium/modules/iphone/
