Ti.Media.showCamera fails on Genymotion emulator, even though other camera apps work fine

I'm building against the Genymotion android emulator, and it appears Ti.Media.showCamera always fails with a Ti.Media.NO_CAMERA error, despite the fact that I have the camera widget enabled and can open other camera apps on the emulated device and it works just fine.

  • That's strange, because I had just the opposite problem. I didn't want the showCamera() to fire on the Genymotion emulator and had to add this:

    if (Ti.Media.isCameraSupported && Ti.Platform.model != 'google_sdk' && Ti.Platform.manufacturer != 'Genymotion') {
    Ti.Media.showCamera(cameraOptions);
} else {
    Ti.Media.openPhotoGallery(cameraOptions);
}
