Android resume fails/forces restart

This has been an irritation so long that it's actually stopped being an irritation and I've learned to live with it.

I have a webview-based app which, on Android, if it's being resumed sometimes fails and forces instead a restart (sometimes; sometimes the resume just fails and I go back to the home screen).

Essentially, I'll have the app open, switch to something else sizable (like Chrome) then back to the app. The app's splash screen will come up, then immediately close (showing the home screen). Then (usually) the splash screen will come back up again and the app will restart.

This is nuisance, but a minor one. It just looks really amateurish.

Has anyone else seen this sort of thing?
