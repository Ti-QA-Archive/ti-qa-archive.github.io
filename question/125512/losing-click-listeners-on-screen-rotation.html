Hello all..
I'm losing my click listeners on screen rotation.
all click events works fine until i change screen orientation, if i change the orientation i loose the event.

the only thing i do i change the position of the object using:

    I have hit this issue as well. Mine was due to another component being over the top of the button in orientation mode. This was because I set the values in one mode and they kept them as they changed…

    The only solution I had was to set the zIndex on the components to make sure my buttons were always on top. It was really confusing initially not sure why the components didn't automatically resize.
