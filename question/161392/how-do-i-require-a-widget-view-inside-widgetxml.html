How do I require a widget view inside widget.xml?

Titanium SDK: 3.2.0, OSX: Mavericks, Platform: iOS 7

In widget.xml I want to require the list.xml (located in the widgets views folder) like so:

<Alloy>
    <Require type="view" src="list" id="list"/>
</Alloy>

However when I compile it appears to look for that view in the main apps views folder:

[ERROR] :  view "list" does not exist.
[ERROR] :  The following paths were inspected:
[ERROR] :  /Users/user/apps/myapp/app/views/ios/list
[ERROR] :  /Users/user/apps/myapp/app/views/list
[ERROR] :  Alloy compiler failed

How to I make it use the view in the widgets folder?
