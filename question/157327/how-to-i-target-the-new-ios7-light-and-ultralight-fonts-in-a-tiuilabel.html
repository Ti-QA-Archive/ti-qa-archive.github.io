How to I target the new iOS7 Light and Ultralight fonts in a Ti.UI.Label?

I've tried "Helvetica Neue Ultralight" to no avail.

Any ideas anyone?

    This works fine for me:

    fontFamily: 'HelveticaNeue-UltraLight'

    See http://www.iosfonts.com for the correct notation of font names.
