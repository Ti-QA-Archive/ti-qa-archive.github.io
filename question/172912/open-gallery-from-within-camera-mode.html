Open gallery from within camera mode.

Hi!

I'm using Alloy, Latest SDK, developing under iPhone.

The case is to open the gallery from camera mode. The steps I make are:

  1. Show camera with standard method:

    Titanium.Media.showCamera({
 success:                successCallback,
 cancel:                cancelCallback,
 error:                    errorCallback,
 showControls:             false,
 saveToPhotoGallery:        true,
 mediaTypes:            [Ti.Media.MEDIA_TYPE_PHOTO],
 overlay:                /*my own view*/
});

  2. In my own view I click on gallery button and call for:

    Ti.Media.openPhotoGallery({
 success:        successCallback
});

  3. Then I got an error from errorCallback (camera callback) with code 1.
    Gallery didn't open & camera will no longer able to be closed.

How could I open gallery within camera mode?
Appreciate any help!
