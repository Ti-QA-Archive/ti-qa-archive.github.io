    • My bad, i focused on audioSessionMode, TY Larrie.

      Ti.Media.audioSessionMode = Ti.Media.AUDIO_SESSION_MODE_PLAYBACK; // In case device is muted
    Ti.Media.appMusicPlayer = Titanium.Media.MusicPlayer; 
    Ti.Media.appMusicPlayer.setVolume(1.0);

      And job is done ;)
