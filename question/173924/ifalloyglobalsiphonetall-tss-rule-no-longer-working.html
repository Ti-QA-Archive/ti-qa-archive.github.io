  • Hi

    The new if conditonal Alloy View attribute is available from Alloy 1.4 and higher. However TI SDK 3.2.2 & Ti SDK 3.2.3 only come with Alloy 1.3.1.

    You would have to have Alloy 1.4 installed.

    A quick check for this would be open terminal and type;

    alloy -v

    What does this return?
