  • Is not about what technology you use (html, json, etc) but the content. If your news feed will display content targeted to 17+ age then you will have to declare this into the app.
    If you don't control the news feed (3rd parties could publish something apple might not like) you might want to select this at the submission time to avoid a rejection.
