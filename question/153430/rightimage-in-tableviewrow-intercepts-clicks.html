rightImage in tableviewRow intercepts clicks

In my iPhone project, I have a tableview.

In any row of my tableview I declared a rightImage.

When I click the row I get no issue, but when I click the rightImage area, it intercepts the click and the event I attached to the row does not fire.

Aside from converting the rightImage to a regular imageView, is there any way to stop the rightImage from intercepting clicks?
