  • Hi , try providing the units of fonsize in sp and borderRadius , left , top , height , width , etc in dp

    Your prezzo_textfield could be like.

    prezzo_textfield = Titanium.UI.createTextField
({
      width: "94%",
      color: "#222",
      backgroundColor: "white",
      backgroundImage: "",
      borderRadius: "3dp",
      border: "4dp",
      borderColor: "orange",
      font:{fontSize:"18sp"},
      height: "40dp",
      hintText: L('price') + ': Es 24.42',
      top: "15dp",
      left: '3%',
      paddingLeft: "5dp",
      keyboardType: Titanium.UI.KEYBOARD_DECIMAL_PAD,
      returnKeyType: Ti.UI.RETURNKEY_DONE ,
});
