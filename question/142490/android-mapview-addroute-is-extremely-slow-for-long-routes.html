[Android] MapView addRoute is extremely slow for long routes

Hi guys,

I'm adding a pretty long (1000km+) route to a MapView, with the points decoded from the polylines downloaded from http:&#x2F;&#x2F;maps.google.com&#x2F;maps&#x2F;api&#x2F;directions&#x2F;xml?mode=&quot; + travelMode + &quot;&amp;origin=&quot; + origin + &quot;&amp;destination=&quot; + destination +&quot;&amp;sensor=false.

The route is very precise and it the performance is at very good levels even on an old ARM11 iPod Touch running iOS 4. The same route drawn on a Galaxy S or Galaxy S II, brings the CPU on it's knees. You're simply not able to pan around the map anymore, your get feedback from panning seconds after you touch the screen. On the Galaxy S I even get a message that my app has stopped responding, and if choose to wait then it's panning a little then stops again. Is there a way we can improve the performance? Or are there any workarounds for this?
