How to change Android Emulator

Currently I am running a sample on Titanium studio using Android Emulator but it is by default picking my Android tablet Emulator and I am unable to change to Android phone Emulator, so can you please let me know how to change the Emulator to Android phone instead of Android Device using titanium studio

— asked January 23rd 2012 by Umaid Saleem

    Hi Umaid,

    Click on the icon in your titanium studio where you get emulator and device options. Below there is another option called "Run Configurations…". Click on that and select Screen for different resolution devices.
