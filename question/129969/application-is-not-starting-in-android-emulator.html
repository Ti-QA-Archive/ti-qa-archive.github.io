  • I have encountered the same problem, too. It takes me two hours to figure out how to fix the issue:

    Project->Properties to open the Properties for xxxx dialog.

    There's a 'Run/Debug Settings' item in the left panel. Click it, it will show
    the available launch configurations. Click the one you like, and hit the Edit
    button or double click it. The Edit Configuration will be poped up.

    The 'Android API' is the place you have to modify. Use Google APIs Android
    2.2, for example, will just solve the problem.

    :)
    T
