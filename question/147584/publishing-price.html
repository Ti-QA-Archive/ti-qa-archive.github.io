    ##Yes

    It will be (and always be) completely free. Appcelerator doesn't make any money with the framework. Their revenue model is more based around support plans as well as their cloud services. The more you use their services, the more you pay (makes sense)

    But then again, this is only if you choose to uses those services within your application.
