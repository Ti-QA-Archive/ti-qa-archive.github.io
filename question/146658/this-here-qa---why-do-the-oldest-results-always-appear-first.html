This here Q&A - why do the oldest results always appear first?

When I'm searching this Q&A forum the oldest results appear first, quite often these apply to older versions of Titanium and are no longer applicable - would it be possible to reverse this? Or to use a more sophisticated ranking algorithm that combines the age and popularity of a thread?

I'm no expert, but this thread reckons a good algorithm would use a decaying logic based around:

http://blog.linkibol.com/2010/05/07/how-to-build-a-popularity-algorithm-you-can-be-proud-of/

While not directly Titanium related, a small tweak like this would make my experience vastly improved :-)

    Because we use Apache Solr to do full text searching and it sucks. We tried setting up a boost value for items with a more recent date, but it simply does not work. Things were much better back in the day when we used Google Search, but we had to switch when we were hitting our daily quota by 1pm and that's with a paid account.

    Until we get a better replacement for Apache Solr, I recommend using Google with the "site:" option:

    site:developer.appcelerator.com/question <KEYWORDS HERE>
