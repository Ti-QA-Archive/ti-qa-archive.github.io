  • It's a quite cumbersome proces. You need to first create a Distribution certificate and provisioning profile using member center. Start off by reading this: http://bit.ly/oKuHmI. Download the provisioning profile form member center when you're though. Then, using Titanium Studio, chose Distribute - App store. Fille out the form, choosing the downloaded provisioning profile. Afterwards, go to iTunes Connect and create your app on Appstore. Complete all forms, and hit "Ready to upload binary". The open Xcode, Organizer and select "Archive". Your app should be there, select Validate and see if it passes. Then upload the binary.

    Hope it'll get you started. Good luck.
