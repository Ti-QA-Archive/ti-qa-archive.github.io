Weird behaviour on Android

If I show two alerts like this:

alert('alert 1');
alert('alert 2');

Android will show the 2nd alert and then the 1st alert.

Has anyone got an idea why is this?

I am putting several labels (from DB, so I don't know their heights). Help is much appreciated. :)

    The alert in Android is an activity ( a window if you want ) that is set to be "top-most". That means when you show the alert1 a new activity opens on top of any other windows, then activity 2 will show on top on any other window (including alert1)

    makes sense?
