  • Accepted Answer

    Use Ti.App.Properties

    eg :

    Ti.App.Properties.setString(&#39;property_name&#39;, &#39;some_username&#39;);

    Then you can get it back by using:

    var username = Ti.App.Properties.getString(&#39;property_name&#39;);

    These will survive app updates too. Deleting the app will delete them.

    Or, you can remove them by setting to null:

    Ti.App.Properties.setString(&#39;property_name&#39;, null);

    hth.
