  • Try putting your workspace in a Path with no spaces in it, for example your current path is:

    C:\Users\786\My Documents\Titanium_Studio_Workspace\

    put it in C:\Titanium_Studio_Workspace\

    This little tip I obtained from some people here, saved me from problems. I hope it does this for you too.
