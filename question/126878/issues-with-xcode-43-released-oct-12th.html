Issues with Xcode 4.3 released oct, 12th

Related to issue below, i thought i would put my experience here with latest Xcode 4.3 released on October 12, 2011.

The new xcode relocated former xcode folder to developer-4.2, that seems to trouble TI Developer, the only iOS SDK selection shows up is 4.3, all previous or iOS 5 SDK/previous ios 5 beta are missing.

Also, you will need to download iTunes 10.5.1 beta, otherwise you can't connect your device (error) with newest xcode installed.

To roll back, i trashed the new folder (developer) then simply renamed the developer-4.2 folder back to developer and reboot machine. Everything seems to work (including iTunes 10.5.1 beta)

http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/126873/updated-to-ios5—need-to-build-for-iphone-now
