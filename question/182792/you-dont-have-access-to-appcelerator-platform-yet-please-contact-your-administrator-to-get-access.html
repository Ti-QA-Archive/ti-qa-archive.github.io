    Hi Samuel,

    Be sure to sign up for Appcelerator Platform 4.0 on our website (www.appcelerator.com) with the same email address you've been using for Titanium. From there, you'll be able to log in and begin to migrate your app(s) to the new Appcelerator Platform.

    Let me know if you run into any issues. Thanks!
