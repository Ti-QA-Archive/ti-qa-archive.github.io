AdView (iAd I guess) wont appear on my AppStore App

Hello friends. I would like to ask you something. I have developed an application using titanium, I used adView on it… I have submitted it and Apple has approved my application. Now I can download it from Appstore, but when I opened it, I realized that there is no IAd…
I used <AdView> in several screens, but on my AppStore app only appear a white rectangle there.
Have I missed an intermediate step?
Thank you very much in advance

    You need to sign up for iAd on the iTunes Connect site. Once you do that, then you will get advertisements in your app. It is a separate process from the app submission process.
