Forcing GPS On/Off

Using an Android and soon iPhone, is there a way to turn on the GPS without sending the user to the "location & security" screen?

— asked July 8th 2011 by Pete Berry

  • You can't do anything to override settings if the user has the GPS turned off. iOS is worse in that the GPS could be turned on, but permission denied to your app. The best you can do is test for these conditions and notify the user accordingly. There are examples in the Kitchen Sink for testing on/off and the various iOS permissions.
