  • Best bet would be to check out node-xmpp on GitHub and Google. This would probably be the most "Ti-friendly" way to get started, as it is a component-oriented XMPP platform that appears to offer client functionality.

    I have not seen any specific Ti-mobile XMPP implementations. Of course, depending on the scope of your project, implementing a stateful protocol over mobile is not trivial. :-)
