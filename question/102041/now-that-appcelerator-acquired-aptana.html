    • As a user not affiliated with Appcelerator, let me say I hope your post was sarcastic (which rarely comes across well in written language). Since getting the last venture funding, I think they have more than doubled their staff size and shooting for at least triple. And from where I sit, it seems like they have fixed numerous bugs and increased quality. I do find the documentation lacking, but then having an IDE which offers code completion and argument tooltips will help too.

      Personally, in the few months I have done more serious work in Titanium, I have gotten way more than my money's worth: I have never paid them a cent. Have I had frustrations? Sure, but some are my own doing, like coding a callback listener as 'onError' instead of 'onerror' and taking way too long to figure out why it was not getting called. Having IDE integration will minimize that type of mistake.

      I for one am looking forward to the webinar and a preview of the roadmap. Bring it on!
