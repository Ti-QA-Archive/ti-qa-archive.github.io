Disable scroll on a webview

Hi all,
I'm trying to disable scrolling on a webview because I have this in another view which is already scrollable (I need it that way for other things). I tried the following

var webview= Ti.UI.createWebView({
    //html:xx,
    html:win.content,
    width:Ti.UI.FILL,
    left: 20,
    height:520,
    //right: 20,
    VerticalScrollEnabled: false,
    VerticalScrollBarEnabled: false,
    textAlign: 'left',
    top: 10,
    scalesPageToFit: false
});

But it didn't work. What's wrong with it?

  • The webview scrolls because your html content layout is bigger than the dimensions of the webView. You need to ensure your html content is not bigger than height:520
