  • Hi highly suggest to change approach. Loading the library in a webview eventually make your phone unstable and will crash your app. We used it in one project and I'm not planning on using it again. Ever.

    The problem with android is that you have to convert the blob in a base64 string inside your db, and then re-convert it back to blob in your titanium app. There's the link to a ticket at the end of that blog article.
