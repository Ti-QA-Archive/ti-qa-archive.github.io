TIBarcode does not work well with long barcodes

This is not really a question. More like a notification for appcelerator since I can not post the issue in the help desk. The barcode module does not like any code 39 barcodes over 11 digits long. I was creating an app that decodes automobile vin's and the barcode reader would not work. Testing it by printing out barcodes with varying numbers of digits i found that up to 11 it would read. After that it would not.

I have since moved over to tibar (the free community solution) which is working like a champ. You might want to look into changing the technology of your barcode reader to keep your modules "best in class"
