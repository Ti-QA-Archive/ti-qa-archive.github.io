  • i found the solution and i will share with u

    iam using a custom theme to hide action bar 

    <?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?>
<resources>
    <style name="NoActionBar"  parent="Theme.AppCompat.Light">
        <item name="android:windowNoTitle">true</item>
    </style>


</resources>

    but after i add this line 

    <item name="android:windowActionBar">false</item>

    it work and error message doesn`t appear again

    custom theme after modifications

    <?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?>
<resources>
    <style name="NoActionBar"  parent="Theme.AppCompat.Light">
        <item name="android:windowActionBar">false</item>
        <item name="android:windowNoTitle">true</item>
    </style>


</resources>
