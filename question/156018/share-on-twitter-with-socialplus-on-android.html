    • in my case, it wasn't an actual problem, it's just that the date wasn't set correctly on my testing android device, hence social_plus would generate a wrong timestamp cause it depends on the device's date & time.

      What you 'd do is verify if the date and time are set correctly on your device, if they are correct then your problem is not the same as mine was.
