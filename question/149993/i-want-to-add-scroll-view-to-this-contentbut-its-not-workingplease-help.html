  • Hi

    Chances are either the height of 350dp is less than the device height available space and/or the label content does not have a height greater than the available space.

    A scrollView will only actually "scroll" if the content size is greater than the parent size.

    A few things to tweak to make things easier when the content does exceed.

    • Anything that says auto in your scrollView code above - change it to Ti.UI.FILL
    • The auto in your label change to Ti.UI.SIZE
    • Change the view height from 350dp to Ti.UI.SIZE
    — answered March 25th 2013 by Malcolm Hollingsworth
