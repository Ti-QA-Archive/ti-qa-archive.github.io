App Rejected by Apple due to Crash On Launch iOS7

Hello fellow Titanium Developers,

I am having an issue with getting my iOS App approved by Apple. The Apple testers are saying the App consistently crashes on launch. The trouble is I or none of our testers are able to replicate the problem, it just works for us.

The Apple testers are using the latest iOS - 7.0.2 (iPad and iPhone, both crash). We have also tested against this version of iOS and we have no issue.

iOS version is a strong suspect as a previous build was approved by Apple about 2 months ago, and the only things that have changed in our build from then until now, is the App Name & icon, therefore no code has occurred.

I was wondering if anyone else has experienced any such issues with A) iOS7 crashes on launch, or B) being unable to replicate a crash that Apple can produce?

Some specifics…

The App targets: Titanium SDK 3.1.0 GA
