    Remove navBar hidden in the win instead of in Navigation Window and you would be good to go something like this would work in your case

    var win= Titanium.UI.createWindow({  
    title:'Amazing Newb App',
    backgroundColor:'#0099FF',
    navBarHidden: true
});
