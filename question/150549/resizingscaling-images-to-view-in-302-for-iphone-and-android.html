    I have been reading online that i should use imagefactory, but unless I am missing something, imagefactory doesn't seem to be available for titanium 3

    What makes you say this?
    Since you cant build modules with 3.0.x, you could only use 2.x built modules. The module api has changed since around 1.8.

    • ti.imagefactory-iphone-1.1.1.zip and/or ti.imagefactory-android-2.2.1.zip are the prebuilt modules. You can place these at the root of your project, and they will be extracted for you. However, best to place the ti.imagefactory folders inside the archives in ~Library&#x2F;Application Support&#x2F;Titanium&#x2F;modules&#x2F;{platform}
