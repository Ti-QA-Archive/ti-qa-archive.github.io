  • Hi,

    just use a normal View and add a label with your text to it, then call toImage() from the View. it produces a png or jpg, you cannot produce a "Bitmap" (if you meant the windows "bmp" format).

    var view = Ti.UI.createView({
    backgroundImage:"background.png"
});

var text = Ti.UI.createLabel({
    text:"example text"
});
view.add(text);
var imageBlob = view.toImage();
