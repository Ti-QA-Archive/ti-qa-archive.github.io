Alloy copies .svn folders to Resources folders

I have a client who uses SVN. When running the Alloy build, the compile process is copying all the the .svn folders from the /app folder and subfolders to the corresponding subfolders in Resources. This is wrecking havoc on my workspace and interaction with SVN when I try to check in real changes. I have been searching the forums and various sources to see if anyone has solved this issue yet. I see many references to problems, but haven't found the solution. Before I go hacking away at the alloy.jmk file to remove the .svn folders, I am curious if I am missing something in the configuration. I am using 3.1.3.GA and Latest Alloy version as of today.
