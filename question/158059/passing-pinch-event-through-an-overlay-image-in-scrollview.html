Passing pinch event through an overlay Image in Scrollview

I wan't to crop an image taken by the camera like the crop method used for the contact image in ios7. After taking the photo you can pan and zoom it into a circle. I tried to do it by a ScrollView. I placed the image inside the ScrollView, the Scrollview to the Window and the Overlay Image to the window, but with a greater zIndex than the Scrollview.

When i pinch the overlay i can't get it to work, that I can pass the pinch event to the ScrollView.

When I put the Overlay inside the ScrollView and turn bubbleParent to true, than the Overlay Image will be zoomed to.

So, perhaps anybody have an idea how this could be done.

Thanks,

Timo

  • Don't know if you've found a solution already, but you should set touchEnabled to false on the overlay view. This will pass the pinch event to to parent. Also, create the overlay view outside of the scrolllView, and position it absolute in the center.
