  • Hi

    I am not sure why you are wanting to put the app store badge in the actual app - this badge is designed to be placed on website and printed material to direct users to your app, if you place it in the actual app then they have already downloaded their app so do not need to be shown where to do that again.

    If you are placing the link outside the app then you can locate the link to your app from the iTunes Connect site where you create your app. As soon as you have set the name for the app there is a link inside the App Summary page View in App Store this is the link your app will eventually end up with.
