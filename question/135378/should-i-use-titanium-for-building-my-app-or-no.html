Should I use titanium for building my App or no?

Hi, I am a rookie as far as Titanium platform is concerned. I am building an application, details of which are as under::

Application type: mobile
Titanium SDK: 1.8.2
Device: iOS simulator
Host Operating System: MacOSX 10.7.2

Intended application is a simple password-locker where we can place a lock over the apps installed in the iphone as well as the pre-existing apps. Is building such an app feasible under the titanium platform i.e.,Does titanium provide features to fetch a list of all iphone apps as well as an option to prevent the apps from opening?
If no, then what approach should i take to develop such a kind of an app?

I am expecting a lot of views as well as replies from vast pool of experience that this forum holds.

Thanx Guys for helping!!

  • Due to the sandboxed nature of iOS, you cannot control another application. You cannot list other installed applications.

    It is possible on a jailbroken device but not on a regular iOS install.
