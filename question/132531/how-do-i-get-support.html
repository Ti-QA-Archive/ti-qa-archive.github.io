    There are also many developers like myself, Trevor Ward and many others who do freelance work to do things like code reviews and even training.

    Trevor and I are both Appcelerator Titans, and can provide great insight to app development and debugging of issues on a level Appcelerator normally wont touch (they dont like doing things like code reviews).

    On the flip side, Appcelerstor support will help you out more when you have real Titanium platform bugs when you need priority support on getting bugs fixed in the SDK itself
