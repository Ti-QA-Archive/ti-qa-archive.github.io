  • Accepted Answer

    Evidently, the issue was that the Titanium Mobile SDK had become corrupted on install. To fix, locate your SDK folder (you can discover this in Preferences > Studio > Titanium) and delete the SDK version under /mobilesdk/<platform>/<versionnumber>. Restart Studio and it should ask you to reinstall.
