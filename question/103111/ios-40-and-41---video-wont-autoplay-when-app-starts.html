iOS 4.0 and 4.1 - Video won't autoplay when app starts

Hello,

I have a couple of apps that starts with a short video autoplaying when a user launches the app.

It works well in iOS 3.2, 4.2 and 4.3 beta but won't work in 4.0 and 4.1 for some reason.

Here's my code in app.js:

// opening video
var videoWin = Ti.UI.createWindow({
    backgroundColor: '#000',
    navBarHidden: true
});

var video = Ti.Media.createVideoPlayer({
    url: 'media/intro.mov',
    fullscreen: true,
    movieControlMode: Ti.Media.VIDEO_CONTROL_NONE
});

video.addEventListener('complete', function(e){
    setTimeout(function(){
        videoWin.close();
        video.release();
    }, 1000);
});

videoWin.add(video);
videoWin.open();

Does anyone knows why?

Thanks a lot.
