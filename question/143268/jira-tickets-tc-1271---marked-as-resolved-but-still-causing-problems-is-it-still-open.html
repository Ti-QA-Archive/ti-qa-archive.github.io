    Hello Javier,

    There is absolutely no way in hell the guys at Appcelerator can keep an eye on every resolved ticket from the JIRA tool. Since the number keeps increasing each day, that would become too big of a task.

    Now, if you ever experience regression after upgrading to a newer version of the SDK, ou will have to create a whole new ticket for this specific issue related to this specific version. Of course, you can link to the previous ticket to avoid having to detail everything all over again (if the bugs are similar of course)

    Hope this clears this out for you.
