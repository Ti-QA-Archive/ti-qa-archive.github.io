  • I'm speaking about Titanium Mobile, I don't know if desktop API is different.

    As far as I know, your builds are not sent to appcelerator in any way. You can see a list of all your projects if you are logged in appcelerator wep page, clicking over your username on the top of the page and, after that, click on "APPS" on top menu. But is only a list of projects with access to analytics, no builds downloads at all.

    You can access to your iOs builds in development from iTunes (right click the app and select "reveal in finder"). And your builds for distribution (those you would upload to iTunes connect) in xCode organizer.
