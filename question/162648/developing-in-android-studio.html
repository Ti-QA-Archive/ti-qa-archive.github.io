  • Hi

    No idea - but if it helps, I use Notepad++ when using a PC and Sublime when on a Mac to write all my code and use studio just for compiling. No reason you have to use the Studio product if it does not fit with your needs.

    I do not have any Titanium language plug-ins to help or provide type-assist - I find the lack of them helps me remember every keyword and every parameter.

    I know a lot of people who use their favourite text editor and the CLI to manage everything else - they never use Studio. It is built so you can make the choices you want with your kit.
