  • Aperna,

    Appcelerator has been working towards consolidating the various versions of the jars.
    Be sure to keep up with your updates (3.4.1.GA is the latest release, with 3.5.0 in testing).

    If you have previous SDKs installed, you can look in $HOME/Library/Application Support/Titanium (on OSX) or in c:\ProgramData\Titanium (on Win32) in the modules directory. Both of those directories are hidden/system so, find them appropriately.
