  • Definition of Kitchen Sink #2 Merriam-Webster "being or made up of a hodgepodge of disparate elements or ingredients "

    So, its refers to an app that has a conglomeration of elements to show what can be done and how to go about putting those pieces together.

  • Kitchen Sink is an American idiom which usually reads, "It has everything but the kitchen sink", meaning the only thing it's lacking is the kitchen sink (insert sarcastic smirk). This demo app has "everything from the SDK but the kitchen sink". Thus, the name.
