Android: TextField with no decoration?

I'm looking for a way to have a TextField that has no decoration - no border, no special color, etc. It should look just like a label.

This is easy on the iPhone, but I haven't figured out how to do it on Android. Here's my code:

var notes_input = Titanium.UI.createTextField({
    hintText:'Your Notes', left: UI.edge, 
    borderWidth: 0, borderStyle: Titanium.UI.INPUT_BORDERSTYLE_NONE, 
    borderColor: 'white'
});

I'm setting the border color to white (my background color) and setting the width to zero, but I still get a gray border around the textfield.

Anyway to get rid of the border?
