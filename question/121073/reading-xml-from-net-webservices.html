  • I ran into the same issue. I worked around it by doing the following:

    var firstNames = xmlDoc.documentElement.getElementsByTagName('firstname');
var lastNames = xmlDoc.documentElement.getElementsByTagName('lastname');

    Then, I iterate through the collections …

    for (var i = 0; i < lastNames.length; i++) 
{
    var lastName = lastNames.item(i).text;
    var firstName = firstNames.item(i).text;
    .
    .
    .
}
