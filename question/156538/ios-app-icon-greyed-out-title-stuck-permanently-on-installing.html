iOS App Icon Greyed-Out, title stuck permanently on "Installing..."

App type: mobile
Ti SDK: 3.1.2 GA
Platform: iOS7 beta 6
Device: iPhone5
Host: OSX 10.8.4
Ti Studio: build: 3.1.2.201308091617

OK this is really maddening.

Installing on device gets through the compilation cycle (that is to say, app gets installed in iTunes) but once on the device, the app icon is grayed-out and app title says "Waiting"

When we click on the icon, app title changes to "Installing…" (still gray) and gets stuck there forever more.

Runs great in the simulator.

Have re-installed iOS7beta, have changed name and ID of application, have removed all instances of the app from iTunes and tried and tried with same results.

NOT: I am working on 2.0 of this application. There is a 1.0 version of this application in the app store. "Updating" the app to the 1.0 released version, fixes the broken app and proceeds to run great.

There are some notes in the Apple developer forum along these lines, but those are issues with apps in the app store, not a development and testing situation.

Help? Please.

    • How the app is built isn't relevant. When something on the phone isn't working and you need more info, sometimes hooking the phone up to your computer with the USB cable, and using that phone's normal tools is the answer.

      For iOS, that means launching Xcode, Window > Organizer, selecting Devices, and select Console under your iPhone.

      For Android, that means using adb or Android Device Monitor.
