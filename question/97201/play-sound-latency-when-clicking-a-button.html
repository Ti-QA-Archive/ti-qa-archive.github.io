Play Sound latency when clicking a button

I am willing to make a button/Image which fires a sound when clicked as soon as is pressed (like a drum simulator).
The latency should be very low (the time between the click and the sound playing).
The sound sample has to sound every time the button is pressed, even if the previous has not stopped (like in a real drum).

I have written the following code. However, sometimes the sound doesn´t play and I cannot get the sound to play repeatedly.

Any suggestions? The sound is very short (0.2 seconds) and has only 311 K. I am using SDK 1.6.0 and iOs SDK 4.2.

var sound = Ti.Media.createSound({url:'drum.wav', preload:true});

var play = Titanium.UI.createButton({
title:'Play',
height:40,
width:145,
left:10,
top:10
});

play.addEventListener("touchstart", function()
{ sound.play();
});

win.add(play);
