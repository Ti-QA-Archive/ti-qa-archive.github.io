  • Hi

    It is not possible for iOS and I can only hope it is not possible for Android, there is no Titanium built-in ability to achieve this.

    Whilst your app may respect the privacy of the user, there are so many people who would use this information and abuse it. Having this information would provide valid phone numbers and tracking information that would require a warrant in most countries.
