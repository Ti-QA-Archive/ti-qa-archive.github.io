    I do not fully understand your first question. What do you mean by sliding in the page when the tab is selected? Nothing happens when a table view is selected, unless you code it to open a page or animate something.

    Your second question is a no. There is a limit for how many Tabs a TabGroup can have before it cuts the rest of and adds in a pre-defined "More" tab, which has the rest of them listed in there. This is an Apple-programmed limitation, not Appcelerator.
