problems upgrading an app

I'm trying to update an app that I created a year and a half ago. Everything went fine, until tried to upload it to iTunes Connect. I got an error saying my "binary is not optimized for iPhone 5," but I don't want it to be if that means I lose support for older devices.

I'm using iOS SDK 5.0 and I've got Xcode 4.2.1 installed. Do I have to upgrade Xcode? I was hoping to avoid that, in case it creates new problems.

  • This is not a Titanium problem. This is between the developer, you, and Apple. Apple requires developers to keep up with tooling. That means updated versions of xCode, OSX, etc.

    but I don't want it to be if that means I lose support for older devices.

    You don't really have a choice in the matter if you are working with apples store. You can now upload a newer binary that may not support the older devices, and the older binary can remain. Check your iTunes Connect account for the information regarding this new (last 45 days) feature.

    I do understand your pain though. We had an app with updated features that we held for 6 months so as not to lose the older users. The new feature was a pleasant surprise.
