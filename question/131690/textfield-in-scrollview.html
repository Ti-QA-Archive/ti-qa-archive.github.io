Textfield in scrollview

I understand that the correct way to not cover a textfield with the keyboard is to put it in a scrollview. However, when the textfield is clicked, the scroll view scrolls up juuuust enough so that the very bottom of the textfield is touching the very top of the keyboard. Is there any way to adding some "padding" to the bottom of the textfield so that it's not actually sitting 1 pixel above the keyboard?

Thanks

  • you can use Scrollview scrollto(x,y) function in textfield listner and provide the height as how much above you want to set textfield , this function will automatically scroll view upward and your textfield will get visible.
