Best practice on opening and closing database?

I am tracking user location via gps and on every hit insert a record into the database. Sometimes I can get a few hits a second other times its a hit every 10 - 60 seconds..

Currently on every hit I open the database, insert record, then close database.
I'm wondering if it would be better to leave the database open until I am done tracking and then close it.

Any recommendations?

    The general rule of thumb is to close your SQLite connection after every query. Storing the retrieved data in an object.

    Memory woes an other issues can potentially infest your app otherwise.
