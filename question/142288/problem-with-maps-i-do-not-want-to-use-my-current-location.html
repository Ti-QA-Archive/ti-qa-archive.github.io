Problem with Maps, "I do not want to use my current location"

Hello to all you guys! I have a little problem with the use of Maps.

I need to render a particular location on a map, but I do not care also display the "current location". I can render the given position without problem, but I do not want me to be asked whether or not my current position, and then I do not want to appear on the map the user's current location.

I'll post a picture for you to understand my situation img

And this is the code I use for the map.

var info = Titanium.Map.createAnnotation({ 
    latitude:45.390072, 
    longitude:10.696306, 
    title:"Pizzeria Il Giglio", 
    subtitle:'Monzambano, MN', 
    pincolor:Titanium.Map.ANNOTATION_GREEN, 
    animate:true
});

var mappa1 = Titanium.Map.createView({ 
    mapType: Titanium.Map.STANDARD_TYPE, 
    region: 
    {latitude:45.390072, longitude:10.696306, latitudeDelta:0.01, longitudeDelta:0.01}, animate:true, regionFit:true, userLocation:true, annotations:[info]
});

Thank you all.
