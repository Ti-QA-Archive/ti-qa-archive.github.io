  • Hi antonio,

    Try indexing the fields of your database which are used in your queries. With indexed fields your queries should be a lot faster. For detailed help post your sql queries & your database structure.

    Here is a example:

    db.execute('CREATE INDEX IF NOT EXISTS some_index_name ON table_name(field_name)');
