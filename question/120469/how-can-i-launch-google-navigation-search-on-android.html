How can I launch Google Navigation Search on Android?

I wanted to share this with others who are having trouble with this.

Launch the search screen to pick a destination in Google maps Navigator.

var navIntent = Titanium.Android.createIntent({
  action: Titanium.Android.ACTION_MAIN,
  className: 'com.google.android.maps.driveabout.app.DestinationActivity',
  packageName: 'com.google.android.apps.maps'
});
navIntent.addCategory(Titanium.Android.CATEGORY_LAUNCHER);
Titanium.Android.currentActivity.startActivity(navIntent);

Launch directly into the navigation app while providing a destination.

var navIntent = Titanium.Android.createIntent({
  action: Titanium.Android.ACTION_VIEW,
  data: 'google.navigation:q=New+York+NY'
});
Titanium.Android.currentActivity.startActivity(navIntent);
