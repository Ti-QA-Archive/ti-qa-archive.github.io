    • Even though that closes the emulator, it still could be firing. (You can still open the emulator back up manually too) Have you tried setting a property in your app along with the Ti.API.info you have above? Then you could check that property upon the next fresh open of the app.

      //Within your eventListener...
Ti.App.Properties.setString('didItFireCloseEvent', 'Yes');

      //In your app.js

      var test = Ti.App.Properties.getString('didItFireCloseEvent');
Ti.API.info('Did it fire close event last time?: ' + test);
