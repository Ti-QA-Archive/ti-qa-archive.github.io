Android PACKAGE_REPLACED

I'm developing an android app that uses an urban airship module.
I am supposed to use PACKAGE_REPLACED to make the app know there is a updated version and rerequest push.
However, when I use this the app freezes on the splash.
In my manifest.xml
(com.appbundle is temporary bundle id. This is correct…)

<application...
<receiver android:name="com.urbanairship.push.GCMPushReceiver" android:permission="com.google.android.c2dm.permission.SEND">
<intent-filter>
                        <action android:name="android.intent.action.PACKAGE_REPLACED"/>
                        <data android:scheme="package"/>
                        <category android:name="com.appbundle"/>
                    </intent-filter>

This should be used in other projects as well, not only for urban airship…
