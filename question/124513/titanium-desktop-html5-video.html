  • This works for me(video and style as well):

    <video style="opacity:0.6;border:5px solid red;-webkit-transform: rotateZ(45deg);" width="320" height="240" controls="controls">
  <source src="http://www.w3schools.com/html5/movie.mp4" type="video/mp4" />
Your browser does not support the video tag.
</video>
