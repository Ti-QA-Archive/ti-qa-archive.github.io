How to cope with very large resolutions on mobile?

Hello all

I've created an app that looks good on iphone 3gs, iphone 4 and the HVGA screen with Andrid Emulator.

Now, when I test it on Nexus one, which apparently has very high resolution, everything looks tiny, to the level of unclickable buttons, very small inputs etc…

Is there a way of auto-resizing the app, or any way to cope with this?

    There are several ways of dealing with this… See the below post. However, if you have specified sizes and coordinates in normal pixels, you may want to just change them to dp. For example change width:100 to width:&#39;100dp&#39;…(note the quotes that are needed) that was all I needed. iOS treats this the same, but Android does what you want - makes it bigger
