  • Hi

    Not sure exactly what you are asking, but guessing.

    If you are asking how to show which menu button someone pressed so they know which section of the app they are in - then they do not change, menus are just to tell you (the developer) what to do next.

    If you wish to show users which MAIN section they are in, this would normally be done by;

    • Updating title bar text
    • combining tabs with the action bar
    • opening a new window for the thing they selected - like compose a new email
    • changing the UI to show an editing mode

    Other things tell the user where they are.

    Use some of Google's own apps for ideas and examples.
