    First of all… Databases are a bad place to store images. Too big, no reason for it. I would suggest that you store your images on the device, with a path in the database to where you find the image. This way, you keep the metadata about the image in the database and you keep the image data on the device. Better architecture and I expect your query times will improve by a whole lot.
