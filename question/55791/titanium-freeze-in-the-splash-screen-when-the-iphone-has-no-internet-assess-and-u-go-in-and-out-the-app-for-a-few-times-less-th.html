  • It is confirmed the problem is analytics. It happen to all app when there is no internet access.
    To recreate the problem simply:
    start your app in offline mode, wait at least 20 seconds, press the bottom button to exit, click your app again.
    The app will stuck in the splash screen forever.
    Turn off analytics will fix the problem. Thanks Daniel.
