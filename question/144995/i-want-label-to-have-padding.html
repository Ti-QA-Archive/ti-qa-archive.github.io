    Hey!

    You can try this: http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/3.0/#!/api/Titanium.UI.Label-property-backgroundPaddingLeft

    If it doesn't work, you have few other solutions: Add unbreakable spaces before your text, or add your label inside a view, and put left:20 as the only property for your label (all the other will be on the view).

    Hope it helps!

    Ivan_
