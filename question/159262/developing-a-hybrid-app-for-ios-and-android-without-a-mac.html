Developing a Hybrid App for iOS and Android without a Mac

I have just signed up and am very impressed thus far with the incredible support for the product, both in the forum and documentation. I watched the first " Preparing for mobile development" video and it references downloading the native SDKS from Apple and Android, but I work from a PC and can't load the iOS SDK's.

  • Hello Charles, welcome.
    The answer is -as you suspected- that it's not possible to build for iOS on Linux or Windows.
    You will need a Mac, with Xcode and the iOS SDKs to build your apps for Apple devices.
