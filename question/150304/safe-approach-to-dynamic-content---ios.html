Safe approach to dynamic content - iOS

This is my first time developing using Titanium and I just have a couple questions about a safe approach for App Store approval.

I know Apple prohibits "websites wrapped in an app" and "downloading of code." What I want to do is have various types of dynamic content populate the app, anything from a static xml file on a server to retrieving tweets with a hashtag, to videos hosted on YT or Vimeo. I would probably use a combination of native and webview to accomplish this.

This is sort of a situation where I just won't build it if there's no good chance of being approved, as our time window is pretty narrow (one month.) Does anyone have any advice on how feasible the above will be for app store approval? I don't think we'll be trying to deploy for Android, just iOS.

Thanks!
