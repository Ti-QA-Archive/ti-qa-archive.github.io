Trying to create a hybrid application using webview by integrating Sencha Touch2 framework with Titanium but with no luck. I am wondering whether there is any tutorial that will guide me to configure it on Titanium IDE. Help is appreciated!!!

  • You're probably not going to find a tutorial on the matter, but we did recently drop the Sencha KitchenSink demo application into our own KitchenSink demo just to show that it can be done. You can see how we did it here: https://github.com/appcelerator-developer-relations/KitchenSink/blob/master/Resources/ui/common/mashups/senchatouch.js

    The short of it all is basically to develop your Sencha application as you normally would, then just point your webview to the completed application. If you are having problems with something specific, you may need to share the specifics of your issue in order to get help.
