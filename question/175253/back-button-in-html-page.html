  • Hello IVIedia Power,

    What you can do is specifiy an event listener, inside your Titanium application, like this:

    Ti.App.addEventListener("app:back", function(e) {
    // go back by closing your webview or something
});

    In the html of your webview, specify the onclick like this: 

    <!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
    <head>
        <title> Your Web Page </title>
    </head>
    <body>
        <!-- Somewhere in your html: -->
        <div onclick="Ti.App.fireEvent("app:back");">
            Back
        </div>
    </body>
</html>

    When you click the div in your html, the app:back event is fired inside your titanium application, and you can go on from there to process your back action.
