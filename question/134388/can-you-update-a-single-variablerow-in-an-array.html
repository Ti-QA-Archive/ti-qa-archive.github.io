Can you update a single variable/row in an array?

Just curious, if you do a loop and narrow the array down to a couple rows or even a single row. Can you update one of the fields? Or do you have to remove it and reinject it into the array with the updated field.

var data = [
    {color:'red', pattern: 'stripes'},
    {color:'blue', pattern: 'stars'},
];

Can I simply update the pattern on one of the arrays or no?
