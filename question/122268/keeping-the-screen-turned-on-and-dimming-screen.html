Keeping the screen turned on and dimming screen

I would like to force the screen to stay on while my app is visible. Currently I am developing on an Android, soon we will support the iPhone as well. This is similar to the screen staying on when the the power is connected however, my users may not have a power source but still need the app visible. I would also like to be able to dim the screen to save battery power.
