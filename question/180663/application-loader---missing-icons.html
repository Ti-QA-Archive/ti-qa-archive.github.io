    Hi

    Inside your resources, you must be missing the relevant app icon images, which is why it is failing its check.

    Try creating the necessary files, placing them where your "appicon.png" is found. This guide should provide you with the additional information you need for file naming etc. Guide - Icons and Splash Screens

    Hope this helps.
