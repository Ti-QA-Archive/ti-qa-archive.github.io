  • Hi,

    To distribute application on App Store you need to create the certificate and provisioning profile. For this you have to first purchase the App Store account of 99 $. For doing this step you can use your father apple id. After that you need to login to developer.apple.com and log in to member centre, from there you can generate provisioning file and certificates. After that you can distribute app on App store from same apple Id. So here there is no need of your apple id.

    You can refer this link for more details: https://developer.apple.com/library/mac/Documentation/IDEs/Conceptual/AppDistributionGuide/SubmittingYourApp/SubmittingYourApp.html
