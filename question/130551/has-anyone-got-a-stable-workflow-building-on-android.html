Has anyone got a stable workflow building on android?

I've been using Titanium for quite a while now, intermittently. I recently started using it again on 1.8 hoping that it had evolved and matured since last time. Building for iPhone is working ok, but for Android it's like a russian roulette. I never know if it's going to work.
You click on play, and you'll get an unrelated error message, and while you wait for your app to appear on the android emulator… nothing happens.
So you click on play again, and then it works.

Here's a bunch of error messages that I still get occasionally:

  • java.net.SocketException: Address family not supported by protocol

  • Timed out waiting for emulator to be ready, you may need to close the emulator and try again

  • adb devices returned 0 devices/emulators

  • Error generating R.java from manifest

I want to know if anyone has this working in a stable manner.
Is there anything I can do to have it stable, or is it just buggy and do I have to accept it?
I am getting really frustrated with developing for android with Titanium.

My platform: Mac OSX Lion.
Everything up to date.
