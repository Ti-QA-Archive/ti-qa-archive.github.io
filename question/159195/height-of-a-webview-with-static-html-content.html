  • The behaviour is correct. The webview is designed so that if the content if larger than the dimensions of the webview, it will automatically scroll. Therefore to ask a webview to be the same 'size' as its content makes no sense. You need to specify an explicit height.

    Also, you are specifying a top AND bottom on your webview so height contradicts that

    Also, you are specifying Ti.UI.FIT which doesnt exist. I think you mean Ti.UI.FILL
