  • It looks like your style settings may be overwriting each other. Without context, it's hard to tell. You give your .instruShadow class

    top:"80dp"

    assign the class to the view instruShadowfirst, and then immediately overwrite it in the index.js

    $.instruShadowfirst.setTop("0dp");

    Also, unless you're planning to change the opacity and visibility, there's no need to set them. They already have those default values. Play around with your style properties. My guess is that's what's causing the problem.
