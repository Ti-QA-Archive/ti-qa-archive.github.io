  • Most of what is available to you will be in the Ti.Platform class, and you can look at the Kitchen Sink example source here. However, the phone number is not available. Apple considers it a potential source of abuse or privacy considerations and prohibits its disclosure. At least by public APIs (and private APIs get an app rejected from the store).

    Edit: Also refer to the Ti.Network class docs for information on the connectivity status.
