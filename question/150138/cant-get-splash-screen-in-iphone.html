Titanium SDK 3.0.0GA
iPhone4/OS5.1.1

I installed my Titanium app from Titanium Studio to my iphone, but app doesn't display splash screen. On iPhone Simulator, the same app displays splash screen normaly.

Could anyone advise me what to check?

    Hello Sherlock,

    - The filename must be Default.png with an uppercase D. Because this is platform specific, this file will typically be found in your project's Resources/iphone directory.

- You can provide a retina version of your splash screen, named Default@2x.png.
