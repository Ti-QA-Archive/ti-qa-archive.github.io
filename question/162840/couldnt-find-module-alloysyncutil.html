  • It sounds as though you've upgraded Studio, but not the CLI or SDK. There may be some compatibility issues as a result, and now not all files are being copied correctly with the build. You could try updating to the newest CLI, SDK, and Alloy versions. You could try building from the command line (outside of Studio) to eliminate Studio as the source of the error.
