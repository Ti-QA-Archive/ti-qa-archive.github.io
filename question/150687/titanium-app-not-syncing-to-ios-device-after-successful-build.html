  • I figured out that you pretty much have to turn on auto sync from your iTunes app to your device apps which I had previously turned off. This seems obvious but it's not coming from a native iOS development background since you can deploy directly to a device without iTunes being open or handling syncing. I think this is a fine method but should probably be documented here:
    http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/latest/#!/guide/Deploying_to_iOS_devices
