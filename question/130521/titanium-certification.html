  • Hi Ramkumar

    There are a few ways to do this.. The best way to decide is to look at the training section of the web site

    http://training.appcelerator.com/

    If your an indi member you should have access to the BNAPP videos which is what you need to study to get TCAD. If you not an indi member you can pay to use them. But be aware that with all the changes for 1.8.0.1 SDK a couple of the videos need to be updated.

    The other way is to attend one of the official courses.
