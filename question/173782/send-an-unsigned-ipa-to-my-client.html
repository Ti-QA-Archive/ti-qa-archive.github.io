I'm developing an app for a client that wants to publish the app in Android and iOS stores with his account, but he wants me to provide him with unsigned apk and IPA so he can sign them with his certificate without giving me access to its accounts. Their rule is not to allow external developers to access their developer accounts.

I can not provide him with the source code because the app is customized for every client but the client does not own the source code.

The unsigned apk is simple because it is generated in the android build folder, but I've not seen where to get an unsigned IPA and Titanium Studio does not let build for production without the production certificate
