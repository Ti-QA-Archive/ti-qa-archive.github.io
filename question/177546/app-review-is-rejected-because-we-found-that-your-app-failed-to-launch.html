App review is rejected because "We found that your app failed to launch"

I distributed my app created by Titanium Mobile but it was rejected.

Apple says that:

"We found that your app failed to launch on iPad running iOS 7.1.2 and iPhone 5s running iOS 7.1.2, on both Wi-Fi and cellular networks.

We encountered the issue when selecting the application on the Home screen - the app displayed a launch image then quit unexpectedly. This may be because iOS 7.1.2 uses a watchdog timer for applications; if an application takes too long to complete its initial startup, the operating system terminates the application. "

Of course my app can launch on my device (iPod touch, iOS 7.1.2) and startup time is less than 3 seconds. So I wonder what I should do.
Have anyone encountered and solved this problem??

  • You'll need to try testing on the devices Apple claims is crashing. Also, be sure to try running a production build on your device, not just a "Run on Device" dev build.

    • Startup time of 3 seconds is pretty long. You will often see guides suggest anything over 10ms (milliseconds) is too long. You should consider what you can do to optimize the load time – see what you can delay after the app initially opens, don't hit the network right away, lazy load information, etc.
