    This worked to get a vertical slider in a small popover on an iPad app. I had to adjust the position by trial and error.

            var t = Titanium.UI.create2DMatrix();
    t = t.rotate(-90);
    slider = Titanium.UI.createSlider({
        min:1.0,
        max:10.0,
        value:1,
        width:270,
        height:'auto',
        top:140,    
        transform:t,
    });
