Third application in App Store!!

Hi,

Just wanted to let you all know, i have my third app approved in the AppStore!
The title of the app: Cracking World (iPad only, sorry)

URL: http://rs.gs/crackingworld

Price: Free!!

In a short time, i was able to create/rebuild 3 apps with Titanium Studio for iOS. I want to thank the whole crew of Appcelerator for giving away this free tool!! Easy to use, fast way to build apps!!

At this moment i have 2 other projects running. One is a weather application for the Netherlands only, the other is for my URL Shorting service Real Short Good Short URL (RSGS) world wide use. Both already have an application in the AppStore written with PhoneGap, but are a bit unstable. After i finished it rewriting, they will be better, faster and a lot stable then the current versions.

Again Appcelerator, thanks for the tools and the support (also the community thanks for the support!)

André
