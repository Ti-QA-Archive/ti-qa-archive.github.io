Is there a way to get Native Message Bubbles or are there any alternatives to achieve this?

I am making a messaging app and was wondering if its possible to incorporate message bubbles that look or are native . If this is not possible are there any alternatives to achieve similar UI?

I read something about stretching images etc but i am having trouble with that. In case of an image my requirement would be that the max width should be 80% and the text placed inside should wrap like native bubbles or bubbles on popular apps(whatsapp,messenger etc).

Can someone help me create this UI. i am targeting IOS and Android . I have not been able to find a definite solution to achieve this .

  • Hi

    Yes there is, you make an image much smaller than the expected maximum, but contains all the unique details of each edge, then you use some built in capabilities of Titanium to have that image intelligently resize so that the centre section expands at the edge stay at each edge - this happens without any distortion.

    I answered this one a while ago - so rather than repeating it here - this is the link to that, it includes a step by step to the explanation I just gave.

    • How do I make a View with a bubble, but with a continuous border

    This is a little iOS focussed by android can do the same you just create something called patch 9 images. They are the same basic idea but you add little 'clues' into the image edge that do not show up but Android uses them as tips on how to stretch the image. I posted a related (no identical) article on the new tidev.io blog a few of us community members put together.

    • Android Splash Screens – Using 9-Patch Images

    The patch 9 info is related, the splash stuff is not. As is this not related to splash the special image location part can be ignored - your go where there normally do.
