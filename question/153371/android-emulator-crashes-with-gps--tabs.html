  • After a ton of debugging and pulling the app apart and slowly adding pieces back in, the culprit is the label definition inthe tss file

    [code]
    "Label": {
    top: "0",
    width: "100%",
    size:"24",
    textAlign: Ti.UI.TEXT_ALIGNMENT_LEFT
    }
    [/code]

    The iOS version of the app is more forgiving in managing this it seems. After yanking out that section, things look much better and seem to work.
