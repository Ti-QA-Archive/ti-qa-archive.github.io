Avoid tableViewRow selectedBackgroundColor

How to avoid createTableViewRow selected backgroundColor, I have used selectedBackgroundColor:&#39;transparent&#39;, even though background color changed, Is there any option available to avoid the selectedBackgroundColor while clicking on the row.

var nicknamerow = Titanium.UI.createTableViewRow({
    height:'auto', 
    selectedBackgroundColor:'transparent'
});

win.add(detailTable);

I am using Titanium 1.7.5
