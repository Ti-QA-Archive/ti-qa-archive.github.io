    • Well, I've found this solution:

      row=    Ti.UI.createTableViewRow();
view=   Ti.UI.createView({layout:'horizontal'});
button= Ti.UI.createButton({
    image:'/iconos/info.png',
    width:100});
label=  Ti.UI.createLabel({
    text:<some text>,
    width :Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformWidth - 100
});

view.add(button);
view.add(label);
row.add(view);

      and then 

      Titanium.Gesture.addEventListener('orientationchange', function() {
    <execute again the tableview show process>
})

    This problem occur due to the label width. It mean Your Label Width is greater than the view.

row.add(view);
