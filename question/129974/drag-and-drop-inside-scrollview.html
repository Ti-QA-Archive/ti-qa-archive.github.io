Drag and Drop inside ScrollView

Hi,

I have 100 images listed inside a ScrollView and I should be able to re-order those images using drag & drop.

I have tried using touch eventlistener, but it does not seem to listen when those images are being dragged. Instead, it's scrolling the view.

It seems that both touch events are not integrated correctly.

Is there any work-around solution to this matter?

Thanks

5 Comments

  • The scrollview will take over most of the touch events. It isn't recommended to nest one scrolling action within another for example a scrollviews within a tableview.

    Assuming iOS and vertical scrolling, maybe you could use a tableview instead? This would give you re-ordering options for free.

    — commented December 31st 2011 by Ben Bahrenburg

  • Thanks. I got it working by modifying dragDropKit module from marketplace. Basically, I create an extra layer on top of the scrollable/scroll view and remove this layer on touchend event.
