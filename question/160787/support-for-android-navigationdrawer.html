Support for Android NavigationDrawer?

If I'm incorrect, please someone correct me, either from the Titanium dev team or from the community. I have enjoyed (on the whole) developing several apps for iOS and Android using Titanium, but the lack of basic UI features such as the NavigationDrawer has really gotten to me during my last project.

Why does Titanium still have no exposed functionality for the Android NavigationDrawer. If you read Android's docs at google NavigationDrawer google docs, you'll see that it is a core piece of app design that google encourages developers to utilise.

How can Appcelerator expect devs to take the Titanium platform seriously when there's no support for a basic UI paradigm in 1 of their 2 supported platforms (the other being iOS obviously)?

  • One of the things most people love about this platform is that you are not limited to only the standard features. You can extend with native modules or Titanium Widgets. If that's not your strngth, more often than not someone else in the community has already done it. In the case of the drawer, for both Android, and iOS, I think the Napp modules do a fantastic job. Or read the blog entry here for other choices.
