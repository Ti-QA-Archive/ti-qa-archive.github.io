    Hello,

    there are two steps when removing objects from memory. First, you remove the label from the window, like: win.remove(label);. Then, you should do something like: label=null;

    The first one, will remove the native part, the second part will remove the javascript reference.

    Just remember that the GC won't kick in immediately.
