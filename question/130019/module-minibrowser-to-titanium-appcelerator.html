[Module] MiniBrowser to Titanium Appcelerator

Hi guys,

I just wanna share with you my new open source module.

https://github.com/Nyvra/titanium-appcelerator-mini-browser

It is a MiniBrowser, and it's very simple to use.

See an example:

var modalBrowser = new MiniBrowser({
    url:"http://www.google.com/",
    barColor:"#000",
    modal:true
});

var winModalBrowser = modalBrowser.create();

winModalBrowser.open();

Thanks!
