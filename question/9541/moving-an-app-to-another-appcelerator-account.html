Moving an app to another appcelerator account

Can I move my app to another appcelerator account for analytics? I tried changing my details in Titanium Developer and then recreating the app but it just recreated it in my old account (Bug in developer!!)

  • It might be really late answer for the original reporter. However, this might benefit the community to know that you can't transfer an app to another account for analytics. However, you can always upgrade your account if you need analytics. Please refer to the Appcelerator plans & pricing page for further details.
