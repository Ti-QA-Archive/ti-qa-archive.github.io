    Hi narasimha,

    you need to encode your input from the textfield with encodeURIComponent()

    var commercialurl = "http://173.230.148.65/openbravo/org.openbravo.service.json.jsonrest/BusinessPartner?_where=name%20like%20%27%25"+encodeURIComponent($.commercialField.value)+"%25%27";
