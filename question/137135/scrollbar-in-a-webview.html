    Can you please be omre specific ? What do you mean by "i can't see all of the content and i need to use a scrollbar" ? Horizontal or vertical scrollbar ?

    By any chance, you could add CSS statements giving width:100% and / or width:100% to your page if that's what you need.

    You could also try to add the following meta tag in your <head> tag :

    <meta name='viewport' content='width=device-width,initial-scale=1.0'>
