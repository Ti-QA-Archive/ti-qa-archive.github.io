Bug? Ti Studio with analytics enabed crashes on android device

On windows with ti studio and sdk 1.7.1,

I finally needed to look at my new project (first since swtiching from developer) on my device. I used the direct deploy to device. When I went to launch it, it would give splash screen, then force close.

I created a new project, left it virgin and sent that to run on device - and it did the same thing (so it wasnt my project)

Searching thru here I saw posts from a year ago when 1.6.0 came out of a similar problem.. one of the debugging steps I saw a few times was setting analytics to false in tiapp.xml so I did that and gave it a shot - and no more force close - both the test and my project ran fine.
