  • I am aware this is an old post but it's actually titleAttributes that is needed now.

    var tabGroup = Titanium.UI.createTabGroup({
    barColor:'#6d0a0c',
    tabsBackgroundColor:'#000',
    titleAttributes: {color:'#FFF'}
});
