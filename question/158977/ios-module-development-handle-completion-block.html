  • Hi All,

    Finally I have found it, may be this will help someone

    [self performSelectorOnMainThread:@selector(canConnectToServiceWithUrl:) withObject:url1 waitUntilDone:YES];

    here canConnectToServiceWithUrl is native method and url1 is the argument to passed for that
