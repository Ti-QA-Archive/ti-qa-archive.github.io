Push Notification

For iPhone:

I use this method Titanium.Network.registerForPushNotifications to register and receive notification.
What's I need:

  • If I'm using my app -> do nothing
  • If I'm outside of my app, the notification show. When I slide (at lock screen) or tap (at unlock screen), my app will open and show detail of the notification.

But my problem: In callback function of Titanium.Network.registerForPushNotifications, I don't know user is inside my app or come from outside of my app.

Please help!!

    Ti.App.addEventListener('resume',function(e){
         //if app is resume then you are using your app
});
