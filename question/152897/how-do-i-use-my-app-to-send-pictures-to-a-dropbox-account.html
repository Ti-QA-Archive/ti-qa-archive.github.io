    Hi

    If you are trying to use dropbox to host files from your app will rather than your app users connecting their drop accounts then you are very likely going to be breaching the terms of dropbox. This is because drop box is ot designed to host files for your app, it is used to transfer files in and out of dropbox accounts rather than hosting.

    You should instead look at something like ACS (Appcelerator Cloud Services), Amazon S3 or your own web space.

    Dropbox is not the solution for this problem.
