Migrating from PhoneGap to Appcelerator

Hi Appcelerators,

Our company will move from PhoneGap development to Appcelerator for various reasons.
However, we're wondering how the upgrade in terms of Local storage on the devices will/should be done.
Today we e.g. have a key called "deviceToken" which is a key generated by our servers, and it's stored ion the local storage. This is used for device identification and automatic login to the app.

Question:
When we do the migration (uppgrade) from PhoneGap to Appcelerator, will Accelerator be able to read the local storage from the former application?

This will allow us the continue the use of it's already installed identifier and just automatically signin the user upon new version, Appcelerator plattform.
As the app will have the same identifier (e.g. com.company.app), will that be enought to actually pick up the local storage?

What's your experience in plattform changes? What do we need to think extra about?

Application type: mobile
Titanium SDK: 3.0 Alloy
Plattform: iOS 6
Device: iOS simulator or iOS physical devices
Host Operating System: OSX 10.3

Thanks!
