I have just downloaded Titanium Studion (Java, Android SDK, NodeJS installed). After logging in it shows a pop up with Titanium Updates that I can not skip. I insist to update the following parts:
Titanium CLI, NODE.ACS, Alloy.

When I restart Titanium Studim i receieve a pop up stating Computing Sdk info has encoutered a problem. In details it states that NullPointerException has occurred.

    Hi Sergiy Mokiyenko,

    Thanks for your concern.

    Looks like your update is broken. There are some workarounds that might work for you.

    Workarounds

    • Restart the Ti Studio connected to the internet. It will try to update itself and will update and work.
      Click on Ti Studio on check for updates and let the Ti Studio update.

    • Remember: After updating your Ti Studio, you will need to restart the Ti Studio.

    • If updates don't work, then follow the installation and configuration and appcelerator titanium links to manually update and install the required tools or use commands for that purpose.

    Hope it'll help you to solve your problem.
