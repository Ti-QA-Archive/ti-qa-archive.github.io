  • Don't do a complete shutdown of the computer. Hibernate or go into sleep / standby mode, and Studio should remain logged in and allow you to continue to work.

    This of course is not a real solution either – there are times you must reboot for a variety of different reasons. But a full shutdown is really not needed on a routine basis, even when traveling.

    • You don't need to use Studio to develop Titanium applications. You can use your editor and build on the command line if you please. There are some limitations with doing so, like you can't build for more than one platform using the cloud, but you can build for your platform your working on.
