My new app launched - Rippleffekt!

I've developed about a dozen apps with Appcelerator, and this has been the most daunting. Thanks for making it easy to develop!

http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/rippleffekt-for-iphone/id561339777?mt=8

Rippleffekt creates temporary private groups revolving around events thereby promoting
active social participation rather than passive voyeurism.

The events, i.e., "Ripples", can range from an impromptu dinner with friends, a bachelor/bachelorette weekend, a week-long business
convention/conference, the months of build-up from an engagement to the wedding ceremony, and an indefinite period of time dedicated to a child's memories.
Rippleffekt optimizes social experiences by capturing and organizing all of the social content (photos, videos, check-ins, updates, missed connections, and comments)
revolving around the events that define individuals��� friendships, life, and shared experiences with the people that matter the most.
