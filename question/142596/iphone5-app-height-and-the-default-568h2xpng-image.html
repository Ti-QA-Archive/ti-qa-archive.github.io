iPhone5 app height and the Default-568h@2x.png image

It very useful to find out that all that is needed to make an app iPhone height compatible is adding a splash image called Default-568h@2x.png - with dimensions of 640 X 1136 pixels.

This works fine, but has anyone else noticed that on launch, after showing the tall splash image, you briefly see a flash of the shorter one (stretched, so is noticeable) before opening the app?

Using 2.1.3 Ti SDK, BUT I'm still on Snow Leopard and not targeting iOS6 SDK yet (due to XCode 4.2)

Is it possible that I just have to bite the bullet and 'update' my OSX to Mountain Lion? (I hope not, since I can't stand the Lion interfaces).

As a side note, I managed to stay on XCode4.2 on Snow Leopard by hacking in the iOS5.1 SDK requirements from the dmg installer in to my local environment using these instructions. I have not tried with iOS6 yet : http://chadscira.com/post/4f66b44dbc2f008577000001/iOS-SDK-5-1-on-Snow-Leopard
