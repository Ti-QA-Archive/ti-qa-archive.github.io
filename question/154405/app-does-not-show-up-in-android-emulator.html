  • Hello Casper,

    I recently corresponded with another developer who ran into a similar issue. After a reinstall of the alloy and titanium npm modules this fixed his problem. Also Check that your emulator's API level is <= android:minSdkVersion in your manifest, updating to a new Android SDK can sometimes raise your API levels on the emulator. Try both of these possible resolutions and let me know if this fixes it for you.

    Best,

    Carter
