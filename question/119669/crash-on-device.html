Crash on device

Is there a way to get a stack trace or crash log info from an android device? My application crashes on my device but not in the emulator and I don't know why. Is ADB the answer?

Thanks
Gino

    In the tools folder of the android SDK you have ddms. Run it and after this start your app on the device. You can see in realtime what the device is saying :)
