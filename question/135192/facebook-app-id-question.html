  • When you are in your Settings > Basic page on Facebook developer site, all you need is the website checked. This will still allow Facebook to provide you with a App ID and a App Secret key. Place them in your app remembering to add your property to your tiapp.xml as well as where you initialise it.

    eg

    <property name="ti.facebook.appid">REPLACE_WITH_YOUR_APP_ID</property>`

    and 

    Titanium.Facebook.appid = "REPLACE_WITH_YOUR_APP_ID";
Titanium.Facebook.permissions = ['publish_stream', 'read_stream'];

    You should then be able to publish to the users wall.
