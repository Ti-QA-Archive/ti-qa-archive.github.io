iOS 7 - right / leftNavButton are not showing in window of NavigationWindow?

I'm trying to display the Right and LeftNavButtons, though they are not displaying, though if I tap on the area (where the button is supposed to be displayed), the event triggers, it's just not displaying… any reason as to why?

var closeBtn = Ti.UI.createButton({ title:'Cancel', tintColor:'#000000', color:'#000000', backgroundColor:'#FFFFFF'});
    closeBtn.addEventListener('click', function(){
        navWin.close({animated:true});
    });

    var self = Ti.UI.createWindow({ backgroundColor:'#000000', navTintColor:'#000000', barColor:'#C7EFED', translucent:false, tintColor:'#ff8941', leftNavButton: closeBtn, navBarHidden: false,
                                    titleControl:Ti.UI.createLabel({ text:'Window Title', font:{fontSize:21, fontFamily:'AvenirNextCondensed-Regular'}, color:'#3A4950',})
    });

    var navWin = Ti.UI.iOS.createNavigationWindow({
        modal: true, window: self
    });

note I am able to see the Window Title, and Nav Bar, just not the Right/Left NavButton.

    The right/left nav buttons take on the navTintColor as set on the window. So, you have a black bar with black button text. Try setting self.navTintColor to something like #fff.
