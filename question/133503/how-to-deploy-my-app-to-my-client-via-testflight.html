How to deploy my app to my client via TestFlight?

I installed TestFlight and registered it on my iPad. My client did the the same. I see him in my Team on TestFlight. So now we're stuck…I don't know how to actually get the app to him. I saw some reference about uploading something to Apple, but I already registered his UDID on developer.apple.com. Not sure if I did that right. I can Export his Device, and I get a little text file with his UDID in it. I have no idea what to do with it. TestFlight does not explain this part at all. My app appears in MY TestFlight on MY iPad – but doesn't show up for him. Help.

  • Download the Provision Profile from Apple, follow the instructions in [https://wiki.appcelerator.org/display/guides/Deploying+to+iOS+devices] - the 'Build your app, embedding the profile within the app's bundle' section. Once you do this, the App will be installed into your iTunes. Go to the project\build\iphone\build\Debug-iphoneos directory and you'll find an .ipa you upload to testflight.

  • Add their deviceid to our Team Profile on the Apple Dev website

    redownload the profile (or refresh in Xcode Organizer)

    under your build settings, change the 'release' setting to use the Team profile

    edit your build/run, so that Archive runs the 'release' setting

    archive your project

    when it finishes building and opens Organizer, click the 'share', and upload that file to testflight

    Send other person the link generated at the end of the Testflight

    The end
