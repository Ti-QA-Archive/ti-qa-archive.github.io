Change button color on iOS?

You can easily change the button-colors (iPad) of ButtonBars by applying style:Titanium.UI.iPhone.SystemButtonStyle.BAR to it.

However when you do that for Buttons it adds an arrow-icon to the button and it doesn't look like it should. So how do I change a buttons color?
