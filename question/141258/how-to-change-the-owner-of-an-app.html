  • I needed to do this myself and eventually found the solution below if you don't want to deal with support(In my experience it's either pay the rather large(if you're small company/single developer) $$ or support is limited). It seems long but it shouldn't take you more than 15 minutes or so.

    1. Deactivate the app for the account you're moving it FROM.
    2. Rename(Outside of titanium) the folder holding all of your files too YourAppName-bak(You'll need this later) and remove it from your titanium workspace(Project explorer -> Right click -> Delete).
    3. Sign out of the account you're moving FROM.
    4. Sign into titanium with the new account.
    5. Create a new mobile project with the exact same name as your app(You should be able to since you renamed the app folder and removed it from the workspace)
    6. Once the new app has been created open the tiapp.xml and copy the settings from the tiapp you've saved in YourAppName-bak into the "new" app(modules/properties), but DO NOT copy the guid property from it.
    7. Save tiapp.xml.
    8. Move all other files/directories from YourAppName-bak into the new app folder.
    9. Build/Run the project and it should now show up in the new account.
    10. Delete YourAppName-bak after you're 100% certain the transfer is complete.

    Keep in mind if you've got prior Analytics/Cloud stuff I'm fairly certain it will not transfer over.
