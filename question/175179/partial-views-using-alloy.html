Partial Views using Alloy

Hello all.

I have the index.xml in my project. It made sense to me to separate the register and login elements. (This is because I plan to reuse them in different pages later in my project)

So..I would like to have something like this in my index.xml

<Alloy>
    <PartialView>login.xml</PartialView>
    <PartialView>register.xml</PartialView>
</Alloy>

I hope my question makes sense. Thank you for your help.

James

    Well, you will need a container (view or window) to hold your two subviews.

    Use require() in your xml or Alloy.createController() in your js to pull in your subviews and add them to the parent container.
