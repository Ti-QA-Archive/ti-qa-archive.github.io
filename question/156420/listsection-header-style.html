ListSection header style

    Hi

    ListView and in this case ListSection is designed for speed and data mapping rather than the TableView and TableViewSection which are more view and dynamic layout based.

    You are correct there is no option for ListSection.headerView, only ListSection.headerTitle so this is based on the standard UI elements.

    This may change in the future but as of right now - no options I can see.
