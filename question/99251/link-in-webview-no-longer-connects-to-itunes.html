Link in WebView no longer connects to itunes

I have a link in a WebView on the iphone that goes to other apps in itunes:

http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/getting-started-financial/id357479934?mt=8

This link worked in the past but now it does not. The link also works fine in the Safari browser. I am running iOS 4.2 with Titanium SDK 1.5.2. What can I do to fix this? Any help is appreciated.

  • WebView (or more precisly the WebKit) won't handle iTunes links correctly.
    The WebKit is not a Safari!
    You can use the Titanium.Platform.openURL method to open the iTunesApp directly from your app.
    Maybe you can use this for some kind of workaround?
