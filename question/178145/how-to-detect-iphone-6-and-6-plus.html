  • Another method is to use Ti.Platform.model to get the specific model ID. For example:

    Ti.API.info(&#39;Model: &#39; + Ti.Platform.model);

    An iPhone 6 will return iPhone7,2, and the iPhone 6+ will return iPhone7,1.

    Other model IDs can be found here.
