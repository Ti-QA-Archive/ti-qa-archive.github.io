orientation Landscape Left / Landscape Right

When orientation change I always get Landscape Left, even though the device was rotated to the right, is there a way round that?
The app is locked to portrait mode in all windows apart from when the user launch the camera (I am using an overlay).

  • Hi

    Short answer - no.

    This is from the documentation;

    • On Android, Mobile Web and Tizen, returns the orientation of the current window, and never reports the "reverse" orientations (LANDSCAPE_RIGHT and UPSIDE_PORTRAIT).

    Android does not report the right orientation, only that it is landscape*.
