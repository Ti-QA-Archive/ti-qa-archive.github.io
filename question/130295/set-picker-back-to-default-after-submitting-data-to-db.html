  • I got this working for me… Might not be the best way, but its working:

    picker.setSelectedRow(0,0);
picker.setSelectedRow(1,0);
picker.setSelectedRow(2,0);
picker.setSelectedRow(3,0);
picker.setSelectedRow(4,0);
