Android Video Thumbnail

My application will record a video that will then be played through the app video gallery. I have looked all over the place for a way to get the player to display a thumbnail of the video prior to playing it like it happens in iOS. I am wondering if anyone has figured out a way to simply capture a video frame while recording the video to use for this purpose?
