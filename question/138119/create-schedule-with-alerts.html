Then all events choosen displayed in an own window .

When one of the events is 1h away then make some sort of alert even if the phone is locked and the app closed. (Backgroundservice?)

    I think Push Notifications fits best in your case, but it would require a backend and synchronization inorder to be able to send the notifications to the right User.
    You could also use the calendar, if it provides all you need.

    Backgroundservice wont work since its being killed after 10 minutes.
