Inserting an Image...not showing up

Titanium Mobile question- working on a MAC- building an app for the iPhone, iPad, and Android.

I know this is so basic, in fact probably so very basic that its probably never been asked on here because I cant find the answer. Being experienced in Xcode and familiar with Javascript, Im new to Appcelerator and am missing something on getting my logo image in place on my windows. My image is 320x57 pixels and Im trying to get it to appear at the bottom of all 4 windows accept the main tab/ window. Im placing it as a label per the documentation and have tried manipulating the "win2.add"'s. Basically, tab 2 will have the 5 tabs at the bottom, the logo above that, and then a webView…and then finally, the Navbar at the top.

Here is my code:

var win2 = Titanium.UI.createWindow({
backgroundImage: 'images/pdabackground.png',
title: 'Daily Reports'
});
win2.tabGroup = tabGroup;

var logo = Ti.UI.createLabel({
bottom:0,
right:10,
height:"auto",
width:"auto",
image:'toplogo.png'
});

var tab2 = Titanium.UI.createTab({
icon: 'images/barchart.png',
title: 'Daily Reports',
window:win2
});

var webview = Titanium.UI.createWebView({

    url: 'http://secure.preschooldailyapp.com/student-reports',
    height:'auto',
    width:'auto'

});

win2.add(webview);
win2.open();
win2.add(logo);

I have tried putting the "win2.add(logo);" above the add webview. I have tried putting it in the middle, between the add webview and win2.open, and no matter what my logo does not appear. What am I missing?

Im sorry I need to learn how to use the styling to make the code in my questions easier to read. How do I put styling to my code so you can view it as it sits in my app?
