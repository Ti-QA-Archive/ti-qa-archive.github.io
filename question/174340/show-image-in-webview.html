  • Hi Shekhar Saraf,

    You did not share the detailed testing environment information behind your problem. But I have tested this issue on classic project for both android and iOS platform and it seems great on my side. Please take a look of my testing code below.

    var win = Ti.UI.createWindow();

var webview = Ti.UI.createWebView({
    html:'<html><body><img src="images/appcelerator.png"></body></html>',
    backgroundColor:'#fff'
});
win.add(webview);
win.open();
