Make scroll view scroll even it doesn't need to

All,

Anyone know of a way of making a vertical scrollview scroll even it the content doesn't require it to be able to? I have a scrollview with dynamic content. Sometimes the content doesn't fill the whole view, so the scrollview doesn't 'need' to scroll but I would still like it to be able to. I know of one way – adding a big empty view to the bottom – but I was hoping there was a slightly more elegant method.

Thanks!

    Hi Cord,

    The point of using scrollview is, it adapts with the dynamic height. Scrollview will not scroll until the height is more than your screen size vertically. So it doesn't matter how your content height changes, scrollview will adjust accordingly. Please refer this. Yes, you can use an empty view always to make the scrollview scroll whatsoever, but again it is not elegant you know. What's the point in it after all :)
