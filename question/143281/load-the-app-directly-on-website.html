    • Open up safari and head to the website that you would like to make a shortcut for.
    • Once you are there tap on the Share button at the bottom of the screen.
    • Tap Add to Home Screen
    • You can then change the name of the icon to what you would like it to display as and press Add in the top right corner
    • After you tap this the iPhone will add a shortcut to the iPhone’s Home Screen.

  • While such a this is feasible using Titanium.

    I'm afraid your app would be rejected once you try to publish it. Apple's rules are very clear on that, you can't publish an app that simply embeds a mobile site.

    If that's what you want to do, I recommend you make it easier for your users to add your site to their home screen. (as explained here)
