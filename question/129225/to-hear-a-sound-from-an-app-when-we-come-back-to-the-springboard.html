To hear a sound from an app when we come back to the springboard.

Hello,

I've tried to found a solution for my problem, i found severals, but none of them works. I'd like to launch a sound in my app and to be able to hear this sound from the springboard. Does anybody would have a solution ?

    Need to set a background audio mode

    In TIAPP.xml:

    <iphone>
        <backgroundModes>
            <mode>audio</mode>
        </backgroundModes>
</iphone>

    In you app.js (although this may now be deprecated):

    Ti.Media.audioSessionMode = Ti.Media.AUDIO_SESSION_MODE_PLAYBACK;
