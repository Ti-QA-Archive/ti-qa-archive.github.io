RemoveEventListener() alloy

Greetings

I need to remove an event listener; I use the alloy so, this is my .XML:

<Alloy>
    <View class="header" id="header">
        <Require type="widget" src="fileManageUtils" id="fileManageUtils" name="fileManageUtils" />
        <View class="headerview" id="headerview">
            <ImageView class="logo" id="logo" onClick="clickbotonHome">
            </ImageView>
            <Button class="backButton" id="backButton" onClick="clickbotonback" >
            </Button>
            <Button class="btnsearch" id="btnsearch" onClick="clickbotonsearch" >
            </Button>
            <Button class="languageButton" id="languageButton"  onClick="clickbotonlanguage" >
            </Button>
        </View>
    </View>
</Alloy>

and on my .js i use:

$.languageButton.removeEventListener("onClick",clickbotonlanguage);

Throws me :" remove invalid number of arguments. Expected 1 but got 0"

Any idea how can I remove my event listener?

Thanks

  • Because onClick isn't a valid event according to Titanium's Button documentation.
    Try: $.languageButton.removeEventListener("click", clickbotonlanguage);
