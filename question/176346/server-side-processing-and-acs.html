Server Side Processing and ACS

Hello,

I recently started using ACS and I think I have the basics. I am storing product data in Custom Objects and Fields. Now I need to process the data on the server side.

What I am trying to do is calculate the average rating for a product. When a user rates a product that rating is added to the list of ratings for that product. I want to be able to re-calculate the average product rating and store it in the "average" field for that product every time a user's rating is uploaded to ACS.

I am thinking of creating a Node.ACS service to do this. Is there a way to detect a change to an object in ACS and then act on it? Or should I make a call to my ACS.Node service every time a rating is uploaded to have it calculate the average and update the field in ACS?

Any advice/ideas welcome.

Thanks!
