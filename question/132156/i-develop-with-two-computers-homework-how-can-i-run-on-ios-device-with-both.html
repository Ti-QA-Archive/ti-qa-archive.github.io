I develop with two computers (home/work), how can I "Run on iOS Device" with both?

I first started developing with iOS at home, which has my home iTunes account synched with my iPhone. When I want to test the app on my device while at work the app goes into iTunes but I can not get the app onto my phone.

— asked February 15th 2012 by Brett Ryan

    You need to create another provisioning profile for the second computer, and from the original computer you were working with you need to export your developer profile and import it to the new computer.
