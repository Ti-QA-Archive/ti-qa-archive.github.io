  • Do your tableView rows each have a unique 'className' property? That can work wonders for performance. (ymmv : depending on layout etc.)

    Are your imageView sources remote? That can be an issue sometimes (especially without a defaultImage set) - I use a tableView for a Twitter I've built and scrolling is smooth as. Only 'jitter' can sometimes happen with image source urls.

  • Solution: Seems there's a problem with borderRadius.

    If your tableView contains an imageView, and that imageView has a borderRadius, try getting rid of the borderRadius (and borderWidth, just to be safe) and see if that fixes the problem. It did for me.
