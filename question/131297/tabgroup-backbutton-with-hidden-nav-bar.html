tabGroup-Backbutton with hidden Nav-Bar

Hi,

I have a tabgroup with a hidden Nav-Bar and I would like to have a custom back button like it is in the Nav Bar.

When I make a button and add a click event with win.close() everything works fine in the iphone-emulator. The window slides away and the previous window shows up. On the iPhone the window just disappears without animation.
When I add my own animation it's not the same.

So how can I assign a custom button with the same event as when somebody clicks on the back button in the navbar?

Regards,
Joscha
