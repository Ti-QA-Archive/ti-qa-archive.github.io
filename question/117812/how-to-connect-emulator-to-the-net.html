  • Florent

    Note that although Titanium Developer launches the Android emulator, the emulator works completely independently and thus does not rely on TiDev to function. Hence, the problems you describe are separate issues, although the cause may well be the same.

    The Android emulator should automatically be able to connect to the Internet, if your local machine can successfully do so.

    To help you with your issues:

    • what OS are you using?
    • what type of network are you on? What firewall/router is between your machine and the Internet?
    • are you behind a proxy? If so, you need to configure your local proxy settings.
    • are you using local firewall or antivirus software? Try disabling them temporarily to see if they are interfering with Titanium or Android
    • can you successfully ping an Internet-based host? (try ping www.yahoo.com, which works for me)

    Cheers
