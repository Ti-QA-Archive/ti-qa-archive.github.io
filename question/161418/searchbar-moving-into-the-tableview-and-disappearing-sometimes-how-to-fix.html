  • I'm pretty sure you can add the SearchBar to the TableView like this:

    <TableView id="addList">
    <SearchBar id="additiveSearch"></SearchBar>
</TableView>

    I would also try and separate out the styles into an accompanying TSS file, and also try to be explicit with widths and heights.

    Finally, you should make use of Ti.UI.SIZE and Ti.UI.FILL, if your View is going to have a width/height of 100% then you should use Ti.UI.FILL.
