Hey everybody,

I have a background service that check everyday if there is an event in the database "today". But in iOS, after a little time, the background service stops.

Is there a way to prevent this?

And I'm not using audio or location, just checking the database!

I'm using Titanium SDK 2.1

  • background service on iOS is only for task completion and terminates after 10 minutes or whenever the phone feels the urge to free resources. Setting the background mode to audio or location without actually using it will get the app rejected.

    Have you thought about push notifications?

When invoked, an alert dialog is displayed containing a "Close" button to dismiss or a customizable "View" button to bring the application into the foreground.
