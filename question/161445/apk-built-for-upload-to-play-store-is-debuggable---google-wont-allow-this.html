APK built for upload to play store is debuggable - Google won't allow this

The title says it all - the APK I built is apparently debuggable, and Google Play ( quite reasonably ) won't allow this.

I used the standard Package > Android App Store build using the 3.2.0.201312191547 IDE - I don't know why it would be building a debuggable app. But since it is, how can I fix this?

  • SDK: 3.1.3 GA
  • IDE: 3.2.0
  • Platform: Android 2.3+
  • Host OS: Mac OSX 10.9 (Mavericks)

    Maybe you have debuggable turned on in your tiapp.xml.

    <application android:debuggable="true" />

    http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/3.0/#!/guide/Maintaining_a_Custom_AndroidManifest.xml
