  • Hi

    Short answer; NO.

    Longer answer, there are many security implications to this that providing such information would allow many opportunities to people with bad intentions that it is simply unwise. Add to this the issues many of the OS creators have faced about allowing unique device tracking to users and you have a bad mix.

    If you were to achieve such a thing on an iOS device you would likely be banned from the App Store anyway.
