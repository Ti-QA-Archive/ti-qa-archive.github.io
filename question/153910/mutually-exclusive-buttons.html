  • Hello,

    you can always play with the touchEnabled feature. Something like 

    button1.addEventListener('click',function(){
button2.setTouchEnabled = false;
});

    (just from the top of my head, not take it literally).

    Best,

    Mauro

  • As mauro said you can use his code :

    button1.addEventListener('click',function(){
button2.setTouchEnabled = false;
});

    or can do another thing 

    button1.addEventListener('click',function(e){
button2.hide();
});

    you can use hide() and show() methods for this.
