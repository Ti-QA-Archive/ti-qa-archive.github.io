OptionsDialog and TableView question

Hi,

I have a tableview, when I click on any row, it shows a dialog.

How can I know the row that I clicked after click on a dialog option ?.

Here are my code:

My row:

var row = Ti.UI.createTableViewRow({
                title:'' + resultSet.fieldByName('nombre') + '',
                uid:'' + resultSet.fieldByName('id') + '',
                height:'40',
                className:'row'
            });

tableview.addEventListener('click', function(e) {

    dialog.title = e.row.title;
    dialog.options = ['Option 1', 'Option 2', 'Option 3'];
    dialog.destructive = 1;
    dialog.cancel = 2;
    dialog.uid = e.row.uid;

    dialog.show();

});

dialog.addEventListener('click',function(e)
{
    Titanium.API.info('UID:' + e.uid);

});

The result is: [INFO] UID:undefined

Thanks!!!!

    Mateo

    Declare a variable outside of your events, and then set it to the uid inside of the tableview click event. It will then be available to your dialog click event.
