Linked Contacts on Titanium

Hi!

I'm searching how can i get the Linked Contacts of a Contact on the Addressbook with Titanium.   • Hi Javier,

    right now Linked Contacts are not supported by Tiitanium, so it's not possible to actually access a Linked Contact data just as if it was a single unified contact.
    You can of course normally access to contacts you have in your address book.
