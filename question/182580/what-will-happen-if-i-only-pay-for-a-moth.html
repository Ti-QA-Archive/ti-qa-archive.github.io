I'm using the new Appcelerator Studio. I'm a new user of Titanium and I'm really disappointed because it's not free anymore.

What will happen if I only pay the monthly plan once just in favor of export and publish my application?

  • Apps are published in Google Store or Apple Store which is no way connected to Appcelerator. Hence once the application is published in either/both of the stores, it does not matter you continue with Appcelerator or not. But, you need to maintain the code and provides updates and fixed, for which you need to continue with Appcelerator.
