Default to numbers keyboard (but not numbers only!)

Hi

I am trying to make a TextField default to the numbers view of the keyboard on focus, the same as e.g. the "Zip" field in the iPhones contacts/addressbook app. I know the KEYBOARD_* constants of the Ti.UI module, none of those seem to do the trick though, as I do not want to disallow alpha characters - I just want the numbers to be autofocused.

The field will be to describe a value, which in most cases will be a monetary value, and therefor a number, but in some cases it may be something like "2 pieces".

I am working with Titanium SDK 2, iOS 5.1, iOS Simulator on OS X 10.7.

Thanks for any help!

    hi,

    you can use 

    txt.keyboardType = Titanium.UI.KEYBOARD_NUMBERS_PUNCTUATION;
