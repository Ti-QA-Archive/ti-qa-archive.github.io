Can't play videos from applicationDataDirectory on iPad 2

Application type: mobile.

Titanium SDK:1.8.2.

Platform & version: iOS 5.0.1.

Device: iPad.

Host Operating System: OSX 10.6.X.

Titanium Studio: 2.0.1.201204132053.

I can't play a video stored in applicationDataDirectory (black view with loading image) but I can if I compile it inside Resources folder. My code is the next one:

var videoObject = Ti.Media.createVideoPlayer({
//url:'prueba.mp4',
url:videoPath,
backgroundColor:'#111',
mediaControlStyle:Titanium.Media.VIDEO_CONTROL_DEFAULT,
scalingMode:Titanium.Media.VIDEO_SCALING_MODE_FILL,
movieControlStyle:Titanium.Media.VIDEO_CONTROL_EMBEDDED,
height:'400dp',
width:'600dp',
autoplay:false,
});

I get the videoPath variable with the next code:

var videoPath=Ti.Filesystem.applicationDataDirectory+'prueba4.mp4';

I also try:

var videoPath=Ti.Filesystem.getFile(Ti.Filesystem.applicationDataDirectory,'prueba4.mp4');

// I tested url=videoPath and url=videoPath.nativePath() without success.
