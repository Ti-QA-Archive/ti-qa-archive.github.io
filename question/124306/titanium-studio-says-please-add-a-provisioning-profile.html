Titanium Studio says "Please add a provisioning profile."

When I try to package my app for distribution via the App Store using Titanium Studio, it doesn't seem to recognize the provisioning profile I have installed.

I have just recreated all my provisioning profiles and certificates fresh and they all check out in Xcode and Keychain access.

Its just that TiStudio doesn't "see it". And I can't find where in TiStudio I can "point" it to my provisioning profile.

What am I missing here?

    As you mentioned, (I am just repeating in the answer to make it clear for those down the road with the same 'issue'). There is an upload link in the packaging dialog that is used to "upload" the provisioning profile to Ti Studio.
