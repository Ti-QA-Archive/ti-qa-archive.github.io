  • Thomas

    Without knowing your environment, it's difficult to deduce the causes of this. However, the following has been tested on Android 1.7.X and works for me:

    Asset: Resources/web/bg.png

    Resources/app.js:

    Ti.UI.setBackgroundColor('blue');

var win1 = Ti.UI.createWindow({
    backgroundColor:'red',
    fullscreen:false,
    exitOnClose:true
});

var webview = Ti.UI.createWebView({
    url:'web/local_webview.html'
});
win1.add(webview);
win1.open();

    Resources/web/local_webview.html:

    <!DOCTYPE HTML PUBLIC "-//W3C//DTD HTML 4.01//EN" "http://www.w3.org/TR/html4/strict.dtd">
<html>
<head>
    local webview
</head>
<body id="mybody" style="height:500px;background-color:#999;background-image:url(bg.png)">
    Hello from local webview.
    <div id="foo" style="margin-top:20px;font-weight:bold">hello </div>
    <a href="http://www.google.com">google</a>
</body>
</html>

    Let us know if this works for you or, otherwise, please provide all the relevant details.

    Thanks
