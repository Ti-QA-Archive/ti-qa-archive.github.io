Greetings All,

Now I am trying to solve iPhone 5 screen resolution issue for my existing app. I have installed XCode4.5 from Mac App Store. My Ti SKD is update to date as 3.0.0 GA. Here is my local environment details:

  • OXS(10.8.2) Mountain Lion
  • XCode 4.5.2 (I have installed from Mac App Store)
  • Upgraded to Ti 3.0.0 GA

When I run my app, I got this error message and simulator always exit:

dyld: Library not loaded: @rpath/DevToolsFoundation.framework/Versions/A/DevToolsFoundation
  Referenced from: /Applications/Xcode.app/Contents/Developer/Platforms/iPhoneSimulator.platform/Developer/Library/PrivateFrameworks/iPhoneSimulatorRemoteClient.framework/Versions/A/iPhoneSimulatorRemoteClient

When I create new mobile project at Ti Studio, I can able to see at simulator. It is very strange why my

  • Couple of things:

    • Did you download the command line tools? (I'm guessing you did)
    • When you downloaded xCode 4.5.2, did you download the simulators that matched the version to run the previous project?
    • If not, did you go into your TiStudio and change the iOS target in settings?
    • Have you run xcode-select to make sure you are looking at the right SDK location?
