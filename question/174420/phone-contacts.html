  • Hi

    Either I really am not understanding your question or you have missed something obvious;

    Ti.API.info('City: ' + e.person.address.home.City);
Ti.API.info('Country: ' + e.person.address.home.Country);
Ti.API.info('CountryCode: ' + e.person.address.home.CountryCode);
Ti.API.info('State: ' + e.person.address.home.State);
Ti.API.info('City: ' + e.person.address.home.City);
Ti.API.info('ZIP: ' + e.person.address.home.ZIP);

    Is this what you are after?
