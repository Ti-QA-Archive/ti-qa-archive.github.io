[Alloy] require android menu not possible(?)

I am trying to

<Require src="menu" />

my Android Menu, but when I want to compile I get an Error.

menu.xml:

<Alloy>
   <Menu id="menu">
      <MenuItem onClick="doSomething" title="Item 1">
      <MenuItem onClick="doSomething" title="Item 2">
      <MenuItem onClick="doSomething" title="Item 3">
      <MenuItem onClick="doSomething" title="Item 4">
   </Menu>
</Alloy>

index.xml:

<Alloy>
    <Window id="index">
        ....a tableview or something...
        <Require src="menu">
    </Window>
</Alloy>

[ERROR] Alloy compile failed

    In short, no, <Require> doesn't work right now for including Android Menus. The primary reason is that the Android Menu requires knowing what its parent node is in order to identify its containing Window, and in turn identify that Window's activity. This will be possible in Alloy, just not at the moment. The quick and dirty workaround is just to put your menu markup in the markup for the Window itself right now.
