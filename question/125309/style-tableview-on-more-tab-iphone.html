Style tableView on 'More' tab (iPhone)

I'm creating a multi-tab iPhone app that has a black background for all of the views. However, when you click on the 'More' tab to access the other tabs, the tableView that lists all of the extra tabs has a white background and black text. While I can change the barColor for a tabgroup, I can't seem to be able to control the rest of the style of the page in the More tab area. Does anyone know a way to do this, or will I have to create my own More tab?

    I believe that is controlled by Apple's Tab Group and that it cannot be altered (other than button title, bar color and user editability - see Tab Group API), without creating your own custom tab group (with views, subviews, imageviews, eventListeners, etc.) to mimic the tab group feature and style as you want.
