backgroundImage not refreshed on iPad

Hi,
I try to change background image when orientation change on an iPad but the background is not refreshed.
Is there a way to solve this ?
Thanks

    I have found the solution to create the window to be 1024 by 1024 large, and keep the background image details in the top left corner. This will avoid the image to be inshaped when the device is rotated.

    Another way which I also have used is to have the image details in the center of the image, and use the Gesture listener to move he image -128 pixels up when in landscape orientation, and 128 pixels left in the portrait orientation.

    A wee bit of code, but works rather well for me.

    If you have problems having the bg image changed, one way can be to have an ordinary view on z level 0, and update that in the Gesture listener. As a work around…

    Titanium coding is all about work arounds and offering bacon at midnight :)

    Hope this helps!
