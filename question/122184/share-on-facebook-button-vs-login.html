Share on Facebook. Button vs Login?

Hey! I'm adding a 'share this app on facebook' button to an iphone app, but I'm just wondering what you think would be the best way to tackle it?

I'm trying to decide between having a button that simply opens safari to the facebook share url, vs the actual facebook api where the user logs in to their facebook account within the app?

I'm thinking the facebook api is a bit much, just to 'share on facebook' and do nothing else, but it might be a nicer user experience without having to exit to safari and then open the app again?

What would you do?
