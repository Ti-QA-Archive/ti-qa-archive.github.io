Calendar control with customizable date cell for Android and iOS

Hi,
I need a Titanium Calendar module/widget with the following requirements
Month View
For Android and iOS and good performance
Ability to highlight any date/cell
Ability to add custom styling to any cell (Different cells may be highlighted differently based upon different conditions)
Any time a date cell is clicked, there should be a callback invoked to get the clicked date/cell
Controls to go to next/previous months. (It would be good if the next/prev month views slide in though not mandatory)

I tried the following modules/widgets

http://titaniumexplorers.blogspot.in/2012/09/titanium-calendar-month-view.html
Buggy and the scrolling is not reliable; fails sometimes. Written using Titanium javascript and not as Java/ObjectiveC module

http://www.tiresources.org/resource/Ti.SQ
Works on Android and iOS; can swipe to change months. Jerky on iOS7, sometimes hold and drag on a cell causes it to move/up and down several times

https://github.com/pec1985/Calendar-View-for-Appcelerator-v2
From the code it looks it draws in a webview which means the performance may take a hit

https://github.com/hamasyou/titanium_alloy_calendar
This one is the closest to fulfilling most of my requirements. Has next/prev buttons to change month. New month appears after a flicker, would've been great if it slided in. Can add an image to any cell. On changin orientation DOES NOT change the size of the cells so the calendar ends up going out of the viewing area if you change the orientation from landscape to portrait.

Any suggestions would be of great help.

Thank you!
