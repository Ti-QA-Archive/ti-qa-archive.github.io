1.6 Crashing on Exit

Hi, my app is crashing as I exit the app in 1.6. It's not in 1.5.x.

Here is the trace:
https://gist.github.com/842240

It appears that the issue only occurs if I have created at tableviewrow with a backgroundSelectedImage, e.g. row.backgroundSelectedImage = &#39;..&#x2F;images&#x2F;middleRowSelected.png&#39;;

Is this a defect or is this not supported?

UPDATE!!!

The issue does not appear to be related to the tableview (or at least not just the background image) - it appears to be more timing related as it happens randomly on the same build of the app.
