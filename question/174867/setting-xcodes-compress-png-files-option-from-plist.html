  • tiapp.xml has an entire <ios> tag in which you can place applicable settings that will be passed through into the xCode project.

    HOWEVER, there are some problems with Compress PNG files option. This also creates non-standard pngs in most cases. It may also INCREASE the size of some assets. (yeah, weird, no?) We have found that it is better to reduce the size of your assets on your own using a 3rd party util.
