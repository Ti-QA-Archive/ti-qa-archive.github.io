How to correct photos taken in “upside portrait” and “landscape right”(iOS) before upload to web service

iOS devises are able to let left handed users capture photos in orientation modes like “upside portrait” and “landscape right”. The photos becomes fine on the devise and it translates the proper way in iOS-ImagesViews(Titanium), but when the app uploads the photo to the apps web service, it revel that they are actually taken upside down. How can I make a function that automatically corrects the orientation(rotate) of the photo in my app so that it becomes right on the website?
