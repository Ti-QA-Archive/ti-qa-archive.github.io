reading text article in label

hi,

i have in a xml feed an article with tags, <strong/>, <br/>, …
i want to interpret these tags in a label without using a webview…

how can i do it ?

  • You can convert a <br /> tag to \n in a label to do the linebreak/carriage return.

    But you cannot have more than one font style (fontWeight:bold or normal) in a single label.

  • Try this approach for converting html to linefeeds:

    var regex1 = /<br\s+\/>/gi;
var regex2 = /<(p|\/p)>/gi;
var str = "<p>This is the first paragraph.</p><p>This is the second paragraph,<br />followed by the next bit of the sentence.</p><p>And this is the last paragraph</p>";
var newstr = str.replace(regex1, "\n").replace(regex2, "\n\n");
Ti.API.info(newstr);

    or, alternatively, for the label directly:

    thisLabel.text = thisLabel.text.replace(regex1, "\n").replace(regex2, "\n\n");
