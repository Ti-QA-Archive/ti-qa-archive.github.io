Keyboard with Next-Previous navigation

Hello,

I am looking for a way to navigate a long series of textfield in an iOS app. I got five fields vertically and I have seen in same apps that there is a Next - Previous navigation above the keyboard. How can I get the keyboard to look and behave like that?

Thanks!

  • The next/previous field buttons you see in iOS come automatically when using a normal HTML form in a webView.

    If you wanted to do this for native textFields, you would need to construct a keyboardToolbar with your own buttons or tabs, which would call 'focus()' to the next/previous textFields or textAreas as required.
