Facebook status update or posts are always private

When I set a post on my wall or change my Facebook status using Titanium, it's just seen by me and can't be seen by friends even if I change its privacy to be public.
When I post from the web browser, it's seen by friends.
I think it's related to permissions or my Facebook App.
anybody faced this before? and what's the suggested solution?

    It looks like your application is still running under the sandbox mode. Make sure you go to your facebook developer account and uncheck the sandbox mode first and try again.
