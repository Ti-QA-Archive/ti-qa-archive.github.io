  • Are you using the HTML5 video tag?

    You can just use plain javascript in your webview catching all events. Creating your own HTML5 file in your project and let your webview point to that file, create your layout as you wish. Add an ID to your video tag, and add a script tag beneath it to add events to it.

    Checkout: (Media Events)[http://www.w3.org/2010/05/video/mediaevents.html]
