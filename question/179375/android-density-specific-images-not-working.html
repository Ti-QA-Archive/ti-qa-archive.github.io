Android Density specific images not working

I have the following folder structure for my images

app/assets/android/images/res-hdpi
app/assets/android/images/res-ldpi
app/assets/android/images/res-mdpi
app/assets/android/images/res-xhdpi
app/assets/android/images/res-xxhdpi
app/assets/android/images/res-xxxhdpi

but I can't get any images to display when using for example

<ImageView image="images/email.png" />

Not sure what I'm missing

  • You have to place your images/folders inside below path. For e.g.

    app/assets/android/images/res-hdpi app/home/email.png

    app/assets/android/images/res-mdpi app/home/email.png

    app/assets/android/images/res-xxhdpi/home/email.png
    … so on

    <ImageView image="/home/email.png" />

    Don't forgot to give trailing slash in android.

    Hope this helps.
