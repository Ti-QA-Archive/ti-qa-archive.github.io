  • I may say the animation solution works but I found a better solution if anyone is interested.

    The solution is to load your animated image (gif) in a webview, and this is better if you have your image in your application :

    var f1 = Titanium.Filesystem.getFile(Titanium.Filesystem.resourcesDirectory, 'images', 'ActivityIndicator.gif');
        var f2 = Titanium.Filesystem.getFile(Titanium.Filesystem.applicationDataDirectory,'ActivityIndicator.gif');
        f2.write(f1);

        var webview = Titanium.UI.createWebView({
            height: 'auto',
            enableZoomControls:false,
            touchEnabled: false
        })
        webview.html = "<html><head><meta name='viewport' content='width=device-width, minimum-scale=1.0, maximum-scale=2.0, user-scalable=yes' /></head><body><img src="+f2.nativePath+" /></body></html>";
