    As long as your app id is registered with APN then the certificate for that app id is fine for your app. There is an example of Push Notifications in the KitchenSink demo here: https://github.com/appcelerator/KitchenSink/blob/master/Resources/ui/handheld/ios/phone/push_notification.js
