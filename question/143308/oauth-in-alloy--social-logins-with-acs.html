  • The oauth builtin needs some work before it's usable, hence its move to the unstable folder. You can track progress on the here: ALOY-236. Realistically, though, that builtin probably won't be addressed at least until the end of the month (Oct 2012).

    In the meantime, you are free to use any non-Alloy-specific javascript libraries you want for social integrations, and you may want to check out the social.js builtin which is a more specific implementation of some of the oauth functionality.
