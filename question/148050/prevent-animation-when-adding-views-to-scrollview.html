Prevent animation when adding views to Scrollview?

When data is returned from a REST call, I am adding a bunch of Views to a ScrollView which scrolls horizontally. The subviews have background colors and there is a flickery rectangle of each color at the left of the ScrollView as they are added. How can I prevent any animation and just add them in place at the right end of the scroll content?

  • If your ScrollView is disabled until the data has loaded, you can set the views opacity to 0, until all the views have loaded, then set the views opacity back to 1.
