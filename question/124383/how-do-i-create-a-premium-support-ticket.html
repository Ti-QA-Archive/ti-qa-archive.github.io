  • You should be able to login to the normal AppC site and navigate your way to http://support.appcelerator.com. There in the middle of the page, to the left, you should see 'ticketing' and 'create a ticket'. If you can't get there just yet, you might have to e-mail sales@appcelerator.com or community@appcelerator.com to get some initial support.
