    • view 1, view 2, view 3 are the ones referenced in your scrollableView.
    • view 1, view 2, view 3 are the parents of the sub views.
    • swipe event listeners added to the child views to catch up or down swipe.
    • in the event listeners raise or lower the zIndex of the views or hide/show the views as appropriate.
