    Hi Andrew,
    I just built your code . It worked !!! i figured out the problem.
    Problem - Your about controller is a view and not a window . The problem here is that your trying to open a view and not a window . The general rule is - When dealing with View - Add a view to a parent window . And that why the call to about button failed because your were trying to open the view and not add the view .
    There are two approaches you can follow :
    1) Make about.xml a window tag :

    <Alloy>
    <Window id="about" class="container">
        <Label id="heading">About LionFish</Label>
        <Label id="body1"></Label>
        <Label id="body2"></Label>
        <Label id="body3"></Label>
    </Window>
</Alloy>

    and in the home.js make this change :

    var View = Alloy.createController("about",args).getView();
View.open()

    2)Second approach would be , keeping the about as a view itself and adding it to home view.
    Add id tag to home.xml

    <Alloy>
    <View class="container" layout="vertical" id = "home">
        <Button class="label" id="report" name="Report" onClick="showPage">Report Lionfish/Take a Picture</Button>      
        <Button class="label" id="about" name="About" onClick="showPage">About Lionfish</Button>
        <Button class="label" id="handling" name="Handling" onClick="showPage">Safe Handling</Button>
        <Button class="label" id="map" name="Map" onClick="showPage">Interactive Map</Button>       
    </View>
</Alloy>

    and then add the about View to the Home View which ultimately gets added to the index Window

    var testView = Alloy.createController("about",args).getView();
$.home.add(testView);

    These two approaches should hopefully solve your problem . Let us know !!!
