Titanium.Media.createSound on iOS5

Has anyone tried using Ti.Media.createSound on iOS5? I have a simple app that just basically plays an audio file, however it doesn't make a sound anymore on iOS5.

The code is simple, basically this:

var file1 = Titanium.Filesystem.getFile(Titanium.Filesystem.resourcesDirectory,&#39;audio&#x2F;sample.aif&#39;); var sound1 = Titanium.Media.createSound({sound:file1}); sound1.play();

My app is in the app store and works fine on iOS 4.x as well as the emulator. But on iOS5 simply nothing happens - no sound is played.

I did have at the top of my app the following which seems to have no effect on my app in 4.x but in iOS5 it makes the difference between other standard controls having any audio. (for example the spinner control makes a clicking sound, however if this line is present in app.js then the spinner no longer clicks)

Ti.Media.defaultAudioSessionMode = Ti.Media.AUDIO_SESSION_MODE_PLAYBACK;
