    There are some rest connectors out there in the wild, none have worked the way we needed them too. In our usages, depending on the client, have stored data on Parse, Kinvey, custom nodejs implementation, or wordpress. Each requires an API, sometimes easily available, sometimes have to write your own middleware.

    I believe there is an android ti-firebase module out there, you will need to search for it. Don't know of one for ios, and neither is listed on gitt.io.
