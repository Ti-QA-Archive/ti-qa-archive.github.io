Can't get registerForPushNotifications to work

I have put the following into my app.js but when I open the app in the simulator nothing happens. I would have thought I'd either get a success or error message.

Titanium.Network.registerForPushNotifications({
    types: [
        Titanium.Network.NOTIFICATION_TYPE_BADGE,
        Titanium.Network.NOTIFICATION_TYPE_ALERT,
        Titanium.Network.NOTIFICATION_TYPE_SOUND
    ],

success:function(e)
{
    deviceToken = e.deviceToken;
    alert("deviceToken = "+deviceToken);
},
error:function(e)
{
    alert("Error: "+e.message);
},
callback:function(e)
{
    alert("push notification received"+JSON.stringify(e.data));
}
});

I have updated my app, and downloaded and installed a new provisioning profile. I guess I must be missing something but can't work out what.

Titanium SDK 2.1.1
iOS SDK 5.1
Titanium Studio, build: 2.1.1.201207271312
