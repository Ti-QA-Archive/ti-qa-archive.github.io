  • First you need to know whats are the coordinates of your lower button with respect to the screen. you can get the coordinates from any of your buttons conners ei: top & left, right & top bottom & left or bottom & right. then you need to know what size is your button ei: width & height. if you dont know the values because they are set dynamic, you can access them on the postLayout event of the button.

    Second add a listener (click, touchstart, touchend…) to your high zIndex view and on touch you are going to check the coordinates of your touch.

    Third check for the to see if you touch coordinates are inside the button area if they are execute your code for that button.

    To get the area where the buttons is compare the 

    (x,y)touch > (top, left)button and (x,y)touch < (top+width, left+height)button

    if true execute.

    It sounds a lil convoluted but its pretty easy to implement. hope this helps
