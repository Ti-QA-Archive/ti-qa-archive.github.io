  • If your db file is located in the root of the resources folder, try accessing it like:

    var db = Ti.Database.install(&#39;example.sqlite&#39;,&#39;example&#39;);

    You will also want to look at how your table rows are being added tot he dataArray. Try this:

    dataArray.push(Ti.UI.createTableViewRow({title:rows.fieldByName(&#39;name&#39;), hasChild:true}));
