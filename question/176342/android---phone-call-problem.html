Android - Phone Call problem

I am trying to make a phone call (real device, not emulator) from my App but unfortunately it is not working. I have already tried:

Attemp 1:
I added this to my manifest tag in Tiapp.xml

<uses-permission android:name="android.permission.READ_PHONE_STATE"/>

My script

var intent = Ti.Android.createIntent({
     action: Ti.Android.ACTION_CALL,
     data: 'tel:1234567'
});
Ti.Android.currentActivity.startActivity(intent);

This one does NOTHING!

Attemp 2:

Titanium.Platform.openURL('telprompt://12345678');

Attemp 3:

Titanium.Platform.openURL('telprompt:12345678');

Attemp 4:

Titanium.Platform.openURL('tel://12345678');

Attemp 5:

Titanium.Platform.openURL('tel:12345678');

These return the same error:

Do any of you guys know what is happening?

  • My mistake! I have copied the wrong line of code.
    I used this:

    <uses-permission android:name="android.permission.CALL_PHONE" />

    instead of this: 

    <uses-permission android:name="android.permission. READ_PHONE_STATE" />
