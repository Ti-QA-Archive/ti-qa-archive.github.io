  • The thread may be old but for anyone wondering ;
    For iOS you can also use

    Titanium.Platform.openURL(&#39;telprompt:&#x2F;&#x2F;[number]&#39; );

    instead of

    Titanium.Platform.openURL(&#39;tel:&#x2F;&#x2F;[number]&#39; );
