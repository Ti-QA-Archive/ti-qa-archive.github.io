What happened to the Indie subscription?

Hello, does anyone knows what happened with the Indie subscription? It has apparently disappeared from the sales plans … Have I missed a communication on this?
I would like to use one of the modules available under Indie (Urban Airship), but I'm worried it won't be supported on the new plans, or will be an additional charge …
Anyone has more info on these billing changes?

  • Accepted Answer

    Jean-Christophe,

    The Indie subscription is no longer offered. As an Indie subscriber you have the following options:

    1. Maintain Indie subscription: You keep the "indie" module entitlements and also get access to the free level of ACS and Analytics. If you takes no action and your info on file is accurate, the Indie subscription will automatically be renewed.

    2. Cancel Indie Subscription: If you cancel the Indie subscription, you cannot purchase it again and you lose the Indie module entitlements. In your case, that means that you would have to purchase the urban airship module.

    I hope that addresses your question. We are working on a post explaining the migration plan and the various options for existing subscribers. Please let me know if you have any further questions
