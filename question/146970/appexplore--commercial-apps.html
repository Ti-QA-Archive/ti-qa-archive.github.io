    Hi

    To make it as clear as possible - because I do understand where you are coming from.

    YES…

    …you can create commercial apps with AppExplore

    You do have to pay for any optional elements like ACS (cloud) usage over the current limits or custom modules or enhanced tech support (beyond the Q&A), training, conferences and so on.

    But if you create an app, put it in the store safe in the knowledge - no-one is coming is going to come after you for any money.
