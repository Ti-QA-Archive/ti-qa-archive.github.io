Multiple channels or one channel to rule them all?

Hi,

I have just started to develop with Titanium Appcelerator and have a question about Push Notifications and channels.

The idea is that my service will alert my users when changes have been made on various webpages. Each page is represented by a row in my MySQL database (> 20.000 rows), along with timestamps for changes.

The question now is, should I have one channel per page, or only one channel that handles all and then use to_ids attribute (POST-REST) with the user IDs that subscribe to the page beeing updated?

If I have one channel per page I am worried that I will end up having a whole lot of channels that are just lying around (as most pages will in time be removed).

I'd be very grateful for answers.
