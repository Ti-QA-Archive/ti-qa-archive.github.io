App crashes are back

I have a one-tab app that manages several windows, opened by a simple Ti.UI.currentTab.open(win).
There are modals and popovers that occasionally can be opened on top of them.
I had customers complaining a few months bac, reporting crashes (back in TiSDK 1.7.2). After updating to TiSDK 1.8 they magically disappeared and they were all happy with that.

I've been working on a major update now for a couple of months, that includes upgrading my app to TiSDK 2.1.3 ( tried all of the 2.x ), and the crashes are back.
If I open and close windows too fast, the app will crash. If I show/hide popovers too fast, the app will crash. It's not even a random thing, it will happen quite systematically.

    Well, you didn't provide nearly enough info to give you an exact answer, but I have one possible suggestion…

    "If I open and close windows too fast, the app will crash. If I show/hide popovers too fast, the app will crash." << I remember I had problems when animations weren't finished, if I tried another animation apps would crash. The solution is probably going to not animate the "fast" closings.
