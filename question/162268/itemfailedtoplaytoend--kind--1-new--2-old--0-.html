  • Same here, just now! But my workaround is a VideoPlayer with a MP4-Link:

        var video = Titanium.Media.createVideoPlayer({
        url: "http://remote.com/video.mp4",
        mediaControlStyle: Titanium.Media.VIDEO_CONTROL_EMBEDDED,
        autoplay: true,
        mediaTypes: Ti.Media.VIDEO_MEDIA_TYPE_VIDEO
    });

    In your case, i would prefer to use a Webview to show the Youtube-Link:

    var web = Ti.UI.createWebView({
    url: "http://youtubelink.com"
});
