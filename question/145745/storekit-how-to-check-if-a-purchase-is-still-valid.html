Storekit: how to check if a purchase is still valid

Our app will be free, but limited in content (from a feed). With an in-app purchase we want to give the user access to all content, for a period of 12 months, auto renewed.

How to check if a purchase is still valid? And what if a user has deleted/cleaned/reinstalled his device?

Whats the best approach?

  • you need to be pulling purchases from apple into your own backend.
    Your own API call to that backend will be used to confirm subscription status.

    Each transaction has a unique id.

    • We have a couple of apps with subscriptions, and Apple says you run your own backend to keep track.

      My business view of lite vs full and in-app ads:

      I go down to the local mall. There are a number of stores. But the three we will talk about are Sears-Lite, Sears-Full and JcPenny.

      I walk into JcPenny. They have great reviews. The lady at the front is real nice, greats me, helps me setup an account, then hands me the weekly flyer for Sears…. in-app ads, not a great continuity engine.

      So i go next door to Sears-Lite (after all, the lady at JcPenny seems to think its a nice play, she gave me a flyer!) I look around, I like it. I try to buy something, sorry, you need to be in our Sears-Full store…

      In both cases you, the developer, have erected a barrier that prevents us from a continued and long term relationship by insisting, in both cases, that the best business model is for me to abandon your app.
