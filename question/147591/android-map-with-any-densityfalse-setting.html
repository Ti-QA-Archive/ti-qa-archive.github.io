android map with any density=false setting

I set my android app to use anyDensity="false". All my images in the app have somehow low quality kind of looking, but in general, they are still acceptable. However, the google map looks really bad. Anyway to adjust it to make it look better?

    This is because you've set your app to compatibility mode with anyDensity="false", which basically means that the DPI is always 160 (and width=320dp) thus the app will always use only medium density resources even if it runs on hi or xhi devices.
