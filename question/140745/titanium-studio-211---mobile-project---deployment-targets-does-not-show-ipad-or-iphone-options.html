  • Kris,

    A little more information would be great, like what platform you are on and so forth. I'll throw out a few ideas that may steer you in the right direction.

    1. Are the development tools for Android and iOS properly installed? Both the iOS and Android SDK's have to be installed separately for those options to show up. Studio should be able to guide you through that process if you haven't done it already.

    2. If you have made certain modifications to the tiapp.xml by any chance that were incorrect, it might not let you deploy to those 2 targets.

    These are just guesses i'm throwing out, maybe if you give more info though, we can help you further.
