ScrollView: scroll to bottom

Hi everybody,

I have a ScrollView in my iPad App with lots of content and a Button, if I click on the bottom I want the scrollView to scroll to bottom.
can anybody tell me if this is possible?

Thanks

  • You have no way to scroll to the bottom if u use Ti.UI.Size or Ti.UI.Fill or 'auto' as the height is unknown. But this is most of the case as the scroll view height is always unknown most of the time. As this require all view, element height in the scroll view to be fixed. If you developing iphone, scrollToBottom() is not available, too.

  • I'd say you can probably just use the scrollTo() method on your scrollView. To find out how what to set your scrollView y argument to, you should be able to use the height() property to find out how tall the view is.
