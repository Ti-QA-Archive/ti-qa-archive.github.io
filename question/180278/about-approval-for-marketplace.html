  • Accepted Answer

    Hi

    The approval process fluctuates in time based on multiple factors. Whilst the average time is often often around 7 days it can sometimes take longer or shorter.

    There is a large shift towards iOS 64 bit modules right now - that is likely causing a queue delay. Additionally we are coming out of a seasonal holiday which means submissions will not have been processed in the normal time as much fewer staff would have been around - this causes a delay whilst everything is being "caught up".

    If you submitted the app 8 days ago it also means you will have spanned two weekends were staffing levels in this department differ at the weekend to the working days.
