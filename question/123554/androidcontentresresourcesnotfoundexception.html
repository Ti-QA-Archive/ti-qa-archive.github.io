android.content.res.Resources$NotFoundException

I kept getting "android.content.res.Resources$NotFoundException" on various Android devices like tablets (Android 3.1 Honeycomb) etc. and had a little trouble figuring out how to fix it so I wanted to share my solution.

I had to put a copy of all the images in my general images folder (Resources/images) in each android resource folder:<br>
<br>
res-long-land-hdpi<br>
res-long-land-ldpi<br>
res-long-port-hdpi<br>
res-long-port-ldpi<br>
res-notlong-land-hdpi<br>
res-notlong-land-ldpi<br>
res-notlong-land-mdpi<br>
res-notlong-port-hdpi<br>
res-notlong-port-ldpi<br>
res-notlong-port-mdpi<br>
<br>
For the sizes of those images I used my @2x images from iPhone in the high and medium folders and the small (non @2x) files in the low folders. The splash screen sizes are in there already from when the project was created.<br><br>
Also I removed all the .DS_store files in those folders. I don't know if that affected it, but it was giving some errors on compile when they were there.<br><br>
I didn't think originally that I HAD to put images in every folder but I guess I do.

— asked August 1st 2011 by Dan Frist
