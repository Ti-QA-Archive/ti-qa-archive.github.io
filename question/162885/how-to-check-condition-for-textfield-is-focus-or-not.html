    you simply have to keep record of when the textfield gets focused and blurred:

      $.txt_username.addEventListener('focus', function() {
        txt_hasFocus = true;
    });

    $.txt_username..addEventListener('blur', function() {
        txt_hasFocus = false;
    });

    so your condition would become:

      if(txt_hasFocus){

    ....
