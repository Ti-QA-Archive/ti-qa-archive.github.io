      I was confused above. The cli.httpProxyServer is not escaped and you must escape the ":" yourself by replacing the ":" with "%3A".

      If your password is "xxx:xxx", then your proxy settings should be:

      http://myusername:xxx%3Axxx@my.proxy.server
