  • Thomas

    I tried to run your script in my environment but, as it relies on images to exist, obviously it wouldn't execute. Please read the use-case guide I posted above, and then whittle your script down to the bare essentials required in order to demonstrate the crash. For example, do you really need 2 x 3 views? Doesn't it fail when you have 2 x 2 views? Often this process helps you to isolate the issue yourself, while also giving you a much better understanding of how Titanium works.

    One thing I have noticed is that you are toggling the visible properties to show and hide your scrollableViews. Personally, I would add/remove them instead.

    A minor suggestion; there is no need to type Titanium out in full; use the Ti alias, ie Ti.UI.createView() instead.

    Cheers
