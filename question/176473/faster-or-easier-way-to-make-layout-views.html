Faster or Easier Way to Make Layout Views

I mastered making layout and GUI displays via XML programming in Titanium Alloy. Now, I am planning to make development of layouts much faster and more convenient. Comparing to Eclipse, since it has a dropdown GUI feature where you can create layout views and widgets in a snap, Titanium doesn't have one. Are there any upgrades, plugins, or something to make this feature available?

  • Hi

    Many people (including myself) consider GUI builders a bad solution. They allow devs to make short cuts without understanding the code underneath and then have zero idea how to get the most out of the platform or how to tune the thing they are working on or how to choose a better UI/UX example of what they are trying.

    If you have ever used Visual Studio (the recent stuff last 5 years) then you may know what I mean. Too much is done for you that many applications come out from people who cannot then understand how to fix a problem - make something faster and so on.

    I do NOT mean this is you. I am having a rant. They can be used for good - not just evil :)

    Currently there are no plug-in or otherwise visual editors - however;

    • Ti-Browser

    It is worth giving this a go and seeing if it helps you out. Also take a look at TiShadow not a gui builder but speeds up rebuild on multiple test devices very fast - so refreshes of UIs are much faster.

    Personally I use Zen Coding plug-in to my text editor (not studio) this makes creating the xml extremely fast. 

    // this
Alloy>Window>(View#header>Label{Footer})+(TableView#body>TableViewRow[title=Row]*5)+(View#footer>Label{Footer})

    Turns into;

    <Alloy>
  <Window>
    <View id="header">
      <Label>
        Footer
      </Label>
    </View>
    <TableView id="body">
      <TableViewRow title="Row">
      </TableViewRow>
      <TableViewRow title="Row">
      </TableViewRow>
      <TableViewRow title="Row">
      </TableViewRow>
      <TableViewRow title="Row">
      </TableViewRow>
      <TableViewRow title="Row">
      </TableViewRow>
    </TableView>
    <View id="footer">
      <Label>
        Footer
      </Label>
    </View>
  </Window>
</Alloy>
