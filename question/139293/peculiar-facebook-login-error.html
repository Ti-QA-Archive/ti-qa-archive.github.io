Peculiar Facebook login error

I've just started with some facebook integration for my app. It's just a really simple posting of pictures and I've used the Kitchen Sink as a starting point.

I'm using SDK 2.1.0GA, up-to-date Titanium Studio (2.1.0.201206251749). Targeting ( for now ) iOS. Developing on OSX, 10.7

I'm using the convenient Ti.Facebook.LoginButton, and when clicked I get the expected login dialog. My app's facebook app registration and icon show correctly in the dialog. However, when I log in, generally one of two things will happen:

  • Nothing happens after entering login/pass and I press "connect". It just sits there until I give up and click the 'X' on the top right.

  • The login part succeeds, but instead of being taken to the screen where the user is informed of the app's required permissions, I instead get this "Page Not Found" error (screenshot from simulator):

In both cases, If I dismiss the popup (with the 'X') and click the Facebook login button again, the dialog opens, and then immediately shuts, and the Facebook login button switches to the "Log out" button.

Once this is done, my app sucessfully posts to the user's stream, so that's good. I'm connected.

Clearly, the login is successful, but I'm not getting the slick experience that I get with the Kitchen Sink demo app.

Obviously, I have registered the app with Facebook and gotten an appid. I've followed the instructions and put it in my app's tiapp.xml and also I register the appid and permissions (in my case, only ['publish_stream']) near the beginning of my app's startup sequence.

I've been scratching my head and I can't figure out what I'm doing wrong!
