Looks like Titanium.Media has settings for video quality (QUALITY_LOW, QUALITY_MEDIUM, etc). Is there an equivalent for the camera?

Is it possible to get a list of the available camera resolutions and to set the camera resolution?

[Update] I spoke to a native iPhone developer and he told me it's not possible to specify camera resolution even in native apps, so if he's right there wouldn't be a way in appcelerator either.
