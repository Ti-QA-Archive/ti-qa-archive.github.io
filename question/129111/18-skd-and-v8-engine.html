    • Most probably current stable version of Titanium Studio doesn't have that option yet. You could update to beta version, or you could modify "tiapp.xml":

      <ti:app xmlns:ti="http://ti.appcelerator.org">
    <property name="ti.android.runtime">v8</property>
    <sdk-version>1.8.0.1</sdk-version>
