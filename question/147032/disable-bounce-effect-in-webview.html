  • Hi, you can use 

    var myWebViewWithoutBounceEffect = Ti.UI.createWebView({
    url:'urlOfLocalOrRemotePage.html',
    width:Ti.UI.FILL,
    height:Ti.UI.FILL,
    touchEnabled:true, //optional
    disableBounce:true
});

    to disable bounce effect.
