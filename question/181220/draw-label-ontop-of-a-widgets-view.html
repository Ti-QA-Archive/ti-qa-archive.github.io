    • Strange, I just tried a copying over your code to have a look, seems it makes everything invisible.

      Anyway I managed to get it working by not encapsulating the progress inside another view like this:

      <View id="cashOutCircleWidget">
    <Widget src="de.manumaticx.circularprogress" id="progress" />
</View>
<View id="cashOutCircleText">
    <Label id="circleLabel">Hello, World this is a long label
</View>
