  • You can integrate Game Center into your Titanium project using the GameKit Module, available in the Marketplace (https://marketplace.appcelerator.com/apps/775). Each module comes with documentation when you download it from the Marketplace.

    Unfortunately it's not free, and worse, it actually hase an ongoing monthly fee to use it. I can understand a onetime fee to buy the module but the ongoing fee is what I hate.
