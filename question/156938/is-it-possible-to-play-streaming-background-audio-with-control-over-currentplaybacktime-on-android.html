Is it possible to play streaming background audio with control over currentPlaybackTime on Android?

On Android, is it possible to play streaming background audio with a scrubber/slider to control play position?

VideoPlayer: Has scrubber/slider but stops when home button or power button is pressed.
AudioPlayer: Plays in background but loses it's place when connection is interrupted and has no seek/setCurrentPlaybackTime, so the stream starts over.

I am open to 3rd party modules and native language solutions if anyone has one.
