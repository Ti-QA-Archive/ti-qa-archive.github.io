  • Accepted Answer

    Have you tried using the Ti.App.Properties to store your global variables?

    Ti.App.Properties.setString('foo', 'bar');

Ti.App.Properties.setBool('givenBool', true);

Ti.App.Properties.setObject('globalObj', {'foo':'bar'});
