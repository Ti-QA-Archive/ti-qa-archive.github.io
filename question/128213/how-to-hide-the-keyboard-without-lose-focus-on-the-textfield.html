    You didn't specify a platform. But in iOS at least, this is not possible. Since the keyboard is analogous to the TextField.

    Can't you just use the built in numberic keyboard?

    Another solution would be to set enabled: false in the TextFied, add a click event listener to the TextField and then open your custom numeric pad, when you click the buttons on your pad, you could update the textField.value.
