Ti.Media.openPhotoGallery causing open Modal to close?

So my app is set up that there is one main window, then you click on a table row and it opens up a modal. In the modal, there are 4 views that you can click and add images, through either your Photo Gallery (Ti.Media.openPhotoGallery()) or through your Camera on your device (Ti.Media.showCamera()).

Before iOS 5 / Xcode 4.2 and Titanium Mobile SDK 1.7.3, everything was working fine. But now that I've updated, it seems that whenever I choose an image from either the openPhotoGallery() or showCamera(), the gallery/camera overlay closes as well as the modal below it, bringing me to the very beginning first window. Which is not what I want.

There seems to be no way to stop this, as there is nothing in my code that causes the windows to close and no listener for when the camera/gallery overlay closes. It seems a bug with the SDK, as I can find no possible way to stop this.

Thanks,
Colton
