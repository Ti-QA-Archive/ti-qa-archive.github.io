    Hello David,

    that's nothing to do with Titanium, it's a Android issue. The emulator wont use wifi, since it's already connected. Take a look into this reply with more android specifics.

    Best,

    Mauro

    Ps. Please take a look into the DDMS manual so you know what is an issue related to your app and which one is related to the OS. The log will throw every single event happening in the emulator (OS events, other apps, etc) and you need to just focus on your app events/logs.
