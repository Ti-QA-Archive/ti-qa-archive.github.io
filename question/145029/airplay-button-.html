  • Accepted Answer

    Not a problem.

    The AirPlay features for video and Audio are built into their respective players. So if you are using one and you have AirPlay support and it is working then the buttons should automatically appear.

    • If you are creating your own players then you will need to use a third-party module to achieve your goals.
    • If you want to use AirPlay as a "second screen" then you can but again with a third-party module.

    The TV Out Module will solve the third-party needs. I have used in a couple of apps and works reasonably well. Requires the AirPlay mirroring option to be enabled by the user - but that is a requirement for all apps of any type to utilise it beyond the standard streaming.

    I hope this gives you enough info.
