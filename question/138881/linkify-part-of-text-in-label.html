var win = Ti.UI.currentWindow;
var _text = Ti.UI.createLabel(
{
    html:"you have read and agree to the <a href='http://www.mysite.com/privacy'>terms of services </a>",
});
win.add(_text);
win.open();

It's showing terms of services as link but when I click on it; not opening web page. Is there any another solution to do this.
