Youtube forces landscape orientation

Hi,
my app is in portrait mode designed. On detail view a webview with youtube video embedded. At the time the video also in portrait (fullscreen) modus. Better idea is to watch video in landscape mode.
How can I force this mode? It is not a new window and thatswhy I cannot set the modus.

Any ideas?

Rainer

  • You can set the orientation mode of the window:

    win.orientationModes = [Ti.UI.LANDSCAPE_LEFT]
