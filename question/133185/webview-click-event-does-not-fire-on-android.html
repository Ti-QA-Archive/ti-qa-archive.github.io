Webview Click Event Does Not Fire on Android

Mobile SDK 1.8.1 or 1.8.2. I'm trying to capture the click event on a webview. It works fine on iOS but not on Android. Is it not possible or am I doing something wrong?

var tabGroup = Ti.UI.createTabGroup();

var win = Ti.UI.createWindow({});

var tab = Ti.UI.createTab({
    title:'My Tab',
    window:win
});

var wv = Ti.UI.createWebView({
    url:'http://www.google.com'
});

wv.addEventListener('click', function(e){
    Ti.API.info('wv click');
});

win.add(wv);
tabGroup.addTab(tab);
tabGroup.open();

The site opens on both platforms, but on iOS I get the expected .info message, where on Android I do not. Android behaves like a webview with no captured events.
