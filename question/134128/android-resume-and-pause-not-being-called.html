Android Resume and Pause not being called

I have put in resume and pause eventlisteners in my android application:

Ti.Android.currentActivity.addEventListener('pause', function(e)
        {
            Ti.API.info("application paused");
        });
        Ti.Android.currentActivity.addEventListener('destroy', function(e) {
           Ti.API.info("destroy event received");
          removeGeolocation();
        });
        Ti.Android.currentActivity.addEventListener('resume', function(e)
        {
            Ti.API.info("activity resumed");
        });

I have a login page, and following that, I create a tabgroup and open it. The pause and resume eventlisteners are called after the login window closes and the tabgroup opens, but they aren't called when the home button is clicked (when the application should pause or resume).
