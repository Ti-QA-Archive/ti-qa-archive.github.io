    • Hi Gagan,
      You don't send array, send the parameters in key Value pairs like this,

      signUpControl.client.send({
    'emailId':emailId, 
    'password':password,
    'username':username, 
    'phone':phone
});

      or try like this 

      signUpControl.client.send(JSON.stringify([emailId, password, username, phone]));

      Hope one out of this would work, well the name of the keys depends on your web service that what keys it accepts, it might be userName also instead of username. Also the first method is the common way of sending the params in POST.
