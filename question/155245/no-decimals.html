No decimals

Hi.

I make some calculations in my app and i get decimals all over the place. I want it to round up to the nearest non decimal nr.

for ex:

var bt = 0.10125;
var wTot = 42;
var tot_b = wTot/bt;
var total= tot_b*1.06;

var alertDialog = Titanium.UI.createAlertDialog({
    title: 'Calc',
    message:  total,


});

alertDialog.show();

will give total: 439,703703703703

I would like to have it 440

How do i achieve that?
