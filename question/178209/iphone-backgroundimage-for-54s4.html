iPhone backgroundImage for 5,4S,4

I have png files for the iPhone 5,4S, and 4 resolutions correct. How do I use a backgroundImage for iPhone 5,4S, and 4? I am not sure on what code I need to use to check if the screen sizes change. Can anyone help? Thanks

I am using Titanium SDK 3.2, iOS7 now iOS8

    Titanium has a neat shortcut where you can just name the file correctly and it will pick the right image for the right screen resolution:

    • myImage.png << iPhone 3/etc
    • myImage@2x.png << iPhone 4/4S
    • myImage-568h@2x.png << iPhone 5/5S

    I would assume that in 3.4.0 you can now have these images, too… but I haven't checked.

    • myImage-667h@2x << iPhone 6
    • myImage-Portrait-736@3x << iPhone 6 Plus Portrait
    • myImage-Landscape-736@3x << iPhone 6 Plus Landscape

    Then, in your code, just say image:&#39;myImage.png&#39;
