Assistance with Window formatting

Looking for an example of a label in a window and a webview in a window so that when you scroll down the page the label goes up under the navbar. Right now when i scroll the webview scrolls but the label remains at the top. I appreciate any assistance. 

var Window = Ti.UI.createWindow({
    layout: 'vertical'
});

var label = Ti.UI.createLabel({
    text: 'Top of page'
});

var webView = Ti.UI.createWebView({
    html: '<html><body><p>test web info</p></body></html>'
});

Window.add(label);
Window.add(webView);
Window.open();

  • You could try putting everything in a scroll view, but the real issue is going to be the scrolling on the webview. When you scroll on the webview, its going to assume that you want the webview to scroll, not the entire screen. If you've got control over the webview, just put the label in the webview and your problems are solved!
