    • Thanks! Regarding your questions:

      1. The display of the dictionary results are displayed on a web view. I used CSS to accomplish this.

      2. To make a universal app, you will have to modify builder.py under Library/Application Support/Titanium/mobilesdk/osx/1.x.x/iphone/builder.py and modify the TARGETED_DEVICE_FAMILY to be "1,2" instead of just "1" or "2".

      Hope this information helps!
