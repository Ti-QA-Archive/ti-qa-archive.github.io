Android : Audio stops in navigation

Hello ,

I need to play music from the server by streaming.

I have some 4 - 5 screens to navigate to reach the music player.

The music plays using create audio player(audiosessionmode:playback) when I reach the music player screen .But when I navigate back to the previous screen , the music stops as the window is closed.

I used tab bar to navigate.

Can anyone help me out in this issue.

  • Here's something that worked for me - create a global player object on the application, then reference this in your views. If you place the player object on Ti.App, it's then available as a global across the app, and won't be destroyed with the player screen -

    In app.js:

    Ti.App.player = Titanium.Media.createAudioPlayer({});

    then in your player screen:

    Ti.App.player.start(); etc.

    • @Primoris online and anyone that comes across this page:
      ignoring the different references you call (audioPLayer and audioPlayer), you don't appear to be calling the global audio player here as stated by Steve Carpenter. You should ALWAYS refer to the global singleton here, ie Ti.App.player.

      example based on your code and assuming that the global audio player is called "player":

      if(Ti.App.player){]

Ti.App.player.stop();

Ti.App.player.setUrl(url_name)

}

      Should work fine ;)

      Hope this helps.
