  • Hi

    Short answer; You don't.

    Longer answer;

    TableViews do not get placed in tableViews, doing so would be contrary to all UI guidelines for each platform. If you want a tableRow to give more information;

    • You show the child or disclosure icons and spawn a new window when select. This is the most common way.
    • You expand the height of the selected row and reveal previously hidden extra information
    • When a row is selected show a pop up or option dialog

    Basically ANYTHING else other than placing a tableView inside a tableView.

    Incase your question has been list in translation - please supply a screenshot of an example app showing what you think you are trying to achieve.
