Difference between <Require type="widget"> and

Does anyone know the difference between requiring a widget using the syntax, 

"<Require type="widget" src="myWidget" id="myWidget" />

or using 

<Widget id="loadIcon" src="com.appcelerator.loading" />

Does one load faster or anything?
