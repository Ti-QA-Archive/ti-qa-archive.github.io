Free to develop, pay to publish?

As an Information Science student, geek and household hacker I was amazed by Titanium Mobile. I participated in a webinar and was even more amazed! So I downloaded Studio as soon as it was released.

I saw that "mobile debugging" was a premium feature, but guessed that was just for debugging on device. Then I realized debugging was disabled all together. But why? How can you build and more importantly learn effectively without such a fundamental feature for any IDE?

I don't want to seem cocky or anything (more nerd sadness than nerd rage). I have a few ideas in my head but your "indie" subscription just doesn't fit my current budget. I could always develop native iOS apps with Obj-C, but I'd feel much better without the lock-in :)

Cheers!
