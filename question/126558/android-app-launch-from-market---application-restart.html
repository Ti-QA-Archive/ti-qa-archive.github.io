<property name="ti.android.bug2373.buttonText">Restart</property>

  • The accepted answer is out of date. You should be using the following work around described in https://jira.appcelerator.org/browse/TIMOB-9285: add the following to your tiapp.xml:

    <property name="ti.android.bug2373.finishfalseroot" type="bool">true</property>
