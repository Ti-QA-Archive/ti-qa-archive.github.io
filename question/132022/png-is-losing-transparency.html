PNG is losing transparency

Hello!

I have this really weird issue. I have created an app where i've created a "custom tabview". That means that every "tabs" content is inside a view, and i've created buttons at the bottom to control which tab is active. Now, i've added a image at the bottom of my window (the main window) to make the design look a little nicer. The image is a PNG with a transparency-gradient that ends with 0% opacity. Now, for every time i change "tab" that gradient is loosing some of its transparency, and after a couple of tab-switches it is almost no transparency-gradient at all. What could cause this kind of behaviour? I don't do anything with the gradient image when i change tabs at all in the code. It's just a static picture supposed to be there all the time.

I am running 1.8.0.1 and testing for iOS

    You must add the image over and over again. You have something fishy in the code that doesn't remove the old image or adds the image again each time you are hitting the tab.
