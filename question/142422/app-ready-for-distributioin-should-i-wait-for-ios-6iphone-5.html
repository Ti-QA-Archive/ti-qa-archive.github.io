App Ready for Distributioin. Should I wait for IOS 6/iPhone 5?

I have an app that is ready for distribution and am trying to decide whether or not to wait for IOS 6 / iPhone 5 or just submit it for review right away.

Granted, I may (will) have issues to resolve with IOS 6 / iPhone 5. Not sure if Apple will measure compatibility with IOS 6 when reviewing apps right before the release. I know it's probably best to wait and refine for IOS 6 before releasing, but I also don't want to get hung up with IOS 6 issues for an extended period.

Suggestions?

    Best idea would be to now wait for Ti to add support for iOS6. As soon as that is out, test your app on iOS6 and make sure everything runs fine.

    I can definitely point out, that if somethings don't work on iOS6 as expected, or break or the app crashes, it's definitely wise to wait. Apple will straight away reject your app for these reasons.
