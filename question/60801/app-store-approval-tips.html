App Store Approval Tips

I thought it would be nice to have a thread of items not covered by Apple's guidelines but worthy to know when submitting an App to the App Store.

Any items not covered by the following (login required):
https://developer.apple.com/appstore/resources/approval/guidelines.html

  • When submitting an live streaming application, a stream less than 64 kbps is required. Also, the live http stream must validate without errors with the mediastreamvalidator so make sure you check before submission.

  • A free app cannot be feature limited with links to upgrade to the full version. So don't include any links from icons of features not enabled.
