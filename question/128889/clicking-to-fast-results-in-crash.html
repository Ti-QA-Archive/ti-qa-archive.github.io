Clicking to fast results in crash...

Wanted to know if anyone has experienced a similar issue… It's issues like this that make me want to stop using Appcelerator…

I have an iPad Application that has a simple popover menu… In this popover, I have 5 buttons. This is the problem…

BUT… If I do the same thing and just wait a second before clicking the button, it works fine… It's driving me absolutely nuts… I am sure I can think of a hack to cause an event listern to delay or something, but I am sick og having to create these hacks just to get Appcelerator to work properly…

    it's because you press the button when the popover is still animating during the show and you force an hide() on the button click. The problem with popovers and windows is that you can't have multiple animations at the same time (I'm sure the debugger gave you that error, I had the same problem).

    You should put a timeout in the hide function, or use:

    popover.show({view:category_button, animated:false});

    and 

    popover.hide({animated:false})

    there's no other way to fix it really.
