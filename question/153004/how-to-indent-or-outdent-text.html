  • Hi

    You have not specified WHERE you were hoping for this to appear.

    There is no support for any indents in; label, textfield, textarea, buttons. If you wish to have an indent/outdent you will need to either use a webView (not recommended for general UI) or manage how you add things to the windows. If you require large paragraphs of text with initial lines indented then there is very little you can do, however use of views with the property of layout and carefully placed child views/labels etc will give you many choices.
