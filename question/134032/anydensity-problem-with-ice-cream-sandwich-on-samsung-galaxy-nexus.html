anyDensity problem with Ice Cream Sandwich on Samsung Galaxy Nexus

Hi,

my new app is in the android market (search for "54House") but now I found out there is a problem, that the graphics screen width is not filled completly on Samsung Galaxy Nexus phones with Ice Cream Sandwich.
All my graphics have a maximum width of '320'.
On all phones with a lower version of android my graphics fit to the screen width. Also if the real screen width is bigger than 400 pixels in portrait mode.

I don't know is it a problem of Ice Cream Sandwich, or is it a problem with the nexus phone.

In the manifest part I used: 

<supports-screens android:anyDensity="false"/>

My app is compiled with SDK 2.0.xxxxxx

What can I do to fix this problem?

Thanks in advance
