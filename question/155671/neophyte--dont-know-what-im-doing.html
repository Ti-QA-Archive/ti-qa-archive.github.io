  • I'm afraid you will have to hire a real developer if you want anything done.

    While Titanium has a very large and vibrant community, we still are in the business of making mobile applications. I chose the term business carefully to make sure you understand that making an application requires an investment (Whether it's time or money; you usually need both) If you don't commit to an app, you will be left with the people's good-will and the hope they will take you a little further up the hill. But from experience, you will have a hard time finding someone that will take you all the way to your goal without expecting something in return. Same would go for a carpenter, electrician or any other contractor

    ####Just try to apply your question to something else than a mobile application shall we?

    I have a very exciting idea for a building and I would like to know if there are any construction workers out there that could build it for me. Can someone help me or do I have to pay big bucks and get it built?

    Hum, now that is something you would never see in the wanted ads. I guess you see get point.

    That being said, the community is more than happy to help out fellow developers who are faced with a technical problem. And you can count us to be there to help if you have any issue getting your application to work.
