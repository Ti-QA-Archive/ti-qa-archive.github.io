Paypal Question..will this get rejected?

I have a free app, and I'm looking at this as a solution for users to upgrade, since the in-app module price is ridiculous.

  • A user clicks a "register button"
  • it takes them paypal (in app) site
  • they pay, get a code emailed to them
  • enter the code, app is registered, ads are removed and all features are released

Otherwise, they would have to register on the free to sync their data to the website, then delete the free app and download the paid one, re-login and download their changes.

Question is, does anyone know if this is something Apple will reject, because of the purchasing?
