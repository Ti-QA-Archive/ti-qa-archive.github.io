    • Android and iOS are different beasts. Because Titanium maps its UI api to native objects, those objects may or may not exist on a particular platform, may or may not look the same, may or may not behave the same.

      If you read the API docs located at http://docs.appcelerator.com you will find that there are android specific api calls and iOS specific api calls.

      As such, when posting, please be as thorough as you can be. Answers may vary depending on the platform you are targeting.

      In other cases, users have failed to mention the platform, and get all ticked off when an ios solution fails to work on their android only project.
