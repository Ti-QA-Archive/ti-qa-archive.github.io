    Are you testing this on device? If so, is your device connected via USB cable in storage mode? If you said yes to theseâ¦

    You need to either disconnect your phone when testing or set your phone to "charge only" mode when testing. The reason for this is that when you have your phone connected in storage mode, your apps won't have access to the SD card. It would appear as though your app is trying to access cached remote images on the SD card and is failing. This could be preventing admob from delivering your ads.
