Publish app with Developer Plan

Hi guys,

I have a question about my Developer Plan.
I want to publish an app on the stores (I have never done this before) and I have a question: can I publish app on the Google Play or the App Store or I have to upgrade my plan to be able to do this?

Let me explain myself better.

Here there's a link

At the bottom of the page, under Frequently Asked Questions there are written this words:

Yes! The Platform is free for use for development with all the features of the Team plan. You only need to choose your preferred plan and pay when you’re ready to deploy your app(s) to production.

It means that I must upgrade my plan to put my app(s) on the stores or that simply I do not have access to all the services that Appcelerator Platform offers (such as push notifications, support, analytics…) but I'm able to publish app on the stores?

Thanks everyone for your help.

    If you are using the Appcelerator platform (Appcelerator Studio, Appcelerator CLI, etc) then you need an actual plan to push to the stores.

    If you download and compile the Titanium SDK from github, the Titanium CLI from npm, etc, you can do with it as you please, without paying anything to anyone.
