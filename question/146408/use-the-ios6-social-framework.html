  • Howto:

    • editing of titanium.xcconfig.

    In most cases: TITANIUM_SDK_VERSION = 3.0.0.GA and TITANIUM_SDK = &#x2F;User&#x2F;_YOURACCOUNT_&#x2F;Library&#x2F;Application Support&#x2F;Titanium&#x2F;mobilesdk&#x2F;osx&#x2F;$(TITANIUM_SDK_VERSION)

    • start of sudo python build.py

    • unzip the new Zip-file and copy to

    • module folder.

    Now you can configure your project and use the moduel. Good luck!
