iOS App Icons Being Overwritten?

Ok, so I'm getting an app ready for beta testing and am having trouble getting the app icons and splash page to work properly.

I've created and saved all the different versions of my icons and splash pages.

I put them in the directory: /Users/macuser/Documents/Titanium_Studio_Workspace/MyProject/Resources/iphone

I go build/test my project, and all the icons and splash pages get overwritten with the default Alloy icons….

Am I putting these files in the wrong location? Why are they being overwritten at all?

    Icons in an Alloy project need to go in your_project/app/assets Putting them in the iphone, android, etc. directories is not required but lets you use different icons & graphics for each platform.
