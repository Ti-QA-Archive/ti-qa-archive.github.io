  • Oh sweet never mind I got it working :)

    Since each header has its own content view, I just made 

        content.visible = !content.visible;
    if(content.visible)
        content.height = "auto";
    else
        content.height = "0";

    I tried doing content.parent.remove(content); but I couldn't add it again afterwards since the view was empty after being removed.

    But I'm happy now
