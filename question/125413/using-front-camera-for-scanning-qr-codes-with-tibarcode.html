  • Are you using iOS or android or both? (It is always a good idea to mention that, pleas.)

    Looking at the Ti.barcode docs, it is clear there is not currently an option to specify the camera.

    Since the docs say it uses the Zxing library, I did a simple google search and can see several people asking how to do this and even one who requested bids for modifying the library for iPhone to allow this. (That request was canceled and not awarded.)

    On many devices, the front facing camera is much lower resolution than the rear facing camera. Therefore I would expect the scanning speed or success ratio to be much lower, even if you could do it.

    But you have my curiosity piqued, what is the usage scenario where the front camera is better positioned for QR scanning then the rear camera?
