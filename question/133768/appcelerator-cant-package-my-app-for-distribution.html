  • In your second log, look at iPhone Distribution: My Organization: ambiguous, that's the key.

    Seems that you have two distribution certificates with the same name "iPhone Distribution: My Organization". Clean the build folder, go to your keychain and rename the wrong certificate (maybe one of them has expired) or delete it if you are sure. Maybe you should generate again the provisioning profiles and load again on titanium.
