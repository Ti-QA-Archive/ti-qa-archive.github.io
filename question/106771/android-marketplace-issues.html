Android MarketPlace issues

I cannot get my app, built with Titanium, to show up in the MarketPlace on a device. I can find it via web portals to the MarketPlace, but never in a device or simulator. Scanning the QR code from the app listing on a web portal tells me the app is not found.

I've tried:

  1. changing the sdk version from 4 to 1 in the AndroidManifest.xml
  2. removing the calls for extra permissions in the AndroidManifest.xml (my app doesn't need internet access or gps or anything)

Wondering if anyone else has run into this and what you did to fix it?

I've posted my issues to the Android MarketPlace support forum (http://www.google.com/support/forum/p/Android+Market/thread?tid=141cbb03c1b110eb&hl=en), with no replies :(

  • Perhaps this is because you do not have a supported device, like HTC Wildfire. The reason that the device isn't supported is that a titanium app as standard do not support low desity devices, you have to add a tag in your android section of the tiapp.xml

    <supports-screens android:anyDensity="false" android:largeScreens="true" android:normalScreens="true" android:smallScreens="true"/>

    as you can see in the code, the smallScreens are set to true. This enables small screen device in market
