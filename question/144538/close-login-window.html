Close Login Window

Hi there,

I am trying to write an application where the user is presented with a login screen and then redirected to a navigation view with the relevant data displayed. I can't seem to find a concise answer - perhaps I have missed it - but how do I go about closing the login view once the user has navigated away from it?

Thanks,

Roy

  • What you can do is …

    Open your application window immediately followed by your login window. This way, your login window is opened above your application. Close your login window, and now your application window is the main window. Make sense?

    
var mainWindow...
var loginWindow...

mainWindow.open();
loginWindow.open();
