Animate Font Size Increase

I would like to increase the fontSize with an animation over a duration of x seconds.

Take the example where you have a label with some text in a view, like so.

var v = Ti.UI.createView({
height: Ti.UI.SIZE,
width: Ti.UI.SIZE,
});

var l = Ti.UI.createLabel({
height: Ti.UI.SIZE,
width: Ti.UI.SIZE,
text: "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Donec accumsan dictum sagittis. Mauris vehicula sit amet justo sed consectetur. Ut consectetur nec metus ut ullamcorper. Nulla luctus tortor ipsum, id vehicula diam convallis vitae. Praesent vel nunc erat. Phasellus eleifend purus in libero euismod dictum. Cras at est faucibus, dapibus mauris non, consectetur lectus. Nulla egestas turpis ac erat porta, a pretium mi sodales. Maecenas eu nisl condimentum, cursus ante sed, malesuada eros. Phasellus a lectus et elit tincidunt iaculis."
font: {
fontSize: 20,
});
v.add(l);

How would I go about increasing the fontSize of the label through an animation?

I could use a transform with a scale, but this is not the same as increasing the font-size, part of the label could appear outside of the view area.

I could use an interval and increment the fontSize increase but this is messy and results in a flicker where … appears briefly during the animation.

I want it to be as smooth as possible with word wrapping being taken care of, anyway to do this?

    • l.animate({transform:Ti.UI.create2DMatrix({scale:2.0})})
