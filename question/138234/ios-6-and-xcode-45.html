  • I dont think the NDA with Apple applies to discussing getting xcode working, just the features itself.

    That said, Apple says you can't use the Xcode 4.5 to even submit to the store, so I wouldnt install it yet. You CAN install the beta on your phone, but then you'll have to use something like Testflight to actually get it, since Xcode wont recognize the device without the SDK.
