How to enable user-selection on a div (Titanium desktop)

I am using HTML5-contentEditble on a div and the text is editable but not selectable by default. Does anyone know a quick fix to enable selection on a certain div. (On Titanium desktop). Tried -> {user-select:all !important;} doesn't seem to work.

  • I spent a few hours on that issue, as I, too, deeply needed contenteditable divs. (Target: Titanium Desktop, OSX). Here's the trick I found.

    Your div declaration should look something like this: 

    <div contenteditable="true" style="-webkit-user-select:auto;-webkit-user-modify:read-write;" id="myeditablediv">

    Please note that the property -webkit-user-modify is absolutely mandatory.

    Another (more brutal) way to get contenteditable to work is to edit the file
    /Library/Application Support/Titanium/modules/osx/tiui/1.1.0/default.css and remove the line:

    -webkit-user-select: none;

    It modifies the global behaviour about selection. You probably don't want that.
