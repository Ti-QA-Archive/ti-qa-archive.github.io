Facebook login crashes on first authorize/login attempt, CAUSE: privacy settings in the FB account - help!

My app keeps crashing when loggin in with facebook. It happens the first time after accepting the "app requirements" and installing it. The modal popup closes and console outputs a lot of errors.

After some research I found the cause: it works perfectly when I change my facebook account privacy posting settings to PUBLIC instead of FRIENDS (Control Privacy When You Post).

I tested it on 3 different iPhones, but they all crash, including the emulator. After changing the privacy settings they all work.
