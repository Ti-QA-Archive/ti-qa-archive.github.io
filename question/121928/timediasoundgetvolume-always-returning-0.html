Ti.Media.Sound.getVolume() always returning 0

Recently I got contracted to build an app that makes big use of sound effects and music, so I looked into the Ti.Media.Sound and Ti.Media.AudioPlayer modules and got to work digging into these two.

After working with Ti.Media.Sound for about 4 hours, I got to the volume part of my coding and began working out a system that uses a Slider to change the volume of the Ti.Media.Sound file. As I tested it out, I started to find that the .getVolume() function of the module always returned a 0, no matter what I did.

I tried: (value = 10)

Ti.Media.Sound.setVolume(value);

Ti.Media.Sound.volume = value;

But neither of those worked, so I tried setting the value as a Property and setting it through firing an event from the Slider, but that didn't work either. No matter what I did, .getVolume() and .volume always returned a 0.

Note that just because it returned a 0, the actual volume of the sound changed, although very slightly. The change between the volume 1 and 100 is so slight it's hard to even notice.
