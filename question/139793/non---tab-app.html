  • The native iOS tab group brings the 'more' tab behavior in that it automatically takes all tabs > 5 (index 4) and creates a 'More' tab that has a window listing the other 'tabs' as rows in a tableView. Thus, those other tabs can't truly be 'selected' in the typical manner -it's up to the user to do the selecting. So, I would recommend you reassess the UI conventions.
