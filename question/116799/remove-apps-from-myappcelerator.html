  • I just found it in my Application Data, in my system it was in:

    C:\Documents and Settings\myusername\Application Data\Titanium\appdata\com.appcelerator.titanium.developer\app_com.appcelerator.titanium.developer_0\0000000000000001.db

    I just deleted that file and restarted Titanium, it asked me to login again, but it got rid of all the projects. I just had to do the "Import Project" to open back up the projects I cared about.

    I don't know if there is a better way, but when I grepped my directories for one of my projects, that was the only file that came up.
