  • Yes, you can have a paid app without using the license verification. I've build both paid apps and in-app purchase apps and have them in the Android Market. All you need to do is set up a Google Checkout account and set your price when you upload the apk file.

    What Android's License Verfication does is make sure someone who knows what they're doing can't pay for your app and then set up a website to give it away for free. While I'd love to have an Appcelerator module that does that for us, I don't think it exists right now. Fortunately, my apps are based on weekend events so I haven't really seen any pirating (that I know of anyway).
