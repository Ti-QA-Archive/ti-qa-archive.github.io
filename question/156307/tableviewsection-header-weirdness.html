Tableviewsection header weirdness

Testing it out on iOS, Appcelerator SDK 3.1.2

I've got a table with several sections that populate fine. However, the custom header for the section exhibits strange scroll behaviour. Basically it doesn't scroll until the next section reaches it at which point the header changes to the new section header?

I'm after a scroll with section heade

    Hi

    That is the expected behaviour, on iOS any table that is not grouped has a "sticky" section header and footer, the current sections' header will appear and the top any footer will appear at the bottom of the tableView. Once the a new section comes into a view the header will move up and take over the sticky top position of the tableView.

    You can tell the tableView to have a style of grouped, this will then stay with the rows and not be sticky - but you may not be happy with the UI.
