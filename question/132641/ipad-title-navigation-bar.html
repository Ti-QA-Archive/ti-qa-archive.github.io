  • title bar are only available in tabbed apps. You must open a tabgroup and hide the tabBar. It may sound strange, but is the "official" way.

    var tabGroup = Titanium.UI.createTabGroup();

var win = Titanium.UI.createWindow({
  title:'title',
  tabBarHidden: true
});

var tab = Titanium.UI.createTab({  
  title:'whatever',
  window:win
});

tabGroup.addTab(tab);
tabGroup.open();

    Other options with a title bar are modal windows, as you said, and navigationGroups.
