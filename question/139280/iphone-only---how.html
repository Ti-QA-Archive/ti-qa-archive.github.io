IPHONE ONLY - How?

I'm getting crazy over this. I have a working app ready for submit. It has to be an IPHONE ONLY app, thus I changed everything in app.xml and manifest accordingly. When I hit "Distribute" in Studio it nicely shows me the archive in XCode 4. It validates and submits without problems, but then iTunes Store asks me for a missing iPad screenshot!

Binary details say: your binary indicates support for iPad.

Then I recompile with Studio and open the proj with XCode4 and I do see THREE targets (iphone, ipad, universal). Ok, I build and archive the iphone version only (in my case I have to restate signatures, but ok). Everything succeeds. But there is NO WAY I can see the archive in XCode's organizer. I look into /Library/Developer/XCode/Archives and the archive is missing there too (or in some cases it's empty).

Please: is there a way to deploy an IPHONE ONLY app?
