    Reinstalling everything is quite pointless, because you never discover what the true source of the issue is.

    Have you tried deleting everything inside of the subdirectories of $PROJECT\build\android\ and relaunching the emu? In other words, delete "$PROJECT\build\android\<subdir>\*".
