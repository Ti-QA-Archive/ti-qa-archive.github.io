  • As an extremely new user, I would strongly suggest that you ditch the coffeescript while writing your Titanium apps. The learning curve for new users is just steep enough to not need another layer of complexity added to it. That being said, all new projects are packaged with an api.jsca file that serves as the auto-completion and code lookup file from within studio. You may be able to use it to achieve what you need in your coffee files.
