sign an IOs App for another Company

Hi,

My development team is working on an Ios App for another company. I want to sign The IOS App to publish it for the IPads of the company.
What Apple says : You need to be invited on the Company Developper Account. There is no other way.
What the Company Say : with all the others developpement team we only provide 3 files : the distribution certificate, the private key and the provisioning profile. No need to be invited on our Development account.

I try the Company Solution but impossible to sign the App with Titanium. So here are my questions :

  • Is there any limitations with Titanium to use the Company way of signing an IOS App
  • Is it the good way to do it ?

Thanks by advance for any help you would provide.. I'm lost in apple security…

  • As your company is building iOS products for other company then your company will surely have an Apple account. you can create the development certificate from your account and can use those in the other company app. Even with your account you can even generate the production certificate that will help that app to go in marketplace.
    but with this way the other company wont be able to manage or check the app review at all, as it was uploaded from your account.

    The way is that tell the other company to have the apple developer account and either you or those company people generate the required certificates with same appID you used and then use those certificate for your development.

    If the appID for the certificate is same as that of your project App ID then titanium will not create any problem.

    • If company is creating the appID for that App than you dont have to worry about it, just ask then for the provision profile with all the ipad UDID added in that provisioning profile.

      on your end you just have use their provisioning profile to create the app and send the .app file to them.
      As you will be using their provisioning profile then the app will run on all the devices whose UDID is added to the provisioning profile,

      I am saying this approach because the app will be used within the company and if we use the distribution certificate here then any person outside of that company can use that app.

      So in short just ask them the provisioning profile with the necessary UDID added in it.
