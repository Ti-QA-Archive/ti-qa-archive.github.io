XML or SQLite? That is the question...

So I am building an App for a client that lets the user add people to the app and each user gets a weekly chart where they can log whether they completed a task very well, ok or poorly. Each person can have multiple tasks.

The client wants to give users the opportunity to compare the stats from the last week with the average of all times.

That brings up my question.

I need to keep a database of each user and this database has to keep record of even day since the person was added of how they performed their task.

I am very comfortable working with XML and not at all with SQL. Heck, I don't even get why there seems to be dozens of varieties of SQL.

I am asking those of you more familiar with databases if you think wether XML will be viable for this or if I should bite the bullet and do an intense, remedial, lighting, self-taught course on SQL? I don't really know what a schema is or what any SQL commands beyond SELECT * FROM tablename is completely foreign to me.

Opinions?
