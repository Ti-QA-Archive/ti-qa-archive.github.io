  • Sorry – the two answers you got were completely wrong. moment.js is a great library for formatting date/time strings, but it doesn't do anything like what you're looking for. And setInterval() would only work if the user kept his phone on and your app active for hours on end (not very practical, or even possible on battery).

    You are going to need to use LocalNotifications on iOS and AlarmManager on android. I have written a tutorial app, GrillTime, that covers this topic. You will be especially interested in part 3 of the article.
