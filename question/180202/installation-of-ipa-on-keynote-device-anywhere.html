  • Hello Soumya Kanti Kar,

    You can upload your app to the iTune so that anybody can download your app from anywhere and can use it.

    Or if you want to package your app for testing remote devices or self, of course, you will need to gather the UUIDs for each of those devices and used them when creating the provisioning profile. And Ad Hoc distribution is better for that.

    Ad Hoc: Publish your application as a package that can be distributed on a limited number of devices for testing.

    Distribute and install your ad hoc application

    For Ad Hoc builds, you need to give the provision profile (.mobileprovision) and package to your testers to install it to their device. Their device needs to be included in the provisioning profile for them to use it.

    1. Launch iTunes.
    2. Drag the provision profile to iTunes.
    3. Drag the package file to iTunes.
    4. Make sure your application is selected and sync your device.
