Scrollableview - Circullar Scroll

Can scrollableview scroll views circularly..?
It means it continue to scroll event at the end of view..

If user scrolling and reach at last view at that time it continue to start again with 1st view…

  • Ive seen a post somewhere by Aaron Saunders that details a technique for this - Cant remember where - maybe on his website.

    The idea essentially is that you only maintain a list of the current view and its immediate left and right neighbour. Then as you swipe right , you move the righthand view into position and load up the next one to the right. There is nothing at this point to stop you "wrapping" around back to the first view if you get to the end
