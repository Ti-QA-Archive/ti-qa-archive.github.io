  • paste your module at following path:

    C:\Documents and Settings\gagantiwari\Application Data\Titanium\mobilesdk\win32\1.8.1\module\android

    or parallel to resource folder in your project.
    In tiapp.xml write:

    <modules>
   <module plateformName="android"version="1.0">ti.volumeslider</module>
</modules>

    And finally in your project use Ti.require(&#39;ti.volumeslider&#39;);
    this whole process will include your module in your project/application.This process for android only.If u r working on mac the path for pasting module will be different .it will be .....\1.8.1\module\iPhone and the plateformName in tiapp.xml will be iphone.Thanx
