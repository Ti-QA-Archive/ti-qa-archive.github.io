  • Failure [INSTALL_FAILED_MISSING_SHARED_LIBRARY]

    This most often occurs when the emulator you are targeting doesn't have the GOOGLE APIs installed. Because TIShadow preloads ALL Ti Modules so they are available to you, it is possible that you have never use a Ti module that required the GOOGLE APIs before.
