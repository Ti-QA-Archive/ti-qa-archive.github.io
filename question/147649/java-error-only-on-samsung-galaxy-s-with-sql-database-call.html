db.execute('begin');

This is the result from DDMS. It seems to say the 'string is out of bounds…'. But it works for all my other devices! Anyone have any ideas on this one?

  • Well, I guess I get to answer my own question… didn't and doesn't necessarily make sense to me but, on the Galaxy S, you cannot use simply 'BEGIN' or you get the error… changed the transaction to begin with 'BEGIN IMMEDIATE TRANSACTION'. Once I made the change, everything worked properly… and it REALLY sped up the database update.

    Ray
