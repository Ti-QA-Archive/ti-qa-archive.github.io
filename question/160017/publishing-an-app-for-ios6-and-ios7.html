Publishing an app for iOS6 and iOS7

Hi.

I have built and tested my app using SDK version 7.0 and 6.1 and its working great in iOS7 simulator and also in iOS6.1 simulator. Now I would like to know how can I publish my app in order to keep it iOS6 compatible.

What I need to do? Must I compile and publish 2 versions? Or publishing only the iOS7 version will be compatible with iOS6 (I'm not using any special iOS7 items?

I have seen in the app store apps saying that are IOS7 compatible but also IOS6.
