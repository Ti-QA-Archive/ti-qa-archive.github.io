Alloy list refreshes all items

I'm using Alloy with databinding and it seems that whenever I call fetch, the entire collection is redrawn.

Is there a way to refresh only the new entities?
Maybe I should a different backbone method?

    Data-bound multi-view controls (like ListViews, TableViews, ScrollableViews) are reset with new data when a fetch is called. It's one of the trade-offs of the databinding system we provide. It's simple – drop in a tag and couple of attributes and your views stay current with your data without any controller code. But if that's not what you want, you need to resort to code in the controller to manipulate the views directly.

    • Right, you would populate your ListView within the controller code. There are methods on ListView and ListViewSection, such as insertItemsAt() and replaceItemsAt() that will give you the granular control that Alloy's simple binding technique does not. You could bind to the events and use those methods to update your list. Or, you could manually update your list as part of any update/insert operations (not as MVC).
