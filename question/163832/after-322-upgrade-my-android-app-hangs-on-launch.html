  • I finally had to give up on trying to figure out WHY this was happening. I created a new, empty, project that would install and run without hanging, then slowly dropped in my application libs, views, controls etc. Now the app works, and the bug that I was working on when this all happened to me was also "fixed". Apparently, what I thought was a bug in my application was simply the first symptom of whatever went wrong.
