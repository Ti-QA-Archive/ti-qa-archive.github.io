  • much better… Was going to take all day just to scroll to the bottom in the other thread grin

    A lot of those warnings are 'normal' (I know, how can a warning be expected, particularly nearly a hundred of them, right?) with the emulator.

    [ERROR][PackageManager( 77)] Package com.appcelerator.kitchensink requires unavailable shared library com.google.android.maps; failing!

    Your target emulator needs to be one that has the Google APIS in it.
