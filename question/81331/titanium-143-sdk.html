    Marcin

    You haven't stated what platform you are using. On Linux, which is what I use, the sdks I have downloaded are located in:

    /opt/titanium/mobilesdk/linux/

    Just extract the zip file, and move the directory named after the version number (ie the dir named 1.4.3) into it.

    If you are on another platform, just search your the mobilesdk directory.

    Hope this helps
