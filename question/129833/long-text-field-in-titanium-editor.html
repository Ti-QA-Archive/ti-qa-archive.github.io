Long text field in Titanium Editor

Merry Christmas to ALL!

My question might be dumb but I am really bothered. I created a label with a lot of stuff in the text field. Hence whenever I need to edit the text, I have to drag the scroll bar from the left to the right. In addition, I want my code to line neatly in the editor window. I cannot figure a way to do it. Whenever I press return key to break a line (like in a word document), there's an error. 

var intro_page1 = Titanium.UI.createLabel({
    text:'Earth (or the Earth) is the third planet from the Sun, and the densest and fifth-largest of the eight planets in the Solar System. It is also the largest of the Solar System's four terrestrial planets. It is sometimes referred to as the World, the Blue Planet, or by its Latin name, Terra.\n\nEarth interacts with other objects in space, especially the Sun and the Moon. At present, Earth orbits the Sun once every 366.26 times it rotates about its own axis, which is equal to 365.26 solar days, or one sidereal year.',
    top:'80',
    left:'80',
    width:'600',
    height:'auto',
    color:'black',
    textAlign:'left',
    });

    Hi, This is how you write the text, if not everything has to be displayed on a single line in text editor

    var intro_page1 = Titanium.UI.createLabel({
    text:'Earth (or the Earth) is the third planet from the Sun,'+ 
        'and the densest and fifth-largest of the eight planets'+ 
        'in the  Solar System. It is also the largest of the Solar '+
        'System\'s four terrestrial planets. It is sometimes referred'+ 
        'to as the World, the Blue Planet, or by its Latin name, Terra.'+
        '\n\nEarth interacts with other objects in space, especially '+
        'the Sun and the Moon. At present, Earth orbits the Sun once '+
        'every 366.26 times it rotates about its own axis, which is '+
        'equal to 365.26 solar days, or one sidereal year.',
    top:'80',
    left:'80',
    width:'600',
    height:'auto',
    color:'black',
    textAlign:'left',
});
