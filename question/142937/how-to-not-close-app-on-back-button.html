How to NOT close app on back button?

I'm developing an app that has a main window with a button to open another window that contains a picture gallery, using the following project:

https://github.com/novelys/titanium-picture-gallery

I cannot figure out how to make Android simply return to my main window. It always just quits the entire application. I've tried to understand Android's heavyweight and lightweight windows and have tried a combination of fullscreen, navBarHidden, modal, and windowSoftInputMode settings on the PictureGallery window, but with no luck.

Any advice of how to get this to work? Which windows should be heavyweight? The main and the picture gallery? neither? I'm confused.
