VideoPlayer "click" eventListener doesn't seem to fire

I want to call a function via fireEvent when someone plays a video (with hidden controls on repeat). If I place an image over the top of the video, it works perfectly. But when I try to use the listener directly on the video (or on the window), it doesn't fire. What do I need to do differently?

This works:

image.addEventListener('click', function(e){
   Ti.App.fireEvent('hideTabGroup');    
});

This doesn't work:

activeMovie.addEventListener('click', function(e){
   Ti.App.fireEvent('hideTabGroup');    
});

This doesn't work either:

win.addEventListener('click', function(e){
   Ti.App.fireEvent('hideTabGroup');    
});
