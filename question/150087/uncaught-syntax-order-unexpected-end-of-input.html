  • You say you are using a webview, well, what are you doing in the webview? Simple html? javascript? jquery? firing events into or out of the webview?

    You have been around these parts long enough to know better.

    Once in a webview, you are much more in androids world than appcelerator/titanium. Missing html tags, improper json formatting, extra or missing parenths, brackets, braces can all cause this error.
