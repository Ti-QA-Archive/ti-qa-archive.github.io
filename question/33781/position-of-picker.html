Position of Picker

It seems like the picker always positioned at center vertically. Is there a way to position and size the picker? (iphone)

— asked June 10th 2010 by Peter Lum
  • iphone

  • You can just use left, top, width and height to customize the picker e.g.

    var picker = Ti.UI.createPicker({
    top:40,
    width:150
    });
