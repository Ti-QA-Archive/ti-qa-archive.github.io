  • Titanium has a forward Geocoder which does exactly what you want:

    Ti.Geolocation.forwardGeocoder('440 N Bernardo Ave, Mountain View, CA 94043', function(e){
    Ti.API.info(e.latitude + ', ' + e.longitude);
});
