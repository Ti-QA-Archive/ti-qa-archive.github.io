    Despite your recent childishness, I am going to answer your question in the hopes that you'll be able to conduct yourself like an adult in this forum. If you are unable to do so, you will no longer be welcome to ask your questions here.

    With that said, the easiest way to just use what you've already built would be to simply wrap it in a webview. Put your existing code in a file called clock.html, then just point a webview at that file. And that will give you something that can be used by Android.

    var webview = Ti.UI.createWebView({
    url:'clock.html'
});
