  • This has been a problem for quite awhile, and hasn't been addressed. The root of the issue has to do with the labels on the email that are synced from various services (such as exchange, google, etc.) don't necessarily match the native labels for email. (For example the label for type of email may just be 'email' instead of 'home').

    You can look at this previous question, this previous question from over 6 months ago, and this support ticket which offers a work around if you edit the titanium framework yourself. Personally the changes to the framework didn't fix my issue.
