  • Hi,

    In 1.8.0.1 sdk version, annotation are in a separate jar. This is not well documented. In previous version, everything were located in titanium.jar.

    You must add kroll-common.jar and kroll-apt.jar in your buildPath. They are located in in your sdk installation.
