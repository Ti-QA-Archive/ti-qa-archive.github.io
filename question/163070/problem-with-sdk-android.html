  • Couple of things:

    Do you have your ANDROID_HOME and ANDROID_SDK set up?

    declare -x ANDROID_HOME="/sdks/android-sdk-macosx"
declare -x ANDROID_SDK="/sdks/android-sdk-macosx"

    Is your android tooling installed completely?

    run the android utility from within the android-sdk-install (probably in the tools subfolder)

    When it comes up, you will be presented with the sdk manager view. Under tools, do you have Android SDK Tools, Android SDK Platform-Tools, and at least one Android SDK Build-tools installed?

    Update your Titanium Tooling

    Lastly, I know you are talking about android, but really need to be thinking about moving to the 3.2.X tooling and SDK.
