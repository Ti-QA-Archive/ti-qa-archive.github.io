    Just follow this guide if you'd like to use 1.4.1. It's pretty simple :P

    Taken from that page:

    WARNING: Builds from our continuous builds site can be very unstable and unpredictable

That being said, if you are looking to stay on the bleeding edge, or get a bug fix before the official release, this is probably your best bet.

    I use 1.4.1 just because it has a bug fix that I really needed (For the Android SearchBar). If there is an unstable version I just don't update. Pretty simple :P
