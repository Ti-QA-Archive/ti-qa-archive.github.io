passwordMask breaks textfield on ios8

I'm come across a strange problem where one of my textfields had stopped working and would only allow me to enter 1 character. I found that by removing passwordMask="true" from the field it started working again.

<TextField id="password" hintText="Password" autocorrect="false" passwordMask="true" left="40" right="10" returnKeyType="Titanium.UI.RETURNKEY_DONE"></TextField>

Titanium Studio, build: 3.4.0.201409161931
CLI version 3.4.0-rc4, Titanium SDK version 3.4.0.v20140922135716

  • Hello, Marcus. I have tested this with your Code. It's working fine.

    <TextField id="password" hintText="Password" autocorrect="false" passwordMask="true" borderStyle="Ti.UI.INPUT_BORDERSTYLE_ROUNDED" left="40" top="30" right="10" returnKeyType="Titanium.UI.RETURNKEY_DONE"></TextField>
