  • For querying custom objects, please refer to following page, yes, you can use where:{user_id:user_id}
    http://cloud.appcelerator.com/docs/search_query#where

    For ACL:
    You can create one ACL object, owner will be in its reader and writer list by default, then attach it to the objects you want to control.
