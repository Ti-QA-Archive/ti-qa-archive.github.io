New iPad App in AppStore!!

I finished a new weather app for iPad only. Apple approved the app last night.
The name of the App: Mono Weather
link: Click here

Why is Mono Weather so special:
- 1 screen application
- can be used around the world
- shows current weather of your current location
- forecast for the next 4 days
- update on resume
- update on restart
- sunrise/-set
- moonrise/-set
- moon phase as image and text
- Local date & time
- Geolocation 2 decimal
- City & country code
- layout in complete retro digital weather station style
