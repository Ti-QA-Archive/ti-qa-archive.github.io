How to open spesific window when focus a tab ?

Hi, my simple alloy structure as below;

<Alloy>
    <TabGroup id="tabGroup">
        <Tab id="tab1" title="TAB 1">
            <Window id="Win1" title="Windows 1">
                <TableView id="tableView1">
                    <SearchBar hintText="search" showCancel="true" barColor="#000"  />
                </TableView>
            </Window>
        </Tab>
        <Tab id="tab2" title="TAB 2">
            <Window id="Win2" title="Windows 2"></Window>
        </Tab>
    </TabGroup>
    <Window id="Win1Child" title="Windows 1 Child"></Window>
</Alloy>

I open Win1Child window with tableView1's rows click's event then Win1Child open as child window in this step. But when i click tab2 wihout change child window and reopen tab1 Win1Child shows on screen .

My question is: How can i always open Win1 when i focus tab1 from tab2?

Thanks in advance for your help.

  • Accepted Answer

    You just need to close Win1Child whenever you leave tab1 by using the "blur" event. Here's how I would do it based on your snippet (tested and works):

    index.xml

    <Alloy>
    <TabGroup id="tabGroup">
        <Tab id="tab1" title="TAB 1" onBlur="closeWindow">
            <Window id="Win1" title="Windows 1">
                <TableView id="tableView1" onClick="doClick">
                    <SearchBar hintText="search" showCancel="true" barColor="#000"  />
                    <TableViewRow title="click me"/>
                </TableView>
            </Window>
        </Tab>
        <Tab id="tab2" title="TAB 2">
            <Window id="Win2" title="Windows 2"></Window>
        </Tab>
    </TabGroup>
    <Window id="Win1Child" title="Windows 1 Child" backgroundColor="#00f"></Window>
</Alloy>

    index.js

    function doClick() {
    $.tab1.open($.Win1Child);
}

function closeWindow() {
    $.tab1.close($.Win1Child);
}

$.tabGroup.open();
