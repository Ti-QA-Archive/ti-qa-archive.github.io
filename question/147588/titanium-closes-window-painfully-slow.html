Titanium closes window painfully slow

I have an application that opens a login window, then a navaigation window, and then the window of the module they wish to use. For one particular module it displays the most recent 30 blocks of information and displays them in a tableview. It looks like this link.

When the window closes it starts at the top and takes one element out at a time at about half second intervals. On my iphone version this was not an issue as it closed the window quickly and smoothly. What could be causing this?

  • Solved it. Turns out it was slow because I was placing multiple views on the window. I fixed it by adding everything to one view and then adding that one and only view to the window. This wasn't an issue with iOS, but I guess it has to do with how Android closes its windows.
