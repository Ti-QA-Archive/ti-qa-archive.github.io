    This may not be apple recommended, but you can scale the switch using a transform:

    // Create a switch twice as big using a transform
var SliderTracking = Titanium.UI.createSwitch({
  height: 120,
  width: 150,
  title: L("switchTracking"),
  titleOn: L("switchTrackingOn"),
  titleOff: L("switchTrackingOff"),
  transform :  Titanium.UI.create2DMatrix().scale(2.0, 2.0)
});

    This has the benefit of functioning the same way and being quick and easy, but it also may not be acceptable in the app store, and may look a little grainy. I would not use this in production personally, only for prototyping.
