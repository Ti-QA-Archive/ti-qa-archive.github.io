close/stop video fired from webview

Hi.

I have a flash player in a webview that plays a video stream. When I remove the webview from the parent window the video keeps playing. (The audio goes even if there is no view holding the video).

How can I tell Titanium to stop and release the video? In other words how can I handle the videoplayer fired from the webview?

Thanks a lot

    Here's a few things you can try:

    • after you remove the webview, set it equal to null
    • try calling stopLoading() on the webview
    • try loading a dummy page into the webview before you remove it

    • Thanks. Some feedback. Nullifying gives me errors and crashes, stoploading doesn't work (I guess because the page is already loaded). What worked for me was the dummy page:

      player.url='blank.html';
player.reload();
win.remove(player);

      Thanks for help.

  • A better solution is to use webview plugin pause function. Snippet of my code is as follows:

                webView.pause();
            webWindow.remove(webView);
                webView = null;
