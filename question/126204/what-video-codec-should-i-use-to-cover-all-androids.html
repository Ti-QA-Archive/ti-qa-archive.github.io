what video codec should i use to cover all Androids

I built an app and is on the market now, the app has a video that plays at the start with an animated instructions on how to use it. The video is encoded as mp4/h264 It works great, except in some Androids that it fail all together, it says something like not supported or something. for example it works perfectly on HTC Desire and Samsung Galaxy S2 but it doesn't work on the Galaxy Ace.
Any idea if I can use other format that would be recognised by any android with 2.2 and above?

  • When encoding videos for mobile, I usually create dual encodings as its nearly impossible to cover ever single device on every single OS. I have found that one mp4 and one m4a usually covers the broad spectrum of major devices.
