Keep android process runing even if app is closed

I want to have a GPS tracker running in the background even if the app is closed (back button AND from the app switcher). But as soon as I swipe it out of the app switcher the process is killed (service still running) but it won't execute the service.

I'm using benCoding.AlarmManager module (https://github.com/benbahrenburg/benCoding.AlarmManager)
like this:

var alarmModule = require('bencoding.alarmmanager');
var alarmManager = alarmModule.createAlarmManager();

var now = new Date();
alarmManager.addAlarmService({
    requestCode : 10, service : 'com.app.appname.ServiceService', minute : now.getMinutes(), second : 30, interval : 1000 * 30
});

service.js is just containing a Ti.API.info() to debug it.

When the app is running its working fine (Settings - Apps - Running: 1 Proccess - 1 Service)
When I close the app with the back button: fine (still: 1 Proccess - 1 Service)
but as soon as I swipe it out of the app switcher the process is killed and it won't execute the service (0 Proccess - 1 Service)

I have other apps which are completely closed (e.g. Google Currents) but it still has the process running and the service (app switcher is completely empty).

Is there a way to achieve the same with titanium? I've also tried the Ti android service but is behaving the same as the alarmmanager.
