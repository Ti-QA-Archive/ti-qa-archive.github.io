  • Andre, welcome! :)

    Regarding "Android Honeycomb Preview, revision 1 File not found", I get that error too. It's a problem with Google's SDK rather than Titanium. Anyway, Titanium doesn't support any of the Honeycomb packages at this time, so there isn't any point installing them.

    Also note that Titanium currently supports Android versions 1.6 to 2.2 (although you should install, but not use, 1.5, due to an outstanding bug that may or may not affect you).

    Have you seen the Installing on Windows 7 guide? This is your most reliable way to get Titanium and all the 3rd-party components configured correctly.

    However, to shortcut to your specific issue, see Preparing for Android development.

    Report back if you have any further problems with your installation.

    Good luck!
