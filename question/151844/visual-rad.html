  • While ForgedUI was not a very convincing product this is true. I must say that VisualUI is pretty good form what I'v see of it. You can even design Alloy views as well a "classic" Titanium Views.

    I wouldn't wait for Appcelerator do put any effort into such a tool though. I was at a conference where Jeff Haynie himself said that the workload was to heavy for a company such as theirs. When you compare it to how much it will bring them, it makes total sense.

    So in conclusion, VisualUI would be your best bet. You can even test it on a 30 day trial period and it's 30$. Come on, these guys deserve at least that much don't you think?
