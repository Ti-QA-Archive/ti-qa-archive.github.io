Is there a way to make openPhotoGallery show ONLY pictures, and not videos?

Right now, Titanium.Media.openPhotoGallery shows the user all of his photos and videos. I want it to only show his photos.

Any way to make this happen?

    I believe all you need to do is at this property within openPhotoGallery:

    mediaTypes:[Ti.Media.MEDIA_TYPE_PHOTO],

    For the record, the other choices would be:

    mediaTypes:[Ti.Media.MEDIA_TYPE_VIDEO],

    mediaTypes:[Ti.Media.MEDIA_TYPE_VIDEO,Ti.Media.MEDIA_TYPE_PHOTO],
