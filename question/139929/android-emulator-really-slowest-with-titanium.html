Android Emulator Really Slowest with Titanium

I realize that Ti has the overload of the respective js engines to install, etc but running apps even in an already running emulator is so extremely slow through Ti. With Eclipse/Android ADT…bang! You're up and running in 1-3 seconds, even with debugging.

I use debug in Ti as a very, very last resort on Android. It literally takes 1-1.5 seconds just to step over a breakpoint, for Pete's sake.

70% of my coding time is spent tweaking UI because of the time required to simply make a change (or changes) and view it live in emulator.

I've been developing in one environment or another for 15 years and this is by far the worst and most frustrating development I have ever done. Throw in the newer, $7K/year/every-year price tag…well not much value that I can see at this point.
