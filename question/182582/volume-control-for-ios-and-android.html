Volume control for iOS and Android

Hi everyone,

How can I control the volume? For example: I would like to be able to read the volume value first, save it in a variable, then bring volume low then after 10 seconds bring the volume back to the original volume.
The problem is I want to control the volume whether audio, video or streaming is being playing, I want to be able to control the global volume so it does not matter what app is playing the audio/video.

    But there is no way to setVolume() since it's read-only property.
