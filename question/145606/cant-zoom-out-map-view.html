Can't zoom out map View

Hi, I use this little piece of code to display a map in a view contained in a window (iOS):

    var w = Ti.UI.createWindow({title:"Cerca",backgroundColor:'#000000'});
    var v = Ti.UI.createView({backgroundColor:'#ffffff'});

    var mapView = Titanium.Map.createView({
      mapType: Titanium.Map.STANDARD_TYPE,
      region: {latitude:41.871940, longitude:12.567380,latitudeDelta:0.04, longitudeDelta:0.04},
      animate:false,
      regionFit:false,
      userLocation:false
    });

    v.add(mapView); 
    w.add(v);

    var currentRegion={
            latitude: 41.871940,
            longitude: 12.567380,
            animate:false
        };
    mapView.setLocation(currentRegion);
    mapView.zoom(-15);

When I test it with my iOS device, I notice it doesn't zoom out (I want to center the map on an whole country). How is the zoom method supposed to work? Am I doing something wrong?

Thanks in advance.

    Hi

    Increase the values for these properties; latitudeDelta & longitudeDelta, right now have your map very zoomed in, make them larger numbers to zoom out.

    The deltas are the distance in lat/lon terms. Imagine you had 10 pins on the map in various locations and wanted to centre the map and have it zoomed to show all ten. You would loop the array adding all last together and all lons together, divide by ten at the end and that would be your centre point. To work out deltas, work out max and min of each direction and at the end subtract lowest from highest for each direction, that distance would be the delta, keep in mind the pins may fall more to one side than equal so either calculate for that or add a bit to the delta as a cheat.

    May seem complicated but just simple (and boring math).

    This gives a better zoom as it actually takes all data into account.

    Btw one small problem, the map may adjust the deltas slightly to account for optimum tile sizing, so allowing for that when adding a "fudge" factor.
