Problem publishing on Google Play

I have a problem publishing my app on Google Play.
When I upload the app I created the Google Play website states that my app will be available on 0 devices.

I then made a test, created a Single View App from the template, changed nothing, created an APK to publish and uploaded to Google Play. The same problem. "The app will be available on 0 devices".

Tried it with building against 4.0.3 Google APIs SDK and 2.3.3. Standard SDK.
I am using the Titanium 2.0.1 GA2, but also tried a Ti 2.1.0 SDK.

Why are apps built with Titanium not available on any device in the Google Market?

    The question should actually be: Why is the app that YOU are trying to submit not available on any devices on the Google Market.

    • Maybe you haven't ACTIVATED a particular APK that you uploaded. (I'm leaning towards this one)
    • You somehow have created an application with such a unique combination of hardware and permission requirements that no known device can run your application (highly unlikely)
