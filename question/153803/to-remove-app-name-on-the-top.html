  • Hi

    I am going to assume you are talking about Android given the likelihood.

    • Edit {appname}/tiapp.xml
    • Find <navbar-hidden>
    • Change false to true
    • Close your emulator

    Then

    • Menu
    • Tools
    • Clean
    • Select Project
    • Confirm

    Then re-build your app, the grey (Android) nav bar should have gone.
