      Luc

      Upcoming changes to the way debugging works in TiStudio have been included in the Nightly and Beta updates, but not the Stable ones.

      To be clear, debugging will only be available to developers with an Indie subscription or above, after the official TiStudio release at the beginning of July. For details about the differences between the upcoming Titanium Studio Standard (the Community version) and Premium, see the announcement about them here.

      To hopefully resolve your problem in the meantime, you can disable the Beta and Nightly sites and roll back the last, most recent, updates using the plugin manager. Alternatively, my preference would be to reinstall with only the Stable update site enabled.

      Let me know if this helps
