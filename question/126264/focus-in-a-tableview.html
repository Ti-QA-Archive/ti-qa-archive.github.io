Focus in a TableView

Hi,

I have a tableView with a scrollView in the headerView (code):

var headerView = Ti.UI.createView({
    height:84
});

var scrollView = Ti.UI.createScrollView({
    height:40,
    top:0,
    backgroundColor:"transparent"
});
headerView.add(scrollView);

var searchBar = Ti.UI.createSearchBar({
    top:40
});
headerView.add(searchBar);

var tableView = Ti.UI.createTableView({
    headerView:headerView
});
Ti.UI.currentWindow.add(tableView);

But, I need to do the focus in the TableView. In Objective-C, I do this:

[tableView resignFirstResponder];

But, if I doesn't this, the scrollToTop when I click in the status bar of iPhone doesn't work.

Who know how to fix this?
