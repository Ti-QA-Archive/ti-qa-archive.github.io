  • The short answer is No. You do not have to pay Appcelerator a single penny. The prices are for enterprise users who want enterprise level support. They have a slightly improved product called Appcelerator Studio that has a few nice to have extra features but are not neccessary. You can give away or sell your App in App Store and Play without any fees due to Appcelerator
