    On iOS there is no "click" event for tabGroup.

    If you need it (besides the "focus" event), there is a solution. I have posted a snippet (in Objective-C for TiUITabGroup.m) with a quick workaround here:

    https://jira.appcelerator.org/browse/TIMOB-6499
