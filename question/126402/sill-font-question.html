Sill Font Question

OK this really is basic and for that I apologise, but I'm really struggling to change a label's font. I have this code:

var title = Ti.UI.createLabel({
    text:page.Title,
    left:90,
    color:'#9d0d1e',
    top:5,
    height:'auto',
    width:'auto',
    font:{
        fontFamily:'Georgia',
        //fontWeight:'bold'
    }    
});

But it doesn't appear to be changing in the simulator, it's just sticking on the default font - am I doing something stupidly wrong here?

Many thanks,

    • Apologies keep makign that mistake. Just to let you know I've fixed it, by allocating the fontFamily property after the label's initialisation:

      var title = Ti.UI.createLabel({
    text:page.Title,
    left:90,
    color:'#9d0d1e',
    top:5,
    height:'auto',
    width:'auto',
    font:{
        fontfamily:'Georgia', //this doesnt seem to work
        //fontWeight:'bold'
    }    
});

title.font = {fontFamily: 'Georgia'}; //this does
