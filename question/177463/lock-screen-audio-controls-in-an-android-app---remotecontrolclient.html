Lock Screen Audio Controls In An Android App - RemoteControlClient

Hi

Does anyone have any experience / information in implementing the RemoteControlClient for an Android app built in Titanium.

I want to be able to play pause and skip audio tracks from the lock screen of my Android app.

I have implemented this functionality into my iOS app using a module from the marketplace but doesn't seem to be anything similar for Android.

Also as a side note does any one know how to allow audio to be played from your app even when it is in the background(via pressing the home button) it seems to play if i got to the lock screen but not if i press home button.

Cheers
Gary

  • Hi Gary,

    I'm trying to find a solution to controlling audio from the lock screen as well as the notification bar in an Android app. I haven't tried the RemoteControlClient yet, but I'll go ahead and give it a shot.

    I see you asked this 7 months ago, but just in case you're still on the hunt… As far as background audio, you can pass the 'allowBackground' option when creating your audio object and that will enable audio to continue playing when then app in the background (Android only, think the iOS implementation is bugged).

    var audioPlayer = Ti.Media.createAudioPlayer({
    url: 'http://url.toresource.com:8000/file.mp3',
    allowBackground: true
});
