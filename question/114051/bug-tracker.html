  • Daniel

    It's important that you post your issue here first, so that the community can verify it before you raise a ticket. As there a human resource cost to managing tickets, false tickets will hamper the development process. Obviously we would all rather the core developers concentrate on coding rather than to waste any time chasing bugs that do not exist.

    With this in mind, it's vital that the Contribute to Titanium guidelines are followed.
