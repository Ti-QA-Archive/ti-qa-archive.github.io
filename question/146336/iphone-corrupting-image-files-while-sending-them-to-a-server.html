Iphone "corrupting" image files while sending them to a server.

Hi all and happy new year.

Say we've got one image called image.png that is included with the app installation.

That image is correctly seen in iPhone and android devices.
That image is correctly sent to a server from iphone and android devices.

The issue is that iPhones and emulators can read any of those images sent with any device, but android devices cannot read the images sent to the server by iphone devices (they can download the files, but they are "corrupted" and nothing is seen). Android devices can read images sent by emulators or other android devices.

Why can this be? Or how can i fix it?
