WebView - No Wrap

town = town.replace(/ /ig, "&nbsp;");

I have noticed that if I replace all spaces with the non breaking space character, that the webview will not wrap anymore.

But if the original content is rendered it wraps fine.

Example Content:

<p><strong>Towns close by down river:</strong><br />

Replaced Content:

<p><strong>Towns&nbsp;close&nbsp;by&nbsp;down&nbsp;river:</strong><br />

So if the content is longer than the screen width it will not wrap, when '&nbsp;' is used.

  • This is indeed the default behaviour for all browsers, including Titanium's webview object. Browsers will not insert breaks into words since they don't know which language the content is and in general there are just too many exceptions in the rules that determine where you can insert a break.

    When a word is too long, it will be moved to the next line or users will need to scroll horizontally to read it completely, depending on the design of the webpage. A non-breaking space, like Goran said, does indeed what the name implies: it will connect the words together and prevent the browser from inserting a new line break in between.
