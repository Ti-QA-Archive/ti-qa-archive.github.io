    • You can refer this thread. The problem is that your simulator is crashing due to DTiPhoneSimulatorErrorDomain Code=1 , and solution is :

      Manually launch the iOS Simulator, then from the iOS Simulator menu, select "Reset Content and Settings". Rebuild your app and it should install. Assuming that does the trick, please build a couple times and see if the error comes back.
