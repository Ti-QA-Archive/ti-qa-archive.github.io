Centered text in Table View

I've got a TableView on the iPad and would like to display the text of a row aligned to the middle (center).

How can I do this?

  • Accepted Answer

    Add the following property to your label:

    textAlign:'center'

    Such as:

    var label = Ti.UI.createLabel({
   text:'This should be in the middle',
   width:'auto',
   height:'auto',
   textAlign:'center',
   font:{fontSize:16,fontWeight:'bold'}
});
