cannot develop apps that run on iOS?

I was reading the start guide and it seems like I can't make iOS applications unless I am using a Mac? I am running Titanium Studio on Windows. Am I out of luck if I don't have a Mac? I thought titanium generates iOS apps from a single codebase.
