    self.removeEventListener('touchstart', function(){});

    the remove method must receive the same params as the add method. The function must match. Its best to declare a function myfunction() and pass that in your add method.

    self.addEventlistener('touchstart', myfunction);

    then remove

    self.removeEventListener('touchstart, myfunction);
