How-to buid DIRECTLY to iOS devices WITHOUT opening iTunes or Xcode !

So the biggest slow-down I have with Ti is the lack of building directly to device for iOS… you have to build, then sync with iTunes… and that can take a while. Alternatively you could open Xcode, but thats a pain, esp if you need to do a clean build.

So in my MakeTi project (a CLI tool for building titanium apps) I have been working on building directly to an iOS device!

It is not done yet… but thought I would share what I have thus far :)
You can grab an early copy here!
