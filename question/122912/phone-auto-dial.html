Phone Auto-dial

This should be trivial. Using Android I have a callback to app.js from a webview to fire off a phone call. I fire the phone in the conventional way, all is successful and it brings up the dialer as expected but no number, even if hardwired, is transferred to the phone. Here is the simple hardwired call.

Titanium.App.addEventListener('fromwebview', function(data) {Titanium.Platform.openURL("tel:18001234567");} );

My understanding is that the number should be loaded into the dialer. Am I missing something fundamental here. As I mentioned the phone dialer launches fine just no number transfer.

Thanks, Steve

  • Aaron, Thanks but the +1 on the phone number has no effect. There is something basic in how the .openURL call is handled by the dialer.

    — commented July 20th 2011 by Steve Hansen

  • Ultimately Aaron was correct. Thephone numbers are very sensitive to URL encoding. Ultimately the following worked, auto-populating the dialer.

    {Titanium.Platform.openURL(&quot;tel:+1-8001234567&quot;);}

    Thanks, Steve
