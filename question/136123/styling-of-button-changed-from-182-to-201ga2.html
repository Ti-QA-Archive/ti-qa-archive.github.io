Styling of button changed from 1.8.2 to 2.0.1GA2

When I styled buttons with a pillbutton image (15px by 44px) you could style your buttons after 9 part slicing, so that the corners were not stretched.

To ensure it wasn't a problem with our .jss file I have created this test example which works fine in 1.8.2 but under 2.0.1GA2 it has a weird halo stretched effect on the backgroundImage.

In the pressed state the button image works as expected. Is this a bug or has something changed that I need to know? 

var win1 = Titanium.UI.createWindow({  
    title:'ui styling test',
    backgroundColor:'#dcd'
});

var btn1 = Titanium.UI.createButton({
    title: 'Login',
    left: 150,
    top: 110,
    width: 90,
    height: 35,
    borderRadius: 1,
    backgroundImage: '/images/pillbutton_focused.png',
    backgroundFocusedImage: '/images/pillbutton_focused.png',
    backgroundSelectedImage: '/images/pillbutton_selected.png',
    // backgroundDisabledImage: '/images/pillbutton_selected.png',
    font:{fontFamily:'Arial',fontWeight:'bold',fontSize:14}
});

win1.add(btn1);
win1.open();
