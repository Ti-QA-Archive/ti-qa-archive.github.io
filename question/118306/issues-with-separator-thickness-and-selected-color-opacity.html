Issues with Separator thickness and selected color opacity

I am building an iPhone app and I just ran into a couple of issues that I can’t figure out. First: the separator lines in Table View are usually between 1 and 2 pixels thick. Is it possible actually get rid of them, or at least make them considerably thinner? I tried using ‘transparent’ in the separatorColor but they are still visible, as the background of the row is an image and not a native usual color. I found in the forums here that this question was answered for Android, but not for the iPhone.

Second issue: the default color for the selected rows in Table View is blue. I am fine with that, the question is: how can I work with its opacity? I would like the blue to be 50% transparent, so you can see the background image set on that row. Thank you!
