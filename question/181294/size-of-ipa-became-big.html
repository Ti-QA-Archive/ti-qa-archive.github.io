Size of ipa became big

Hi there,

I change the Titanium SDK from 3.1.3GA to 3.5.0GA, was re-build.
Despite not changed the contents, the file size of the ipa is increased.
From 5MB to about 9 MB. (Universal binary is not included.)

Is this on the specification of Titanium SDK? and can to reduce this size?
