    Hi

    If the app is not running and a push notification comes in - the ONLY way your app will directly know about it is if the user chooses to interact with the push notification so that your app is returned to the foreground.

    Push notifications do NOT always interact with your app and (unfortunately) cannot be guaranteed to fire.

    At best you should consider push notifications a passive notice/alert, if there is information you need the user to know once you are back in the app - you must poll your server for updated information. Also there is no guarantee that every single push will arrive, or arrive in the order they were sent.
