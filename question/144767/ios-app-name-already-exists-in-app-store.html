iOS App Name Already Exists in App Store

My app is named after a tradeshow event that is trademarked. But there is another very large company in another industry that has an app by the same name which is also their trademark. We share a name but in different industries. Can I still submit my app under this name, or will it be rejected?

Thanks,

David

  • Hi David,

    Apple says in App Store Review Guidelines:

    • 3.1 Apps or metadata that mentions the name of any other mobile platform will be rejected

    • 22.2 Apps that contain false, fraudulent or misleading representations or use names or icons similar to other Apps will be rejected

    Some others say yes, some no.

    Try Google
