I have read through the hundreds of posts on this subject over the past few days and have followed the advice there. Nothing seems to work for the XP platform. The posts have discussed Android SDK 1.x or 2.x but not 3.x.

Any advice would be appreciated or perhaps a pointer to the forum entries that have addressed this problem with later versions of the Android SDK.
