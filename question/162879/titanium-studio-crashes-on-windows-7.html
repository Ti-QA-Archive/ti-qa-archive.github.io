  • For your problem their is a perfect solution (I was also facing this problem and fixed) : -

    Just keep in mind titanium studio needs internet connection at a particular point.
    Even though you cannot start it without an appcelerator account.

    Whenever you feel that your titanium studio is not working as expected and freezes too much, just connect your pc/laptop to high speed internet connection and wait for a while until it doesn't get what studio wants.

    You can see the background process of titanium in progress view.
    On menu bar : - window = > show view => other => general => progress.

    Hope so this works for you.
