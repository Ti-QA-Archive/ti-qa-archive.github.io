OptionDialog Conflicting with TabGroup in 1.8

Ti.UI.OptionDialog can be called multiple times in SDK 1.8 without problems, but not after a TabGroup is opened and then closed.

The problem happens in 1.8 (both with V8 and Rhino) but not in 1.7.5.

To reproduce the problem, the app.js, tiapp.xml and log files (using V8 and verbose mode) can be found at:

https://gist.github.com/1608602

Open the app then press "click", "dismiss", "click",
"open", "close", "click" in that sequence. Check the log.
