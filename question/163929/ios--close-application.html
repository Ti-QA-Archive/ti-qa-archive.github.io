  • As mentioned, you cannot explicitly 'end' an iOS App. You can however force it to terminate instead of backgrounding when you press the home button by adding this to your TiApp.xml

    

<ios>
<plist>
<dict>
  <key>UIApplicationExitsOnSuspend</key>
   <true />
</dict>
</plist>
</ios>
