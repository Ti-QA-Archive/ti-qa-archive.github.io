  • Hi Ivan (nice name you have :) ).

    I think App properties are saved on hard drive, they "live" as long as your application lives. You may clean this data by using "removeProperty" method. There is more info here: https://developer.appcelerator.com/apidoc/mobile/latest/Titanium.App.Properties-module
