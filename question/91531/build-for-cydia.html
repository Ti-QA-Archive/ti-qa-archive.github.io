  • Heureka !
    It IS possible :D I've managed to get an app of mine to work on jailbroken iphone :)

    It's simple just write your app as you would normaly in Titanium, test it and when it comes to packaging - just go around with xcode project which is hidden inside of the Titanium's project.
    It's actually not an Appcelerator's mistake but Apples. Just google "Xcode cydia" and package for development (not release) - but don't forget to go and by the developer license later on (at last :D )
