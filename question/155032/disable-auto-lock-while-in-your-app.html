Disable Auto-Lock while in your app

I have been looking everywhere for this but cannot seem to find it. I have a score keeper app I built and the #1 feature request I have been getting has been been to disable their iPhone from auto-locking while the app is up, for example when they play foosball they pull up the app, place it to the side and update the score when they score a point but if they have not score in a little while their phone automatically locks and they find it annoying having to unlock their phone to update the score. Anyone know how to do that in Alloy?
