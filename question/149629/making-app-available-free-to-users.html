  • Hi

    • iOS = No

    One app in the store has no influence over another in the store - even if both are yours.

    Whilst you can offer one of your apps free of charge this would be for everyone, not just users of a specific app.

    • Android = No (think)

    As far as I know the situation is the same for Android.
