Detect key pressed?

Hello:

How I can detect volume up/down buttons pressed with app in background and with mobile screen off?. Likes when you have the phone in pocket with screen off, but my App is in brackground mode.

Can be done?.

Thanks!!.

  • Hi

    Short answer; You don't.

    Longer answer;

    If your app is in background mode (IE not running) then it cannot listen out for things like a volume change. Even though background listeners can do things they do them at fixed intervals and not react to changes the user makes to the system.

    It is possible to detect the volume change in iOS but not in Android it seems;

    • Volume Event

    It "might" be possible to create a custom module to solve this, but you would be on your own for that.
