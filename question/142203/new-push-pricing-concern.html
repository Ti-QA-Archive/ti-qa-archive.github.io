New Push Pricing Concern

Hi Everyone.

I received notification of the new pricing put in place for Push notifications and i'm actually quite shocked at the new pricing structure. Let me provide some calculations if you don't mind…

If I have just under 25,000 downloads of my app (not unreasonable) which is based on 0.69p unit price the total (before tax but with Apples 30% taken is) £12075 total. Push with ACS over 4 years to support this App would be £9532 if all instance of the app are active.

Now am I missing something here because, based on my calculations above, its darn expensive… I don't know too much about Urban Airship but it appears they have up to 1 million per month which does not cost anything on a basic service. And even if you do go over 1 million the costs are still manageable.

Thoughts or have I slipped up here in my calculations?

Regards, Steven
