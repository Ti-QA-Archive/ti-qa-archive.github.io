Changing the Titanium Studion login user

Hi,
I've logged in to Titanium Studio with my own username and password.
My boss just purchased one of Titanium's plans under a different account so we could check out the Mobile Web development, so in order to check it out I need to log out from my account in the Titanium Studio environment and login back in with the new account we have.

The problem is that I can't seem to find how to logout / login from Titanium Studio.

Help would be appreciated.

Thanks

  • In the lower right-hand corner of Studio, you should see either your name or email address as a blue link. Click that link and then click OK to exit Studio. The next time you open Studio you should be prompted to log in.
