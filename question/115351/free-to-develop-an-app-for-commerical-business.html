Free to develop an App for Commerical Business

Hello

I work for a company in Australia Sydney, & we wish to develop an app using appcelerator that we intend to sell.

Do we need to purchase a subscription/licence to publish an app because we are developing for commercial reasons?

Any info would be very helpful

  • Appcelerator/Titanium: no, but you might find the pro subscription helpful if you need tech support.

    iOS: for distribution through the AppStore, you'll need their $99/year standard developer program and your app will be subject to their terms & approval.

    iOS: for private/corporate distribution, you'll need an Apple Enterprise program subscription at $299. Terms are a bit more rigorous…you have to have a Dun & Bradstreet number, have a certain number of employees, etc.

    Android: to publish to Google's Android Market you'll need to subscribe to Google's basic developer program for $25/year. To my knowledge that's their only requirement.

    Amazon/AppBrain/GetJar/etc. - you'll have to check those markets yourself. I don't believe you can use any of those third-party markets to sell iOS apps without also having the Apple agreement.

    All prices in USD and programs are not available in every country.
