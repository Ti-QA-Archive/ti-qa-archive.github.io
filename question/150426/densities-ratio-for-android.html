  • There is no such ratio on Android. Higher density doesn't necessarily mean the same proportion only bigger.

    You have to keep those thing in mind when developing for multiple densities on Android. And if the divide doesn't match the exact size of you asset, it will simply scale it to the best of its abilities. Which cans sometimes be good and awful on other occasions.

    My advice to you would be to have a small set of target devices and develop with to in mind. By small set* an e devices that are most likely to be used by your target customers. This may not be perfect, but these are the same issues every Android developers face everyday.

    Welcome the the big leagues ;)
