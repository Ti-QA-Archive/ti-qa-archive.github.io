    I had same problem as you. I went to the /Titanium Studio Workspace/<Your Project>/build/iphone directory and deleted everything in the directory, but not the /build/iphone directory itself. Then in Titanium Studio I ran my project in iOS Simulator and it worked again. Hope this helps you too.
