  • Accepted Answer

    Titanium.Filesystem.applicationDataDirectory is not the path of the image gallery but the path of the application specific data. Usually it's on the SD card. check this previous question:
    http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/132708/tell-me-the-way-to-save-images-in-gallery-android
