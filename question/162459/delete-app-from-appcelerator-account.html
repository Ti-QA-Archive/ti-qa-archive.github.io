    Best you can do is:

    1. visit appclerator.com
    2. Login
    3. Visit 'my apps' from the upper right drop down
    4. Select your app details
    5. App Actions on the right side for the details view.
    6. Deactivate
    7. Check 'hide inactive' on main my apps page.

  • You cannot delete apps, but you can deactivate them. They are then hidden by default. Click an app to view its details. On the right, click App Actions then click Deactivate.
