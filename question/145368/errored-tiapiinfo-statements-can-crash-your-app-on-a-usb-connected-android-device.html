Errored Ti.API.info() statements can crash your app on a USB connected Android device.

I totally love Titanium for iPhone apps… however.. Android is a different beast.

Just spent half a day debugging for why my app did crash on my phone in USB mode… and after 4 hours research i finally found that a:

Ti.API.info(JSON.stringify(imageObject));

where imageObject is a Ti.UI.createView() actually caused the problem crash…

Frustrated looking for a problem in my perfectly working code on iPhone, not working on Android… where it turned out to be a debug comment causing the crash on the USB connected device.

Just another proof that you live and burn every day… :)

After 18 apps on Titanium, I love the tool for smaller apps… but the above snags… miniscule as they are, really makes me tear my hair out…
