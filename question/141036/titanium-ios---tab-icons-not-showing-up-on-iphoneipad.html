  • According to Apple's UI Guidelines, PNG files are recommended. I expect the reason they're not showing up is that JPG format doesn't support opacity. The tab bar icons rely on opacity. You must create a transparent file and make your icon pure white, with opacity. Have a look at the tab bar icons in an app that you know works. The black part is actually transparent. You'll notice that (unless there's some trickery involved) the icons are always white, and blue when selected. This is done by the OS itself.

    Here's a link to Apple's docs: http://developer.apple.com/library/ios/#documentation/userexperience/conceptual/mobilehig/IconsImages/IconsImages.html#//apple_ref/doc/uid/TP40006556-CH14-SW8

    A quick Google image search for Apple tab bar icons gives plenty of results, if you need help: tab bar icon search

    Hope that helps!
