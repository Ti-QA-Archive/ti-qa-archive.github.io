    Well, with Android, you will have to use the Android Debug Monitor (installed in your [android-sdk]&#x2F;tools&#x2F; directory.

    From there, in the LogCat viewer, you can create filters on whatever expression you like. In that particular case it would be on Ti.API.info or Titanium.API.info. (don't really remember the exact label. But you'll find it easily)
