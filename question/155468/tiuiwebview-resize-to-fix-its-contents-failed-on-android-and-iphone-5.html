Ti.UI.WebView resize to fix its contents failed on android and iPhone 5

Hi,

I have used Ti.UI.WebView.size.height and resize the height once real size is rendered. but is not stable in Android and iPhone 5 which the height keep returning the wrong height. work fine on samsung s2, iPhone 4, iPhone 3Gs. failed on samsung s1,iphone 5 ,nexus 4.

any1 have any suggestion on rendered webview height fix by its contents.

thanks

    Hi Kim,

    In your html content set &lt;meta&gt; tag in &lt;head&gt;tag ::

    <meta name=\"viewport\" content=\"width=90%, height=auto, initial-scale=1, maximum-scale=1\">

    This will adjust webview hieght according to content.
