    Some suggestions to try if you haven't already:

    • Test on multiple resolutions, as getBottom() might be returning pixels rather than display-independent pixels
    • Use ($.grayView.rect.x + $.grayView.height) to determine the rendered bottom position of the view instead of the bottom property or accessor
