    • Registering the user to push notifications is not a form they have to fill in. You can do like, on first start, you can show a popup if they want news push notifications. If they click yes, you can register them by following the steps in the link I gave you. If they don't click yes, you can choose yourself as developer if you ask that question again in like a month or something, or everytime you update your app.

      To be clear:
      The user's device needs to be registered and listen to a specific channel, like an event listener to a specific event. If it's listening to that channel, and you push a notification, it shows up for the listening devices.

      Hope this helps.
      Patrick van Vuuren
