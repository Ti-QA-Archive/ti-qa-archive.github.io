Android causing OutOfMemory while loading high quality images from app resources

I am having around 8 large high quality images in app resources (in images folder). Now I am trying to load them & want to show in a single screen. I am using Ti.UI.createImageView in a for loop for 8 images. It is causing OutOfMemory problem in Android & the app crashes inconsistently as the Heap size is increasing drastically.
Can some one please suggest me, what how should I handle this?
Thanks in advance

  • "high quality images"

    Forget it. A bitmap generated from these will quickly exhaust the amount of memory that android will allocate to your app. Reduce them to the best compression/quality you can, your users really wont notice

  • hope you can help but I did the following after you have already created the imageView and had a 50% improvement in memory consumption in IOS: 

    // Convert your imageView into a blob
var blob = imageView.toImage();

// Turn blob into a square thumbnail
blob = blob.imageAsThumbnail(args.hImage);

// Create new imageView for thumbnail
imageView.setImage(blob);
