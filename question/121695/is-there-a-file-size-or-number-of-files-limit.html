  • The limit for submission for apps to the iTunes App Store is not 20MB. It is, in fact, 2GB as Paul McLaren said. The limit that your iOS device can download over a 3G network is 20MB. Beyond that you need a WiFi network to download an app.

    There are multiple apps (Usually games, such as Battleheart and Army of Darkness Defense to name two) that are larger than 20MB (The former being about 60MB, the latter being about 100MB).

    Why Titanium is causing timeout errors, I cannot say. I would suggest posting a bug report. Maybe there is a problem in the source that causes it to timeout after too long? I haven't seen an Appcelerator staff post in here yet, so we cannot really say. But I can say that 20MB is not the limit.
