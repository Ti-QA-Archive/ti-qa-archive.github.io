  • The analytics line in the tiapp.xml enables the Titanium Analytics module.
    Community, Indie devs get one week of analytics (available from your Apps) to see how your apps are being used. As you or a user walks through your app, this analytics module tracks the usage in the background. It has some great potential in seeking how a user navigates through your app, what areas are used the most, or the least, and can help you determine what direction to take with either new products, or new versions. Now this only applies to release builds, not to test/debug builds.

    I am a Google Analytics junkie so I find the potential benefits intriguing.
