Custom iPhone tab icons don't show up

Application type: mobile. Titanium SDK: 1.8.2. Platform & version: iOS 5.x. Device: iOS simulator. Host Operating System: OSX 10.6.8. Titanium Studio: 1.0.9.

My images are the same size and format as the delivered ones. Is there a trick to creating your own tab icons?
