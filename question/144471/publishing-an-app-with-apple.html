Publishing an App with Apple

I am almost ready to publish my app but I am wondering if I should publish the app under my account or ask my client to create their own. This is my first time creating and publishing and I'm not sure what the best practices are legally and professionally. Any advice?

Thanks,

David

    As somebody on the other side of the equation (we have had some apps built for us out of house), I would highly recommend that the client manage the apple developer account.

    Someday, they may wish to bring the maintenance of the app in house, or they may even need to use another contractor. If the app wasn't published under their own account, they would have to move the app into their own account, which is a nasty process (it requires all app users to uninstall the old app and install the app from the new publisher).
