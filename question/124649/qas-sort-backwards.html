    Brad

    Yes, we totally agree that there needs to be better sorting controls in this Q&A. We have it on our work list, but unfortunately other issues are taking priority at the moment.

    To help with your situation, you can actually add plus and minus symbol search modifiers, to narrow down the search results. For example, to find all the questions that include a post by "Kevin Whinnery" and include the terms "tableview" and "json" but not "database", enter:

    +tableview +json -database +"kevin whinnery"

    This would return these results.

    Personally, I prefer the behavior of the plus symbol in our search than the way Google uses it, as it can return more targeted results.

    Anyway, thanks for your patience in the meantime.

    Cheers
