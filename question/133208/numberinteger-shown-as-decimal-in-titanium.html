Number/Integer shown as decimal in titanium

I have a number like var n=0; and I am increasing and decreasing it and setting it to a Labels text , but it appears as 0.0, 1.0…etc not as 0, 1…etc. Why does it become decimal.? Any one can help please.

    try:

    var x = 5.0
var y = parseInt(x); // which gives 5
var z = y.toString();
textField.value = z;

    may be this can help you.
