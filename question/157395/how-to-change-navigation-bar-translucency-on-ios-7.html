  • Ha, what threw me off was that extendEdges value should be array, not number.
    So:

    extendEdges: [Ti.UI.EXTEND_EDGE_ALL]

    NOT:

    extendEdges: Ti.UI.EXTEND_EDGE_ALL

    Translucency should be true by default.
