background services in iOS

Hi I am trying to run a background services in iOS. But while I am using Ti.App.iOS.registerBackgroundService, service is invoked only when I close my app. But I want to invoke the background service while app is opened. Is it possible in iOS. Thanks in advance.

  • The iOS background service will only run when your app is in the background.

    If you use an interval or other timer you can re-use the code in your background service and also when your app is in the foreground.

    You would just need to hook the resume, pause, and close events and fire the correct logic.

    // fired when an app resumes for suspension
Ti.App.addEventListener('resume',function(e){
    //Stop your background Service
    //Remember to unregister your service
    //Start a timer or process that is running in your app.js context
    //This will give you a foreground "service"
});

// fire when the app is suspended
Ti.App.addEventListener('pause',function(e){
    //Stop your foreground "service"
    //Start your iOS Background Service
    //Best to re-register the service
});

// trap app shutdown event
Ti.App.addEventListener('close',function(e){
    //Close all of your services
});
