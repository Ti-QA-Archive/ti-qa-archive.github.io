    • For wich OS are you developing? With Android you have to do some "funny" stuff:

      1.- In your project's root create a new folder called platform and inside this one create other called android (watch out, case sensitive).

      2.- Go to the project's folder in your hard disk device and go to Build/Android and take the AndroidManifest.xml and copy it to your platform/android in the project.

      3.- Edit that manifest and for each activity you see you have to put this:
      android:screenOrientation="portrait"

      Or the orientation you want.

      This is what you can not call "beautiful" but is a solution in android, and that is something…
