    Hi,

    Try This..:

    index.xml

    <Alloy>
    <TabGroup backgroundColor="white" >
        <Tab id="tab1" title="Tab 1" icon="KS_nav_views.png">
            <Window id="win1" title="Tab 1">
                <Require src="ListCurrencies" id="ListCurrencies"/>
            </Window>
        </Tab>
        <!-- Use the Require tag to include external Ti.UI.Tab views -->
    </TabGroup>
</Alloy>

    and ListCurrencies.xml : 

    <Alloy> 
    <View>
        <ScrollView contentWidth= "auto" contentHeight= "auto" showVerticalScrollIndicator= "true">
            <TableView  id="table">
                <SearchBar id="searchbar" height="43" />
            </TableView>
        </ScrollView>

        <ActivityIndicator id="activityIndicator" message="Loading..."/> 
    </View>
</Alloy>
