iPad Popover doesn't get hidden when focus is lost

As I understand it, normally a Popover gets hidden when you tap away from it, such as to the larger window behind.

I have a window with a toolbar at the bottom, set via win.setToolbar(). On that toolbar is a button that opens the Popover.

Everything works fine – the Popover gets hidden when tapping on the window, or on a button in the nav bar – except for anything on the toolbar. If, for instance, I click on the same button again, I get another instance of my Popover: the shadow around the Popover darkens, and I have to tap twice on the window behind in order to dismiss it/them both.

Am I missing something obvious for making the Popover "more modal"?

Note: After doing a little research, it seems I may be running into the same thing as: http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/41101/ipad-popover-dont-close-with-sdk-32–titanium-132

  • My current workaround is to create an empty 1x1 view at the bottom of the window and use that for the Popover's view property:

    
    popoverAnchor = Ti.UI.createView({
        width: 1,
        height: 1,
        left: 0,
        bottom: 0,
        visible: false,
        touchEnabled: false
    });
    win.add(popoverAnchor);

    But I don't feel all that great about doing it this way.
