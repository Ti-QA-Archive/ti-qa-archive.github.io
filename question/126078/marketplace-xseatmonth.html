Marketplace; $X/seat/month

Hi guys, what does this $X/seat/month mean exactly ? Will the user have to pay $X each month to continue the use of the module ? What if the module is already active on App Store or Android market, and the user don't pay the monthly fee, will the module be inactive ?

  • My SWAG (but unofficial answer) is that it will be similar to using Ti+ modules while a paid subscriber (Indie or higher) then letting your subscription lapse.

    For paid subscribers with access to all the Ti+ modules, the rule is that the subscription must be valid at the time the project is built. In other words, projects which are built will continue to run whether from the App Store, Android market, adhoc or sideload install, etc. Even if installed after your subscription lapses – provided the build was created while the subscription was current.

    What you can't do is modify the project to add features/bug fixes and be able to build it again until you renew a subscription.

    YMMV. IANAL.
