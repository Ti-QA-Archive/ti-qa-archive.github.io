  • Your tiapp.xml has 2 <modules> tags. Maybe this is confusing the builder.

    <modules/>
<modules>
    <module version="1.5">ti.storekit</module>
</modules>

    I also suggest you yo add the platform on the module (not really mandatory but makes things more clear :) )

    <module platform="iphone" version="1.5">ti.storekit</module>
