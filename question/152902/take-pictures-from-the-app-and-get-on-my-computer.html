    Hello,

    you can use a service like twitpic or use your own server with a small script with a form that receives the pictures. Check kitchensink about how to upload files to a server. What you can do is:

    1. Take the picture.
    2. Once you get it, call the upload method in kitchensink
    3. Get all them in one account.

    BTW, there is a FTP module in the Marketplace, if that is easier to solve your issue.
