  • One way to do this is to create an image blob with your required sizes using imageAsThumbnail.

    So, in your success event, after taking or choosing a photo:

    
var image_100 = event.media.imageAsThumbnail(100,0);
// the zero is the border width

    Then you can use image_100 as the 'image' parameter on an imageView (or save it locally for later).
