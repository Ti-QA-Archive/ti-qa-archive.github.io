Titanium Studio Login

Can I use Titanium Studio without logging in? I am not always online when working and do not need access outside of my computer to do my development. Is it possible to turn off logging in as a requirement unless you access something that needs internet access?

  • If you have logged in once, you will not need an internet connection, as the details and license are saved on your disk. You will need to login again only if you pressed the "logout" link at the bottom right corner of the app.
