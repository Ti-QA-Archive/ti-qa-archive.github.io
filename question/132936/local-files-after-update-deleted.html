local files after update deleted?

Hi,

I think I got a simple question for all the Titanium geeks out there ;)

I let the user save files himself to the device.
Until now, i used Ti.Filesystem.applicationDataDirectory

But now I saw, that all saved images are gone, after I installed an update of the app on device.

What can I do to hold the files on device until the deletion of the app?
Can I use Ti.Filesystem.applicationSupportDirectory for that?

— asked February 29th 2012 by Sebastian Klaus

  • Based upon the new iOS guidelines, you should be storing your data in:
    Titanium.Filesystem.applicationDataDirectory+&#39;..&#x2F;Library&#x2F;Private%20Documents&#x2F;&#39;

    This data should persist until destroyed by an uninstall event.
