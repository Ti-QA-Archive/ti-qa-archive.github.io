    you should have a look at:
    http://openlayers.org/dev/examples/

    I had a go at the offline map browsing, and all that I came up with was:

    • tiles weigh a lot (consider the zoom level too), and can affect the size of the app.
    • there's no routing involved, it would be just loading tiles and moving the map around. You would need to calculate the route with google and convert it to GPX coordinates and draw it back on the map.

    there are a couple of software that let you choose which part of the world you want to download, so that you get the tiles that OpenLayers will use for the offline browsing.

    Btw, this would be all inside a webview.
