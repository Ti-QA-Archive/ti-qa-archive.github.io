  • Ti.Database calls are all synchronous. This is due somewhat to native platform specifics but moreso due to some historical baggage in the Titanium SDK. In fact, this is one of the reasons why Ti.Database is not supported on MobileWeb – we'd have to either replace the synchronous Ti.Database system with an asyc on the native platforms and likely break any app that uses database calls, or we'd have to force-fit a synchronous solution into the asynchronous mobileweb environment. We are looking at solutions. None are likely to happen until Ti.Next is released.
