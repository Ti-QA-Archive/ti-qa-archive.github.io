How to specify an app is for iOS7 only w/Titanium

When building an iOS app to generate the IPA (from within Titanium Studio) and selecting the SDK level as 7.0, is there a way to specify the app is strictly for iOS 7 (and not iOS 6.0)?

    Add the following to your tiapp.xml:

    <ios>
    <min-ios-ver>7.0</min-ios-ver>
</ios>
