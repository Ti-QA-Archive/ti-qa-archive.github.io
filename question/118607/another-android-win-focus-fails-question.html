Another Android win focus fails question.

[SOLVED]

Open a window in the same tab.
Do something (like choose to take a pic from gallery or camera).
Return to original tab to use that image.
[here is where the focus event fails]

Solution
From the original window add a close event listener that does what you wanted to happen on focus.
