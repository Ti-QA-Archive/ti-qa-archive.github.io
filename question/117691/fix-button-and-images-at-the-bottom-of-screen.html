Fix button and images at the bottom of screen

I want to fix button and images appearance at the bottom of screen, no matter how high or low screen data is. The button and images should need to stick at the bottom always. Anyone can help me regarding this issue ?

  • As long as you are using layout: 'absolute' (which is the default), just name the bottom property but NOT the top property. That is, specify bottom:0 if you want it on the bottom edge of the view/window, or bottom:5 for a 5 pixel margin etc.

    You should also specify the height, even if it is height:'auto'. Just leave off the top property.
