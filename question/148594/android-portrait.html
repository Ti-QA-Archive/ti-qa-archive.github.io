    • btw: here is an android manifest I use that works perfectly

      <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
        <manifest android:versionCode="9" android:versionName="1.0.9">
            <application>
                <activity
                    android:configChanges="keyboardHidden|orientation"
                    android:label="Hickstead"
                    android:name=".HicksteadActivity"
                    android:screenOrientation="portrait" android:theme="@style/Theme.Titanium">
                    <intent-filter>
                        <action android:name="android.intent.action.MAIN"/>
                        <category android:name="android.intent.category.LAUNCHER"/>
                    </intent-filter>
                </activity>
                <activity
                    android:configChanges="keyboardHidden|orientation"
                    android:name="org.appcelerator.titanium.TiActivity" android:screenOrientation="portrait"/>
            </application>
            <property name="ti.android.httpclient.maxbuffersize" type="int">1048575</property>
            <supports-screens android:anyDensity="false"
                android:largeScreens="true" android:normalScreens="true" android:smallScreens="false"/>
        </manifest>
    </android>

      There are two places you need to set portrait, one in the TiActivity and another against your app's activity. In this instance the App is called Hickstead
