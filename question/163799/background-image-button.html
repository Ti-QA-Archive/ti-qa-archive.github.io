  • Hi

    Assuming dataDeps is an empty array, why don't you push each row into that array.

    Then after the loop, use 'setData' to set the rows for your table.

    tableDependentsMedicalCare.setData(dataDeps);

    and as for changing your background image, you should use the method setBackgroundImage()
