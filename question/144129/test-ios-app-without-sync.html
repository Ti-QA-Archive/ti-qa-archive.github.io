  • You can build with Ti (let it sync with iTunes and close iTunes), then open your project with XCode (proj under build folder) and deploy directly to the device. (you have to reload the proj everytime you build).
    This used to work… It's been a while since I don't use this method.
    It's useful when you have a friend's device that you DON'T WANT to sync with your iTunes.
