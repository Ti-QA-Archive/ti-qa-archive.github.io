    Hello Klaas,

    please look to this solution. The view calls getAllPhotos(). Callbacks are onprogress for displaying the download progress and onload for finishing. In case of 'onload' the result is the url. In my view on line 23 this call works. On load the 'returned' image will add to scrollview.

    Hope this helps you.
