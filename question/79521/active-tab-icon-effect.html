    • Thanks for your interest Hal, however, I've learned that this has little to do with Titanium and is just the nature of things with iOS. Icons are not meant to be colored, and it is not possible to remove the blue effect on selected tabs. The alternative approach is to use a custom made TabGroup control, such as one at: http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/46801/giving-to-the-community-custom-tabgroup-class-working-on-android-and-iphone

      I've taken this JS class and adjusted it to my preferences/needs and am pretty happy with the results.
