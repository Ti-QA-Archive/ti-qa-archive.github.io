Isn't it possible to have scrollViews nested on each other in android ?

In my app i have some scrollViews nested on each other and this worked fine on ios but on android always the inner scrollView doesn't scroll (all of them should make a vertical scroll). i saw some native android developpers saying it's not possible here.

  • A quick answer is its possible, but will mess up.

    This is a very commonly asked question. If you have a scrollview inside a scrollview, which takes precedence.. Thus the touch and scroll events get confused.

    And really trying to implement this sort of design means you should revisit your design …
