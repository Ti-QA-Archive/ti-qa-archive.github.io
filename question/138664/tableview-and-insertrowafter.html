Tableview and insertRowAfter

Hey All,

I'm working with tableviews and I want to do an animation when I load the view.

Basically I have 3 rows and want them to come from the left each one on a different time.

I've managed to get it working on the simulator but when I tested on the device the last row sometimes appears sometimes doesnt.

Because I used setimeout to control the animation I reckon this might have something to do it with this bug.

anyway, I'm trying different things to have the effect I want.

So I have the following code:

tableView.appendRow(rowDate);
Ti.API.info("Added 1: " + tableView.data[0].rows.length)
tableView.insertRowAfter(0,rowProject,{animationStyle:Titanium.UI.iPhone.RowAnimationStyle.RIGHT});
Ti.API.info("Added 2: " + tableView.data[0].rows.length)

Basically if I "appendRow" on the first one, the first rows.length will work fine, but after i insertRowAfter i get the followin error

}

I'm trying to understand the reason why it works on the first one after the append but wont after the insertRowAfter.
