    Hi

    Android tabs go on the bottom of iOS and on the top of Android - at least the do according to the respective style guides.

    Android has not shown much love for tabs and has actually moved away from them towards the ActionBar. This should be the native UI element of choice for Android apps (at least 3.0 or higher ones).

    It is to be expected that different platforms have different approaches and even different versions of those platforms (think 2.x and 3.x and higher for Android).

    Older apps on Android tried to mimic the iOS way of thinking, it is more common now to adhere and respect the platform the app is on.
