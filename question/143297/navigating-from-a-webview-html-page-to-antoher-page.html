  • Inside a webview you don't have that much access to the Titanium API. And if the HTML is remote, you have even less access. See Communication Between WebViews and Titanium for the gory details.

    Bottom line is this: assuming your HTML content is local (either resident on the device or dynamically generated by your code), you can fire events back to the app. Your app listens for those events and can then create the new window and open it.

    It is a robust mechanism, but it takes some getting used to. I encourage you to read the documentation I linked to; it contains full examples of exactly this technique.
