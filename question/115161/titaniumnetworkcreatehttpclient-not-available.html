Titanium.Network.createHTTPClient() not available

TypeError: Cannot call method &quot;createHTTPClient&quot; of undefined (app:&#x2F;&#x2F;app.js#67)

Neither Titanium.Network.createHTTPClient() nor Titanium.Network.addEventListener(...) does work.

Do I have to add a library?

  • Accepted Answer

    Presumably you are talking about an Android app. You need to be more specific in your post if you want to get a specific answer. If it is android you need to add

    <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
    <manifest>
    <uses-permission android:name="android.permission.INTERNET"/>
    </manifest>
    </android>

    to your tiapp.xml file.
