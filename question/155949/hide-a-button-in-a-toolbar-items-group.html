hide a button in a toolbar items group

I have the following markup

<Toolbar id="upperBar"  top="0" borderTop="true" borderBottom="false">
            <Items>
            <Button id="navPrev" class="navButton" title="11" style="Ti.UI.iPhone.SystemButtonStyle.BORDERED"/>
            <FlexSpace/>
            <Label id="title"></Label>
            <FlexSpace/>
            <Button id="navNext" class="navButton" title="" style="Ti.UI.iPhone.SystemButtonStyle.BORDERED"/>
            </Items>
        </Toolbar>

I need to keep the <items> for the spacing but I can't call something like

$.navPrev.setVisible("false")

or even

visibile="false"

in the markup. It works fine if I remove the <items> but i'd like to keep that

  • So i actually got around it by doing

    $.navPrev.enabled = false;
$.navPrev.title = "";
$.navPrev.setStyle("Ti.UI.iPhone.SystemButtonStyle.PLAIN");
