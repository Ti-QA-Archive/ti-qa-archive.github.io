  • Accepted Answer

    You can install it, however you won't be able to use it. You need to log into Titanium Studio at least every 72 hours. This is to make sure our internal Analytics servers are in-sync with your account, and unfortunately this is unavoidable.

    You can sign into Titanium Studio once, disconnect from the internet, use it for up to 72 hours, and then you would need to connect to the internet again and log back in, in order to make sure everything works smoothly.

    Hope this helps.
