    The encryption module appears to be for both iOS and Android, just as separate downloads.. I'd suggest you contact the module provider with any questions for your POC..

    In my mind, it would cost me more than $99 dollars to implement a module for iOS & Android, I don't work for this company by the way.. I use modules that are a fixed price (I'd never use a subscription bull dust module) as they are a time saver. I'd only buy if the module provider responds to questions. For example the guy who created the Dropbox module responded very quickly to all my questions and even implemented my suggestion of a missing function from the Dropbox API..
