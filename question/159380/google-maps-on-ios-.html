Hello,
We are a group of students and we would like to use titanium to develop an application for both android and iOS.

For that we need the titanium google maps api, but from what we have seen, only Android is supported for google maps v3, and v1 which works for both ios and android is now deprecated.

–> Is google maps v3 going to be supported for ios any soon ?

Thanks in advance.

    Hi

    Yes Google Maps for Android is supported - there is a paid for Module in the Marketplace that provides native support within your titanium app.

    • Google Maps for iOS

    It costs $30 which is not much considering and there is also a free trial.

    It currently has a 5 start rating from 6 people who seem to love it - I have also heard great things, although i have not yet had a project to use it on.
