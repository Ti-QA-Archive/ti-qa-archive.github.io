  • Titanium SDK 3.2.0 supports Xcode 5.0. Log into developer.apple.com and download Xcode 5.0.

    As of right now, Xcode 5.1 is in beta and Titanium SDK 3.2.0 does not yet support Xcode 5.1 for simulator builds. We will eventually support Xcode 5.1, but in the meantime, stick with Xcode 5.0.

    — answered December 21st 2013 by Chris Barber
