Modules in lib folder in APK

I am compiling a new version of my app which uses around 5 modules. It is a very simple web wrapper, however my APK is 35MB. This is completely unacceptable, even 15MB would be unacceptable even for titanium. The culprit is the lib folder in the apk, each platform has all of the module "libraries" and the x86 alone is 16MB. If i delete these two folders I get 14MB for the app size, but my phone cannot parse the APK correctly after a re-zip. How can I reduce the size of these libraries? Why are they so large?

  • Hi Gabriel Aguirre,

    Thanks for your concern .

    If you need to reduce size, You have to create two separate apks for older (armeabi) and newer (armeabi-v7a) devices. If you delete armeabi , your APK would not work on lower version of Android .

    Please try this steps:

    1. unzip app.apk
2. remove some things (temp/lib/armeabi and temp/lib/x86i)- you may want to remove other also
3. zip it
4. sign it
5. install apk

    Hope this helps.

    Thanks.
