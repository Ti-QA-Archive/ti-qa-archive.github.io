  • I've had a rejection similar to this where the app was stuck on the splash screen. To reproduce it I opened the project in Xcode and ran it in the simulator and saw the same behaviour that the reviewer reported.

    I had to clean the build directory and rebuild the project. Then opened it up in Xcode and verified that actually ran. Once I resubmitted it the app was approved.
