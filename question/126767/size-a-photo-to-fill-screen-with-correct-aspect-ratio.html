Size a photo to fill screen with correct aspect ratio

Titanium 1.7.2
Android

I am trying to do what I think should be something very simple. I have a photo that is widescreen ratio (16x9) that I want to sized properly on a device when it is portrait or landscape and when you change the orientation.

I have a simple app.js I have been using:

var win = Titanium.UI.createWindow({color:'#FFFFFF'});

var img = Ti.UI.createImageView({
    image: Titanium.Filesystem.resourcesDirectory+'images/kids.jpg',
});
win.exitOnClose = true;
win.add(img);

win.open();

What I expect is something like the following:

Portrait Orientation:

Top 1/3 of screen is blank

image displayed in the middle of the screen, left edge to right edge

bottom 1/3 of the screen is blank

Landscape orientation:

image fills the complete screen

I want to be able to switch between portrait and landscape and have the image size properly. I have tried many variations of properties on the imageView such as

left:0,right:0,height:'auto' or

left:0,right:0 or

width:'auto',height:'auto' etc…

Is there a way to get this to work the way I expect?

Thanks,
