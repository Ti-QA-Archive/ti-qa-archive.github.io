    • Ok, a clean rebuild (2nd or 3rd one) it finally loaded on the genymotion emulator and has started on the android arm emulator as well.

      tiapp.xml

      <modules>
        <module platform="android">com.mirasense.scanditsdk</module>
        <module platform="iphone">com.mirasense.scanditsdk</module>
</modules>

      app.js

      var scanditsdk = require("com.mirasense.scanditsdk");
