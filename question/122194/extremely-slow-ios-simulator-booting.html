Extremely slow iOS Simulator Booting?

I was getting an error when trying to compile and run on iPad or iPhone simulator so I reinstalled Titanium Studio and XCode from the Mac AppStore. It does show me my application in iOS simulator but it takes a long time for the simulator to popup.

Here is where it stalls for several minutes. 

[INFO] Titanium SDK version: 1.7.1
[INFO] iPhone Device family: universal
[INFO] iPhone SDK version: 4.3

[DEBUG] executing command: /usr/bin/killall iPhone Simulator

[DEBUG] executing command: mdfind -onlyin /Users/myname/Library/Application Support/iPhone Simulator/4.3 -name ede90dc0-83bf-4441-9b04-4a4904ede796.log

NOTE : This is with a newly created Mobile Project in Titanium Studio. I have made no edits to it at all. Its just two tabs with basically no content.

    I came across this issue as well and essentially figured out a workaround (on my system at least) was to go into Disk Utility and unmount my Bootcamp/Windows partition. mdfind was lightning fast and my iOS simulator booted up nicely after that.

    There's a more detailed explanation about mdfind and bootcamp here if you are interested why this worked in my particular setup.
