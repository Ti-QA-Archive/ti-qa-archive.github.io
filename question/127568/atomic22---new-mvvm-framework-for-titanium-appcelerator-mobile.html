Atomic22 - New MVVM Framework for Titanium Appcelerator Mobile

Since I've decided to stick around and continue on with mobile development with Appcelerator, I want to try to contribute as much as I can.

I had previously started to build a custom framework for my apps (one launched, one overcoming bugs with deploy still). This weekend I have it a major overhaul and converted it to be CommonJS module compliant. I think this is a big move in the right direction.

With that in mind, I have decided to open source this framework. I personally think it's a pretty good base, but I'm biased. Maybe you will think it's garbage, but I hope some people will like what I'm doing with it… maybe even want to contribute extensions to it who knows.

Right now it is very basic, partially by design and partially because it's just built on my needs. I have some "widgets" or controls that I have written that I haven't ported over to CommonJS yet… so I'll be adding that type stuff and whatever else I need as I go. Let me know if you'd like to see something specific in it also.

Please give your feedback positive or negative.

https://github.com/milesplit/Atomic22
