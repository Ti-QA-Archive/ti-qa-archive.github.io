This worked fine on 1.8.0.1, but with 1.8.1 I cannot get rid of the barImage, I even tried setting barImage to null, 'none', on the new Window, nada…

Any help would be greatly appreciated. Thanks!

  • I was still running into this issue with the latest SDKs (OSX Lion, XCode 4, Titanium 2.0.1) but I found a work around. If you use .setBarImage("mybarimg.jpg") on your windows after hitting back it will reset your bar image correctly and your buttons will show again.
