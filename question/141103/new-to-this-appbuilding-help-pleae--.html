  • Hi Tim

    Unfortunately due to restrictions by Apple you can only develop any type of iOS app (iPod, iPhone & iPad) using an Apple computer. This is for many technical reasons including the requirement to have XCODE as part of the compile and distribution process - which ONLY works on OSX.
