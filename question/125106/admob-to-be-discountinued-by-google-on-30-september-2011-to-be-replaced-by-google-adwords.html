Admob to be discountinued by Google on 30 September 2011, to be replaced by Google Adwords

I am currently using ti.admob

When admob goes away, will there be a module or someone working at it for Google Adwords?

— asked September 3rd 2011 by Peter Lum

  • On September 30th, AdMob will stop supporting older WAP mobile web sites, nothing will change for mobile applications for some time to come.

    So in short, for mobile app developers (and everyone using ti.admob), there's nothing to worry about at the moment - nothing will change after September 30th.

    For more information, check this blog post on Google Mobile Ads blog written by Google's product management director for Mobile Ads.
