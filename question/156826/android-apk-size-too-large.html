  • Hello Vijay,

    The first question that comes to mind is How is this a problem for you? Besides the fact that it is an inconvenience, how does it affect your application?

    While it is true that native applications are smaller; but in all fairness, is that really a problem. People today have absolutely no problem downloading games that range in the 20MB these days. So 4MB doesn't look half bad in this context.

    I will attribute the part where you mention wanting to leave the Titanium as a rant. Sometimes, frustration can push people to say things. I had my fair share of frustration with Titanium trust me, but somehow I always found that the benefits outweigh the inconveniences.

  • You already know that v8 is included in the build and that's why the size of the app is bigger than the native one. Rhino is deprecated for the performance reasons being inferior to V8.

    For your last question, nobody holds you here, you can move on and start developing with any other platform or natively if you are not satisfied with the results Titanium gives you.
