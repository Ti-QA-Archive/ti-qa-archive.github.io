  • Hello Matt,

    Unfortunately since scrollableView is a subclass of scrollView, the function you desire will not be possible when using both a scrollView and a scrollableView together. The OS does not know which one of the two to focus on in order to complete the "scroll to top" function. You may only use one of the two at a time to get the status bar "scroll to top" function to work.

    Hope this helps.
