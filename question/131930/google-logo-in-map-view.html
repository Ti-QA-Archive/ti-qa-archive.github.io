    Yes, this is mandatory whether you are using a webview or the native component - the same rule applies even if you were to build your app using XCode. The only answer is as Dan states - you must be covering up the logo with another view or tab bar etc, or you are sizing the MapView so it's larger than the viewable area of the screen.
