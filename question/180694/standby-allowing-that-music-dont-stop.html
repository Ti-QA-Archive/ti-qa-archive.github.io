Standby allowing that music don't stop

Hi everybody.

I have an application for listening to music.
When my app is in standby, i would an function as deezer or itune music. Allowing that music don't stop and be able to play and stop the music with a button.
This is it possible with titanium ?
What is the name of this mode please?

Thanks for your help.

  • Accepted Answer

    Place in the iOS section of your tiapp.xml

    <backgroundModes>
    <mode>audio</mode>
</backgroundModes>

    Reference: https://developer.apple.com/library/ios/documentation/iPhone/Conceptual/iPhoneOSProgrammingGuide/BackgroundExecution/BackgroundExecution.html
