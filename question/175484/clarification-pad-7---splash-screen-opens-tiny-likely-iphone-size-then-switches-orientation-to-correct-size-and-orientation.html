(Clarification) Pad 7 - Splash screen opens tiny (likely iPhone size) then switches orientation to correct size and orientation.

To begin with, the orientation of the app is locked to portrait. The splash screen operates correctly on the iPhone.

Here is a video of the behavior: http:&#x2F;&#x2F;youtu.be&#x2F;jDOTgZLetcE (successfully reproduced around ~25 seconds into the video until the trim processes on youtube)

However, on the iPad the orientation isn't locked at first, it begins in landscape mode in iPhone size, then a moment later switches to the correct portrait or upside down portrait orientation with the correct size.

It's probably important to state that this behavior only occurs in landscape mode; when the iPad is oriented in portrait or upside-down portrait, all is well.

Locking the orientation of the splash screen is the goal if that is achievable.

The orientation lock for the iPad is here in index.js:

//attempt to force orientation
if( Alloy.isTablet ){
    $.index.orientationModes = [ Ti.UI.PORTRAIT, Ti.UI.UPSIDE_PORTRAIT ];
}else{
    //lock in portrait so a phone may not get an upside down phone call
    $.index.orientationModes = [ Ti.UI.PORTRAIT ];
}

and here for iphone in tiapp.xml:

<iphone>
        <orientations device="iphone">
            <orientation>Ti.UI.PORTRAIT</orientation>
        </orientations>
        <orientations device="ipad">
            <orientation>Ti.UI.PORTRAIT</orientation>
            <orientation>Ti.UI.UPSIDE_PORTRAIT</orientation>
        </orientations>
        <statusbar-hidden>true</statusbar-hidden>
        <fullscreen>true</fullscreen>
    </iphone>
