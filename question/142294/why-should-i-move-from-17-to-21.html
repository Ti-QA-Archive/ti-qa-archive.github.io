Why should i move from 1.7 to 2.1?

In 2.0 the app size was a big down , no matter if both JS engines , or just Rhino. Is this fixed?

How much the stability will be improved if I move?

How much time I will need to convert the new UI system , currently i have ~2000 lines of code?

  • All of these questions depend completely on your app.

    • V8 is smaller than Rhino, but 2000 lines of code spread over multiple files with assets will still determine the app size. Chances are, moving to 2.1 will not change the size of your app a great deal.
    • The stability is much improved but only if your app is properly structured and well written.
    • Depending on how you've done your layout, this could be a simple update that doesn't take long, or a pain in the ass. There is no real way to tell until you try it.
