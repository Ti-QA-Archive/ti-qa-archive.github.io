Removing project from Titanium Studio

I have a bunch of projects I created that I'm not working on anymore, is there a way to remove them? I've imported projects that I'm not using anymore and I'd like to get them out of Studio, how do I do this? Thanks…

    In Project Explorer, Right Click select Delete – you will have the option to delete the files from the disk or leave them and just remove the project from Studio.
