    Hi

    It tells you this in the documentation;

    • totalItemCount

    Total number of rows in the view.

    • visibleItemCount

    Number of rows currently visible in the view.

    If your table has 100 rows, but only 6 can be seen at any one time then the fields will be as follows (at the current position);

    • totalItemCount = 100
    • visibleItemCount = 6

    Simple.

    If your rows have varying heights then the currently visible rows will be returned.
