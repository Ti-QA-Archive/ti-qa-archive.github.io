Titanium community on StackExchange

I just created a proposal for a Titanium community on StackExchange.

I'm tired of the bad questions, angry users and negative attitude. I want a place where good questions and answers are rewarded. Where the search is not useless. Where we can start from scratch, with a solid QA system. I want an unofficial alternative to this QA. With stricter rules on quality and community moderators that control the mess.

Help me achieve this by voting on my proposal.
