Emulator Issues - Android Tablet

It seems like whenever I try to run my app on the Android emulator for a tablet (3.x or 4.x) things just dont work. No errors, but the app doesn't even seem to install. I think it is related to my machine being slow as the emulator is painfully slow. Any advice for just getting the app on the emulator? (btw, same app works fine on my android phone, iphone, ipad).

  • Hello,

    besides using a device, my advice it's to close as much stuff as you can. Then, press the compile button to the tablet, wait until the emulator looks like working fine. Then don't close the emulator and press the build button again. Should work.

    The same happened to me when using a netbook and 1 GB of RAM.

    Best,

    Mauro

    Ps. This only works if you have fastdev enabled, that should be the default in 1.8.x
