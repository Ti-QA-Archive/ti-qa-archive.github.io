Titanium Android Background Service Restarts

I need a service to run forever, even after the application is closed. I implemented the following tutorial:

  • Hello,

    Android Details

    As you might recall, a Titanium app is governed by the native behavior of the apps in the platform. In the case of Android, the services will be killed if your application is actually killed (check this doc, that describes exactly the swipe of the app as killing the app and the services).

    Alternative solution

    If the user decides to actually kill your app, and you want to continue doing "something" in the back, you can use the Alarm Manager module, that will allow you to run a task or so every 5 minutes, even if your app is not running anymore (neither the UI nor the services).

    Full Solution

    A full working example of Alarm Manager Module, can be found here so you can adapt it to your needs.
