What does api.appcelerator.net do?

I have noticed that when I attempt a simple http request - the app (in the simulator) also tries to connect by https to api.appcelerator.net (which resolves to an amazonaws ec2 server).

Why would this be needed?

What data is being passed there?

  • This is the back end for our analytics service, which collects data for your app for you to crunch later. You can disable this by switching

    <analytics>false</analytics>

    in tiapp.xml.
