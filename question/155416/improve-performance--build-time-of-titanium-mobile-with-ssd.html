Improve performance / build time of Titanium Mobile with SSD?

Hi,

I know this topic has been discussed 2 or 3 times in the last 2 years (used the search) but there were only comparisons between newer (with SSD) and older MacBooks (without SSD).

Does anyone have a direct comparison HDD vs SSD?

Compiling stuff it's usually a filesystem intensive task. Therefore, I see that even my i5 it's A LOT FASTER than my i7 because of the SSD, even using less RAM.
