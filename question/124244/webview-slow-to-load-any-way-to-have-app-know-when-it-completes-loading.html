Webview slow to load, any way to have app know when it completes loading?

I have a series of webviews on my scrollview, but they are slow to load and so you see the data inside of them propagate in real time (instead of all being ready) when you enter a new window.

I was thinking of putting up a LOADING splash page to cover up the content loading in the background and then have the splash page fade away once the webviews have all completed.

The question is, is there any way to trigger an event when the webviews have completed their internal loading ?

thanks!

    webView.addEventListener('load', function(e) {
    // your post-load code
});
