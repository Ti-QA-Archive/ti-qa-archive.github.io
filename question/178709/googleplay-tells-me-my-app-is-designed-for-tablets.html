GooglePlay tells me my app is designed for tablets

Hello! I'm now uploading an android application to the Google Play store, however when uploading screenshots I'm told to upload 7-inch and 10-inch tablet screenshots because the APK is designed for tablets and not for phones. Where are the settings to change this? as the app is designed for phones, I cannot find anything in tiapp to specify phone vs tablet.

Thanks!

  • Usually specified using the following

    <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
    <manifest>
    ...
        <supports-screens
            android:anyDensity="true"
            android:largeScreens="true"
            android:normalScreens="false"
            android:smallScreens="false"
            android:xlargeScreens="true"/>
    </manifest>
</android>
