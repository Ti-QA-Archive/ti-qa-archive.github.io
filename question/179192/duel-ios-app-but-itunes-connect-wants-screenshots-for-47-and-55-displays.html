Having prepared a dual iOS app - with support for the iPhone and iPad - and getting it validated in Xcode on it's way to iTunes Connect I am now being blocked from submitting in iTunes Connect by the message:

You must provide a screenshot for 5.5-inch Retina display, because your app binary supports 5.5-inch Retina displays.
