New App on the Stores - iSaxaboom

My new app is available on both the iTunes App Store and Android Marketplace! Big thanks to the Appcelerator Q&A board for their help with the Ti.Media.Sound module, it was a big help! :)

iSaxaboom is a digital replica of the Sax-a-boom toy made famous by Tenacious D during their concerts, played by Jack Black and Kyle Gass. My brother recently got his hands on a Sax-a-boom (Not sold anymore; Collectors item) and we thought why not make an app using the sounds for those people who can't buy their own sax-a-boom but wouldn't mind having one to play!
