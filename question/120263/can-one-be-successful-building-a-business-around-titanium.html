Can one be successful building a business around Titanium

Hi all,

I wanted to put out the question hopefully for a good discussion:

Are there people or businesses out there that have built a thriving business around the Titanium platforms?

or put another way

Can one be successful solely riding the Titanium train?

I'd love to here success stories from the group.

  • It depends on the nature of your business. A few things Titanium still can't deliver on its own: magazine readers, fast tableviews, complex games. With that said, for pretty much anything else you're good to go. Watch out for the "cross-platform" part, however, because a lot of features don't translate very well from iOS to Android and viceversa.

  • The other answers are all correct, but I must interject, that to those saying that games, magazine reader, and such… can not be made, this is not 100% true, but also not 100% false.

    Titanium allows for modules to be added to the system to extend it, there are already open source modules for flipbook style magazine readers, and you could, and I am sure someone will make a module to help in the creation of games.

    That is the beauty of Titanium, it can forever be expanded to do new things, and add new functionality.
