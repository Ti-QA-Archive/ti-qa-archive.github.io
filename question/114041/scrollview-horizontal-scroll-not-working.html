ScrollView horizontal scroll not working.

Hello. I have a scrollView in my app (for Android), but I can't scroll horizontally. (Vertical scrolling works fine though.)

I have tried adding a View, and toying around with the input parameters of createScrollView.

The code can be find here: http://pastie.org/1581802

Can someone explain how to get it to scroll horizontally?

Thanks in advance.

    Hi,

    Try giving it a 'width' property as well as the contentWidth property. (The 'width' is the kind of 'window' area you scroll through, with the contentWidth being the width of the full content you are scrolling, if that makes sense)

    You could also try adding the scrollType:'horizontal' property.

    Also, if you added the layout:'horizontal' property too, you could then add your labels with just 'left' properties and they will 'stack' to the right automatically (with the 'left' value as the space between them), without you having to calculate their exact position.

    hope this helps.
    K
