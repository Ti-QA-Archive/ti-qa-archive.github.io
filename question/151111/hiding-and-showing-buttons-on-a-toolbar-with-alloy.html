Hiding and Showing Buttons on a Toolbar with Alloy

Is there a way to hide and show buttons on a toolbar with Alloy? I have a Toolbar as such which is placed on index.xml, under a ScrollableView:

<Toolbar layout='horizontal'>
  <Button id='b1'>B1</Button>
  <Button id='b2'>B2</Button>
</Toolbar>

What I would ideally like to do is only initially display b1 at first. As the user performs an action on one of my views within the ScrollableView, the view in question flips, and button b1 will hide, and B2 (which is actually a Cancel button) will show. onClick of b2, the view will flip back, and B2 will hide, and b1 will show again.

I have tried to use the visible property of the button to no success. Any other ideas?
