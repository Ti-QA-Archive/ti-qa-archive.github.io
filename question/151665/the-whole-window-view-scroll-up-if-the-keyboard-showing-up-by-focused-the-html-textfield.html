The whole window view scroll up if the keyboard showing up by focused the HTML-Textfield

Hi Dudes,

I've build a webview in a window. also i add one button in the window. In the webview i've build htmlcode with some checkboxes and a textfield.

Any Idea/Solution?

    • Hey ,

      you can also put the Button and Webview in the ScrollView.
      But WebViews do not play well inside of other scrollable components. This is a limitation of the underlying native components. WebViews are pretty greedy when it comes to touch interactions and this will often steal those events from any containing views that have their own touch handlers. If you must include WebViews inside other scrollable components, your best bet will be to disable touch events on your WebView by setting its touchEnabled property to false
