  • Now I'm not associated with Ti / AppC in anyway. So I don't know their exact revenue sources, etc.

    But from looking around the site, articles, etc these are / probably are some of their revenue sources…

    • Investors
    • Classes
    • Marketplace
    • Their help services (they don't have pricing but I'd say it's safe to assume that any company working with Ti somewhat regularly would probably purchase a package, and since it's not exactly public, I'm sure it's not the cheapest).

    As far as how much revenue they're bringing in? Who knows…but they've been around 4 years and it's only gotten better and grown into what it is now. A lot of the industry (at least those who are regularly on blogs, etc) knows about Ti and it's a great tool…so I doubt it will be going away anytime soon. A merge with a company? Maybe, but that'd (hopefully) only bring more good stuff and less bad lol.

  • If you start using Appcelerator for a while and begin to see any kind of success in the app store, you will probably sign up for a support plan. I see that they've changed their support options as of the 2.0 update. The 'indie' option used to be priced at $49 per month. I've inquired to see what the pricing is now. It seems to be dependent upon how big of an operation you are running.
