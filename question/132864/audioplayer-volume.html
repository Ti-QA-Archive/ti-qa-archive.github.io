AudioPlayer Volume

Hello,

I have created a nice audio player. Generally, everything works nice. However, whenever I build with the Target being my iPhone, there is no voice. I have added debugging so I can see that the iPhone is indeed receiving and buffering the audio file, but there is no sound.

Whenever I build on the iPhone simulator, it works very well with sound.

What am I doing wrong? Has anyone had this experience before?

As I mentioned, I created volume buttons even to ensure that that this wasn't an obstacle.

Thanks in advance for any support you can provide.

  • Guys,

    I found out what the problem was!

    My iPhone was on mute. THere's this button on the side of the 4S that stops the sound. I had to push it up.

    What's weired is that the Apple Audio Player still played when I had the mute button down.. so I was debugging like crazy thinking that my app wasn't working.
