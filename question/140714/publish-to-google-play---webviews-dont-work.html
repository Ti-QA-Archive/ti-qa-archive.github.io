Publish to Google Play - webViews dont work?

This is strange.. I can deploy to an Android device with any Ti SDK version 2.0.1 GA, 2.0.2Ga, 2.1.0, 2.1.1, and everything works as expected and tested.

When I now publish the app for Google Play through Studio.. I find that none of the events that fire against my webView work anymore.. I've tried building against all the different Google API's down to 3.0 and the behaviour is the same. I've noticed if I go above API12 then my app is full sized but all the buttons are squeezed into a small area at the top so most are unresponsive.

Here's my Tiapp.xml <android /> declaration:

 <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
        <tool-api-level>15</tool-api-level>
        <uses-sdk android:minSdkVersion="8"/>
        <manifest android:installLocation="preferExternal"
            android:versionCode="18" android:versionName="1.28"/>
    </android>

Anyone seen this webView behaviour?

On the compatibility matrix Appcelerator Document: http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/2.1/index.html#!/guide/Titanium_Compatibility_Matrix it says:

Due to this Android bug, webViews created on a virtual device running Android 2.3.x cause applications to behave erratically and crash. Thus, if developing on an emulator, it is recommended to use one of the other supported versions (Android 2.2, for instance). In contrast, applications that include webViews run as expected on (physical) devices installed with Android 2.3.2+. Note that Google has pronounced 2.3.1 obsolete and, as such, it is not supported.

I don't think I am falling foul of this..

Also yes I do need to use a webView as Android cannot produce the required functionality natively.

Thanks for any help!!
