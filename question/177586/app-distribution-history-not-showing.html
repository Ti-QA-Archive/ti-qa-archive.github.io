App Distribution History not showing?

I just updated to 3.3.0 and now my distribution history doesn't show when I go through the publish menu. Does anyone know how to get it back?

http://i.imgur.com/3VuiNWa.png

There used to be a list of recent distributions that you could quickly use to make a build.

  • They have removed the recent list, but if you click on "Configurations…" a new window will appear with the list still there - just click "Package" and you are on your way. It means an extra click, but hey ho!
