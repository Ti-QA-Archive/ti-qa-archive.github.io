layout: vertical with animated close like jQuery's slideUp()

<View layout="vertical">
    <View />
    <View />
    <View id="closeMe" />
    <View />
    <View />
</View>

I would like to close view $.closeMe above with an animation similar to jQuery's slideUp(). The expected behavior is $.closeMe's height is reduced in a loop until it's invisible.

The problem is,

animation = Ti.UI.createAnimation();
animation.height = 50
animation.duration = 1000
$.closeMe.animate animation

Will cause all other vertical view items to instantly reflow and take the place of closeMe as if it were hidden instantly. $.closeMe will continue animating on top of the others, out of general layout.

Is there a different way to approach this?
