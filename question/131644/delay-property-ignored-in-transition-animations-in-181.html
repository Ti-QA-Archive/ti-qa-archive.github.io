delay property ignored in transition animations in 1.8.1

I'm using animations to transition between views and in writing a test program about what appears to be a bug in transitions, I noticed that the delay property was not having an effect on the transition animation - ran the same test program under 1.7.5 and 1.8.0.1 and the delay parameter works.

In this snippet, the newView is transitioning in to replace the oldView. On 1.8.1 this happens immediately. Also tested this by adding delays to the transitions examples in Kitchen Sink - same result - no delay for transitions under 1.8.1

    oldView.animate ({
        view : newView,
        transition : Ti.UI.iPhone.AnimationStyle.FLIP_FROM_LEFT,
        delay : 5000
    }, completion);
