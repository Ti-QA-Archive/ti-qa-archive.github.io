  • Accepted Answer

    Hi

    Apple have not yet released iOS 6.1 it is still in beta, the fourth beta for 6.1 in fact. You should be testing your apps against this version before it is released so you can head of any potential issues.

    Whilst 6.1 is not a major update there are changes and as such depending on what features your app supports it may need minor changes, these may be none or a handful - again depends on your app which is why you should be testing now.

    Appcelerator does not officially support it yet, so if you find a problem there may not be an immediate fix if the change required is for Appcelerator to do.

    Start testing and start testing now.
