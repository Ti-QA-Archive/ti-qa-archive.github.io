Best Practices to store credit card information on the device

I want to store the credit card information provided by the customer in the local database on the device. Can anyone share best practices on storing this information. Are there any PCI compliance issues that I need to consider. I am thinking of using Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) to encrypt the data and stored the encrypted data into the loacl databse.

  • There are some serious PCI issues here. Even if you had a fully PCI compliant database on the device, you'd may have a hard time getting that one past Apple. Personally, I think its just bad juju to store cc info locally anywhere. Thats why debit and credit card machines at your local merchants connect to the other services to handle this.

    That being said, the answer to your question is less 'mobile' or Titanium specific, you should head over and see what the Standards Council says about PCI compliance in their hardware and software section: https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org/
