Android webview - standard HTML links do not open

Hi, I have a very basic webview which loads an external webpage. The external webpage is valid compliant HTML.

var webview = Titanium.UI.createWebView({url:'http://anywebsite.com'});
var window = Titanium.UI.createWindow();
window.add(webview);
window.open({modal:true});

Works fine on iOS but on Android the majority of the links do not open. All the links open fine in the Android browser so it's not a limitation of the device.

  1. Links to MP3 files don't open/download

  2. Links to PDF files don't open/download

  3. <a href links with _blank properties don't open

Any plans to make the Android webview functionality consistent with the iOS webview functionality (if that's even possible) or does anyone know of any workarounds in the mean time?

Many Thanks

  • The limitations of the platform specific webviews are imposed on them by Apple or Google, not Titanium. That said, you can view PDFs in Android by utilizing Android Intents. This blog post shows you exactly how to do this with Titanium: http://developer.appcelerator.com/blog/2011/09/sharing-project-assets-with-android-intents.html
