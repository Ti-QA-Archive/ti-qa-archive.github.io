  • Hi Ursin,

    It will not be possible to improve on the performance significantly if you decide to use PDFs. Since PDFs are vectors (infinite resolution), it will cause hiccups in rendering.

    You will really want to consider building a native view for the content, that way, you will have access to much more optimization options.

    Cheers
