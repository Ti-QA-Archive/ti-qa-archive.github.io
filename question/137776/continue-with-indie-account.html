  • With the Indie model, you have a flat fee for a year regardless of the number of applications. You also may now use the free level of ACS and analytics. All new pricing plans are on a per application basis, though you get a higher level of ACS and analytics included and a higher level of support.

    Since they still have not posted those prices publicly, I am assuming they don't want them in a thread here either. I believe you have to request those from the Sales department. I suggest you do that so you can make an informed decision. But see also this and this thread, including all comments.

    FWIW, my personal decision was to renew my Indie status.
