    Place your form in a scrollview.

    onFocus, bring your txfield up above the keyboards top (change the values to match your needs):

    txtComments.addEventListener('focus',function(){
        scrollview.scrollTo(0, 10);
}

    onBlur of your txtfield, return the scrollview to the top.

    txtComments.addEventListener('blur',function(){
        scrollview.scrollTo(0, 0);
}
