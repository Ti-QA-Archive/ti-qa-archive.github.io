Hi there,

I'm using the latest versions of TiStudio and SDK and my app works fine on iPhone 3GS and iPhone 4 (got approved and released in 6 days - Thanks Appcelerator!)

However, the final release to the AppStore for iPhone 3G has screwed the background images for the buttons - screen shot:

    I would assume that it's showing an iPhone 4 sized image. Did you not have two images for the button background?

    Remember you have to have one image for iPhone 3g/3gs (named something like btn-details.png) and one that is twice the size for iPhone 4 (named btn-details@2x.png – the @2x part being the operative part here).
