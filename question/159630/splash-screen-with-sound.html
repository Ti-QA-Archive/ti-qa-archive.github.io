splash screen with sound

hi i have a doubt in my project . that is i kept the sound to my app it was working fine , but that sound has to work when app started, the splash screen will come then only it has to work , when other screen will come at that time that should not work.can any one guide me please

  • Hi

    Short answer - no.

    You cannot have sound automatically play whilst the app is in the "app loading" state.

    You can start sound once the app has completed the normal "app loading" state, but this should NOT stop the user from using the app.

    Anything you do to slow down the user is likely to cause the user to not use your app.
