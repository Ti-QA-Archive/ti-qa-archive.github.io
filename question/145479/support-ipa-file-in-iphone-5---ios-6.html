  • It works for me, but here's what I do.

    • I do the all the builds in titanium
    • I create the ipa from Xcode by opening xcode project from build directory
    • After opening xcode project I do Product - Archive
    • Then I go to Archives and click Distribute (select AD hock)
    • Save .ipa file and drop it to Testflight

    I have tested with iphone 4, 4s, and 5. (ios 6) They all work fine.

    Another potential issue that you didn't add your iphone device into developer.apple.com.
