Where to get TI+Plus Modules

http://developer.appcelerator.com/titanium-plus says you can get the modules free for evaluation, but wondering where/how to get them? I am a one man show and want to verify if I can make something work, specifically the Commerce Modules.

If anyone knows how I can get the Commerce Modules for evaluation, that would be great.

    After clicking the link : http://developer.appcelerator.com/titanium-plus

    See the menu on the left? Click 'Commerce' on the left menu : https://developer.appcelerator.com/titanium-plus/commerce

    Then click the module you want. Then you can download using the buttons on the right (after checking the checkbox)
