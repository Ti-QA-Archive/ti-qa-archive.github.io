    Hi Wombat,

    you can't modify the look and feel of default date picker . Even u can't do anything about the background color of its columns and rows in DATE_PICKER. Alternative is to create your custom date picker. So you can't have date picker's custom UI instead you have to make a custom date picker. But i don't think it is a good option to use custom date picker as you need sufficient effort and also it is going to effect the performance of the application.
