Multiple WebViews: Do they share caches, cookies and etc?

If I have 3 - 5 WebViews in a single app, and each WebView would many times load the same remote files (javascripts, images, stylesheets), will each WebView have to reload the remote files anew every time? Or, is the cache somehow shared by the WebViews in a single app?

  • Static resources cache is shared between webviews. It behaves very similar to iframes in the typical web page. On the side note, I wouldn't recommend creating 3 separate webviews, since it is very expensive operation and will make your app slow.
