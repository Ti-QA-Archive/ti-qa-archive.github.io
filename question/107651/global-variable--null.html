  • Accepted Answer

    Brieuc

    It is not recommended to use the Titanium namespace for your own data, except in the ways that are explicitly stated in the docs, as you may get unexpected and inconsistent results.

    For sub-contexts, use the Ti.UI.currentWindow property.

    Otherwise, use custom events or Application Properties.

    Hope this helps
