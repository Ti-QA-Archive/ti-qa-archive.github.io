iD-Weather approved by Apple!

Hi all!

Today Apple approved iD-Weather for iPad! The app is made with Appcelerator's Titanium SDK.
Almost all app's of iD-Service are made with Titanium SDK and they are all easy approved by Apple!

iD-Weather is a weather station app, which shows you the current weather condition of your location and the forecast for your location. By moving the little map and pinpoint the location, you get the conditions and forecast of the pointed location! It is also posible to change from metric to british system. The app is using only 1 screen!!

You can download it for free: http://rs.gs/idweather
