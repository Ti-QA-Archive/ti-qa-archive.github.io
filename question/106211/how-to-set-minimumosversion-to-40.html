  • There's an open issue about this here

    Their recommended fix is to go into the /Library/Titanium/mobilesdk/<your_sdk_version>/iphone/Info.plist and add the MinimiumOSVersion there. I have tried this and it does appear to work. I haven't submitted an app yet to ensure it works but now the Info.plist has the property in the generated Info.plist files.
