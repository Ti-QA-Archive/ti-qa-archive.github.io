Is there a way to find out the number of objects that meet the criteria without fetching the whole data?

Hi,

I am trying to figure out how to just get the number of people who check-in to a particular place without
fetching the whole data array. Particularly, if I have 10,000 checkins to the Grand Palace, Bangkok, is
there a way to just get the number '10,000' given the id of the Grand Palance in the where clause? At the moment, I am fetching everything and just do e.checkins.length to get such a number? (<– this won't work either since the ACS has a fetching limit of 100 per request :-( ).

Thanks so much for any suggestions.

  • I'm assume you're trying to query an SQL database…

    You can use an SQL statement with something like :

    SELECT Count(id) FROM checkins WHERE checkin_id = ...(whatever the place id is)...

    naturally, this will depend on the actual field names in your database.
