  • Ok Fixed this interesting issue, and have checked it now works with the latest build. On reviewing again the build.log files I investigated the line:

    iPhone Developer: Ian Tearle (N75FDMTW6S): ambiguous (matches "iPhone Developer: Ian Tearle (N75FDMTW6S)" in /Users/ian/Library/Keychains/Microsoft_Intermediate_Certificates and "iPhone Developer: Ian Tearle (N75FDMTW6S)" in /Users/ian/Library/Keychains/login.keychain)

    It seemed strange to me why anything to do with Apple development would be in anything to do with Microsoft so I deleted the Microsoft_Intermediate_Certificates from the Keychains folder ~/Library/Keychains/ and it all now works. A little confused why anything would get mixed up in the keychains like that?!?!

  • On an error that looked very similar, I realized that I could fix it by shortening the <description> tag contents in my tiapp.xml file. Hope that may help someone!
