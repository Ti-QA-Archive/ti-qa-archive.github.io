Declaring android activities inside tiApp.xmo

Hi ALL,

I am newbie in titanium studio.I am developing a mediaplayer application with lot more functionalities embedded in it.I have 8 screen in my application.I want to declare android activities inside the tiApp.xml file (like what we do in Androidmanifest file) and also want to create js file corresponding to the activity declared in the tiApp.xml.
But I am not getting proper guidance how to do it.can anyone guide me how to do it.

  • Activities are just windows, you don't declare them in the tiapp.xml file. However you can create intents to launch activities. In which case you would declare them in the tiapp.xml file. The base idea behind this can be seen in the docs for intents and activities: http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/2.1/index.html#!/api/Titanium.Android.Intent
