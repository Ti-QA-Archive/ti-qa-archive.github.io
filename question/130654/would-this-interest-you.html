Would this interest you...?

Hi Guys

Moderators please remove this if you feel it's not appropriate but the only people who can answer my question are the people in here.

Is it worth me spending hundreds of hours on the following…?

Like everybody here I love titanium and believe Appcelerator have done a great job in creating such an excellent development platform for us to use. Many great apps have been developed using titanium but I can't help but feel that many of them lack in the graphics department and need a little extra sparkle, after all we all know good looking apps attract more downloads.

Currently there are a few design templates in the marketplace but they where not designed and developed with titanium in mind, which makes them more difficult to integrate into your app.

My idea is to create a large range of design templates and design elements complete with video tutorials and code examples so you can more easily integrate them into your titanium apps.

The Template packages would include all the main design components you would need in an app including, icons, splash screens, buttons, tabs, badges, backgrounds and more, plus the psd photoshop and illustrator files so you can alter colours and anything else.

Initially I will create templates for the iPhone, followed by Android and the iPad.

The graphics will be of the highest quality and will cover many industry categories.

I'm not sure of the price yet but each Template package will probably cost between $59 to $99 depending on how much I decide to put in each package.

Please let me know what your think.

I have created a mailing list so I can send updates to people if I decide to go ahead, based on the interest shown here.

You can add yourself to the list here -> http://eepurl.com/irc1g

Thank you for reading…
