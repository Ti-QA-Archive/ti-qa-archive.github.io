How to remove scrollTo animation?

I want to scroll to a specific point in a scrollView but without the animation.
I tried to change contentOffset instead of scrollTo but it also animates to that point. Anyone an idea how I can change this?

  • You can use the setContentOffset instead of the property, in this method you can specify animation.
    _scroll.setContentOffset({x:1000,y:0},{animated:false});
