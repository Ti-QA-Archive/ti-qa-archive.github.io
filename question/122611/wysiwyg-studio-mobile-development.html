  • Yes, it's been discussed as something they're looking
    into, but nothing definite and no timeframe I'm aware of.
    One thing that could help in some respects, if you are
    creating a complex UI with many objects, is to mock-up
    something in IB and then get the measurements to plug into
    Ti (e.g., heights, widths, positioning, etc.)

  • I've never tried it, but there's http://blog.frogonmobile.com/apps/xib2js/ you could try (xib to Titanium JS translator).

    Another workflow to try would be to use a vector drawing tool (Inkscape, Illustrator, Freehand, etc.) to draw your UI. You can get measurements with its drawing tools and export UI-specific graphics.

    As for adding a GUI builder, I know it's been talked about a lot. I'm not "in the know" with any plans for when or if something would be released. Sorry, I can't give details I don't have.
