  • Unfortunately, there's no way to provide links for twitter (@usernames and #hashtags) with labels. You'd need to do that in a small webview where you could then build/replace links to thing with custom events back to your app.

    I would (as I have) do this with a separate 'tweet view' window, viewable after clicking on a table row (with the label) list of tweets.
