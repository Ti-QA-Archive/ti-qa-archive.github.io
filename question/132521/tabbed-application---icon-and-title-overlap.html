tabbed application - icon and title overlap

Platform: Android 2.2
Device: htc wildfire S

I have created a tabbed group using:

var tabGroup = Titanium.UI.createTabGroup();

var tab1 = Titanium.UI.createTab({  
    icon:'icons/home.png',
    title:'Home',
    window:win1
});

When I try the application on the Samsung Galaxy Tab P-1000 it looks nicely, but when I run the application on the HTC phone (with lower resolution compared to Galaxy), icons and title overlap (home.png, and Home title from the example above), and text is poorly visible.
Is there a way to place the title below the icon or to better highlight the title text?

Thanks
