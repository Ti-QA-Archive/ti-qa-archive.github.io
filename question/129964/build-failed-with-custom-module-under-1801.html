    • actually, i was not suggesting to remove armv7, i was suggesting to add armv6.

      there is another trick which always worked for me (i had your problem on and off with the latest releases all the time):

      you can remove armv6 from the titanium builder.py, and, at least for me, this problem was going away 100% of the time.

      so:

      – in the sdk of your choice, open file …./mobilesdk/osx/<sdk version>/iphone/builder.py;

      – find the following line:

      extra_args = ["VALID_ARCHS=armv6 armv7 i386"]

      for 1.8.0.1.RC3 it is the line #1142;

      – remove armv6 from there:

      extra_args = ["VALID_ARCHS=armv7 i386"]

      – save and try to build your app now.

      good luck!
