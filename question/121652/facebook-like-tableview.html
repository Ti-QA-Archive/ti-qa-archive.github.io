    Well, there two sides to your question.

    First, there is the custom TableViewRow woth a blue Label on the left and another one on the right. There is no such basic control for this. But you can very easily replicate this by making a custom row look&feel yourself.

    Here's a link that explains how to achieve this

    As for the more rounded look of the table, simply set the style to Titanium.UI.iPhone.TableViewStyle.GROUPED then creating the TableView.

    Please note that this style is only available on iOS devices.
