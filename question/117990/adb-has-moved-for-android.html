    Hi Dan

    The workaround is explained in the Getting Started guide, under Preparing for Android development and the Installing on Windows 7 guide under Workaround for a missing adb.

    If you are unable to create symbolic links on your system, you can simply copy adb.exe and AdbWinApi.dll from platform-tools into the tools directory.

    Hope this helps
