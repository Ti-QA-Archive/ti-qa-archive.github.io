myscrollView.addChildAt(child, pos)

myscrollView.children[pos] = child ?

  • You have to use zIndex. In a scrollView, something with a zIndex of 1 will be placed below something with a zIndex of 0 regardless of when they were added to the view. You can also dynamically change the zIndexes of child views in a scrollView to change their order.
