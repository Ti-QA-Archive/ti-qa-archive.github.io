  • If you want to drag only one pin, you can just use a trick.
    add an imageview with the pin as a child of window( this is important) that hold the map too and at the center position of the map.
    now you can move the map behind the pin and the location of the map is the location of the pin.
    I know that this is not the same of dragging the pin but can be work.
