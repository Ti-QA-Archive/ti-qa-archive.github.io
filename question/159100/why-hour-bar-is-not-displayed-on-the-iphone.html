    iOS 7 has a different way of displaying windows as explained in the iOS7 Migration Guide

    Simply put, you will have to have your window's top property to 20 pixels. Also, by default, the information shown in the status bar is now black. So if you are using a black background, you will see nothing since it will be black on black.
