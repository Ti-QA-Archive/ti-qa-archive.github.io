Map Region and Route Display

I have a mapview that's doing almost the right thing and I'm having trouble closing the gap on the last 2% of what needs to be done. The problems boil down to these 2 things:

  • Zooming the map so that it's as large as it can possibly be in order to display 2 annotations. One annotation is the user's current location and the other is a facility location. Setting deltas in the region property seems pretty brittle since I don't know how wide the area may need to be. It shouldn't be all that big, but I can't guarantee that nor do I know how far "all that big" is.
  • Displaying route info. Right know I define a route with 2 points (the current location and the facility location). The route that gets drawn is simply a straight line between the 2 points. I'd like something a little less, um, rudimentary than that. I thought the route would at least follow the streets on the map. In the Kitchen Sink app, the "Map View with Routing" looks much better, but the route is also insanely complex. Maybe that's what it takes to look reasonable?

Any advice on how to tackle this last 2%?
