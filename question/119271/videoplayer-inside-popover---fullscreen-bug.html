Videoplayer inside popover -> fullscreen bug

Hi,

I have a videoplayer inside a popover box:


var v_obj = Titanium.Media.createVideoPlayer({
        contentURL:'video.mp4',
        backgroundColor:'#111',
        movieControlMode:Titanium.Media.VIDEO_CONTROL_DEFAULT,
        scalingMode:Titanium.Media.VIDEO_SCALING_NONE,
        movieControlStyle:Titanium.Media.VIDEO_CONTROL_DEFAULT,
        height:404,
        width:720,
        autoplay:false
    });


var view_detail1 = Ti.UI.createView({});
var btn_close = Ti.UI.createButton({title:'Close'});
var popover = Ti.UI.iPad.createPopover({height:404,width:720,title:"Details", rightNavButton:btn_close});
view_detail1.add(v_obj);
popover.add(view_detail1);

When the popover is shown and I click the fullscreen button inside my videoplayer the video goes into fullscreen but the popover is still in front of if. I tried to hide the popover but since the videoplayer is part of it it will be removed too. Is there a way to bring my video to the foreground OR remove the fullscreen control?
