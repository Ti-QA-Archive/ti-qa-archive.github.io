    You are trying to change the backgroundImage property of the ImageView, whilst it's the image property you want to change

    It's not showing anything because in your xml you have:

    <View><ImageView id="imgv" image="/Image/plus.jpg" right="10db" /></View>

    This

    e.ImageView.backgroundImage = '/Image/plus.jpg';

    Should be:

    e.ImageView.image = '/Image/plus.jpg';

    Patrick van Vuuren
