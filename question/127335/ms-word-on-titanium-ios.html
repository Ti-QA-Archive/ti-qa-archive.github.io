  • I am not sure you can do this..

    You would need to have a word document reader built into either the phone or the app.. and then use this to read the document.

    PDF readers etc are readily available as the PDF document format has been open sourced for ages and people have written tools. I am not sure people have written a word plug in for Titanium.

    So look if there is word available on the phone and try and utilise that software.
