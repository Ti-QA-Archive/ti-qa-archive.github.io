  • Not sure what IOS device you are targeting, but on the iPad the easiest way to "Open in iBooks" would be to use Titanium.UI.iPad.DocumentViewer. This will allow you to print the PDF or open in iBooks or other supported app. The Titanium.UI.iPad.DocumentViewer is avalible in TI 1.8.0.1. This also provides a nice thumbnail view off all the pages down the right hand side of the screen.

    Hope this helps.
