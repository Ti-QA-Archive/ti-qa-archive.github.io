  • Hi,

    I add the same problem. A quick dirty fix.

    1. Open this file

          ~/Library/Application Support/Titanium/mobilesdk/osx/<your titanium version>/iphone/builder.py

      (or whereever you installed the titanium sdk)

    2. Find those lines

      • os.putenv(&#39;DYLD_FRAMEWORK_PATH&#39;, iphoneprivateframeworkspath) (line 1432 for version 3.0.0.GA)
      • os.unsetenv(&#39;DYLD_FRAMEWORK_PATH

    3. Comment them

    This is a quick and dirty fix. I do not investigate why and how it works. It just get me out my problem
    .

    Hope this help
