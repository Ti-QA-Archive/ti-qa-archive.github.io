  • Hi, I'm having the same issue, it seems to be a caching issue. Sometimes the app crashes after heavy zooming and scrolling in the map.
    Deleteing the cache (in the settings), solves the problem temporarily, but after some time (a few seconds) the same behaviour occurs.

    I created a JIRA ticket.
    Please report your experiences there so that the programmers can find a solution asap.
    The more information they get the better.

    https://jira.appcelerator.org/browse/TC-4467
