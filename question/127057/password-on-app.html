Password on App

Hey.
There is no way to password protect an App from Appstore from being downloaded, but is there a way to put a password inside the app? A password that you only have to enter once?
Im making an app for my employer and they don't want that everyone can download it and be able to view the content.
Thanks …

  • Accepted Answer

    Another option is to offer an application that does require a password to access the content. Examples of two such applications include Gulfstream's PlaneBook and Flight Safety's application found in the app store. The Gulfstream application allows a guest login but only includes marketing materials about the company however, upon entering a password your device downloads materials only for customers or employees.
