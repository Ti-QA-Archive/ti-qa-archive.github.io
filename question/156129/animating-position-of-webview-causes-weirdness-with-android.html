This is mainly for Android, on iOS this works without the weirdness.

Okay, so we are animating the postion from left:0 to left:-100 for a webview so that it shows only half part. The other part is off screen.

When we animate the webview back to left:0 the offscreen part is completely white, until the animation ends, and it "rerenders" the content.

Is it possible somehow to make sure that the webiew is showing all content during the animation?
