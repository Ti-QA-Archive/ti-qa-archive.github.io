Working with iOS 6 and Appcelerator. Just need to update xcode?

  • Accepted Answer

    Since we don't know when you first installed Titanium on your Mac, no one can really answer that question accurately. Some basic apps already in the App Store work without issue on the new iOS 6 despite being built with an older Titanium SDK and/or iOS sdk.

    You will require Titanium Mobile SDK 2.1.3 or greater at minimal + the version of xCode (4.5+) that supports iOS 6.0 from this point forward

    Additionally, you will require a Default-586@2x.png splash screen so that your app will not display 'letterboxed' on the new iPhone 5.

    In the future:

    I suggest you take a look at the Using Questions and Answers article, specifically the Q&A Question Checklist. The missing information is critical to reproducing problems in a test environment and often indicates other factors that cause the undesirable outcome you are experiencing.
