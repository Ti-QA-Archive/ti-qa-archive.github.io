Getting the app-icon in the status bar

Hey there,

I was wondering if(and how) it's possible to get the icon of the app in the statusbar of the phone. So when you minimize the screen(Home button usually) the icon appears there making it easy to turn back to the app again. So not really a notification that something changed, just a heads up that it's still on and an easy to return to thing.

Android 2.2+, iOS 4.3+, Titanium SDK 3.0.0

  • I'm afraid you will have to rely on a local Notification to achieve that.

    The main problem with this is not that it's technically feasible or not. But more to the fact that it goes against the Android good practices in terms of navigation. Thus increasing the chances of your application being rejected when submitting it to Google Play.
