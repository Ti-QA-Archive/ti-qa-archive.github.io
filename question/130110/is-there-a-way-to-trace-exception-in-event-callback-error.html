    For IOS you can build and run your app with xcode. And when an exception threw, you can know exactly what happened.

    Here to go: Open your project in Finder.. Then open it with xCode by double click .xcodeproj file at: YourProject > build > iphone > YourProject.xcodeproj

    Hope that help.
