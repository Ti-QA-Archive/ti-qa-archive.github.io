    • The API docs state about getFile(), "one or more path arguments to form the full path joined together with the platform specific path separator."

      Hence, I think your call should be:

      Ti.Filesystem.getFile(Ti.Filesystem.externalStorageDirectory, '..', 'data.xml');

    • This is how to force a full rebuild…

      Caution, though; currently the process for Android and iOS is different. This is due to ticket #2852.

      Hence:

      For iOS/iphone, delete the contents of the project's build&#x2F;iphone directory, select Edit Project in Titanium Developer and click the Save Changes button to force a full rebuild. Then launch the app.

      For android, only delete the contents of the immediate subdirectories of &#x2F;build&#x2F;android, otherwise you may encounter problems. Watch ticket #2852 to be updated by email of a resolution to this issue. Then click the Save Changes button to force a full rebuild and launch the app, as above.

      To understand the reason why you receive the error, and why it isn't such a bad thing, see How does Titanium work?
