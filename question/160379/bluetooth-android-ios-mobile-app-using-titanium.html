  • JN, Logical Labs has multiple module solutions for using Bluetooth with Titanium on both iOS and Android available in the marketplace:

    • Bluetooth LE iOS
    • Bluetooth LE Android
    • Bluetooth Classic Android
    • External Accessories (BT Classic for iOS)
    • Beacons (iOS & Android)

    All of our modules come with full support, complete documentation (and now online documentation) and working examples. Contact us directly using titanium @ logicallabs.com with any issues or questions you may using our modules in your project.
