Traffic in Apple Maps

Is there a way to display the current traffic view on Apple Maps? The following works fine on Android, but not on iOS.

var map = Map.createView({
  mapType: Map.NORMAL_TYPE,
  traffic: true
});

Am I missing something or is traffic not available through Ti Apple Maps?

Mobile App. Titanium 3.2. iOS 7+

  • The native iOS MapKit does not support traffic data, the property traffic is therefore only for Android:

    http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/latest/#!/api/Modules.Map.View-property-traffic

    If traffic data is required, you can implement Google Maps for iOS, e.g. with this custom module from Marketplace:

    https://marketplace.appcelerator.com/apps/5076
