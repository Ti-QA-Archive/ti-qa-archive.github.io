hintText not centering after rotation

I'm wondering if anyone has had this issue or knows of a solution:

In portrait mode the text field is 60% the width of the screen, but in landscape I resize it to just 30%. In portrait mode the hintText is centered just fine, but in landscape the spacing to the left of the hintText stays the same. This causes the hintText to be cut off - not good.

Just been working in the emulator so far with Ti Mobile SDK 1.7.3.

Thank you for your help!

  • I think your issue maybe that when the box is created then it centres the text based on its size and applies and appropriate from left position.

    When the screen is rotated then it still uses the left position.

    Two potential option reset the left based on what space is available… or reset the text align..

    You can probably check this by doing an alert of the left before and after rotation.
