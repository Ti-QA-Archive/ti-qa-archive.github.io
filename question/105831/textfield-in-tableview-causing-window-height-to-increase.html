My solution was to add the button as a footerView. The problem is that this has introduced a bug with a TextField I have towards to top of the window. When you click on the TextField and the Toolbar comes up it extends the height of the window, even when the Toolbar is closed and the TextField is no longer focused. I have no idea how to stop this or put the window back to its normal height.

Can someone please help me find a way to add a button to the TableView without a background or border… Or find out a way for the TextArea to stop increasing the size of the window?
