  • Accepted Answer

    1. Do you have the appropriate android icons in their correct location within the project?
    2. Sometimes in 3.x assets are not properly copied from the project into the build location. You can copy your appicon into the correct location (build/android/bin/assets/Resources/ to jump start the build.
    3. Try cleaning the project to force a complete rebuild.
