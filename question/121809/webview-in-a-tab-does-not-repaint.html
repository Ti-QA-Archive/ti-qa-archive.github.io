    • Titanium is superior than PhoneGap from a technical perspective.

      Unfortunately, at least for the BlackBerry, it doesn't work. That makes PhoneGap the better option.

      In a nutshell, PhoneGap just makes a simple app with a browser window in it. From there you use basic HTML, CSS and JavaScript to make the app work. Anything you can do with the web, you can do in your app. You make the controls, the content and the navigation. For me, that was enough.

      You can compile PhoneGap locally. You don't need their cloud service. It's all command line, but it works well.

      Good luck.
