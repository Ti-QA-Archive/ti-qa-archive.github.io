  • I briefly looked at Android's build script and it looks like it only ignores the usual suspects (.DS_Store, .git, .svn, and a few others).

    You could open a terminal, change to your project's directory and run:

    find . -name ".Icon" -print0 | xargs -0 rm

    Be careful when running that command, there is no way to undo it.

    — commented December 18th 2012 by Chris Barber
