How to get Acktie QR and Barcode scanner BOTH working in the same Android project

Acktie QR Code scanner works great. Acktie Barcode scanner works great. However, getting both of them to work in the same project in Android does not due to duplicate libraries and graphics.

After hours and hours of hopelessness, and a bit of help from Tony at Acktie support, here is how I got BOTH Acktie QR Code scanner and Acktie Barcode scanner working in the same project on Android. Note: in iOS it wasn't a problem including the modules in the project "as is"… all worked fine out of the box. Just in Android, the app would not compile due to an assortment of compiler errors.

Note: Support provided me version 1.4 of the barcode module. I am not sure if that was needed or not. I didn't try going back to 1.3 as I was just thankful I got the darn thing working and wanted to move on with my life :). I don't think I missed anything in these instructions… hopefully anyway.

Delete the following files from your Titanium project:

modules/android/com.acktie.mobile.android.barcode/1.4/lib/guava-12.0.jar
modules/android/com.acktie.mobile.android.barcode/1.4/lib/zbar.jar

modules/android/com.acktie.mobile.android.barcode/1.4/libs/armeabi/libiconv.so
modules/android/com.acktie.mobile.android.barcode/1.4/libs/armeabi/libzbarjni.so

modules/android/com.acktie.mobile.android.barcode/1.4/libs/armeabi-v7a/libiconv.so
modules/android/com.acktie.mobile.android.barcode/1.4/libs/armeabi-v7a/libzbarjni.so

Leave all files in the following folder structure in tact:

modules/android/com.acktie.mobile.android.qr

From Mac Terminal (obviously adjust the path to your own project folder):

zip -d "/Developer/Projects/Titanium/MyAppName/modules/android/com.acktie.mobile.android.barcode/1.4/acktiemobileandroidbarcode.jar" /assets/Center-Blue.png

zip -d "/Developer/Projects/Titanium/MyAppName/modules/android/com.acktie.mobile.android.barcode/1.4/acktiemobileandroidbarcode.jar" /assets/Center-Purple.png

zip -d "/Developer/Projects/Titanium/MyAppName/modules/android/com.acktie.mobile.android.barcode/1.4/acktiemobileandroidbarcode.jar" /assets/Center-Yellow.png

zip -d "/Developer/Projects/Titanium/MyAppName/modules/android/com.acktie.mobile.android.barcode/1.4/acktiemobileandroidbarcode.jar" /assets/Center-Red.png

zip -d "/Developer/Projects/Titanium/MyAppName/modules/android/com.acktie.mobile.android.barcode/1.4/acktiemobileandroidbarcode.jar" /assets/FullScreen-Blue.png

zip -d "/Developer/Projects/Titanium/MyAppName/modules/android/com.acktie.mobile.android.barcode/1.4/acktiemobileandroidbarcode.jar" /assets/FullScreen-Purple.png

zip -d "/Developer/Projects/Titanium/MyAppName/modules/android/com.acktie.mobile.android.barcode/1.4/acktiemobileandroidbarcode.jar" /assets/FullScreen-Yellow.png

zip -d "/Developer/Projects/Titanium/MyAppName/modules/android/com.acktie.mobile.android.barcode/1.4/acktiemobileandroidbarcode.jar" /assets/FullScreen-Red.png

Clean your project and rebuild.
