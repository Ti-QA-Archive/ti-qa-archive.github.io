Does the Loading Widget work?

I am creating a mobile app on Android. I am trying to use the loading widget, and am doing exactly what is required in the doc:

http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/3.0/#!/api/Alloy.widgets.loading

and I cannot get the Loading icon to show at all. Is there something I'm missing or do I have to use an ActivityIndicator?

  • It's literally exact as done in the doc there:

    index.xml

    <Alloy>
    <Window id="index" class="container">
        <Require id="loading" type="widget" src="com.appcelerator.loading"/>
    . . . .

    Config.json

    {
    "global": {}, 
    "env:development": {}, 
    "env:test": {}, 
    "env:production": {}, 
    "os:ios": {}, 
    "os:android": {}, 
    "dependencies": {
            "com.appcelerator.loading":"1.0"
    },
. . .

    index.js

    $.loading.setOpacity(1.0);
alert("Icon should be loaded");

    That's it. . .plain and simple. I am seeing the alert, but I do not see a loading icon.

  • Nevermind. I'm using a Galaxy Note 2 to debug with and had a white background. The loading icon was imperceptible in the background.

    Lessons learned: Loading icon has to be on a dark background. It's 20x20, on a modern 5" phone, TINY.
