  • After poking myself in the eye with this problem on and off for a couple of weeks, I finally got it to work with my existing 3.1.2 installation that had worked previously.

    I installed xulrunner 3.6.28/32-bit in /opt/mozilla and then added the following two lines to the end of my TitaniumStudio.ini:

    -Dorg.eclipse.swt.browser.DefaultType=mozilla
-Dorg.eclipse.swt.browser.XULRunnerPath=/opt/mozilla/xulrunner

    Thought this might be of some help to someone else.
