Tried to Upgrade to 3.4.0 Now Issues

I tried to upgrade to 3.4.0 and now every time I try to open Titanium Studio I get a 10810 error "The application TitaniumStudio.app can't be opened. I ran the sudo command lines for upgrading. Afterwards, I tried to back out and I still get the 10810 error. I have restarted, thinking I might be have memory leaks in the processes (I have been a long time windows user) and still no joy. Any help would be appreciated as I am dead in the water at the moment.

MAC OSX 10.9.4
XCode 6.0.1
I was up to date on Titanium releases (3.3.0)

Thank you.

  • Accepted Answer

    I believe this might be due to a bad download of the update. If you search for "eclipse 10810" in Google, this apparently happens to a number of Eclipse-based products if a file is corrupted. The quickest fix would be to remove the installation folder from /Applications and replace it with a fresh download from http://preview.appcelerator.com.
