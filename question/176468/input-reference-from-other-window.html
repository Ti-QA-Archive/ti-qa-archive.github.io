    Without knowing how your are architecting your project, its hard to give a specific answer. Generally speaking, you'll want to add an export method that fetches the input's value:

    exports.getValue = function(){
    return $.text.value;
};

    You could also pass an onChange method to call when you create the controller that contains text field.

    var args = arguments[0];

$.text.addEventListener('change', args.onChange);
