  • I pretty sure those adapters are designed to sync data between local backbone models and collections (in device memory) and ACS. I think what you may be looking for is some kind of dual sync adapter for Backbone to keep data synced between ACS and a local SQLite (or indeed Ti.properties) datastore.

    I'm looking into the same thing myself and have recently found the following which may be a good starting point:

    https://github.com/viezel/napp.alloy.adapter.restsql
