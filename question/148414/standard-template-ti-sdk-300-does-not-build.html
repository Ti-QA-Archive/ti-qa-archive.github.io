    Titanium 3.0.0.GA does not work on case-sensitive filesystems because of of this one "resources" folder in the SDK's iphone directory. It should be "Resources", not "resources".

    It's been fixed for the upcoming v3.0.2 release.

    In the meantime, you can simply rename it yourself:

    mv /Users/pschweitzer/Library/Application\ Support/Titanium/mobilesdk/osx/3.0.0.GA/iphone/resources /Users/pschweitzer/Library/Application\ Support/Titanium/mobilesdk/osx/3.0.0.GA/iphone/Resources

    I would definitely recommend 3.0.0.GA over 2.1.4.GA. There are many bug fixes and improvements. The soon-to-be-released v3.0.2 is even better and has fixed many issues since 3.0.0.GA shipped back in Dec 2012.

    — answered February 18th 2013 by Chris Barber
