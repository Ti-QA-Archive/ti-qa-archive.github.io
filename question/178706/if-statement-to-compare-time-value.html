    Hi Sameera Mudalige,

    Thanks for your concern.

    If your times are based on a 24 hour clock (if there's no AM/PM) and times are always in the format HH:MM:SS then you can do a direct string comparison like:

    var str1 = "10:20:45";
var str2 = "05:10:10";

if(str1 == str2)
    alert("Equal");
else if (str1 > str2)
    alert("Time 1 is later than time 2");
else
    alert("Time 2 is later than time 1");

    Or you can also parse date and time like:

    var str1 = Date.parse('01/01/2011 10:20:45');
var str2 = Date.parse('01/01/2011 5:10:10');

if(str1 == str2)
    alert("Equal");
else if (str1 > str2)
    alert("Time 1 is later than time 2");
else
    alert("Time 2 is later than time 1");

    Here 1st January is an arbitrary date, doesn't mean anything. Hope it'll help you to solve your problem.

    If you have any concern, then feel free to let us know.
