I need to clean my Project… and I would not want to keep adding the Fonts again and again…

    Hi

    Good news, if the only reason you are using a custom plist is for fonts - you can now use a simpler method that requires no duplication of files plist or otherwise.

    There is now a new section of the tiapp.xml you can add (via a text editor or raw mode) that lets you handle fonts very simply - plus a load of other stuff you can look up later.

    I provided an explanation of how this works in a previous answer;

    Give this a go and you should find fonts become a very simple addition to your app - without the pain you are going through now.
