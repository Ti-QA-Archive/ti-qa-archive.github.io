  • Well, for what it's worth, I got this solved. For anyone else who may find this post in the future, here's what I did.

    1: My iMac had some encryption software on it (PGP) because it used to be a corporate machine. (I talked my boss into letting me keep it.) I decrypted the hard drive and removed PGP.

    2: I ungraded to Lion (10.7.3)

    3: I upgraded to xCode 4.3.2

    4: I upgraded the TS SDK (2.0.1)

    After all that, and a few extra patches and fixes along the way, I fired up TS again, tested the app, and it found the module. I'm not sure which of all of the above solved the problem, but I'm very happy, five days after running into this problem, to finally have it solved.
