  • Max,

    WebGL was enabled in webkit gtk+ 1.3.x, but in 1.4.x it is marked as "experimental" and turned off by default. We have left it off for the time being as it is marked "experimental". The only way to get it in would be to compile your own version and replace the one in the thirdparty bundle which gets downloaded when you build.
