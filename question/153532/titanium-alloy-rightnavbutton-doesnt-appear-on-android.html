titanium alloy rightNavButton doesnt appear on android.

Hi, i am new to titanium. i am creating a window on xml that has rightNavButton as save.However,this button appears on ios but it doesn't appear on android. Thanks!

    Android doesn't have a Navbar, so there's no setRightNavButton. You would have to create the Bar (UIView) by yourself and add a NavButton (UIButton) to it on Android.
