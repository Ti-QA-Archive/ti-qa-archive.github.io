Best app structure for login flow?

Hi there,

I'm trying to figure out what would be the best approach while creating an app with a login screen before accessing the main application.

Is it possible to have two navigationGroup on iOS? One for the login / registration steps and another one for the main application?

Or open the login window as a modal then load the navigationGroup? But what happen when the user logs out, can you close the navigationGroup and reopen once logged back in?

I have couple of solutions in mind but wondering which one would be the most elegant one.
