Subscription Models and Pricing

Are there any plans to reintroduce a monthly subscription option or perhaps create a new lower priority response model at a more affordable rate ?

The product is great but the support cost is prohibitive, at the current rate i'm sure it wont be long before some enterprising individuals set up an alternative support channel and start filtering off those of us willing to pay for some extra help, but not yet generating enough income from our development efforts to justify the expense of the annual pro subscription.

If you would like to see appcelerator make an option like this available and would subscribe to it, please leave a comment below so they can gauge the level of interest from the community.

Cheers,
