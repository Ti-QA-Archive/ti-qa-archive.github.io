Node.ACS - Switching from Development to Production

So I have a custom Node.ACS that connects to an existing ACS:

// Connect Node.ACS to existing ACS
var Cloud = require('acs').ACS;
    Cloud.init('XXXxxx_DEVELOPMENT_xxxXXXxXXx'); // ACS  DEVELOPMENT APP KEY

What is the proper way to switch to Production… should I simply update the ACS Development Key to ACS Production Key, and Deploy the Node.ACS project again, after App Store Approves the App?

  • In standard case you have in your tiapp.xml both keys (for dev and prod) and the system uses the right keys automaticly.

    On the other hand you can use 'Ti.App.deployType' to switch:

    Cloud.init((Ti.App.deployType=='development' ? 'XXXxxx_DEVELOPMENT_xxxXXXxXXx' : 'XXXxxx_PRODUCTION_xxxXXXxXXx'));
