Universal app published to iPhone only

I created an app to be universal, but to my surprise and horror, when published to the iOS app store it shows up as an iPhone app only!
I have iPhone and iPad checked under deployment targets in the tiapp.xml file.
Used Titanium SDK 3.0.2.GA and latest Xcode and iOS.

  • Have same problem. Small workaround:

    • Remove build of project.
    • Before submitting to Apple, run in iPad Simulator
    • Close simulator and from Titanium deploy to AppStore.

    It worked for me
