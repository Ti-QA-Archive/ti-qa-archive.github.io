  • To exit the app when the home button is pressed or the app goes in the background add this in you tiapp.xml ( only latest version of the TiSDK)

    <ios>
        <plist>
            <dict>
                <key>UIApplicationExitsOnSuspend</key>
                <true/>
            </dict>
        </plist>
    </ios>

    Apple might reject your app if you try to do this programmatically (as result of an action inside the app, like an "exit" button, etc).
