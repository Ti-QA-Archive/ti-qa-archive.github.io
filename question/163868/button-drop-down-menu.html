    Hi Herve,
    Yes it is possible, you need to create a view with all your options either in a table view or a scroll view . And on click of a button just add it just below of the button and remove it on another click, You can have a bool variable to keep track of shown and removed for a view. Please feel free to ask if any confusion or else mark the answer if your problem is solved to help others.
