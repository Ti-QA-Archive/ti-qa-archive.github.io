Any way to "force" Garbage Collection on Android, or Bitmap recycle?

I'm using a lot of high-res images in my Android app (with custom tabs, different view for each), including one tab for remote Instagram photos. I notice that most of the time, my app will function fine, but there are times when it doesn't garbage collect before going to another tab (I've tried the setTimeouts, and they work to a certain extent) it will crash OR only display some of the images.

If my app doesn't crash due to memory, and only some of the images are showing, if I switch tabs (which will free up memory from previous view, and reallocate another view),and then go back to it, all of the images will display.

So, I'm wondering is there a way I can force GC in Titanium, or at least "bitmap recycle" on Android.
