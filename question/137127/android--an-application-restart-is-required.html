    • Nearly all our apps have a large database of information. Users expect it to update, so our message says something about new database being installed…restart required.

      — commented May 18th 2012 by Stephen Feather 

    • <property name="ti.android.bug2373.title">Database Update Complete</property>
<property name="ti.android.bug2373.message">Please restart to continue.</property>
<property name="ti.android.bug2373.buttonText">Restart</property>
