xlargeScreens - can't get it to run on Android

Hi,

I'm trying to publish an app to the Android market. The app supports multiple Android screens but whenever I'm trying to enable 'xlargeScreens' on the tiapp.xml, the app never starts. When I remove the xlargeScreens line everything works fine.

This is happening both and the Emulator and when trying to publish the app.

Here's the tiapp code:

    <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
    <manifest>
        <supports-screens 
        android:anyDensity="true"
            android:xlargeScreens="true"
            android:largeScreens="true"
        android:normalScreens="true"
        android:smallScreens="true"
        />
    </manifest>
     </android>

Thanks,
Idan

    You may need to set the api level to 9 or above, since that is when xlargeScreen was introduced.

      <tool-api-level>9</tool-api-level>
