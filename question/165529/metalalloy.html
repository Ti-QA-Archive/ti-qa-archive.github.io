    Ok, from a quick once over.

    You have a <View> with the .content class that is layout vertical. Within that you have 2 views, your <ScrollView> containing your icon array, and your <View> used to contain the footer elements.

    Remove the layout:vertical from .content, make the bottom of your <Scrollview> equal to the height of your footer view (40dp).
