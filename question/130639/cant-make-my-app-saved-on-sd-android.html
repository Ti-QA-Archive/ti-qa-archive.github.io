can't make my app saved on SD (Android)

Hi,

I developed an app using Titanium 1.7.5 for Android.
Although in my phone (HTC Sensation, Android 2.3.3) it is saved on SD card, in other phones like HTC Sensation 4G or HTC Desire, it is not saved on SD and it does not let me to move it to the SD Card. Anyone other having this issue? This is my manifest:

<manifest android:versionCode="27" android:versionName="1" android:installLocation="preferExternal" package="com.pgs.app" xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
    <uses-sdk android:maxSdkVersion="10" android:minSdkVersion="7" android:targetSdkVersion="8"/>

Anyone other had this issue? Any ideas?
