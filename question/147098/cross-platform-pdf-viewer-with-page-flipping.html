  • This is a pretty tall order. I don't know of any cross platform viewers with even the page flip. And maybe only 1 or 2 iOS viewers that have even a few of the features that you're looking for. The PDFTouchKit for iOS might come close to most of those features. But for Android, I haven't seen much beyond just a simple pdf view. Regardless, a cross-platform answer may not exist for what you want. You'll either have to create one, or find 2 different viewers to implement.
