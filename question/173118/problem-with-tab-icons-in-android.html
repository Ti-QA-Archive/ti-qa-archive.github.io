  • Because you are referencing your window content via url: I will NOT be assisting you, enabling you in a bad programming practice.

    Along with that, you should not be relying on Ti.UI.currentTab but should be passing a reference to the parent tab to your child windows.

    You need to accept a commonJS pattern of programming.
