Sound doesn't play every time when clicking a button on Android

I have a button that should play a sound everytime I click a button on Android device/emulator, and it works fine at first but after a while the sound doesn't play, then after a while it plays again.

Here is my code:

var button = Ti.UI.createButton({
    width:'90dp',
    height:'90dp'
});
win.add(button);

button.addEventListener('touchstart', function(e){
    var sound = Ti.Media.createSound({
    url:'punch.mp3',
    preload:true
});
sound.play();
});

I have tried 'click' instead of 'touchstart', I have tried preload:false, I have tried creating the sound outside the function, I have tried clean but nothing works.

Anyone knows what the problem is and how to fix it?
The code is working on iOS

  • In my projects this code works fine on iOS:

    button.addEventListener('touchstart', require('play').play(e.sound));

    play.js:

    exports.play = function(ji) {
    var player = Ti.Media.createSound({url:"/assets/sounds/" + ji + '.mp3'});
    player.play();
}
