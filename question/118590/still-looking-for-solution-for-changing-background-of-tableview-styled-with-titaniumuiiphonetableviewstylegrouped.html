    Hi Aaron,

    Assuming you already have you rows in a 'data' var, this works for me :

    
var tableView = Ti.UI.createTableView({
    top:0,
    data:data,
    height:120,
    style: Titanium.UI.iPhone.TableViewStyle.GROUPED,
    backgroundColor:'#ff9900',
    separatorColor:'#000'
});

    Though, I actually use backgroundColor:'transparent' in my case, to show the window/view background behind it.
