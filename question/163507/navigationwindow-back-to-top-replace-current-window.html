What I want to do is : if I click on an item in the menu, "Level 3" and "Level 2" windows are closed, the "Level 1" window is "replaced" by the one chosen in the menu.
I found a solution with many "windowClose" but it is not very fast and sexy (it makes some glitches)

Do you have a suggestion to make to me ?
