  • As far as I know, Ti.Platform.openURL() isn't available from webview JS. You have a limited amount of webview-side Javascript functionality that is connected to the application.

    In theory, you can communicate back to your application using Ti.API.fireEvent('myevent'), {object data}), and then do your opening in a 'myevent' listener which gets the URL info from your object.

    However:

    This won't work on Android. See: Android: evalJS() / no Ti Javascript injection to webview
