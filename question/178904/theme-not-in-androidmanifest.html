  • Use Custom Theme for android and

    values ->MyTheme.xml

    <style name="Theme.MyTheme" parent="Theme.AppCompat.Translucent.NoTitleBar">
    <!– Any customizations for your app running on pre-3.0 devices here Theme.Light.NoTitleBar–>
    <item name="android:windowBackground">@drawable/background</item>
    <!– <item name="android:windowNoTitle">true</item> –>
    </style>

    values-v11 ->MyTheme.xml

    <style name="Theme.MyTheme" parent="Theme.AppCompat.Light">
    <!– Any customizations for your app running on 3.0+ devices here Theme.Holo.Light–>
    <item name="android:windowBackground">@drawable/background</item>
    <!– <item name="android:windowNoTitle">true</item> –>
    </style>

    values-v14 ->MyTheme.xml

    <style name="Theme.MyTheme" parent="Theme.AppCompat.Translucent.NoTitleBar">
    <!– Any customizations for your app running on 4.0+ devices here Theme.DeviceDefault.Light.NoActionBar–>
    <item name="android:windowBackground">@drawable/background</item>
    <!– <item name="android:windowNoTitle">true</item> –>
    </style>

    and use this custom the in tiapp.xml like android:theme="@style/Theme.MyTheme" using custom manifest file for TiActivity and AppActivity i,e launcher activity.
