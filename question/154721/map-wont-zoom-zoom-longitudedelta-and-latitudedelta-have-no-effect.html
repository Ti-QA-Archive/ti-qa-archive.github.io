Map won't zoom. (Zoom, longitudeDelta, and latitudeDelta have no effect).

I'm testing an iOS app/Ti 3.1.1 GA on the emulator.

I'm creating a map with a SF location. It shows me a map of the US. I want to see a centered, street level view of the location.

Here is the code:

$.win.addEventListener('open', function(){
    var testMap=Ti.Map.createView({
        userLocation: false,
        regionFit: true,
        animate: true,
        mapType: Ti.Map.STANDARD_TYPE,
        zoom: 5,
        region: {latititude: 37.7700304985046,
            longitude: -122.410190999508,
            latitudeDelta: 0.01,
            longitudeDelta: 0.01}
    })
    $.win.add(testMap)
});

Changing zoom, or the delta values has no effect. How do I get a centered, street-level version of the map?
