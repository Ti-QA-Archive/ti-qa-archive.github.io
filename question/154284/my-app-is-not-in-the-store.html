  • Hi

    Given your use of the phrase "Read to sale" I am going to assume that you are talking about the iOS AppStore.

    Inside your iTunes Connect entry for your app there should be a link called View in App Store, that should give you access to the app directly.

    Make sure you have agreed to any outstanding contracts (been at least one since iOS7), this can hold things up.
