App Store approved.

Just wanted to say a quick thanks to the Appcelerator community. My app was approved by Apple over the weekend without any resistance. It took approximately nine days including the holiday. The app is very much a prototype, and I was afraid that it would be rejected for a variety of reasons. I'll look forward to answering any questions that I can about the process, and hopefully soon I will have a world-class app that Appcelerator can be proud of.
