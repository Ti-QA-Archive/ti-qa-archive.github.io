    style:Titanium.UI.iPhone.ProgressBarStyle.PLAIN

    Possibly a stupid question, but have you 'add'ed it?

    win.add(progressBar);

    If 'win' is your Ti.UI.currentWindow

  • You have to show the ProgressBar once you added it to the view.

    var progressBar = Titanium.UI.createProgressBar({ ... });

win.add(progressBar);
progressBar.show();
