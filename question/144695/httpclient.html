  • There isn't a definite number of concurrent requests you can run. It can depend on a great number
    of factors actually (network bandwidth, available memory, etc.)

    But to me, if you need to do too many http requests at any given time, you may want to rethink your application's architecture. Because even if you limit your calls, it will not fix a bad design and you will pay for it down the road.

    — commented November 14th 2012 by Christian Brousseau
