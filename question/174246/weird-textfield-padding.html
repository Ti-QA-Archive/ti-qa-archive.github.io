  • You're not the first person I've heard having troubles with strange layout issues on Galaxy S4 devices. It sounds like their theme files are setting more padding on the S4 than on their other devices. Your workaround would be to create your own custom Android theme, and specifically set styles on textfields to specify the padding you want to use. Custom themes are essentially merged, so if you don't specify other settings (colors, styles on components other than textfield, etc.) then users should get the stock Galaxy theme settings.
