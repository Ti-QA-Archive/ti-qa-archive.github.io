    • You can't run logs, but you can view the crashlog from the device. Plug in and sync your device, then either check the crashlog dir:
      ~&#x2F;Library&#x2F;Logs&#x2F;CrashReporter&#x2F;MobileDevice&#x2F;&lt;your iPhone’s name&gt;&#x2F;
      or open the console utility and find your device in the list of logs.
