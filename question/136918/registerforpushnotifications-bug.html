registerForPushNotifications bug

A number of threads on this forum appear to suggest that the behavior I have seen with registerForPushNotifications is an Appcelerator bug.

a. I have configured the AppID correctly, then created the provisioning profile and installed it.
b. I request the Push notification registration and wait…. . Nothing happens, no errors, no success… .
