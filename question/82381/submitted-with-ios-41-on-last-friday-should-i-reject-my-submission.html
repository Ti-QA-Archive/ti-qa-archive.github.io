Hello everybody!

    No, I wouldn't reject it. Keep it in the queue.

    I submitted mine as 4.1 last week, and it was approved and works fine on 4.2 too. I've now submitted the 4.2 version and they're waiting for review now, so my apps are all 4.1 running on 4.2 ok.

    It's taking about 7 days at the moment for them to go to In Review, so I'd leave it as is, and get it reviewed. If it's approved, then submit an update.
