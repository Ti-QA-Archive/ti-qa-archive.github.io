Facebook bug (Titanium 3.0)

My project and app is fine on titanium 2.1.xx, but on 3.0, Facebook native app or Safari not turn back my app after user authenticated.
I found, in Info.plist, at URL stream section, have a value:"fb[object Object]".
I think this is an internal bug of titanium 3.0, after change to "fbN" (N is my Facebook app id), everything ok!
Hope this help to workaround! But i think we need a official solution!

  • Just notified this problem too.

    To have a valid entry for CFBundleURLSchemes with your Facebook AppID (used in tiapp.xml), you have to modify iphone/cli/commands/_build.js in line 1037:

    fbAppId = (this.tiapp.properties && this.tiapp.properties['ti.facebook.appid']) ? this.tiapp.properties['ti.facebook.appid']['value'] : '',
