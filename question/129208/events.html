  • Since you haven't specified a platform, I'll say that in 1.7.x and earlier, long click doesn't work on Android. It is supposed to appear in 1.8.x

    As for horizontal and vertical swipes, you would need to add two event listeners to your view, 'touchstart' and 'touchend'. Gather the x,y positions from each and mathematically determine the swipe direction. This would also allow for diagonal readings as well.
