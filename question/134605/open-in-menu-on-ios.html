    • yes you can modify info.plist and also use that at the time of complilation.

      so follow the steps..

      • copy your info.plist
      • paste it in your titanium project folder not in the resources folder
      • modify your .plist file
      • then fully rebuild your app so at the time of compliation it access your .plist from the project folder
      • fully rebuild means clean all your sources from the folder build/iphone
