nine-patch problem with landscape splash on Android

I followed the tutorial to setup a nine-patch splash. Thanks Malcolm, it is very clear and detailed.

Unlike the tutorial, I put the splash.9.png on a folder called drawable-nodpi to use the same image with any dpi. This worked well until I needed to force the splash screen to show in landscape mode.

To do this I customized the manifest:

           <application>
                <activity android:name=".MyAppActivity" 
                    android:label="@string/app_name" 
                    android:theme="@style/Theme.Titanium" 
                    android:screenOrientation="landscape" 
                    android:configChanges="keyboardHidden|orientation">
                    <intent-filter>
                        <action android:name="android.intent.action.MAIN"/>
                        <category android:name="android.intent.category.LAUNCHER"/>
                    </intent-filter>
                </activity>
                <activity android:name="org.appcelerator.titanium.TiActivity" 
                    android:screenOrientation="landscape" 
                    android:configChanges="keyboardHidden|orientation"/>
            </application>

Now the app correctly shows the splash screen (the nine-patch image) only if it starts in landscape. No splash screen (black screen) if the app starts in portrait mode.

What is the problem? Image not found?
