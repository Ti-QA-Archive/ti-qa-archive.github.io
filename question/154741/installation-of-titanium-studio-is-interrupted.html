Installation of Titanium Studio is Interrupted.

I was trying to install Titanium studio on my PC, but the installation process interrupted due to the need to connect to the internet to download some of the required components and I connect to the internet through a proxy. My question is, is there any way to download the required components through the proxy or to bypass the proxy settings?

  • editing the following file: "%Titanium_Studio_Path%\jre\lib\net.properties".

    You simply have to edit the following lines:

    http.proxyHost=yourProxy.com
http.proxyPort=8080
http.nonProxyHosts=localhost|127.0.0.1

https.proxyHost=yourProxy.com
https.proxyPort=8080
