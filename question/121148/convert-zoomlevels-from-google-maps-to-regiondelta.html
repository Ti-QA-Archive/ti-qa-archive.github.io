Convert Zoomlevels from Google Maps to RegionDelta

Hello

I'm building an app with two or more MapViews for both Android and iPhone. And due to Androids limit to just use one MapView per app I need to use regular Google Maps through a WebView and MapView in iPhone.
After a while I discovered that Google Maps doesn't give you any RegionDelta data, only zoomlevels etc.

On the iPhone I send my current location/current region and RegionDelta to a database which then finds out which perticular annotations that are near me and I add them to the map.

I want to do the same on the Android, but I cant figure out how I am suppose to get/convert the data from Google Maps so they can match the MapViews data.

I've found this blogpost http://troybrant.net/blog/2010/01/mkmapview-and-zoom-levels-a-visual-guide/ but it's a bit cloudy for me.

Anyone have any ideas?
