Our iPad App for Kids is approved!

For some reason I've neglected to toot my proverbial horn and announce that our iPad app Melvin Says There's Monsters! is up in the App Store getting comfy and making friends. It was developed with the very hands on help of Nolan and the Premium Support crew at Appcelerator. If you haven't utilized Premium Support during crunch time, you are missing out.

Melvin is a digital storybook that features, background music, original artwork and background music,thumbnail navigation and 3d page turn effects. I owe it's original release to the straightforward work-flow and cutting-edge, well,coolness of Titanium. Here's the link in case you have kids and would like to check it out. So there you go. Oh,and before you ask,yes we will port to iPhone and Android soon as we can.

http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/melvin-says-theres-monsters/id365637687?mt=8

thanks, and code strong!

B. Gallagher,
