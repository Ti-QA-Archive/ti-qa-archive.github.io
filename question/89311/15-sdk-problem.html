    * Go to your [appname]/build/[iphone] directory, and delete the contents. (for android, replace iphone with android above)
* Titanium should now do a clean build with from scratch.

  • I had the same Problem.

    I solved it this way: Create a new project in Titanium and copy your Resources-directory and your tiapp.xml in the new project directory (overwrite).

    You may want to edit the tiapp.xml with the new orientationmode-settings (see new KitchenSink).

    That solved it for me (with 4 projects).

    Hope that helps!
