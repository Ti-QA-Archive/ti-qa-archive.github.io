Many modules in the market are broken - no way of knowing!

Many modules were broken when Titanium changed its api at 1.8 adding a new check in the module manifest. There is no way of knowing which apis a module supports without downloading (mainly paying) and then finding out. This is clumsy and actually unaccpetable situation. Have other people faced this?

I currently unable to use cloudkit that I paid for. Cloudkit say they are unable to make the fix without something from Titanium..

I also can't use the Bugsense module I downloaded.

This is the typical message I get.

[code]The 'apiversion' for 'com.droisys.bugsense' in the module manifest is not a valid value[/code]

One of the reasons I see Appcelerator as mainly to blame for this is that going from 1.7 to 1.8 should not have broken modules in the market. At the 2.0 I could have accepted it more but only if there was a clear recognition of the issue and an upgrade path.

Is there anyone who can point out whats going on with this either from the staff end or community.

— asked April 27th 2012 by Matt Bourne
