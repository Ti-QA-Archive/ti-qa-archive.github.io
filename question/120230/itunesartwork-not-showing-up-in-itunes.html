iTunesArtwork not showing up in iTunes

Icon on the device is ok, artwork shows ok in Organizer in XCode. The file is png, filename "iTunesArtwork" without extension, it's in Resources/iphone/. But when I import the .ipa into iTunes - no icon, no artwork. What I'm doing wrong? Or icons/artworks supposed to show up only after submitting the app to the AppStore?
