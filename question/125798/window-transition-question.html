Window Transition Question

I am using FLIP transition between windows. However during the transition the background is black… Is there such a property such that during the transition I show a background image that isn't bound to a view or window?

— asked September 20th 2011 by Bill Bixby

3 Answers

  • Accepted Answer

    Ti.UI.setBackgroundImage('YOUR_IMAGE_PATH');

    This sets the main application window's background image. Try setting this before using the transition.
