  • At least I was not able to submit my new Version to the Apple App Store.

    Cannt passed validation from archive

    Phone/iPod Touch: application executable is missing a required architecture.

    At least one of the following architecture(s) must be present: armv6

    Unable to validate your application.

    I searched a the web and found
    http://developer.apple.com/library/ios/#qa/qa1623/_index.html

    I also tryed to set the Info.plist manually from the root Titanium Project and add:

    <key>UIRequiredDeviceCapabilities</key> 
    <array>
        <string>armv6</string>   
        <string>armv7</string>    
    </array>

    Still error on validation … fuck! :-)

    I saw that Titanium uses the 4.0 iOS Target as default…
    Then my last way was to change the builder.py in the sdk 2.1.3 RC to 4.3

    Just modified the LINE 1097 from
    min_ver = 4.0
    to
    min_ver = 4.3

    I deleted the UIRequiredDeviceCapabilities Parameters in my Info.plist and build new

    -> All worked fine !

    -> The app has successfully past the Validation and was submitted to the appstore.

    Hope this Problem will be solved in the official Realese from the Titanium SDK.
    Or was it a problem by myself?! :-) Dont know but i am happy now :-)
