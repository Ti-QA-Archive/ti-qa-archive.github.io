    Penetration of 1.5 is pretty low currently - with 1.6 having about 1/3 of the Android market currently.

    I'd like the option of having more of the 2+ APIs available within Ti - bring the whole Android API stack much more current. While we can currently target 2+ in the emulator I don't think it does anything more than specify which emulator image to run.

    There's a couple of additions to 2.2 that I'd like to see - parent visibility events being one of them.
