  • Try to create your toolbar like this

    var toolbar = Titanium.UI.createToolbar({
    items:[flexSpace,FOO,flexSpace,BAR,flexSpace],
    bottom:0,
    barColor:'#000'
});
win.add(toolbar);

    Then you can use the show() and hide() in your event listeners.
