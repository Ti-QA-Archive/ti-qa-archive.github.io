  • For anyone who's interested:
    In the update to 3.2 most windows became heavyweight by default. heavyweight windows crash the app when a nfc tag is discovered. the solution for us was to use
    &lt;property name=&quot;ti.android.useLegacyWindow&quot; type=&quot;bool&quot;&gt;true&lt;&#x2F;property&gt;
    to get the old behavior back.
