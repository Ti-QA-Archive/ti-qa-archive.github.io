Control size problem in WVGA800 density

Hi,
when testing my app under Android Emulator in different densities, it seems to work fine except in WVGA800.
I use "dp" as suggested instead of pixels but the controls go outside the right part of the screen, I mean it doesn't fit the screen in width, it seems bigger. For instance I use a picker with width = "126dp", on other densities it's about 1/3 of the screen width, while in WVGA it's about 2/3 width.
I use 1.8.1
Is it a known issue ? how to solve this

Regards

  • Have you specified anyDenisity: false in tiapp.xml ? This may cause your controls to look bigger in WVGA800 emulator.
