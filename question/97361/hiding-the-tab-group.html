  • Ashok

    If you don't want to use a tabGroup on either platforms, then simply remove tabgroups from your code. iOS does not require the use of tabGroups, from what I know.

    Try commenting out all of your app.js code, and inserting this at the top to confirm:

    var window = Ti.UI.createWindow({  
    backgroundColor:'red'
});

var view = Ti.UI.createView({
    backgroundColor:"blue"
});

var label = Ti.UI.createLabel({
    backgroundColor: "white",
    text:"This is a label"
});

window.add(view);
view.add(label);
window.open();
