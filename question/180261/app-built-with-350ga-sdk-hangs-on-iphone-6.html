App built with 3.5.0.GA SDK hangs on iPhone 6

A build that works fine on an iPod touch 5th gen (iOS 8.1) hangs upon startup on an iPhone 6 with the same iOS version (8.1). This just started happening after I updated my Ti SDK to 3.5.0.GA. Anyone else encounter freezes after this SDK update?
