iOS - Local Notification - determine if the notification is received in the foreground or not

With remote notification Titanium provides this so you know if the notification is received in the foreground or not. Is there a way to do so for the local notification?

Here is my sample code:

var notification = Ti.App.iOS.scheduleLocalNotification({
    alertAction:'Check',
    alertBody:'This is a test',
    userInfo:{id:100},
    date:new Date(new Date().getTime() + 3000)
});
Ti.App.iOS.addEventListener('notification',function(e){
    Ti.API.info(JSON.stringify(e.userInfo));
    notification.cancel();
});

Whether app is launched from the background by clicking the notification, or the notification is received when app is in the foreground, the event is always fired, with same event properties. How can I determine if it's received in the foreground or not?

Tested on iOS7, SDK 3.3.0.
