show 2 columns in tableview

I want to show a table which has 2 columns and data in this column is fetched from SQLite.
How do this??

    You could achieve it by adding a custom view to tableviewrow. Something like below in Alloy View.

    < TableViewRow >
    <View layout="horizontal">
        <Label text="Cell1" width="50%" />
        <Label text="Cell2" width="50%" />
    </View>
</TableViewRow>
