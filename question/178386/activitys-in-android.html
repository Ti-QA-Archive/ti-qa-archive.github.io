  • You do not need to specify activities in the tiapp.xml. It will be done for you automatically as part of the compilation process. The only reason you'd add activity notations to the tiapp.xml is if you want to customize themes or make other such changes. See the Maintaining a Custom AndroidManifest.xml guide for the proper steps to follow for doing so.
