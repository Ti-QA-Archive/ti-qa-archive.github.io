Android 4.0.0+ animation on a view with rounded corners causes crash

Not sure if its a bug, or me doing something stupidly, but running this little demo snippet I made on the emulator shows the animation happen, and then Android causes a crash, with no error report.

var win = Ti.UI.currentWindow;
var txtBtnView=Ti.UI.createView({
    width:100,backgroundColor:'#16a085',
    height:140,left:0,top:80,borderRadius:10
});

win.add(txtBtnView);
var slideLeft=Ti.UI.createAnimation({
            left:20,
            duration:300
    });
txtBtnView.animate(slideLeft);
— asked May 2nd 2013 by George L
