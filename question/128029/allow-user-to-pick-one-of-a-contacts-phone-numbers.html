Allow user to pick one of a contact's phone numbers

Hello,

I currently display a contact picker on the iPhone via Titanium.Contacts.showContacts which enables me, once the user picks a contact, to retreive eg. the first mobile number of the selected contact:

Titanium.Contacts.showContacts({  
    selectedPerson: function(e){
        Ti.API.info("First mobile #: " + e.person.mobile[0]);
    }
});

However it is not rare that contacts have multiple phone numbers associated with them.

a) How can I show the contact picker and let the user choose one of the contact's phone numbers ? (like in the sms contact picker for instance)

b) How do I access the label for this phone number (ie. 'home', 'mobile', 'mobile 2' etc.) ?

Many thanks in advance for your help.

PS: iPhone-only solution acceptable

    • Thanks actually digging down a little bit more all I had to do was:

      Titanium.Contacts.showContacts({  
    selectedProperty: function(e){
        Ti.API.info("Selected phone #: " + e.value);
        Ti.API.info("Type of phone #: "+ e.label);
    },
    fields: ['phone']
});

      Thanks!
