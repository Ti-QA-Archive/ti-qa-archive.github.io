    Yes you can. Absolutely.

    You will (of course) see it in the middle of a black screen, but it will work just as well. One tip if you ever want to test on devices without having to pay for an iPhone (and a subscription plan to go with it) You can always by an iPod touch, it runs the exact same version of iOS and it does pretty much everything an iPhone can do. (except phone calls and SMS)

    That's what I'm using and it's working out pretty well so far.
