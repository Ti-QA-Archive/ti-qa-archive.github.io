  • I got a tip from someone at stackoverflow (since I asked there too). I tried telnet sgr203.apple.com 44001 in terminal.app and it made us realized that my Mac Display was connected to an Ethernet at the same time the MacBook Air was also connected to a Wifi. So we unhooked the Ethernet from the Display and we uploaded just via Wifi and it worked!

    I hope this helps for anyone who gets these "failed to open ssh sessions. (16)" since it seems that not everyone has the same situations.
