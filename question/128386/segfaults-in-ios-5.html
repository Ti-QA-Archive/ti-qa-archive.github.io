Segfaults in iOS 5

Our app works fine on iOS 4 devices, but segfaults like crazy on iOS 5 devices. I don't think these are application errors, but some sort of issue with Titanium's memory management.

On a hunch, I went through the source code of the XCode project generated by Appcelerator and replaced every single "release" with a "retain" and deleted a few which this wouldn't work for. This fixed all of the crashes I had been getting, meaning some sort of Titanium/iOS 5 conflict is leading to free's which shouldn't be happening, but obviously this isn't something I'm going to use as a production fix (and the hackish nature of the experiment led to a couple of different issues).

Has anyone encountered anything similar, and are there any general things to look for to fix my app? I even tried copy/pasting all of my JS into a single file after reading something about multiple js instances causing an issue but that made no difference.

Thanks,
Dan
