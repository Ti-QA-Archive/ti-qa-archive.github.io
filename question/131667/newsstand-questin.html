  • Newsstand has its own special way of downloading content in the background. As far as I know this would require a module to be written in the current version of Titanium. You could possibly do it by making a project in Titanium and then modifying it in Xcode itself. I suspect some form of newsstand implementation will come after the Ti 2.0 SDK until then it will require a fair bit of custom coding.
