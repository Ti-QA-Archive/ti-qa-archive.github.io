On Android I get the message

and on iOS I get

I have all of the Android SDKs installed, the 5.0 is obsolete so I don't understand why Titanium is trying to use it, as for the iOS SDK I don't understand what changed it, I'm not sure if the Xcode update or if the Titanium Studio update ruined it.
