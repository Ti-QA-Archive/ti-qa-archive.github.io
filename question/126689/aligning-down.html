  • It works fine for me:

    var win = Ti.UI.createWindow({
    backgroundColor: '#000'
});

var button = Ti.UI.createButton({
    title: 'Bottom',
    bottom: 0
});

win.add(button);

win.open();

    I use 1.73, Android 2.2

    You should provide your environment and simple use-case that demonstrates the issue.
