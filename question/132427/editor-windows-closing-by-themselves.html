Editor windows closing by themselves.

I have a problem all of a sudden where my editor windows close automatically. Sometimes 1 or 2, sometimes all of them at once, so I have to keep reopening them. Sometimes the file I'm editing suddenly jumps around a bit. It's not lack of sleep I'm sure of it. I've checked the preferences setting and it's not set to autoclose. But even then it would close the oldest one I assume, not all of them at once.
