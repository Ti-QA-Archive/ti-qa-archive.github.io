  • I can certainly appreciate your frustration when stuff isn't working. KS is sort of the goto app for answering a lot of developer questions. This Q&A blog-forum is primarily for User-to-User. The Appcelerator team will randomly pop in to reply to something, but in the current incarnation of their business plan, Premium or Enterprise Support is the defacto way to get guaranteed 24-48 hour customer support.

    One's feeling about if that is cool or not aside, I can tell you that Premium support has been a Godsend for me. While I was freaking out two weeks ago to get my first iPad app up on the store, the guys were elbow deep in helping me with code and keeping me from jumping out a window. The few issues that remain, they work through to get to usable solutions. And often it was more like a few hours than 48. If you have a project that 'needs' to get out, Premium support is well worth the monthly or annual fee that you will pay. Beats the hell out of hitting your head on the desk wondering why something isn't doing what it is supposed to.

    • launch your app. It took 3 attempts to launch, but then it worked fine
