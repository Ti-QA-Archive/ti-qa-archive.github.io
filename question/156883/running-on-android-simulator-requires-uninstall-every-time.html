Running on Android Simulator Requires Uninstall Every Time

Any change I make to my app, however small, seems to require me to uninstall the app off of the Android simulator before I can launch the app again with the changes.

I get this error:

[ERROR] Failed installing com.myapp.app:   pkg: /data/local/tmp/app.apk
Failure [INSTALL_FAILED_DEXOPT]

I had already tried a few things from looking around for answers, which may have changed my error message. (can't recall, but I think I was getting INSUFFICIENT_STORAGE before)

The change that I had made was to update tiapp.xml and add:
(however note that I had to change installLocation to "auto" because the app would not launch at all with the "preferExternal" option)

<android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
    <tool-api-level>8</tool-api-level>
    <manifest android:installLocation="auto"> 
        <uses-sdk android:minSdkVersion="7" />
        <supports-screens android:anyDensity="false"
        />
    </manifest>
</android>

How can I go about solving this? It takes a long time already to launch the app into the Android simulator without having to also uninstall it first. I'd love to fix that. Thanks!
