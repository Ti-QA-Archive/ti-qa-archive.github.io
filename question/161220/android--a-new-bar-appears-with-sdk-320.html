    • You can add the following to the appropriate section of your tiapp.xml file:

      <application android:theme="@android:style/Theme.Holo.NoActionBar"/>

  <manifest>

    <uses-sdk android:minSdkVersion="14" android:targetSdkVersion="15"/>
    <application android:theme="@android:style/Theme.Holo.NoActionBar"/>
  </manifest>
</android>
