backgroundImage shows up in iPad-simulator but not on device

I'm developing an iPad-application.

Strangely I can't see background-pictures I use in the application when I deploy to a device. On the other hand they show up fine in the iPad simulator.

Here is an example:

var button2 = Titanium.UI.createButton({
    width: 344,
    height: 291,
    backgroundImage : '../iphone/bilder/ui.png',
    backgroundSelectedImage: '../iphone/bilder/ui.png',
});
