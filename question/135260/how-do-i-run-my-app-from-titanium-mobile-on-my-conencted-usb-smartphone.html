How do I run my App from Titanium Mobile on my conencted USB-Smartphone?

I connected my Samsung Galaxy S via USB to a macbook running OSX 10.7 and set Debugging-Mode on my device.

./adb devices is given me:

List of devices attached
0123456789ABCDEF device

So I guess my smartphone is detected correctly.

What do I have to do to built&run my app from Titanium Mobile directly on the connected android device?

  • First of all go to your device in

    Settings->Applications and enable Unknown sources

    Then go to

    Settings->Applications->Development and enable USB debugging

    Connect you phone to the computer, and in Ti Studio run this:

    https://wiki.appcelerator.org/download/attachments/27590663/android_deploy.png

    Anyway here you have better info
