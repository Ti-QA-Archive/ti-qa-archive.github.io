WebView on BlackBerry 10 - app crashes

I'm making a BlackBerry 10 app using Titanium Studio 3.0.0.201211301903. I have a very simple web-view code that makes the app crash as soon as it starts.

The app compiles just fine.

Following is the code that fails:

var win = Ti.UI.createWindow();
var webview = Ti.UI.createWebView({
html: '<html><body><p>Here is my paragraph</p></body></html>'
});
win.add(webview);
win.open();

If I show a simple Label, the app just opens and shows just fine.

Am I missing something? Do I have to add any permissions or change any settings? I'm testing the app on BlackBerry 10 Simulator (latest downloaded just a while back)

I'm using Windows 7 as dev environment btw.

Any help is highly appreciated.
