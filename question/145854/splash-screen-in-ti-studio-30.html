    Hope it helps

  • I have had this problem as well, both on a new app and on one that has been in the App Store for over a year

    One workaround I have found that works is to rename your Default.png (that is the only one that seems to have a problem) to anything else. And then rename it back … seems to get picked up at that point. But this does not last … and you have to do it again.
