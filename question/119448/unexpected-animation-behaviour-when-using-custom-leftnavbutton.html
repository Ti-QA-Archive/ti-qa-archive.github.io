Unexpected animation behaviour when using custom leftNavButton

Please check the kitchensink app in order to understand what I mean:

Base UI -> Window NavBar -> Back button BG

Now push the "BG nav" button at the top and notice that the navbar will not be animated any longer.
Normally I would expect the backbutton and the Title to slide to the right and fade out, but that doesn't work as soon as leftNavButton is set to something.

Does anybody know how to make it work as expected?

  • resolved in below solution,but I don't recommend this…

    Open your TiUIWindow.m from terminal or text editor,

    [controller.navigationItem setLeftBarButtonItem:[proxy barButtonItem] animated:animated];

    change Line831 like this.

    [controller.navigationItem setLeftBarButtonItem:[proxy barButtonItem] animated:false];

    I wished this for your help.
