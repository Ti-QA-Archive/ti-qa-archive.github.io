Ad Hoc Provisioning Profile on Apple

Hello everyone,

I want to create an Add Hoc Provissioning Profile to distribute my app to my clients. But it asks me to specify all the devices. Can I give the app and the profile to someone even if i have not included their device in the profile ?

On the Apple's developer site says that selecting a device is optional.

  • Accepted Answer

    You must always specify the devices to include in an adhoc build. This is to prevent you from just creating an App outside of App Store and distributing it yourself. Note you can only distribute to up to 100 devices in one year
