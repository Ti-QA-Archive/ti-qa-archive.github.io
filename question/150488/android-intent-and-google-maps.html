    I cannot understand why you would not use the built in support for Google Maps v2 in Titanium when the user already has a Google Android handset with Google Maps on it.

    You have the full API to play with, annotations and events - seems strange to simply push them to another map, just in case they have a different map.

    If you had to, you would offer this as an option to the map support YOUR app provides.
