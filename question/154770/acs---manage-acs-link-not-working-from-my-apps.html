For a couple of days now I haven't been able to manage my ACS app data from: https://my.appcelerator.com/apps - it was working fine before. Clicking the "Manage ACS" link results in an error page: "We're sorry, but something went wrong. We've been notified about this issue and we'll take a look at it shortly.". The app itself is responding to the cloud API calls though. At first I wasn't getting any error, just a hang and then timeout.

I tried starting a new session in a different browser (IE) and instead of getting the error page, I get the appcelerator login page. In both sessions/browsers, the target url is: https://cloud.appcelerator.com/apps/APP_ID.

Is it just me or is there a known issue at the moment? I looked on the appcelerator twitter feed but didn't see anything.
