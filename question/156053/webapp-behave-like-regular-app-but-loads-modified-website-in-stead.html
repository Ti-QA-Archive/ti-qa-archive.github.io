  • Hello,

    1. If you build a Mobile App, you can use any webservices. Check this. You need to do them by yourself, or use ACS. YOu won't be able to actually connect directly to your server's mysql within the mobile app.
    2. If you want to do the icon thing, you don't need appcelerator. You can do that from iOS' safari.
    3. You can just create a mobile app that only includes a webview and just open your site from the app. Which is not the best option for your customers/users, but it's your decision to make.
