Titanium.network.online and iPhone

I am checking for internet connection using Titanium.Network.online, however my test iPhones (4s, 5s, 6) all report this as true when cellular data is turned on, of which they have none. The devices show a valid network connection even though they aren't tied to any carrier, they were bought standalone. If I try to open safari an alert pops up saying "Could not activate the cellular network". This means my iPhones will not hit my internet check. Is there any way around this?
