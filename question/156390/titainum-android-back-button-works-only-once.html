Titainum Android Back Button works only once

Hi Guys, Actually I have app.js, a.js and b.js 3 files and each of them have a window. Now in app.js say I have button1 and button2, when clicked takes to a.js and b.js respectively. The Problem is when I click button1 and navigate to a.js from app.js , clicking on back button I come back to app.js, again clicking on button2 and navigate to b.js and again clicking back button doesn't show app.js instead shows blank screen. That means, the back button is working only once in navigation between 3 windows. Why is this happening?
I tested the app in Emulator as well as device both seems to trouble the same!
I use this way to call a back button in a.js and b.js to navigate back to app.js :

window1.addEventListener('android:back',function(){
        window1.close();
        require('app');
    });
