  • You need different sized versions of your image in the following locations:

    • Resources/android/images/low/maps.png
    • Resources/android/images/medium/maps.png
    • Resources/android/images/high/maps.png

    Then you point to them like this (notice the lack of ".." in the path):

    var b = Ti.UI.createButton({
        image:'/images/maps.png',  // NOTICE no ".."
        left:10,
        top:20
    })
