  • Accepted Answer

    If it was an Apple Itunes App that was rejected, it was most likely for not meeting App StoreReview Guidelines section 9. Media content, specifically 9.4:

    Video streaming content over a cellular network longer than 10 minutes must use HTTP Live Streaming and include a baseline 64 kbps audio-only HTTP Live stream
