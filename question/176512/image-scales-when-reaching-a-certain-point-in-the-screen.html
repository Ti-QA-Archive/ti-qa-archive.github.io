Image Scales when reaching a certain point in the screen

Can you someone suggest a tip to do the following apps that I am making. The scenario is that scroller view consisting of 7 images (buttons) and when one image (button) reaches a certain point in the screen (say middle portion) the image scales bigger then goes back to original size when scrolling down, then the next image scales big when it reaches the same point (middle). It is like a scroller view in the main front menu with images. Thanks.

reference: Ti 3.2.3.GA, Mac Mavericks, App for both Android and IOS.

    Hi

    You need to look at a 2DMatrix Transform. Sounds way scarier than it is.

    You detect when you need the event to occur and then apply the transform. Given your need, you would continue to monitor as it enters, moves through and exits your virtual region. You would apply a transform on each movement and adjust the value needed to make your images expand. Best bet would be the scale property. The value of scale would increase as it moves to centre and decrease as it moves out.
