  • The "App Badge" in some android devices is the count of unread received push notifications; There's no support for this in an native android device; There's no way to control the bagde like as in iOS.

    • Within Android app you can "clear" all pending notifications or "mark as read" specifics notifications, but the bagde counter can not be changed without it being directly related to the notifications received.
