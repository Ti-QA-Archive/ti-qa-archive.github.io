Split rightNavButton and have two different addEventListener

Hello,

I was wondering if it is possible to split a rightNavButton into two parts (Left|right) and have two different addEventListener associated with either part depending which side of the rightNavButton was selected. Is this possible?

Thanks in advance

  • Accepted Answer

    Use a buttonbar instead of a button when setting the rightNavButton.

    var buttonbar = Titanium.UI.createButtonBar({
    labels:['one', 'two'],
    style:Titanium.UI.iPhone.SystemButtonStyle.BAR
});

buttonbar.addEventListener('click', function(e){
    alert(e.index);
});

win.rightNavButton = buttonbar;
