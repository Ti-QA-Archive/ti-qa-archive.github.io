  • The built in Android emulator to be honest, is a little less than reliable.

    Unfortunately from this I can't see why your emulator is failing. A lot more info is required, i.e. what OS are you etc please see the guidelines.

    Also when asking a question please take your time to provide as much info as possible and go through the checklist on the page where you ask the question to make sure you have provided the require info. You will get a much quicker and more helpful response.

    Now having said that have a look at geny motion, it is a usable Android emulator and with a little work, you will be able to get it up and running. There is also support for this emulator coming in version 3.2 of the titanium SDK (hopefully). Here is a guide on setting it up
