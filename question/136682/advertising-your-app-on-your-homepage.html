Advertising your app on your homepage

Hi,
Here is a tiny script that I built to use in a existing website, to notify visitors from mobile phones that there is an App Available.

The idea is to notify users with native looking alert message, and let them choose either keep looking at the site, or open up the App Store (either Apple or Google, depending on which platform they are) to buy the app.

Yes, I'm using evil UA sniffing, but in this scenario, I don't think there is any harm done, if someone either doesn't get the message because of a changed UA, or gets the message at the wrong time. In this scenario users don't care, techy people do :)

Any suggestions, comments, welcome :).
https://github.com/justnorris/App-Nagger

p.s. It's written in CoffeeScript, but you have a JS version available there as well
