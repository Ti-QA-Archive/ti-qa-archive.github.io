  • Accepted Answer

    To answer the one question that you actually asked, Titanium Studio requires you to login every 24-48 hours in order to authenticate you for any modules used within your apps. We understand that not everyone will have constant net access, but a single login every other day hardly constitutes requiring constant internet access.

    Even though there are no other questions in this particular thread, I will say that I've noticed that in previous threads you seem to have possibly found bugs or working issues, yet I don't see any tickets or issues filed by you. Nor do I see a CLA, PR, or any other form of contribution with your name on it. You've used the board to successfully rant about Titanium's shortcomings, yet you have done nothing to help identify and resolve the issues. As with any open source project, we rely on the community just as much as the community relies on us. Bugs can't be fixed unless they are brought to the attention of the Platform team dealt with properly. We rely heavily on our community for this. I guarantee that if you contribute your knowledge to the project, you'll get just as much out as you put in.
