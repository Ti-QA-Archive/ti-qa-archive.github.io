Best method for showing a 3D object/model on iOS and Android?

What's currently the best way to go about loading and showing 3D objects or models within a Titanium app?

Some options off the top of my head:

  1. Webview using three.js (I suspect this would be slow)

  2. Using Platino from Lanica

  3. Using the old ti.opengl - but not sure it exists for Android

    A webview with three.js will probably not be as slow as you fear. Give it a try. It's probably your best cross-platform option. Also check out WebGL and Canvas support on your target platforms and OS versions. Old Android versions will have issues but newer versions and iOS should be fine. This might give you additional options beyond three.js.

    Platino would probably do a great job, though their software is not free. The old ti.opengl is unsupported, probably won't work on iOS7 or 8, and I'm not sure about an Android version (never had need to use it so I don't know).

    Beyond that, you could look for native 3D viewers (iOS & Android libraries) and use them as the basis for extension modules. I did a very quick search and didn't find any existing Titanium modules, though they may exist.
