Click events from Required views in Scrollview

How would I get the click events to fire from &lt;Require &#x2F;&gt; views when inside a scrollview?

Take the following code:

scrollview.xml

<ScrollView id="content" dataCollection="properties">
    <Require src="property.row" />
</ScrollView>

property.row.xml

<View id="row" class="row" className="property">
    <View id="button" />
</View>

property.row.js

$.button.addEventListener('click', function() {
    alert('This never fires');
})
