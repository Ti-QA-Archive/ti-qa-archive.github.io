iOS Notification Badge

Hi,
Recently I came across a scenario in which I want to display the app badge when the iOS notification style is none. I have changed the style to None and left everything on as in this link.
As far as I know, notifications will not be displayed when we keep the style as "None". But when I was referring to other applications like Facebook, I saw that the iOS app badge has been displayed even when I moved the notification style to none. In order to test this, I sent some push notifications to my application in the following format

{"badge":"+1","alert":"Have a nice day","sound":"default"}

Using the Cloud.PushNotifications.notify method. But unfortunately app badge was not incremented as I expected. It displayed only when I changed the style to banner/dialog. I checked the push notification log in the ACS server and I found that the notification has delivered in all the cases.

  1. Can anybody tell me what I am doing wrong here?

  2. Why this is working in apps like Facebook even when I changed the style to none?
