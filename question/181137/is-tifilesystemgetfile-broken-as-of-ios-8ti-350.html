It used to be I could getFile(filename) for filename in the same directory as app.js (i.e., Resources).

But now, at least in the iOS 8 simulator, I have to getFile(Ti.Filesystem.resourceDirectory + filename), which seems contradictory to the default behaviour in:

http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/latest/#!/api/Titanium.Filesystem-property-resourcesDirectory

Note that getFile(filename) still works just fine in latest Android (for a change).
