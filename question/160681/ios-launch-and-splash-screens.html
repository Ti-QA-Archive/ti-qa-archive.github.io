  • Perhaps the best way to handle this is to use Fokke Zandbergen's excellent TiCons tool.

    http://ticons.fokkezb.nl/

    Upload your full-res graphics, click a button, get a zip file with all the properly sized graphics ready to copy to your project.

    You can see the details of what you need and where you must put the graphics in our guides. But frankly, Fokke's site is a lot easier.
