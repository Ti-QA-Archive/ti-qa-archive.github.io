Controls not appearing on camera overlay

Hi,

I'm building an app for iPad that does video recording and playback and I'm trying to create a custom overlay that adds things like a pause/resume button, a time display and a cancel button. It starts with a view that's placed at the top of the screen and the adds controls to that view. The problem is that only some of the controls actually appear (the timer label does not) and of those, not all send to their listeners. The basic code is taken directly from the Kitchen Sink "record_video.js" example. Is there some trick to this?

  • Got tired of waiting for an answer here so, after doing other things for the last month and change, I decided to get back to this problem today:

    I discovered – almost by accident – that the controls on the video overlay don't like dimensions expressed in terms of percent! If you specify your top, left, width and height in terms of pixels, all is well. This is a PITA for apps that incorporate orientation change but it can be worked around.
