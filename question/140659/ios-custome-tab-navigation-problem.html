  • Kind of a tough situation. I don't know of a way to change the loading direction for opening sub pages in a TabGroup or NavigationGroup, like when opening with Ti.Ui.currentTab.open(newWin);. And if you open with newWin.open(); it will load the new window on top of any previous window or nav/tab/group, including any other views or controls.

    zIndex won't help you, as I think it only applies to controls within the same container, such as a window or view.

    My thought would be to build your own navigation controller, and use views as windows that are positioned off screen when opened and then animate into view. Better yet, you may want to reconsider your need for right-to-left animation. Left-to-right is standard behavior for iPhone apps.
