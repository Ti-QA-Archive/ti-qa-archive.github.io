  • You have to create a form with these 5 fields and then send it to a mailer on your webserver which sends the mail to the receipient.

    • It is something like this:
      In Titanium:

      var client = Titanium.Network.createHTTPClient();
client.open('GET', "http://www.example.com/sendmail.php?formfield1="+formfield1+"&formfield2="+formfield2+"&formfiel3="+formfield3+"&formfield4="+formfield4+"&formfield5="+formfield5, true);
client.send();

      In your sendmail.php:

      <?php
mail("your@address.tld", "subject", $_GET["formfield1"].$_GET["formfield1"].$_GET["formfield2"].$_GET["formfield3"].$_GET["formfield4"].$_GET["formfield5"]);

      Please do not use exactly this code as it doesn't urlencode the formfield and doesn't check for email injections. But this is the way it works.
