Android Huge Navbar

Hi,
I'm running on the Android Simulator and the first window has a huge "navbar", here is a screenshot: http://cl.ly/Al92

I've tried:

navBarHidden = true;
fullscreen = true;

And it doesn't change anything.
My app is based on a tabGroup layout, and the first window is add like this:

var tab = Titanium.UI.createTab({
    window:win
});
var tabGroup = Titanium.UI.createTabGroup({});
tabGroup.addTab(tab);

    Thats not a navbar, its a tabbar with just one tab that has no title or icon set. If you dont wish to have a tabbar, then simply open a window rather then using a tabgroup.
