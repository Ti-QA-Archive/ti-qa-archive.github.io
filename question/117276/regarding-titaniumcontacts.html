Regarding Titanium.Contacts

What I would like to do is press a button called "select contact" and it let them pick from their contacts, but only ones that have a value for SMS.

Is this possible with the Titanium.Contacts part of the API, and if so, does anyone have a working example?

Thank you,
Ben

  • You could likely do this by doing a Titanium.Contacts.getAllPeople() call, then filtering the list based on your criteria, in this case whether or not they have a value for SMS. You could display these results in a TableView and then perform your additional operations after selection.

    One thing to note is that Android cannot add/edit contacts via the core Titanium.Contacts API, but you can do it by using Android Intents. Check out the "Forging Titanium Episode 9: Android Intent Cookbook" screencast to see exactly how to do this.
