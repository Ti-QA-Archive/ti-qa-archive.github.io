  • From time to time, several trials are needed so that Titanium Studio and Android emulator get sync.

    Quit the Android emulator and relaunch your app (repeat about 5 times if necessary). If it still fails: quit the emulator AND Titanium Studio, relaunch Titanium Studio and try again).

    In the end, you will make it :-)
