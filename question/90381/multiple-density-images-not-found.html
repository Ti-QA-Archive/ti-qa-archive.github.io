Multiple density images not found

to use high & medium density images for android.

/Resources
/Resources/android

    The guide is wrong – it should be ..&#x2F;images&#x2F;ic_menu_add.png or &#x2F;images&#x2F;ic_menu_add.png. I've notified the powers-that-be (in terms of documentation), and I'm told that it'll be updated tomorrow.

    But then you also ran into this bug, which is why it still didn't display:

    https://appcelerator.lighthouseapp.com/projects/32238/tickets/2627

    That's been fixed in our master branch. I'm not sure if you're familiar with getting our continuous builds? If you are – or if you would like to try it out – visit our continuous build server and get an updated mobilesdk.
