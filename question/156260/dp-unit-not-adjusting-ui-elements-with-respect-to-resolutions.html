  • You could check the width:

    WIDTH = Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformWidth / Ti.Platform.displayCaps.logicalDensityFactor;
HEIGHT = Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformHeight / Ti.Platform.displayCaps.logicalDensityFactor;

    and see if the element is outside and place it back inside.

    Or change the way you position your elements. Use left for left side elements and right for right aligned elements, so they won't get out of the screen. Its hard to say what would help if you don't say how you place your elements
