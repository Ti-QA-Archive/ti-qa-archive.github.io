Missing: Android Y offset in tableView scroll listener

I've implemented an automatic paging capability when scrolling to the bottom of a tableView control in iOS by adopting the dynamic scroll method in the Kitchen Sink project.

Turns out that this method is not compatible with android due to lack of Y offset information in the scroll listener of the tableview control.

Are there any plans to incorporate this event property in Android, or is there a similar auto-paging method that can be incorporated on Android devices?
