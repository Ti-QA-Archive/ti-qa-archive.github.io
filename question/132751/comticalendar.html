  • It has to work.

    1. Tiapp.xml :
    <modules>
    <module platform="iphone" version="0.1">ti.com.calendar</module>
</modules>

    1. Depending on how your installation is done you need to copy the unzipped module in (this is done I see)

      /Library/Application Support/Titanium/modules/iphone/

      or 

      ~/Library/Application\ Support/Titanium/modules/iphone/

    2. Make a clean in your project

    3. Compile again
