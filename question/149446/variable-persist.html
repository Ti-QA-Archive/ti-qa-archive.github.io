  • The best way (in your case), would be to uses custom properties that you assing when creating the window.

    ####win1.js

    var win2 = Ti.UI.createWindow({
    url:'win2.js',
    customProperty: 'It can be anythig you want.',
    customProperty2: 'You can even have many.'
});

win2.open();

    And then access those same properties in the next file.

    ####win2.js

    var win = Ti.UI.currentWindow;

Ti.API.info(win.customProperty);
Ti.API.info(win.customProperty2);
