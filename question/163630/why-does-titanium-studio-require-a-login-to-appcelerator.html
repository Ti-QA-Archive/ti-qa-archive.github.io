Why does Titanium Studio require a login to Appcelerator?

I understand that both the Titanium SDK and Titanium Studio are open source. What I don't understand is the reason behind the need to login with an Appcelerator account to start using Titanium Studio.
Could anyone enlighten me?

  • If in the future, you purchase a module through the marketplace, it is associated with your account and your app id. In order for that association to take place, your app id and your account must be associated. For that to occur, you need an account.
