App rejected - non public API/s: visibleBounds

Hi,

My app just got rejected on the following grounds:

"We found the following non-public API/s in your app: ??visibleBounds"

Has anyone else encountered this problem?? I'm using Titanium Studio build: 2.1.2.201208301612 and SDK 2.1.4.GA

If I do a string search on the built files, I can see that "visibleBounds" is being used in TiUIScrollableView.m, TiUIScrollableView.h and TiUIScrollView.m.

I found this similar Q&A from about 1 year ago regarding "Layout".
http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/128036/app-rejection-non-public-api-layout-api

Or could it be that Apple just updated their screening of reserved names?

Thanks!
