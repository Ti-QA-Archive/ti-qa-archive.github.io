Restrict orientation for splash screen to landscape in iOS

Hi , I am new to appcelerator. I am trying to restrict the app orientation to landscape only . I need my
splash screen to open in landscape by default. I tried with this
<iphone>
<orientations device="ipad">
<orientation>Ti.UI.LANDSCAPE_LEFT</orientation>
<orientation>Ti.UI.LANDSCAPE_RIGHT</orientation>
</orientations>
</iphone>
I have put this in tiapp.xml in the tag <ios></ios> after the <plist> tag. But it is not working. Am i missing any thing . Kindly help . Thanks in advance.

  • Try this in tiapp.xml:

    <ios>
   <plist>
        <dict>
        <key>UISupportedInterfaceOrientations~ipad</key>
             <array>
                    <string>UIInterfaceOrientationLandscapeLeft</string>
                    <string>UIInterfaceOrientationLandscapeRight</string>
             </array>
          </dict>
  </plist>
</ios>

  • Hi

    I have this working in an app right now with a landscape splash screen;

    <?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<ti:app xmlns:ti="http://ti.appcelerator.org">
    <id>com.somone.something</id>
    <name>Something</name>
...
    <iphone>
        <orientations device="iphone">
            <orientation>Ti.UI.LANDSCAPE_LEFT</orientation>
            <orientation>Ti.UI.LANDSCAPE_RIGHT</orientation>
        </orientations>
        <orientations device="ipad">
            <orientation>Ti.UI.LANDSCAPE_LEFT</orientation>
            <orientation>Ti.UI.LANDSCAPE_RIGHT</orientation>
        </orientations>
    </iphone>
...
</ti:app>

    There is an ios block, this app does not use it so I have shown the level at which it appears so you know where to place it. Remove any other properties that aim to achieve the same result.

    No special changes needed with the images themselves. The default ones that appear with a new app work fine, should you wish to create a new test app to confirm.
