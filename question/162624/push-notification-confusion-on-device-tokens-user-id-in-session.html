Push Notification confusion on Device Tokens, User ID, In session.

Hi I have been reading the documentation on push notifications and I am very confused and was hoping to get some clarification. Here is what I need to do.

In my app a user needs to be able to send a message(custom object) to another user. The two users know each other from Facebook. The approach that I was taking was fairly straight forward. I would pull user1 list of Facebook friends and then he would send a push notification to a channel named after user2 Facebook id.

This approach doesn't seem to be working. Reason being that I need user2 user_id or device token to send him a push notification.

So my question is. How am I supposed to get user2 user_id or device_token? Must I use the Friend object?

Also I don't clearly get the difference between device_token method or user_id method. They talk about the second one having to be in-session but don't explain what in-session means. Does that mean that the app must be on? If the app is in background on iOS is that out of session? I want users to get notifications no matter what.

Thanks for your help in advance.
