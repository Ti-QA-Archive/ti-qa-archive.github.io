Event Not Firing

Howdy.

Could you give me a clue as to why clicking on the image in this (admittedly bizarre) scenario isn't firing the event? I have sneaking suspicion it is related to zindex, but I can't seem to make it work.

When I take out the middle view it works fine, but I can't get it to work in embedded views.


var nowWin = Titanium.UI.currentWindow;

var nowView = Ti.UI.createView({        
  width:'auto',
  height:2000,
  top:0,
  left:0,
  layout:'vertical'
});

var perfView = Titanium.UI.createView({
  height:55,
  width:"100%",
  left:5
});

var projectThumb = Titanium.UI.createImageView({
  image:"http://s3.amazonaws.com/FringeUser/p_758_t_642430.jpg",
  width:45,
  height:45,
  left:5,
  borderColor:'black',
  borderWidth:1
});     

projectThumb.addEventListener('click', function(e){
  alert("hello");
});

perfView.add(projectThumb);
nowView.add(perfView);      
nowWin.add(nowView)

  • There're 2 ways to make it work:

    • Change nowView width other than auto:
    var nowView = Ti.UI.createView({        
  width:'100%',
  height:2000,
  top:0,
  left:0,
  layout:'vertical'
});
    • Or change perfView width to a fixed value:
      var perfView = Titanium.UI.createView({
height:55,
width:100,
left:5
});
