Button Bar will refresh

I have a button bar which has 2 options, 'Standard' and 'Metric'.
When you click one of the buttons, the visibility of the button bar will turn into false then it will perform a certain function. Now, I want to create a refresh button. Once I click the refresh, the button bar should not retain the previous value, meaning, the button bar will be unselected again to perform new operation.

  • If index or UI setting doesn't solve your problem here you can :
    customview or window has buttonbar :

    ** initialize part **
    var buttonbar = TI.UI.createButtonBar({… your settings …});
    customview.add(buttonbar);

    ** behavior declaretions *
    buttonbar -> any button -> onclick -> customview.remove(buttonbar);

    refreshbutton -> onclick -> customview.add(buttonbar);
