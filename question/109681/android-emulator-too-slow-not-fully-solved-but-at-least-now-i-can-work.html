  • Alberto

    If you are going to install a product without following the documentation, then you are bound to have trouble.

    I am surprised Titanium even worked at all, if you didn't have python instsalled, as it is a prerequisite.

    Note that Android 1.5 is not supported by Titanium.

    The time it takes to boot an Android emulator or launch an app to an emulator is almost entirely dependent on Google's SDK. However, with this in mind and to give you an example for comparison, on my Ubuntu (i7 processor, 6GB memory) machine it takes 40 seconds to launch an application to an already-booted emulator, as this video video demonstrates.

    What is the specification of your machine (processor make and model, and amount of RAM)? What OS are you using?

    Cheers
