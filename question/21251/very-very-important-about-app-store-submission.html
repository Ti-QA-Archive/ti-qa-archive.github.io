VERY VERY IMPORTANT about app store submission

4.0 SDK Beta will not compile your application for app store. I had to downgrade to 3.2.2 to get it to work. iTunes connect complained about a invalid certificate but the real problem was 4.0 SDK beta not compiling the app with the appropriate certificate.

Be very careful using 4.0 Beta
