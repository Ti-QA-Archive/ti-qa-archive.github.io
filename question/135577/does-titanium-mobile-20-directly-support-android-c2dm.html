    No, c2dm is not supported directly in the sdk.
    With the new ACS stuff you can use the appcelerator cloud to send pushes to android/ios, besides the alternatives you mentioned.

    http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/2.0/index.html#!/api/Titanium.Cloud.PushNotifications
