    • There's no magic "local to global" function like some other languages. However, the function I showed you, convertPointToView will work for you. Let's say your window is "$.win" and your view is "$.btn":

      // untested code, but you'll at least get the idea
$.btn.convertPointToView({x:0,y:0},$.win),

      The x & y are the coordinates inside $.btn…. aka the upper left corner. You can provide alternative x/y coordinates if you like.

      — commented March 9th 2015 by Shannon Hicks

    • That said, $.btn must be an active child (or sub-child, sub-sub-child, etc) of $.win
