Making a kioskbrowser with Ti

Hi!

On tablets:

Is it possible to have an app start automatically on bootup, and take over the device, so that you can't use the tablet for anything else?

The app does not have to be crossplatform, so either a solution for Android or a solution for iPad would be great.

I can't imagine that the iPad is very friendly towards such an app, but with Android you could make an homescreen replacement.

Is it possible to make an Android homescreen replacement with Ti?

  • Don't know of a way to do that programmatically, but since it's a Kiosk, simply not providing access to the physical 'Home' button and not including a 'close' button in your app might do what you need. On iOS, you can turn 'auto-lock' on the device to 'Never' and Android you can have the screen not go to sleep.
