Hi,

I have a custom toolbar in my iphone application and now i want to create a button out of only an image (like the info-button), but with my own icon-image.

But when set the image it's always distorted, see image:

I have the idea it has something to do my application being in landscape-mode and therefore having a smaller toolbar, but whatever dimensions the image is, it is always distorted (i've tried everything between 24px in height and 17px in height).
