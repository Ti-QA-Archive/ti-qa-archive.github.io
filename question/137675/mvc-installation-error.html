  • Your android emulator is full. You need to delete apps off of it to make space for new ones. On the emulator, go to the home screen and press the menu button. From there, go to Settings -> Application -> Manage Applications and then start uninstalling apps you don't need. After that you'll be able to deploy more apps to your emulator.
