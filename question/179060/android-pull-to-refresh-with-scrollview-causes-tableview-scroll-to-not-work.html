Android pull to refresh with scrollview causes tableview scroll to not work

I have implemented tableview pull to refresh on Android by putting my tableview within a scrollview. It works great, except for one thing..

The scrollview causes the tableview scroll to not work. I guess it "hijacks" the scroll event. I can make the scroll work again by setting the scrollviews canCancelEvents to false. However, the pull to refresh doesn't work.

How can i make both pull to refresh and scrolling in the tableview work at the same time?
