Modal window only open on first launch, how to open on activate?

At the end of my app.js, after I open the main tab group, I call for a modal window to display welcome info/branding:

 wIntro.open({
 modal : true,
 ipad_modal_presentation_style : "UIModalPresentationFormSheet",
 ipad_modal_transition_style : "UIModalTransitionStyleCoverVertical",
 navBarHidden : true
 });

However, this only opens when the initial launch of the app on the iPad. Returning to the app later by tapping its icon does not open it again. Although in many cases this is perfectly what I want, how would I set it to open whenever the app is opened or re-activated?

SDK 1.7.2, Os 10.7, Studio 1.0, iPad 4.x
