  • Which RSS demo are you referring to? There are two in KitchenSink.

    One under Platform > XML RSS

    And another at Mashups > RSS (which I wrote)

    They both list all the items in each of the available RSS feeds.

    Notice the line : 

    for (var c=0; c < items.length; c++){
...

    and

    for (var c=0; c < itemList.length; c++){
...

    in each demo.

    What is the URL of the RSS feed you are trying to parse? It's possible the limitation is set by the RSS provider.
