iOS Device displays splash screen with 1px white line at bottom

  • App Type: mobile
  • Titanium SDK: 3.0.1.201212181159
  • Platform: iOS 5.0.1
  • Device: iPhone 4S

I've noticed something really strange - when I deploy my app to my device, the slash screen displays a white line along the bottom with a height of 1px (maybe 1pt, but looks like 1px to me) Anyway, I can't seem to find the source of the problem. It works perfectly fine in the iOS simulator.

I also noticed that on my device, some icons are getting cut off by 1 pixel at the bottom as well. Is this a known bug?

  • Ok, here's what I believe is the answer to this. It's a very obscure problem! So much so, that the iOS development team overlooked it - the problem is with iOS itself 5.1 apparently. Here's the solution: http://stackoverflow.com/questions/9707084/on-xcode-4-3-1-4e1019-launch-screen-default-png-etc-has-white-line-1-pt-tal

    Incase that ever gets removed:

    (Answered by iccir on StackOverflow)

    This is the same issue as: iOS 5.1 : -[UIColor colorWithPatternImage:] background color draws solid black

Basically: iOS 5.1 fails to decode the last line of pixels in an indexed-color PNG (PNG8) or any grayscale PNG.

The workaround is to use PNG32, or add dummy lines to the end of the PNG8.

I filed a bug with Apple (#11069678) and advise you to do the same :)
