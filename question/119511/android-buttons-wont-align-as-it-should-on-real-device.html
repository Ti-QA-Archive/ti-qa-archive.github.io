Android buttons won't align as it should on real device!!

I have a couple of buttons that are positioned in the center of a main window. Once I ran it using the emulator, its positioned correctly as it should. But once installed on a real device, the buttons are no longer centered. Whats happening here?
API 2.3 HVGA screen - emulator
Real device - Nexus S

    You can't hardcode values for screens that are different sizes and expect it to work. You need to adjust your controls based on the screen and orientation of the device. There are many topics regarding layout in Q&A around this problem.
