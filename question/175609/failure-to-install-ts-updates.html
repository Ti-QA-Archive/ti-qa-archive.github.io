    • Tx again Fazlul.

      I would note that none of the comments really nail this issue. And I note further that the topic occurs many times, over an extended period of time.

      The solution to the problem I encountered (failure to accept the user's Mac password during the update sequence) is as follows:

      1. Ensure you are running as the administrator. Not just any administrator, but THE administrator of the machine you are installing on. i.e. the first declared administrator and typically the owner of the machine.
      2. Ensure you have write privileges on usr/local/bin/
