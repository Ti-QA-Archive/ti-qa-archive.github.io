      The loader:

      
var loader = Ti.UI.createActivityIndicator({
    color:'#fff',

    borderRadius:10,

      you are trying to remove a webview that is not attached to it's parent. While in iOs is not a big deal, in Android it will give you headaches. Try to remove it only if is attached, but this you will figure out by yourself how to do it :)
