  • Calling Number() on Titanium.Platform.displayCaps.platformWidth shouldn't really be a factor, but it's also not necessary.

    Also, judging by your exception log, it looks like this is happening when you set the dimensions of an ImageView. Perhaps that will help you track down what component may be causing the issue.
