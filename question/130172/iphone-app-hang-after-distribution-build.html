  • Hello Frank,

    1. The build for distribution is for device (arm). The one for simulator is for your machine (x86, likely). So, this should differ.
    2. Your module should be updated, you can't mix modules between 1.7.5 and 1.8, so please go to the marketplace or the helpdesk and grab the latest module for 1.8.

    Best,

    Mauro
