Sliding a view from beyond the screen

I want to have a view (a vertical menu bar) slide in from the right from beyond the screen upon a button press. The whole screen thus shifts to the left (shifting out the left part of the regular screen). I do this by setting the menu bar position to just beyond the right of the screen and having the whole view (inside the main window) animate() to the left. This goes good and well on iOS, but not so on Android.

It seems the view doesn't get layed out until after animate(). I.e. it gets layed out just as much as it will fit on screen. If I set view.left to -5px just before the animate, I will see five extra pixels being animated onto the screen, but not any more.

How do you solve something like this? I have seen at least one Android-native solution (http://stackoverflow.com/questions/2554871/android-how-to-position-view-off-screen), but how would you do that with Titatnium?
