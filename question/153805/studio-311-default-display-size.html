    Hi

    In regard to your display setting choice, I think your are suffering from a tweak/improvement over the old way (I know only just swapped before).

    Back in the days of Ti SDK 3.1 you were able to set the default device resolution for all iOS apps. This meant you could stop doing the weird dance with simulator to swap resolutions. Major improvement.

    However ere was a better plan they did not have enough time to get for Ti SDK 3.1 and instead have put it in Ti SDK 3.1.1, now you can control the device resolution on an app by app basis - great if you juggle a few apps at at once (like me).

    All you do now is;

    • from the project explorer
    • select the run menu
    • (green circle - white play button)
    • choose run configurations
    • on the top right a new choose appears for the resolution
    • set your preference

    As I say you can now have different apps with different settings, much better - but telling people would also have been good that it changed.
