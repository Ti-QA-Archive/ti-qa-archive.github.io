  • Hi

    This sounds more about your internal network than anything on the device or code. When you say "normal wifi", do you mean when the physical device is using wifi or when you are outside your office wifi?

    It might be that your office network is;

    • Not very good
    • Rate limited
    • Resource limited
    • Stressed

    However you should look at this as a good thing. Now you can make sure your app handles failures gracefully. A good app should NEVER assume all will work as if often doesn't - although usually outside the office rather than in it. So your testing can now test real-world issues.
