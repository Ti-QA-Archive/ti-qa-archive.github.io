Possible to avoid background scaling when keypad showing?

Everytime I click a textfield in my app and the keyboard pops up, my background image scales to match part of the screen not covered by the keyboard..

Any chance this can be avoided? - So that the image i just half covered by the keyboard..

I have only tried this on android so far..

This is my window..

var win_menu = Titanium.UI.createWindow({
title:'Menu',
backgroundImage: './images/bg/background.jpg'
});

Tanks!

    I think you need to set windowSoftInputMode on your window.

    var win_menu = Titanium.UI.createWindow({
    title:'Menu', 
    backgroundImage: './images/bg/background.jpg',
    windowSoftInputMode: Ti.UI.Android.SOFT_INPUT_ADJUST_PAN
});

    See also: http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/latest/#!/api/Titanium.UI.Window-property-windowSoftInputMode
