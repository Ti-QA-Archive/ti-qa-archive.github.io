Android webview open target="_blank"

When I click a link in a webview on iOS with a target="_blank" it opens in the webview. I can override this with evalJS to inject my own code. I can listen for the beforeload event and open the url in safari.

On Android this is not possible. A click on a target="_blank" link just opens a new web browser activity. I want to intercept this and open in my own webview or open in the system browser with navigation abilities.
Doing evalJS doesn't work on remote websites on android. The load and beforeload events are not called either.

Can I intercept the loading of the webpage and load the url in my own webview?
