How to troubleshoot app on device?

Hi,
On Xcode when you build on device you can troubleshoot the app within the console. How can i do that with Titanium? It's very difficult without troubleshooting

  • Once installed on your device, keep it plugged in via USB and open the XCode 'Organizer'. (Go to Window > Organizer in XCode).

    Then you should see your connected device in the list on the left.

    Select the 'Console' option below it and now you should be able to see the debug/info/log from your device, the same as the output/log console in Titanium.
