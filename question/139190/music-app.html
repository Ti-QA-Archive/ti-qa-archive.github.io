    Just open your config.js and change songpath to: 

    songsPath: 'http://www.whatever.com/mp3/',        //audio files

    Then change the Songs to:

    SONGS : [
        {title: 'Your Title 1', file: '01.mp3'},    ///located in your mp3 folder on your domain.

    Dont forget your iTunes links.

    Works for me!
