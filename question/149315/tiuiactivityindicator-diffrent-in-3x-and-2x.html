Ti.UI.ActivityIndicator diffrent in 3.x and 2.x

In Titanium mobile 2.x the basic ActivityIndicator have a mask view, but in 3.x just a loading image and text.

Is it only to do this by custom widget in 3.x?

    Hi Chang Wang ,

    try to put a "view" below the "activity indicator" : 

    
var actIndView = Ti.UI.createView({
    height:175,
    width : 150 ,
    backgroundColor:'#000',
    opacity:0.60
})    

var actInd = Titanium.UI.createActivityIndicator({
    color    :'white',
    style : Ti.UI.ActivityIndicatorStyle.BIG,
    message    :'Attendere...',
});

actIndView.add(actInd)

    and try to add the actIndView to your window.
