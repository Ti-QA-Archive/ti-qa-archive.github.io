  • You have 2 ways of solution:

    • using of webview, in this case you can google, bing or osm
    • using of native Titanium mapview with annotations. The images of annotations must be a local png. You can prebuild all pngs with i.e. convert (imagemagick) or try to use a adhoc png builder such pnglets
