Open Settings via URLScheme

I believe you can now do this is in iOS8 via:
[[UIApplication sharedApplication] openURL:[NSURL URLWithString:UIApplicationOpenSettingsURLString]];

This will open to the Settings>Privacy tab of your app.

Is there an equivalent approach to doing this in Titanium.

I was thinking something like: Ti.Platform.openURL('prefs://');

  • Should have looked a little harder before posting, found it and it works perfect in iOS8:

    var settingsURL = Ti.App.iOS.applicationOpenSettingsURL;
    if (Ti.Platform.canOpenURL(settingsURL)) {
    Ti.Platform.openURL(settingsURL);
    }
