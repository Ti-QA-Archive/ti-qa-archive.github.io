Hi,

Can anyone tell me how to turn off the infuriating habit of Studio to put double quotes every time i type just one? (same applies to braces, brackets etc)

    Go into preferences => select "java" from the sidebar => select "editor" from the sub category => select "typing" from that sub category

    You will now see checkboxes for auto closing element, just uncheck the ones you dont want and click apply
