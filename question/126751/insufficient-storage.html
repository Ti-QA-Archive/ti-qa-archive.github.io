Insufficient Storage?

I am getting the error INSTALL_FAILED_INSUFFICIENT_STORAGE, but my phone has like 56m of free space. Just wondering if anyone knew how much storage Ti needs to install an app (mine is only about 15m). If I delete the app and get the free space around 70m it will install.

  • You usually need the size of the apk plus the unzip size of the apk. That said, it's common during development for the package manager to not actually release the space until you reboot the device. It happens to me often on my Nexus One running 2.3. I use the following command to reboot the device faster.

    adb -d reboot

  • change the Android manifest to include:
    android:installLocation="preferExternal"

    <manifest android:installLocation="preferExternal">
