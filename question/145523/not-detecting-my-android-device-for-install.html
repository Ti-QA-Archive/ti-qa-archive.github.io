I am trying to test my app on an older android phone Sprint Samsung SPH-M920. The phone is attached to my Mac computer. I can see that the phone is being charged now. But when I tried to install the app to the phone, I keep getting the following:

[TRACE] adb devices returned 0 devices/emulators
[TRACE] adb devices returned 0 devices/emulators
[TRACE] adb devices returned 0 devices/emulators

Any idea?
