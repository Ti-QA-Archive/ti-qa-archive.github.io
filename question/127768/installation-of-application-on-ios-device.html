  • I had this problem too. I am going to give you the solution that I discovered:

    You need to configure iTunes to sync application. The default setting is to NOT sync apps, but this needs to be changed. To do so:

    1. Open iTunes
    2. Connect your device
    3. Click the Apps tab
    4. Check "sync apps" (top) and "automatically sync new apps" (bottom)

    This worked for me.
