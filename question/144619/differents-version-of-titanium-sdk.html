    What I usually do is to check the Release Notes page (link on the Home page of the Appcelerator Docs)

    http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/release-notes/?version=2.1.3.GA

    From there, you can simply change the version number in the URL to the one you want to see. It's not the easiest way, but it's the best I've found so far.
