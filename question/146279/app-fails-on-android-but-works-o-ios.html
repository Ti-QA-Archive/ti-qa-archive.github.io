    • I first came across this about a year ago and it pops its ugly little head up from time to time. Has to do with the index of project files thats checked for changes. Sometimes a bad entry gets into the index and you have to delete the database. Happens more often with high ascii chars in filenames or directories, but also happens from just bad file corruption.

      It is not Titanium specific, although I've never experienced it outside of Titanium (don't really deltafy anything else).
