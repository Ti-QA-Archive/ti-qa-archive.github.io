Remove whitespace from beginning of rss feed tag

I am pulling a feed from a horrible source, but it is inevitable. Within the feed, all the tags have about 2 line breaks worth of white space. What is a good function to remove this whitespace?

Example:

<title>


        Swenson&apos;s Blog: Kansas track stars make noise at the Olympic Trials, USATF and AAU Qualifying 
</title>

Thank you!

    Easiest way:

    Add underscore.js and the understore.string extension to your project.

    From the docs:

    _("   epeli  ").chain().trim().capitalize().value()
=> "Epeli"

    You have access to trim, trimRight, trimLeft
