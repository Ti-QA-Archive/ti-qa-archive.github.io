Android Unnecessary Camera Permission

Does anyone know why the generated AndroidManifest.xml file will require the CAMERA permission while I believe that I have not use any camera related function? It is strange and I have to use custom manifest to remove that unnecessary permission.

— asked August 8th 2012 by Potato Curry

  • You have Media.showCamera somewhere in your code, perhaps just in a comment, perhaps in a module you're using. Titanium's build process detects this string and adds the CAMERA permission to your AndroidManifest.xml file.
