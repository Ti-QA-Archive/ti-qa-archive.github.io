iOS webview fullscreen video orientation

Hi

Tricky issue today on iOS, orientation locked in portrait.

I have a webview with HTML video content. When I click the play button, the regular player pops in and goes fullscreen. But when I turn my phone in landscape mode, it does not switch.
Problem is how can I lock my screen orientation in portrait everywhere in my app except for the webview videos ? As the fullscreen mode is not a classic Titanium window, I can't apply any specific orientation mode on this.

Any idea about this ? Thx !

FYI, Titanium 2.1

  • when you switch the orientation , you need the web view to reload,

    try using :

    webview.reload();

    on orientation change.
