  • I hope I have understood your question correctly.

    Titanium informs you of updates in the top right hand side of the window. The logo animates to the left with a link to download/install. (going on memory!)

    Once you installed the update you need to go into your project and edit it Update to the new version/latest SDK from the Dropdown. Save and then recompile it.

    Hope that make sense.
