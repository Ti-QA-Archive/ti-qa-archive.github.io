  • It totally depends on how your app structure and logic works.

    To give you some sort of hint, if your views have drastic difference for android and ios keep it in different directories. Same goes for styles.
    If it doesn't have major difference then use os conditional statements for view and styles.

    For controllers may be you can keep one common controller for android and ios and have conditional check in js file.

    Hope this will help you to decide the approach.
