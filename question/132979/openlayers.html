  • 1.

    var webview =Ti.UI.createWebView({url:'http://openlayers.org/dev/examples/bing-tiles.html'});
var win = Ti.UI.createWindow();
win.add.webView();
win.open();
    1. "native": often weblibraries are using DOM. In Titanium we do not use DOM elements.But you can you use bing webservice to get tiles and place in scrollView.
