    Hi

    I think you mean the short title bar at the top of your app screens and under the phones status bar on android devices.

    Edit your tiapp.xml file and change the following entry as noted below;

    // find this
<navbar-hidden>false</navbar-hidden>
// change to this
<navbar-hidden>true</navbar-hidden>

    Re-run your app, if still there clean the project and try again;

    • Studio > Project > Clean> Select App > Confirm
