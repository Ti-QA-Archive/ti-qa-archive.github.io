Button with background image vs. imageview

I have some custom button images for my application. It seems like I can implement them as either buttons with a background image or as imageviews. I can't find many noticeable differences that would make one a better option than the other. From what I can see imageviews scale the images better than button with a background image.

What other characteristics would make you choose one over the other?

  • Your about right, there is very little difference between the two. I personally don't use buttons just a standard view and theme it.

    Performance wise very little difference as both will scale the images (Better if you make the images the same size as the button as that improves performance).

    And as you say the imageview is written to handle images better than setting a background, but backgroundimage is pretty solid.
