1D Barcode Scanning

Hi there,

I need to integrate 1D barcode scanning capabilities into my app, and am curious to know what others are doing successfully in a production-grade app.

AFAIK, here are the options:

  1. Ti+Plus "ti.barcode" module. Currently supports QR codes only, despite what the Barcode Reader sales page says (https://developer.appcelerator.com/package/12/barcode-reader/commerce). If 1D barcodes are supported, they didn't work for me and I could be doing something wrong.

  2. TiBar. Their documentation states it is beta grade and not suitable for production use. I was only able to get it working in the Simulator, and not on the device itself for some reason. Also required some additional steps in XCode to properly compile.

Any suggestions on getting 1D barcode scanning to work in Titanium? I'm focusing on iOS now, in case that makes things easier.

Thanks!
Adam

  • Probably not the answer you are looking for, but in my opinion 1D scanning via the camera is only suitable for casual scanning like the occasional UPC lookup. I need fast, error-free scanning for point-of-sale so can't have users framing the barcode in an image view.

    I settled on the Linea Pro scanners which required me to write an objC module to interface with their SDK. But it gives me very fast, reliable laser scanning, and a credit card swipe too.

    I also dabbled with TiBar back at SDK 1.4.x and got it to work on a device, but I don't remember having to jump through any extra hoops. But camera based 1D scanning just isn't up to snuff for POS use, IMHO. Cameras do much better at QR codes than they do 1D.
