1.9 Master bugs

FYI, for some reason, when submitting to Apple using the 1.9 Master, I get an invalid icon size. Turns out you have to go into your project settings and tell it NOT to compress PNG files.

After that, it doesnt seem to detect the new version number, even though its set in the info.plist. Downgrading the mobile sdk fixes these.
