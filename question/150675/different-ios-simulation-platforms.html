  • In Ti Studio go to the TiApp Editor window (opening tiapp.xml) and down to Deployment targets. Click configure.

    Then fold out the tiny menu to iOS and from there you can choose the default simulator to use.

    • Brilliant… This certainly fixed it for me.

      @Vincenzo - In the tiapp.xml file, click "configure" under Deployment Targets. Then unfold "Platforms", then click on iOS. You'll find a dropdown for "Default Display" which will allow you to choose your default simulator resolution. Choose "retina."

      In order to launch via iPad, you'll need to launch the simulator separately from Titanium via XCode, and then change the hardware to iPad.
