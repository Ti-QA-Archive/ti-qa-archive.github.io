Content and Data call intensive Application in Titanium

I started working on a Titanium application which is Content and data call intensive application.

I need some guidance regarding the best practices to be followed when building such application. I am pretty much aware of the commonJS approach. I want to know best-practice coding to limit or eliminate memory/cacheing problems in Appcelerator. How do one approach or deal with these issues?

    A good place to start are the Codestrong 2011 Videos.

    Specifically the one by Kevin Whinnery entitled "Write Better JavaScript", this will provide a good framework for working with titanium level Javascript, Do's and Dont's, and is just a great video. Also the talk by Fred Spencer & Blain Hamon "Advanced Titanium Development for iOS", the first half of this video gives a great overview of how memory is managed on the iOS side and is necessary for a deep understanding.

    If, you find that your app is slow or leaking memory I would also highly recommend profiling your app (really you should be doing this the whole time), check the talk on "Your Apps are Leaking – Controlling Memory Leaks" by Rick Blalock.

    Generally if your very careful about what you put in your global namespace, encapsulate functionality well, and null out reference trails (this is very important), Titanium is more than capable of handling intensive data applications.
