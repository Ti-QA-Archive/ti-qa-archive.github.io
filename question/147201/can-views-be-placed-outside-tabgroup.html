  • Hi

    In short no.

    The tabs implementation in Android was simple and has not been improved much since.

    Google themselves have been moving the OS over to ActionBars and Titanium now has support for those (more features coming). Keep in mind that native ActionBars are only available on Android 3 and higher - most of the Android users are on 2.3.x though.

    What you are suggesting is more like an ActionBar check this out http://tihelp.me/?p=52 it may give you enough code to work with.
