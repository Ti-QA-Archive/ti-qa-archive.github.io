Trouble using Keychain module

I bought the Keychain module and can't seem to get it up and working with my iPad app. I am using 1.7.5 and have followed the steps on these websites:

http://titaniumtips.com/files/modules_installation.php
http://titaniumtips.com/files/modules_testing.php

Here is a snippet from my app.xml:

<modules>
    <module version="0.5">com.0x82.key.chain</module>
</modules>

After downloading the module, I placed the folder as follows:

Library/Application Support/Titanium/modules/iphone/com.0x82.key.chain/0.5

In my app.js I have the following line of code:

var keychain = require('com.0x82.key.chain');

I have ran a clean build, restarted Titanium, and even restarted my laptop (running OS 10.6.8).

Any idea what I'm doing wrong?

    Try to put on your project kind of:

    &#x2F;Documents&#x2F;Titanium Studio Workspace&#x2F;projectName&#x2F;modules&#x2F;......
