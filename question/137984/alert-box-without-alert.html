Alert box without "Alert"

Is it possible to display an alert box without the word "Alert" or to write another word instead?

  • Accepted Answer

    Titanium.UI.createAlertDialog({
 title: 'Not an Alert',
 message: 'Nothing to see here, move along people.'
}).show();
