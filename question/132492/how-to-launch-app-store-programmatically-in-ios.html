How to launch App store programmatically in iOS

Hi.. I am developing a mobile app for Android and iOS. In a certain flow of this app I have to launch Market (in case of Android platform) or App Store (in case of iOS).

I was able to tackle this problem for Androind Platform. I was able to open the market and show my app using the following code.

Ti.Platform.openURL('market://details?id=com.myapp');

I would like to know if there is a similar way to launch App store for iOS platform. Thanks!!

  • The link shoould look something like this:

    http:&#x2F;&#x2F;itunes.apple.com&#x2F;dk&#x2F;app&#x2F;nordjyske-busplaner&#x2F;id411751364?mt=8

    So you would do like this, for opening directly in App store on device:

    Ti.Platform.openURL(&#39;http:&#x2F;&#x2F;itunes.apple.com&#x2F;dk&#x2F;app&#x2F;nordjyske-busplaner&#x2F;id411751364?mt=8);

    Apple has provided a "iTunes Link Maker" to help create the correct link, for more information on that, have look over here and have a look at "iTunes Link Maker" FAQ here

    Hope this helps.
