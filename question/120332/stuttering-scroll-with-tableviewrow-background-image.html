Stuttering scroll with tableviewrow background image

One of my applications was made with SDK 1.4, I had a tableview with around 20 rows and each row had the same background image. Everything worked perfect, but since SDK 1.5, when I scroll it isn't smooth, it's stuttering/lagging, and it became even worse in SDK 1.6.

I've been looking into this and if I remove my background on each row it's "OK", alot smoother. My background is only a jpg that's 8kb large, I had a png earlier but it's no difference.

Why is my application stuttering/lagging if I have a background image in my tableview row?

Tested on: iPhone 4 (4.3)
Appceleartor: SDK 1.6.2 (also tested on 1.7 a couple of days ago, not fixed)

It runs smooth in the simulator but not on my device.
