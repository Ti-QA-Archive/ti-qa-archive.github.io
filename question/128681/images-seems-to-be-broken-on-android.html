  • I agree with Trevor - on Android I have found that having backgroundColor and backgroundImage and/or image will not work (or not consistently) together - even though it might work on iPhone (or be ignored on iPhone and therefore not show you any problems). I would choose the most important of those 3 that you need. I have done something like the below to make it work for both platforms:

    backgroundColor : (isAndroid) ? null: 'lime',
...

    (Where somewhere in your code you have defined isAndroid and captured the flag appropriately)
