How do you store/cache your Data ?

After a quick look-around I found that there is a MongoDB implementation: https://github.com/euforic/monglodb

And a NoSQL Post in Appcelerator Blog:
http://developer.appcelerator.com/blog/2012/01/jsondb-nosql-db-for-your-mobile-apps.html

So my question to you is - How do you Cache/Store the data on your Applications ? Please do share your experiences!

I'm using SQLite right now, but never felt entirely happy with it.
