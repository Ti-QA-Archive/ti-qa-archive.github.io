tiConf US 2013 - Baltimore, MD 28-29 JUNE. Super early bird tickets end soon!

Hi Everyone,

tiConf, the community Titanium Developers conference, will take place in Baltimore MD from June 28 - June 29, 2013. Currently we're offering a limited number of super early bird tickets with pricing of $200 (use promo code 'RAVENS'), so if you are interested act now - there's only about 24 hours until this super early bird promotion ends. Get your tickets either from http://2013.ticonf.us or direct from EventBrite.

Also if you're looking to get into Alloy then part of the 2-day event consists of a 2.5 hour workshop on Alloy with Jason Kneen and it's creator, Tony Lukasavage. An opportunity not to be missed!

From the site:
ticonf.us is a new mobile development conference primarily based around JavaScript and Appcelerator Titanium, based on the highly successful ticonf.eu format that run in Valencia, Spain earlier in 2013. It's a 2-day, not-for-profit, community run event featuring some of the best JavaScript and Titanium Mobile developers from the US and abroad. The event is to take place between the 28th-29th of June 2013 - entry and tickets are open to all regardless of your skill level! We welcome beginners and experts alike.
