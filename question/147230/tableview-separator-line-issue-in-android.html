Tableview separator line issue in android

Tableview separator line image appears to be of uneven width in android.

  • You can disable the auto scaling and manually manage your sizes/densities by setting anyDensity to true in your tiapp.xml:

    <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
    <manifest>
        <supports-screens 
            android:anyDensity="true"
        />
    </manifest>
</android>

    I think this will solve your issue.
