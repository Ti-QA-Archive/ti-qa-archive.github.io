Android Bar

Hello,
i nee a bit of help with my android application.
On the top of the Screen you always see the Titanium Defaultimage (appicon) and the app name. Unfortunately i don't know the exact name of that UI Element but i hope you will get what i mean.
I want to hide the whole "Titlebar", but don't know how.
Thanks for your help!
BTW: it's Titanium 3.4.1

    This is called action bar and you can hide it by using window's activity.

    var theActionBar = win.activity.actionBar;
theActionBar.hide();

    do that on window's open method.
