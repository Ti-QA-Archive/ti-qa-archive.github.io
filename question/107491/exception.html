  • ButtonBar is an iOS-only control, and so isn't available natively in Android. Thus, Titanium does not provide it either. You can determine this yourself by observing the "apple" icon in the top right hand corner of the Ti.UI.ButtonBar page. To compare, as both icons are shown on the Ti.UI.Button page, the simple button control is available for both platforms.

    Hope this clarifies it.
