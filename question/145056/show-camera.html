  • You can do this in iOs but not in android.

    
    Titanium.Media.showCamera({
         allowEditing:false,
        saveToPhotoGallery:false,
        //Options for ios when customizing camera.
        showControls: false, //iOS only
        autohide:false, //iOS only
    overlay: yourButtonsView //This is your own view that will be seen on top when loading the camera
        success:function(event)
        {
            //stuff
        },
    })

    Use the takePicture and hideCamera methods to use the camera directly.

  • Hi

    You can, but you have to create the interface yourself. Using showCamera, setting showControls to false as well as setting overlay.

    Then you assign the methods takePicture and hideCamera to your own buttons.
