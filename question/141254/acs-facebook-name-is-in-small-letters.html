ACS Facebook name is in small letters?

Hello.

I am giving my users a chance of logging into my app with their Facebook account. This works fine and I am retrieving their firstname and lastname just fine.

However, the firstname and lastname is in small letters.

Is there a way for me to somehow make the firstletter .toUpperCase(); so that it will spelled, for example: "James Petersen" and not "james petersen".

Thanks!
This is for iPhone.

    Hi Nicolai,

    Refer below Q&A

    http://stackoverflow.com/questions/5122402/javascript-jquery-uppercase-first-letter-of-variable

    var str = "hello world";
str = str.toLowerCase().replace(/\b[a-z]/g, function(letter) {
    return letter.toUpperCase();
});
alert(str); //Displays "Hello World"

    Hope this helps you
