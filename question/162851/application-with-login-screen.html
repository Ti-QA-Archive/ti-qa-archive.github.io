  • this is terrible practice. You either use normal windows or a tabgroup.

    My suggestion would be to open your login window after you loaded the tabgroup.

    Example: load a modal window on top of the other (hidden) windows in the tabgroup. After you login, you hide the modal window and show the tabgroup.
