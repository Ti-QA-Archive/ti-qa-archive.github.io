Using createUUID for identifing a device

I am trying to identify the mobile device using Titanium.Platform.createUUID().

The ID of the mobile device should be used for authenticating/identifing itself on a webservice.

Running on the simulator or on the device itself Titanium.Platform.createUUID() creates on every call another ID:

"653A61A3-611D-44CA-9508-E9870651D7AE"
"D30F9AD7-FB5E-4639-9EAB-5B85DA71760C"
"7EF4B7C6-304F-4E99-8A22-3E17A95B9E1B"
"DA5A9899-2D68-4E24-9ED2-6B3352AA3AFD"
"7F2F6BE1-3599-47D7-8C03-C8C0EB0E126D"
"6D3EE24F-A1CE-4EE5-9926-23DB52B7C297"
etc.

Is there a way to get the UDID of a device?

Thank you for your help!

ps: I am using SDK 1.8.1 and 1.8.2
