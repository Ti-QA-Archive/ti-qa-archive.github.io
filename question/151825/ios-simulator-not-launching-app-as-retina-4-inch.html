iOS Simulator not launching app as Retina 4 inch

I am not sure what changed or what went wrong with my code. It has been working fine forever and all of the sudden, no matter what, in the iOS Simulator, the simulator launches the app like if it was using Retinan 3.5 even though simulator device is set to Retina 4 inch. Weird as hell.

I have a detect that would tell me the height and based on that displayed graphics and so forth. To make sure it wasn't something in my code. I deleted everything from app.js and all I have there is the following:

  // DETECT DEVICE AND DIMENSIONS ================================
  var osname = Ti.Platform.osname,
     version = Ti.Platform.version,
      height = Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformHeight,
       width = Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformWidth;  
  // =============================================================  

  alert(height);

The simulator launches as Retina 4 inch, but when the app launches, it doesn't take up the full screen and if I do an alert on the height, it says 480.

I have reset the simulator content.

Please help. This is driving me nuts.

    Have you created a Default-568h@2x.png image (with dimensions of 640X1136 pixels) file in your Resources or Resources/iphone folders.

    Normally, it's the only thing you need to declare your app as usable on Retina 4 inch.
