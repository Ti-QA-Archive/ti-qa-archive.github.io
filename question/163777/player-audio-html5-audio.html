    • ok since now you are basically coding blind.
      The basics:
      when you add this line to your app.js (or index.js if you are using alloy) 

      var someVariable = "hello world 2";
Ti.API.info("hello world");
Ti.API.info(someVariable);

      on the console "hello world" and "hello world 2" will be printed. If you have more complex variables like an object, use 

      Ti.API.info(JSON.stringify(someVariable));

      to the concrete problem:

      i assume you added the line 

      Ti.API.info("html: "+des.html);

      after the creation of your webview.

      start the app, then click on simulator to the point, where the weview should be visible. then look at the console. Voila you'll see the output.
