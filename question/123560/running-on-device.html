Running on Device

Hi All,
I'm trying to run my first android app on a HTC android 2.2 device through Titanium Developer (not Studio). I am connecting the handset via usb to my pc and clicking "run on device". I have also installed the HTC syncing drivers so the handset is recognised.

Unfortunately nothing seems to happen after clicking Install.The app isn't installed but neither are there any notifications on the phone or through the developer interface e.g. "starting installation" or "installation failed/successful". Total nothingness. Grrr. What am I supposed to see during the installation?

Can I debug whats going wrong? Is there maybe a log somewhere?
Does anyone have any suggestions about what I may be doing wrong?

Obviously I could do it through the market store but the app isn't ready for that type of distribution.

Thanks in advance,
Lee

  • Try this (on device):

    go to Settings->Applications and check "Unknown sources"
    go to Settings->Applications->Development and check "USB debugging"

    My HTC Hero had to be connected to computer in any configuration EXCEPT "charge only" ( now is rooted and works with any connection)

    Connect again your device to the computer and try to run on device once more.
