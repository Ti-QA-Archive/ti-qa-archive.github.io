Can the geo location, gps icon on the status bar of iOS be hidden?

Hi guys, I'm making and app that gets the geo location of the device but when the app is put to run on the background the small arrow icon for the gps/geolocation still shows. Is there a way to hide that when the application is being ran on the background?

  • No. This is the way Apple lets the user know the gps is on. There is nothing you can do except closing the gps when the app is put in the background and restart when is active again.
