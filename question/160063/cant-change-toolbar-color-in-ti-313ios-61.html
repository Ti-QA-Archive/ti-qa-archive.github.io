Can't Change Toolbar Color in Ti 3.1.3/iOS 6.1

I'm updating our mobile app for iOS 6.1 and I've updated the Ti SDK to 3.1.3.

We had a toolbar that was black in colour, however, now it's the iOS 6.1 default blue and I cannot get it to change.

I've tried using window.setToolbar() and also creating the toolbar and nothing works. What am I doing wrong?

Code:

var toolbar = Ti.UI.iOS.createToolbar({
    bottom: 0,
    borderTop: true,
    borderBottom: false,
    translucent: false,
    barColor: '#000000',
    animated: false
});

mainWindow.add(toolbar);

I've also tried:

mainMenuWindow.setToolbar(loggedOutBtns, {
    animated: false,
    barColor: '#000000',
    translucent: false 
});

Incidently, the nav/title bar at the top stays blue too in tabgroups and navigation windows.

  • The cause of this was XCode 5. Even though I was compiling against iOS 6.1, XCode 5 was making it impossible to change the barColor property.

    I installed XCode 4.6 again and compiled against iOS 6.1, using the iOS 6.1 simulator and the barColor displayed black like it was supposed to.
