Deploying App Directly to Device, and not to iTunes

From what I have read in places like Titanium Studio's tutorial, it is possible to deploy an app directly to your device (iPhone in my case). But when I do a Run > iOS Device, the app is sent to iTunes. I sync my phone with a different computer and I really don't want to sync it to my dev machine.

There must be a way to deploy right to the device without a third party tool like testFlight, yeah?

Thanks!

Application type: mobile. Titanium SDK: 1.8.2. Platform & version: iOS 5.x. Device: iOS simulator. Host Operating System: OSX 10.7.3. Titanium Studio: 2.0.1.

    Yes - just use XCode. Build your project in Titanium and then open the the .xproj file located under <Your Project>/iphone/build in XCode.

    You can then provision the build using either a team certificate or an app certificate and run it directly on your device without having to use iTunes.
