Views of ScrollableView are not visible and disappear after swiping on Android

I have a ScrollableView containing 5 views with the identical structure. This is all working fine on iOS, but not on Android.

The problem is that only the first two views are visible and swiping to other views (not visible) causes all views to disappear. One weird observation is that swiping to the third view and then back causes only the first view to disappear, but as soon as I swipe to the fourth or fifth view, all of them disappear.

Basically, the ScrollableView has a specific height (the window's area below the header) and contains 5 views, which all have height: Ti.UI.FILL and width: Ti.UI.FILL. These views then contain some other labels, views and images, positioned by left and top attributes.

This clearly looks like a bug to me, but maybe I have to consider something in my code, so I am asking if anyone knows possible reasons and/or fixes?

SDK: 2.0.1, Platform: Android 2.3.4
