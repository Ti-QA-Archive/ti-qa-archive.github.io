touchstart and touchend on andorid events not firing

Hi,

the 'touchstart' and 'touchend' event's are not firing for UITableView or UITableView rows on Android. I've tried SDK version 1.7.2, 1.7.3, 1.7.5, and the nightly of 1.7.6. I've tried Android 2.1-Update and 2.3.3. It works fine on iPhone and iPad.

  • Is this a known issue?
  • Is there a workaround?
  • Is there a time frame for fixing this?

Here's how I'm adding the listeners:

var touched = false;
tableview.addEventListener('touchstart', function(e){
    Ti.API.info('touchstart');
    touched = true;
    setTimeout(function(){
        if (touched)
            alert("Long press");
    },50);
});
tableview.addEventListener('touchmove', function(e){
    Ti.API.info('touchmove');
    touched = false;
});
tableview.addEventListener('touchend', function(e){
    Ti.API.info('touchend');
    touched = false;
});
