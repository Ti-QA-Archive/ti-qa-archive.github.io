  • Not sure how you have setup your views and Windows but you might want to check this out for layout specific information http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/2.1/index.html#!/guide/Transitioning_to_the_New_UI_Layout_System

    The other item to look at is http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/2.1/index.html#!/guide/Using_density-specific_resources_on_Android. Specifically there is a section about disabling multiple density handling. It might help you in the short term to turn it on. This will scale the app automatically but you do pay for it in not allowing finer control of layout objects.
