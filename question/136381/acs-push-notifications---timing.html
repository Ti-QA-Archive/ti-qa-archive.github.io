  • Hi Judy,

    For scheduling notification, it is not support currently. Maybe we will add new feature for sending scheduled notification in the future, but it has not been discussed.

    For lost notifications, just want to remind you that, do not add "aps" into your payload json, and put "sound", "alert", "badge" into second level inner json. Because we support both iOS and Android, we will pick up "sound", "alert", "badge", "icon", "title" etc, and put them into correct place as needed. Please refer to our document for push notifications about json payload.

    Maybe you didn't meet that problem I mentioned above, just a reminder to ensure there is no format problem on payload.
