    Perhaps a partial answer… I also could not get touch events from an AdView. I tried transparent overlays, etc. I finally gave-up on touch events but I do create EventListeners for "load" and "action". "action" fires when a user has clicked an iAd and is returning to the app (or is dismissing ad via home button or closing ad). "load" fires on first ad display or when subsequent ads are displayed. I also noticed that iAds are "flakey" in that there are network requests for ads that are not filled/completed.

    Not sure this helps?
