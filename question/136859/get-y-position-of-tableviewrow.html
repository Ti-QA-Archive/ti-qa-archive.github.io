Get y position of TableViewRow?

Hi everyone!

I'm looking for a way to detect which row in a tableView is closest to the center on the screen. Basically, I need a way to read the y-axis positions of all the rows in the table. But I haven't found any way that allows me to do this. Any ideas, fellow developers?

My eventual plan is to create a table view in such a way that whenever the user stops scrolling it, the closest row to the center of the screen moves & 'slots/snaps' to the center of the screen.

I hope I have been clear enough. Please ask if any kinds of clarifications are required.

Many Thanks!

  • Hi Inder,

    try this:

    var yposition = null;
tv.addEventListener('scroll',function(e){yposition=e.});

    Hope this helps.
