  • Accepted Answer

    Here is a 2-way AES encryption CommonJS module https://gist.github.com/boydlee/5302389

    Use it to encrypt data on the way in and out of your SQLite database. The hash key should be something either randomly generated or something specific to your device (Such as the Ti.Platform.ID).

    This method is NOT foolproof, but if you want a decent data encryption mechanism, in JS, that works both ways… then you might find it useful.
