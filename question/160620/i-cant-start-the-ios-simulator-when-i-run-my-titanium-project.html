    Hello Zhang :

    Reset Simulator. In the iOS Simulator, press iOS Simulator in the menu at the top of the screen, and press 'Reset Content and Settings…'. This will clear the entire simulator. and then Clean Your Project and check again. i hope this is solved your problem thanks.
