Mobclix integration - A serious question....does Appcelerator give a damn?

I'm sorry to put this in such a blunt tone but when you look at all the Q/A questions out there it makes you wonder. Appcelerator staff must have seen the multiple questions asking if Mobclix was ever going to make it to Appcelerator.

Examples of such questions:
http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/69371/mobclix-and-appcelerator
http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/79441/using-mobclix-with-appcelerator
http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/88571/advertising-modules—modules-in-general

I'm not expecting an official response simply because it seems Appcelerator staff don't even bother checking any advertising related Q/A posts, but here goes:

WILL TITANIUM EVER SUPPORT MOBCLIX?

As I'm sure many are aware, since Google took over AdMob its turned into a pretty poor platform, with many developers having their accounts closed the day before payout, being told they are not producing apps in the AdMob target market. (I was one such victim of this shoddy practice).

Devs who have no ability to use AdMob are now at a loss…theres no other ad networks that integrate, and MobClix seems logically like the next option.

IF you arent going to add it yourself, please at least provide better docs on how to create custom modules so someone else can have a go!

Thanks for your time, and apologies if this message comes across as aggressive - I'm just frustrated that after all this time theres no progress being made on ad integration.

    Rick,
    So a couple of things.

    1) We've never stated whether or not we'd do a module for MobClix.

    2) We are an Open Source platform.

    Here are links to the Module Developer Guides:
    Module Developer Guide for Android

    Module Developer Guide for iOS

    Here is a link to Open Source Modules that we've developed:

    Open Source Modules for Titanium

    You can take the bull by the horns and contribute back by doing a MobClix module. Nothing is preventing you from doing so. The same hold's true for MobClix.

    Our resources are stretched thin and the vast majority of our Engineering resources are working on the core platform and not on modules. At least on something that wouldn't be considered a core module.
