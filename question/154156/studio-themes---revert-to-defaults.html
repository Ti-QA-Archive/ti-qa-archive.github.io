Studio themes - revert to defaults?

I modified some colors in my editor, only to find that not everything was configurable via:
Preferences->Editors->Text Editors

I could not modify keyword coloring for example, so now they are effectively invisible. I tried pressing the "Restore Defaults" button in the Text Editors preferences pane, but it has no effect on the syntax coloring. Can anyone help? Ideally I'd like to use a theme other than the default with the black background, but restoring to the defaults would at least be better than what I have now.

thanks!

    In the Titanium Studio Dashboard, you can select two default themes. Selecting one of these works for me. At least after a restart.

    Also try "Restore Defaults" in both,

    • Preferences > General > Editors ( > Text Editors )
    • Preferences > Studio > Editors

    [Apply] > [OK] > restart TiStudio
