  • Looking at the debug/trace output from the build, it appears that titanium-xml.jar isn't getting included in the references. This causes any calls to Titanium.XML.* to fail, citing "undefined".

    The temporary fix I've found is to explicitly add titanium-xml.jar as a dependency, in your framework dependency file C:\ProgramData\Titanium\mobilesdk\win32\1.5.1\android\dependency.json

    Note the addition of the "xml" and "titanium-xml.jar" entries to in following lines:

        "required": ["analytics","android","ui","json","locale","xml"],
    "libraries": 
    {
        "android":["titanium.jar","ti-commons-codec-1.3.jar","smalljs.jar","jaxen-1.1.1.jar"],
        "xml":["jaxen-1.1.1.jar","titanium-xml.jar"],
        "bump":["bump-api.jar"]
    }

    Works for me. I know it's a bit of a hack - am hoping the dependencies are fixed properly in future releases.
