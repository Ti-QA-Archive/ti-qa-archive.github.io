Ti.map module and iOS8, blank grid

No matter what I do since upgrading to 3.4 and xcode 6, my apple maps are blank with just a grid showing. I even grabbed the 2.0.5 module off of the github. Tried a new project and loaded just a simple example from scratch and get the same result. Anyone having any issues like this and been able to fix?

  • Answered my own question essentially. The iOS8 simulator that comes with xCode6 appears to have a bug in it. The simulator appears to fail on HTTP requests. I suppose apple maps is delivering data over HTTP and not HTTPS. You can verify this by trying http://google.com and https://google.com in Safari on the simulator. My maps work when installed on my device. A huge pain for testing, and was a huge waste of my time trying to track down something that wasn't actually broken. This is an apple problem
