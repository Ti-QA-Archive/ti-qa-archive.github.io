Android Device database update issue

I have just updated by Android app, however, before I release to the app store, I am trying to figure out a performance problem on a specific device.

At the start of the app, I update a local SQL database with data from ACS. On newer phones (like my HTC evo 3d) it takes about 3 seconds to startup and put the data in the database. On an original Samsung Galaxy S, it takes almost 3 minutes to do the same thing. Looking at DDMS, it takes about 3 seconds to run the insert query on the Galaxy S and fractions of a second on the EVO.

So, is there anything I can do in this case or just chalk it up to 'old phone = poor performance'…

Ray

  • Have you tried bundling up all your queries into a transaction? We work a lot with Samsung Galaxy S & have some fairly large inserts at times (about 3Mb)
