    Now, shot in the dark. Some place in the magic world of license validation, your app's generated guid is tied to your account which is also tied to your licensed modules.

    Projects created before you 'purchase' a module may not be associated. If this occurs, i usually bump the guid, clean the project and rebuild.

    If this is an app that was working with the module, clean the project, rebuild.

    If all else fails, email support.
