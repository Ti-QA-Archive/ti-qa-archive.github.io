Cant Manage Reviews on ACS Still

OK,

So this is such a major thing that I created a JIRA ticket because of it. Found here: https://jira.appcelerator.org/browse/TC-857

I have a moan publicly on twitter, and coincidentally the Appc account notify the developer community that SDK questions should be asked here, so here goes - even though someone at Appc must be aware of this…
