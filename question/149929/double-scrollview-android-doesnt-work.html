    Hello Mohamed,
    you cannot just put a scroll view inside a scroll view, as when you touch the screen, which scroll view would listen to it?
    So you have to decide:

    • If you want to make the all other screen component fixed, then you have to remove the outer scrollview and just keep the text scrollview.
    • If you want just to make all the screen to scroll, you keep the outer scrollView and remove the one for the label. And do not forget to assign the label height to Ti.UI.SIZE so it will expand to fit the text height.
