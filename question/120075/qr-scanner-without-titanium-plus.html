QR Scanner Without Titanium Plus

Hi,

I am currently planning on building a QR Scanner into an existing iPhone app. I know that Titanium Plus contains a barcode scanner module for paying Titanium customers, but I was wondering whether there are any open source solutions out there of if anyone knows of a way to implement this without a Titanium subscription.

    The most common way to do this in iOS without Ti+Plus is to use the TiBar module. Look here for suggestions on how to install it.

    Note that the first thing they say on the page is a disclaimer:

    "This is a beta version, not meant for production. We are not professional Objective-C developers so there may will be better ways to approach this."

    I have played with it a little (but only for testing) and it seems to do a pretty good job with QR. My need is for high-speed and reliability UPC/EAN scanning, and you just can't get there with any camera solution, including Ti+Plus. I use the laser scanner from Linea Pro instead, and wrote a small obj C module wrapper around their SDK so I could use it from Titanium.
