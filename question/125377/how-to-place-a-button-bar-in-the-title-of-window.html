How to Place a button bar in the title of window?

I am pretty new and sorry if my question is simple.

I want to create a button bar with three buttons and place the whole bar on the tile of the window.

Creating button bar is pretty easy but the part of placing it on the title got me. Can anybody help me a little? Thanks in advance.

    Here is an example. The important part is setTitleControl()

    var win = Titanium.UI.createWindow({
    title:'test',
    backgroundColor:'#ffffff',
    modal:true});

var bb = Titanium.UI.createButtonBar({
    labels:['One', 'Two'],
    index:0,
    backgroundColor:'red',
    style:Titanium.UI.iPhone.SystemButtonStyle.BAR
});
win.setTitleControl(bb);

win.open();

    You can also do this with a TabbedBar.
