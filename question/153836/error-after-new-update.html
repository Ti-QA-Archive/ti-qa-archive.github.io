  • This appears to be due to the /library expanded copy of the module collecting jars. It doesn't remove the previous version before unpacking the new one, hence if you have tried different versions of Jars previously, they will collect in the modules lib folder and cause problems. A manual deletion of the module when copying the zip over is needed.
