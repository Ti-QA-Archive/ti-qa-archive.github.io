Delete Cookies and Session on iOS

Hi,

I have a problem with removing the session of my webview on iOS. I want to do this because i want to log off. I login on a site with a SAML authentication. This stores 2 cookies in the webview.

When i want to logoff i delete all cookies for that domain. For Android it works and i'm logged off. I pulled that off with the Ti.Network.removeAllSystemCookies(); function. iOS doesnt have that function.

With the following code i managed to delete the cookies for iOS: 

Ti.Network.createHTTPClient().clearCookies('https://domain.ext');

Problem is, when i open an SAML secured page where i just logged off, i still can open it, because for some reason the webview holds the session/cookie.

How can i delete this sesson / cookies? How can i stop this session?!
