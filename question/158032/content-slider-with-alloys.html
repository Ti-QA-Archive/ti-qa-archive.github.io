    Hi

    Despite your lack of information it is likely the thing you are looking for is a ScrollableView.

    <Alloy>
    <Window id="win">
        <ScrollableView id="scrollableView" showPagingControl="true">
            <View id="view1" backgroundColor="#123" />
            <View id="view2" backgroundColor="#246" />
            <View id="view3" backgroundColor="#48b" />
        </ScrollableView>
    </Window>
</Alloy>

    The child view can contain anything you need per "page".
