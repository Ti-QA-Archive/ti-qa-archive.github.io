App signing for app store (Valid signing identity not found).

Hi.
Please, please can anyone help.

I'm trying to sign my app so I can put it on the app store.
I've tested okay on my device, so I've created a Distribution Provisioning Profile, and downloaded it.

When I double click on it, it adds it into Xcode, but… it says Valid signing identity not found.

Can anyone tell me what I'm doing wrong, because Im about to kill a MAC.

Cheers

    In keychain tool (Applications > Tools > Keychain), you should have an iPhone developer certificate with your name AND an iPhone Distribution certificate with your name. If not, go to your iOS Provisioning portal > Certificates and go in Distribution tab. There, you should be able to download your current Distribution certificate. Install and try again.
