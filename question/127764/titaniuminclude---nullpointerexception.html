Titanium.include - NullPointerException

Ubuntu 10.10, Titanium Studio build: 1.0.6.201110251616, API 1.7.5

Mobile project, deployment target Android (2.1)

At the beginning of app.js I have 

Titanium.include('file1.js');
Titanium.include('file2.js');

Getting such an error at second include:
E/KrollContext( 246): (kroll$1: app://app.js) [187,6219] Error evaluating source: Wrapped java.lang.NullPointerException (file:///android_asset/Resources/app.js#2)

Both files exist, first one included without problems. What could it be?
