"CodeSign build/Release-iphoneos" error prevents Packaging for Ad hoc or iTunes Store

I am having problems Packaging my app for iOS Ad hoc (or even iTunes Store). At compilation stage I get this error in Titanium Studio:

[ERROR] ** BUILD FAILED **
[ERROR] The following build commands failed:
[ERROR]         CodeSign build/Release-iphoneos/MyApp.app
[ERROR] (1 failure)

I have tried killing off all my keys and certificates and re-generating a Production Certificate and Ad hoc Provisioning Profile, but I get the same error.

I am running the most recent version of Studio and using the iOS 6.1 SDK.

It looks like the problem I am having has been reported before - has it been fixed?
https://jira.appcelerator.org/browse/TIMOB-13640?page=com.googlecode.jira-suite-utilities:transitions-summary-tabpanel

This is really critical for us, so any help very much appreciated!

Thank you for your attention

Caroline

  • When running CodeSign from the terminal, the user gets the issue of:
    "User interaction is not allowed"

    This seems to be related to login.keychain.

    When running "security unlock-keychain /Users/yannooo/Library/Keychains/login.keychain" before triggering
    the build will solve the issue.

    Solutions to how this can be solved on automatic builds are found here:

    http://stackoverflow.com/questions/577750/running-xcodebuild-from-a-forked-terminal
    and
    http://stackoverflow.com/questions/16550594/jenkins-xcode-build-works-codesign-fails
    and
    http://stackoverflow.com/questions/20205162/user-interaction-is-not-allowed-trying-to-sign-an-osx-app-using-codesign

    Hope this helps a little. :)

  • I was having the very same problem, but after looking into 50+ websites/blogs/forums, i realized that my provisioning profile was invalid (i had renewed developer certificates but not provisioning profiles) and after updating the provisioning profile at the iOS developer portal, downloaded and installed in xCode (just double click on the certificate) it worked in Titanium!

    Just be sure to select the right provisioning profile in titanium configuration, just in case you have both old (invalid) and new provisioning profiles installed, and also check that the selected certificate is included in the provisioning profile.
