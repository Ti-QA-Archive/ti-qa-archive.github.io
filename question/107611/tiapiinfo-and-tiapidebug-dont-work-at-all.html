Ti.API.info and TI.API.debug don't work at all

Ti.API.debug has never worked since I have installed Titanium.
As for Ti.API.info, it doesn't work since I have upgraded XCode to the lastest version.

In fact, I can put instructions anywhere in my application, the info is never displayed while I execute my app on the simulator.

Does anyone have an idea on how to fix this?

    To resolve this, you may need to delete your build files and force a full rebuild.

    For iOS/iphone, delete the contents of the project build&#x2F;iphone directory, select Edit Project in Titanium Developer, click the Save Changes button to force a full rebuild. Then launch the app.

    You may need to select one of the other log levels, like trace, from the Run Emulator, Filter menu.

    Let us know!
