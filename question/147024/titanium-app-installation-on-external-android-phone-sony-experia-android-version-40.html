Titanium App installation on external Android phone (Sony Experia, Android version 4.0)

Hi,
I want to install & run my titanium app on my Android phone (Sony Experia, Android version 4.0).
Could anyone please help me out through step by step.

I am using Android SDK version 2.2
Window 7 is my host machine.

  • Just right click on project in the options available, there will be option of "Install on Android device" just click on that and wait till the message "Application installed Launch from Home drawer" appears on console. then you can launch your application. Please be sure that you have installed your device drivers properly. Also do required settings in your phone.
