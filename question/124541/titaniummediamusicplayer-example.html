  • If you want to roll your own music player, use either Titanium.Media.AudioPlayer or Titanium.Media.Sound. The former will stream remote audio while the latter will not play until the entire audio file is loaded into memory. Here's the API links for both:

    http://developer.appcelerator.com/apidoc/mobile/latest/Titanium.Media.AudioPlayer-object
    http://developer.appcelerator.com/apidoc/mobile/latest/Titanium.Media.Sound-object
