Saving recorded videos to camera roll

Using Ti.Media.showCamera and the recoded video is not stored in Camera Roll. Here is the code:

Ti.Media.showCamera ({autohide:true, // saveToPhotoGallery:true,
mediaTypes:Ti.Media.MEDIA_TYPE_VIDEO, videoQuality:Ti.Media.QUALITY_HIGH});

I tried with and without saveToPhotoGallery.

Has anybody been able to save videos?

Thanks
