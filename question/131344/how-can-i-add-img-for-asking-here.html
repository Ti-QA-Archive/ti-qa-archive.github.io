    Well, first you need to upload your image on the web itself. Using any public image hosting service.

    • http://imageshack.us/
    • http://imgur.com/
    • http://picpaste.com/

    Once the image is online, simply copy the url and paste it in the markdown of your message.

    ![This is my image](http:&#x2F;&#x2F;my.image.url)

    My advice to you is to avoid the Title part, it only makes it more difficult.
