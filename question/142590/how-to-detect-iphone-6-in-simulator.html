    Check out the property Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformHeight

    If it shows 568, you're on an iPhone 5 - BUT beware: if your app is not designed for iPhone 5, it will show only 480, even on iPhone 5 (your app will have black bars on top and bottom).

    To prepare you app for iPhone 5, add a Default-568h@2x.png with 640x1136 pixels to your Resources/iphone folder and use at least SDK 2.13x
