Animate thumbImage of slider

Is there a way to animate the thumbImage of a slider such as in 

var slider = Titanium.UI.createSlider({
        min : 0,
        max : 10,
        value: 5,
        top:183,
        backgroundImage: Ti.Filesystem.resourcesDirectory + "/images/slider.png",
        thumbImage: Ti.Filesystem.resourcesDirectory + "/images/sliderMarker.png", 
        highlightedThumbImage: Ti.Filesystem.resourcesDirectory + "/images/sliderMarker.png", 
        rightTrackImage: Ti.Filesystem.resourcesDirectory + "/images/sliderTransparent.png",    
        leftTrackImage: Ti.Filesystem.resourcesDirectory + "/images/sliderTransparent.png"
    });

So something like the following but only for the thumbImage?

slider.animate({
    height:50,
    width:50,
    duration: 1000,
    background: "transparent",
    repeat: 300, //TODO: repeat 300?
    autoreverse:true,
    curve:Titanium.UI.ANIMATION_CURVE_EASE_IN_OUT
});
