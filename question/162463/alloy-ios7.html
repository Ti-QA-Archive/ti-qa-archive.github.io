    Do this in index.xml

    <Alloy>
       <NavigationWindow id="navWindow" platform="ios">
            <Window class="container" id="appCont" navBarHidden="true">
                <Button id="araLang" onClick="arClick" />
                <Button id="enLang" onClick="enClick" />
            </Window>
        </NavigationWindow>
</Alloy>

    and in index.js

    $.navWindow.open()

    and you would be good to go

    • To add to Muhammad's answer, and explain WHY it wont work,

      <Window navBarHidden="true" id="index">
        <NavigationWindow id="nav" platform="ios">

      READ the documentation. NavigationWindow CAN NOT BE THE CHILD OF ANOTHER WINDOW. The exception to this is it can be used inside a SplitWindow.
