  • Well, you can cheat a little bit by adding

    <supports-screens android:anyDensity="false"/>

    to the manifest section of your tiapp.xml file.

    REF: http://developer.appcelerator.com/blog/2011/06/new-defaults-for-android-layouts-in-1-7.html

    However, this is a really ugly bit of hacking. Images get stretched all over the place, buttons can be abnormally HUGE on larger screens. But, it can get your version 1 out the door, and then you can start working on building up your assets for the various screen sizes out there.

        <manifest>
            <supports-screens android:anyDensity="false"/>
        </manifest>
</android>
