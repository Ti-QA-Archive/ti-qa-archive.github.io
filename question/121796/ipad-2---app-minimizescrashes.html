iPad 2 - app minimizes/crashes

Hi all, I'm using Titanium Studio to develop my app. I have created an Adhoc distribution .ipa and loaded the app onto many devices. It works perfectly on iPhone4 and iPad, but when loading it up on an iPad2 for the first time, it gets past the splash screen, then right as I am about to do an XHR (XML HTTP Request) to retrieve an RSS feed from my server, it is "crashing".

By "crashing", I mean it exits out to the homescreen, but the app remains in memory. If I open the app, the XHR goes through no problem (I dont even need to take the app out of memory and reopen it). So it's almost like it is minimizing the app (putting it into the background process mode).

This doesn't happen in the simulator, or on any other device besides iPad2.

I am running Titanium 1.62 and iOS 4.33.

This problem only happens on the first ever run of the app after installing.

Any clue what might be going on?
