    Hi Venkat

    Thanks for your gist. Please make the following corrections to your system:

    The environment variable android_sdk should be C:\Program Files\Android\android-sdk-windows\ (without the "tools" suffix).

    Your PATH variable is currently:

    C:\Windows\system32;
C:\Windows;
C:\Windows\System32\Wbem;
C:\Windows\System32\WindowsPowerShell\v1.0\;
C:\Program Files\Intel\DMIX;
C:\Program Files\QuickTime\QTSystem\;
C:\Program Files\Android\android-sdk-windows\tools;
C:\Program Files\Git\cmd;
C:\Program Files\Python27;
C:\Program Files\Git\bin;
C:\Program Files\Python27\Scripts;
C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_23\bin;
C:\Program Files\Android\android-sdk-windows\platform-tools

    Change this to the following order:

    C:\Windows\system32;
C:\Windows;
C:\Windows\System32\Wbem;
C:\Windows\System32\WindowsPowerShell\v1.0\;
C:\Program Files\Intel\DMIX;
C:\Program Files\QuickTime\QTSystem\;
C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_23\bin;
C:\Program Files\Android\android-sdk-windows\tools;
C:\Program Files\Android\android-sdk-windows\platform-tools
C:\Program Files\Python27;
C:\Program Files\Python27\Scripts;
C:\Program Files\Git\cmd;
C:\Program Files\Git\bin;

    Have you followed the instructions about adb.exe at the end of the Preparing for Android development guide?

    Please take a screenshot of your android installed packages, and share it here.

    When you have resolved the above issues, and provided the information, I can help you further.

    Cheers
