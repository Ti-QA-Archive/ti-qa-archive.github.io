    • If you wish to "clip" the image, create a parent view of the size you wish and place the images inside this view, you can change the width and height of the parent view and if the "porthole" is smaller than the views inside they are clipped.

      <View class="porthole">
    <ImageView class="star" />
    <ImageView class="star-disabled" />
</View>

      Something like this - fill in your exact needs and styles of course.
