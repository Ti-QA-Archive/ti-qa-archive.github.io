    • I don't understand exactly your point.

      This is what I usually do if I need to test my app on a device and read the logs:

      • Compile it with Titanium
      • open the xcode project, Clean the project and Archive it
      • click on Share and save the file.ipa
      • Install the file.ipa through iPhone Configuration Utility or through an itms-service URL
      • Connect the Device to the USB and open the Organizer, under Device - Console.
        You will read all of the Ti.API.info and every kind of debug message from your app. No need of red screens whatsoever.
