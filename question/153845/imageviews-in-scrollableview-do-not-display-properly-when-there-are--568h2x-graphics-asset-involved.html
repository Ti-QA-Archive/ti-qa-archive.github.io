ImageViews in ScrollableView do not display properly when there are -568h@2x graphics asset involved

Hi my Appcelerator comrades,

I am using scrollableView and putting a couple imageViews in there to make a tutorial for the app. Things work fine until we start supporting iPhone 5 screensize (Titanium SDK 3.0.2 and above). There is a strange behavior happening from the SDK that we are observing. Particularly, when the scrollable view first loaded, the imageViews in there got shrunk and did not display properly. However, after a couple scroll action back and forth between the views, they magically appear correctly again. I am wondering if anybody else are seeing this issue as well. I made a Youtube video from my app to show this behavior.

Application type: mobile
Titanium SDK: 3.0.2., 3.1.0, 3.1.1
Platform & version: iOS 4.3
Device: iOS simulator, iPhone 4/4S/5
Titanium Studio: 3.1.1.201306112235

I also filed the issue report on Appcelerator JIRA, but so far noone looked at my issue yet.

  • Hi Mickey,

    From the video I saw that you didn't define height values when creating the imageViews. I would suggest that you add the height property to rule out any unforeseen results of the lack of declarations.

    Next thing I would try is to populate the scrollableView within a callback of the 'postlayout' - event of your parent view / window.

    Let me know if you succeeded.
