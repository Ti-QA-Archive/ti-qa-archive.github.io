Is it possible to make Push Notifications work in IOS Simulator?

Push notifications seem to be working fine when I run my app on a device. But same code doesn't work on a simulator.

Do I understand it correctly that there's no way to make Push Notifications work on IOS Simulator? There's no even a setting for simulator where you can assign a provisioning profile.

    Hi Artem,

    Sorry to say, No you cant test in simulator.

    Push notifications are not available in the simulator. They require a provisioning profile from iTunes Connect, and thus are required to be installed on a device. That also means you'll probably have to be accepted into the apple iPhone developer program and pay your $99.

    On the bright side, with the iPhone OS 3.0 update, you can test this functionality on any device, including the first gen iPhone.
