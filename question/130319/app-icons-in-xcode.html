App Icons in XCode

I am about to submit my first application to the Apple App store, so I am double checking everything. When I open up the project in XCode, the Summary page only shows one Launch Image, the Default.png. There is no entry for the app icon, retina app icon or retina default image.

In the iOS simulator and on my local phone, all the appicons and default images show up properly, so I am assuming Titanium is packaging everything correctly. Is there any way to double check that all the icons are packaged correctly before I submit to the App Store?

Titanium 1.8.0.1, XCode 4.2

  • Double check that the Default.png is the correct one. Almost everytime I go from Titanium to Xcode, its the Titanium Default ones.

    Delete if (if needed), then drag and drop the @2x and the icon files into the Resources directory, and check the 'Copy' on the popup dialogue.
