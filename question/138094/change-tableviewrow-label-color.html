  • Hint:

    fill the tableviewRow with a view and colorize it:

    var view = Ti.UI.createView({
    backgroundColor:'red';
})
var row = Ti.UI.createTabelViewRow();
row.add(row);
row.backgroundColor = view.backgroundColor;

    Now you can do:

    row.backgroundColor = 'blue';

    Hope this helps you
