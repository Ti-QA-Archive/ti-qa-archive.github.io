  • Hi

    I know you've already solved it. But Just wanted to point out that your modules should be local to the project you're working in. The modules should be placed in the root of your application, same location as tiapp.xml.

    If they are in the right location, then when you open your tiapp.xml inside the studio editor you will be able to find your module, whatever it may be, inside the list of available modules.

    Hope this clears it up.
