  • ListViews don't work like TableViews (or probably any of the other Views for that matter). You will never get x/y coordinates from a click on a ListView. And you might not get a bindId.

    Please read the ListView guide especially the Eventing section. You will get an e.bindId only if you tapped within the item over a subcomponent. Because ListViews aren't view-based, there are never x/y coordinates available.

    You might want to read through the ListView vs TableView section for some additional considerations that might help you understand better how ListViews work.
