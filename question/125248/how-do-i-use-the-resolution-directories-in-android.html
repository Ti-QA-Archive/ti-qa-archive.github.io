    • Gino, yes, delete the default.png in the Resources\android\images\ directory. I don't know why they even put one there…it's bad practice. I think that one will get used if it doesn't find one (or a better one) in one of the res- directories.

      Search your entire Resources directory tree for all default.png files, and make sure that they are all your own. Delete your "build" directory, clean and rebuild your project. If you still see an Appcelerator splash, post your Resources\android\images\ directory listing and tell us which screen size (and density) you're using on the emulator.
