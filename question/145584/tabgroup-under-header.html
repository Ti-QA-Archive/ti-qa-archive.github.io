    Hi

    The short answer is no, not without using a custom tab module. The android tab implementation has not really moved on from the early days and more recently is being dropped in favour of ActionBars.

    So the ability to add something outside of the tab container is just not possible as it stands.

    You will either need to create your own solution or take a look and the wide variety of custom solutions out there for Titanium in the market place and a google search.
