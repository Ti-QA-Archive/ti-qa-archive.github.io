Paint module not linking under Titanium 1.8 on Mac OsX

When last we left this problem,

I tried to compile simple mobile app.
app.js:
var paint = require('ti.paint');

Into tiapp.xml I added…
<modules><module version="1.0">ti.paint</module></modules>

It was discovered that I needed to move the paint module's contents into ~/Library/Aapplication Support/Titanium/modules. That's both the iPhone and Android subfolders.

This then permitted compile and run of the above. Not much, but a non crashing start.

Now when I try to execute ti.paint.createPaintView as in all the examples, I get a function not found error. Using the var above, paint.creatPaintView also returns same error. What gives?

Just as an aside, Appcelerator, what a wonderful job you do of installing and checking the iOS and android sdk's. Module installs should be just as easy.
