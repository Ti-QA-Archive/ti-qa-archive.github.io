Is there a nice visual animation tool for Ti?

Anyone come across an animation tool for Ti that lets you visually create your animations with nice smooth curves, paths etc. that can then be exported as Ti JS code and pasted right into your project to animate the views, images etc.?

IMHO nice animation, be it grand or subtle in design, really adds an extra level of shine to an otherwise ordinary app, e.g. like in Yahoo News Digest.

I'm thinking along the lines of animation tools like QuartzCode in terms of functionality, but that's just for vector output in iOS Obj-C and Swift.

Also, am I right in thinking Ti uses QuartzCore lib under the hood for iOS? What's used on Android?

Regards.
