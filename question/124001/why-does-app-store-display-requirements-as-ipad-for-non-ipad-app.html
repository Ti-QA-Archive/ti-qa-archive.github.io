Why does app store display requirements as iPad for non-iPad app?

I have an app in the store that is an iPhone app, yet the app store lists it as compatible with an iPad (in addition to iPhone and iPod touch, which is fine).

Also, it says OS 3.1 or higher, whereas we have found in testing our app really only works reliably in 4.0 or higher.

Where so these "requirements" come from, and is there something in my app that can control it? The app.xml already says &lt;target device=&quot;ipad&quot;&gt;false&lt;&#x2F;target&gt;.

    They are in the settings for you app upload .. usually set via iTunesConnect. The Connect verification usually decides whether or not the app will run on iPad as most iPhone apps will.
