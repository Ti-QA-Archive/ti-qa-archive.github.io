Our new Titanium-built app is ready for sale! Eyejot vCard

We're happy to report that our newest iPhone app built with Titanium is now available for sale in the Apple iTunes App Store. It's designed to create and send video business cards - allowing you to completely ditch your old, traditional paper ones.

We had a lot of fun building this app and utilized several Marketplace components. We utilized a non-standard UI to make porting to Android easier (when we get around to doing it) and took advantage of some simple animation techniques to make the app experience more fun. Thanks Appcelerator!
