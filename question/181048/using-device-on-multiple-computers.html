Using device on multiple computers

Hello.

I use Titanium at work, and at home. I use my personal iPhone to test projects for both computers.

A common issue I come across, is Titanium doesn't list my phone as a 'run as' option. This usually happens immediately after switching locations, and once I get it to show, it is no longer an issue, so I am assuming it is related to having multiple developer accounts or something.

I can usually get the device to show by restarting both the phone and the computer several times.

Is there something I can do to properly handle this? I just need Titanium to always recognize my device, regardless of where I was last developing.
