[SOLVED] Aptosid:

Error: symbol lookup error: &#x2F;usr&#x2F;lib&#x2F;libgdk-x11-2.0.so.0: undefined symbol: g_malloc_n

This solution works for Aptosid, and will likely work for other Debian-based distros as well, including Ubuntu.

Same error as http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/14471/symbol-lookup-error-usrliblibgdk-x11-20so0-undefined-symbol-gmallocn-solved, but with a slightly different solution –

cd ~/.titanium/runtime/linux/1.0.0
mkdir moved
mv libgobject-2.0.so* moved/
mv libglib-2.0.so* moved/
mv libgio-2.0.so* moved/
mv libgthread-2.0.so* moved/
— asked April 24th 2011 by Steve Phillips
