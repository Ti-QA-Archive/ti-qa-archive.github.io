How can you keep functions minimized in Titanium editor

I would like to keep all of my functions closed, (using the little - sign to the left of them) by default. I want to open them to edit them, and then leave them closed when I am done. The software keeps reopening them automatically and I would love it if someone could point me to a setting or a plugin that would fix this.

    Check the keyboard shortcuts in Help/Key Assist. You can see the keystrokes to use to collapse/expand for your platform.

    Ray
