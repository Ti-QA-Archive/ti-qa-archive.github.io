After 1.5 years my app is finally in the App Store

My new app, NextWord, was distributed to the Apple App Store 10/19/2012 after a 1.5 year development effort. It is a word association app played live in groups of users. The app makes use of asynchronous web service calls, In-App Purchases, SMS messaging, and a database-driven architecture. It is free, so please check it out at http://www.NextWordGame.com
