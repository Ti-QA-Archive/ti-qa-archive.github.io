  • My understanding is Indie may be purchased at $49/mth, but then you do not get access to the on-demand video training. Since 12 months of that would be $588, it seems to me that this is mostly only practical as a means of trying it out.

    Also remember that if you use any Ti Plus+ modules while in any subscription plan that if you do not renew, you can no longer recompile those apps unless you remove the module usage.

    I'm just a user, so don't hold me accountable as an official answer. But the above is my understanding of how it works.
