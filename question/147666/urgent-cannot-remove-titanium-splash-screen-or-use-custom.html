  • For anyone else viewing this and expecting a proper solution without changing the Titanium folders read on.

    Remove the iphone/android folders containing the your custom splash screens, copy them to the desktop / back them up.

    While not strictly necessary rebuild the app without these folders.

    Place the folders back into your app directory.

    Rebuild and enjoy your splash screens.
