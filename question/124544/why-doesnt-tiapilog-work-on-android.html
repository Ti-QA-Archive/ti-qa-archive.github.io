Why doesn't Ti.API.log work on Android?

I am near suicide at present trying to port an iPhone app over to Android, I cannot even get Ti.API.log to work. Just keeps saying it expects two arguments…

Just creating an array and then trying to log it for debugging, even tried just putting a string into it with same results.

  • That's because Titanium.API.log does in fact require 2 arguments. You need to give it a log level and a message. For example:

    Titanium.API.log(YOUR_LOG_LEVEL, 'your message');

    The shorthand methods in Titanium.API make this easier

    Titanium.API.info('your message');

    • The valid strings for the log level are the same as the names of the shorthand methods used to call log()

      • info
      • debug
      • warn
      • error
      Ti.API.info('message');

      is equivalent to

      Ti.API.log('info', 'message');
