  • Neither Jade nor Coffeescript is actually supported by Alloy. You are free to use them, but you are likely to encounter issues like this during conversion. This is not the first time I've heard of markup events not firing when someone was using a coffeescript plugin for their controllers. Here's an example of what coffeescript is doing wrong: http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/145981/datafilterdatatransform-not-work-by-using-coffeescript
