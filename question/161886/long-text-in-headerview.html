    Hi

    What you are seeing is normal; by normal I mean that is what happens - rather than it should happen that way.

    The maximum height of the header/footer during creation is the same as the available space of the list/table. You can extend it after the view has be added. You could always add event to determine that the postlayout event and then extend the height.

    However I have always made sure the header/footer sections contain small amount of information - not always possible though - I know.
