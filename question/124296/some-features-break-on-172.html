Some features break on 1.7.2

Hello. I have two Android apps complied and working properly on SDK 1.6.2. I started making changes, and unknowingly started compiling with 1.7.2 and two problems started to appear: The splash image now has a "title bar". Once inside the App, there's a part where I use a webview, and now the webview does not fit on the screen. It keeps spreading to the right as if the screen was larger. Some hours later I noticed the change in SDK and I'm now able to confirm that these two problems were "introduced" on 1.7.2.

I went through the release notes of the 1.7.2 (1.7.1 was iOS only), and can't find anything making reference to these problems.

Are these bugs? Has anything changed that I need to apply to my code.

Thanks!
