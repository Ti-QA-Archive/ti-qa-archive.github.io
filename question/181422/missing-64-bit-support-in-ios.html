    Application type:mobile,Titanium sdk:3.4.1

    You need TiSDK 3.5.0.GA or higher to build a binary with 64bit slices. All of your modules will have to have been built with 3.4.1.GA or higher and have had their projects updated. The utility mentioned by Rainer has nothing to do with actually building a 64bit compatible binary, it simply makes the checking of the modules used in your projects for 64 bit support a bit easier.

    http://www.appcelerator.com/blog/2015/01/titanium-sdk-3-5-0-ga-now-available-with-64-bit-ios-support/
