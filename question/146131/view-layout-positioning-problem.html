View layout positioning problem

Hi,
This question is may be easy for this group but I could not find out the reason why it does not behave as i expeced.

What I want is branch and accountno to be shown on the left side of the row and balance to be on the right edge. However balance also appears on the left side. My guess is appendRow cannot lay the label correctly with appendRow. Any idea?

            <Label id="accountno" left="5dp" top="40dp" height="20dp"  />
            <Label id="balance" right="5dp" top="20dp" class="bigFont"/>
        </View>
    </TableViewRow>

    Hi

    I think your problem is a simple one of not enough properties and maybe you could do with a nested layout.

    Let's try the properties one first.

    Your inner view does not have a width set so it is likely defaulting to size of contents which would explain the location you arebseeing.

    Try adding property width="Ti.UI.FILL" to the inner view and see what happens.

    Let me know what happens, if this does not resolve it I have another technique for you.
