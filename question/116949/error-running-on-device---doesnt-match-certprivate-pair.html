  • Jamie, try this:

    go to /Library/Application Support/Titanium/mobilesdk/osx/1.6.0/iphone and open the builder.py and in line 989 change

    args+=["CODE_SIGN_ENTITLEMENTS = Resources/Entitlements.plist"]

    to

    args+=["CODE_SIGN_ENTITLEMENTS=Resources/Entitlements.plist"]
