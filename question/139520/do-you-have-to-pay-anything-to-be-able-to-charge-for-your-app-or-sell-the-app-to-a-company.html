  • This is completely bogus.

    You can sell applications on the app store without having to pay a single cent to Appcelerator. (you already pay a fee to apple or Google to sell your apps on their store, so that would amount to double-dipping).

    I remember seeing a post a few weeks back with someone having the exact same experience you had. And the outcome of that was that this was more a case of shady selling practices from the person on the phone than anything else.
