Localised Date Strings - Day Of Week

Hi All,

I have been using String.formatDate and String.formatTime to generate localised string formats.

Now I have hit a limitation with String.formatDate, none of the formats {short,medium,long} include the day of week name short-name (e.g. Mon).

I had previously been using my own date formatter function but it is not localisation friendly.

In iOS it should be easy to get the localised day of week short-name using NSDateFormatter, but it seems that String.formatDate is not capable of taking custom formats.

BTW the date pickers are localised and include the day of week short-name.

Has anyone got any ideas about localised days of week?

Also has anyone encountered this with users or am I making a big deal out of nothing?
