OpenLayers inside a WebView

Hi there,

for those who are trying to get it to work with offline tiles/js:

it seems that webview prevents sublibraries from being injected in the html page <script> tag.

Solution:

-Comment/Remove "document.write(scriptTags.join(""));" in OpenLayers.js
-add manually the js you need AFTER<script type="text/javascript" src="js/libs/openlayers/OpenLayers.js"></script>

Hope it helps. I wonder why there isn't a debug tool like the webkit one for webview debugging, or I missed it ?
