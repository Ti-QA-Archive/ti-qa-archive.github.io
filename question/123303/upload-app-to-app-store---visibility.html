Upload app to app store -> Visibility

Hello

Is it possible to upload an app to the app store, but make it not visible for the people?

Let's say I want it to be published on September 1st, can I upload it, set it visible for 1 day for come people who can download it, and set it invisible again?

So to tell it short, can you set an app invisible and visible whenever you want?

    Not as easy as that, but you can select which regions of the world will see the app. If you de-select all, the app will not be visible at all (in theory). I've not tried this never, so I don't know how much time takes apple to switch from visible to unvisible and viceversa.

    Apart of this, when you publish an app, you can choose to publish the app as soon as has been approved or publish manually, when you receive the ok from apple.
