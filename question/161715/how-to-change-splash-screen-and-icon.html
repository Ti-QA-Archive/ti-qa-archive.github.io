    • As they say a picture is worth a thousand words - @Meshaal Al-Saffar - your picture would have been better yesterday.

      The "title" that you said was in the image is the app title and has to be changed for the app when shown here. If you wish to hide it then there is a setting you change in the tiapp.xml;

      <navbar-hidden>true</navbar-hidden>

      False by default - set to true.

      As @Stephen has said all this information is in the documentation - I highly recommend you review it.
