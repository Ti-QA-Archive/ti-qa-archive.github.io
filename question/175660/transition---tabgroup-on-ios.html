    This is less an answer and more a comment;

    Just because you can, doesn't mean you should

    If this is the only tabGroup in the app, then it should just be there anyway. If this is a tabGroup that appears later; then all you are doing is adding fluff without substance.

    This is MY opinion and opinions vary "if you are not adding something don't do it". This falls into the taking something away category.
