  • Accepted Answer

    Normally you need to compile a module, but the guys have put the two prebuilt zips in that repo for you. An alternative, it to visit the Marketplace and purchase, for less than $10 one of the two updated Testflight modules. I use the $9.99 one, the guy has a track record of keeping it updated. The new $8.99 one has no track record and while the idea of it using whatever testflight sdk you have downloaded, their website did not leave me with a lot of confidence.
