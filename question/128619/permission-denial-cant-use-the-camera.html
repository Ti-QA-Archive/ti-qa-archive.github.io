Permission Denial: can't use the camera

In my Titanium application I need to take pictures from camera, while invoking the camera action I am getting following error

Permission failure: android.permission.CAMERA from uid=10037 pid=407
E/CameraService(   34): Permission Denial: can't use the camera pid=407, uid=10037

I am invoking the camera by following code

Titanium.Media.showCamera({
 .....
 .....
});

Where I have to add permission to use Camera?
Anyone help me to solve this issue.

    Try adding this to your tiapp.xml file inside the <android> section. Make sure you clean out your build before building again with your new configuration.

    <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android"/>
    <manifest>
        <uses-permission android:name="android.android.permission.CAMERA"/>
    </manifest>
</android>

    • In addition make sure you have an SD Card installed on your device, and make sure if your device is plugged into your Mac/PC that it's in "Charge Only" mode, meaning the camera is free to utilize the SD Card.
