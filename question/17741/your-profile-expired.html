  • Go to your iPhone 'Settings' then 'General' and scroll to the bottom to 'Profiles'. Then delete the old one.

    Also, when you double-click the new profile to add to your xCode, you might also see the old one listed there. It's probably a good idea to select and delete this one too in xCode.
