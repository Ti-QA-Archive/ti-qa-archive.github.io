Fade in after Loading

Hi All

I have noticed that when I’m loading a tableview from my database, it can take a few seconds to load and then appears. I have a loading wheel showing whilst this happens.

Its become more obvious since I started browsing the national trust app and it loads the content is there immediately and slides into view.

So is there away to fade the table into view once loaded to make it softer. Or if I was to use a plist, would that be a faster method and show the content instantly?

Plus, any pointers on updating a database via wifi would be fantastic!
