  • It seems that either Titanium or Monkey Patch is very sensitive to ";" semicolons. I had one missing and for some reason it was crashing at a "require" file statement. The file target was missing a semicolon at the end. Keep an eye out for your semicolons!!!!

    I always thought they were optional in Java Script. It seems that they are not.
