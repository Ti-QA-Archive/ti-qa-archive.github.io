Why is anyDensity for Android being ignored in 1.7.1 & 1.7.2?

I must be doing something wrong. As per many, many posts on here the solution for high density screens is to add the following to your tiapp.xml:

    <android >
        <manifest>
            <supports-screens android:anyDensity="true" android:largeScreens="true" android:normalScreens="true" android:smallScreens="true"/>
        </manifest>
    </android>

Why then when I build my app does it completely ignore this settings? It works fine if I built the app using 1.5.1 but with 1.7.1 or 1.7.2 it completely ignores it (even though it is writing it to the manifest file).

Any ideas?
