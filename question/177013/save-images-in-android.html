  • Accepted Answer

    From the look of your code, it seems you are constantly overwriting the one image &#39;album[win.id].png&#39; in the filesystem. Maybe f should be changed to:

    var f = Ti.Filesystem.getFile(Ti.Filesystem.externalStorageDirectory, 'album['+win.id+'].png');
