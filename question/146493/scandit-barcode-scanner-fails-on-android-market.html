Scandit Barcode Scanner fails on Android Market

Scandit is a great and fast barcode scanner, used as a module for Titanium Studio. I've developed an app with Scandit. I've installed the app in two different android phones and works ok.

When you upload the app to the Android Market(Google Play), you activate the app and save, it appears the message "This application is available to over 0 devices". I've uploaded five different apps to Google Play in the past, and I have never had this problem.

It seems that Scandit, as a 3rd party module of Titanium, have problems with Google Play. Any solution?
Perhaps something related with the .jar files of Scandit is causing the problem?

thanks in advance

message in Google Play:

This application is only available to devices with these features, as defined in your application manifest.

Screen layouts: SMALL NORMAL LARGE XLARGE

Required device features:

android.hardware.autofocus
android.hardware.camera
android.hardware.flash
android.hardware.touchscreen

This application is available to over 0 devices.

API level: 8-17+
Supported screens: small-xlarge
OpenGL textures: all
Native platforms: armeabi-v7a, armeabi, x86

  • It seems that I've found the solution myself ;)

    Deleting "android.hardware.autofocus" and "android.hardware.flash" from the custom Android Manifest, it seems that the message "This application is available to over 0 devices." changes to "This application is available to over 2141 devices". I will wait a little to see the app published in Google Play and do the final test.
