    • var activity = Ti.Android.currentActivity;
        var intent = Ti.Android.createIntent({
            action: Ti.Android.ACTION_SEND,
            type: 'text/plain'
        });

        intent.putExtra(Ti.Android.EXTRA_TEXT,'Your Share Text Here');
        intent.putExtra(Ti.Android.EXTRA_SUBJECT, 'Your Subject Line Here');
        activity.startActivity(Ti.Android.createIntentChooser(intent,'Share'));

      This would essentially tell the system you have something to share that is of the text/plain mime type, and it will throw up a list of the apps that claim to be able to handle them.
