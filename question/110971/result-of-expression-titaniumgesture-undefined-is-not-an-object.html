  • Generally speaking, when you get an unexpected undefined error it is because you are now referencing a titanium module you did not during the last full rebuild. Because of the way the compiler optimizations are done, titanium only brings in modules during a full rebuild.

    Perform a full rebuild and the error is very likely to go away.
