Android Cloudpush min sdk undefined

After upgrading Ti again, I get the below error message con compiling my previously working app

[ERROR] : Found incompatible Titanium Modules:
[ERROR] : id: ti.cloudpush version: 3.3.0 platform: android min sdk: undefined

How to rectify?

  • Hello Bastien

    in this case you need an entry in your tiapp.xml (in manifest section):

     <uses-sdk android:minSdkVersion="11"/>
<uses-sdk android:targetSdkVersion="19"/>

    Alternatively, you can set the <uses-sdk> in the <android> <manifest> section of the tiapp.xml to
    one of the following installed Android target SDKs: 11, 12, 13, 14, 15, 16, 17, 18
    <ti:app xmlns:ti="http://ti.appcelerator.org">
    <android>
    <manifest>
    <uses-sdk android:minSdkVersion="11" android:targetSdkVersion="11" android:maxSdkVersion="19"/>

    </android>
    </ti:app>
