  • There is no 'startup' event that you can use.

    However, because 'resume' and 'resumed are fired on returning to a background app, you could use the absence of a semaphore flag, either as a global variable, or as an App.Property to determine if it was a fresh clean launch.

  • Hi,

    Build you App once and go to => MY_APP/build/iphone/ and copy the info.plist and paste it to =>MY_APP/ so that it exists among resources and build folder.
    now edit this info.plist an add this key at the end

    <key>UIApplicationExitsOnSuspend</key>
<true/>

    now delete the contents of MY_APP/build/iphone/ and rebuild your App.when you hit the home button it will exit the app(if you are trying from Ti-studio the simulator itself will close)
