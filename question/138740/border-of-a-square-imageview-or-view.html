  • your image view is probably bigger that the image itself. To test it correctly, I suggest you to set a fixed size to the imageView equal to the size of the image. For example, for a JPG image of 300x300, do this way

    //use top and left to positionate, but do not use right and bottom or the view size could be bigger than the image itself
var img = Ti.UI.createImageView({
    top:0, left:0,        
    width:300, height:300,
    image:'foo.jpg',
    borderWidth:1,
    borderColor:'#fff',
    //borderRadius:10        //for rounded corners
});

    not tested in android, but works correctly for iphone
