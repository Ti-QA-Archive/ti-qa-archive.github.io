Confused about push notifications

Hello,

We would like to have notifications popup to users in some way, we already have this working with Android with intent's, so only iOS remaining now. Just have a few questions:

  1. With Ti.Cloud am I right in thinking that the device needs to be online to receive them since they are pushed from Appcelerator's servers?

  2. Is it possible to have things appear in the notification centre on iOS?

  3. What would be the easiest way to just get an alert in front of the user for certain times (whilst offline)?

Thanks!

    Yes the device does have to be online in some way to receive push notifications. With iOS you can have a local notification pop up at a certain specified time though which would not require the device to be online.
