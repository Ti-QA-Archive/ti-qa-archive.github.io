Android app not closing with background service and Ti.platform.openURL() EXCEPTION

Hello all,

we are using a background service in an android app in the tiapp.xml

<services>
<service type="interval" url="LiveWebcastBackgroundChk.js"/>
</services>.

The service runs fine.
But the problem is that we use "Ti.platform.openURL()" to open a web url from the app on the click of a button.

The first time the app is launched and the button is clicked the "Ti.platform.openURL()" executes and launches the url in the browser.

Now we press the back button and exit out of the app.

Again we launch the app.

Due to the background service the app seems to RESUME from the previous state RATHER from starting fresh - i mean the window view state is restored instead of being created again.

It seems the app doesn't closed FULLY when a background service is used.

And after launching for the second time we click the button and "Ti.platform.openURL()" throws null pointer exception.

This is not the case when we donot use background service.

How to make the app start afresh when we use background service.
