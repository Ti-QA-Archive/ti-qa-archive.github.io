  • I can't imagine a situation where it would make sense to take a screenshot of a runtime error. Android itself provides a mechanism to share crash reports to the developer through Google Play if a runtime error happens. You'll get the thy type of the runtime error (ANR, Exception, etc.) including the error logs which are more helpful than a screenshot.
