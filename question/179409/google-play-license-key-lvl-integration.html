Google Play License key LVL integration

Is there a standard way to integrate a Google Play License with a Titanium Studio Android App?

As stated in the Google Developer Console:

"Licensing allows you to prevent unauthorised distribution of your app. It can also be used to verify in-app billing purchases."

I think this is very necessary to control the authorized distribution of Android Apps.

The Appcelerator Marketplace Module apparently does not support Apps created with the latest 3.4.1.GA Titanium SDK since I receive an error when integrating the module: "SDK not supported".
