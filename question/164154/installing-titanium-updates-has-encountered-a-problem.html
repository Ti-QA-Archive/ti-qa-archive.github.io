  • SOLVED!!
    I have followed the two suggestionfs founded here: https://developer.appcelerator.com/question/121993/updating-titanium-sdks-has-encountered-a-problem

    Scilicet:

    • enabling and reload the "Release Updates" in Preferences > Install/Update > Available Software Sites.

    • Clicking on edit on "Titanium Studio Stable Updates" and adding '//' at the end.

    This two suggestions (i don't know if both are required) worked for me!
