  • Hi Simon,

    Assuming you are uploading the image to profile, page is different from profiles. Looking into the error message, seems you have an old fb module in use. Though I would recommend you to check some valid points like

    • did you authorize your facebook app? with fb.appid and fb.getAccessToken?
    • You have to include facebook as a module in for targeted platform(iOS, android) in tiApp.xml.
      ~~~
      <property name="ti.facebook.appid">xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx</property>
    
- Also include the facebook appid in tiApp.xml as a property.

    <modules>
    <module platform="android">facebook</module>
    <module platform="iphone">facebook</module>
    </modules>

    ~~~
