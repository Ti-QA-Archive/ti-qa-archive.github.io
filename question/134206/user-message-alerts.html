    • Urban airship offers 1 million free push messages per month, and you can read here for implementation if you do not want to use the module:
      http://urbanairship.com/blog/2010/05/26/appcelerator-and-urban-airship/

      I have never personally used them, but it seems to be the most used push notification service with titanium (basing this on the amount of QA I have read about it). I guess the idea is, why jump through all the hoops of getting your personal notification relay working when there is one already available, for free?

      If you are planning on having millions of users for your app, put it on the app store at $0.99 and dont worry anymore about urban airships upgrade costs… you'll have enough money to pay for it :)
