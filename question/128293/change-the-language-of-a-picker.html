  • Picker use the device's configuration language. The only way to go around this would be to develop a custom date picker yourself. (but I would advise against it)

    Keep in mind that the simulator usually defaults to English and this might be the reason you see the picker in English.

    In the iOS simulator:

    • Go to Settings
    • General
    • International
    • Language

    In the Android Emulator:

    • Open menu
    • Setting
    • Language & Keyboard
    • Select Locale

    Your picker should now be in the language you selected.
