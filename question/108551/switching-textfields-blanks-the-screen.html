Switching textfields blanks the screen

I had this problem earlier when switching between two textfields. One of them had autocorrect enabled and the other one had it disabled. This would cause my app to show a blank white window. There is something that is causing this as I could not recreate the issue by simply recreating the two textfields in an empty project. I fixed the issue by setting both of the fields to have the same autocorrect. I'm writing now because I have the same problem between a textfield and a textarea because of the suppressReturn on the textarea. This I can't work with. I need the suppressReturn. This is happening only on the iphone device. Nothing is showing up in the log either. Is there possibly another more detailed log somewhere? Any ideas on a fix?
