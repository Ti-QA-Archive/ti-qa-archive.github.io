How to slide navbar without moving the window background?

I would like to be able to slide the navbar out while keeping the rest of the window static. The obvious choice win.hideNavBar({animated:true});
causes the window to move up. I'd rather have a "layer feeling".

I guess I could hide the navbar completely and add a toolbar on top to fake the navbar, but that would require faking the back button etc… Is there any simpler way to achieve this?

Alternatively it would be nice to fade the navbar in and out (http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/29041/fade-out-statusbar-and-navbar), but it looks like nobody has found a solution for that yet.

  • I am guessing the elements in your UI are based off of distance from top… in using win.hideNavBar({animated:true}); you are changing where "top" is on your screen… if you base your elements position off bottom, then win.hideNavBar({animated:true}); should work just fine for you

  • When you create the window that the navbar is attached to, you just need to add 'translucent: true' to the window properties.

    e.g.

    var window = Ti.UI.createWindow({
    translucent: true
    });

    When you hide and show the navbar in that window everything will remain static.
