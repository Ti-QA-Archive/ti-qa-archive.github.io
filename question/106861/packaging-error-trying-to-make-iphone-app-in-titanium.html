  • My mistake, was my own fault. I was using the wrong certificate. I created a specific certificate and identifier for my client. When I was uploading to the store I chose the certificate that was 'Recommended' by Xcode. I should have used the one specific to my app.

    After fixing that, it all went smoothly.

    In fact here it is:

    http://itunes.apple.com/gb/app/synthbasics/id432299726?mt=8
