Screenshot + crop + resize= bad quality?

Hi,

I am generating a PDF which include a chart. To include in the pdf the chart I am getting an screenshot of the chart is being displayed in the screen, so far as the char is not fullscreen I am cropping that screenshot and resizing it to fit in my pdf. This has been working but the quality of the images is not being good.

What I have noticed after a lot of trials is that the quality of the image is start being blurry when I resize the image to do it smaller. If I just add the screenshot in the pdf the quality is not bad so, how can I resize it with a good image quality?. Currently I am using an imageView:

var croppedImg = screenshot.imageAsCropped({x:0,y:205,width:605,height:427});
var imageViewChart = Titanium.UI.createImageView({
      image:croppedImg,
      width:605*0.65,
      height:427*0.65
});     
croppedImg = imageViewChart.toImage();

Extra info: This is for iPad only and I am using Titanium 3.1.2.GA

Thanks!
