    The app will always launch with the index controller. You could however make the index controller have no UI, and give it the sole responsibility of launching a different controller that you actually want to start your app with, based on whether you are testing or not. I do something similar to this in the login test app in the Github repo. Pay special attention to the index.js controller where I essentially do nothing but determine which controller I actually want to open based on the app state:

    https://github.com/appcelerator/alloy/blob/master/test/apps/models/login/controllers/index.js
