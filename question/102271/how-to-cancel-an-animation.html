How to cancel an animation

I spent a few minutes trying to find a cancel or a stop animation function in the API, and then after looking at several KitchenSink examples I found the solution in animation_points.js

Basically you call animate() with nothing to stop the current animation.

Eg:
If you have an animation:

var animation = Titanium.UI.createAnimation();
animation.left = 200;
animation.duration = 10000;
myView.animate(animation);

to cancel it just call:

myView.animate();
