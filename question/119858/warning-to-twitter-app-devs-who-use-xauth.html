Warning to Twitter app devs who use xAuth

Twitter (in their infinite ermm.. wisdom) have announced some changes to their API regarding permissions and authorization, in particular for apps which need to send and receive Direct Messages:

See the blog post here : http://blog.twitter.com/2011/05/mission-permission.html

And the developer post here : https://dev.twitter.com/pages/application-permission-model

It states that "Applications that use "Sign-in with Twitter" or xAuth will only be able to receive Read or Read/Write tokens."

We're waiting for some clarification from @twitterapi on this matter.
