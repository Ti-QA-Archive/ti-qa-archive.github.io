  • Take a look at this guide on utilizing different local data storage to persist app states between sessions.

    Essentially, you will want to save the blob data of the selected image using one of the methods in the article above. On app launch, set your ImageView's image attribute with the blob data. If no data is available/saved, present the user with your image picker.

    • On your success callback from your Ti.Media.openPhotoGallery method, grab e.media. This is a blob of the selected image. You can then either save this as a file to the device, or you could convert it to a string and save it in a db, app property, or other local data storage.
