iPhone: Orientation portrait only doesn't work after tab switch

Hello

In my app i would like the homescreen to be portrait only, since it has a dashboard and otherwise it goes out of the screen.

Now this works perfectly when i run my app and i hold my phone sideways. The problem is when i go to another tab, and go back to my home screen, the phone switches back to portrait, but when I hold my phone sideways it starts switching the orientation to landscape etc..

Can anyone tell me why the orientation isn't just portrait anymore?

This is how i do it:

currentWin.addEventListener('focus',function(){
    Titanium.UI.orientation = Titanium.UI.PORTRAIT;
    currentWin.orientationModes = [Titanium.UI.PORTRAIT];
});

I'm using iPhone SDK 4.2 and Titanium SDK 1.6.2
