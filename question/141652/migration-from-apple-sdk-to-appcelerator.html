Migration from Apple Sdk to Appcelerator

Actually we develop our mobile app for IOS using apple SDK. We' d like to migrate our project to Appcelerator, is possible to open our Xcode project in Appcelerator and continue developing on it? Is there any suggestion or best procedure for this operation?
