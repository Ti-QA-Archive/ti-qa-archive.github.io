    Be aware that you cannot actually delete your apps from your online account; you can only hide them from the "Active Apps" list.

    To do this, click on the "Active Apps" tab (indicated [1] in the screenshot) and hover the mouse pointer to the left of the app you wish to hide (indicated [2] in the screenshot). A popup will display the message, "Click to deactivate the app, which will hide it from this view and turn off analytics. You can reactivate it from the 'All Apps' tab.".

    Note that you will need javascript enabled in your browser for the popup to display.
