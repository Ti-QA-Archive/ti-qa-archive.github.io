What to use for a large text-based article

Hi,

Hopefully a quick question. I have a large piece of text to include (it's basically an article linked through from a title screen) and am wondering the best method for displaying on the iPhone.

Label doesn't seem appropriate as it doesn't scroll. So is the best method Titanium.UI.TextArea or is there another way of handling large bits of texts?

Thanks.

  • Accepted Answer

    IMHO, if the text does not need formatting, then a TextArea with editable:false seems like a reasonable option. If the text does need formatting then I think a WebView using html would be a reasonable option.

    Note that in android, labels can actually support a html property for simple formatting, but you asked about displaying on the iPhone where the html property for labels is not supported by the OS.
