    Hi

    ListView is designed to work with data, TableView is designed to work with Views - two very different approaches.

    Whilst you can define templates for ListViewItems, these become static are are only changed based on data, because of this trying to add scrollView to a ListView is neither sensible or fit for the purpose it was designed for.

    Think of it this way; ListView is designed for very fast display and access to lots of rows of information. TableView if much better for displaying information in many different ways very easily.

    This is a job for the TableView NOT the ListView.
