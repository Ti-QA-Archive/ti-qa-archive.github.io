  • I got this when I used the wrong keystore password. Better check that out with "keytool -list -keystore <name of your .keystore file>" from the command line as it gives better error messages.

    I do believe there is an error in the Titanium run.py script that crashes on the error.

  • In general, that first step to try when you get a packaging error is to force a full rebuild. The easiest way to do that is visit the Edit tab and click "Save Changes" even if you do not update the version number or anything. This will "touch" the tiapp.xml file with a new timestamp, thereby triggering a full compile.

    On a full compile it will always pick up any new classes/Ti modules needed due to new references in your code.
