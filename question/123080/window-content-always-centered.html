Window content always centered

I simply don't understand, why the content of a window is always centered and not on 0,0 ? I simply can not find the switch to turn center,center into left:0 and top:0

var w = Titanium.UI.createWindow({
        backgroundColor:'#000',
        barColor:'#000',
        top:0,
        left:0,
    });
    var lbl = Ti.UI.createLabel({
         text:'Test.Me!',
        top:0,
        left:5,
        color: '#fff',
        textAlign:'left',
        font:{fontSize:22, fontStyle:'bold'}
    });
    w.add(lbl);
    Titanium.UI.currentTab.open(w,{animated:true});

    In this case, your label has a height of 100% by default. Try adding a background color to see how it is working.

    var lbl = Ti.UI.createLabel({
    text:'Test.Me!',
    backgroundColor:'green',
    top:0,
    left:5,
    color: '#fff',
    textAlign:'left',
    font:{fontSize:22, fontStyle:'bold'}
});

    To accomplish your goal, add a height property to your label.

    var lbl = Ti.UI.createLabel({
    text:'Test.Me!',
    height:50,
    top:0,
    left:5,
    color: '#fff',
    textAlign:'left',
    font:{fontSize:22, fontStyle:'bold'}
});
