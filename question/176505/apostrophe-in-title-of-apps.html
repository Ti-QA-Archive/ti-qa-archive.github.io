Apostrophe in title of apps

How can one put ' (apostrophe) in the title of the apps. I went to tiapp.xml as follows and insert \'s to show sample's as the app name title but did not work. I also inserted the same in the manifest shown below. Anyone can recommend solution? Thanks.

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<ti:app xmlns:ti="http://ti.appcelerator.org">
    <id>com.sample.sample</id>
    <name>sample \'s</name>
    <version>1.7</version>

manifest

#appname: sample \'s

    Hi

    You should try and keep the app name in this location as simple as possible. But yes you can add special characters and even names in Chinese and Japanese (symbolic languages).

    You use the internationalisation features of the app to provide whatever name you need. Given the phrase - you can also achieve this in any other language you choose - but you do not have to - just stick to the default English (all apps default) and add what you want.

    I answered how to do this more than once, this is one of those answers and provides a great level of detail with full examples. It also explains how to use internationalisation throughout the app so either skip that part or learn that as well. You have not said Android or iOS - always specify this information - as often it is important - like it is here, two different solutions.

    • i18n internationalization…

    BTW i18n if the short form for internationalisation (or internationalization) comes from the number of letters between i and n, how cool is that.

      here is where I put the app name which contains an apostrophe.
      Under i18n/en/strings.xml

      <?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<resources>
    <string name="home">Home</string>    
    <string name="settings">Settings</string>
    <string name="openWindow">Open New Window</string>
    <string name="newWindow">New Window</string>
    <string name="app_name">Sample\'s App</string>
</resources>
