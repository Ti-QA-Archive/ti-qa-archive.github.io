Tabgroup icons are now small in 1.7.0.RCI

Since updating to 1.7.0.RCI, the tabgroup icons shown in the tabs are now tiny. I would imagine a bug but not sure why they didn't catch this or how it gets fixed.

To prove it wasn't my code, I re-installed Kitchen Sink on my Android device after changing the Kitchen Sink TIApp.xml to use 1.7.0.RCI, and then even the Kitchen Sink tab icons were then tiny. I switched Kitchen Sink back to 1.6.3 and they returned to normal size.

Unfortunately, in my app, ever since running it with 1.7.0.RCI, even when I switch it back to an earlier version, they won't return to their proper sizes. Very frustrating. Any ideas anyone?

  • This is to better support more screen resolutions better, however if you add the following to your tiapp.xml it will display more like you are expecting.

        <manifest>
        <supports-screens android:anyDensity="false"/>
    </manifest>

    after the line that has:

    <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
