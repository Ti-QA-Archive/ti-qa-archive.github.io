    • And you do know that ads are NOT always available, right?

      This is called fill rate. If your content/app does not match anyone who wishes to advertise, then no ad is displayed.

      If you are going to base your revenue on ads, you might want to actually learn about the various ad programs, their payouts, their fill rates, their historic click-thru rates.
