  • you can just use a toolbar on the bottom and remove the tabBars completely.

    you can also do this, when you create the window and the tabBar will be hidden

        var win = Titanium.UI.createWindow({
        title:'title',
        barColor: 'black',
        tabBarHidden:true,
        width: Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformWidth,
        height: Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformHeight,
    });
