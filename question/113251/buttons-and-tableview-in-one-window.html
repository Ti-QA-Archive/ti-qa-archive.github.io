Buttons and TableView in one window

i have created two buttons in my child window that already has a tableView. However, when i run it in the emulator, both buttons and tableview appears BUT when i scroll, the tableview scrolls, however the buttons stays stationary on the screen. How do i add it to the current child window?

PS: Ti.UI.currentWindow.add(buttons); –> gives me error when i compile.

var buttons = Titanium.UI.createButtonBar({
labels:['One', 'Two'],
backgroundColor:'#336699',
top:0,
style:Titanium.UI.iPhone.SystemButtonStyle.BAR,
height:25,
width:200
});

tableview.add(buttons);
Ti.UI.currentWindow.add(tableview);

    Are you asking how to get the buttonbar to scroll as well as the table view?

    If so, create a ScrollView object and place both the table and the buttonbar on the scrollview. You may also want to look at the various scroll view examples in the kitchen sink source code.
