How to simultanously play and record sound with high volume?

I need to play a sound and record it simultanously:

var player = Ti.Media.createSound({url:"1000Hz_0dBFS_0.5s.wav", preload: true});
var recording = Ti.Media.createAudioRecorder();
recording.start();
Ti.Media.startMicrophoneMonitor();
player.play();    
...

This works with Titanium.Media.audioSessionMode = Ti.Media.AUDIO_SESSION_MODE_PLAY_AND_RECORD; However, this results in a very low volume on the iphone of the sound played, which cannot be increased by player.volume=1;. The workaround to change the audioSessionMode each time (AUDIO_SESSION_MODE_PLAYBACK and AUDIO_SESSION_MODE_RECORD) as described here: http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/130066/volume-too-low-on-iphone-with-createsound does not work for me, because I need to play and record simultanously. I get the warning [WARN] : Setting audio mode while playing audio... changes will not take effect until audio is restarted.

Does anyone have a hint/workaround?

  • I know this question is super old but I had the same problem and after googling for a while I found the answer on the API itself (kinda), it turns out that the answer was pretty simple so I'm gonna share it hehe.

    Set the audioSessionMode like this:

    Titanium.Media.audioSessionMode = Ti.Media.AUDIO_SESSION_MODE_PLAY_AND_RECORD;

    and then set the audioRoute to this:

    Titanium.Media.setOverrideAudioRoute(Ti.Media.AUDIO_SESSION_OVERRIDE_ROUTE_SPEAKER)

    This way the audio goes through the built-in speaker instead of the speaker in the phone receiver which is why the sound is so low.

    Hope this helps someone :)

    R.
