Hide status bar when using the camera

In my application, I am using the showCamera method to bring up the camera. I have the status bar set to opaque, which is what I want/have for every other window in my application. What I've noticed is that since the status bar is visible in my application, it covers the camera controls (i.e.: flash, camera flip button) when I call the showCamera method.

Is there a way that I can hide the status bar when the camera opens and show it again when the camera closes?
