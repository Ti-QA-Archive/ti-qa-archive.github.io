  • If you want the Search field to occupy the full width of your app, I would do the following:

    Set your 'width' as 100% and 'left' to 0, or just remove it.

    var searchbox = Titanium.UI.createView({
    top:63,
    height:40,
    left:0,
    width: '100%'
