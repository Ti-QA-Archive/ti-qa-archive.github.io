  • Have you added the font to the tiapp.xml too?

    Check the &lt;ios&gt; section …

        <ios>
        <plist>
            <dict>

                .... you might already have some entries here... 

                <key>UIAppFonts</key>
                <array>
                    <string>fontawesome-webfont.ttf</string>
                </array>
            </dict>
        </plist>
    </ios>
