  • Accepted Answer

    You're going to have to elaborate on "doesn't work as expected". Also you might want to remove "instance=true" from the <Collection> element since you are accessing it globally in your markup. Also you only need to put the <Require> under the table once, it will be repeated for every model in your collection automatically.
