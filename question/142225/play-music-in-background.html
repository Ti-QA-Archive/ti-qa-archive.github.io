Play music in background

Hi, I'd like to play my music in background or when the iphone is locked, it is played with the help of a webview.
How can I do that?

var webview = Titanium.UI.createWebView({url:e.row.sourceurl,    backgroundColor:'#f4f4f4',}); 
fourth.add(webview);

    I would not use a webview and check out the docs for streaming audio using the AudioPlayer.

    ** using a WebView is not going to allow you to play your music in the background. As soon as the app goes to background, all web activity will stop.

    • There are no other workarounds for a webview OR browser. Could you imagine if music kept playing from a webpage even after you closed your browser? Your phone would still be making noise with nothing running, it would be mayhem. That is why background tasks are reserved for native application components.
      If you want to make this work, figure out a way to scrape the urls from the html in the webview and play them through the AudioPlayer. YQL should suffice for your scraping needs.

      NOTE: Before you do that though, I'd make sure that you can play soundcloud urls in the AudioPlayer
