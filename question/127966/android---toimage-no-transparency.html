  • Hi Tony, I put up a quick example with 1.8.0.v20111106143455

    As you can see in the screenshots, the Android alpha channel is black vs being transparent on iOS.

    var win = Ti.UI.createWindow({
    backgroundColor: '#ffffff'
});

var view = Ti.UI.createView({
    backgroundImage: 'alpha.png',
    width: 300,
    height: 300
});

var blob = view.toImage();

var rendered = Ti.UI.createImageView({
    image: blob,
    width: 300,
    height: 300
})
win.add(rendered);

win.open();
