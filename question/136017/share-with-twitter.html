Share with Twitter

Hi everyone, I was trying to do a share with twitter (but a very simple one). So i did this:

var page = "http://twitter.com/share?text='hey there'&via=" + encodeURIComponent("sthing") + "&related=myaccount";
    var win = Ti.UI.createWindow({
        width: '80%',
        height: '60%'
    });
    win.add(Ti.UI.createWebView({
        url: page
    }));
    win.open();

It does the tweet and everything (Asks the user email and password and everything), but I have 2 problems:

  • Adjust the window size.
  • Close the window/webview when the twitter is done
  • (well, maybe 3) Have a close button, like the Facebook view has.

Does anyboday has a simple example (no the Twitanium or David R's example) or tell me how can I improve my "solution"?

Thanks!
