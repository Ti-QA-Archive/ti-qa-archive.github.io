[android] How to resize the ScrollView when show/hide keyboard?

In android, I have a ScrollView and a Textarea. When click on textarea, the keyboad show, click back button -> keyboard hide. But I cannot catch the event when the keyboard show/hide to resize the Scrollview.

I tried addeventlistener("blur") or ("android:back") but not work.

    Hi,

    you don't have to interfere with the keyboard or the ScrollView at all, just set the "windowSoftInputMode" of the related window to Ti.UI.Android.SOFT_INPUT_ADJUST_RESIZE and the content of the window (including your scrollview) will automatically resize if a keyboard is visible. Read the docs for details.

    Hope this helps
