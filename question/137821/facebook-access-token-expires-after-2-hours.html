Facebook access token expires after 2 hours

Since May 2, 2012 Facebook disabled "offline_access permission". So the access token expires after 2 hours by default and Users have to authorize the app again and again even if he is already logged in. The offline_access permission is deprecated and will be removed July 5, 2012.

Does anybody have an idea, how to handle it in Titanium?
