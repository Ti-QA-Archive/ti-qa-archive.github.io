  • There isn't really a datagridview for iPhone UI. The closest thing is a tableview that drills down.

    The events aren't the same, c# has a lot more events, for fill, select, etc. But you can create your own custom events.

    As for 90% of the questions on here check out the kitchen sink for examples of custom events and all kinds of tableview designs and implementations.

  • The iPhone UI doesn't allow for columns like a DataGridView. One approach to it though could be a scrollable view with tableviews. You could flip left and right for the columns. And then each view within the scrollable view could hold the tableview with a header containing the column name.
