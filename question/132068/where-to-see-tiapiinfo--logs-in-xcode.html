    Are you running the app on the device or within an xcode simulator?

    If the latter, why?
    If the former, simply open the Organizer, click the the 'Devices' button, find the appropriate device on the left side and click on the 'Console' option. That will bring up the live console log for the device, assuming said device is attached to your computer.
