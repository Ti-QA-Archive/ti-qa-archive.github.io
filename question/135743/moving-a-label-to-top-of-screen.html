Moving a label to top of screen

Application type: mobile. Titanium SDK: 1.8.2. Platform & version: iOS 5.x. Device: iOS simulator. Host Operating System: OSX 10.6.8. Titanium Studio: 1.0.9.

var win = Ti.UI.createWindow({  
            url:e.rowData.path,  
            title:'Add Contacts',
            backgroundColor:'#fff',
            barColor: 'orange'
        });  

        var label1 = Titanium.UI.createLabel({
            color:'#999',
            text:'People in "' + e.rowData.title + '":',
            font:{fontSize:20,fontFamily:'Helvetica Neue'},
            textAlign:'center',
            width:'auto',
            top:0
        });

        win.add(label1);

Despite top:0, this label still shows in the middle vertically. Do I need to do something with the window height?
