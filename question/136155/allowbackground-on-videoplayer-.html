  • For iOS:

    Early in your app:
    Ti.Media.audioSessionMode = Ti.Media.AUDIO_SESSION_MODE_PLAYBACK;

    In your tiapp.xml:

    <iphone>
        <backgroundModes>
            <mode>audio</mode>
        </backgroundModes>
</iphone>
