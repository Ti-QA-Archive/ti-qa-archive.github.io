    Hi

    This is not a OptionDialog you know it from Titanium OptionDialog - but you are correct it is a Common Dialog. You do not create this yourself by adding icons it is part of the iOS6 and iOS7 frame work.

    It is called a Share Sheet and you can include it in your app. You need a custom module to access this;

    • TiSocial.Framework

    This is one example and it works well.
