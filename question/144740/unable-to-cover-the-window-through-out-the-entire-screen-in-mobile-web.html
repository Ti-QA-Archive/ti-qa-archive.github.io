  • button in app.js is not defined, and so the app does not run, also you use 'window' as an identifier, which is not allowed in mobile web.

    Is the window covering about 80% of the screen, with an even border around the edge? If so, that's the expected behavior of modal windows on mobile web. You can override it by setting the width/height to 100% or Ti.UI.FILL
