Internationalization String.format() example from wiki is crashing iPhone simulator

I'm using the code from the wiki for internationalization, which is working, but string replacement unfortunately is not. I'm trying to follow the date example for internationalization of dates. In the strings.xml file:

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<resources>
  <string name="headingDate">%2$s %1$d, %3$d</string>
  <string name="January">enero</string>
  <string name="February">febrero</string>
...
</resources>

and in the code:

Ti.API.info(String.format(L('headingDate'),thisDate.getDate(),L(thisMonth),thisDate.getFullYear()));

(These come from the internationalization section in the wiki)

Whenever I use $ in the headingDate, the app promptly crashes in the simulator when it gets to that point. Is there a bug in String.format()?
