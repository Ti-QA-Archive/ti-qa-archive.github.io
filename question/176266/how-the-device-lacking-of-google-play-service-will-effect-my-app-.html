How the device lacking of Google Play Service will effect my App ?

there are many Android phone manufactures in China have removed Google Play Service from their products , i am wondering how would this effect my app when running on these devices ? or to be exact ,which module or functionality of Titanium SDK/Android would be effected ? any idea would be very appreciated !

  • Accepted Answer

    If you do not use any of the Google Play Services, your App won't be affected.

    • Maps
    • Cloud Messaging (Push Notifications)
    • Play In App Purchases
    • Analytics
    • Mobile Ads
    • etc.
