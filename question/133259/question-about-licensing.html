Question about licensing

Do I need to purchase an Indie account to be able to purchase and use modules from the market place or can I purchase and use a module without upgrading to an indie account.

    Hi,
    There is no need to have an Indie account to purchase and use modules from the market place. But you get a bunch of modules free with an Indie subscription ($49). Here is a list of modules that you get for free with Indie subscription

    1. Urban Airship
    2. PayPal
    3. SoundSampler
    4. StoreKit
    5. Canvas
    6. ImageFactory
    7. GreyStripe
    8. Millennial Media
    9. In-AppBilling
    10. Admob
    11. Flurry
    12. TiPHP
    13. Paint | iOS
    14. TiPython
    15. Box2D
