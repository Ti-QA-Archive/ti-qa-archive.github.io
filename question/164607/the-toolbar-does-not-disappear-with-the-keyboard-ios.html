  • Maria,

    You failed to mention what version of the SDK you are using. There are two tickets that address this problem, TIMOB-15967 iOS: keyboardToolbar fails to disappear and and TIMOB-15940 iOS: keyboardToolbar disappears after multiple displays. I custom rolled an sdk, and we released an app with the fixes in it so for us the fixes worked. Ygor's complaint seems to indicate a different use case, and possible the potential for another race condition.
