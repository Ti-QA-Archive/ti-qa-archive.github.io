  • I have 3 xcodes installed, 4.x, 5.0 DP6, 5.0 GM. easy enough to switch between them:

    sudo xcode-select -switch ~&#x2F;Desktop&#x2F;Xcode5-DP6.app&#x2F;

    sudo xcode-select -switch ~&#x2F;Desktop&#x2F;Xcode.app&#x2F;

    sudo xcode-select -switch &#x2F;Applications&#x2F;Xcode.app
