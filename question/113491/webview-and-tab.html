    Does it state that Titanium.UI.WebView is not defined, or webview?

    If it's the first, then you may need to delete your build files and force a full rebuild:

    For android, delete the contents of the immediate subdirectories of &#x2F;build&#x2F;android only, otherwise you may encounter problems. See ticket #2852 for more info. Then click the Save Changes button to force a full rebuild and launch the app.

    To understand the reason why you receive the error, and why it isn't such a bad thing, see How does Titanium work?

    If this does not fix it, then Dan is right - you need to provide some code. See the Participating in the Q&A to discover how to get more accurate answers more quickly in this forum.
