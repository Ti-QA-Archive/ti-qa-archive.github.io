Jelly Bean AudioPlayer issue?

I've just had a client tell me he's upgraded his Galaxy S3 to Android 4.1.1 (Jelly Bean) and the Icecast audio streams no longer play on the app I built for him last month which were working fine under various other flavours of Android. I do not have a device with Jelly Bean on it so can't test - I can test under 4.1.2 on the simulator though and it works - but are there any known issues with audio streams under Jelly Bean?

I am using Appcelerator's AudioPlayer API and the streams are presented in this format 'http://radio.foobar:8080/stream1.mp3'

    • Hello Greg, No solution as such - yet - but we did find that the radio station in question was running an old version of Icecast. The new one, version 2.0.2, has 'burst on connect' which boosts the start time for the stream which otherwise was something like 9 seconds on Android compared to half that on iOS. So it appeared that their Icecast server may be the culprit. But why this should have such an affect on the Galaxy S3 is a complete mystery.
