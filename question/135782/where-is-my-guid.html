    • Using TiStudio 2.0.1, targeting SDK 2.0.1GA, open your Tiapp.xml file.

      There are new options in the tiapp.xml editor.

      Click the "Enable Cloud Services" enable button.

      TiStudio will contact the appcelerator servers, some keys will be generated and inserted into your tiapp.xml file.

      That might just bring your app to life on the my.appcelerator.com site.
