  • First,

    Check out this post http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/105471/short-ipad-iphone-image-size-guide.

    In general, images are a bit tricky. When you publish to the App store, you need to make sure that the images listed in the TiApp.xml matches the image file names. Once you have the names matched up, it should be pretty straight forward. The easiest way may be to make sure your clients artwork is named the same, but that is not required.
