Android Does Not Receive Push Notifications Until WiFi is disabled and re-enabled

The Android Simulator is able to register for notifications but does not receive notifications until I disable and re-enable the network on the simulator. I also see this same behavior on my Xoom tablet which is WiFi only with no cellular. I do not have this problem on IOS devices or with my Android Pantech Element which has cellular but no SIM card. I'm able to send notifications from the simulator and the Xoom with no problems but can not receive notifications until MQTT disconnects and reconnects.

I have these permissions in the AndroidManifest.xml

<uses-permission android:name="android.permission.RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED"/> <uses-permission android:name="android.permission.INTERNET"/> <uses-permission android:name="android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE"/> <uses-permission android:name="android.permission.READ_PHONE_STATE"/> <uses-permission android:name="android.permission.ACCESS_WIFI_STATE"/> <uses-permission android:name="android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE"/>
