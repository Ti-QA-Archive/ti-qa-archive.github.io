  • Accepted Answer

    You don't have an eventListener attached to the button object.

    btnSearchcompany.addEventListener('click', function(e){
alert('clicked');
});

    Or, if you want it to run your function, you could do:

    btnSearchcompany.addEventListener('click', searchcompanyyql);
