  • Think this is a valid question, might be a bit late in dev to be asking though, but some thoughts.

    • Is your goal a mobile version of your website?

    If this is your goal, then make the best darn mobile version of your app possible.

    • Is your goal a mobile app?

    As an app user, I do NOT want to see a website in a frame (nor does Apple, who will often not pass your app through to the store). I want a native experience when on my device. I don't want HTML buttons, I want native iOS buttons, and native Android buttons and native Blackberry buttons.

    I want local audio, I want cached data, I want a rich experience.

    Taking a look at Fred Spencer's Codestrong 2012 session on UI/UX on vimeo.

    Also, take a look at Don and Ben's keynote on Web vs App also available on vimeo (the microsoft bashing was a bit excessive, and got old fast, but the content was good otherwise.)
