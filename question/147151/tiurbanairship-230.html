    • @Idan Jan, why you paying again? The module is open source and UA is free up to a certain amount of pushes. GCM support is on 2.3.2 module.
      And as for as the configuration goes, all you need is this in platform/android/bin/assets/airshiconfig.properties

      developmentAppKey = UA_DEV_KEY

      developmentAppSecret = UA_DEV_APP_SECRET

      productionAppKey = UA_PROD_KEY

      productionAppSecret = UA_PROD_APP_SECRET

      NOTE: There should NO quotes around the AppKey and AppSecret

      transport = gcm
      gcmSender = YOUR_GCM_ID

      inProduction = true

      iapEnabled = false
