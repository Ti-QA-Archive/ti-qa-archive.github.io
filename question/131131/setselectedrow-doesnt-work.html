    Hello,

    in setSelectedRow method first argument is column index, second one is row, so if you only have one column, your row selection should look like:

    pickerTextiel.setSelectedRow(0, 0,true);
//or
pickerTextiel.setSelectedRow(0, 1,true);
//or
pickerTextiel.setSelectedRow(0, 2,true);
//or
pickerTextiel.setSelectedRow(0, 3,true);
//etc
