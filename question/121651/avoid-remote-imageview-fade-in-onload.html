Avoid remote ImageView fade in onload

Hi guys

Quick question: when I load a remote image in an ImageView there's a quick fade in. How do I avoid this animation and instead just show the image right away when it's loaded?

    You create a "spare" imageview, and replace the current one with the new loaded one, on the load event. Makes sense?
