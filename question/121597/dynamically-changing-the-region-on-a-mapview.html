dynamically changing the region on a mapView

I have a mapView that centers to the users current view. There is a slider that can be used to zoom in or out from 5-50 miles radius of the users current location. I am using setLocation instead of zoom because I want the screen to only show the radius in miles around the user.

The problem is the slider changes the map region for only 5, 10, 15, and 30 miles. For some reason the mapView does not update for 20, 25, 35, 40, 45, or 50 miles. I'm thinking it is a problem with the math logic, but I have been racking my brain for days on this to no avail.

If someone has any idea or suggestions on how to get this working or even a better way to do it, I'd be very grateful!

BTW - I have tried toggling regionFit on the mapView and it doesn't seem to make a difference.

  • David,

    I build your project and I see what you're talking about, but it doesn't have anything to do with your code. This is a map restriction. You can see this by visiting google maps using the zoom function on the map controls. You will notice that each zoom out matches the zoom outs that are available within your control. So your map will only zoom to positions where you meet your own requirements as well as the map restrictions. Does that make sense?
