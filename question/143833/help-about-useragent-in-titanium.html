Help about UserAgent in Titanium

Hi! I already posted a similar question, but this time, I need your help testing one thing.
If you have an iOS device, I need to know what output you get with the following code, especially if you access from South America:

var country = Ti.Platform.currentLocale;
alert(country);
Ti.API.debug("Country: " + country);
country = Ti.userAgent;
alert(country);
Ti.API.debug("Country: " + country);

You should get something similar to this:

es
Oct 19 10:08:21 iPhone-Name KitchenSink[41014] <Warning>: [DEBUG] user agent established in Appcelerator Titanium/2.1.3.v20120915120319 (iPhone/5.0.1; iPhone OS; es_ES;)

This is because I want to run an app on iOS in different countries in the World, and I'd like to know whether the currentLocale variable is reliable enough for me, and whether the userAgent is a more reliable variable.

Thanks for your help!!

    I believe userAgent content and the currentLocale depends on your handset configuration not the country where it runs ! Just change the language & regional setting in the Settings app of your simulator (or your device) and run your test again … That's how I test localisation, for instance.
