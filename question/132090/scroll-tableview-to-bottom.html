  • You have solved your problem that's nice, it's just for your information.

    table.data.length will not give you total number of rows in tableview. It will return you total number of sections in tableview.

    So if you having only one section (default section if you have not created any section) than table.data[0].rows.length will give you total number of rows what you want.
