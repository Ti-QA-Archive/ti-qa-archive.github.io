Titanium Studio dual monitor issue

Just an FYI, when you first load up Titanium Studio if you have dual monitors then the login window pops up with half of it in 1 monitor and the other half on the other monitor. An acceptable solution would be to make the window draggable, as currently there's no way to get it out of this "split window" state.
