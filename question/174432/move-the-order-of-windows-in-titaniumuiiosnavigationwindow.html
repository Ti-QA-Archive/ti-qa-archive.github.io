    • A NavigationWindow is designed to show a series of Windows; one after the other. Automatic back buttons are provided for the user to go back and then if available choose another branch to follow.

      It is not designed to change the navigational structure part way through.

      I am not sure that the NavigationWindow is the correct choice to manage those simultaneous chats. As I have no idea what UI you are actually hoping for I would suggest two things;

      • Review the UIs of other apps that support multiple chats at one time - do they use a NavigationWIndow?
      • I would consider using ScrollableView and add it to a single window. Each new chat becomes a new 'page' of the ScrollableView. The user can cuikly swap between chats by sliding sideways and navigate each chat history by sliding up and down. The 'pages' can be removed and added easily.
