  • Hi

    Whilst you could try using verticalAlign and setting the value to TEXT_VERTICAL_ALIGNMENT_CENTER - I believe this is the default setting.

    The problem is very likely one simply of perception. I you were to add a lower case letter with a descender; g, j, p, q or y as examples you will notice the positioning is exactly correct. But as you have chosen to only use upper case it may still be allowing for space for the descenders.
