  • If you looked at the spec for f4m files you'd see it's just XML markup. http://sourceforge.net/apps/mediawiki/osmf.adobe/index.php?title=Flash_MediaManifest(F4M)_File_Format_File_Format)

    There's no reason you can't parse the incoming XML and then load the video stream using Ti.Media.VideoPlayer - as long as the incoming stream is compatible with the device (i.e. not an FLV file)
