So what is maxDistance in Titanium.Cloud.Places actually measured in?

I'm using appcelerator cloud places to build a database of places, I've got queries working quite well but have no idea what maxdistance is measured in?

The example code uses a maxdistance of 0.00126 - what I'm looking for is the equivalent to 100m (easy to scale up to 1km I'm guessing)

    • $maxDistance used with $nearSphere to limit maximum search distance. All distances use radians. This allows you to easily multiply by the radius of the earth (about 6371 km or 3959 miles) to get the distance in your choice of units. Conversely, divide by the radius of the earth when doing queries
