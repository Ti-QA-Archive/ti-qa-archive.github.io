Detect when app loses focus

Hi,
How can I detect when app loses focus and then run and even that does something? by "losing focus" I mean when the user is clicking the "home" button in either iPhone or Android.

I saw applications both on iPhone and Android that use that capabilities.

Thanks

    Make use of the resume and pause events on Ti.App

    Ti.App.addEventListener('pause', function(){
    // app loses focus
});
Ti.App.addEventListener('resume', function(){
    // app regains focus
});
