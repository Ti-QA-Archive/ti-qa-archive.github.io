Best way of storing relational data (including files/blobs)

Hi,
I'm in the phase of planning a new app (iOS and Andrioid). One part of the app will consist of a picture (selected from camera roll or taken with the camera) that belongs to an audio clip and a text. What i need to do is to somehow store the relationship between these three "components". As i understand it, storing images and audio as blobs inside a local sqlite db isn't a pretty good approach.

What i'm thinking of is to save the audio clip and the image in the filesystem with subfolders, and then have a local sqlite db which consists of only references to the names and paths of the files with unique keys, and with that being able to query a complete set containing of a image, audio and text.

CompleteItem-Table:
CompleteItemId: GUID,
AudioId: GUID,
PictureId: GUID,
TextId: GUID,

Audio-Table:
AudioId: GUID,
AudioPath: Path to Audio clip,
AudioName: Name of Audio clip,

Picture-Table:
PictureId: GUID,
PicturePath: Path to picture,
PictureName: Name of picture,

Text-Table:
TextId = GUID,
Text: The text,

Then do something like this (but in the sqlite way of course):
Select at.AudioPath, at.AudioName, pt.PicturePath, pt.PictureName, tt.Text
from CompleteItem ci
join AudioTable at on at.AudioId=ci.AudioId
join PictureTable pt on pt.PictureId=ci.PictureId
join TextTable tt on tt.TextId=ci.TextId
where ci.CompleteItemId = "[The GUID]"

How would you have tackled this? Is there a better way of grouping objects like this?

Thanks in advance for inputs!
