    Here are 2 places to help you incorporate email. First, naturally would be the docs for EmailDialog and the second would be the email dialog example in the KitchenSink demo, which you can find on GitHub: https://github.com/appcelerator/KitchenSink/blob/master/Resources/ui/common/baseui/email_dialog.js
