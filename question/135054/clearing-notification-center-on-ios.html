Clearing Notification Center on iOS

Hello,

I have got Push Notifications working perfectly in my Titanium app. The only issue I have is that the notifications always seem to remain in Notification Center, even after the app has been launched via the notification.

My question is, is there any way to clear the notifications from Notification Center? I was hoping that when the notification callback is called in my app then it would automatically remove this notification from Notification Center, is there some code I can add to my callback to clear Notification Center for my app?

Thanks for your time,

  • Hi.

    I know this is an old thread and most people who had asked the question have probably found the solutions, but just for the sake of beginners like me, i will post what worked for me.

    • In the Titanium.Network.registerForPushNotifications method, when you include the types of Notifications, make sure you include Titanium.Network.NOTIFICATION_TYPE_BADGE.

    • The first line of the callback function should be - Titanium.UI.iPhone.appBadge = 1;

    • The last line of the callback function should be - Titanium.UI.iPhone.appBadge = 0;

    This approach works for me in Titanium SDK 3.2.3.GA.
