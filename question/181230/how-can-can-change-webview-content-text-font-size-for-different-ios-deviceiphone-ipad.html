  • I'm not sure why you're seeing this – my experience has been that text sizes are pretty consistent across iphone and ipad.

    So you might need to dig a little deeper into why this is happening, rather than applying a quick fix. Maybe you're doing something non-standard in your CSS?

    At any rate, if you can't get the fonts to render consistently on their own, you could always use server-side detection of ipad/iphone to render content a little differently, or you could add some Javascript to the content to do the detection and dynamically apply the appropriate styles to the text.
