Internationalization...

Hi,
I've read Jeff Haynie article about Dealing with multiple screens and multiple languages.

I tried implementing it in my app in 2 ways:

First:

var lblTitle = Ti.UI.createLabel({ id: 'lblTitle', titleid: 'register_welcome' });

Second:

alert(Ti.Locale.getString('register_welcome'));

both just returned "register_welcome" as the result and not the word from my dictionary file.

My xml dictionary file (under /Resources/i18n/en/strings.xml):

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<resources>
    <string name="register_welcome">Welcome!</string>
</resources>

SDK: 1.7.2

What's the problem?

Thanks
