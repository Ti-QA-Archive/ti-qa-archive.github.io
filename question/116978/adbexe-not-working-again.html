    Rob, I haven't seen those errors, and you've probably tried all these steps. But just in case…

    Empty the your_project/build/android directory (but leave the android directory there)

    Remove your virtual devices and let TiDeveloper re-create them

    Kill adb.exe, though I would assume over 5 days you did a shutdown/restart. The process hangs fairly frequently for me and has to be killed.

    Try creating a new project, don't change anything, and build it. Does that work? If so, maybe it's something in your code?
