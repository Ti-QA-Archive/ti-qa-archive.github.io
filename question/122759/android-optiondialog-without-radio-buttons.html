Android OptionDialog without radio buttons

Is there a way to create an OptionDialog without the radio buttons on the right? They're (often) unnecessary, and make it look like the wrong control is being used.

  • There is a special property of the OptionDialog called AndroidView which allows for arbitrary content inside your dialog. You can use this to customize your dialog, otherwise you just end up with the native control. See the OptionDialog docs for more: http://developer.appcelerator.com/apidoc/mobile/latest/Titanium.UI.OptionDialog-object
