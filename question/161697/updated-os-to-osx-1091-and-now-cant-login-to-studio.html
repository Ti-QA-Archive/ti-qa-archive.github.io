    • Hello!

      From the issue you are documenting, the breaking part is your Java. Your SSL storage certs is broken or was removed during the upgrade (right now it's empty, that's the usual origin of the error you are pasting here). I recommend to remove your java and force a reinstall by opening your Ti Studio.

      Let me know how it goes.

      Best,

      Mauro

      — commented January 20th 2014 by Mauro Parra

    • ALRIGHT!!! This fixed it.

      To delete Java I needed to delete the JAVA folder from:
      /library

      And I found a alias to JavaAppletPlugin.plugin in :
      /library/internet plug-ins

      I followed that to the source and deleted that as well - sorry can't remember the path.

      I found this link that was very helpful …. Java broken under Mavericks.

      After that, Studio helpfully installed everything else for me.

      Thanks again for your help Mauro.
