iPad App got accepted by App Store!

We created a iPad virtual tour guide with almost 500MB of multimedia in it for the iPad using Titanium. It got accepted by the App Store on the first try. All revenues from the application get donated to the Old Dutch Church - one of the oldest churches in the U.S. and the source of the Legend of Sleepy Hollow.

http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/old-dutch-church-tour-guide/id388176022?mt=8

Thank you Jim and team for great support!
