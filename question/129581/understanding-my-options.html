  • Modules either come with a subscription or you can buy them. The Indie subscription has some modules for free, and the Enterprise subscription obviously has a few more. I'm not sure which modules come bundled with either subscription. I do recommend at least an Indie subscription, however, because debugging Titanium apps is completely impossible without Titanium Studio's built-in debugger.
