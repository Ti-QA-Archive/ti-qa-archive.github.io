  • As suspected while asking the question it has nothing to do with iOS or even the Titanium Studio version. It has everything to do the "workspace" index. I emptied the .metadata&#x2F;.plugins&#x2F;com.aptana.index.core inside the workspace then restarted Titanium Studio and it's working perfectly now.
