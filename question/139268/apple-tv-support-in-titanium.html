  • Currently there is no way to develop an app that will actually run solely on the AppleTV, although there are rumours that there will be an SDK for that purpose soon. I imagine Appcelerator will support it pretty fast as it is likely to be very similar to that of iPhone and iPad.

    There is a half-way-house that works well bt means having an iPhone, iPod or iPad in the mix. You can support AirPlay and AirPlay mirroring.

    Check out the TVout Titanium+ Mobile in support.appcelerator.com, it lets you use AirPlay via Apple TV and the HDMI and VGA cables.
