    • You may be confusing two different things, the methods; openPhotoGalley and saveToPhotoGallery are the devices built in image library/galleries, neither provide access to the folder structure as neither is part of the filesystem you are able to access.

      There are filesystem methods part of the file object where you can access folders, but these do not present a gallery/library and are within your control as to what you store there. Files stored in this way are not accessible by the galley/library afterwards.
