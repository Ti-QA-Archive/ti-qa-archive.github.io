  • Well, after you have the users location (lat/lon) and the points location (either lat/lon or address), you just do this (depending on your variable names)

    var url="http://maps.google.com/maps?saddr=" + latitude +"," + longitude + "&daddr="+endLat+ ","+ endLon;
Ti.Platform.openURL(url);
