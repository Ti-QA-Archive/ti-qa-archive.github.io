  • Accepted Answer

    I assume you're asking about the separator line inset. It appears that the line is starting under the text instead of the leftImage. That behavior is possibly related to ticket TIMOB-14986, which is fixed in the nightly builds (at least of the 3.2 branch). Though that might be a bug of its own.

    You might try using an ImageView and Label rather than leftImage and title to show the images and text in your rows.
