Flip transition when changing window of a tab?

I can't seem to get my window to open with the flip transition inside a tab.
No matter what I do, it always slides to the left. Is there some other way to change/open windows in a tab?

Titanium.UI.currentTab.open(myWin,{animated:true, transition:Titanium.UI.iPhone.AnimationStyle.FLIP_FROM_LEFT});

  • You have to specify the transition at the TabGroup level and not the tab level.

    tabGroup.open({
    transition:Titanium.UI.iPhone.AnimationStyle.FLIP_FROM_LEFT
});
