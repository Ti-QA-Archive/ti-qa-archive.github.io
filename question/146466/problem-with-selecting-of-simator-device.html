Problem with selecting of simator device

Hi *,
may be a stupid question:

I have changed the device in simulator menu from default iphone to retina iphone 4". The simulator has crached.

After restarting it leaves on this Retina 4". I cannot switch back to normal iphone. I have always triggered "Reset Settings".

Any idea to restore simulated device?

I'm working with SDK 3.0.0.GA with xcode 5.4.2.

  • Start up the simulator without launching your app. Change the hardware from the sim menu, then kill the simulator. Go back and launch your app. The sim should use the last chosen settings.
