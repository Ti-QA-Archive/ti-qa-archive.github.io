Map in scrollableview intercepts click

Hello, I have a map inside a scrollableview page: when I try to change page by swiping, if the cursor is on the map, the map scrolls and the scrollableview page doesn't turn. I want to fix the map and I tried touchEnabled property: the map is now fixed BUT, of course, I can't click its annotation anymore. Is there any way to "fix" the map, "send" the swipe event to its container and have the pinpoint still "touchable"?
