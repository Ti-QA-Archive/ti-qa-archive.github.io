  • Hi Chandrakanta Sahoo,
    The flash files are not supported in titanium, but you can do a series of animation with a duration property in animation which will exactly look like flash. Also you can give all the series of frames for animation in the images property of an image view and do animation there also.

    Please feel free to ask anything

  • Hi

    Just to clarify because this is very important ; iOS has never supported flash - Android no longer does. This has nothing to do with Titanium.

    As Ishan says, use a series of images if the job is simple. For more complicated jobs use a video file.

    If you are trying to play a flash interactive file - then that has no direct equivalent and yu would need to recreate it.
