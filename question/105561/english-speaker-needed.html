    I think you underestimate your language skills, Chris! ;) I take a lot of notice of grammar and punctuation, regardless of the medium, as I feel it reflects the professionalism of the author.

    Your app contains very few mistakes, and even those that do exist are trivial. Here is the list of corrections for the screens shown:

    • @0:03: "wish to analyze" is the US spelling. This may be what you intended. If not, "analyse" is the GB spelling.
    • @1:33: "numbers et symbols" should be "and"
    • @1:45: same error as above
    • @2:33: "you're kidding" should begin with a capital letter, to be consistent with the following bullet point in the app
    • @2:57: "very clear ?" does not need a space after "clear"

    Other than those pernickety points, it's great! :)
