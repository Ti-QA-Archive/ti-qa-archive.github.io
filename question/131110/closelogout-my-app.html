close/logout my app

how to close my applcation (by clicking a button for example) or to get the first screen (in this case it's a logging screen)?!

  • Your first (parent) window should have exitOnClose: true. 

    var win = Ti.UI.createWindow({ exitOnClose: true });
win.open();

    After you have child windows open and are ready to go back, just close them:

    Ti.UI.currentWindow.close();

    That will take you back to the last window. If you want to close multiple windows at once then store each window you open in an array, then loop through the array and close all windows stored in it.

    Once you close your first window, the app will close due to that exitOnClose property.
