Display objects in blocks after each other

Let's say i have two objects, one label and one image and i want to display the image 10dip from the bottom of the label, but i don't know where the bottom of the label is, how should i do then?

    You can position every element in relation of top or bottom of parent element, If the parent element has the property layout='vertical', the child elements are positioned relative (like in web)
