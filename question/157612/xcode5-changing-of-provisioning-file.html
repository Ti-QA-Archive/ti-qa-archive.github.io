Xcode5: changing of provisioning file

Hi,
since one week I have the new xcode ;-))

Today I have added a new device to a development profile, have downloaded the file. Doubleclick – noting happens. Next step: Using of XCODE/Editor/Provisioning Profiles/Import New Profile I have successful imported.

In Titanium 'Run configuration' I have now two profile and I cannot see which profile is the newer.

In older XCODE/Organizer I could delete old profiles.

Is anyone here, who knows the way?

Best regards

Rainer

    Assuming you're using Xcode 5, under Preferences -> Account, you can sync your profiles with those on the dev portal. This removes old/expired profiles, loads new unsynced ones and updates the out of sync profiles as well (as in your case, when you add/remove a device)
