Excessive pricing

I'd be surprised if I'm the only one. After researching the available tools I ended up looking at Titanium as the ideal tool of choice. Then I got a look at pricing. $195 a month? I mean, what?! I don't mind paying say $495 for a license, but a price per month? Get serious. And I gotta call a sales guy if there's more than a few devs in the team? Great, enterprise pricing.

Honestly, the tool looks good, but this kind of pricing only works without competition. This is no way to build a developer base. Just my opinion.

Kind regards,
Marc
