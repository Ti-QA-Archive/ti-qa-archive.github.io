  • Hello Gagan,

    Try Ti.App.Properties.

    login.loginWin.addEventListener('close',function(e){
    Ti.App.Properties.setString('username', username);
win.open();
});

    win.addEventListener('open',function(){
    label.text = Ti.App.Properties.getString('username');
});

    i hope this helps. thanks.
