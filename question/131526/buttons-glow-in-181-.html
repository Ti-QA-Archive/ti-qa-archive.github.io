  • Hey guys. As Clifton mentioned, it does go away when setting the backgroundImage + backgroundSelected image to different values. If you didn't have a specific design for your buttons, you could just change the button.style property to PLAIN (see the API docs for the exact structure to use), but then your bg colors are removed.

    The solution I had (since my client at the moment is really picky about colors) is to set the backgroundImage to 'none' and the backgroundSelectedImage to '' (empty string). This disables the glow effect and keeps your custom button style.
