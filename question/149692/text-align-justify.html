Text Align Justify

Is Text align (justify) supported in Titanium? If not, what is the reason why justifying is not supported? I am using Titanium to develop an application for enterprise and the UI looks not so professional with only left, right, center alignments. Any milestone date when this feature would be available?

    Hi

    There is no support for Justify as an alignment choice. There is no milestone for inclusion and I would not expect anytime soon.

    Many studies have shown that left aligned text in paragraphs allow the reader to quickly read content and especially on small screens or narrower paragraphs. I agree that justified text appears neater but it is harder to read as there are no visual breaks between lines to indicate the difference between one and the other.

    Having developed many apps for many levels of target audience I can assure I have never been asked by a client to justify the text, or by any user experience testing to do the same.

    A good quality well built app with care taken on the visuals and user experience is a good app - justifying the text is not part of that process in my opinion and 25 years of software development.

    Justified text text is a hang over from newspapers, see how many books and websites avoid justified text - significantly more than actually use it.
