My custom module no longer found in 3.0.2

I have developed a small custom module for iOS that worked fine until I switched to 3.0.2.

When I used 3.0.0 I followed the instructions on http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/latest/#!/guide/iOS_Module_Development_Guide for how to use the new module (i.e. copy to ~/Library/Application Support/Titanium) and then adding the module to TiApp.xml under the modules-tag. When I did require("my.module.name") it all worked fine.

Until I switched to 3.0.2, which is when the require-statement started to report that the module was no longer found.

If I switch back to 3.0.0 all works just fine again.

I have tried to re-copy the module. Am I doing something else wrong?
