  • Hi

    You realise that the iPhone does NOT have an infra-red led built into the device?

    You would have to look for a third-party hardware dongle that would plug into the headphone jack, dock or lightning connectors. Then you would need to hope they have an API - although it is more likely the would have their own APP that would not allow you to control the output of the infra-red signal.
