  • Accepted Answer

    Hi Aparna, follow these simple steps just for once & it will surely fix your problem:

    • in tiapp.xml

      <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
      <manifest>
          <application android:theme="@style/Theme.NoActionBar"/>
      </manifest>
  </android>

    • in PROJECT_FOLEDR/platform/android/res/values/customtheme.xml

      <resources>
  <style name="Theme.NoActionBar" parent="@style/Theme.AppCompat">
      <item name="windowActionBar">false</item>
      <item name="android:windowNoTitle">true</item>
  </style>
</resources>

    take care of naming the theme file exactly as i have mentioned above, & it is almost the same thing you were doing until now but there were very minor errors you were not taking care of….
    You are good to go now & please leave a review after implementing the above thing.
    Good Luck!
