  • Here's your answer.

    no such table: USERS

    It seems your database isn't being copied to the device correctly or you haven't created the USERS table. Has it ever worked on the device?

    I had a problem before where I had the DB installed on the device but then added a new table to the DB, added some calls to this table and redeployed the app. It crashed every time on opening because it was still using the old DB. I had to delete the app from the device and re-install it to get the new DB changes.

    • Have you made any changes to the DB recently? If so, the old DB may be held in the cache on the device. Just redeploying your app "on top of" your existing app won't copy over the new DB changes.

      If so you'll probably need to either DROP the table and recreate it. Otherwise delete the app completely from the app by holding down on it until it starts to shake, click X, then re-install the app.

      I've found these to rectify my DB issues but not sure if it's the same here.
