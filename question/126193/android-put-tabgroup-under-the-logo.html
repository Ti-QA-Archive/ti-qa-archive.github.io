  • I hope I am understanding your intent properly…Because you are using a tab group, you shouldn't need a 'root window'. (See the defqult project when creating a new Ti Mobile project). Therefore, you shouldn't open win1 separately, outside of the tabGroup unless you are trying to create a separate type of 'launch' screen that goes away before opening the tabGroup - in which case you might want to do that a little differently. So for now, I would just open the tab group - it will open the windows associated with its tabs. If you are trying to add just a logo like you said, you may want to define a simple view or imageView (with appropriate height and width), add the logo to it and add that to the appropriate window of your tabGroup - or perhaps to all of them if you want it to be across all tab windows. Note that the standard Tab Group will be on bottom in iOS and on top in Android. If you need it to be in another location, create a custom one with views and subviews sized and located appropriately and corresponding eventListeners on the 'click' event.
