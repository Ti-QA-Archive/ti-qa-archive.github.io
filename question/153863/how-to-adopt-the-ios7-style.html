How to adopt the iOS7 style?

Looking at the upcoming style of iOS 7 - the fonts, the translucent backgrounds for instance - are these these things that we need to actively write into our apps, or is it something that just happens when a user upgrades the OS?

I'm working on an app and that will be released around the time of iOS 7's release (Autumn ish) and I don't want it to look immediately outdated upon release.

    Hi

    A quick search of the Q&A](http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/153484/ios-7-compatibility-with-titanium-app) and a look on the (developer blog would have shown you that these things are obviously in hand.

    For reference;

    • Fonts; these you can start checking now, titanium has built in support for all os fonts and custom fonts
    • Translucent background (assuming you mean the diffused background); this will "likely" be a new colour constant in the same way as the special scroll and table backgrounds are.

    iOS7 and the look are not set in stone yet, it is possible that some of the effects, icons, colour schemes and more will change or evolve prior to actual release.

    Focus your app on its features and then closer to the time play with some of the "flare" elements.
