ListView in Alloy

Hi all,

I am rewriting an existing app from 'normal' commonJS to Alloy. I am also trying to replace tableview by ListView. I am using SDK 3.1.2.GA and Allow 1.2.1

My view:

<Alloy>
    <Collection src="questions" />
    <Window class="mainwin">
        <Require type="view" src="header" id="header"/>
        <View id="textbarview">    
            <TextField id="questionfld"></TextField>                        
            <View id="askbtnbg" onClick="askBtnClick">    
                <Label id="askbtnlbl"></Label>
            </View>            
        </View>        
        <ListView id="maintable" dataCollection="questions" defaultItemTemplate="listitem">    
            <Templates>
                <ItemTemplate name="listitem">
                    <Label bindId="userName" id="username" />
                </ItemTemplate>
            </Templates>    
            <ListSection>
                <ListItem template="listitem" />
            </ListSection>                            
        </ListView>
    </Window>
</Alloy>

And this gives an error:

I tried a lot (clean, empty Resources etc) but no succes.
What am I doing wrong?
