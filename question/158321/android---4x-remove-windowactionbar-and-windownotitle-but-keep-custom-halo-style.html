Android - 4.x: Remove windowActionBar and windowNoTitle But Keep Custom Halo Style

Android is bugging me with Styling:

I'd like to remove the windowActionBar and the windowNoTitle by using my custom theme:

<style name="rsaTheme" parent="@android:style/Theme.Holo.Light">
        <item name="android:actionBarTabTextStyle">@style/TabTextrsaTheme</item>
        <item name="android:actionBarTabStyle">@style/TabrsaTheme</item>
 </style>

Once I add these two lines to the above code:

<style name="rsaTheme" parent="@android:style/Theme.Holo.Light">
    <item name="android:actionBarTabTextStyle">@style/TabTextrsaTheme</item>
        <item name="android:actionBarTabStyle">@style/TabrsaTheme</item>
    <item name="android:windowActionBar">false</item>
    <item name="android:windowNoTitle">true</item>
</style>

Removes the ActionBar and the NoTitle, Though the Theme for the Tabs gets ignored (tabs look all white)… Any way to remove the ActionBar and WindowTitle and keep the Styles for ActionBarTabs?
