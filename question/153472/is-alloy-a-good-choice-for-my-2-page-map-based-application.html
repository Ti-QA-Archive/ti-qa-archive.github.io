  • As I'm the project and engineering lead on Alloy and obviously incredibly biased, I'll keep it brief and allow other developers to weigh in. This 2 screen app seems like a perfect fit for a simple Alloy app. Beyond that, what is even more compelling about Alloy is that if you choose to extend or modify your app, Alloy makes it easier. Whenever you decide to add more views, platform or form factor specific functionality, color and asset themes, etc… Alloy will make it brain dead simple.
