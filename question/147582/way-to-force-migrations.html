  • Accepted Answer

    There's no builtin relational connection between models in Alloy / Backbone. There's an external project that attempts to bring that to models in backbone-relational, but this isn't something built in to Alloy.

    In short, though, the migrations provide a standard way to move data in a model from one state to another elegantly in your app. Alloy is never going to try and be too smart and do too much automatically with them as there are millions of possible use cases. You have the freedom to use and Javascript and Titanium APIs in the migrations, so you are able to achieve anything you ned for your own custom solution.
