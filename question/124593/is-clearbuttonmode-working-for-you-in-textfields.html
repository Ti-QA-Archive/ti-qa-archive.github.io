Is clearButtonMode working for you in textfields?

Can anyone out there confirm that clearButtonMode is working ok? (It's the little 'x' which appears on the right of a textfield to clear the field.

I have a weird setup here (due to foolishly trying the xCode beta ;p ) and I'm not seeing it at all.

Here's some code to try :

var win = Ti.UI.currentWindow;

var text_field = Ti.UI.createTextField({
  autocapitalization:Ti.UI.TEXT_AUTOCAPITALIZATION_NONE,
  keyboardType:Ti.UI.KEYBOARD_EMAIL,
  hintText:'enter your alias',
  width:300,
  height:30,
  left: 10,
  top: 20,
  borderStyle:Ti.UI.INPUT_BORDERSTYLE_ROUNDED,
  returnKeyType:Titanium.UI.RETURNKEY_NEXT,
  clearButtonMode:Ti.UI.INPUT_BUTTONMODE_ALWAYS,
  autocorrect: false
});
win.add(text_field);

Thanks.
