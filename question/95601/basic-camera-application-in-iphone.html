  • To answer your questions:

    1. Yes, camera support exists for both iOS and android. Video recording, last I knew, was only supported in iOS. But photos work in both (provided of course the device has a camera).

    2. Define "adjust" in this context. Are you referring to just the ability to rescale a snapshot to fit within a given size? Or do you want to crop the image? Are you talking about adjustments like red-eye reduction or other edits?

    Look at the kitchen sink camera example such as camera_basic to see how basic camera operations are done. For crop/clip support, look at this thread. If you mean something else by adjust, then please clarify the question.
