Android Maps v2 comes out black on modal window

I've got the v2 maps up and running just fine.

Annotations work, and the maps shows up if it's in a non-modal window.

As soon as I put it in to a modal window (creating a heavyweight window for android) the map just comes out black.

I need it to be heavyweight so that the hardware back button works properly to go back through my windows.

Has anyone come in to this issue, or has a solution?

    Well I just jimmy rigged the functionality by creating an event listener:

    self.addEventListener("androidBack", function(e){
        self.close();
    });
