  • Jim,

    Before changing distribution, its always good to Clean the project which forces a complete rebuild. This should happen on its own, but it doesn't take long and always better to be sure.

    That being said, a few folks have had incomplete installs of the SDK. Take a look in 

    ~/Library/Application Support/Titanium/mobilesdk/osx/2.0.1.GA2/iphone

    and see if libTiCore.a is actually there.
