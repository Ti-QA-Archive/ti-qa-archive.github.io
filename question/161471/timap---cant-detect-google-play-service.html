  • In my experience, this message is a distraction and not the cause of maps not showing up. From what I've seen, an error in your map code, such as using an unsupported map type constant, will prevent the map from being displayed. This message in the logs appears to always be there. I found this in a project when testing on a Galaxy Nexus (4.2.2) and Xoom (4.?.?) both of which have active Play account connections, and thus have Play Services installed. Once I fixed the map constant problem, my maps showed up yet that Play Services not found error was still present in the Monitor logs.
