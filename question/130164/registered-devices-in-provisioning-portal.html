Registered Devices in "Provisioning Portal"

Hi,
in this portal I found the sentence: "You can register up to 100 devices per year for development purposes."

In last, my first year as developer I have used 12 devices. Text: "You can register 88 additional devices." OK.

In end of december I had remove 5 devices. The same text appear. Ok.

Now I have renewed my abo and we have a new year. But the text with 89 device ist still here.

What is with "per year"?

Rainer

    Hello Rainer,

    This is more an issue of apple support ;-) But, the per year number of devices is not accumulative. i.e., if you renew it, won't add 100 devices, it will continue with your current device count.

    One trick, in case you need to get more devices to test, is opening a second account under your company name or your wife's name or so.
