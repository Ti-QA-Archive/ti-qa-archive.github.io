    Honestly, not worth my time to figure it out or investigate it.

    Why do I say that?

    Because if you plan on putting this in the Apple app store, then you need an sdk more advanced than 1.8.2, and you should be building with an Apple SDK target thats up to date.

    The exception to this would be ad hoc/enterprise distro to support older hardware that will never be upgradable to a 'modern' apple ios version.
