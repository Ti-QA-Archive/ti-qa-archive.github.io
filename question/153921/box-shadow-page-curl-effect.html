  • Hi

    There are no built in methods to achieve the equivalent of box-shadows from CSS. 2D transformations would not achieve it and even if you could get a 3D transformation to work, it would only be suitable for iOS as Android does not support them.

    You can either use images that are tuned to provide the effect and place them below the views that require the effect or purchase one of the two custom modules designed to assist;

    • Skins
    • Drop Shadow (iOS only)

    Neither of these will give you exactly what you want, you can get close.
