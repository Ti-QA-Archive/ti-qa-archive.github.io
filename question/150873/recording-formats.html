Recording formats

Abit lost at the moment in a world of recording formats and thought i would ask for some advice.

I am building a recording app at the moment with titanium which works great.

http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/3.0/#!/api/Titanium.Media-property-AUDIO_FORMAT_LINEAR_PCM

I am using the following compression and format

recording.compression = Ti.Media.AUDIO_FORMAT_LINEAR_PCM;
recording.format = Ti.Media.AUDIO_FILEFORMAT_WAVE;
Problem i am having is the size of the recordings are hug and when i upload them to my server a 5min recording can be over 1gig which i can then compress but i am looking to record up to 1 hour.

Has anyone got some sound advice on this regarding the best formats to use for quality and compression i would really appreciate it

Thanks
