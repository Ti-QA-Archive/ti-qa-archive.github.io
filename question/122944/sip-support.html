  • Titanium does not support SIP natively. Maybe you could implement the protocol using TCPSockets, but I think you could not access to audio streaming from microphone device neither to video streaming from camera. So the answer is no, unless you develop modules (in android or iOs native language) to access the hardware and stream audio/video.
