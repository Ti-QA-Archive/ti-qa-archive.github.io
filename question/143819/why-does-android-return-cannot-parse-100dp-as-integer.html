  • ####Just add the following to your tiapp,xnl.

    <property name="ti.ui.defaultunit">dp</property>

    With that dp will be the default unit for your app. (you won't even have to specify it explicitly in the code. So when you put animation.bottom = 100;, it will interpret it as 100dp.
