SearchBar with a TableView Header

Hi,

It appears there is a small bug on Alloy? or maybe I am doing something wrong here. But if I have this code:

<TableView id="table" onClick="openDetail" filterAttribute="fullname" searchHidden="false">
    <HeaderView>
        <View id="header"></View>
    </HeaderView>
    <SearchBar></SearchBar> <!-- or on top of the headerview, it doesn't matter -->
</TableView>

The search bar is not rendered, if I remove the HeaderView section, then the searchBar is present. Has anyone seen this before?

  • After moving things around for the sake of it. It appears this is the correct order / nesting

    <TableView id="table" onClick="openDetail" filterAttribute="fullname" searchHidden="false">
<SearchBar></SearchBar>
    <HeaderView>
        <View id="header">
            <SearchBar></SearchBar>
        </View>
    </HeaderView>
</TableView>
