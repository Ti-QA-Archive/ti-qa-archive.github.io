  • The guys at appcelerator are quite approachable.

    The bug system…. http://jira.appcelerator.org

    And if you are an indi or pro member then you should be able to post bugs under the community area.

    I am aware that at the moment the documentation is being updated and they are looking at ways of doing this to enable the docs to be updated, but it has to be done in such a way as to make the updates monitarable as just blind posting of changes and comments could be counter productive.

    At the moment Appcelerator are consulting with the community Titans on how best to achieve this.

    So really watch the docs space and it should improve shortly.
