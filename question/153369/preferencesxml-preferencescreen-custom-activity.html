preferences.xml PreferenceScreen custom Activity

I found out that I can call a Activity within a Preferences Activity. That is the closest thing, I was able to find, to what I wanna do. It is like:

preferences.xml:

...
<PreferenceScreen
        android:title="Misc. Preferences"
        android:summary="Click to see more options">
    <intent android:action=".activities.MyActivity"/>
</PreferenceScreen>
...

What I would like to do with that is call a Titanium Alloy View/Controller within that PreferenceScreen option. In other words, I have a window (View/Controller) that I would like to open when I click in the "Misc. Preferences" option in my Preferences screen.

It is possible in Titanium to do that?

  • Hello,

    as any other android app, your app has at least one activity related to it. After you build for android within Ti Studio, go to the Finder/File Explorer to your Titanium Workspace, go to your project, inside it go to build directory, then to android and open AndroidManifest.xml. YOu will see something like this:

            <!-- TI_APPLICATION -->

        <activity android:name=".EllipsisActivity"

    that's the name for the activity.

    Mauro
