  • Hi, i just changed youtube.html in this way and now the app run on device.

    <html>
    <head>
        <meta name="viewport" content="initial-scale=1.0, user-scalable=no, width=320"/>
        <title>YouTube Demo</title>
    </head>
    <body style="background:#fff;margin-top:0px;margin-left:0px">
    <div>
        <object width="320" height="150">
            <param name="movie" value="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y-mv6_YVqto"/>
            <param name="wmode" value="transparent"/>
            <embed src="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y-mv6_YVqto" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="320" height="150">
            </embed>
        </object>
    </div>
    </body>
</html>

    I hope it helps you
