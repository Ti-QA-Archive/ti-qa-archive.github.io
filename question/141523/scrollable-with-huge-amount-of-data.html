Scrollable with huge amount of data

Hello,

i am making a scrollable gallery app, sources of file are an xml on the net…

the problem is that the sum of the size of all files is about 2GB…

what is the best way to do such application (Scrollable view with more then 2 GB of items)..

    Well it all depends…

    How do you access the information. (in chunks? as a whole? using a web service?) From there, you can identify the best course of action you can take to implement you data retrieval.

    One approach would be to show a certain amount of items and load the following ones dynamically when reaching a certain row. Many social applications apply such a principle.

    Keep in mind though, that having 2GB of data shown as a list might prove not very functional in many cases.
