  • var slide_it_left = Titanium.UI.createAnimation();

slide_it_left.left = 0;

slide_it_left.duration = 300;

var slide_it_right = Titanium.UI.createAnimation();
slide_it_right.right = 0;
slide_it_right.duration = 300;

    and simply when you are opening the window just do as this

    win.animate(slide_it_left.left);//for right to left

win.animate(slide_it_right);//for left to right

    but remember if you are opening left to right the give your window right:320 and if right to left then give left:320
