Titanium Studio Crashes on Startup

I am running the latest Titanium Studio with SDK 1.7.2. Every few days or so me, and everyone at my organization have to reinstall Ti Studio in order to be able to continue working with Ti.

We have read about removing the .lock files in the studio folder and even in our project, but after a time or two that even fails us and we have to reinstall.

Has anyone come across a solution? I haven't seen one that works past deleting the .lock files.

  • I've had that problem ever since I installed the svn plugin (subversive) with titanium studio. The temporary workaround that I use it to delete or rename the .metadata folder under your titanium studio workspace directory. Ofcourse, this will cause loss of preferences and settings and project explorer clears up as well.
