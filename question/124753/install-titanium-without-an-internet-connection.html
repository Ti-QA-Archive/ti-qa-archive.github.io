  • To complete the install, you must have an Internet connection- as I think it must at least connect to the Internet to validate your community/Indie/professional or enterprise login account. After that, I've understood that you don't necessarily always need the connection. However, how would you get periodic SDK updates, download Android SDK updates, test any app feature through the sim that requires an Internet connection, etc?
