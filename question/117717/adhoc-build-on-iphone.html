  • TestFlight won't keep from having to sign up for the $99 per year dev program, or going through the complications of getting an initial build ready.

    What it does a great job with is helping you share your build with devices that you don't synch to the machine you're developing with, whether they be your own or your friends.

    I've been using TestFlight for sharing an iPhone build with tester friends (who are non-technical) as well as for an iPad project at work that I'm using my home iPad to test with (for now).

    It's a really well designed service that I recommend highly for any iOS development.
