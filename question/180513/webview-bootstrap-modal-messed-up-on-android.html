  • Hi

    Looking at your code, the first thing that stands out seeing as you are developing on Android, is that you haven't taken the scale factor into consideration when calculating platform height and Width.

    Here is a link to the documentation: Titanium.Platform.DisplayCaps-property-logicalDensityFactor

    var platWidth = OS_ANDROID ? Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformWidth/Ti.Platform.displayCaps.logicalDensityFactor : Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformWidth;
var platHeight = OS_ANDROID ? Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformHeight/Ti.Platform.displayCaps.logicalDensityFactor : Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformHeight;

    The example above should give you your platform height and width relative to the density factor of your device.
    Hopefully this might be the cure.

  • Figured it out.
    Turns out Titanium's Webview is not correctly setting the height of the body to comply with the height of the content.

    I fixed it by setting the body's min-height to be the modal's height onload.
