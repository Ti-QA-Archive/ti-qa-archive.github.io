  • Thats not really how the forum works. You post your question following the guidelines below, and someone answers a very specific question with a very specific answer.

    When you move to 'please look at my entire project' you are moving from peer assistance into a more client/consulting relationship to which fees are connected (unless of course you are an enterprise client of Appcelerator and in such a case, your contact person at your firm should submit a help desk request).

    If you are interested in pursuing a client/consultant relationship, expect to pay upwards of $200 an hour for that service by an insured, certified, qualified, and experienced development shop.

    Missing from your question are a number of important pieces of information. I suggest you take a look at the Using Questions and Answers article, specifically the Q&A Question Checklist. The missing information is critical to reproducing problems in a test environment and often indicates other factors that cause the undesirable outcome you are experiencing.
