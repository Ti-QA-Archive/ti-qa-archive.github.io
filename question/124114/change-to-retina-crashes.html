    Follow this guideline:

    • Open the iPhone simulator (search in spotlight)
    • Open your app installed in the simulator
    • Than switch to retina or iPad
    • Rebuild from Titanium and you will get the app running on the latest selected device

  • Open Xcode and open iPhone simulator from there:

    Xcode > Open Developer Tool > iOS Simulator

    Once in there, go to:

    Hardware > Device

    and change the device.

    Next time you launch the app in Titanium Studio it will use which ever device you selected in Xcode. If you try to do this from the simulator launched through Ti, it crashes. You must open through Xcode, as mentioned above.
