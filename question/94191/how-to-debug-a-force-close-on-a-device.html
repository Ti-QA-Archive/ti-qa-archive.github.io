How to Debug a Force Close on a Device

Hello,

I've been developing an application for Android. I recently installed the application on my HTC Evo and it worked perfectly, just like it does on the emulator.

Recently Sprint pushed an update to my EVO. Ever since, my application fails to work. I can log in and get to a home screen (which involves network access) but when I click some options on the home screen, it immediately fails with a Force Close.

The application works fine on the emulator. I also installed it on a Motorola Droid (the original) and it works fine.

So, from what I can tell, the latest update pushed by Sprint broke my application!

How can I actually debug the application? Is there any way to know why it fails on a device but not the emulator? Is there any sort of debug log on the device I can look at? I don't really know where to start.

Thank you!!!

-Ryan
