Hi

I recently created a Node.ACS project from Titanium studio. It's all working fine, and can be deployed without issue. However, I don't see the any apps listed under my ACS Apps in https://my.appcelerator.com/apps/acs

Am I missing a step? I have set the CNAME already.

    Hi James,

    The Node.ACS should not be viewed at ACS web console. You can view your app list through Node.ACS CLI, Ref: http://docs.appcelerator.com/cloud/latest/#!/guide/node_cli.
