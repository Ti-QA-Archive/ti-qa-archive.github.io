Sound plays back full volume on phone but not emulator...

Hi all,

I've loaded a few sounds and set the volume as I'm supposed to.

it all works just fine on my Android emulator and they play back at (say) 0.2 or 0.5 as appropriate.

However on my actual phone itself all sounds play back at full volume regardless of how they are set.

The emulator is running Google APIs 2.3.3 V8 runtime
The phone is a Sony Ericsson Xperia Arc S, Android 2.3.4

Any ideas anyone please?

Many thanks in advance…

  • I found an answer on another thread:

    createSound() doesn't work correctly at time of declaration (at least on Android). Add the volume property AFTERWARDS

    ie:

    soundAsset = Ti.Media.createSound({ url: etc });
soundAsset.volume = 0.1;

    Also you need to use very small numbers to see significant reductions in volume - although this seems to vary a little from device to device as some devices seem to run some kind of audio compression
