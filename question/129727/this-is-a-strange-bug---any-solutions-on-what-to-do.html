This is a strange bug - any solutions on what to do?

Hi All.
I am trying to get a simple button with a background image to work. Somehow it just doesn't work…. And I cannot find out why. It doesn't respond when I click it. In the same application in the opening window I have another button which works fine.
I am using Titanium 1.7.5 and building for Android - testing on device as the emulator is not working properly. My entire app is working perfectly fine on iPhone. Can anyone help me out or have some kind of workaround?

var myButton = Ti.UI.createButton ({
            backgroundImage:'myImage.png',
            width: '20%',
            height: '20%',
            top:'76%',
            left:'3%',
            title: '',
            color: 'white'
        }); 

window1.add(myButton);

myButton.addEventListener('click', function(e){
        window1.close();
        win2.open();
        myVar = 4;
        var reset = resetVars();
        });

Dearest Louise

    Louise

    Try using z-index for your button, to ensure that it is at the top of the view stack:

    var myButton = Ti.UI.createButton ({
  backgroundImage:'myImage.png',
  width: '20%',
  height: '20%',
  top:'76%',
  left:'3%',
  title: '',
  color: 'white',
  zIndex:10
});

    Cheers
