    • Mike,

      There is a problem when you have libraries in both /Library/Application\ Support/Titanium and ~/Library/Application\ Support/Titanium, you should get rid of the stuff in /Library or copy it to your home Library folder so you don't loose any of them. I have seen a problem where it picks up only what is in /Library even if there are other SDKs located in the ~/Library hierarchy.
