  • I had the same issue as you have, and I didn't find a good solution for it. The ugly thing about first opening a heavyweight window is that when you hit the back button you get to see another empty activity, then if you set exitOnClose: true on your heavyweight window you won't be able to open the TabGroup.

    All the windows inside my TabGroup have exitOnClose: true. So a logout button isn't needed, I simply close the app and reopen it again, and I can login. Pressing the "Home" button while the TabGroup is open keeps you logged in.
