    i think you've alligned the view by fixing their width and height.You shoul try:

    Titanium.Platform.displayCaps.platformWidth;
//and
Titanium.Platform.displayCaps.platformHeight;

    this will give you the device's height and width according to which you can easily arrange your views at any screen resolution.For more information refer this doc
