  • I have developed an application that goes the other way, stream live video to the application, but most of that legwork lies on the server.

    Based on the API reference I am not sure if this is possible with Titanium.
    http://developer.appcelerator.com/apidoc/mobile/latest/Titanium.Media-module

    Doing something through a webview would not be the approach I would take.

    Chances are you are going to have to input the video, stream the data in to a tcp socket to a server and rebroadcast or store at that point.

    Once Titanium has the ability to do this I plan on implementing this.
