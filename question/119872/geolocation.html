  • Update:

    I located the remaining bug. I am not sure if it is Google or Titanium. There was some extra text in the AVD field for GPS.

    Here is the fix:

    1. Go to "SDK Manager > Virtual devices"
    2. Find the AVD you want to use and click "Edit"
    3. Make sure the field for Use GPS = yes

    On my AVD it had some additional info about the camera. Once I cleaned up the fields the "geo fix" telnet started to work.
