    As a general rule, please have in mind that without some code we cannot know what you are doing there to cause the crash. There so many reasons the app might crash that you have to give way more details.
    Failing to do this will only make you to ignored or even down voted by the community

    Now going back to your problem it seems to be a memory issue. The emulator easily crashes when you are using many images, or big images.

    Try to add this in in your tiapp.xml, clean the project and try again:

    <property name="ti.android.threadstacksize" type="int">131072</property>
<property name="ti.android.httpclient.maxbuffersize" type="int">131072</property>
