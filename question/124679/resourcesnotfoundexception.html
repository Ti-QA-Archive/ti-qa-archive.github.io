  • Accepted Answer

    Your best bet for identifying the source of on-device Android issues is to fire up ddms from your android SDK's tools directory, click on your connected device, and watch the logs and various other statistics there. Often the description of exceptions and errors found there will give you a good indication of what is causing the error. And if it doesn't, share the logs here as someone in the Appcelerator community will likely be able to help.
