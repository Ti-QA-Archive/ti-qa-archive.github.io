ti.admob can show ads in emulator but cant in real devices.

http://developer.appcelerator.com/blog/2011/09/admob-for-android-module-tutorial-and-code.html

  1. download the latest google admob sdk, put it to the modules/android/ti.admob/1.0/lib/, then delete the older one ti.admov v1.0 have.

    and <modules><module version="1.0">ti.admob</module></modules>

  4. copy the example from ti.admob module to my own app.js, put my own pubisher id;

after the 4 steps,it can load ads in the emulator(mobile sdk 1.7.5+goolge

    A couple things to check:

    • If you haven't already, go to your google ad settings and enable google adwords to increase the fill rate of your ads. When people gets ads working, but all the sudden they are not appearing, more often than not it is simply a matter of having a low fill rate for your app.
    • Do you have any errors in your Titanium Studio console?
    • If you have no errors in the console, have you checked DDMS? Sometimes the Android specific tools can give more low-level, detailed information. If you see warnings or errors stating that admob was unable to "fill" ads, it's likely just what I mentioned in the first bullet point here.
