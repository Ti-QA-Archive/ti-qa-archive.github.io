  • Accepted Answer

    Alloy has a failsafe to ensure that you don't delete any of the necessary platform-specific resources during the re-creation of the Resources directory on every compile. Basically, if Alloy sees a platform-specific folder in Resources that is not in your app/assets directory, in this case mobileweb, it won't delete the contents of your Resources folder when it recreates content. This prevents any necessary platform-specific files from being erased. You can resolve it one of 2 ways.

    1. Copy the missing platform-specific folders (android, iphone, mobileweb) from Resources to app/assets
    2. If you no longer need it, delete the platform-specific folder from both Resources and app/assets
