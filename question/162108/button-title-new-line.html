    • General Tip - if you are going to make the most out of Alloy, remove the presentation from the structure.

      At the very least width and height from this should be in the TSS.

      <Button id="buttonTop" class="standardbutton" title="Some long piece of text\nI want split" />

      TSS

      ".standardbutton": {
    height: "100px",
    width: "140px"
}

      This way you can repeat your button style as many times as you want - even if you only use it once - all the presentation i in one location.
