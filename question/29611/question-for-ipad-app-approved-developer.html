How did you go about this option?

  • Answer to my own question, just in case some of your ran into this topic.

    Select "NO" and go ahead with submission, the store will determine automatically from your binary app. In my case, after uploading the app, it says "Compatible with iPad." on the application overview panel as it's built with SDK 3.2.
