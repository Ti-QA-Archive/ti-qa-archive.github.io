  • I don't think our implementation of JSS ever supported inheritance. JSS is not recommended (I don't think there's any active development on it any more either). Alloy's TSS files support a CSS-like cascade through the use of the app.tss (global styles), IDs, classes, and components. I know it's not what you're looking for. Perhaps something like David Bankier's ltss (Less-like TSS) would do what you need? https://github.com/dbankier/ltss
