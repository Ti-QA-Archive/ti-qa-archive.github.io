Cross Platform Layout Best Practices

Hi All,

I have been developing iOS apps for a while and am finding that more and more of my clients are looking for Android support as well which is cool and understandable. Before I said yep no problems I thought I would try and convert my simplest app and see what happens.

After some initial syntax woes I had in fact got it all working on the emulator. My only real issue is the that the views seem to not to fit on the emulator phone screen (WQVGA400) despite defining all my original sizes from standard numbers to be suffixed with dp.

I have run it on a couple of the available emulators and the larger screens (WVGA800 & WQVGA4432) it fits fine (may be a couple a pixels left at the bottom set to the background colour but I can live with that)

I suppose what I am asking is am I approaching this correctly, or do I have to apply further calculations to the widths and heights of any views to get them to fit perfectly.

I will post an example app.js when I get back to my Mac so you can see what I am gibbering on about.

    Hi Steve

    I suggest using percentage value instead of fixed value or 'dp'. Then set 

     <supports-screens android:anyDensity="false"/>

    in tiapp.xml

    The control will stretch out or shrink upon different screen resolutions.
