  • If you're trying to do what I think you're trying to do, try using:

    win.setLeftNavButton(backButton);

    in great_deals.js, instead of 'add'ing it to the window.

    Also, if you want the backButton to go back to the home.js, I think you just need to 'close' the window.

    eg:

    
backButton.addEventListener('click',function(e) {
   win.close();
});
