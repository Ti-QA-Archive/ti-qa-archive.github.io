    • Hi

      This code makes a perfect and sharp semi-circle ends on an iPhone.

      var txt = Ti.UI.createTextField({
    backgroundColor: '#fff',
    borderRadius: 30,
    borderWidth: 2,
    borderColor: '#666',
    bottom: 20,
    height: 60,
    paddingLeft: 20,
    value: 'hello',
    width: 200
});
win.add(txt);

      However I ran the same on the Android and got a very pixelated internal curve.

      Example side by side image
