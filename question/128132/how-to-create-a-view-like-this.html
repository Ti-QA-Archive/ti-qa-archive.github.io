  • Vishal

    I don't think you are doing this here, but I will state it anyway to be clear to others, a scrolling view, such as a ScrollView, TableView or TextArea, should not be added to any other scrolling view. In other words, the parents (and ancestors) to the scrolling view must be static.

    Regarding a vertical layout, you may have encountered some of these issues, particularly TIMOB-1798.

    To resolve your issue, I would typically use absolute positioning in the ScrollView.

    Hope this helps
