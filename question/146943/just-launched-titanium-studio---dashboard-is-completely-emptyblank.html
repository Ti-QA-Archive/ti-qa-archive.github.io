  • ATTENTION - SOLUTION TO THIS ANNOYING PROBLEM

    After hours of mucking around I managed to fix this issue and it is a quick fix. Go into IE and go into your compatibility view settings. Uncheck the setting that makes all websites viewed in compatibility mode. It wont display correctly in compatibility mode. Hope this saves many hours of confusion.
