    • Ti.UI.SIZE is used to give the size of the parent let me give you ex:

      if you have a view and give height and width as Ti.UI.SIZE and inside the view you add imageView and give some specific height & width of image . view will consider the childview size(IMAGEVIEW) height and width. so view and image view have same size and width. parent view will consider the child size( height and width).
