  • Hi sudhakar singh,

    Thanks for your concern .

    Step 1 

    a. Delete the build folder from your project.
b. Clean your project.
c. Open your task manager and there would be more than one adb.exe remove others there should be only one

    Step 2

    You can increase emulatorStartTimeout by running: 

     ti config android.emulatorStartTimeout = 600000
ti config android.appStartTimeout     = 600000
