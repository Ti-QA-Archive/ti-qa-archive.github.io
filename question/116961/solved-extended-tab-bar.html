[Solved] Extended Tab Bar!

Hey guys!

Just wanted to share my most recent project :)

This script will "extend" the tab bar in iPhone/iTouch projects to allow any image to be used.

This means things like larger full color center tabs, or even full color icons for all tabs!

All you need to do is add a couple lines of code to your project and include the file!

Full instructions / a small example are included!

Here is the link: https://github.com/mattapperson/Titanium-Mobile-custom-tab-bar

Enjoy!
