  • add your downloaded module folder to project's root and replace module tag in tiapp.xml with 

    <modules>
 <module platform="android" version="0.3">com.mwaysolutions.barcode</module>
</modules>

    your project should look like

     projectName
       +modules
       +Resources
         .
         .
       tiapp.xml
