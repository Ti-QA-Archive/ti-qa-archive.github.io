  • You DON'T need this:

    var itemNavGroup = Ti.UI.iPhone.createNavigationGroup({
    window: itemWindow
});

    Just do this:

    var itemWindowController=Titanium.UI.createWindow({
    backgroundColor:'#fff',
    title:'Items',
    navBarHidden:false
});

var itemTab=Titanium.UI.createTab({
    title:'Items',
    icon: 'KS_nav_views.png',
    window:itemWindowController
});

    and itemWindowController will automaticaly be inside a Navigation controller.

    I guess you were writting xcode and switched to Titanium, so I can understand why you tried to do this..

    Now, to add the rightNavButtun to this window, use this: 

    itemWindowController.rightNavButton = addItemButton;
