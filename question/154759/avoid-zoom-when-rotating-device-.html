  • This works for me on iOS

                function resetWebView() {
                setTimeout(function() {
                    webView.url = 'your url....';
                }, 500);
            }


            Ti.Gesture.addEventListener('orientationchange', resetWebView);

    Basically, I listen for a rotation change and then reload the content after a small delay
