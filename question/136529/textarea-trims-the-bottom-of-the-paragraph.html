    You've set the height for questionArea to 280 and that will limit the height of your questionText to 280 at max. You can either bring down the size of your text or make the area larger.

    When you are retrieving the text from the source you can do a quick word/character count and set your font according to that.

  • Give this a try…

    questionText.addEventListener('postlayout', fixHeight);

function fixHeight(e){
    if (e.source.rect.height>0){ //wait for the source to be ready
        e.source.height = e.source.rect.height + 18;
        e.source.removeEventListener('postlayout', fixHeight);
    }
}

    You may also need to force the parent view to refresh its height as well during this event.
