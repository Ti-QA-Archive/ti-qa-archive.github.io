Window Open Failing on second call in IOS Simulator, works on Android Device

I have an application that has been completely built using commonjs and is cross platform for everything I have worked with so far. Now I have a problem where I do the following:
I have a window that I am trying to add a tableview that I build in the offertable.js module I have built.

var offerView = require('offertable');
    Ti.API.info('offertable returned');
var offerT = offerView.makeoffertable(orgID);

On my android device I can open this window with the associated table, press the back button, open again, back button… all works. On the IOS simulator, the first time I call my module, I get the expected tableview returned and the window displays in a Navigation View, when I click the 'Return' button, I return to the calling window, but when I click to go back to that window a second time, I get the notice 'Application has exited from the simulator'. In the first line of offertable.js I have a Ti.API.info entry that doesn't even get fired, so it seems as if the offertable.js is not loading. I am using 1.8RC3 and LION OS.

I am thinking this has something to do with commonjs and the way things are called or available in memory in IOS, because it works in android, but I'm not sure how to troubleshoot.
