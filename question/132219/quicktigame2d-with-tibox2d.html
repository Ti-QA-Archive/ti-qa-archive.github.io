QuickTiGame2D with Ti.Box2D

Has anyone attempted using the QuickTiGame2D module with the Ti.Box2D module? Is it possible? The Ti.Box2D module seems to be heavily dependent on the normal Titanium views.

  • Accepted Answer

    I guess it is not possible to integrate Ti.Box2D with QuickTiGame2d because Ti.Box2D is heavily dependent on Ti.UI views, as you expect. Actually the GameView of QuickTiGame2d is a subclass of Ti.UI.View in fact, however the sprites and scenes of QuickTiGame2d are not the Ti views. So it is clear that they can not integrate with Ti.Box2D. Hmm :(
