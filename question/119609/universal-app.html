Universal app?

I see when I select "mobile" there are 2 versions of splah screens, but the app runs as "iPhone" on both the iPhone and iPad simulator. What do I need to do in order to make my app Universal?

Thanks

    You have to either use TiStudio or Titanium Developer 1.3.0 and select the "Universal iOS Application" from the dropdown menu when you create your application.
