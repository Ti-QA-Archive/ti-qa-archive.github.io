Working in Titanium Studio with multiple accounts?

Up until this point, I have coded and built apps with Titanium Studio for my main job, with our Appcelerator account.

Now I need to do work with it for myself and for other freelance clients, who have their own Appcelerator accounts, and I need to keep everything separate.

How is this handled with Titanium Studio? Has anyone here done this regularly, and could you share any tips?

OSX 10.7.3, SDK 1.8.1. 1.0.9.201202141347

  • Yes, you can do this. I mean at a time you can use only one account but you can logout and than login again with another account. I am doing this. You have to take care when you are using modules from market place. If you will use module downloaded from your account to another account than it will prompt you error dialog and/or message. Except that you can login with any account and continue your development. You can check applications created using your account from my.appcelerator.com.
