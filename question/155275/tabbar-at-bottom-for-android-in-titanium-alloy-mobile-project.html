TabBar at bottom for Android in Titanium Alloy mobile project

My tabBar appears in bottom for iPhone but at the top for Android. I want the tabBar to appear at bottom for Android as well.

  • Hi

    A few of things to keep in mind.

    • Your apps SHOULD honour the device they are used on and the expected pattern is iOS on the bottom, Android on the top
    • Older Android apps copied the style of iOS apps, which lessened the appeal of the app as it looked like iOS people got what they should and Android got hand-me-downs
    • The tabgroup implementation has been changed so that tabs can no longer be placed at the bottom to bring the compliance into effect with the Android ActionBar which is the correct UI pattern for Android which supports and inherits tabs at the top of the screen.

    Your app can function the same but should honour the users expectations, think "same but different".
