  • So, editable:false is not working for me with a textfield and here's what I did as a work around:

    tf.addEventListener('focus', function()
{
   tf.blur();
});

    So, basically, as soon as the textfield receives the focus, it dismisses the keyboard. It seems to be an acceptable solution for the time being, but I'd like to know why editable:false isn't working.
