Why abandon Titanium developer

I'm new to Titanium, but looking at Titanium developer, I was wondering why the project has been abandoned? Being a Mac user, I don't find Eclipse a nice IDE. To me Eclipse is the best example of what's a native apps should not look like!

Can I uses Xcode instead? If so, can anybody direct me to a good "getting started" place for developing Titanium app with Xcode.

Thanks.

  • Well, they did so (I think) because they considered an IDE is more suited to the development. In some aspects it is (debugger, git integration, etc).
    Sadly the guys like you (and me :)) were left outside this way :)

    You have 2 choices:

    1. Use the command line to generate/build projects
    2. Use the ti developer community continued and further developed by Adriano here: https://github.com/adrianopaladini/titanium_developer
