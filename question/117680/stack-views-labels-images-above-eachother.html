Stack Views, labels, images above eachother?

Hello, I've successfully created alot of different views. Anyway, that's not the problem. The problem is the position of all views, labels and stuff.

Scenario: First I have a image, top: 10; Right under, there's a header, 10px under the image. Basicly I just want to position the headerLabel 10px under the image, which has auto-height. (Cannot get the height, therefor I just want the to "stack").

I first create the imageView, place it on top. Then I create the Label, and want to place it directly under the image.

        var view = Ti.UI.createImageView({
            image: mediaPath,
            width: "auto",
            height: "auto",
            top: 10
        });

    var titleHeader = Ti.UI.createLabel({
        text: titleText,
        font: { fontSize: 20 },
        textAlign: "center"
    });

    itemWindow.add(itemObject);
    itemWindow.add(titleHeader);

And when I open the window, I see the image up top, but the text is placed in the middle of the window. Though the image has more height than half the window.

My problem is that I cant calculate the image height.

What would you guys do? Why can't I just display the views as block or something :P

  • On your label, when you do not specify a top/bottom/height, by default it uses the entire window/view height, which is why the text appears in the center (text in labels is vertically centered within the label).

    What you will probably want to do is specify layout:'vertical' when creating the window or view. Then values for the top property become relative to the preceding object. Thus top:0 leaves zero pixels between objects, while top:5 leaves 5 pixels padding etc.

    On your label use top:0, height:'auto' and you should get what you want, provided you also add layout:'vertical' to the window or view properties.

    Incidentally, if you want to place something to the side of a label, you can use negative values for top to offset objects backwards.
