Clicking an annotation in Android crashes app

My code isnt complicated, and I think i've taken care of the cross development.

                    userLocation:true

if(osname!='android')

    mapview.region={latitude:Titanium.App.Properties.getString("latitude"), longitude:Titanium.App.Properties.getString("longitude"), latitudeDelta:0.5, longitudeDelta:0.5};

    mapview.regionFit=true;
