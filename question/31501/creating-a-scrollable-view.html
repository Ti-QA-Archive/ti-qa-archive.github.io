  • There are scroll views examples in Kitchensink

    example:

    ButtonBar_ScrollableView = Titanium.UI.createScrollView({
    contentWidth:ScrollViewWidth,
    contentHeight:50,
    top:0,
    height:50,
    width:'auto',
    borderRadius:10,
    opacity:0.7,
    visible:false,
    backgroundColor:'transparent'
    });
