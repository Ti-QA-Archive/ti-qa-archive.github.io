  • In theory, most of your source can be ported from iOS to Android. Of course, those two are different in many ways, UI is just one of them. So, there are UI elements that are platform specific and you can have them only on one OS. You can, for example, mimic that elements on other OS, or you can have platform specific code, see: https://developer.appcelerator.com/apidoc/mobile/latest/Titanium.Platform-module on how to detect what OS is currently running from your application.
