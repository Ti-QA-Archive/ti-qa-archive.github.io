Android Webview content size is too large

I created a simple webview like below:

        var webview = Ti.UI.createWebView({
        width: Ti.Platform.DisplayCaps.platformWidth,
        bottom: 100,
        top: 0,
        scalesPageToFit:false,
        html: 'Hello world'
    });

But when I test it on Android emulator and device, the content size of the webview is too large and the scrollbars appear inside of the webview.

Screenshot

Is there anyway to make the content size fit with the size of the webview, so there're no scrollbars at all?

Hope for your help.

  • Finally, I know how to resolve it, just add this meta tag into my html:

    <meta name="viewport" content="initial-scale=1.0, user-scalable=no" />

    Thank for your support.
