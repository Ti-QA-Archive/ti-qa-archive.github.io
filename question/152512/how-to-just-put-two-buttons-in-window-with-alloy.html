How to just put two buttons in window with Alloy?

<Alloy>
    <Window class="container">
        <Button id="button1" onClick="startA">Start A</Button>
        <Button id="button2" onClick="startB">Start bla bla bla</Button>
    </Window>
</Alloy>

This will center both buttons, i.e. they overlay each other. Isn't there something simple like horizontal/vertical layouting for this case??

    "Herp Derp"… am I being trolled with this easy self-answering question?

    <Alloy>
    <Window class="container" layout="vertical">
        <Button id="button1" onClick="startA">Start A</Button>
        <Button id="button2" onClick="startB">Start bla bla bla</Button>
    </Window>
</Alloy>
