  • You can influence a toast's position with these properties:

    • offsetX
    • offsetY
    • gravity

    To show a notification in the top-left corner, you could do this:

    var toast = Ti.UI.createNotification({
    message:"Please Stand By",
    duration: Ti.UI.NOTIFICATION_DURATION_LONG,
    offsetX: 0,
    offsetY: 0,
    gravity: 48 | 3
});
toast.show();

    Afaik, there aren't Titanium Proxies for the gravity constants, so I took them from the Android docs.

    I hope, this helps you. If you need more customizable toasts (that also works on iOS), you may want to check out my toasty widget ;)
