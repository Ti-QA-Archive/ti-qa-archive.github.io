  • in a splitView, you have to assing windows to masterView and detailView. So, when you click an item in masterView, assign the new window to detailView.

    splitView.detailView = yourNewWindow

    Even better, you can use a navigationGroup, I suggest you to take a look to the KitchenSinkiPad sample:

    https://github.com/appcelerator/KitchenSinkiPad/blob/master/Resources/split_view_nav.js
