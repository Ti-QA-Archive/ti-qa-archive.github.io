  • First of all, I must say I don't understand why the great guys at Appcelerator haven't solved this issue, being the navigation component maybe the most famous and widely used by iOS developers. Anyway, I solved by putting that code in the folder ~/Library/Application Support/Titanium/mobilesdk/osx/3.5.1.GA/iphone/Classes, I reached pressing (Cmd+Shift+G). You must remember to do it again anytime the SDK updates (changing the folder with the corresponding SDK name). In TiUIWindowProxy.m you should substitute (at your own risk) the methods

    * -(void)setRightNavButton:(id)proxy withObject:(id)properties
* -(void)setLeftNavButton:(id)proxy withObject:(id)properties
    — commented April 22nd 2015 by London Moe
