  • TCP sockets will actually be the focus of this coming Wednesday's Forging Titanium episode. Personally, I've not seen latency coming from Titanium itself. The network performance seems to depend largely on the device running it, as well as the amount of data you are attempting to send. You may want to consider restructuring your data to be more compact. Again, depending on the volume of data, you could also see ios running faster than android as the ios JS engine outperforms the android one… for now. When 1.8.0 is officially released with the android v8 engine, the 2 platforms should perform more similarly with this type of processing.
