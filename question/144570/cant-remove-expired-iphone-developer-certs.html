  • Open the keychain and find your certificate and provisioning profile, select the one which you want to delete and delete them by pressing the delete button. also provisioning profile is associated with your mobile(iPHone) so go to your iPhone settings app and scroll down to see all your profiles and delete the profile associated with you expired certificate.

    Hope that may help? for more help i may need more detail about exact error message or steps you do to delete the profile.
