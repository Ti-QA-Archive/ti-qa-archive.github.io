ScrollView : get absolute position of elements

Hello,

I have a problem while I am trying to retrieve absolute position of elements, placed on a scrollview.
I have a layout vertical, and I am creating on the fly lots of labels, with top properties set to 30dp.
I am applying for each label an ID, for e.g. labelA, labelB and so on.

I am trying to make an anchor system, so when I click on a button, I would like to get a label ID top position, and scrollTo to it. Unfortunally, I find no way to get the absolute position.

I have tried many things. I am using SDK 3.2.4. I've tried toImage(), getTop and so on, but getTop is returning 30dp and not the absolute position.

Any idea ?
