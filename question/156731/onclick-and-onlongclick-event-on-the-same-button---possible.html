onClick and OnLongclick event on the same button - possible?

Hi,

I am trying to make efficient use of available space and so, instead of creating two buttons to do two separate action, i want to use a single button in two state.

Single click = opens a modal "A"
Long Click = opens modal "B"

When I tested this, it's always the "onClick" that is getting executed.

Does this means, you can't have "onClick" and "onLongclick" on the same button?
