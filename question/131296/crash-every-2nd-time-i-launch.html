  • I fixed this by editing builder.py and escaping the spaces in the path.

    Change:
    simulator_dir = os.path.expanduser('~/Library/Application Support/iPhone Simulator/%s' % iphone_version)

    To:
    simulator_dir = os.path.expanduser('~/Library/Application\ Support/iPhone\ Simulator/%s' % iphone_version)
