  • Hi Nadeem, good to see you from the other question.

    The problem you are seeing does not happen on iOS.

    Also it does not happen on Android 4.1.2 and Ti SDK 3.0.0 for me using just your code.

    To fully test I need the details requested above to match scenarios; but here is one way to work-around the issue. NOT ideal and you may need to increase the delay.

    var firstname = Titanium.UI.createTextField({
    enabled: false,
    top:'auto',
    borderRadius:3,
    hintText:'First name',
    keyboardType:Titanium.UI.KEYBOARD_DEFAULT,
    borderStyle: Ti.UI.INPUT_BORDERSTYLE_ROUNDED,
    width:'84%',
    height:'37dp',
    font: {fontSize: 22, fontWeight: 'bold'},
    left:'8%',
    right:'8%'
});
win.add(firstname);

win.addEventListener('open', function (e) {
    setTimeout(function () {
        firstname.setEnabled(true);
    }, 1000);
});
