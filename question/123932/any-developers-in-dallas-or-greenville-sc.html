  • Yes, I have. Some have been helpful, but others not so much. The most helpful thing I've found so far has been a tutorial on another website, but even it just walks you through the steps of what code to put, and I need a bit of coaching on the concepts. I feel certain if I had a human to talk to and bounce questions off of for an hour or two, I'd easily get past that hump, so to speak.

    As an example, when I started my switch from FileMaker to MySQL as a database, I kept getting stuck in this concept of establishing relationships. In FileMaker, you must predefine relationships. In MySQL, not so much. After a number of leading questions to a co-worker, I finally was able to ask the question… "So, in MySQL, relationships do not exists until you define them within your query?", after which he said, that's right. Believe it or not, that fundamental conceptual question was all I needed. I already understood enough of the basics to teach myself the rest.
