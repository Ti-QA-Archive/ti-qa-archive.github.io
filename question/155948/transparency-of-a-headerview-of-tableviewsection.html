Transparency of a HeaderView of TableViewSection

Hello there!

I'm trying to make the background of my HeaderView transparent. Of course you can do this in the tss file by using backgroundColor: &#39;transparent&#39;, but when I'm scrolling, the text of this HeaderView is sometimes unreadable because of the TableViewRows I got. Because of it's transparency, you can see the TableViewRow through the TableViewSection. Any way to make this transparent to a certain level (that of the TableView)?

'Duplicating' the background of the TableView to the header is actually not an option, because of animations.
