Android - Add Support to Open Files

I'm want my app to open PDF files on Android (Titanium Mobile 3.1.2).

I have created an intent-filter in the tiapp.xml so that users can open PDF files in my app:

<intent-filter>
    <action android:name="android.intent.action.VIEW"/>
    <category android:name="android.intent.category.DEFAULT"/>
    <data android:mimeType="application/pdf"/>
</intent-filter>

Could somebody assist me in the second part, detecting when somebody opens a PDF file with my app, and then displaying it?

I'm new to android, so I'm still a little weak on the Android specific features of Titanium Mobile.
