"More" tab does not initialise

The more tab does not initialise the tabs that are within it. I have a button that links to one of these tabs but the window is blank unless I click on the more tab first. I have been unable to switch to the more tab automatically as soon as the app launches to force initialise.
Is there a work around?

    Open the window that belongs to that tab after u activate the tab.
    I do this in a project and works nice and smooth.

        tabGroup.setActiveTab(4);
    win_settings.open();

    where win_settings is the window assigned to the 5th tab.
