How to detect iPhone 5 or 1136x640

I'm updating my app to be properly formatted for iPhone 5.

What is the correct way to detect that my app is running on an iPhone 5?

I'm reluctant use the the property "Ti.Platform.model" as this would require logic to be updated once a new iPhone such as iPhone 5s or 6 comes out.

Is there a way to detect the screen resolution OR determine that the current device is newer than the iPhone 4s?

  • Accepted Answer

    Titanium.Platform.displayCaps.platformHeight should return 576 if you're on an iPhone 5 / iPod Touch Gen 5 device.
