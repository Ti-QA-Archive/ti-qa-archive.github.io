How can I access the Titanium Studio Premium Version

I have subscribed to indie package stating I am entitled to debugging feature in premium version of Titanium Studio. But How can I get it?
When I was trying to click the debugger icon in studio, it is saying debugging feature requires a subscription and please visit page……

How can I access it???

  • Accepted Answer

    Make sure you log out of Titanium Studio after purchasing the Indie subscription. You can do this by clicking on your email address at the lower right corner of the Titanium Studio window. It will show a dialog box that says "Are you sure…?". Click "OK" and then Titanium Studio should close. Restart Titanium Studio and you should be "logged in" as an Indie Developer.

    You know it worked when the little bug icon in the App Explorer or Project Explorer tabs is a solid color (and is now clickable).

    Also remember that you need the 1.7.0 SDK.
    http://preview.appcelerator.com/studio/
