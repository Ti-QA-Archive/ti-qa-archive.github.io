Animate borderRadius - Trying to make a circle grow

Hey i'm stuck i have a view with a borderRadius to create a circle, trouble is I'm animating the size of the view and the borderRadius stays the same meaning it doesn't stay as a circle. It seems you cannot use percentages or animate the borderRadius. Any ideas? Heres my code:

var view = Titanium.UI.createView({
  backgroundColor:'transparent',
  width:2,
  height:2,
  borderRadius:1,
  borderWidth:2,
  borderColor:'white',
});
var animation = Titanium.UI.createAnimation();
animation.height = 30;
animation.width = 30;
animation.borderRadius = 15;
animation.duration = 1000;

view.animate(animation);

win1.add(view);
