    Well, from looking at your diagnostic output, here's what's wrong with setup.

    ###Your JAVA_HOME is wrong

    • Your JAVA_HOME should be C:\Java\jdk1.6.0_25 (you have two different directories in yours)
      ###Your PATHis incomplete.
    • It should contain the Java executables directory C:\Java\jdk1.6.0_25\bin. (this is why javac isn't recognized as a command)
    • It should contain the android platform tools directory [android-sdk]\platform-tools. (this is why adb isn't recognized as a command)
    • It should also contain the [android-sdk]\tools. (this is why android isn't recognized as a command)

    Where [android-sdk] is replaced by the location where you installed your Android SDK. (ex. C:\android-sdk)

    Keep in mind that all the directories from your PATH environment variable must be separated by ;.
