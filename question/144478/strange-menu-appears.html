Strange menu appears

Hi,

I'm using Titanium with my HTC One X. When testing on my device, I get this strange menu, with 3 dots in the middle, in the bottom of the screen. No actions attached. How do I get rid of it - or put it to good use?

See image: http://bit.ly/SCpduS

    In your tiapp.xml, you need to update your targetSDK for android.

    — answered November 7th 2012 by Stephen Feather
    permalink
    2 Comments

    • <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
        <tool-api-level>8</tool-api-level>
        <manifest>
            <uses-sdk android:minSdkVersion="14" android:targetSdkVersion="16"/>
        </manifest>
</android>

      Not necessarily level 16 (this is from the tiapp.xml of a JB or higher tablet only application) but needs to be 12 or higher.
