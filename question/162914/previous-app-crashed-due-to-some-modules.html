    Well,

    You modules should be in {project}&#x2F;modules or in $HOME&#x2F;Library&#x2F;Application Support&#x2F;Titanium&#x2F;modules

    Be sure they actually exist. Be sure your tiapp.xml accurately reflects the modules and legit versions if applicable.
