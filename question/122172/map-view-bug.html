    • Another thing you can try: modify the default AndroidManifest.xml (build folder) so that

      android:theme="@android:style/Theme.Translucent.NoTitleBar.Fullscreen"

      becomes

      android:theme="@android:style/Theme.Translucent"

      To do so, check at http://wiki.appcelerator.org/display/guides/Maintaining+a+Custom+AndroidManifest.xml
