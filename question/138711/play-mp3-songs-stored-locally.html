PLay mp3 songs stored locally

Hi all,

I want to play local mp3 files when one of the several button is clicked. I have 17 buttons on a screen , each corresponds to different song. My question is where should we store the mp3 files ,in the resource folder or the filesystem ? Like for storing the app images we generally use a separate folder say img likewise , should i create a folder in resource folder and save all the mp3 files or is there any better way.Is there any way to compress the mp3 files to make the app size small . ?

  • You can store your mp3 files in a folder the same way you store your images to keep them separate from the rest of your files. There are ways to compress mp3 files, but for the most part, an mp3 file is already pretty compressed, which is why its such a popular format. If you want to keep your app size small, you can ship the app without the mp3 files and then download them on either first run, or the first time it is played.
