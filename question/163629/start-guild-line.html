    • Nick is absolutely correct. You best first step is to read the bountiful documentation that is now available. When we old guys started, we didn't have such luxuries. :sad_violin_plays:

      Another great resource to be checking on regularly is the new tidev.io site put together by some folks from the community. Some of it might be over your head at times, but a lot of small quick tips appear that benefit the new and the old alike.

      A weekly feature on tidev is from Jeff Bonnes. He collects news from the web about titanium, and often has links to articles about titanium and links to apps developed in titanium, so you can take a look at what is possible.
