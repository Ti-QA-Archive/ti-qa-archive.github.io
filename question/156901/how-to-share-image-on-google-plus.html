  • As far as I know, you can't. But have a look around the Picassa API, since there may be a way through that.

    I've been able to share urls (often with photos from a system of mine which provide the correct meta data for Google to automatically pull in a thumbnail) to Google Plus within a Ti app by having a window with a webView open to : &#39;https:&#x2F;&#x2F;plus.google.com&#x2F;share?url=&#39;+encodeURI(some_url_with_metadata)
