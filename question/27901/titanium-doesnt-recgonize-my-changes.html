Titanium doesn't recgonize my changes

Hi everyone,

I have a problem. I'm developing a project, and everythings works fine until now. The last time I change my source, titanium didn't change the the source that was deployed to the emulator.
I tried to restart Titanium and the emulator and launch the project again, but that didn't help.
I tried to change the version in the tiapp.xml to force a rebuild. What's happening now is, that I get the message "[INFO] Detected tiapp.xml change, forcing full re-build…" and thats it. Titanium doesn't go any further. I've waited about 10 min and nothing happend. Can anybody help me please?

Thank you :)
