    I had a similar issue. Although this isn't exactly a solution to your problem, a nice work around I used was to use my own, higher res image and create my own button. Simply use an on click listener to call Ti.Facebook.authorize(), and it behaves more or less identically to the build in facebook button.

    var loginButton = Ti.UI.createButton({
    backgroundImage:'/images/fb_login_button.png',
    backgroundSelectedImage:'/images/fb_login_button_selected.png',
    bottom: '20dp',
    height:'50dp',
    width: '250dp'
});

loginButton.addEventListener('click', function()
{
    Ti.Facebook.authorize();
})
