    We use gennymotion.
    Its a x86 based emulator that runs blazingly fast.

    Ti 3.2.0 brings built in support for gennymotion.

    The only drawback (but it exposes a larger issue with your released apps) is that any module you use needs to have been built post ti 3.1.3 in order to have x86 support. The problem here is NOT Ti, but with module developers who released once and haven't updated their work.
