  • If you are talking about the big red full screen "PROGRAMMER SCREWED UP" errors, those also say they will not show up in production.

    Try/Catch/Finally (too many forget the Finally to let users out of a bad trap) when used with caution and wisdom (these carry some serious overhead) are a good way to catch and control problems that may occur from outside sources (bad remote data, no remote data) that would hinder a users pleasant experience.
