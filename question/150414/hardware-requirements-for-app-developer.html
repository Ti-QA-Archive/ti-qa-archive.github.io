Hardware requirements for App Developer

Hi,

I have just started my App Development journey and for the last couple of weeks have been busy learning how to use Appcelerator.

I currently use Appcelerator on a Windows PC so am restricted to creating Android apps, however as I own an iPhone and an iPad I cannot test any of the apps I create on an actual phone (Android emulator is painfully slow)

I was therefore looking at purchasing an Android Phone and Tablet but don't want to spend out a fortune on brand new devices or get stuck in a long contract.

Do I actually need the latest model or can I just get an old handset from eBay ?

Having never owned an Android based smart phone, I have no idea on what spec or o/s I should be looking for ?

Any recommendations for phones/tablets makes/models ? (anything but Samsung as I wont buy anything from that brand)

Thanks
David

  • You don't need an expensive phone to develop and test Android application. Just make sure that you are at least running Android 2.2 or higher.

    Older version of Android will still allow you to develop, but you will have to use older versions of the Titanium SDK. Which I would recommend against since those older versions are not supported.

    You can have a look at the Compatibility Matrix if you require more specific information.

    Hope this made it clear for you.
