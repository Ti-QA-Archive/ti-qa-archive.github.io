  • did you install Android NDK?
    did you fill out the variables in the "build.properties" file?

    here is a example file, you have to adjust these paths to match the locations of the sdks and ndk on your drive

    titanium.platform=/Users/mo/Library/Application Support/Titanium/mobilesdk/osx/3.2.3.GA/android
android.platform=/Users/mo/Documents/AndroidSDK/platforms/android-19
google.apis=/Users/mo/Documents/AndroidSDK/add-ons/addon-google_apis-google-19
android.ndk=/Users/mo/Documents/android-ndk-r8e
