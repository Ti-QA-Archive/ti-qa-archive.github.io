Message "An application restart is required" fires incorrectly.

The simple solution is the add this properties in tiapp.xml to costumize this Message .

    <property name="ti.android.bug2373.disableDetection" type="bool">false</property>
<property name="ti.android.bug2373.disableDetection" type="bool">false</property>
<property name="ti.android.bug2373.restartDelay" type="int">500</property>
<property name="ti.android.bug2373.finishDelay" type="int">0</property>
<property name="ti.android.bug2373.skipAlert" type="bool">true</property>
<property name="ti.android.bug2373.message"></property>
<property name="ti.android.bug2373.title"></property>
<property name="ti.android.bug2373.buttonText"></property>

Those are the important properties to manage the problem.

For me i use just <property name="ti.android.bug2373.skipAlert" type="bool">true</property>
to skip the alert message .

Good Luck
