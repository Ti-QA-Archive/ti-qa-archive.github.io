Android.Service addition in tiapp.xml returns INSTALL_PARSE_FAILED_MANIFEST_MALFORMED

I am deploying to Galaxy S4Active device using 3.1.1.GA with the KitchenSink android_service.js and testservice.js files. I have changed the tiapp.xml file to reflect the changes below which were extracted from http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/3.0/#!/api/Titanium.Android.Service

<android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
    <services>
            <service url="testservice.js" type="interval"/>
    </services>
</android>

Fails to load and returns:
[ERROR] Failed installing AB0C123D4E.com.website.projectname: pkg: /data/local/tmp/app.apk
Failure [INSTALL_PARSE_FAILED_MANIFEST_MALFORMED]

Restoring the above to read as follows,

<android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android"/>

works.

I've tried placing testservice.js in both Resources/android/ as well as the Resources/ folders, placing a forward slash in front of the url and adding additional lines from the <android> section of KitchenSink's tiapp.xml, all in mixed pairings/configurations with no change. No camel casing nor period placement resolutions found in other posts seem to pertain. Works in KitchenSink. Any ideas?

  • Hello,
    I think you are missing the tag manifest before and after services.

    <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
    <manifest>
        <uses-sdk android:minSdkVersion="7" />
        <supports-screens
            android:smallScreens="false"
            android:normalScreens="true"
            android:largeScreens="true"
            android:anyDensity="false"
        />
    </manifest>
</android>
