  • There is not a great deal of information yet regarding the Google TV market, but be sure to review this blog post if you have not already: http://android-developers.blogspot.com/2011/08/preview-of-google-tv-add-on-for-android.html

    In it they state that the existing Android market will be accessible from your Google TV, but only apps that conform to the requirements will be present in the Android market from a Google TV. This is just like how they filter apps in the market now based on minimum SDKs and device requirements. You will have to do things like disable touchscreen support just to be able to make your app accessible on the Google TV. The details of how to prepare an Android app for Google TV are contained in the above blog post, and the other links contained in it.
