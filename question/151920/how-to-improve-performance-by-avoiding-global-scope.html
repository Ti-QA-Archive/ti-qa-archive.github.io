  • No, that shouldn't impact performance one way or the other. Those functions are contained by the generated controller, so they aren't actually global, they are technically private to instances of your controller. This should impact efficiency or performance in no way.

    Also, I highly discourage Ti.include() and suggest getting on the CommonJS train: https://wiki.appcelerator.org/display/guides/CommonJS+Modules+in+Titanium
