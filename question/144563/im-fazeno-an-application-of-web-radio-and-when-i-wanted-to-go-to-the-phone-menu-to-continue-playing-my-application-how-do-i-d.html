  • Have you set Titanium.Media.audioSessionMode in your app.js?

    Titanium.Media.audioSessionMode = Ti.Media.AUDIO_SESSION_MODE_PLAYBACK;

    That lets the Titanium.Media.AudioPlayer play in the background on android just fine (local and mp3 stream)
