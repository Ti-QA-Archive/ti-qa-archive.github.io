  • http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/92311/show-and-hide-the-tabgroup

    I assume you mean the TabGroup. This method worked well for me.

    Basically, to hide it, do:

    tabGroup.animate({bottom:-50,duration:500});

    And to Show it, do:

    tabGroup.animate({bottom:0,duration:500});
