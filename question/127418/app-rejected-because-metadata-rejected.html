  • Well, you should delete the default ones for one.

    When you're creating our app, you have to submit a 512x512 image. This is what is displayed in iTunes. Your app icon should ALSO be this image, but at the 52x52 resolution.

    Are you compiling and submitting your application through Xcode? If so, when you open the project, on the directory structure on the left under Resources, delete the default ones and drag your own into that folder.
