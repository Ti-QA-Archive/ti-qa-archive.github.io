Audio streaming app approved - thanks Titanium!

So after many months of dev, I finally submitted my iOS app and it got approved on the first try. It took 1 week for Apple to review it and within a couple hours of being In Review, it was Ready for Sale. I'm pretty happy. I was a bit worried about how much streaming the app does (and allows for over cell connection).
The app is free and is in support of http://dubplate.fm. It allows users to listen to the 4 radio stations and also listen to the complete archive of dj uploaded mixes. It also allows for chatting (via a webview).
The app uses google analytics and I was glad to see that Apple had no problems with this. I had read that some forms of user tracking were not allowed and there seemed to be some confusion about whether google analytics is ok to use.
Despite the problems and frustrations I had while developing it, I'm happy to see that Titanium is a viable solution for iOS apps.

Check it out - Dubplate.fm - on iTunes
