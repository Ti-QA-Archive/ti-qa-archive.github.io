  • HealthKit: TIMOB-17333 They say it's on the radar, but not on the schedule. That said, there's already a native module that supports it, so why wait?

    Google Fit: Nobody seems to have requested such a feature in JIRA. However, just like HealthKit, someone already wrote a module. It may not be feature complete, but welcome to the wonderful world of Open Source Software. You could finish it yourself, bribe or commission the author, or hire someone else to work on it. If you want Appcelerator to do anything with it, JIRA is the place to file a ticket. Use TIMOB-17333 as a template for what you should provide in a quality ticket.
