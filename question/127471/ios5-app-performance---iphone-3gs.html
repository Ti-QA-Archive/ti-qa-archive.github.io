  • Well, I figured it out. Turned out my database, which I bundle with my app and contains about 45000 rows, had no indexes and as a result of some changes I had made (I think I added a sort clause), it started to crawl. Adding some indexes to the db sorted it out and the app now runs fast. Yay.
