Appcellerator modules / premium SDK

Hi,

can someone explain a couple of things about appcelerator modules and api's please:

1) what is included in the different mobile apis that come with the various appcelerator subscriptions? Depending on your subscription you can have either basic, premium or enterprise API's

2) When you buy appcelerator modules ( like the barcode scanner module ) the price apears to be $19.99 per month. Is this correct? If so is it for the development time of the app or is it payable for the entire life of the app once it is deployed?

Any help much appreciated

Bill

  • Accepted Answer

    1. You don't get a different APIs. The difference is in the level of support and modules.
      That means that the free edition will get you no module at all - you will have to buy them from market place.
      We are talking here about Appcelerator made modules. The modules done by 3rd parties you will pay it anyway.

    http://www.appcelerator.com/products/plans-pricing/

    1. It means you can use it for a month to develop your app. Once the app is released in the wild you can cease the subscription. The only problem comes when you need to make an update - you will have to subscribe again.
