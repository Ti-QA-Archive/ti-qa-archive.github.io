  • Our push and email has been on a free extended trial since ACS went public. But now we're in the process of rolling out pricing for those. Existing users will receive an email about it soon, and http://www.appcelerator.com/plans-pricing will be updated.

    Our push pricing for iOS and Android differs from Urban Airship in two major ways:

    1. We will charge only for the number of devices actually pushed to during the month. Urban Airship charges for every registered device, whether or not they are pushed to at all during the month.

    2. Once your usage exceed's Urban Airship's free tier, our pricing is a fraction of theirs.

    The new limits and pricing will start being enforced at the end of September. However, developers have a month's grace period before they must start paying for it.
