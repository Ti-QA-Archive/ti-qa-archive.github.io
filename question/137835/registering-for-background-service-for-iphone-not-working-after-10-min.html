Registering for background service for iphone not working after 10 min

Hi I am used "Ti.App.iOS.registerBackgroundService" to run my application in the background for long time. But it just work for 10 min after that the application exits. My simple code to test until what time the application runs in the background is shown below and it stops printing the log message after 10 min. why this happening even I did registered for background service?

Ti.App.addEventListener('pause',function(e){
        Ti.API.info("app was paused from the foreground");
        service = Ti.App.iOS.registerBackgroundService({url:'bg.js'});
        Ti.API.info("registered background service = "+service);

});

and my bg.js just contains:

function notify () {
    Ti.API.info('checking feed in bg.. ');
}
var timer = setInterval(notify, 10000);

I am using titanium 2.0.1 and ios5 please let me know the solution to run the application in background for a long time.

  • Hi, this is normal - the iOS device terminates the backgroundservice after that time.
    See the documentation:

    • The OS limits the total amount of time a background service can run for after the application is paused, typically to no more than 10 minutes.
    • The OS may terminate the background service at any point to reclaim resources.
