    I figured it out.

    This:

     var label = Ti.UI.createLabel({
                        text:title,
                        left:5,
                        top:5,
                        bottom:5,
                        right:5             
                    });
                    row.add(label);

    becomes:

     var label = Ti.UI.createLabel({
                        text:title,
                        left:5,
                        top:5,
                        bottom:5,
                        right:5,
                        color:'#ffffff'           
                    });
                    row.add(label);
