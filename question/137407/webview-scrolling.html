  • You have a lot of white space right and under the content you want to show in the webview?
    Try adding this

    <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width />

    to the <head> of the html you want to be shown.

    larger example:

    newstext = '<!DOCTYPE html> <html> <head><meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width /><meta http-equiv="content-type" content="text/html; charset=utf-8" /></head> <body style="margin:5px">'+pre_tidle+title+newstext+'</body> </html>';
var ww_content = Ti.UI.createWebView({
    html:newstext,
    height:'auto',
    width:'auto',
    top:0
});
win.add(ww_content);
