Get view attributes after a click ?

Hi,

I have a view with a custom attribute and I want to get it after the click.

I have this:

myView = Titanium.UI.createView({
                            backgroundImage:'http://mydomain.com/back.jpg',
                            width: '150dp',
                            height: '200dp',
                            top: 0,
                            uid: 12
                        }); 
myView.addEventListener('click', function(e){
                            alert(JSON.stringify(e));
});

But returns:
> {"type":"click","y":209,"x":169}

How can I get the "uid" attribute ?

Thanks
