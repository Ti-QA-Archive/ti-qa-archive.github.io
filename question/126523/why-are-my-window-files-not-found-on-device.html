Why are my window files not found on device?

I got a strange problem.
I'm coding an app for android and iphone the same time. On the simulator everthing works fine.

in my tabGroup I open a new window with the url:
'windows/' + Ti.Platform.osname + '/home.js'

my structure is as follows:
Resources/windows/iphone/home.js
for android
Resources/windows/android/home.js

Where does Titanium copy the files? Or doesn't Titanium copy them??
