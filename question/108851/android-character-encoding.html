  • Bruno

    I think you need to escape() content before it is inserted to your db. Then, to use it, unescape it after it's retrieved:

    var window = Titanium.UI.createWindow({  
    backgroundColor:'red'
});
var thisLabelTemp = Ti.UI.createLabel({
    color:'white',
    font:{ fontSize: 20, fontWeight:"bold" },
    text:unescape("Ol%e1%20ma%f1ana%21")
});
window.add(thisLabelTemp);
window.open();
