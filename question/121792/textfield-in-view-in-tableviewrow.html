  • Hey Todd,

    This is because the views for a table row are already provided within the actual row itself. When you add items to the row, they populate those views. When you add a new view to the already existing views within the row, I believe something happens with the stacking of those views. I know at one point in time you could actually pop open your interface builder and replicate this behavior.
