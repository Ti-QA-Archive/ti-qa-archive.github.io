  • UPDATE :: PROBLEM RESOLVED

    I'm an idiot. I looked back to make sure I followed instructions correctly in the solutions to this problem from other posts and… I typo'd a fix to env.py that is in [this post] (http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/104711/could-not-find-sdk-version-110), at the bottom.

    Once I fixed my typo, everything was dandy!

    Still have no clue as to why it was working the last 2 weeks and then it decided to re-write its own code or whatever, but I'm not staring a gifthorse in the mouth.

    Once again, the fix in the aforementioned post worked for me on this issue.
