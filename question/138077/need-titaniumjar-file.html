  • I think it's in the /Application Support/Titanium directory. Xcode uses it when making a module. I'll paste in the file path from Xcode, it may or may not lead to a .jar, but it'll get you on the right track

    &#x2F;Users&#x2F;YourUsername&#x2F;Library&#x2F;Application Support&#x2F;Titanium&#x2F;mobilesdk&#x2F;
