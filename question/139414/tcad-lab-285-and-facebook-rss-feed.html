TCAD Lab 285 and Facebook RSS feed

The TCAD Lab 285 is an RSS reader found here:
Lab 285

In the Lab you tap into a site's RSS feed through the URL's found in the files: RSSWindow.js and network.js

I switched out the URL with the feed of my own self hosted wordpress site… and it worked like a charm.

But when I tried to put in the RSS feed of my public facebook fan page (which works fine in a browser)
http://www.facebook.com/feeds/page.php?format=rss20&id=147776638572171

I receive an error:
Location: [11, 20] lib/network.js
Message: TypeError: Cannot read proprty 'documentElement' of null
sources: var channel = xml.documentElement.getElementByTagName("channel");

My question is, if the FB feed is readable by a desktop browser, why not the app?
Is this correctable, or is this Facebook securing it's content and there's no way around it?

  • Short answer: The FB feed has invalid chars into it. That's why your app rejects it.

    Long answer: I've been playing with a web application(CodeIgniter and XML PHP Parser) and after long hours of ripit out my hair I discovered that FB uses some invalid characters into your FB feed. So, many parsers will be not able to understand it.
