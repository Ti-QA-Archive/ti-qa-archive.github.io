  • Hope you know where and how to edit Obj-C source files.

    In TiUITabProxy.m search for "(void)updateTabBarItem" and insert this line:

    [ourItem setImageInsets:UIEdgeInsetsMake(6, 0, -6, 0)];

    right before

    [rootController setTabBarItem:ourItem];
return;

    There is also an opened Jira ticket for this:

    https://jira.appcelerator.org/browse/TIMOB-18515
