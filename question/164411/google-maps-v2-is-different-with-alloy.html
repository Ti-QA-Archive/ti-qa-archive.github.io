  • The solution was in that post
    I was using google map V1 instead of V2.

    The sample "Geocoder" for alloy is false: You have to replace in "alloy.js"

    Ti.Map = require(‘ti.map’);

    by

    Alloy.Globals.Map = require(‘ti.map’);

    and in the view.

    <View ns="Alloy.Globals.Map" id="map" mapType="Ti.Map.STANDARD_TYPE"></View>
