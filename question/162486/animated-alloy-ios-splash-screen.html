Animated Alloy iOS splash screen?

Hi, I know to change Titanium Alloy's default splash screen you can just replace Default.png in assets, but is there a way to change how this screen transitions or make the splash screen itself animate? Thanks

    You cannot do so directly. The best you can do is:

    1. Make sure your app loads as quickly as possible
    2. Create a window containing the animation you want to use, then show it as the first window of your app
    3. After showing the animation briefly, close this window and open your app's "main" window

    Keep in mind that most users will see the splash screen only once unless the app is killed (by the os or user). Otherwise, your app would be in a paused state and when reopened would go directly to the screen the user left off at, bypassing the opening animation.

    Further, keep in mind that Apple recommends you don't use a launch image (splash screen) at all. They recommend you simply launch directly into the app so that the user can get started without any unnecessary delay.
