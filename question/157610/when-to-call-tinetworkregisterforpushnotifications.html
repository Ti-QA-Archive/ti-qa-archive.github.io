When to call Ti.Network.registerForPushNotifications

Quick question: When should the application invoke the Ti.Network.registerForPushNotifications, every time during start up of the app or only once at the beginning all throughout the lifetime of the app?

  • If you provide a switch in your app to turn them on and off, do it then.

    Also, using a boolean Ti.App.Property set by the switch to know if the user wants them, you could also trigger it on app launch.
