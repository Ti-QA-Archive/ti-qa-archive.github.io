  • Hi Niraj, I guess that your problem is related to the fact that since version 3.X Titanium stores SDKs in the $HOME&#x2F;Library&#x2F;Application\ Support&#x2F;Titanium, while before you probably had it under the root &#x2F;Library one.

    Can you ensure that you have the module installed in your $HOME&#x2F;Library&#x2F;Application\ Support&#x2F;Titanium&#x2F;modules&#x2F;iphone&#x2F;com.omorandi directory?
