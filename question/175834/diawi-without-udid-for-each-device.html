DIAWI without UDID for each device

Hi.

I have seen that people that build iOS native can share their apps with anyone through DIAWI.

Can we do that?

And if so how do we make the provisioning to work for all devices without adding all UDID's?

Thanx

  • Hi Richard,

    to distribute your app without UDID checks is only possible with an iOS Enterprise Account.

    But there is another solution: http://installrapp.com has a feature which receives the UDID of a device and automatically registers this device to your normal iOS Developer Account. So you will not be bothered to register the devices manually
