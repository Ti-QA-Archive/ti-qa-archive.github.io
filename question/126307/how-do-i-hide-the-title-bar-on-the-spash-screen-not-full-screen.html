How do I hide the title bar on the spash screen? (not full screen)

Hello,
On the splash graphic screen, how do I lose the ugly gray title bar that shows up on Android (not there on iPhone)? I was reading about how to revise a theme.xml file to hide the title bar if it's a full screen app. The app I'm working on is not full screen.

It's ugly. I don't want it. Nor does my customer. x(

Thanks for the advise in advance!

Best regards,

Chris

    Have you tried changing tiapp.xml as shown below… this worked for me but may hide it on other windows too - but you can add it back in on those.

    <navbar-hidden>true</navbar-hidden>
