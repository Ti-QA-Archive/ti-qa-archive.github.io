Prevent tableview that acts as a menu (facebook style) from shifting up and down after selection

The layout of our app is just like facebook and youtube (and 100000 more apps), with a mainwindow and a menu behind it. When you click the menu icon on the left-top, the main window slides to the right and the menu is visible. The menu is a view with a tableviewrow.

The problem we have is that the tableview shifts/moves up & down when you click a menu item/ (tableviewrow). This ONLY happens when the menu height is longer than the screen and some menu items are outside of the scope of the screen.

I tried disabling scrolling, moving, but that's not the solution, because you can't reach bottom items anymore. Also, I tried to give the view that contains the tableview a height bigger than screen (same height as tableview with all rows), but that results in the same problem: you can't scroll to the bottom rows.
