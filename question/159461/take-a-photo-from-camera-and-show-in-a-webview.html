    I would need to see more of your html, but after you take a photo on the titanium side, convert the blob to base64 like this:

    var imgStr = Ti.Utils.base64encode(image).toString();

    Then you can use the evalJS function on the web view to do something like this:

    webview.evalJS(' $("#your_img).attr("src","data:image/png;base64, '+ imgStr + '" );'
