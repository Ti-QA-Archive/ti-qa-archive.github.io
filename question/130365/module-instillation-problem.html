    Inside your Library&#x2F;Application Support&#x2F;Titanium folder, you should have these folders:

    /mobilesdk
/modules
/runtime
/sdk

    For what I see in your folder structure, I would say that your titanium copy is installed in other path. Maybe in /users/Library, which is not common, but possible.

    Appart of this, the correct path to the module from your titanium folder will be

    ..&#x2F;Titanium&#x2F;modules&#x2F;iphone&#x2F;ti.box2d&#x2F;0.9

    When you find the correct path to Titanium, you can simply copy the .zip file inside (just in Titanium folder, not in modules) and Titanium will do the rest.
