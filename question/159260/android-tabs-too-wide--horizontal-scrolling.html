Android Tabs too wide / Horizontal scrolling

The TabGroup in the Android version of our app is rendering too wide for the device/emulator.

The individual tabs are much wider than the image/text defined.

The tabs appear wider than necessary - maybe 30% of screen width each.

The tabs scroll horizontally. I have tried specifying a width and Ti.UI.SIZE without ant success.

Has anyone had this experience and been able to overcome this issue?

  • Hi

    Depending on your target platform version and actual platform version the app is running on - then yes this is by design.

    If for example you are using ActionBars with tabs then the actual location of the tabs is automatic, it could display as a spinner in the action bar, tabs under the action bar, scrolling tabs under the action bar. The determination is made based on content size, device dpi and orientation.

    Try your app on more than one device with different dpis and ascept ratios.

    Please provide a screenshot if you think the problem is something else.
