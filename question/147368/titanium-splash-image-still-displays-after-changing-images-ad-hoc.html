Titanium splash image still displays after changing images (Ad-hoc)

This is the last thing I have to deal with before submitting my app, so your help is appreciated!

I built and tested my iPhone app without changing the Default.png and Default@2x.png, since I figured it would be a quick fix at the end of my workflow before submitting.

However, I have now added my new Default.png and Default@2x.png files, and I am seeing a problem: While the app runs fine and displays the correct (updated) images in the Simulator, adding the app to my device and running it displays the old ("Powered by Titanium") splash image!

I have tried both in development mode ("Run > iOS Device") and also Ad-hoc distribution mode ("Distribute > Ad Hoc/Enterprise"). The app runs fine in either mode, but it is displaying the wrong splash image.

I have tried cleaning my project, and deleting the "build" folder. I have tried deleting my app off the phone, deleting it out of iTunes, rebuilding, re-adding, etc… No luck.

Any ideas?

  • From the bug report (TC-1660) provided by Rick Myers, in the comments I found the _build.js modification from Seth Benjamin to solve the issue in the interim while we wait for SDK 3.1 to be released which supposedly fixes this issue.

    You can find the _build.js file you need to modify here: mobilesdk&#x2F;osx&#x2F;3.0.2.GA&#x2F;iphone&#x2F;cli&#x2F;commands&#x2F;

    FYI: Not to be confused with the build.js (no underscore) inside 3.0.2GA/cli/commands/ folder.
