Have Developer 1.1, being offered download of "New" 1.0

I was having some problems with Titanium Developer yesterday so I deleted the app and the support libraries (I'm using Snow Leopard) and reinstalled. Was offered the 1.1.0 mobile SDK and things were more or less okay.

Now I see in the upper right of Developer "New Titanium Developer available (version 1.0). Click to download"

By my math 1.1 is newer than 1.0, I'm assuming I should ignore this? LOL

    Sorry, this is a issue with how our update services detect new release. It's currently not smart enough to know that you're on an unreleased version since the public version is still 1.0. Because of that, it's offering you the most recent "public" version which is still 1.0. You can safely ignore the message for now.
