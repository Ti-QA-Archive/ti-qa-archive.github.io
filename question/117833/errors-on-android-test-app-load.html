    Daryl

    Try deleting your build files and forcing a full rebuild.

    For android, only delete the contents of the immediate subdirectories of &#x2F;build&#x2F;android, otherwise you may encounter problems. Watch ticket #2852 to be updated by email of a resolution to this issue. Then click the Save Changes button to force a full rebuild and launch the app, as above.

    To understand the reason why you receive the error, and why it isn't such a bad thing, see How does Titanium work?

    Report back if you continue to have these issues.

    Cheers
