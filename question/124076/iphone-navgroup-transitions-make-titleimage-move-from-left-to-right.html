iPhone NavGroup Transitions Make TitleImage Move from Left to Right?

Hello,

I have a main window with a navbar, and a title image and it looks fine. The window also has a tableview list for a menu.

Now when I click on one of the menu items it transitions (slides left to right) to the new window, but the problem is the titleImage on the new window moves left (overlapping the back button) to the right.

Is there a way to stop this?

Example window creation

var news = Ti.UI.createWindow({
    url:'main/news.js',
    height:Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformHeight,
    width:Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformWidth,
    fullscreen:true,
    navBarHidden:false,
      backgroundImage:'images/aff_mobile_bg.jpg'
});

News.js

var win = Ti.UI.currentWindow;

win.titleControl = null;
win.titleImage = "../images/logo.png";
win.barImage = '../images/mobile_header_bg.png';

Any help?
