  • Hi

    This usually comes down to one of these things;

    • You have not followed the instructions in the documentation for Google_Maps_v2_for_Android
    • The entry you have added into the Google APIs console is not correctly formatted;
    BB:0D:AC:74:D3:21:E1:43:67:71:9B:62:91:AF:A1:66:6E:44:5D:75;com.example.android.mapexample

    Note the pattern of hex followed by a semi-colon followed by the exact same app id you have in your tiapp.xml file.

    • You are using an emulator, when you should be using a physical device for testing. Whilst it is possible to achieve with an emulator that takes a lot more configuration than you should be concerned with at this stage.
    • You physical device does not have a Google account registered to it. Google Play services are not available until you have done this.
