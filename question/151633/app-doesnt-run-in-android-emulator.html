    Hi

    You say "I was able to run my app in Titanium Studio – now it won't launch in the emulator". From this can I assume your app code was working but now it is not?

    If it was working and now it is not;

    • have you tried rolling back to the last known good edits?
    • what have you recently added to the app since it stopped working?
    • you are using Android and in particular - the emulator - have you had issues before?
    • have you tried, shutting down the emulator and letting Titanium start it up again?
    • have you tried deleting the emulator AVD and letting Titanium re-create it again?
    • have you tried rebooting the entire computer - whilst a common suggestion - a useful one in this sort of situation
    • strictly speaking Android 4.2 is not supported in Titanium yet - but many (including myself have had it working) - so as long as you have not just jumped to this - you should be fine.
    • have you tried the app on a physical device, were the results different?
