  • When you took the exam, did you use the same email address as you use to log onto Studio? (That's also the address you use to log onto the Developer Center/QA forums) If so, results should be reflected automatically in your account. If you did not, your score results must be manually marked in your account.

    This is by far the most common reason for certified developers not being listed in DevLink. Please, those of you reading this thread later, please use your Studio/DevLink email address when taking your exams. The instructions on the exam ask you to do so, but those instructions are frequently ignored.

    If you used an alternate mail, you'll need to email training@appcelerator.com with the following information:

    • The email you took the exam under
    • Your DevLink email address
    • Proof of your score results (the summary email or the link to the certificate provided in that email)
    • A 300-word essay on why you didn't follow the instructions on the exam that asked you to use your DevLink email address….just kidding on this last one. :o)

    There are two other frequent problems we see leading to people not being listed. These might not apply specifically to you:

    • People don't sufficiently fill out their DevLink profiles and are thus not listed on the site at all. You have to complete your profile to an 80% completion level before you're listed. The "completion meter" will tell you when you've provided enough information.
    • People leave the "Show in DevLink" option set to "No"

    One last tip, if you tag your questions with the "certification" or "training" tags, the Appcelerator training team will get notification. I found this thread by chance. I'd rather you folks tagged the questions so I'm notified and can respond in a timely manner.
