I need your help! I've created a window, and I've added a scrollView. Inside this scrollview, there's a view. Inside this view, there are a couple of labels, and a listview. On Android (TiSDK 3.2.3), when this window is opened, the first thing I see is the listView, but to see labels I need to scroll up the scrollview. I've tried with:

scrollView.scrollTo(0,0);

but no luck. Can someone help me? Thank you!
