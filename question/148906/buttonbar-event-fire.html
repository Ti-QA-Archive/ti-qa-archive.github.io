  • Cant you just simulate the event ?

    myButtonBar.fireEvent('click', {index:2, type='click', source:myButtonBar});

    where index is the index of the button to fire….

    • I think there is a typo here;

      myButtonBar.fireEvent('click', {index:2, type='click', source:myButtonBar});

      Should be;

      myButtonBar.fireEvent('click', {index:2, type:'click', source:myButtonBar});

      Note the type:'click' section.
