  • Simply move your alert into your callback function. Because now, your code will show the alert before your server has time to respond.

    Also, you might wand to change the way you show your alert.

    alert( db.execute('select * from produit') );

    (Keep in mind that this will be pretty much unreadable in the alert window. But at least you will see if it works or not.)
