Minimum Hardware Requirements

I might purchase a Macintosh machine, what are the minimum requirements for me to create Titanium generated Android/iPhone mobile apps?

  • A dual core processor with 4gb of ram, and enough hard drive space to store your projects and the various sdks and tooling would be your bottom end.

    The mini's will work, but have been reported to be sluggish at times. Not entirely sure why that is.

    An i5, i7 processor would be my recommendation with 8gb of ram. If you are buying a desktop, ram is EASY to add, 3 screws, pop em in and you are good to go. Cheaper to buy memory upgrade from Crucial and install yourself than to buy from Apple.
