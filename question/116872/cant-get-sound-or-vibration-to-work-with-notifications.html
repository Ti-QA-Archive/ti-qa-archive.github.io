  • I dont know if you are still looking for it, but I landed up at your question while searching for this but I coulnt find answer or anywhere else in the forum. Finally after so many trials , I finally found the code for it, may be it could help someone else. You have to set the default sound or vibration in defaults and not in sound

    Ti.Android.createNotification({
            contentTitle: 'title',
            contentText: 'text',
               defaults:Titanium.Android.NotificationManager.DEFAULT_SOUND,
            tickerText: 'Wow!',
            contentIntent:pending
        })
