Move rows out of a tableView

Hi,

I have a table with views as rows in my IOS app. I need to be able to drag them out of the table and then leave em in another table (found the movable att but just works inside the table).

Is there anyway to do this? i haven't found many information about drag and drop and even less information about drag and drop with tables.

Thanks in advance.

  • There's no functionality for this out of the box, but you could listen for touch events on the table, create a new "draggable" view based on the row clicked (not the same one in the row), and then potentially drag that over a new table view and append a row. This would have to be all custom functionality based on your own employment of touch events. There's one simple drag and drop example in the Kitchen Sink, but that's the only D&D example I know of.
