    Hi Tobias

    Currently Titanium does not provide a way to access the exif metadata stored in an image file. As a workaround for this, you can store the geo data in the local database (or possibly webservice) at the time a photo is taken. There are tickets at #3426 and #3427 to address this functionality, which you may watch to be kept up to date via email of their developments.

    Hope this helps
