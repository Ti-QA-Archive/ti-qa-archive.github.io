How do I figure out the "name" of an Android phone's screen density?

https://wiki.appcelerator.org/display/guides/Using+density-specific+resources+on+Android

According to that article, I can sort images of different resolutions into three folders, and, based on what kind of resolution the phone the app is on has, the app will draw images directly from that sub-directory. My quesion: is there any way to know what the "name" of the density? My Droid 4 is "hdpi," a Samsung Galaxy "xhdpi," a crappy old droid phone is "ldpi;" is there any method of figuring this out from within the app? Thanks!
