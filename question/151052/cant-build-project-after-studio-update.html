  • @John, you have found a bug in the Titanium CLI. I've filed a ticket here: https://jira.appcelerator.org/browse/TIMOB-13634.

    As a workaround, you can go into your "C:\Android\add-ons" folder and move the "android-ndk-r7" folder into "C:\Android". You will need to update your Android NDK path too:

    ti config android.ndkPath C:\Android

    Also be sure to update the ANDROID_NDK environment variable.
