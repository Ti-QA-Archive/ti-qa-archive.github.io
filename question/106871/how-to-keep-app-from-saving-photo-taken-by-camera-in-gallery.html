How to keep app from saving photo taken by camera in gallery

I've got an app for android, where I use the camera to take a photo, then load the photo into a view for use in the app. I don't need to save this photo once it's been used in the view.
I noticed however, that if I go into the gallery on the phone (outside of my app), that it is saving all of these photos into the gallery. Potentially, the app could use all the memory by doing this.
* How do I keep the app from saving these photos to the gallery?

I want to be able to take a photo, use it in a view, then discard the photo (and not save it to the gallery). How is this accomplished?

  • You didn't show us your code, but I suspect that among your properties inside the Titanium.Media.showCamera() method you are including:

    saveToPhotoGallery: true

    Either specify false, or leave the property out. You should also look at some kitchen sink examples such as this which saves the image to a file but not the gallery. Or this which takes a picture and loads it into an ImageView without saving it anywhere. The second may be closest to what your current app is trying to accomplish.
