Control how long android app stays active in "background?"

I have an app that will often be minimized by the user for long periods of time. However, when I select the task from the home overlay (when you longclick the home key), if its been longer than 15 minutes, the app restarts. I save all my information so this really isn't a huge deal, but is there any way to control how long an app stays open in the "background?"

    Unfortunatly you'll never know when the system decides to destroy an app. There are no rules like a limit of available memory. Apps should always be prepared to get destroyed and save their information.

    You can also add an EventListener for 'destroy' on your Ti.Android.Activity - thats your last chance to save something.
