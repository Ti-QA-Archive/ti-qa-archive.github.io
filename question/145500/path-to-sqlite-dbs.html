  • For iOS you can get the .file property

    For Android:

    var fileName=Ti.Filesystem.getFile(&quot;file:&#x2F;&#x2F;data&#x2F;data&#x2F;&quot;+ Ti.App.getId()+&quot;&#x2F;databases&#x2F;&quot;+ Nameyoudatabase);
