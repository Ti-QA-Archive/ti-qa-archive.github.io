    • Hmm. This may not apply any more but at the time if you wanted any bespoke entries in your android app manifest you needed to first perform a build, then go to the build folder and take a copy of the auto-generated ApplicationManifest.xml and place it in your project root. You could then add your app specific values and they would get pulled into the build.

      These days you can add sections to your app TiApp.xml that relate to Android and these get merged into the built copy.
