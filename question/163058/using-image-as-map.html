Using Image as map

Hello,
I am working on the university campus informative app in which university map plan is given as images. I would like to do the specific points clicked on images to display the building name with some overlay details.
Even how to cover the specific region with same details. because buildings are wider in the map.
Other thing is how to integrate the campus entrance with the actual geo location points to detect user's current position.

Thanks

  • Your options might be:

    • Use a web view that loads an Open Street Maps or Google Map with your graphic(s) as overlays
    • If your map graphic is 100% linear – meaning that anywhere on the map, X pixels of distance correspond to Y degrees/mins/secs of lat/long – then you can associate the top/left corner of the graphic with a lat/long location and translate pixel distances into deg/min/sec distances.
    • Use a standard MapView, especially on iOS you can use the 3D projection view to see representations of buildings, etc. Use annotations to add campus-specific details.

    Of them, option 3 is the easiest.
