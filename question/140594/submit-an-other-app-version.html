  • Hi Zozo

    Yes it is easy, just log back into the iTunes connect again, go to that app you created, choose add version and follow the instructions.

    Tip: Make sur eyou increase the version number in the Tiapp.xml file in the root of your app. This MUST match the version number you provided in the App update information or it will not work.

    So if your first app was 1.0 make this one 1.1 or 2.0.

    It will take a few days, recently 9-10 days for my apps, but should be getting faster.

    Good luck.
