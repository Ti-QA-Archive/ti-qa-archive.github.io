  • Hi guys,
    I just came across this question as I was having the same problem, Titanium Studio 3.3.0 on OSX Mavericks 10.9.4, and I managed to fix it quite simply.
    OSX Mavericks.

    Simply backup and then delete your .eclipse/org.eclipse.equinox.security/secure_storage file.

    WARNING! This also removes any other authenticated services you may have stored details for, such as CVS or SVN or GIT or whatever else TitaniumStudio stores. I was OK with this, you may not be.

    Cheers
    ~Aubrey
