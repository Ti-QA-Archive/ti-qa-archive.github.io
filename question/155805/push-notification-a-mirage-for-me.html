Push Notification? a mirage for me!

Hello to all guys.

It is clear, my huge problem is the push notifications.
Implement these blessed push notifications in my app is becoming something really impossible.

There are 1000 services that offer a service for Push Notifications: Those of Appcelerator, Urban Airship, Appoxee, Parse etc etc

I had tried those of Urban Airship (following this guide: https://wiki.appcelerator.org/display/guides/Push+Notifications+with+Urban+Airship), I thought I finally got there (during the state of the development notifications - using the device token - worked, but when I entered the state of Production notifications did not work)

Each service offers for notifications 200,000 types of notifications, a pain for me …

I want something simple, very simple indeed ….
The user starts the app, enable push notifications, and after that can receive notifications. I do not want to send notifications to a certain group of users, send notifications at a certain time, send notifications programmed etc etc, I would just send it to all users who have activated notifications the same message … a simple thing but it seems almost impossible to achieve.

Now I ask you … Which service should I use to have a basic service of Push Notification? (send the same message to all users)

Thank you very much, and I hope that with your experience can give me a hand
