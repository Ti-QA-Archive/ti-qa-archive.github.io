  • Simple modification to build.py resolve the problem.

    def package_module(manifest,mf,config):
...
    if os.path.exists(license_file):
        zf.write(license_file,'%s/LICENSE' % modulepath)

    my_lib = os.path.join(cwd,'libMy.a')
    if os.path.exists(my_lib):
        zf.write(my_lib,'%s/libMy.a' % modulepath)

    Be care with tabs (no spaces!) - it's python.

    And of cos don't forget to add -lMy and all needed frameworks to module.xcconfig.

  • add this line to the module.xcconfig

    OTHER_LDFLAGS=$(inherited) -framework GameKit

    where GameKit is your missing framework that is used in your library.
