    You need to make sure that the screens you are navigating through are "heavyweight" windows. Check this guide entry to see what heavyweight windows actually are, and how you can use them in your apps: https://wiki.appcelerator.org/display/guides2/Android+Platform+Overview#AndroidPlatformOverview-Heavyweightandlightweightwindows
