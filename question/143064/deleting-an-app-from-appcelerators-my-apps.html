Deleting an app from Appcelerator's 'my apps'

How can I delete an app, appearing in Appcelerator's 'my apps' page? I made some test apps that I don't want to be appeared there.

    The short answer is: You can't (well, at least for now)

    All you can do for now is to deacivate the apps you are not using anymore.
    Simply:

    • Go into the application's details page,
    • In the App Actions section
    • Click the deactivate link

    From there, the application will be hidden from your lists. I remember it used to bug me (having all those test apps in my profile section). But I got over it, like most people here ;)

    Follow this.

    1. go to My Apps.
    2. Click on App Details
    3. On the right side of App Details page you can See a Column having 3 rows (Subscription plan, App Actions and Billing Info)
    4. Click on App Actions and then Click Deactivate to delete
