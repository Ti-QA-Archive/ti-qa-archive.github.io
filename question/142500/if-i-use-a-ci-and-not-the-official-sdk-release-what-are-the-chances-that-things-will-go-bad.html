  • I mostly use CI builds in development because they contain newest bug fixes. Mostly they work fine, but sometimes problems can occur because they are not fully tested. I recommend that final version of application is compiled with released version or with CI build that seems the most stable one (usually, by the time you finish application, something gets released).
