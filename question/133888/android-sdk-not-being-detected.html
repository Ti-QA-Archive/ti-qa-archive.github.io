Android SDK not being detected

Application type: mobile

Titanium SDK: Mobile 1.8.1, build: 1.0.9.201202141208, jenkins-titanium-rcp-master-138 (origin/master)

Platform & version: Android 2.1. + 2.2.

Device: Android emulator

Host Operating System: Windows 7

Titanium Studio: version 1.09

Debug log: http://pastebin.com/cmVp2sMT

No matter what I do, I can't get Titanium to see my android SDK. I have installed almost everything (Except those that require me to make an additional account with HTC).

Here is a screenshot of what I have left to install: http://i.imgur.com/FNim8.png

And, I'm still getting the error message: •An Android SDK is missing. Titanium requires Android platforms 2.1. and 2.2.

  • Okay, I got this working. I updated to the nightly build, went into preferences->Titanium Studio->Titanium. At the bottem there was a "click for details".

    It gave me an xcopy error. So, I googled the issue, and it was a broken paths error.

    All I had to do was add "%SystemRoot%\system32;%SystemRoot%;%SystemRoot%\System32\Wbem;" to the start of my path variable, restart titanium, and it worked just fine.
