Installing on Desire HD

My apps install fine on my old HTC but will not on my new (just out of the box) HTC Desire HD.

I am using Developer 1.2.1 with Android SDK 1.5.1

I notice that the Desire HD does not have a USB debugging mode. It is connected normally and I can see the disk drive on my Mac screen.

When I click install on device it takes 20 secs to revert the progress message (implying all is OK). Then I look in my apps on the HTC and the app is not there.

Any suggestions pls.

Regards, Paul

  • Paul

    Follow the link from OEM USB Drivers to download the HTC Sync application, and install it to your machine.

    On your phone, experiment with one of the following settings:

    Settings > Applications > Development > "USB Debugging"

    Settings > Connect to PC > "Ask Me"

    I don't have an HTC Desire myself. However, try the above and please report back the exact instructions, so that other users may benefit.

    Cheers
