How to add click and longclick to Android Table Row?

I have a table that should have two behaviors:

  • check the row, preferably through a simple click
  • show a details page, preferably through a long click (in the future I would show here a menu to choose between details and delete, maybe)

I noticed there's no "hasDetail" button on Android, and I can't manage to use click/longclick/longpress to work on this: longclick blocks click event, and the longpress does not happen on the row, so I can't discover on what row the user longpressed!

Am I missing something or Titanium is just missing some features on this field?

Additionally: is there any way to launch a simple context menu?
