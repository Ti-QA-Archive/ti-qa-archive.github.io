  • Here is the answer:

    go to this site: http://builds.appcelerator.com.s3.amazonaws.com/index.html

    Find the SDK that you wan't and copy the download link.

    In Ti Studio select menu HELP->INSTALL TITANIUM SDK FROM URL - paste in the new URL and click 'OK'

    Nothing will seem to happen, you get no feedback, but if you're connected to the net, the process is started, and soon Ti will alert you that the new SDK has been installed.

    I've just seen my recently build app, and it's a lot faster with 1.8 than with 1.7.5
