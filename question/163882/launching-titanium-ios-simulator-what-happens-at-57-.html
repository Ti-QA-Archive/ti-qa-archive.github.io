Launching Titanium iOS Simulator: What happens at 57% ?

Every time I launch the iOS Simulator, the launch process wastes a nerve-racking time of 30 seconds at 57% ("Launching delegate…"). When I disable the internet connection it skips this critical point and starts up immediately. What happens at 57% ? Is there something I can do (I am working with the latest versions of everything)?
