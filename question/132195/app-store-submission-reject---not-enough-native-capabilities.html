App Store Submission Reject - Not Enough Native Capabilities

We just had our iOS app rejected from the App Store for not having enough native capabilities built-in. (In fairness, it's currently just a window that loads an HTML5 app.)

The app in question is a web booking app for a taxi company. It allows users to quickly and easily book a cab to come pick them up.

Here's my question: Does anyone have any thoughts on what we could do to get the app accepted? Has anyone else run into this issue before?

Some thoughts we have include:

  • Tapping into the user's data on the phone to grab their name and number.
  • Use native geolocation to suggest a pickup address.
  • Save previous entries for quick-entry.

Any suggestions would be most welcome!
