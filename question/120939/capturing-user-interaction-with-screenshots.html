    Hi,

    I just tried an experiment with this. While it is possible to trigger the Ti.Media.takeScreenShot() from a background servive and also save the resulting image, the problem is, as I suspected, that the resulting images saved are simply the screenshot of the actual app at the point of 'pausing' in to the background.

    Hopefully, this will save you some time! :)
