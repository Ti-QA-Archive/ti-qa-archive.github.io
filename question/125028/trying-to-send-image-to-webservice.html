    There's currently a JIRA item open around our base64encode function, so I am using a pure JS implementation at the bottom of this gist: https://gist.github.com/1174956.

    If you read the text property of the Blob and use this function, you should be able to post to your SOAP endpoint.

      because standard Ti.Utils base64encode doesn't work as expected.

var encoded_string1 = utils.base64encode( temp );

      after you can send encoded information by xhr or suds as you wish.
