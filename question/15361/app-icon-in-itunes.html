App Icon in iTunes

I've noticed that my app's icon displays fine everywhere except in iTunes under Applications. In Titanium I have it 'packaging' to iTunes.

Is this something that will be fixed once I submit to App Store?

— asked April 11th 2010 by Ryan Gartin

    This is normal during development. Your icon will appear when you package for distribution.

    If you are determined to see your icon in iTunes (during development), copy your app icon and rename it iTunesArtwork. Put this new file in your resources&#x2F;iphone directory, then rebuild.
