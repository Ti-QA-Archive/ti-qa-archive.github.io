BarImage hides the left and right NavButtons in a navigation group on successive window open ?

In a Navigation Group, TitleBar left & right nav buttons are added as well as an BarImage.
When a new window in the navigationGroup opens over the previous window. The left and Right nav buttons are not visible but their functionalities are working.
For eachWindow lef,right buttons and BarImages are included If we include simple title text to windows instead of BarImage, everthing(title,left & right buttons) are visible in every windows in the navigation group
*This persists even after reducing the size of buttons and Barimage

  • As far as i can see this is still a bug in 1.8.1. If you set a barimage for the navigation bar buttons will start disappearing the further into the navigation stack you go. For me it is always guaranteed on child window 2. If you comment out the barimage everything works fine. Sounds like exactly what i am experiencing.

    Andy

    • I should add that rotation seems to make this worse. Try navigating to child1 then its sub-child. Now rotate the phone (or simulator) and go back to child 1. Chances are some or all of the navbar buttons and title are missing but you can still click on them. If you can then go back to child 2 and it will be broken as well. You can bring the navBar back to its desired state by doing another rotate but that isn't a fix.
