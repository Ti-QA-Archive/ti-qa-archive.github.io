White flash Android when Hardware accelerated

There seems to be a white flash when the webView comes into the main application view. This works fine when the application hardware acceleration is turned off.

This seems to be an issue with the Android device and I have read countless posts and Jira tickets trying to find a work around.

I was wondering is this has been fixed at all?

Thanks

  • Hello,

    hardware accelerated 2d operations doesn't work the same in all the devices, since every single company is implementing it as they want.

    Workaround: you can hide the webview, load it, and then show it, after the event load is fired. Or, put an activityIndicator in the front of the webview, take it out when the loaded event is fired.

    Best,

    Mauro
