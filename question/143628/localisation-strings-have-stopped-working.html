  • Sorry, this is a little embarrassing, however my locale file is quite huge and I didn't notice a typo until recently.

    One of my locale strings had the following

    <string name="airplane-mode">"Airplane Mode</string>

    The double quote before "Airplane" caused the issue, after I removed it it was fine.
