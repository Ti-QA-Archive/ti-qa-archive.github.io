    • url is used for both internal and external properties, you are using it the right way, but what window are you adding the webview on. You have to create a new window, add the webview and call open on the excisting window with the new window as parameter

      var webwindow = Ti.UI.createWindow();

var webview = Titanium.UI.createWebView({url:'http://www.google.com'});
webwindow.add(webview);

win.open(webwindow);

  • The url property is for all kinds of links.

    You need to consider the downside of the webView, its slower then safari. The webView is not really that quick and you need to make your own button bar for going back and forwards or refreshing.

    So do you really need a new window with a webView? iPhone users must know allready how to switch between apps. Double click the middle button and go back to your app.

    Take this into account when opening a website.
