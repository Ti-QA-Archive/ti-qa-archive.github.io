[UIImage resizableWithCapInsets:resizingMode:] unrecognized selector sent to instance

Application type: mobile
Titanium SDK: 3.2GA
Platform & version:iPhone 5.0 and later
Host Operating System: OSX Mavericks

I tested My iPhone app in iOS 7 and it used to work fine. Later I tested it on iOS 5 and it started giving error [UIImage resizableWithCapInsets:resizingMode:] unrecognized selector sent to instance at app.js line 1. I know that this method is not supported by iOS 5.

Can somebody tell me which method in Titanium refers to this method. Since it is giving error on app.js line 1. It is getting difficult to find the error.

  • Hi Ravindra,

    iOS 5 was deprecated in Ti. 3.1.3. Then Titanium 3.2.x dropped support for iOS 5. It's iOS6+ only. You have to set the right minimum ios version in tiapp.xml

    <ios>
    <min-ios-ver>6.0</min-ios-ver>
</ios>
