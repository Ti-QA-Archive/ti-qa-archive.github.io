    Hi

    The start up entry point to your app is always as follows;

    • Alloy apps;

      index.js with supporting; index.xml & index.tss as required. These can be found;

    {appname}/app/controllers/index.js
{appname}/app/views/index.xml
{appname}/app/styles/index.tss

    • Non-Alloy apps

      app.js. this can be found;

    {appname}/resources/app.js
