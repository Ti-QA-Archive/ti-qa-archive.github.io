    Hi

    Xcode 4.6 is not currently officially supported by Titanium, you can try it but you do this at your own risk. Consider running it from another location rather than simply replacing your existing version.

    The Titanium Compatibility Matrix - particularity the xcode version information is where to check.

    Appcelerator do not immediately support new versions and not beta versions - unless there are special circumstances. There is usually a period of around a month after the official launch of any product ready third-party tools.
