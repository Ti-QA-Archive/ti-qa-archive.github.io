Index Problems with ButtonBar

So I have been using a ButtonBar in my app that has 4 options:

var progressBar = Ti.UI.createButtonBar({
    labels:['Nothing', 'Found', 'Bought', 'Wrapped'],
    style:Titanium.UI.iPhone.SystemButtonStyle.BAR,
    backgroundColor:'red',
    left:10,
    right:10,
    width:280,
    height:30,
    index:0
});

progressBar.addEventListener('click', function(e) {
    progressBar.setIndex(e.index);
});

And as you can see, I have it so when you click one of the 4 options, it sets the Index (Highlighted button) to whatever the Index of the clicked button is.

The problem is… In the app (Simulator and Device) the first option ("Nothing") is highlighted, and then when you click one of the other options it becomes unhighlighted and the one you selected does not become highlighted, although when tested the Index property of the progressBar does in fact change, it just does not visually highlight itself.

This can potentially be confusing to the user, and I was wondering if there was a way around this. I've looked around and haven't found anything pertaining to this.

I am using:

  • iOS 6
  • iPhone 4S
  • Titanium Mobile SDK 2.1.4GA

Any help would be amazing!
