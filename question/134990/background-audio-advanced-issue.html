Background Audio (Advanced Issue)

Hello everyone,

I have an app that requires background audio. I got it to work. I play some music in the app… i make the app lose focus (i.e. i just launch another app), and the music continues.

That's great.

So, there I am with my iPhone and the music playing in the background.

If I double click on the iPhone Home button, what's marvelous is that I see my app in the place where the iPod player icon is.

Do you all know what one I'm talking about?

You know where you double click the home button, it gives you a list of all icons / apps that are open? If you do this and then scroll leftwards, you get the iPod player. My app icon is now there (when music is in the background).

As you know, there's the fix orientation button, the left button, the pause/play button, the right button, and the app icon.

My question is this:

Whilst the app is playing background audio, these buttons do not work. I.e. I cannot stop/play/pause the music from this menu.

How does that work?

If I submit the app the way it is, I have a strange feeling that Apple may reject it. They'll say " Oi oi oi… you've got background music, but you need to stop the music from within the app - that needs to be corrected, unfortunately we have to reject your miserable app until this is resolved".

Given that I cannot bear another rejection, can someone kindly tell me how I can interface with these "iPod" controls???

I don't care if the back button or forward button work or not.. i do not have a playlist NOR do i care for a playlist even to have them work. I just care that the stop button works. 

Application type: mobile

Titanium SDK: 1.8.2

Platform & version: iOS 4.3, (i'm focusing only on iPhone)

Device: iOS simulator, Android emulator or physical device model

Host Operating System: OSX Lion

Titanium Studio: version, copied and pasted from Help / About
