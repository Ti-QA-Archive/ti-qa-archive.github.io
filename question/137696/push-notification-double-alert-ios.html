Push Notification Double Alert (iOS)

I have push notifications working correctly for iOS but seem to have come across some strange behavior. In the callback function of the registerForPushNotifications function, I show an alert dialog of the message from the push notification.

This works great if you already have the app open. However, if you get the notification in your Notification Center, and then click on that notification that opens your app, the callback function is called and the alert is displayed again. Is there a way to detect this behavior so I can prevent the dialog from being shown?
