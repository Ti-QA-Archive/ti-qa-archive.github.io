Application Loader: What Do I Upload? - OR - How to Make a Production Release with Titanium Studio

Ok, so I have everything ready to submit with my first app to the iTunes store. I have my Distribution Profile in place, I have the app created in iTunes Connect and it's listed as "Waiting for Upload". I am in the Application Loader, and I am looking for something to actually upload.

What, exactly, am I looking for?

Or, another way (maybe?) of asking the same question might be: Is it possible to create something like a Production or Distribution build within Titanium Studio? Or is this something I need to do in xCode? Either way, how do I create and then find the file (or files?) that I upload with Application Loader?

Thanks!

  • In TitaniumStudio, near where you click run/debug, there is a little brown box/trunk/chest icon. It opens and there is an option Distribute - Apple Itunes Store. It runs you through a wizard of about 3 pages, asks for permission to access the keychain, then after a while launches xcode and you should be at the Organizer. Select your app on the left if it isn't already, select the archive you want to submit from (newest at the top). You can validate the package to ensure no glaring errors (missing icons: did you put your 72x72 one in for ipad? Yes, even if its not an ipad app) If all is good, Click distribute and Submit to iOS App Store.

    This assumes latest released version of xCode 4.3.x or higher, some of the buttons moved around from 4.2
