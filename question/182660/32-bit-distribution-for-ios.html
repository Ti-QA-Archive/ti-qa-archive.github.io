32-bit Distribution for iOS

Is there a way for force Titanium to publish an app for Ad Hoc/Enterprise distribution using Titanium SDK 3.5.0 for iOS 8.3 32-bit only? I can create a 64-bit ipa, but I need a 32 bit ipa (armv7 or armv7s architectures NOT arm64).

  • You'll have to do that in Xcode. First use Titanium to build your project. Then, go into the build/iphone directory, and open the Xcode project. Proceed as if it's a native app from there (i.e. Google how to do 32-bit-only).
