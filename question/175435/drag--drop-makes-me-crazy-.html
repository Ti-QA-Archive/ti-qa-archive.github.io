    • ok that's simple. here you can download the compiled modules. Unpack this zip and copy the contained two zip archives to your project folder (the folder above the resources directory) then add this to tiapp.xml (also in the project folder)

      <modules>
    <module>ti.draggable</module>
</modules>

      now rebuild your project, the modules will be installed then. You'll find an example how to use the module here
