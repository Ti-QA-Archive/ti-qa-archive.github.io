ColdFusion Reference App

Hi guys,

Just letting everyone know that there is a ColdFusion Reference App on the AppStore that might be very helpful for ColdFusion Developers who wants to integrate their ColdFusion skills with Titanium Mobile or Desktop. It has all the tags, functions, ajax calls and CFC's that you can find in Adobe documentations.

Thought I'd should share this with you, it might be a good App to have.

The App is called ColdFusion Reference and it's located at: http://itunes.apple.com/au/app/coldfusion-reference/id366614299?mt=8

Good luck,
