    You have to define Intent Filters for your Activity. This can be done in a custom Manifest or in the tiapp.xml.

    Have a look at Receiving Content from Other Apps to learn about how your Intent Filters have to look like.

    To read the received data, you have to parse data property of the Titanium.Android.Intent. You should also add a newIntent event-listener to the Titanium.Android.Activity wich has the Intent Filter.

    Here is the Android's guide to Intents and Intent Filters.
