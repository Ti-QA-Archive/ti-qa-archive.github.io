  • 1.6.2 was released April 18th, 2011.

    In a environment that is only 5 years old, you have an app developed with a 2+ year old SDK, using 2+ year old Apple tools.

    The solution? Rebuild with modern tooling. The exception to that would be if you are trying to support old hardware, then you are pretty much just out of luck. Even Apple has abandoned 4.x and less
