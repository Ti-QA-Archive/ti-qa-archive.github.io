Can't get a NavBar to appear on my window.

I just started to use Titanium and it seems very cool, but I can't seem to get a NavBar on my window. This is my code. I am just trying to get a blue window with the title NavBar on top. 

var win = Titanium.UI.createWindow({
        backgroundColor:'#336699',
        title:'Database App'
});


win.open();

I am not sure why it only shows a blue screen.

    Try:

    var win = Titanium.UI.createWindow({
        backgroundColor:'#336699',
        title:'Database App',
        navBarHidden:false
});

win.open({modal:true});

    Also, if you use the tab group the windows have the navbar by default.
