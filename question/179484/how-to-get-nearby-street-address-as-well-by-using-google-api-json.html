    Hi E PL!

    Try using Reverse Geocoding by passing your current location to:

    http://maps.googleapis.com/maps/api/geocode/json?latlng=40.714224,-73.961452&sensor=true_or_false

  • Since most cell providers will NAT your device's internet connection, you may reach IP address geocoding limits enforced by Google.

    You can also use a 3rd party reverse geocoding solution like http://www.geobulk.com – they offer paid and free service levels.
