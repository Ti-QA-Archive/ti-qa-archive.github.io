BarImage and leftNavButton of childs

Hi,
in my main windows I use a barImage and not the title property.
When I open a child window, since the title property of the parent window is not set, the leftNavButton of the child display "Back". This is very problematic for me since my app is not in English.

Is there a way to change the text of this button with creating for each child a new button (that look unfortunatelly different to the original leftNavButton (arrow shaped) ?

In Obj-C it was possible to use at the same time the barImage and the title (hidden by the barImage), here no :-(
