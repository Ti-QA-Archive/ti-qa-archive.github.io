landscape only app

How can I fix my titanium app so that when people put their device horizontally, it stays vertical? Like if they put their device in horizontal, the app stays in its vertical format. How can I do that? thanks!

    Hello Weina,

    if you want app only in landscape then just set in your tiapp.xml…

    <orientations device="iphone">
            <orientation>Ti.UI.LANDSCAPE_LEFT</orientation>
            <orientation>Ti.UI.LANDSCAPE_RIGHT</orientation>
</orientations>

    Clean build from simulator & resource and run it again…
