ListView complex layout

I have a tableview row that is rather complex, and in my striving toward a smoother experience I was wondering if this could be converted to a ListView. I keep taking a look but get completely confused when trying to convert.

This is what I currently have in my table:

<Alloy>
    <TableViewRow id="row" class="row" className="property">
        <View id="rowInner" class="rowInner">
            <ImageView id="photo" class="photo" />
            <View id="buttons" class="buttons">
                <Label id="title" class="title" text="{title}"/>
                <Widget id="toolbar" src="lpa.toolbar" />
            </View>
            <Label id="excerpt" class="excerpt" />
            <Label id="distance" class="distance" />
            <Widget id="location" class="location" icon="location" src="be.k0suke.ssgizmo"/>
            <View height="1" bottom="10" backgroundColor="#AAAAAA" />
            <View height="10" bottom="0" backgroundColor="#F1F1F1" />
        </View>
    </TableViewRow>
</Alloy>

One point to make, whilst this works perfectly and is pretty stable if not a little jerky on previous versions of Ti SDK and Alloy, the latest versions of both crash iPad2 device running iOS7. Bugs are consistent on both latest versions - the table view cannot load more than 10 items. SDK 3.2.0 and Alloy 1.2.2 work ok, but inconsistently renders or doesn't render the rows some of the time (the first two will be visible, then you can scroll down what looks like the rendered height but the rows do not show).

I am hoping ListView will perform much better on both latest versions of Ti and Alloy.

Any pointers or help is much appreciated. I thank you for your time.

  • Haven't played around with ListView much myself, but looking at the docs, I am assuming it should look something like this:

    <Alloy>
    <Window backgroundColor="#FFFFFF">
        <ListView id="listView" defaultItemTemplate="template">
            <Templates>
                <ItemTemplate name="template">
                    <View>
                        <ImageView bindId="photo" id="photo" class="photo" />
                        <View>
                            <Label bindId="title" id="title" class="title" />
                            <Widget id="toolbar" src="lpa.toolbar"/>
                        </View>
                        <Label bindId="excerpt" id="excerpt" class="excerpt" />
                        <Label bindId="distance" id="distance" class="distance" />
                        <Widget id="location" class="location" icon="location" src="be.k0suke.ssgizmo"/>
                        <View height="1" bottom="10" backgroundColor="#AAAAAA" />
                        <View height="10" bottom="0" backgroundColor="#F1F1F1" />
                    </View>
                </ItemTemplate>
            </Templates>

            <ListItem title:text="Title" excerpt:text="Excerpt" distance:text="Distance" photo:image="/apple.png" />
            <ListItem title:text="Title2" excerpt:text="Excerpt2" distance:text="Distance2" photo:image="/apple2.png" />
            <ListItem title:text="Title3" excerpt:text="Excerpt3" distance:text="Distance3" photo:image="/apple3.png" />
        </ListView>
    </Window>
</Alloy>

    Taken from the docs:
    "ListView is analogous to TableView, ListSection is analogous to TableViewSection, and ListItem is analogous to TableViewRow."

    Hope this helps or at least gets you moving in the right direction.
