What would be the best way to store Facebook/Twitter Tokens for OFFLINE_ACCESS?

What would be the better idea to store the tokens or important information such as ID and PASSWORD other than using noSql? I don't think SQLite is suitable.     App.Properties is persistent. As long as you don't delete the app or overwrite/delete the property yourself, App.Properties remains stored on device.
