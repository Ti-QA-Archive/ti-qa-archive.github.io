  • Probably a bit late in the day for this answer, but I used this to solve my orientation issue with Android, as setting orientationModes and making changes to the tiapp.xml weren't working

    if (OS_ANDROID) {
Ti.Android.currentActivity.setRequestedOrientation(Ti.Android.SCREEN_ORIENTATION_LANDSCAPE);
}

    Then use this for your click event

    Ti.Android.currentActivity.setRequestedOrientation(Ti.Android.SCREEN_ORIENTATION_PORTRAIT);
