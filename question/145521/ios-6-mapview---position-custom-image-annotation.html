iOS 6 MapView - Position custom image annotation

In my app i have a mapview full screen with annotations. I want to customize annotation's image, whith an other pin image. When i set the image property of annotation object with my custom image, the image's center is positionned at the annotation coordinates(lat et long).
But i want the bottom of my custom image at the annotation coordinates like the default pin (red).

    Hi

    Short answer; Unfortunately there is not.

    Longer answer;

    There is currently no offset parameter that you can specify to allow the iOS to position your custom image relative to the impact point of your visual. iOS centres the custom image to the point, so whatever you add will simply be centred in both vertical and horizontal directions.

    Android (I believe) actually centres to the bottom middle - just for information - I know it does not help.

    There have been requests for the offset position in the past, I hope it is on a list as I would really like this myself.

    There is also no property for this in the upcoming Ti SDK 3.0 either - I just checked.

    You could always try and create a larger image canvas and place the "hit point" of your pin graphic to the centre - which will leave a large blank section - but it might work - I have not had time to try this myself though.

    Not the answer you are after - sorry.
