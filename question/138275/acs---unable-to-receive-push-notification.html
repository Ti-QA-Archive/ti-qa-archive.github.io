    • Hi Vijay,

      You can subscribe for push notification only once. If you subscribe next time you will get error. Because your device token already register for push notification.

      I will suggest you following things

      1. Whether your in Development mode or Production Mode(use receptive push certificates )
      2. Use valid push cerificates(.p12)
      3. Check in your device Settings/Notifications, whether your app configured for push notification
