Splitview hide masterwindow

Hi, in my iPad app i have a splitview, when my app is in landscape i am trying to hide the master window and show only my detail window what can i do?

    Hi Raffaele,

    It is not possible to hide master in landscape, because this is the functionality of Splitview. If you want to hide master view in landscape then you have to customize two view that look like split view and have to manage it in orientation change.

    it is better to use splitview as it is.

    if you are well familiar to Native iOS and XCode then you can able to change the behavior of split view. but for that you have to know proxy method in Titanium. following links are useful for you.

    http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/64371/splitwindow-widths

    I done this before in 1.7.2 SDK but you have to do some stuff to build it in SDK 1.8.X or later.
