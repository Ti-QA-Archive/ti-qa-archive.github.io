    Hi Stefan

    I have tested this on Android "APIs 2.2" and Titanium 1.6.0 (2 Feb 2011), and it works fine. However, note that your app.js files were not encoded with utf-8, and caused me issues where the projects would not boot when they contained non-ascii characters.

    I have changed the projects slightly, so that non-ascii characters are in both app.js and the localization file:

    app.js:

    var label1 = Titanium.UI.createLabel({
    color:'#999',
    text: "OlÃ¡ "+L('welcome_message') + " (app without space)",
    font:{fontSize:20,fontFamily:'Helvetica Neue'},
    textAlign:'center',
    width:'auto'
});

    strings.xml:

    <?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<resources>
    <string name="welcome_message">mañana</string>
</resources>

    The two projects, one without a space in the app name and one with, gave the following results respectively:
