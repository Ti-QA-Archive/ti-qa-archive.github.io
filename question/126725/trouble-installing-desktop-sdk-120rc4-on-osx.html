  • After setting up a new macbook with Lion with Migration Assistant from one with Snow Leopard, and after reinstalling Titanium Studio, I had two folders: /Library/Application Support/Titanium & /User/Library/Application Support/Titanium. In the Studio preferences it pointed to the right /User/Library/Application Support/Titanium folder, but it seems the buildscript is still checking if the /Library/Application Support/Titanium folder exists.

    Removing the old /Library/Application Support/Titanium folder fixed this for me.
