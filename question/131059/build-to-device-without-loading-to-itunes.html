    Open your project (build/iphone) in xCode. On the top right, change it to your ios device (should detect it if its plugged in), and hit Run. It'll compile and install to your device without iTunes. You can also look in the info window at the bottom of xcode, itwill show anything you have written to Ti.API.info

  • As this seems to be a high hitter on Google I thought I would update the answer as Ti now allows build to device without iTunes sync. See the appcelertor docs

    In essence:

    ti build -p ios -T device  -C <DEVICE_UDID> [-V "<DEVELOPER_CERTIFICATE_NAME>"  -P <PROVISIONING_PROFILE_UUID>]

    does the trick.

    and to get some of the info for above. Like the connected device's UUID use ti info -t ios
