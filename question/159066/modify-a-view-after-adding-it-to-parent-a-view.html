modify a view after adding it to parent a view

Hello,

I am unable to edit views after adding them to a parent view, basically:

parentView = Ti.UI.createScrollView({}) ….;
subView = Ti.UI.createView({});

parentView.add(subView);

Now I am unable to change subView, for instance changing it's background color or content, what do you suggest I do?

  • Hi

    This should be very easy as long as you are still in the same scope of where you created the original "sub view";

    subView.setBackgroundColor('red');
// or
subView.setBackgroundColor('#ff0000');
// or
subView.backgroundColor = '#ff0000';

    All will turn the subView background colour "red";
