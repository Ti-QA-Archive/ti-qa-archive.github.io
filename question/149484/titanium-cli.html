    You should specify --target dist-playstore. You'll also have to specify the --output-dir, --alias, --keystore, and --password.

    It's worth noting that the –build-only flag doesn't work as expected in 3.0.0.GA or 3.0.2.GA. We have a ticket here to fix it: https://jira.appcelerator.org/browse/TIMOB-12034.
