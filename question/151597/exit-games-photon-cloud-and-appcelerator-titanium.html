Exit Games Photon Cloud and Appcelerator Titanium

Is it possible to use Exit Games Photon Cloud API with Titanium? Im in planning stages of a menu based card game of sorts and wondering if I can take advantage of Exit Games Photon Cloud service to handle the multiplayer aspect. If not, is Titanium compatible with some sort of similar service? I'd rather not host the backend but I will still need a similar set-up to what Exit offers with waiting rooms and the connections they describe. I dont need "real time" communication necessarily, just enough to allow players to enter a "waiting room" and select other players manually to play with, establish the connection, and play. Its a card based game and played in turns so like I said no need for "real time" communication just quick and sent to the correct person. I will support some back end mainly for player info database and the like and also push some other data out on player request, I just dont want to develop and support the connection protocols.

Photon might even be a bit much for my project but their rooms, matchmaking and event pushes are what interests me… and the fact that they host the back end for that stuff. Of course I would have to pay for the service so I may look at other stuff but just wondering.
