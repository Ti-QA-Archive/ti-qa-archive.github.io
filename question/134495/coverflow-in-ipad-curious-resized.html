  • I got bitten by that one too. My experiements show that you need to ensure that images are at most 348 in height (half the 768 available). If you make the images taller, the stretching looks like your example.

    There are also issue with having really wide images on iPad that are work fine on iPhone.
    My setup uses max 340 (1024/3) for max width.
