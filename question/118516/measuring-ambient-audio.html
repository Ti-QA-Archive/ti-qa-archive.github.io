  • There is a way to get the peak and average microphone levels using Titanium, but note that's only possible when an audio recorder is started.

    You could set off a recording from a shake gesture, then capture a bit of audio without saving it, just so you get the ambient levels over a short amount of time.

    Then use that value to decide what output volume level to set.
