    • Looks like a permission problem.

      • Open finder and navigate to /Users/pladis/Library/Application Support

      • Right click on folder Titanium and select Information

      • inside the new window click the lock icon to unlock and enter your password

      • select your user plaids and give read & write privilege.

      • click the gear icon and propagate permissions to subfolders

      Hope that helps.

      matthias
