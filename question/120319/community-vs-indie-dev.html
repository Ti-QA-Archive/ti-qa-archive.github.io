  • As Ray mentioned, you can use the (free) community edition to create your apps. The main things you will gain by using the Indie subscription:

    • Ability to debugger in Studio IDE
    • Access to another 6 hrs of on-demand video training
    • Access to Ti+Plus modules like in-app purchasing
    • Read-only access to helpdesk to search for known issues

    Access to the video training is only available with an annual subscription. The others should apply to monthly subscriptions as well. Be aware the offer to get one year or Indie for $399 expires May 31, so you may want to make your decision soon.

      — commented August 26th 2011 by Josh Lewis

    • The Ti+ modules already deployed in existing projects continue to work. What you can't do is recompile the project to release an update.

      In other words, the license is enforced at compile/build time. One you have the compiled app there is no time limit on it. However, you would not be able to release an update or fix (unless you remove the modules).
