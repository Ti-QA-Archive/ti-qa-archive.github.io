  • You question title says:

    Can't find "/Library/Application Support/Titanium/

    Indicating thats the location being entered in the terminal,
    yet your command line shows

    ~/Library/Application\ Support/Titanium/

    The later is now the correct location, and if that is not correct, then your Titanium setup most likely would not be working.
