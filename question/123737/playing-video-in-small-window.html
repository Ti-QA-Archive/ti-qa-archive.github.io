Playing VIdeo in small window

Hello

I'm developing an app for both Android and iOS platforms.
What I'm trying to do is to open a small PopUP video player in my current window.

I use Titanium.Media.createVideoPlayer Object,and it's works OK on iOS,but when I'm trying to open this player on android it always opens above black background so I can't interact with the content beneath it.
Well I really lost on this one and I need some help.

                top:0,

                scalingMode:Titanium.Media.VIDEO_SCALING_ASPECT_FIT,
