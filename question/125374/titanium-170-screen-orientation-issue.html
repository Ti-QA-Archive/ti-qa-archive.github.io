currentWindow.orientationModes = [Titanium.UI.PORTRAIT];

on all my windows. When I navigate to any page on my app and if the device orientation is in landscape, my page loads as landscape and then moves back to portrait mode. Don't understand why this flip happens everytime.

I have also modified my androidmanifest to

android:screenOrientation="portrait"

but still doesn't work.
