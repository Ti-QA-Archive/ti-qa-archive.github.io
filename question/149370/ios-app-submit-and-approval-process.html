    • There are no specific features of the Titanium API that will either ensure or preclude your app from App Store approval. As long as the mini-browser is a compelling feature by itself, or just a component of a larger set of features, there is no reason that it alone would keep Apple from approving your app.

      The collective content and features of your app are more important than any single UI element.
