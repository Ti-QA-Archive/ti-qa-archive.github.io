  • The answer is that Android is rather finicky about playing some types of videos. Videos recorded on iPad are apparantly a no-go. My solution was to use ffmpeg serverside to convert videos with baseline profile (max backwards compatibility), so recorded videos from either platform can be played on both platforms.
