setActiveTab and Android, Tab is selected but not Shown

Hi,
in some windows (of a specific tab) I try to switch to another tab, the problem is that the tab is active but on the screen I still get the window where I was when calling setActiveTab, to see the window of the active tab, I have to close all windows or child of the previous active tab.
Of course this works fine in IOS.
Any idea ?
Thanks

  • I encountered this myself, and my solution was to create a "TabGroupWindowManager", or TGWM as part of the TitanUp library.

    It includes a function called closeOnTabInactive. When you open a window from a tabbed window, you call TGWM.closeOnTabInactive(), and that will register an event listener on the tab so that when it becomes inactive, your window will automatically close.

    If you've built a stack of windows, you may have bigger problems, and this mechanism may not be enough.

    Another option for you would be to not use windows, but instead use views that you overlay on top of the tabbed window. Then when you change tabs, the views will be hidden along with the previous tab's window.
