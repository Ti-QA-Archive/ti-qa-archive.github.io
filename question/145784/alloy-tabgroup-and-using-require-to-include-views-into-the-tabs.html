Alloy tabGroup and using require to include views into the tabs

I am testing out titanium and alloy and I can't get the windows and labels to show up in the iphone simulator no matter what. I see the tabs and can switch between them but nothing is showing up inside , just black. There is also no errors in the console.
Can you tell me if I am doing something wrong ?

index.xml

<Alloy>
    <TabGroup activeTab="Sfsd">
        <Tab id="makerequest_tab" title="Make A request" icon="KS_nav_ui.png">
            <Require src="makerequestTab" type="view" /> 
        </Tab>
        <Tab title="Settings Tab" id="settings_tab" icon="KS_nav_views.png">
            <Require src="settingsTab" type="view" /> 
        </Tab>
    </TabGroup>
</Alloy>

makerequestTab.xml

<Alloy>
    <Window title="Tab 1">
        <Label>I am Window 1</Label>
    </Window>
</Alloy>

settingsTab.xml

<Alloy>
    <Window title="Tab 2">
        <Label>I am Window 2</Label>
    </Window>
</Alloy>
