borderColor behaving like backgroundColor in Android

Hi, I am developing an app for android. For certain views (e.g Ti.UI.view) I was trying to give a border color by mentioning properties like borderColor and borderWidth. But I have observed that the whole background of the view is filled with the same color as the background. Is this a bug in Titanium? or is there anything I am missing? Following is the code snippet that I am using.

var view = Ti.UI.createView({
  width: 200,
  height: 200,
  borderColor: '#c00',
  borderWidth: 1
});

win.add(view);

This gives me a red box of 200x200px. Kindly let me know if there is a way to rectify this.
