Android ActionBar on titanium with appcompat-v21

Hi,
I'm struggling to remove the shadow from an ActionBar.
On API 21, we should use getSupportActionBar().setElevation(0) on an activity.

How can I do that on Titanium?

I tried through

Ti.Android.currentActivity.actionbar.setElevation(0), 
Ti.Android.currentActivity.getSupportActionBar().setElevation(0)
Ti.Android.ActionBar.setElevation(0)

and several other things without any success.

— asked June 23rd 2015 by Jose Freitas

  • The according window must be opened before accessing its activity / actionbar.

    var win = Ti.UI.createWindow();

win.addEventListener('open', function(){
  win.getActivity().getSupportActionBar().setElevation(0);
});

win.open();
