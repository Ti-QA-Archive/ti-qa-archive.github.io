[2.1.2.GA / iOS6] Cant deploy app after fixing an iOS6 issue

Hi everybody,

today i fixed an iOS 6 issue in my app. it crashes everytime i launched it.

the reason was just stupid!
Apps, filling a Label with empty text like the following code will crash the app.

But know i'm trying to deploy my app to the appstore. but unsuccessful.
