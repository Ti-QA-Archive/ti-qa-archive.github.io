  • Hi Luis,

    we may no add dot sign to Number pad instead of it you may use this and do validation for only enter number 

    txt.keyboardType = Titanium.UI.KEYBOARD_NUMBERS_PUNCTUATION;

txt.addEventListener('change', function(e) {
    txt.value = txt.value.replace(/[^0-9]+/, "");
});
