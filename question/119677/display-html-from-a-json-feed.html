  • Using a webview should work. Try emptying the CONTENTS of the APP/build/iphone/* folder and rebuilding. It's possible you haven't used a webView yet in your app and it needs to rebuild.

    You have to use a webview if you want the text (with potential links) to be interactive and styled. You can't do that with simple labels.
