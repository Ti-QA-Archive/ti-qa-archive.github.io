Email Dialog works in Emulator but not in device

Funny, this never happens before but I am stuck with it.
I have a button and when click, runs the email script:

button_email.addEventListener('click', function()
{
var emailDialog = Ti.UI.createEmailDialog();
emailDialog.barColor = "#770000";

emailDialog.subject = EmailSubject;
emailDialog.messageBody = EmailBody;

emailDialog.open();

});

Any pointers what I did wrong?

  • Your syntax looks incorrect when creating the dialog. Replace the equal signs with colons. Also, try creating the emaildialog first:

    var emailDialog = Ti.UI.createEmailDialog({
barColor:"#770000";
subject:'Email Subject';
messageBody:'Body of message.';
})

    Then open it with the eventlistener:

    button_email.addEventListener('click', function(){
emailDialog.open();
}

    HTH
