  • This happened to me when testing on a Samsung Galaxy Player 5.0, the device was missing the Google Play Services, do not confuse them with the Play Store app, this app is called Google Settings, if the device doesn't have this app then services like Google Maps API won't work.

    It is odd why Samsung devices present this problem (although Samsung thinks it's a good idea to have this app missing but heaven forbid them to not put unnecessary apps in their devices).

    Try searching for the Google Settings app in the Play Store and install it, if this doesn't solve it or the device does have the app then we can at least discard this as a possibility.
