How can I hide the Action Bar in Android?

Hi everyone. I've recently updated my Titanium SDK to 3.2.0. I have a project that was created with 2.1.4 that ideally I would've kept at 2.1.4, but since upgrading, Titanium won't let me build anything that's not using 3.2.0 (That's a question I have going in another thread).

Anyway, since I'm using 3.2.0 now, there's this damn action bar that is screwing up the layout of all my images, etc., because it's taking up the top part of the screen. Does anyone know how to hide this? I searched around and a few people said to add this to tiapp.xml

<application android:theme="@android:style/Theme.Holo.NoActionBar"/>

But it's not working. Does anyone have a way of hiding it?

And I'm using Titanium, not Alloy.
