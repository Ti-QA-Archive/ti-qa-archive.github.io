A zoomable and pannable view, like in Wordfeud

Hi guys.

I'm making a puzzle game, where it's possible to see the whole playfield. I'd like to be able to zoom in a specific amount, on the playfield, and from here be able to pan around the zoomed playfield. This is to combat too small icons on devices with small screens.

I've searched exhaustively and tried many, many different approaches to get this working, but I'm running out of ideas. So if anyone has any input on how to achieve this in Titanium, I would appreciate it a lot!

The way it works in Wordfeud, is pretty smooth and flawless. You have a board with lots of square tiles, usually a bit too small to hit reliably on small screens. You can double-tap this board, and the view will be zoomed in to 2x scale, so the tiles are bigger, and you can pan around the board.

A few of the things I've tried:

1) Tried to get panning working with a scrollView, however on Android, only one scroll direction can be used. Using a horizontal scrollview within another vertical scrollview, did not have the desired results.

2) For panning, also tried Webview. It works, you can pan/scroll in both directions on Android. But it is of course slow, so I'd like to avoid this solution.

3) Tried using 2dMatrix to scale and translate the view, to achieve both zoom of the "playfield" and panning - but the various objects (imageViews) I have in the playfield, lose the position of their click eventlisteners..

4) Tried modifying zoomScale parameter on scrollView/scrollableView, to achieve zooming, but this seems to do nothing - even if the required values are set on the scrollViews creation, as stated in other posts.
