  • Your issue may be related to memory warnings. The sandbox appears to disallow access to cached information on the system in the middle of a memory panic.

    Try and decrease the amount of memory your application uses, and see if the problem persists. Unfortunately, there doesn't appear to be a better solution right now - we have no control over the sandbox.
