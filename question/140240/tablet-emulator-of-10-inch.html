Tablet emulator of 10 inch

I need to make a emulator for tablet which is 10 inch.How can i do this for android ?

  • Hi,

    You can create a new Android VM with this specs for a 10 inch:

    1. Click on "Run" buttons on top of list of project and select "Run configurations"
    2. Right Click on "Titanium Android Emulator" and select "New"
    3. In top of right window, write name of your Emulator (ex: My Project - Tablet 10 inch)
    4. Click on "Browse" button to choice your project
    5. Choice Android 4.0 minimum SDK or you don't have the good resolution
    6. Choice "WXGA800" (1280x800)
    7. Choice your log level (I work with Debug)
    8. Save your new Emulator!
    9. Launch your app with your Tablet Emulator (choice the good Emulator in "Run" menu on top of list of project)

    Tips : Ctrl + F11 if you want to rotate your Emulator.
