  • In my TitanUp library, I provide a view called TU.UI.Views.SelectBar which allows for deselection. Even better, it renders sensibly on android as an array of Switch controls. Have a look at the source to see how I do it (there's a not-so-elegant hack in there that allows us to implement deselection).
