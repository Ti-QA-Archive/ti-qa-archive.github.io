  • Accepted Answer

    
    var annotation_array = [];
    annotations_array.push(annotation);

    mapview.annotations = annotations_array;

    // or

    mapview.setAnnotations(annotations_array);

    This is to make you understand that you need an array. Normally I'd do

     mapview.annotations = [annotation];

    more here:

    http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/2.1/index.html#!/api/Titanium.Map.View-property-annotations
