  • There is no way of knowing exactly when the thumbnail was taken from in a movie file. (unless of course, you generated it yourself) If that is the case, my best advice would be to save the time index at the moment of the thumbnail creation.

    Once this done, you can set the initialPlaybackTime property from the Titanium.Media.VideoPlayer object.
