  • If you know your target devices, you can set your min-sdk and max-sdk requirements differently in your androidmanifest.xml than you would for an app designed for a broader release.

    As an example, lets say you were targeting only ICS devices, you could set the target-sdk to 14 without fear of a problem from lower end devices, and your activity menus would appear properly at the top of the devices under ICS where if you set it to a lower target, then you get a phantom icon in ICS.
