    • I dont know how to do that, however if you want to set the entire app orientation to portrait you can try adding that to your tiapp.xml

      <android >
    <manifest>
        <application>
            <activity android:name="org.appcelerator.titanium.TiActivity" android:screenOrientation="portrait"
                android:configChanges="keyboardHidden|orientation"
                >
            </activity>
        </application>
    </manifest>
</android>
