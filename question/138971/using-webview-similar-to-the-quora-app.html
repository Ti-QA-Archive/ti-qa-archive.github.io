    1. Do your own networking

      • Yes, with httpclient.

    2. Call out to Cocoa to invoke Native UI

      • The link triggers an event that opens a window inside a navigation group.

    http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/2.0/index.html#!/api/Titanium.UI.iPhone.NavigationGroup

    1. Native JS/Cocoa bridge
      • I think so.

    Now, more "2c"… :)

    While the guy has his point ( just like the guys on phonegap ) I really really don't suggest you to go that path. For small apps this might be optimal, but for more complex apps the app will go painful to use.
    The webview is way to slow, and loads a lot of stuff you don't really need it. Have a look at the FB app - don't tell me the guys at FB don't know to optimize the html/css/js. Is just slow, and this because it uses one or more webviews.
