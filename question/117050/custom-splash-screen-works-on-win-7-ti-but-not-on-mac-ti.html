My custom splash screen will not display on Android emulator or device, when using Titanium on my Mac. A red splash displays there.

But, my custom splash does display (Android device and emulator) when using Titanium under Win 7.

  • I had the exact same issue and tried all the tricks including changing the name from default.png to Default.png and moving the file into different directories. In the end, the answer for me was to delete the images as was not going to use anyhow under resources>andorid>images (all the sample splash images) and then recompiled the app. This will also minimize the size of the app which is an added benefit.
