  • Tnx, only his implementation is a bit buggy found it a couple of weeks ago, my solution is as following,

    on the iphone i use a navigationgroup that i animate to the right to show the menu "behind" it (facebook style)

    on android the problem is that you can't "group" the windows and sliding the windows to the right at once, you have to do this per window and with 10 windows this becomes buggy.

    my solution is a differant approach, just slide the menu from the left over the windows to the right. Just a little differant experience but much more efficient. Plus Android users dont want a iOS experience they want a Crappy android one. So the menu listens to the menu button, the back buttons are gone and you use the hardware back button on the device and so on.

    Bought a android yesterday for testing and allot of the code works so thats nice! Still the compiling time of about 1,2 minuts is a bit slow before it runs on the device.
