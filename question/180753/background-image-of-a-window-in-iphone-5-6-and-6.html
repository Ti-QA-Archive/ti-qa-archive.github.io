Background image of a Window in iPhone 5, 6 and 6+

Hello, I'm writing an app for iPhone 5, 6, 6+.

The three devices have different resolutions and screen sizes.

In a section of my app, I have an image as background of a window, so the image has a width=deviceWidth and height=deviceHeight (same problem with some images in the "header" of another window).

Now, iPhone 5/6/6+ have different dimensions:

  • 640 × 1136
  • 750 × 1334
  • 1242 × 2208

Supposedly I can't have three assets with the same ratio renamed @2x, etc.

Should I provide the app with 3 different images (without using @2x and @3x naming)?
How do you generally deal with this problem?
