iOS Transitions -- Do they hide or close views?

Been looking through the documentation and I can't find anything solid on what happens when you use iOS-only animation transitions.
I looked at the Kitchen Sink and three views are added to an object, and then each individual is transition-animated into view. Does transitioning in one view make all the other views invisible, or does it close them?

    So, did some testing. Looks like when you say

    something.animate({ view: some_view, transition: Ti.UI.iPhone.whatever })

    it hides all of the other elements and only shows the view specified. because i re-showed one of the buttons that it hid, and the "click" listener was still hooked up, leading me to believe that it was not destroyed, merely hidden.
