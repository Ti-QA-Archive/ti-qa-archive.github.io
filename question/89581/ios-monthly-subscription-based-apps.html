iOS monthly subscription based apps?

Does Titanium offer support for subscription based applications? can I create an app that will be sold in the AppStore through a subscription model? I urgently need to confirm this because a customer is considering buying the Titanium professional subscription but he doesn't know if it will be a good fit for his project.

    To the best of my knowledge even fully native apps do not yet support a subscription model. There is rumors of it coming soon…..but nothing official from Apple yet. Considering it's not even natively in iOS, it might be a little while before it comes to Titanium (e.g. we are just now getting local notifications in iOS and we still can't add events to the calendar which were added with iOS 4 this summer).
