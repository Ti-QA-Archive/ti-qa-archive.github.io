Titanium.UI.iOS.NavigationWindow, detect end of animation

Is there any way (eg. any event to listen to) to know when a window opened or closed by Titanium.UI.iOS.NavigationWindow ends its opening/closing animation?

  • Hi Gab,

    you have to listen for the "open" not the "postlayout" event of the window you are opening.

    var win = Ti.UI.createWindow({backgroundColor:"green"});
win.addEventListener('open', function(){
               Ti.API.info("opened");
        });
navGroup.openWindow(win);
