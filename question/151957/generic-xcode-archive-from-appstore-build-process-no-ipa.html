  • I'm not sure I have understood your issue.

    If you're trying to share the archive file, it won't work since it can't be distributed. You have to sign it with a provisioning profile generated here in order to transform it into an ipa file.

    To achieve this, just open your archive file (double-click) with Xcode. It will open the Organizer in the Archives tab. There, click on the Distribute button and choose the correct provisioning profile. It will generate an ipa that will be be able to share…

    • You don't have anything to do.
      Only put this line in your tiapp.xml :

      <icon>app_icon.png</icon>

      and place your 2 images app_icon.png and app_icon@2x.png in your Resources/iphone folder.
