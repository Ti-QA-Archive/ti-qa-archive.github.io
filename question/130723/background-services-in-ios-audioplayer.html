background services in iOS (AudioPlayer)

Hi, there,

I am working on SDK 1.8 and iOS 5.
I coded an app that playing music with Titanium.Media.createAudioPlayer();

Well, it works fine, except it stops playing when I minimized the app.

I wonder if I can put it as background service when I minimize app.

thank you

Simon

    Need to set a background audio mode
    In TIAPP.xml:

    <iphone>
        <backgroundModes>
            <mode>audio</mode>
        </backgroundModes>
</iphone>

    In you app.js (although this may now be deprecated):

    Ti.Media.audioSessionMode = Ti.Media.AUDIO_SESSION_MODE_PLAYBACK;
