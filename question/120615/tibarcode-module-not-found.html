    Your module's zip file should be put either here:
    /Library/Application Support/Titanium/

    or here:
    ~/Library/Application Support/Titanium/

    depending on where you have installed your sdks. Note that it doesn't go in the modules folder. Once you build your app and have the module referenced correctly, then then Titanium will know to extract the module and create/put in the correct subdirectories.

    Check out the packaging and test your module in an app sections here
