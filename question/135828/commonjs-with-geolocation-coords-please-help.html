        if (Ti.Geolocation.getLocationServicesEnabled) {

  • Your prototype overwrites the vars containing data.
    You may want to rename the vars to this._latitude= .. and return by this.getLatitude = function() { rwturn this._latitude; }
    Also 'this' in your getCurrentPosition is Not the same in the getters. The context differs due to anonnymous function.
    You may want to refer through var me = this; at the top of the getGPS function.
