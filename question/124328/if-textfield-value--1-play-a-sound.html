If textfield value == 1, play a sound

I'm trying to figure out how to do the above. I'm punching in numbers through the keypad into a textfield. I want to then read the textfield, and play a sound accordingly.

Here's my textfield:

var tf1 = Titanium.UI.createTextField({
    title:'test',
    color:'#336699',
    height:35,
    top:10,
    left:10,
    width:300,
    keyboardType:Titanium.UI.KEYBOARD_NUMBER_PAD,
    returnKeyType:Titanium.UI.RETURNKEY_DEFAULT,
    borderStyle:Titanium.UI.INPUT_BORDERSTYLE_ROUNDED

});

And here's what I understand to be what plays a sound…

var sound = Titanium.Media.createSound({url:'instrumental.mp3'});
    sound.volume = 1;
  sound.play();

  • Here you go! :)

    var sound = Titanium.Media.createSound({url:'instrumental.mp3'});
    sound.volume = 1;

tf1.addEventListener('change', function(e) {
    if(e.value == 1) {
        sound.play();
    }
});
