After distributing Android MapView doesn't show

Hi I found one weird thing. It is about android mapview.
When I test on android device it works perfect. But When download my app after distribution, map view doesn't show up.
I don't know why
Is it because of android version? or because of google map app key?
Anybody know ?

  • You need a production key from http://code.google.com/android/maps-api-signup.html. Put it into your tiapp.xml:

    <property name="ti.android.google.map.api.key.production">YOUR_KEY_HERE</property>
