    Hi

    Free means free in this case.

    Keep in mind many of the features of this module (which I use) are currently available in the core SDK.

    The following methods are available in the current SDK for iOS users.

    • imageAsCropped
    • imageAsResized
    • imageAsThumbnail
    • imageWithAlpha
    • imageWithTransparentBorder

    In the almost ready 3.0 SDK the methods are available for both iOS and Android.

    • imageAsCropped
    • imageAsResized
    • imageAsThumbnail
    • imageWithAlpha
    • imageWithTransparentBorder

    So if you are only using these you may not require a third-party module.
