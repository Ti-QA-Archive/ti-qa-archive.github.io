Two apps in one

Is it possible to have two apps connected to the same ACS backend? Do I just create a separate app in titanium and change the API key, oAuth Key, and oAuth Secret to the original app and they will both work?

    The quick answer is yes. If you do create a new app that is 'cloud enabled' then the app will be created on the acs backend… So remember to delete it after you change the keys to keep things tidy.
