Upgrading existing app on iOS results in crashing app

We've had an app out in the app store for ages.. I put out a patch release a few days ago that fixed a few bugs from a release 2-3 weeks ago, however one user has reported that after installing the update it would show the splash screen & die with a black screen, before returning to the 'desktop'.

It's difficult to know whether this is a widespread problem. There are no crash reports in the iTunes Connect portal thingy (unless they take some time to come through).

He's using an iPhone on 4.3.3, and I'm using Titanium Mobile 1.7.1. The version of Titanium mobile didn't change between my 2 releases, nor did his iOS version, as far as he's said.

He ended up deleting the app & reinstalling.. after that it worked fine. We therefore don't know whether a reboot of the phone would have sorted it. Either way it's a poor solution.. the user most like thinks we've screwed up the app & moves on, never to use it again :(

Has anyone experienced this kind of thing before? Is it something that sometimes happens with non-Titanium apps?
