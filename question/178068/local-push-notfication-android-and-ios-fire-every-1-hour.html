  • This service technique doesn't really work once the device goes to sleep. To schedule a notification that fires after the device is asleep, you'll need to use android's AlarmManager.

    My GrillTime tutorial will give you a lot of guidance for doing cross-platform local notifications after the device is asleep. Part 3 is the most relevant to what you're trying to accomplish.
