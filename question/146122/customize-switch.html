  • Hi

    Your target OS; you have not said what it is and the image does not show any signs - but I am guessing iOS from previous questions.

    Only if you want to create it yourself, the basic switch does not support any customisation for iOS (Android does however).

    You will need to either use the standard one, purchase one from the market place (cross platform) or make your own.

    To make your own you will need to handle the swipe, movement, toggle action and events. Before you ask I have not got any code for that :)
