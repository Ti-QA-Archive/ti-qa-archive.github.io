  • I just fixed my problem, big mistake from my side.

    I also had a functions.js included in every window wich also captured a click on the whole window to check the idle time of the user. But having this click event on the whole window takes away the click event on all the objects inside this window.

    Instead of a click on the window I used a Blur event now.
