  • To achieve this, you will need to develop a web service on your web server using a language of your choice such as PHP, Java, Ruby, etc. Since your mobile application cannot connect directly to your MySQL server, you will need a service to act as a bridge between your two databases.

    There is no process out-of-the-box to synchronize the local database with a server.

    Hope this clears it out for you.
