Can I play the video in background in iphone?

I use a videoPlayer object for play the m3u8 radio stream in iphone, but how can I keep the playing after I click the "Home" button in iphone? (that's playing in background).

for my case, because the audioPlayer cannot play the m3u8 stream format, so I have to use the videoPlayer.

please help, thanks!!!

    In you tiapp.xml add:

    <iphone>
   <backgroundModes>
      <mode>audio</mode>
   </backgroundModes>
</iphone>

    Early in your app.'s add:

    Ti.Media.defaultAudioSessionMode = Ti.Media.AUDIO_SESSION_MODE_PLAYBACK;
