  • Raul,

    Aside from 1.1 fixing score submissions, you most likely have another issue in your code. When you call showLeaderBoard, you must pass in a string as the first argument:

    `gk.showLeaderBoard('com.custeng.leaders');`

    The example/app.js included with the module was incorrectly using this method (it didn't pass in the identifier). There will be a new beta, 1.2, coming out that will be more aggressive in validating data and warning you when methods are being misused.

    Dawson
