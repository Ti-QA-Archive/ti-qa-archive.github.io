Share: Titanium Mobile SDK 0.8.2

I've seen several folks trying to get their hands on mobile SDK 0.8.2 to work on older Titanium projects and having mixed results tracking it down.

Here you go, mobile SDK 0.8.2:

http://share1t.com/dto0nv

Just unzip it into Macintosh HD > Library > Application Support > Titanium > mobilesdk and restart Titanium Developer. When it launches again it should see the SDK.

Hope that helps someone!
