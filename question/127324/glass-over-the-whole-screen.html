Glass over the whole screen

How can I glass over the whole screen while the activity indicator is showing? In other words, no touch is allowed. I was trying to use the view. But, it does not cover the whole screen (not covering the navigation bar). Any suggestions? Thx in advance.

— asked October 24th 2011 by Jiale He

  • Accepted Answer

    You can open a modal window and set the opacity to around 0.8 and then add an activity indicator to the window. That will cover the entire window and show the indicator at the same time.
