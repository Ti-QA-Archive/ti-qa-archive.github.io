Alloy ListView DataBinding setMarker & animation false on iOS

I'm using ListView 2.0 on the 3.2 RC SDK with Alloy databinding and the new setMarker functionality to load more items into the ListView.

http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/latest/#!/guide/ListViews-section-37521650_ListViews-ListViewmarkerEvent

On Android this works perfectly. However on iOS there is a flicker on the ListView as it rebinds and animates all the items onto the list again.

The following JIRA on Alloy https://jira.appcelerator.org/browse/ALOY-648 suggests its linked to a SDK bug with animated: false being ignored in ListSection.setItems https://jira.appcelerator.org/browse/TIMOB-13737 that is unresolved.

Can anyone confirm whether the ListSection.setItems animation parameter actually works on iOS?
http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/latest/#!/api/Titanium.UI.ListSection-method-setItems
