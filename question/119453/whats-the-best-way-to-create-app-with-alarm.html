    Hi there, simply request, complicate to answer.
    As i know the framework, you can schedule a trigger by certain time while the application is in background,
    but 1) you have to start the application manually each time.if you reboot the device your application is out.

    Anyway due to the iphone architecture you can play just the file audio ( caf ) that you embedded into your resources. If you wanna play mp3 you have to include during the compiling procedure the key UIBackgroundModes with audio value.

    The other option is the push, but you have to be online.
    To enable push is quite easy if you use services such as urbanship.

    hope it helps.
