    The picker/spinner for Android is as I understand it a 3rd party 'widget' and can't be altered from it's default appearance. Which is a pity as it's a pale imitation of the iOS native one. Although that being said, with iOS you still have to add labels with colored text to get what you want. But with the Android picker you can't even add labels.
    The reference to color in the Android Kitchen Sink code is misleading.
