Video is not playing (defaulting to black screen)

I am trying to do a simple click on a button and a video plays. For some reason, this is giving me fits. For iOS and Android, I have gotten this to work very easily, but with Mobile Web I am having nothing but problems. What am I doing wrong? Below is my code. I did get a video play at one point, but that video was 12 kb's..usually my videos are 30-40 mb's. Is this a memory issue possibly?

var videoButtonOne = Ti.UI.createImageView({
    image: 'assets/darkBlueButton.png',
    width: '13%',
    bottom: '83',
    left: '380'
});

videoButtonOne.addEventListener('click', function() {
    var vidWin = Titanium.UI.createWindow({
        title : 'Video View Demo',
        backgroundColor : '#fff'
    });

    var videoPlayer = Titanium.Media.createVideoPlayer({
        top : 2,
        autoplay : true,
        backgroundColor : 'white',
        height : Ti.UI.FILL,
        width : Ti.UI.FILL,
        mediaControlStyle : Titanium.Media.VIDEO_CONTROL_DEFAULT,
        scalingMode : Titanium.Media.VIDEO_SCALING_ASPECT_FIT
    });

    videoPlayer.url = 'assets/dentist.mp4';
    vidWin.add(videoPlayer);
    vidWin.open();
});

  • I managed to figure this out. Google Chromes local storage capacity is only 5 mb's. So, I had to convert it down to below 5 mb's and boom it works.
