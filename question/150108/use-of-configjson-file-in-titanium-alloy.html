    • It's used for setting values on Alloy.CFG that you would like to use throughout your app, potentially based on platform or environment. The 2 additional functionalities in it are "theme" and "dependencies". The former allows you to specify a theme to be used in your app, the latter lists your widgets dependencies. These are discussed in detail in the following 2 links, respectively:

      • http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/latest/#!/guide/Alloy_Styles_and_Themes-section-35621526_AlloyStylesandThemes-Themes
      • http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/latest/#!/guide/Alloy_XML_Markup-section-35621528_AlloyXMLMarkup-ImportingWidgets
