TI 3.0: Custom info.plist broken after compiling

I'm using a custom info.plist to include a font in my iPad app. When I compile the project the info.plist is unreadable afterwards (starting with "bplist00? …") and the fonts aren't included in my app.
Was working fine with TI 2.x. Is there a other way to include a custom font/info.plist now?

TI 3.0,
SDK 3.0,
iOS 6,
iPad

  • There are a few Info.plist bugs in v3.0:

    TIMOB-12105 The first is with CFBundleIcons where the value is a <dict> (array types are fine). CFBundleIcons is only used when you are adding newstand specific icons. Best thing to do here is temporarily remove the CFBundleIcon definition. This will be fixed in 3.0.1.

    TIMOB-12107 If the tiapp.xml is missing things like <persistent-wifi>, then builds will fail. Best thing to do is copy all settings from a fresh tiapp.xml that are missing in your app's tiapp.xml. This will be fixed in 3.0.2, possibly 3.0.1.
