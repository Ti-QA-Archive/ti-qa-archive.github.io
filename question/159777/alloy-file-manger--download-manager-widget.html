  • Apple's Human Interface Guidelines state that they don't want apps that force users to deal with files. The iOS platform is not supposed to be file-centric, like most desktop operating systems. There's a chance if you did write such an app that Apple would reject it from the app store. That said, Dropbox, Google Drive, and a few others do that sort of thing. So, they do let some such apps through.

    I don't know of any widgets or modules that would do what you want.
