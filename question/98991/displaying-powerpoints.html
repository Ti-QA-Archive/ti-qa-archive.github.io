  • Not much to it, here is the code:

    Using pureMVC so I initially create:

    var webView = Titanium.UI.createWebView({
            width:1024,
            height:768
        });

    Then bind in another method - works for video and PDF.

    
var f = Ti.Filesystem.getFile(asset.assetUrl);
        this.webView.url = f.nativePath;
        this.webView.repaint();
