Titanium4j now free to use and under Apache

Hello community,
sometimes ago we announced release 2.0 of Titanium4j. Our GWT binding for Titanium that aims to bring Java devs the the Titanium Plaftform by giving them the ability to use Java instead of JavaScript. To see what s new in 2.0 please visit : http://emitrom.com/releases/titanium4j/2.0.

We are pround to announce that Titanium4j will now be free of charge with an Apache 2 license. Meaning you are free to use and modify it however you want.

We hope you guyz will like this.

Cheers,

The Emitrom team
