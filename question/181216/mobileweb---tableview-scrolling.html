Mobileweb - tableview scrolling

I've encountered an issue where tableview's do not seem to scroll for mobileweb builds. I am testing in the latest version of safari and chrome on the desktop. I see the scroll indicator, but it does not respond and thus does not scroll on long tableviews extending beyond the screen. Is this a known issue or is there some workaround to get this to work.

Ti 3.5.0

    Yes ,this is a known issue that scroll indicator does not respond and disappear after some time . But tableview scroll properly on mobile web .

    • The non-scrolling only occurs on tableview's with a height of FILL and not wrapped.

      My workaround was to not define the tableview height and wrap in the tableview with a scrollview. That allowed scrolling to work, though i still get the scroll bar disappearing, but at least it works for now.
