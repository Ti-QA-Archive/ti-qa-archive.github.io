Android 3-dot menu button showing in bottom of app

I´m creating an app with a simple form and running it on an ICS device (HTC One X, running Android 4.0.4) and I´m getting a 3-dotted menu button on the bottom of the app covering about 10% of the screen. I´m running the same app on a device with android 2.3.3 and I´m not getting the same result.

How can this button be removed?

I´m running Titanium 2.1.2 SDK

  • You need to target a higher SDK in your androidmanifest.xml.

    Your APK claims to only support a min-sdk most likely, so the ICS (or JB) devices try to 'fake it'

    Take a look at <uses-sdk>. Options can be added to your tiapp.xml in the manifest section as needed.

    • <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
   <tool-api-level>8</tool-api-level>
   <manifest>
      <uses-sdk android:minSdkVersion="7" android:targetSdkVersion="14"/>
   </manifest>
</android>
