  • Hi

    You said you have updated Java, please check the version as Appcelerator only supports 1.6 (sometimes referred to as 6) as the most recent. Whilst 1.7 (7) is available this should NOT be used. Also you can ONLY use the 32bit version.

    So if you have upgraded this may be the issue, worth checking.
