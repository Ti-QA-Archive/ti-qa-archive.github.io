  • The convertPointToView converts the curent point to another (parent) view.
    Consider you have a window my_window, and inside the window a view in the next configuration:

    
view  = Ti.UI.createView({
    width:100,
    height:100,
    left:200,
    top:200
});

    Let's assume you click exactly in the center of the view, the x,y coordinates of the click event will be 

    {x:50,y:50}

    If you try convertPointToView this coordinates against the window you will do something like

    var pos_against_win = view.convertPointToView({x:50,y:50}, my_window);

    and pos_against_win will have the value of 

    {x:250, y:250}

    Makes sense?

    Keep in mind that the views will need to be attached, else the reply will be null.
