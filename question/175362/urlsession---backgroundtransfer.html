URLSession - backgroundtransfer

will backgroundtransfer be fired during inactive state? e.g. incoming call

I think not. Since the app is still running, there is no need to fire this event. Can anyone confirm?

  • Hi

    There are three states;

    • Foreground
    • Background
    • Not running

    Currently ONLY the foreground app is "running" so in your case if a phone call is received your app is now "background" and your app is no longer "running" so no foreground operations will occur, but background ones will if you had already defined and started them before the app moved states from foreground to background.

    The documentation explains;

    • This notification (backgroundtransfer) can be received while the application is in the background.

    This would be your app during a phone call. It is also received when the app is in foreground. In this case a background transfer is offloaded to iOS to manage and it simply updates you on the success of its progress.

    So - yes, the event should fire when in the background - including your example of an incoming call.
