  • If you are on windows, Ctrl+F11 or Ctrl+F12 will change the emulator orientation for you.

    You could also use Numpad 7 or Numpad 9 (make sure that NumLock is off)

    The same applies to Mac and Linux too.

    Hope it helps :)
