  • Yeah, you can technically build your own keyboard, but whenever you click on a textfield or textarea, the underlying platform (ios or android) is going to try and open the native keyboard. There's 2 ways you can go about dealing with this, neither of which is a trivial task:

    • Modify the components in native modules to dismiss the keyboard before it appears
    • Create your own custom TextArea and/or TextFields that will only respond to your custom keyboard.

    If you do some research on the underlying platforms, there's lots of chatter about creating custom keyboards, but not many solutions. You might want to check out Emjoi for iOS. Android, on the other hand, seems to be more difficult as I'm having a hard time finding anything but system wide skins for existing keyboards.
