    Hi Jonathan Kendall,

    Thanks for your concern.

    For update your Titanium SDK please follow the steps

    Step 1. Download 3.5.0.GA from here.

    Step 2. Install Location = /Users/xx/Library/Application Support/Titanium/mobilesdk/osx/3.5.0.GA.

    Step 3. Restart your Titanium studio.
