  • I successfully created a custom module for OpenEars text to speech and voice to text. It was a little tricky but worked out nicely. The developer of OpenEars (Halle of Politepix) needs to give you permission though if you include it in your app. Building a Titanium module is outside of the OpenEars license. This is why I did not share the module.

    If you get Halle's permission, feel free to contact me and I will help. Politepix may also be developing a Titanium module also.
