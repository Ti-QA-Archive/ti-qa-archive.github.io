    What you actually want is the custom url scheme for the compass app. A customer url scheme for your app is what others (or web pages) would use to launch your app.

    In the case of Compass, I don't know what that is. Here is a page listing the URLs for some iOS apps, but Compass is not on the list. I don't have an iphone to experiment for you. But I suggest you just open Safari and start experimenting, with the first attempt simply compass://
