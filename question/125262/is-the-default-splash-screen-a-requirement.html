Is the default splash screen a requirement?

Hi! I've just been wondering, the default.png screen, is this a requirement to be approved? or is it just something that Ti has added? I'm wondering because I have made the defaul.png image just completely black, and added my own splash screen that animates (fades in and out).

So in effect, it still has a splash screen, it's just not the default.png file.

Is this ok to do? or will apple reject an app that does this?

    The default.png file is something apple shows to the user while the app loads. It is a requirement to have it. What you are doing though it will have no effect on the approval process. Many apps are doing this.

    What you might want to take in considerations is the user experience. I suggest to show your splash as soon as possible and load the rest of the app only after this loaded.
    This way the app will create the illusion of a fast start.
