  • UPDATE: Thanks to Pegli over on IRC, managed to implement scrollsToTop property on tableview. This solved the issue. I disabled scrollsToTop on the tableview nav menu (doing a facebook style nav menu hidden under main window)

    For the curious, added this to TiUITableView.m

    -(void)setScrollsToTop_:(id)value
{
    [[self tableView] setScrollsToTop:[TiUtils boolValue:value]];
}
