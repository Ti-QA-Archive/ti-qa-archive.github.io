  • Accepted Answer

    Add the _itemnumber as a property of your rows, then. Right now, you're putting that into the title.

    orderData.push({
   title:orderRows.fieldByName('_itemnumber') + '      '  + orderRows.fieldByName('_desc') + '     ' + orderRows.fieldByName('_qty'), 
   hasChild:true,
   _itemnumber: orderRows.fieldByName('_itemnumber')
});

    Then,

    currentOrder.addEventListener('click', function(e){
  Ti.API.info(e.row._itemnumber);
});
