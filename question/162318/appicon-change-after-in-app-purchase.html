AppIcon change after in-app purchase?

I have two apps in the store, one free and one not. The free version has the same features as the paid version, it has only a limited number of operations. If an user wants to buy the paid version, the user must download and then make a backup of the database from the free version to the paid version. But this solution is a bit awkward. Now I have many users for both the free version to the paid version, so I would like those who have the free version can do an app-purchase to remove the limit. The only problem is that I wish I could change, after the im-app purchase, the name and/or appicon image.
I can change the appicon or the name of the app after an in-app purchase?
If not, what other solution could be adopted?

    Hi

    Short answer - no.

    Longer answer;

    Both the app icon and the app title are both stored in the sand-boxed app package you create. They are tied to your marketing materials and the keywords and searching title that the stores use.
