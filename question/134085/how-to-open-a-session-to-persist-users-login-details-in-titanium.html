how to open a session to persist user's login details in titanium?

I am a newbie to titanium, currently doing an ebay-like iphone app with my friends. I am now struggling on how to open a session when user clicks login & persist user's personal details, such as update shopping cart while browsing items… & I also want to know how to close the session after user clicks logout button. I have been google a lot trying to figure out some answers, but don't get any luck at all. can anyone give me some tips?

  • hi ruoli,

    you can use Ti.App.Properties.setBool('login',true); on successful login and you can set Ti.App.Properties.setBool('login',false); on logout…..

    or

    you can use this also…

    Ti.App.login = true;

    Ti.App.login = false;
