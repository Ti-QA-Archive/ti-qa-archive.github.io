  • Hi

    This sounds like a memory leak. You say the app starts off fine but over time appears to be slower - this often means that you are creating new objects but older ones are not being disposed of correctly.

    Watch this video explaining why they happens, how to look for them and what you can do to fix them.

    • Rick Blalock: Your Apps are Leaking - Controlling Memory Leaks

    I strongly recommend reviewing this video. It might be the best 38 minutes you will spend improving the performance of your apps.
