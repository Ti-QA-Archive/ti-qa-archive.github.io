  • If you go to the folder:

    \Users\{yourusername}\Library\Application Support\Titanium\modules

    You can remove appropriate modules from either the iphone & android folders.

    Hope this is what you are looking for.

  • If the reason you need to remove modules is to bring in an updated version of the module just add the updated version like so: modulename/version_2 i.e modulename/version_1 being the older module.
    Then with your tiapp.xml specify which version of the module to use.
