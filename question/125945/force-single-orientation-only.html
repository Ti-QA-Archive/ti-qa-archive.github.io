Force single orientation only

I have an iPad app. I want it to launch in Landscape and only be available in Landscape. I know I would set this in TiApp.xml, but what's the code?

Titanium Studio, build: 1.0.5.201109221620, OS 10.7.1, SDK 1.7.2

    in tiapp.xml:

    <iphone>
        <orientations device="iphone">
            <orientation>Ti.UI.PORTRAIT</orientation>
        </orientations>
        <orientations device="ipad">
            <orientation>Ti.UI.LANDSCAPE_LEFT</orientation>
            <orientation>Ti.UI.LANDSCAPE_RIGHT</orientation>
        </orientations>
</iphone>

    in window creation

        orientationModes: [Titanium.UI.LANDSCAPE_LEFT,    Titanium.UI.LANDSCAPE_RIGHT]

    Please note that 1.7.2 version has a bug that all apps initiates in portrait mode. If you follow described steps, your app will automatically change to landscape, but still you will see a rotation transition that is a bit annoying. I suggest you to use 1.7.1 sdk.

    • Not exactly, I think.

      orientationModes only affect to its window, while the tiapp.xml affects the entire app. By default, tiapp.xml supports 4 modes (2 portrait and 2 landscape), so in this case orientatioModes is more restrictive, allowing you to have, for example, an app with both orientations and one specific window only in one of them.
