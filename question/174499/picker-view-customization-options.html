Picker view customization options

I am currently developing an Android app which utilizes pickers in multiple places. But going through the Titanium documentation I found that picker's customization options are very lacking. Like we can't select backgroundColor, color, borderColor etc. Because of white parts in background image of my view, the picker's white text blends in and it gets hard to read. And also borderColor is transparent too so it really gets hard for people to notice that there is a picker present

I wanted to know when will Titanium be updated with these changes? And is there anyway to around to this problem?

Also I wanted to know what are Appecelarator's plans on including UI elements for radio buttons and check boxes

— asked May 24th 2014 by Sagar Sangani

  • Pickers are transient elements just like alerts. There really are no default styling properties for anything in this category. Your choices are 1) build your own 2) search for a custom picker on the marketplace or open-source like github, 3) use the standard one.
