Webview Alternative

What is a recommended native component alternative to a webview in a full screen mode if I'm just trying to provide a formatted document for reading? (i.e. Headings, numbering, formatted text, etc.) I understand that webviews are quite expensive regarding system resources and will need to have many pages. Ultimately, I have a tableview of items and when a row is selected it would provide the document full screen with a toolbar for navigation at the bottom.

Thanks in advance.

  • Yes loading a webView is expensive, but this only the initial load.

    You can structure your application so it only uses one webView and you just swap out the content for each new page. I believe that approach should work for you based on the information you provided in the question.
