ACS Pricing Information

Has anyone been able to get concrete pricing information on ACS for usage above what is provided for free? I'm working on a couple new apps and I am trying to decide between ACS, Parse.com, Urban Airship and PubNub.com for various pieces of the puzzle and before I spend the time and energy integrating API's, I want to know what the pricing will be. I have information from all of the other vendors but can't get Appcelerator to provide their information. I filled out online forms and sent email but never heard back so I called sales. I spoke with someone in sales and the most concrete answer I could get was that he has seen the pricing graph relating to price as you increase API utilization and it's inline with Parse.com and other competitors. I really like Appcelerator and ACS seems like a good product but they are sending mixed signals about pricing which makes me nervous. Does anyone exceed the free tier limit? Maybe that's the issue…
