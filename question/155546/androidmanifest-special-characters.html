    Hi

    I place my custom app name in the following folder;

    {appname}/platform/android/res/values-en/strings.xml

    And the contents of the file is simply;

    <?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<resources>
    <string name="app_name">custom_name</string>
</resources>

    You seem to have tried many combinations - not sure you have used this one.
