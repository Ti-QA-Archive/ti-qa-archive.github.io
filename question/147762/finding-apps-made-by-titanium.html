Finding apps made by Titanium

Hi, I'm newbie to this nice community. I have a simple question.

Is there any easy way to see if an app is made by using Titanium mobile? (maybe by checking inside ipa/apk files?)

— asked February 2nd 2013 by Sherlock Shellinford

  • Accepted Answer

    Titanium apps are compiled into native bytecode. So, unless you are the developer of the app, it's not likely you'll have access to anything that will identify what developer tool was used to create it. From the outside, it'll look like a native iOS or Android application.
