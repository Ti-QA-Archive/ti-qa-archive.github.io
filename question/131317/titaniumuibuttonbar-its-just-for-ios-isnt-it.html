Titanium.UI.ButtonBar it's just for iOS... isn't it?

Hello… I need to understand this:
I've been pulling my hair for 2 hours becouse my project was crushing in Android but not iOS. The actual reason is Titanium.UI.ButtonBar is not supported in Android (in KitchenSink controls.js it's added only if platformname='iOS').
Why it's not in the documentation?
Why it's in the common UI package and not in a platform specific such as UI.iPhone or UI.iOS ?
Is there any list of unofficially unsupported UI components?

It really would help if each page in documentation could have links to all related official open bugs.
