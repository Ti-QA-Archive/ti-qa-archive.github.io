  • Have you transferred computers recently? It doesn't seem like your keychain is setup properly. Go into iPhone Provision portal, re-download all certificates, import into keychain again and try.

    Also, in Keychain, under 'login' and category 'Certificates' you should have.
    iPhone Distribution: 'Your Name'
    iPhone Developer: 'Your Name' (some letters)
    -Private Key>'Your Name'

    I think you might be missing the private key, which if you transferred computers and did not copy, you'll have to regenerate your certificates in the provisioning portal. Do you have the private key listed there?
