iPhone blog app download Speed

Am developing RSS feeds app, the time taken to download the pages is 45 secs in the iOS device. Is there any way that I can increase download speed there by decrease time taken ?

  • Most of the solutions will be related to server-side, but without knowing more, I can't do much beyond offer some suggestions:

    • Are you able to turn on GZIP compression on the server side? That can reduce the bandwidth of transit.
    • Are you loading from an improperly-indexed databased? Properly indexing tables can reduce query time by several orders of magnitude.
    • Are you able to boost simultaneous server connections allowed? Connections could be queuing up, causing delays.
    • Since this is an RSS feed, can you truncate content for each article or limit retrieval to less articles, as to reduce data being transfered?
