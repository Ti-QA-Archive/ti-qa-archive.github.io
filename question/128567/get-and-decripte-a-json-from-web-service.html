  • Accepted Answer

    var jsona = this.responseText;
    var response = JSON.parse(jsona);

    Use responseText instead of responseData.

    then you can do response.COLUMNS.length and loop through them or response.DATA and loop through those as well.

    or var colArr = response.COLUMNS;

    etc.
