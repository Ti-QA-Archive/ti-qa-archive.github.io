    • You need to download the developer prov profile of your app from the iOS provisioning portal and upload it to Titanium.
      You might need to upload it to XCode too, I sometimes have this issue where Titanium doesn't save it well into XCode.

      Tell me if I'm getting it wrong but once you have your certificate and your prov profile, it just be quite straight forward.
