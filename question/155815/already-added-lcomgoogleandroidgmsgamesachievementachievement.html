  • For anyone else still having this issue, a quick fix (from the Jira ticket):

    "One quick workaround is to go inside one of the modules and delete the jar to prevent duplication during compile time. I.e: remove /modules/ti.cloudpush/[version]/lib/google-play-services.jar from your app's directory"
