  • Maybe force full rebuild? To do this make a slight change in your tiapp.xml and then save it, I usually change the description. This will force titanium to rebuild your project from scratch, next time you compile. If you are still experiencing crashes, close emulator and titanium, check CPU memory, reopen and try again. Sometimes my machine will cause compile issues if my mem or CPU runs too hot.
