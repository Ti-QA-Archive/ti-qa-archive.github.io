  • I asked this question in the past, and oddly the answer was "no spaces in the name". I had previous projects which have spaces in their name, and they work. However, any new project I produce since my previous update this year cannot have a space in the name.

    • The project is generated when you run the project in simulator. It's located in YourProject/build/iphone/YourProject.xcodeproject

      When Xcode is open, you set your build target in the drop down in the toolbar to the left. The target without -ipad or -universal in the end is iPhone-only.
