    Since the Blackberry SDK is still in beta, it is only available to those with "early access" status. In other words, those with a paid subscription level. Once officially released, it will be available free to the community.

    There is a 30-day trial available for the various subscription plans, last I knew. So AFAIK you could sign up for one of those then try out the Blackberry early access beta.
