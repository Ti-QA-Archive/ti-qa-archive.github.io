    Hi Thomas,

    first try it with app uninstall and cleaning your project:

    • open iOS Simulator
    • delete/uninstall your app
    • close iOS Simulator
    • Inside Titanium Studio clean your Project: main menu => Project => Clean… (select your project and click OK)

    If the above don't work you can reset your iOS Simulator:

    • from iOS Simulator main menu select => iOS Simulator => Reset Content and Settings..

    Greetings,

    Matthias
