"Galaxy tab addon android 2.2"

Hi,
I'm testing my app across several Android resolutions.
When choosing the "Galaxy tab addon android 2.2" option from the "Run Configurations" I get the galaxy tab emulator which supposed to have a resolution of 1024x600 (according to Samsung)
But:

  1. When I browse from the emulator to: whatismyscreenresolution.com I get 683x319.
  2. Titanium.Platform.displayCaps.platformWidth/Height isn't returning any value.

So what is the actual resolution of this device?

Thanks,
Idan

    Resolution for the first Galaxy Tab (the 7" version, also known as model P1000) is 1024x600 as you say. I don't know why that web is returning an incorrect value, probably the emulator is resizing the screen by software to accommodate to your screen.

    Stranger thing is that Titanium does not return a valid value, maybe not compatible with this addon, I don't know. Anyway, that is the correct resolution.
