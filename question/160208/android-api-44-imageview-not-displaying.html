    i always lead my android images with a / in classic.

    "#kplLogo":{
    width: "90%",
    height: Ti.UI.SIZE,
    top: 25,
    image: "/logo.png",
    borderWidth: "3dp",
    borderRadius: "10dp",
    borderColor: "transparent"
},

    I've been argued with, but the stuff just works. See if it fixes your alloy as well.
