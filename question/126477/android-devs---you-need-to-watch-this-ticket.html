Android Devs - You Need to watch this ticket

as an Android dev….at some point you probably will be using Android Services - well – currently Ti doesn't support the StartForeGround method - which would provide your service with persistence. Translation > Android randomly kills services…so yours will be a VICTIM.

Please go to Jira and watch this ticket so we can get Ti to start supporting the Android Service StartForeGround method ~~~ and Keep Your Services ALIVE!!!
http://jira.appcelerator.org/browse/TIMOB-3286
