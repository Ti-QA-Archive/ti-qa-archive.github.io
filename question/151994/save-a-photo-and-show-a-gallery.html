  • Hi

    The photos should persist during new versions, the only reason that jumps to mind as to why it would not be right now is if you are un-installing the app before the new one is added to the device.

    Make sure you are not deleting the app from the device before adding the new one as if you are - then you are the one actually deleting the photos.
