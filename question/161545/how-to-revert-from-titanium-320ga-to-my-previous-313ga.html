How to revert from Titanium 3.2.0.GA to my previous 3.1.3.GA?

I made the mistake to update my Titanium 3.1.3.GA to 3.2.0.GA and now I'm facing the same problems many developers.

In my case is the inability of Titanium Studio to detect my Android SDKs anymore and the inability to build Alloy projects on 3.1.3.GA since it wants to use Alloy 1.3.0 when on my tiapp.xml I'm specifying 1.2.2 and previous versions of the plugin.

I've tried to update my Java SDK, change my Android SDKs location, download the obsolte Android API 5, but I gave up, now I want to undo this update on my Titanium Studio and revert to 3.1.3.GA while the Titanium team release a fix for this.

I thought that by going to the About Titanium Studio and then to the Installation Details I could revert, but the last date I get is from last year when I installed Titanium on this computer.

How can I revert back to 3.1.3.GA? Can it be done? Will it be a safe revert or will I encounter other problems down the lane?

  • It seems that while the updates that appear on the bottom right of Studio deliver you the Titanium SDK update, the alloy update, and the rest, it doesn't include the update for Studio itself, that one has to be made from Help > Check for Updates but you must have this link on your update sites.

    After that just run Check for Updates and accept every agreement and your Studio should update.

    I hope this not-updating-studio with the rest of the components has been fixed because most people will be thinking that Studio gets updated together with the rest.
