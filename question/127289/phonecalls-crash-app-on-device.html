Phonecalls crash app on device

Has anyone had experience with an app crashing when a phonecall is received and what could cause it? As you cant simulate a phone call on the emulator (I think?) I can't use the emulator to see if any errors are received in the Trace/Info.

What happens is that the app is running, a phonecall is received, after the call it returns to the app as normal, but about a second later the app closes.

This doesn't happen when putting the app in the background and restoring it, it is only for phone calls. It also doesn't happen when receiving messages.

I'm only getting this behavior when using a complex namespaced architecture, it doesn't seem to happen for much simpler apps:

Basic namespace layout:

(function() {
    myApp.main = {};

    myApp.main.open = function() {
        // open window function
    });
}) ();

Has anyone experienced this and what could be causing it?

Thanks!

    To trace the info/debug on the device, while the deice is plugged in, you can open XCode, then open Window > Organizer, select your device and see the 'console' output. You will then see the Ti info/debug stuff and errors etc.
