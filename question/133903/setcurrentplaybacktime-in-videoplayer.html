  • setCurrentPlaybackTime only works on iOS and appeared in the sdk around 1.7.x. In 1.8.x that time was changed from seconds to milliseconds

    Seek Backwards 15 seconds:

    MediaPlayer.setCurrentPlaybackTime(parseInt(MediaPlayer.currentPlaybackTime/1000,10)-15);

    Seek Forward 15 seconds:

    MediaPlayer.setCurrentPlaybackTime(parseInt(MediaPlayer.currentPlaybackTime/1000,10)+15);
