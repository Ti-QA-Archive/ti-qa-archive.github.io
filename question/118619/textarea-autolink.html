textArea autolink

Hello everyone,
for those like me who struggled with textarea autolink property . here's the solution:

var textarea = Ti.UI.createTextArea({
    top:10,
    left:10,
    right:10,
    bottom:10,
    editable:false,
    value:'some data whith link http://www.google.com',
    font:{fontSize:15},
    autoLink:Ti.UI.AUTODETECT_ALL
});

//Ti.UI.AUTODETECT_ALL
//Ti.UI.AUTODETECT_LINK
//Ti.UI.AUTODETECT_PHONE
//Ti.UI.AUTODETECT_PHONE
//Ti.UI.AUTODETECT_ADDRESS

I hope this can help you
