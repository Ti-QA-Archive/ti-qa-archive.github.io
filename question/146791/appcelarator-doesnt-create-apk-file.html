    Hi

    Have you tried looking in the following folder?

    /appname/tiapp.xml
/appname/build/
/appname/build/android/
/appname/build/android/bin/
/appname/build/android/bin/app.apk // here
/appname/resources/
/appname/resources/app.js

    Where appname is the name you gave your app.
