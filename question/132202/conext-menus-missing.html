Conext menus missing?

I am using Titanium Studio (build: 1.0.8.201201262211) on OSX Lion on a MacBook Pro. Everything seems to work: I can run the demos on both Android and iOS emulators successfully.

However, I cannot for the life of me figure out how to access context menus. We have a Mac Mini that I can TeamViewer onto, and I just have to right-click there to access these menus. The Mac Mini is running OSX Snow Leopard, if that makes a difference.

Is there a configuration setting that I've blown away that disabled them?
