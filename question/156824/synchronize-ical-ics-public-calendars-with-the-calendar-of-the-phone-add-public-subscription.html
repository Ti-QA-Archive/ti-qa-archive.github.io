Synchronize iCal (.ics) public calendars with the calendar of the phone (add public subscription)

Hello,

My goal is to create an schedule app. The schedule is loaded from an feed, and will be shown in the app. But wouldnt it be great if the app could also add this .ics iCal feed to the calendar of the device, so that the calendar of the device always has the most up to date schedule in it through the synchronisation functionality of the calendar itself.

So currently my only option would be to add a subscription to an .ics calendar feed through code, but i dont know how to do that. Does anyone have a clue of how to do this for iOS and Android?
