Android build size more than IOS build size

Why is the build size in Android more than the Iphone build size.After some thought I came to the following reasons:
1.Because images are generated for every screen(High,medium,small etc) resolution.
2.Because class files are generated for Android and binary executables for iphone(I may be wrong).
Please help with this.We want to make some important architectural decisions.Any help will be higly appreciated.

    Depending on the SDK used, you may have more than one runtime packaged with your Android build. With 1.8.x both V8 and Rhino support were being packaged. This may have changed with 1.8.1.

  • it depends on the contents in /Resources/android and /iphone. Each of them are packaged to their platform, as you already mentioned the number of images on android is typically higher than iOS. Another reason could be the sdks/frameworks, which are certainly different on iOS than android and the language obj-c vs Java.
