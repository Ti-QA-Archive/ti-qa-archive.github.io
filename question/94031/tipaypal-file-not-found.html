  • I realised that I had my folder in:

    RichardJBurton/Library/Application Support/Titanium/modules/iphone/ti.paypal/1.0

    Instead of:

    Macintosh HD/Library/Application Support/Titanium/modules/iphone/ti.paypal/1.0

    That cracked it for me!
