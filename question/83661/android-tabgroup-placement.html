Android TabGroup placement

Hi Guys!

Quick question: is it possible to change the placement of the TabGroup? Per default, the TabGroup is at the top of the window on Android, and at the bottom of the window on iPhone. I want the TabGroup to be at the bottom for Android as well.

Thanks in advance.

    No, unfortunately, you can't change it for android, because it's a native control and that's just the way Google designed it.

    However, you could create views to look and behave like tabgroups. Christian has kindly given some code to the community that implements custom tabs like this. It's available here.
