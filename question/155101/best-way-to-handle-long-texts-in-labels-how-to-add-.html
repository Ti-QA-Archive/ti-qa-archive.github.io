    • Excellent - almost there

      This may well be something related to a lack of a width parameter being set. Obviously here you have no idea how wide it needs to be - but it may be possible to set a width and still using your left and right properties.

      var lbl2 = Ti.UI.createLabel({
    width: Ti.UI.FILL,
...

      The use of Ti.UI.FILL width of left and right should mean take up the width but subtract the left and right from the full width.
