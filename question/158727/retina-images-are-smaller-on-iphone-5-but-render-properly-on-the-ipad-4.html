  • Assuming your source images are:

    • Non-retina: 217px tall x 160 px wide
    • Retina: 434 px tall x 320 px wide

    Your image would be specified like this:

    var img = Ti.UI.createImageView({
  image: 'images/settings.png',
  height: 217,
  width: 160
});

    Are these the sized that your network.getHeight/Width functions are generating for you?
