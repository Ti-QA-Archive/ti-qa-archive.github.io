    • Ha… Solved it on my own.

      You have to decide, to put all your translations in i18n or in platform/android/res an so on. I think translations in i18n overrides platform/android/res/…

      Maybe, someone could edit/update the guide?.?

      So, you can't put your "in-app-translations" in i18n and your app-name-translation in platform/android/res/… I needed 2 hours to get solve this issue
