iOS 7: Drag window to go back -- without navbars?

(Titanium 3.1.3, iOS7)

iOS 7 has a new feature where you can swipe from the far left of the screen, which drags the current window to the right and goes back to the previous window. I'd like to do this in my app.

UI.iOS.NavigationWindow handles this nicely, except… it only works when a navbar is visible. And my windows don't have navbars.

I've tried covering the child window with a new full-screen window, but that suppresses the gesture. And I don't know of any way to cover the navbar with a View.

(Apple's own Safari app seems to accomplish this gesture without traditional navbars – it goes back to the previous Web page.)

Any thoughts? Or do I just need to custom-code this?

Thanks in advance!
