iOS App Store Distribution

I've followed all of the steps in the wiki (https://wiki.appcelerator.org/display/guides/Distributing+iOS+apps), but the page appears to be at least slightly dated. When I attempt to distribute via "Distribute - Apple iTunes Store", the first panel has a big "X" next to a message to "Sign up for the iOS developer program".

I have signed up, and everything is in place (if I did it right). What makes this message appear and how can I tell this panel that I'm signed up? As it stands, I'm pretty much at a dead stop since the system won't let me do anything.

Thanks.

  • Accepted Answer

    The latest Ti Studio is very good at detecting all the required items. It sounds like you havent downloaded your Distribution certificate.

    Try this;

    1. Download a copy of your distribution certificate https://developer.apple.com/ios/manage/overview/index.action and install it with a double-click.

    2. Download a copy of your Release provisioning profile https://developer.apple.com/ios/manage/provisioningprofiles/viewDistributionProfiles.action and install it with a double-click

    Re-open Ti Studio
