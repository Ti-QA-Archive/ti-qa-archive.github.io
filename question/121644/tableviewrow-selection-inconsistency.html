TableViewRow selection inconsistency

I've noticed a difference in how selection highlighting for a TableViewRow is handled on iPhone and Android.

For a row with subviews (such as labels of different fonts/sizes and formatting for positioning), tapping the row on Android only highlights the row if the tap occurs in the empty space of the row; tapping on one of the labels still sends a 'click' to the tableview with the row set properly, but the row itself doesn't change color. There is, in the second case, no visual indication that the row has been tapped.

On iPhone it behaves as expected (or at least as I would expect), highlighting the row regardless of where the tap occurs.

Here's the sample: http://pastie.org/2127305

(The oversize text clipping isn't important: I wanted to minimize the chance of accidentally tapping on the row itself instead of a child label.)

Does anyone know a way around this to make the row highlight properly on Android?
