  • Accepted Answer

    Ti.UI.currentWindow; is only available in certain circumstances.

    Namely, when the contents of the window come from the url: property of a Ti.UI.createWindow();

    just define your tableview before call tableview.setData(dataArray);

    Like this : 

    var currentWin = Ti.UI.currentWindow;

var tableview = Ti.UI.createTableView({
});

sendit.onload = function(){
    var json = JSON.parse(this.responseText);
