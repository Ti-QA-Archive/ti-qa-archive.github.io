Fhonk v1.1 - A free Titanium social networking app just released

Hi All

The free iPhone app Fhonk v1.1 was released this morning. It’s a social networking app developed with titanium. It has heaps of cool features and there are many more currently being developed which should be released in the coming weeks so don't forget to update.

Anyway, if you’re keen to see what can be achieved using titanium download a copy and check it out. We'd be happy to get some feedback on how we can improve the app.

Heres the link and remember it’s free :-)

http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/fhonk/id505968635?ls=1&mt=8
