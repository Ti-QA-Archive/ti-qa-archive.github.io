Android passwordMask and hintText

I have a regular ole text field

var password = Titanium.UI.createTextField({
    color:'#336699',
    top:180,
    left:10,
    width:300,
    height:40,
    hintText:'Password (\'example\' for tutorial)',
    value:'',
    passwordMask:true,
    backgroundImage:'../images/textfield.png',
    paddingLeft:8,
    paddingRight:8,    
    keyboardType:Titanium.UI.KEYBOARD_ASCII,
    returnKeyType:Titanium.UI.RETURNKEY_DEFAULT,
    suppressReturn:false,
    autocapitalization:Titanium.UI.TEXT_AUTOCAPITALIZATION_NONE,
    autocorrect:false
});

With passwordMask set to true and hintText with a value, the hintText kerning is wwaaaayyy off. Its widely spaced and looks retarded. Has anyone noticed this? Can anyone confirm? I am using Android 2.2 with Ti SDK 2.0.2.GA
