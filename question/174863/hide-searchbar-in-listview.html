Hide SearchBar in ListView

Is there a way I can hide a SearchBar in a ListView. I want it to show for iOS, but not for Android: I've tried doing a .hide() and setting the height to 0 but neither way worked:

<ListView id="ListView">
    <SearchBar id="Search"/>
    <Templates>
        <Require src="itemTemplate"/>
    </Templates>
    <ListSection id="ListSection"></ListSection>
</ListView>

    Do you not want the search bar to show up at all on Android? If so, you can use Alloy's platform attribute for this:

    <SearchBar id="Search" platform="ios"/>
