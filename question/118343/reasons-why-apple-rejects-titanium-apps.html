  • I haven't heard of anybody that got their app rejected because of Titanium? Awhile ago I know that Apple relaxed their ToS and laws regarding apps built by third parties and not directly through XCode and Objective-C, but even before that I've never heard of an app solely being rejected because it was built using Titanium or any of the other third party iOS development programs out there.

    The only reason I could imagine one being rejected would be because it violated one of Apple's (Quite reasonable) ToS.
