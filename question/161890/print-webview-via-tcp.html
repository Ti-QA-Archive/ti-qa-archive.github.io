  • if you call the getHtml() method you'll get exactly the content of the html value.

    You either need to transform it to an image with 

    webview.toImage();

    or need to convert it to a PDF with one of the modules from the market (you didn't specify your OS).

    Then you'll need to send the stream to the socket, etc.
