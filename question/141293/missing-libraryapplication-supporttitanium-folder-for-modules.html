    Well, couple of things…

    /Library was the default home for such things in Snow Leopard.

    Lion moved things into ~/Library, a hidden directory by default.

    Now, you MUST have one of those two locations for your Titanium SDKs to live in.

    Find which one your setup is using, and thats where you place the module.
