Updating ImageView property after it's added?

I added an ImageView to a Window with a left position. Later, after a user clicks a button, I'd like to move the ImageView to another coordinate.

Here's a example of how that looks:

var image = Titanium.UI.createImageView({
image:'image.jpg',
left:0,
height:'auto',
width:'auto',
});

window.add(image);

button.addEventListener('click',function(e){
image.left = 100
});

Seems like that would do the trick, but it's not updating. I alert the new left value after repositioning and it's correct however the image remains in it's original location.

Any ideas?
