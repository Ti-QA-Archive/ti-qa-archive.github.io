Image resize, compress and upload?

Dear Titanium Developers
I'm facing a really annoying problem: I'm searching in this Q&A section quite a while, but there don't seem to be a solution for the following, simple and really everyday problem:

To avoid big photo file sizes for a xhr upload to a php script, I would like to resize a picture from the camera/photoroll. Resize works fine with the imageView->toImage method. But after this, the filesize is still way to large because we lose the jpg compression.

Is there any way to do this? Without this, I can't use Titanium anymore, because you can't force your customers to upload such huge (in filesize) images? Or I am completely wrong?

Thanks for any hint.

  • Much easier to do this
    Titanium.Media.showCamera({ 

    success:function(event) { 
    var image = event.media;

    //CREATE IMAGEVIEW WITH event.media - USE HEIGHT AND WIDTH AS AUTO TO GET IMAGE DIMENSIONS 
var image1 = Ti.UI.createImageView({ 
    image: image, 
    height: 'auto', 
    width: 'auto' });

    //RESIZE IMAGE - HERE IS DIVIDING THE IMAGE DIMENSIONS BY 4

    var image = image.imageAsResized((image1.width/4), (image1.height/4));

    //…………

    xhr.send({media:image});
