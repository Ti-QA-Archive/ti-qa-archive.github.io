Force caps lock on iOS keyboard

I simply want a TextField's keyboard to enable caps lock (equivalent of double tapping the shift key) by default so that all input characters are uppercase.

Including the property autocapitalization="Ti.UI.TEXT_AUTOCAPITALIZATION_ALL" seems to do nothing.

    If desperate (because I was bored):

    textfield.addEventListener('change', function(e){
    textfield.value = textfield.value.toUpperCase();

});
