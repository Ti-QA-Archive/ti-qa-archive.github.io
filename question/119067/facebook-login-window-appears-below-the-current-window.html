Hi,

navBarHidden : true
windowSoftInputMode : Ti.UI.Android.SOFT_INPUT_ADJUST_RESIZE

and I fire the the facebook login/authorize window afterwards, the facebook window appears below the upmost window.

Weird. Does anyone know a solution? Of course it's working well on iPhone.
