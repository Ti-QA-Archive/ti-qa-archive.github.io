JS debugger unstable... An internal error occurred during: "child count update". 3... freezing...

I'm on Mac OS X 10.8.5, Titanium Studio, build: 3.1.3.201309132423, Build: jenkins-titanium-rcp-master-21 (origin/master) Date: 13 September 2013, 00:23:57

The JS Debugger frequently has the follow problems
1) An error dialog comes up and says "An internal error occurred during: "child count update". 3" and then all my variables disappear and I can't see them anymore. Restarting the app usually recovers the variables.
2) I step over a statement in debugger and the debugger freezes. It says "Stepping" in the debug window. The "Resume", "Step Into", "Step Over" and "Step Return" icons are all disable as well as the corresponding menu items.
3) I click on the "disclosure" icon on a variable, the debugger pauses for a while, acts like it's trying to open up the object for inspection by rotating the disclosure icon, then it just disappears.

Is there something that I can do to make the environment more reliable? Settings? Anything? Are other people having this problem? It is very difficult to get work done when the debugger is broken more than half the time.
