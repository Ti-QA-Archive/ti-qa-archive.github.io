  • Hi

    The language of your device is determined at OS level and not app level. The UI elements inherit their language from the locale of your device. Normally you wouldn't be able to change the language inside your app and there are no methods to set language or locale either in Titanium (that I can see). There are two modules for iOS and Android but I haven't tested them:

    • iOS - Locale Module
    • Android - Locale Module

    But if you simply want your app to translate to different languages based on your users device then you need to use localisation. This guide will tell you what you need to know: Internationalization.

    When you create your folder reference for French, you will need to create an 'fr' folder, instead of 'en' or 'es'.

    Hope that helps
