Push Notification problems in production

I had tested the push notification in development profile of appcelerator cloud services. My app has been uploaded on store. It gives an alert wether the user wants to allow push notification from the app. I get to see the iOS clients in my account but the clients get registered in development settings rather than production one. The same issue was with my previous app and as a result I could not send any notification. How to go about this? Please help as my app is going live on 27th.
