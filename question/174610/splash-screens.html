    You can change but not remove the splash screen that is shown as your app loads.Here is the documentation link. Anyway You can change it easily according to your application type . For this you just need to change the default.png image with the same name from Resource/android and Resource/iPhone directories according to your supported screen size. After changing the image clean the app or uninstall the app from device or simulator/emulator and reinstalled it.
