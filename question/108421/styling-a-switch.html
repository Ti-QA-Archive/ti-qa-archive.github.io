Styling a "Switch"

Is it possible to take a switch and change the location of the indicator graphic (e.g. put it above the text or to the side of the text)?

    Frank

    There is very little control provided for Android's switch. You can't change the graphic for it, for a start. Personally, I use an imageView with custom properties to store the state, enabledImageFileName and disabledImageFilename respectively.

    If you add it to a view and add a label to it also, you have complete control over its presentation/layout.

    Obviously, your mileage may vary. :)
