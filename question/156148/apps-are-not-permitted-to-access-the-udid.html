Apps are not permitted to access the UDID

On trying to submit my app today, I got the following error from Xcode:

> Apps are not permitted to access the UDID and must not use the uniqueIdentifier method of UIDevice. Please update your apps and servers to associate users with the Vendor or Advertising identifiers introduced in iOS 6.

This is all fine except that I'm using the UDID iOS module, so am not calling Ti.Platform.id. I'd initially thought it was the already broken Titanium PayPal module, but having removed that from the app and rebuilt, the issue doesn't seem to have gone.

The only other two modules that are being included are:

  • Ti.Barcode
  • Ti.StyledLabel

I was scheduled to submit the app to Apple today for an immovable deadline, and am now completely stumped. I'd really appreciate anyone's help.
