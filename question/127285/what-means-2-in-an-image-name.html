what means @2 in an image name

I don't understand what means when you have an image box.png and box@2.png. I understand that this is for different platforms to choose their file, but can you give me some more details?

  • It corresponds to an image 2 times bigger, which is used for the iPhone Retina devices. If you have an image.png in your app, you can just add an image@2x.png in the same folder and it will automatically be used on the Retina device. Also, it's @2x, not @2.
