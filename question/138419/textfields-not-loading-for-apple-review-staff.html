TextFields Not Loading for Apple Review Staff

I made a simple app that has 12 text fields in a window and does a calculation depending on what the user enters. Textfields are made like this (below). The app works in my simulator and on my iPhone 4 running iOS 5.1.1. But both times I have submitted it to apple its been rejected for just being a blank background with no textfields or buttons showing up.

app.js

var tabGroup = Titanium.UI.createTabGroup();
var win1 = Titanium.UI.createWindow({
    backgroundImage:'images/bg.png',
    url: 'main.js',
    navBarHidden: true,
    fullscreen:true,
    tabBarHidden:true
});
var rootTab = Titanium.UI.createTab({ 
        title:'Simple Dive Planner',
        window:win1
    });
tabGroup.addTab(rootTab);
tabGroup.open();

main.js

var win1 = Titanium.UI.currentWindow;

var depthfield = Titanium.UI.createTextField({
    color:'#336699',
    value:'',
    height:30,
    width:50,
    left:22,
    top:100,
    borderStyle:Titanium.UI.INPUT_BORDERSTYLE_ROUNDED,
    keyboardType:Titanium.UI.KEYBOARD_NUMBERS_PUNCTUATION,
    returnKeyType:Titanium.UI.RETURNKEY_DONE
});
win1.add(depthfield);

Again the above code works in simulator and on my device, but displays nothing for apple. What am I doing wrong here?
