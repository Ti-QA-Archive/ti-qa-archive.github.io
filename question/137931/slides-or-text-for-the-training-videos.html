    The Building Native Apps portion of the Guides should cover everything in the training program and then some. You can find it here: http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/2.0/index.html#!/guide/Building_Mobile_Applications_with_Titanium. We also have an ebook version (.epub and .mobi) which you can download from http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/2.0/index.html#!/guide/BNAPP_ebook.

    Labs are on our wiki – https://wiki.appcelerator.org/display/td/Home

    Code for the labs is posted on the wiki, but you can also grab starting/finished code as well as demo projects from our GitHub repo - https://github.com/appcelerator-training

    At this time, the slides aren't available publicly (just to our training partners). But really, there's not a lot in them that isn't more fully covered in the Guides I link to above.

    I hope that helps,
    Tim
