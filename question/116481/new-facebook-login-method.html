New Facebook Login method

How can I add the login method to my app???

New support for Facebook's OAuth 2.0 authentication scheme. Enable Facebook authentication from within Titanium apps by simply adding the login method to your app or placing the login button on a view. Basic permissions are set by default, with additional control available through a permissions property. Best of all, we'll manage the login process and session permissions.

UPDATED

SOLUTION:

This is what I was looking for.

Ti.Facebook.appid = 'XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX';

Ti.Facebook.authorize();

The authorize method will call the login.
