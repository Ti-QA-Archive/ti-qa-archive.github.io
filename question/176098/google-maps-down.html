google maps down

Hi, I'm wondering what is the best way to handle when GoogleMaps itself is down, from within my app.

It happened at the weekend for a small period of time where Google maps was not working in my app (blank screen) and it also wasn't working on the apps associated website. Considering it happened to both, I take it that Google maps was temporarily down. Both were back up and running the same day, not too sure how long it was down for.

I am using the module android maps v.2 for Android and the module in the marketplace for iOS as suggested in the docs. Is there some sort of a check I can have in to determine if google maps connection was successful or something like that?

  • Off the top of my head, perhaps you could set your own timer and listen for the complete event on the map view. If that event isn't fired within a reasonable time that you choose, you could show a dialog to the user.
