    Rainer,

    Take a look at the article i wrote last fall Titanium – Open PDF with Intents Resolved (Android). We found that files in the tmp folder on the Nexus 7 were being removed in a very short period of time after we wrote them. Files will stay in the data directory, but 3rd party apps cant read from that location. The key was to push the file through the intent immediately after writing it.
