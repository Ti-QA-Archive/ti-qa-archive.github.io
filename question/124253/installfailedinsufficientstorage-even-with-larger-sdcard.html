INSTALL_FAILED_INSUFFICIENT_STORAGE even with larger sdcard

I have tried installing my app to android emulator after replacing the generated android.sdcard with a 1gb one generated through mksdcard. Still getting the install error, what is wrong? The app is about 45Mb on iPhone when bundled so not sure why I am getting the error.

    This is how I do it. Should work

    <android >
    <tool-api-level>8</tool-api-level>
    <manifest android:installLocation="preferExternal">
        <uses-sdk android:minSdkVersion="7"/>
        <supports-screens android:anyDensity="false"/>
    </manifest>
</android>
