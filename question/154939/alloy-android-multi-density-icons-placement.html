Alloy Android multi-density icons placement

I am having trouble using multiple density icons for android, where are they placed? The documentation says to place them in /platform/android/res/drawable-[density]. I have placed my appicon and tabbar icons in this way but I cannot get them to display. They appeared fine when they were in /app/assets/android/. But now I have made multiple sizes and want to take advantage of the multiple density folders. Any help would be appreciated.

    • I have figured it out if anyone needs it as well

      here is the structure for the appicons

      \app\assets\android\appicon.png
\platform\android\res\drawable-hdpi\appicon.png
\platform\android\res\drawable-mdpi\appicon.png
\platform\android\res\drawable-mdpi\appicon.png
\platform\android\res\drawable-xhdpi\appicon.png

      place your density specific images here

      \app\assets\android\images\high
\app\assets\android\images\medium
\app\assets\android\images\low
