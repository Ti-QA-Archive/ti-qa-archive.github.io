  • You need to compile using xCode 5, linked with the iOS 7 libraries. iOS6 users will still see the previous UX, however you will need to completely create your own controls that mimic the iOS6 look and feel for ios7 users.

  • Hi Sebastian,

    It's a hack, but you can set a preference key in your App to force it to use the old ios6 Design

    Ti.App.Properties.setBool('UIUseLegacyUI', true);

    The only problem is, you have to restart your App to apply the legacy mode, so you can't use it really on Appstore Apps.
