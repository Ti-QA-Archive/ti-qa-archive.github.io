  • Accepted Answer

    The image is added to the window. And the window cannot be scrolled, so this is why the image stays fixed.
    Actually what you have to do is

    scrollView.add(ta1);
scrollView.add(imageView);

    instead of 

    win.add(ta1);
win.add(imageView);
