    • I just figured out that the default 'terms and conditions' for an app may be edited in iTunesConnect. Upon going into "View Details" for your app, then scrolling to the bottom, there a section labeled 'EULA" (End User License Agreement) and an EDIT button. Upon editing the standard EULA and saving, the revised version is made available in the app store.
