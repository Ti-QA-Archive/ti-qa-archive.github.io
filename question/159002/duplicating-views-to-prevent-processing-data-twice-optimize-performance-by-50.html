  • Hi

    If you are looking for a faster tableView, then as long as your needs are based around a consistent template (or a couple) - use listView

    No views, simply define one or more template layouts that your rows will use and provide the data. Depending on your actual needs this is usually much faster as long as your view needs are not very complicated.
