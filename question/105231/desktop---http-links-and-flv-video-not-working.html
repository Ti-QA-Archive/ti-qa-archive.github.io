Desktop - http links and flv video not working

Firstly I think Titanium is great. Well done to all that are involved in its making.

But (there is always a but!) I am having a few problems and I'm not sure it's me or the program (probably me, eh?!) - but any help from anywhere would be so greatly appreciated.

BRIEF:

I want to make a desktop application that simply opens a website I have already designed (in a local folder). It will go on a usb for a client and must be cross platform compatible.

Problems:

a) I've downloaded & installed Titanium (on my mac) and signed up etc all correctly, as well as downloading the desktop version of KitchenSink to start just for experimental testing.

But when I launch KitchenSink within Titanium, it just gives me a blank screen when I open it. I have looked in your Q&A and there seems to be an issue with file linking? Even with a few workarounds all I get is a blank accordian menu only. What is wrong?

b) Meanwhile, I have managed to put my website in the Resources folder (replacing the index.html file) and all works fine until I click on an external link, when the screen fades black 50% and a little image question mark appears. Again what's wrong?

c) I also have a .swf calling a .flv flash video (in the resources folder) on the homepage but it wont play the video - the .swf loads fine though.

Help please!

  • Ah! A little more browsing through the Q&A section revealed the answer from David Kaye in this thread: http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/12181/flash-player-10-swf-files-wont-display-in-titanium-developer-1-for-linux)
