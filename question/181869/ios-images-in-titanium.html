  • Base folder is resources/iphone/images
    There are NO other folders, the image sizes are based on naming convention.
    Naming convention is:

    • image.png : regular non-retina resolution
    • image@2x.png : retina images
    • image@3x.png : iPhone 6plus images

    You can see all of the image info for Android and iOS here http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/3.0/#!/guide/Icons_and_Splash_Screens-section-29004897_IconsandSplashScreens-Locationforyourgraphics

    Ray
