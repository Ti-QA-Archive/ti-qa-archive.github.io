  • open up this file ~/Library/Application Support/Titanium/mobilesdk/osx/"Your Titanium SDK"/android/builder.py

    then search for "if mount_path in ['/sdcard', '/mnt/sdcard']:"

    replace with "if mount_path in ['/sdcard', '/mnt/sdcard', '/mnt/sdcard-ext']:"

    and save
    app will not wait for sd card

  • I had the same problem you are describing. I noticed that it was using "XT928 2.3.3" as the Android API, so I switched it to "Android 2.3.3". After the switch, it started working.

    To change the Android API:

    1. Right click on the project under "Project Explorer" and select "Properties"
    2. Select "Run/Debug Settings"
    3. Select "Titanium Android Emulator - YOUR_PROJECT_NAME"
    4. Click "Edit…"
    5. Under the "Main" tab, change "Android API"
    6. Click "Apply"
    7. Click "OK"
    8. Click "OK"
