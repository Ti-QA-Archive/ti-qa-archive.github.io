Splash Screens & Back Button

I am trying to write an app that has a splash screen. I am reasonably proficient in JavaScript but am having problems with the API (weak documentation/lack of examples).

I have a company logo on the Default.png. My app then loads a splashscreen window for the product using a setTimeout() call. The splashscreen has a "start" button which closes the splashscreen, and starts the app proper.

My problems are:

1) If you press the back button in the main app - you just see the company logo - and then can't get back to the app.

2) When you reload the app - it doesn't always re-run properly. If you pressed the back button twice (back to the logo, then exit) and then reload the app, it doesn't restart and display the splash screen.

Am mainly developing on android, but it needs to work on iPhone as well.
