iOS vs Android Window Close

Hi,

In iOS i tried to close a window with win.close(); and it never worked (it just made everything back but did not go back also) so instead, I use :

Titanium.API.currentTabGroup.getActiveTab().close(win,{animated:true});|

currentTabGroup is custom handler for the tabgroup.

In Android though, I use win.close(); since it works fine.

But, in iOS when I close a window it gets collected by the garbage collector normally as it should and whatever it had declared in its group is GONE.

In Android, though, things declared and used in the scope of that window, dont get released and so I am asking, is there a way to close the window in android JUST LIKE iOS so that the window releases itself and gets collected by garbage collector normally as it should?
