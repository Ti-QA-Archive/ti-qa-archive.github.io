  • I understand your frustration.

    Based on my experience images and interface design are based on the iPhone's native/original design at 320x480. Images are then stretched to the other dimensions. I may be inaccurate on this since I have not seen documentation on it but this is what I have always assumed.

    Basically, I do not believe it is a matter of DPI but the images changing from square pixels to rectangular which will cause more noticeable display issues.
