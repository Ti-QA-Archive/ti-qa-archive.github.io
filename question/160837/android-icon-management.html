    • if you have (note the forward slash to start, androids a pain)

      icon : "/images/icons/ic_login.png"

      then the appropriately matched icon for that resolution is pulled in from the resolution specific folder.

      so to reference that base image, you would have it in lets say

      {project}&#x2F;app&#x2F;assets&#x2F;android&#x2F;images&#x2F;icons&#x2F;ic_login.png

      you would then have your mdpi icon:

      {project}&#x2F;app&#x2F;assets&#x2F;android&#x2F;images&#x2F;res-mdpi&#x2F;icons&#x2F;ic_login.png

      or

      {project}&#x2F;app&#x2F;assets&#x2F;android&#x2F;images&#x2F;icons&#x2F;res-mdpi&#x2F;ic_login.png

      and the rest (ex for xhdpi)

      {project}&#x2F;app&#x2F;assets&#x2F;android&#x2F;images&#x2F;res-xhdpi&#x2F;icons&#x2F;ic_login.png
      {project}&#x2F;app&#x2F;assets&#x2F;android&#x2F;images&#x2F;icons&#x2F;res-xhdpi&#x2F;ic_login.png
