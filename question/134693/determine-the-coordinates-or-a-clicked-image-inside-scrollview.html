Determine the coordinates or a clicked image inside scrollview

Titanium Mobile
SDK 1.8.2
iPhone SDK 4.3

Hi,

I have a row of images, more like icons, in a horizontal scrollview. I applied a touchEnabled: yes on each icons and that is working fine now. My next problem is to alert the x&y coordinate of the clicked item. How can i do this?

thanks for your help.

    hi Nash,

    you can do that on the click event of each view like..

    view.addEventListener('click',function(e){
    alert(e.x + ' >> ' + e.y);
    });
