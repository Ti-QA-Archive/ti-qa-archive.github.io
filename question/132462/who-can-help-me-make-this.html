  • I don't believe standard appcelerator users have time to make this for you. But maybe appcelerator employees will. If they don't , just pray for a Calendar module :D

    Anyway… You need a calendar JavaScript. Using that logic you could easily create a Calendar. Let me help you:

    1. draw a tableview , layout:horizontal

    2. a forloop loops trough days , it includes days that aren't in this month (in your example 31 July and 1/2/3rd September.

    3. every loop you get the number and draw a tableviewrow and if the day is in the month , draw it black , if it isn't draw it gray

    4. make an event listener and your logic for events
