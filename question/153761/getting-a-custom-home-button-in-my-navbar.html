    Hi

    You have told the app to add the button when you pressed the button, but as the button is not on the window anywhere - you cannot press it. I have moved the button outside the click event - this should work.

    var btnHome = Ti.UI.createButton({
    backgroundImage:"images/iconHome.png",
    width:20,
    height:20
});
$.windowRegister.setLeftNavButton(btnHome); // look here
btnHome.addEventListener('click', function(e) {
    alert('Pressed the home button');
});
