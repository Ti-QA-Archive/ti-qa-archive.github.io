  • I think you need to put the API key in your tiapp.xml file, then force a rebuild of your app to push the xml changes.

    Here's a sample of my tiapp.xml:

    ...
    <fullscreen>false</fullscreen>
    <navbar-hidden>false</navbar-hidden>
    <analytics>true</analytics>
<guid>842caf1d-f6b1-4d00-bea7-86fb37d6364f</guid>
<property name="ti.facebook.appid">121565818919827</property>
    <iphone>
        <orientations device="iphone">
            <orientation>Ti.UI.PORTRAIT</orientation>
        </orientations>
...

    The important thing here is the property tag with the appid.

    Hope this helped.
