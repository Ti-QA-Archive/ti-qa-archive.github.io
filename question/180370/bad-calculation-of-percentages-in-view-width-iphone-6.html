Bad calculation of percentages in view width [iPhone 6]

I have a very odd beaviour on iPhone 6. I have a window with horizontal layout, then I add 2 views with width = 50% but instead of this views appear side by side I get the views on in top of the other.

You can test this creating a new alloy project and in index.xml put this:

<Alloy>
    <Window class="container" layout="horizontal">
        <View width="50%" backgroundColor="red" height="100"></View>
        <View width="50%" backgroundColor="green" height="100"></View>
    </Window>
</Alloy>
