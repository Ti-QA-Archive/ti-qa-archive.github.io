  • If your machine is using proxy settings to connect to internet then all you need to put your proxy settings while logging to Titanium Studio. Where you entered username and password to login in studio, you will get proxy setup option below to it along with forgot password. Click on that proxy setup option and enter your proxy settings over there. That's it click OK and login to studio without any hassel.
