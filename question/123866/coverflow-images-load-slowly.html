  • I haven't tried it or tested it, but I would look at how you create and manage the array that coverFlow is reading from. You may need one array to store your list of images and then periodically 'feed' or 'refresh' the separate array that your coverFlow is reading from. See the KiSink example of 'coverflow_replace.js' - it basically shows resetting the array for the coverFlow. In addition, especially if you are using remote images, I would look at the example of table_view_dynamic_scroll for ideas on how to dynamically update the data your view is reading from (albeit not a coverFlow example.
