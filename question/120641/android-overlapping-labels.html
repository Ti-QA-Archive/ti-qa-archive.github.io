Android: Overlapping labels

I have a view with two labels inside of it. The two labels slightly overlap. The one on top has a zIndex one step higher than the one below it.

I had massive issues getting the one on top to even show (see this http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/120617/android-some-elements-not-showing-until-you-change-them) but finally got it to show by changing the text after the window open event fires.

Anyway… but now the one on top is appearing below the other. Neither has a backgroundColor or backgroundImage set… nor does the view they are in for that matter. But the part of the top one that overlaps is blanked out as if the one below had a backgroundColor. If anything I think it should be the other way around, since the top one has a higher zIndex.

I tried setting the backgroundColor to transparent and the backgroundImage to none and that doesn't work either.

If this is relevant, there is a background Image set on a view below all of them, which is what appears to be showing.

Can labels not overlap on Android?
