    • Ok Vincent,

      how to inspecting your app logging on iOS device, here we go.

      • put some logging into your code like: Ti.API.debug('e.data: ' + e.data);

      • deploy your app to your iOS device

      • start Xcode and open Xcode Organizer (upper right side icon) or from xCode menu: Window -> Organizer

      • select Devices icon

      • on the left side in DEVICES locate your iOS device and select Console

      • start your app on device

      • inspect the log output from your app

      • enjoy!

      Greetings,

      Matthias
