  • As usual, it was an issue with my code. :) The web service I'm consuming to get the urls was putting back slashes instead of forward slashes in the url path. Apparently Ti opens urls with back slashes inside the same webview and accepts them as valid and opens them. Once I had the server-side guy fix his urls, Ti opened the urls in Safari correctly.
