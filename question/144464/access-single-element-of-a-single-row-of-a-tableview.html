  • Objects in javascript have a parent and they have children. Children are referenced by the order they added.

    myObject.add(myFirstChild);

    myObject.children[0] references that child.

    if myFirstChild is a label then myObject.children[0].text is then available.

  • Let me try to paraphrase your problem:

    Your button is not within one of the TableViewRows (it lives outside the TableView). When the user clicks the button, you want to access the subviews of one or more rows.

    If that is the case

    You can use tabella_unificatrice.data[i] to access the i-th row of the TableView. Then you could reference the children of the row, which are your label and textfields.

    Example:

    var firstlabeltext = tabella_unificatrice.data[i].children[0].text;
