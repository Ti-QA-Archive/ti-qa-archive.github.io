  • Hi

    Your question is not very clear, I am reading it two ways;

    • Your Apps; You have built some apps using Titanium and when you run them on your jail broken iPod running iOS7 there is a navigational display issue?
    • Built in apps; You have jail broken your iPod and are now running iOS7 - and some of the built in apps have visual issues on the navigation bar?

    If you are talking about your apps then you need to check your apps on another device, make sure the second device has NOT been jail broken.

    If you are talking about your apps then you need to supply an screenshot so we can see what you are talking about as it could be many different things. Also provide a small reproducible snippet of code that we can try to see the problem.

    I would say that by jail breaking the device you have voided any warranty on the device which you likely are aware off. You may not be aware that the development licence may also be in question as you are using non-approved devices.
