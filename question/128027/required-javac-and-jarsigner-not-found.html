  • It looks like a couple requirements, like python, can't be found. You should likely make sure those can be found from your path. Also, there are sometimes issues targeting the Android SDK when its full path has spaces in it. Right now you have your Android SDK in C:\Program Files (x86)\..... Try moving it to another path that has no spaces in it.
