Tableview rows: editing, moving and clicking at the same time

I'm trying to get the same effect of the Bookmarks tableview in Safari.
When you click on the Edit button, the rows change with new icons:

  • red button that let you delete the bookmark

  • the 3 lines on the right that let you move the bookmark

  • a little arrow that open another window

I tried to put both editing and moving to the tableview at the same time, but if I do that, the row is not clickable, and I can't get to another window.

Did anyone find a workaround? besides rebuilding manually all of the rows with that effect.

  • The standard technique is for edit mode to be just that – edit mode. While in edit mode you can delete rows or move them around but NOT tap on a row to act on it until the user presses a Done/Cancel or similar button to exit Edit mode.

    See the kitchen sink tableview edit and move example.
