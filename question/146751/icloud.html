    Hi

    Just to confirm - you know that iCloud and ACS are not directly comparable, ACS goes much further in most cases and is cross platform unlike iCloud.

    This very much depends on what part of iCloud you are trying to gain the benefit of.

    • If you are looking to have your custom content, documents, settings saved and backed up automatically then this is already possible, many of the folders you can store your data in already enabled iCloud backups already and this occurs automatically.
    • If you are wanting to be able to access any of the enhanced features like sharing of data across your devices using iCloud as the single location then these are currently not supported and may not be directly. Appcelerator may choose to leave these integration to custom module providers (I have no inside knowledge). I could not find any iCloud modules in the market place currently though.

    You may need to expand your question to include your specific requirements.
