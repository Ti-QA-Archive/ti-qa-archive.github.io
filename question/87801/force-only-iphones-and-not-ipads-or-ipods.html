  • There's probably a way to do that, but your app would get rejected unfortunately.

    Here's a list of the App Store guidelines:
    http://developer.apple.com/appstore/resources/approval/guidelines.html

    Number 2.10 there would be the one that would get in your way:

    "iPhone apps must also run on iPad without modification, at iPhone resolution, and at 2X iPhone 3GS resolution"
