  • I have the same problem every time we issue a new certificate. Easy solution is to copy your certificate to that directory and give it the name A3418EBD-xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx.mobileprovision and rebuild.

    It's worth noting you can copy/paste the string from the dialogue.
