  • Apple does not allow access to the users phone number through code so Titanium also will not have access to the phone number. See the rejection notice in this post for detail.

    http://stackoverflow.com/questions/193182/programmatically-get-own-phone-number-in-iphone-os

    That is not to say you can't do it on Android, but you can't do it in iOS.

    BTW - The latest release of SpyPhone cannot access your own phone number either.
