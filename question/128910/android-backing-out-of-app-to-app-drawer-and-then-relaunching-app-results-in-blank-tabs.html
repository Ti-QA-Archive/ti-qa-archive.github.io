ANDROID: Backing out of app to app drawer and then relaunching app results in blank tabs

Hi guys, I have an app based on 4 tabs on the design of the default Titanium app template, as follows below.

The Issue i'm having is that when I am in any of the four tabs, and I press the Android Back button, the app will back out into the stock Android app drawer. Which is fine, but then when the app is relaunched, the app will launch (display the splash screen) and then load up the tabs each with blank white screens. If I switch between the tabs, the app functions normally in the background (I can tell because some processing popups Ive desined will pop up as normally), however no data is actually displayed in the windows. Only white screens.

I'm using 1.8.0.1 from the continuous builds FYI.

Thanks so much for any help with this, my app is 90% close to release, but this is a critical hurdle keeping me from releasing the app.
