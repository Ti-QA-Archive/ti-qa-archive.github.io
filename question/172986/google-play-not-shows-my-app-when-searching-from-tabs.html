  • First, things like this requirement blocks at least the nexus 7, since while it DOES have a camera, it has a user facing camera.

    <uses-permission android:name="android.permission.CAMERA"/>

    Adding the following says, 'we dont have to have a camera'.

    <uses-feature android:name="android.hardware.camera"  android:required="false"/>

    Then in your code, if/when you need the camera, check for the camera in code.
