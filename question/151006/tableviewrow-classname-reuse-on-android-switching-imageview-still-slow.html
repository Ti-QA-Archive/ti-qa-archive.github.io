TableViewRow className Reuse on Android: Switching ImageView Still Slow

I discovered TableViewRow className and TableViewRow reuse today. It greatly improves the performance of my TableView on both iPhone and Android. However for Android, if I scroll really fast, there is a noticeable delay in switching the right ImageView into the reused TableViewRow. So, I always see the old image for a split second before it gets switched to the new image.

Is there a way to tweak this? Perhaps by configuring TableView to use more rows of TableViewRow in memory and be more aggressive in switching in new content early?

I notice that the right ImageViews do not get switched in until they are at least 10-15% into the screen.
