Get Video Duration after recording it

I would like to get the duration of the video after recording it on iOS. I know it can be gotten from the videoPlayer, but I would prefer to not go that route unless i have to.

I was just wondering if there is some trick or undocumented feature on the media object that is returned from the success callback that might do the trick

    I don't know of a media object property, but what I do is:

    • When I start recording: var startTime = new Date();
    • When I stop recording: var stopTime = new Date();
    • When I want the duration: var msDiff = stopTime.getTime() - startTime.getTime();

    That gives me the duration in milliseconds which I can convert to any other format I need.
