  • I have talked directly with their sales department and I was told that you can make and sell apps for totally free, no matter if you are an individual or a corporation. The only thing that help them make money (and hence this keep wonderful project alive) is their support plans (you can subscribe and once you have a problem you can open a ticket which they will resolve for you) and also from partnership programs.

    If you are not paying for support then the only source for help is the open community … (this Q&A and of course other resources from the web).

    Hope this helps!
