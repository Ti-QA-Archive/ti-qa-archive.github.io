  • Accepted Answer

    you can't write in that directory.

    You can only write your files in the applicationDataDirectory:

    /Users/myusername/Library/Application Support/iPhone Simulator/6.1/Applications/034EF655-9B27-4041-9230-9752258AC440/Documents

    or in the tempDirectory:

    /Users/myusername/Library/Application Support/iPhone Simulator/6.1/Applications/034EF655-9B27-4041-9230-9752258AC440/tmp
