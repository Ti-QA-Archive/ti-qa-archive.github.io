Development Certificate - Remove

I'm having a problem building apps to any of my devices, I'm pretty sure I found out that my issue happened because my Dev. Cert. expired. I replaced it and it made no difference, I keep getting the same install error. So I cleared everything out, all provisioning profiles and dev. cert. But for some reason titanium is still seeing a dev. certificate. I've looked through the keychain and there are none in there.

I think titanium is still using my old certificate thats expired and thats why I get provisioning problems when building apps to my devices.

How can I clear out the Development Certificate in titanium?

Thanks

  • I figured this one out after about 4 hours of messing around. There was an expired cert. in the system keychain but I couldn't see it, it was hidden. I had to go View > Show Expired Certificates. Why it wouldn't show that expired one, but would show the one in the login keychain is beyond me.
