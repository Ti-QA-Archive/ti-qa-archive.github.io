  • I see you noted you have a Alloy 1.2.2, but this sounds like an issue that would be encountered by Alloy 1.3.0 (from github) users attempting to build for iOS device against the TiSDK prior to 3.2.0. In short, the TiSDK is not properly handling the platform-specific Resources folders that Alloy relies on in the 1.3.X branch. There's details on that ticket here: https://jira.appcelerator.org/browse/TIMOB-14884
