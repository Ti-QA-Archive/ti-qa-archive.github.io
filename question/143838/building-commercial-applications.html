  • There are no fees when you are developing an app. (at all)
    So to answer your question regarding your enterprise application, NO you won't have to give a single cent if you don't develop using Titanium.

    The only instance where you would have to pay something is if you wanted to paid support or if you used a lot of Appcelerator Cloud Services.
