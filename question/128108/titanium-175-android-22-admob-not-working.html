  • If the problem is intermittent, there's a good chance that the adview is just not receiving an appropriate ad for that run. Admob fill rates can be really low sometimes, particularly when you first start testing your app. Make sure you have enabled Adsense ads in your Admob management console, as this will help increase your fill rate.
