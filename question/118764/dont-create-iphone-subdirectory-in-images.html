Don't create iphone subdirectory in /images

Not a question but a heads up for iPhone and iPad development. If you want to have a separate folder for OS specific images, don't name it simply images/iphone or images/ipad – the emulator will show them but the device will not. Instead use images/iphone-images/ or something like that.

Hope this helps someone else. I've made the mistake on two separate projects and took hours to remember my earlier solution!
