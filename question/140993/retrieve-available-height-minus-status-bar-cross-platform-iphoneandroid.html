  • Hi,

    More simple is to use height: Ti.UI.FILL on your view (available since 2.0.1.GA).

    No problem on iPhone/iPad (fix size) but I has same problem on a cross-platform app for mobile & tablet (iOS/Android).

    After lot of tests with height of Android elements on Android 2.x / Android 4.x (mobile & tablet) and screen ratio I foud solution with use of Ti.UI.FILL.

    You must think your design "responsive" on Android and i think the best way is to use new Ti.UI.FILL and Ti.UI.SIZE (size of your view).

    More info in Titanium Documentation

    Have a good day!
