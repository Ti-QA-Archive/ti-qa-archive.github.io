How can I force NO rotation of orientation and keep it portrait

I'd like to keep the orientation the same, no matter what happens. I want it to be set to portrait on startup, and not be affected by rotation in any way, not even going upside down. Currently, if I turn the phone upside down, the screen "helpfully" rotates so that the top is facing towards the sky again. That's normal behavior but not what I want. I'd like to lock the screen so that if I'm holding the phone, say like an astronaut, and am being twirled in the space, the phone orientation never changes.

Currently the problem is just on the iPhone/iOS platform. Here's what I have in tiapp.xml: 

<ios>
    <min-ios-ver>7.0</min-ios-ver>
    <plist>
        <dict>
            <key>UISupportedInterfaceOrientations~iphone</key>
            <array>
                <string>UIInterfaceOrientationPortrait</string>
            </array>
            <key>UISupportedInterfaceO rientations~ipad</key>
            <array>
                <string>UIInterfaceOrientationPortrait</string>
            </array>
            <key>UIRequiresPersistentWiFi</key>
            <false/>
            <key>UIPrerenderedIcon</key>
            <false/>
            <key>UIStatusBarHidden</key>
            <false/>
            <key>UIStatusBarStyle</key>
            <string>UIStatusBarStyleLightContent</string>
            <key>shouldAutorotateToInterfaceOrientation</key>
            <false/>
            <key>shouldAutorotate</key>
            <false/>
        </dict>
    </plist>
</ios>

Thanks for any help.

    Hi Eddie,

    Thanks for your concern.

    You can lock orientation to "Portrait" mode by adding the below code to tiapp.xml file.

    <iphone>
    <orientations device="iphone">
            <orientation>Ti.UI.PORTRAIT</orientation>
    </orientations>
    <orientations device="ipad">
            <orientation>Ti.UI.PORTRAIT</orientation>
    </orientations>
</iphone>

    For more details, check the AppC Doc as reference.

    Limiting orientation modes on iOS

    Hope it'll help you to solve your problem.

    Thanks
