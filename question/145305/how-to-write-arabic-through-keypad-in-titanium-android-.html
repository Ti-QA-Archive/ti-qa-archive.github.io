  • Hi

    In order to get non western alphabets (in fact most symbols as well) in your labels or other views you need to parse the content into Unicode. This will then display nicely.

    Check out the answer I provide a while ago when helping someone create an arabic calculator.

    mathematical operations on arabic numbers

    You will notice here that Unicode was used to show the numbers;

    ...
value: '\u0660\u0661\u0662+\u0663\u0664\u0665+\u0666\u0667', //123+456+67
...

    You will need to find out the codes to replace with Template:Unicode_chart_Arabic

    Not easy but it is possible.

    If you do not encode first the app will fail to run - which you have already likely seen.
