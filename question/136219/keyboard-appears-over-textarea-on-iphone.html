Keyboard appears over textarea on iPhone

I have an app where a textarea is placed at the bottom of the screen. When tapping in the textarea, the iphone keyboard appears over or on top of the text area, making it impossible to read what you are typing.

Is there a good practice for handling this?

    Add all your content in a scrollview, it will scroll for you up when the keyboard slides in.
    If is not enough for you you can animate yourself the textarea when it gets focused.
