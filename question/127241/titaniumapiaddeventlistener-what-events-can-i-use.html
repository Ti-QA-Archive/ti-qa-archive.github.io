    Hi,

    for all widgets or controls or what ever you call them you will have a list of default events like open , close etc.

    To listen to these events you use addEventListener 

    var btn = Ti.UI.createButton({ ... });
btn.addEventListener('click',function(e){
    Ti.API.info('Btn Clicked');
});

    Other than the default Events you can fire custom Events and Listen to them 

    //firing custom Event
Ti.App.fireEvent('CustomEvent1',{data:{'check':1}});

//listening to custom Event
Ti.App.addEventListener('CustomEvent1',function(e){
    Ti.API.info(JSON.stringify(e.data));
});
