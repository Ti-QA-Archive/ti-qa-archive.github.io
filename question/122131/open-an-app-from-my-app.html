Open an app from my app

Hi, i want to know if it's possible to open an other app when i click on a button in my app. I want to open the app Sygic (GPS navigator) when i click on a button.

  • To do this, the app must have registered a custom url scheme. Just like the Navigon example, some apps have an application url that can be used to launch the app from other locations on the device. Here is a list of popular apps with registered urls: http://wiki.akosma.com/IPhone_URL_Schemes

    So, for instance, the Facebook url is "fb://", to launch that in your code, you would simply do this:

    Titanium.Platform.openURL('fb://profile');

    Nothing complicated or C involved, the app you wish to launch just needs to have one of these custom url schemes.
