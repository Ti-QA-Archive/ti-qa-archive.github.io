  • Accepted Answer

    The capability to launch in landscape was added in 4.1.2; older Android devices do not support it. I'm not sure whether that's implemented in our handling of the launch activity, though at one point we had a bug where the splash screen would launch in landscape, developers complained, and that behavior was prevented. There's certainly no documentation on what files you'd need to provide and I'm guessing it would probably work only with 9-patch images for your splash screen. To make it work, you'd have to modify the tiapp.xml as shown by Dharmik and Sanat.

  • Hello Hoang,

    Try this :

    <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
   <manifest>
      <application>
         <activity  android:name=".testActivity"
                     android:label="test"
                       android:theme="@style/Theme.Titanium"
                      android:configChanges="keyboardHidden|orientation"
                     android:screenOrientation="landscape">  // SET THIS
            <intent-filter>
                  <action android:name="android.intent.action.MAIN" />
                  <category android:name="android.intent.category.LAUNCHER" />
             </intent-filter>
         </activity>
      </application>
   </manifest>
</android>
