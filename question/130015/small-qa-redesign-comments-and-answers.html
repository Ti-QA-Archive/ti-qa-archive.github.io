Small Q/A redesign (comments and answers)

Since answers and comments functionality in Q/A can be confusing for new users, but also sometimes experienced users posts an answer instead of comment, I think that this could be improved with small UI redesign.

I suggest that there should be two buttons ("Add comment" that is red for comments and "Add answer" that is green for answers) instead of "Add comment" link (that is very small and hard to notice) for comments and text field for answers. Click of a button would open popup window (or show text field) in which we could write and post text.

I think that way we would get much clearer solution. It's very important to use comments when appropriate because otherwise is awful to read and track discusion.
