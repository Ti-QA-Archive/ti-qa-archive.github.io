iPad Wallpaper App

I am looking to develop a iPad wallpaper app using some photos of mine. I am not much of a developer, but was wondering what a good approach would be to do this?

I plan on having a site which will have static URL's for the galleries and wallpapers. I was just not sure how I should go about doing this in Titanium.

Any ideas are appreciated.

  • Maybe set up an RSS feed on your site that has all of your wallpapers and information about the wallpapers, then use the RSS feed example in the KitchenSink to bring it all together. With the RSS feed your users can be updated when you post new stuff.
