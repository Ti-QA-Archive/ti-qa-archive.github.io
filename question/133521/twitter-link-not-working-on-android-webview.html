Twitter link not working on android webview

Hi,

I use the following type of link for searching through twitter api:

http://twitter.com/#!/jordan
or
https://twitter.com/#!/jordan

I'm adding this link (tried both types) in a webview ( I am forming its html). This is working fine for iphone but it does not work in android (tested on 2.2 & 2.3). The webview opens, showing a white page with the twitter logo in the middle and not the search results. So, my problem is that the content of the search is not loading for android..

Any ideas why? What's wrong with android browser? :/

PS: sdk 1.8.1, android sdk 8 & 10
