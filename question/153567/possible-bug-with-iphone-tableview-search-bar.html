The crash happens when I click on any row resulting from search bar filtering.

  • I found the cause of crash. it is this line in TiUITableView.m which delays hiding the search bar for 200 ms, when I am closing the window immediately.

    [self performSelector:@selector(hideSearchScreen:) withObject:nil afterDelay:0.2];

    my workaround was to delay closing the window.

    https://github.com/appcelerator/titanium_mobile/commit/594bee84c41b352996d6dcf7a304f1e9ae32af67#commitcomment-3446266
