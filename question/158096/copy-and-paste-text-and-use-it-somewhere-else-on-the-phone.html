The text is in a label, textarea or textfield, and then copied the text can be used (paste) anywhere in the users phone.

(For example that you can do in the app "tagsforlikes")

    Hi

    Sounds like you need the clipboard features;

    • Clipboard

    Quick Example;

    var txt = Ti.UI.createTextField({
    left: 10,
    right: 10,
    top: 10,
    width: Ti.UI.FILL
});
var btn = Ti.UI.createButton({
    title: 'Copy to Clibpoard',
    top: 100
});
btn.addfEventListener('click', function (e) {
    Ti.UI.Clipboard.setText(txt.getValue());
    alert('Copied to clipboard');
});

    When you press the button, the text you will have entered into the textField will be placed into the devices clipboard. You can then paste this text into other apps or services within the rest of the device.
