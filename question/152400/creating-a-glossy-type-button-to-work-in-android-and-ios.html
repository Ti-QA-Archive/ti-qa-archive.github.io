  • By the way creating the glossy button is as easy as dirt in titanium Just take an imageview and give it the path of glossy image and you are good to go

    var ImgPodCast = Ti.UI.createImageView({
        top : '28%',
        right : '0%',
        width : '50%',
        height : '24%'
    });
    self.add(ImgPodCast);
    ImgPodCast.addEventListener('click', function(e) {
    alert('I am glassy button');
    });
