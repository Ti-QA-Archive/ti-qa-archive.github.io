Progressbar doesn't show on iOS apps. it does on android

Progressbar doesn't show ?? what should i do ?

var progressBar = Titanium.UI.createProgressBar({
    width:250,
    min:0,
    max:10,
    value:0,
    color:'#fff',
    message:'Downloading 0 of 10',
    font:{fontSize:14, fontWeight:'bold'},
    style:Titanium.UI.iPhone.ProgressBarStyle.PLAIN
});

sorry for asking like twice the same question but i hit on that 'correct answer' thing before and it wasn't answered…

I have added my progressbar to my win object and it shows good on Android systems…. it fails on iOS…

    You have to add it to your view as well as show it.

    Titanium.UI.currentWindow.add(progressBar);
progressBar.show();

    You might have to go to your "APP/build/iphone" directory and delete all the contents and then do a fresh re-build. The module might not have fully loaded.
