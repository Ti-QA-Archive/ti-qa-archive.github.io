Android: Change the cursor in a TextField?

  • Application type: mobile
  • Ti SDK: 3.4.0 GA
  • Platform: Android 4.2.2
  • Device: Genymotion and physical device

http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/178752/cursor-color

  • I decided to take a step back and see if I could get any of the built in themes to work before trying to customize it and was not having any luck. The problem ended up being a detail that I omitted from my original post.

    In my tiapp.xml I had an additional tag to force portrait orientation, as such:

        <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
        <manifest>
            <application android:theme="@style/Theme.Custom"/>
            <application>
                <activity android:name="org.appcelerator.titanium.TiActivity"
                    android:screenOrientation="portrait"
                    android:configChanges="orientation"/>
            </application>
            <uses-sdk android:minSdkVersion="14" android:targetSdkVersion="19"/>
        </manifest>
    </android>

    The second "application" tag was overriding the first.

    I moved the "activity" tags into the top "application" tag and now have the custom themes working properly, as such:

        <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
        <manifest>
            <application android:theme="@style/Theme.Custom"
                <activity android:name="org.appcelerator.titanium.TiActivity"
                    android:screenOrientation="portrait"
                    android:configChanges="orientation"/>
            </application>
            <uses-sdk android:minSdkVersion="14" android:targetSdkVersion="19"/>
        </manifest>
    </android>

    What I had considered an inconsequential detail (forced orientation) was the cause of my troubles.
