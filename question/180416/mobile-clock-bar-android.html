  • Hello Matteo,

    Thanks for your inquiry, In Android Hiding action bar will able you to use the fullscreen of the device. To hide Android action bar follow this link. Here follow the second method. Now hiding action bar and using fullscreen: true property will partially hide the status bar. A downward gesture from top will display the status bar. This way you can access full screen also the status bar. Or as Nilesh described fullscreen: false will permanently enables the status bar in your app.

    Hope this helps. Thanks.
