  • I have partially figured this out and I'm back in business. I want to post what I learned in case it helps someone else. 1st regarding the CLI errors. I believe (though I didn't take the time to prove) that my provisioning profile was blowing up TI or CLI because there are spaces in the name. Spaces in the name should be fine because Apple allows it (in the online dev center, in Keychain, and in Xcode). I learned that in the latest version of the SDK, spaces are allowed. But my TI settings were pointing at an older SDK: 3.0.2 GA. Once I pointed at the newest SDK (3.1.1 GA) the errors went away.

    Next, regarding the annoying error "Initalizing Node.ACS (30%)", this never did go away, but it also didn't cause any problems. I'm able to run and build, no problems. I am certain this is a bug in TI because it has been seen by many others, as evidenced in other posts and bug reports I have seen. Appcelerator: PLEASE fix that in the next release. Thanks.
