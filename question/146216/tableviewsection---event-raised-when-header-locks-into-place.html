  • Hi

    The short answer is "No".

    You can try and work it out, but it may not be as easy as you want.

    The scroll event contains two parameters than can help, however Android and iOS handle this very differently.

    • Android; firstVisibleItem is the indexed item that is the first visible in the viewport.
    • iOS; contentOffset is the dimensional position (x/y)

    Not ideal, but if you must have the answer it "can" be determined.
