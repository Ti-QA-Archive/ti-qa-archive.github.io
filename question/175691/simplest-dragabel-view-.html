    • All elements that have a touchmove event are draggable.

      myView.addEventListener('touchmove', function(e){
    console.log(e);
};

      That will log the data you get when your finger moves across the screen. You now need to take the changes (deltas) of the x/y movement and apply that to the positioning of your view.

      — commented July 2nd 2014 by Stephen Feather

    • You probably ought to start with a touchstart event to save the original starting coords of your view.
