The title does not fit into the TableViewRow in IOS.

I have a tableview with 4 rows. The problem is in IOS, in Android is ok.
The title don't fit into the row. For example the title "Una vez conectado aparecerá 'remote' en la pantalla de su termostato" in android it occupies 2 lines but in IOS cut the title " "Una vez conectado aparecerá 'remote' en la pantal…"

What can i do?

Thanks

    You need to add label inside the tableview row. Below is the sample code.

    
<TableView  top="42%" height="26%" left="5%" zindex="20" width="90%" backgroundColor="white" separatorColor="gray">
    <TableViewRow id="Instrucc1" onClick="Marca1" color="black" title="Ponga en marcha su Termostato">
                    <Label height="Ti.UI.SIZE" width="Ti.UI.SIZE">Una vez conectado aparecerá remote en la pantalla de su termostato</Label>
    </TableViewRow>                    
</TableView>

    Hope this will help you.
