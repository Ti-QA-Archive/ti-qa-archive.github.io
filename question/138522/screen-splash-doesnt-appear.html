  • for iphone

    under resource/iphone you have to put your default.png file it will be good if your png size is of iphone screen size.

    for Android

    Due to lots of android devices the we have different resolution screen, hence, under resources/android/images
    you will see folders like res-long-land-hdpi etc. each of these folder has its own default.png file.
    so based on your screen or device resolution you can change the necessary image file.

    This will fix your problem
