Map v2 with older play services

I'm finding that trying to use the Maps v2 module will crash an app completely on play services that are not completely up to date.

On a test device, play services v4.0.34 (924341-34) will cause a crash.
Updating play services to v5.0.84 (1259630-034) fixed the issue.

The problem here is that I can't assume everyone using my app will have newer play services. Considering that the maps module has been around for a while, it seems likely that the older play services worked at one time.

Is there a documented way to support some of the older play services so users who are not updating their phones will be able to run my app with Maps v2?

Thanks!
