Clicking too quickly / too many times on the tabbar makes the app crash

We have a "Tweetanium"-style app that has a bunch of windows loaded in memory. Things are working well, except that if we "button mash" and switch tabs really quickly on the tab bar the app will crash, presumably due to memory starvation. What's the best way to combat this? Should I add some sort of trigger to kill window loads if there's been another click? Should I simply lock the tabbar until the next window has loaded?
