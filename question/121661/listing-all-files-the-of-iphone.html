    Hi. Short answer: No.

    You can list files and folders in your application Resources directory or the Documents/data folder 'owned' by your app.

    The rest of the devices' filesystem is 'private' with respect to your app.

    I tried to get a listing of the built-in audio recordings on an iPhone, but found that the directory listing was inaccessible, even though I knew the location.
