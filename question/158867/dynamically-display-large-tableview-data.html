Dynamically display large TableView data

I have had a longstanding issue with Titanium and memory. I have this module in my application that loads days-months of data from a data logger. I noticed that if I tried to load too many rows (100+) that scrolling down/up through them would cause the UI to eventually freeze and then my app would crash. So what I HAVE been doing was limiting the amount of rows added to less than 100. Now I recently lost my phone and had to downgrade to a middle tier phone and I noticed that it crashes with even less rows added, so I am trying to go back through and find an efficient way of dealing with this.

Ideally what I would like is to load 30 rows and after the user scrolls passedscrollEvent.firstVisibleItem>20 I add 10 rows and remove the first 10. Likewise if the user scrolls up so that scrollEvent.firstVisibleItem<15 I prepend 10 rows and remove the last 10 rows.

I tried modifying this to my needs: https://gist.github.com/dawsontoth/810391, but I can't get it to work properly. Does anyone know of a good way to implement this?
