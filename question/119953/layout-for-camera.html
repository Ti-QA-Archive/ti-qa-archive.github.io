Hi,

I'd like to add a overlay on my camera in order to take picture of QR code, or sudoku, or something square.
So i made a blaque picture with a transparent square at the center, and a put this code:

QRview.addEventListener('click',function(){
    var overlayView = Titanium.UI.createView({});
    var overlayImageView = Titanium.UI.createImageView({
        Image: '/images/overlay.png'
    });
    overlayView.add(overlayImageView);

    Titanium.Media.showCamera({
        autohide: true,
        allowImageEditing:false,
        saveToPhotoGallery:false,
        overlay: overlayView
    });
});

however nothing happend, i just have my camera like i just want to take a picture without the overlay. Is it because this option is not available on android device? Or have i something wrong???

Thanks for your help

  • Is this exactly as you have it pasted from your code? One thing I can see is that you have "Image:" with a capital 'I'. It should be lowercase. image:'/images/overlay.png'

    I might also want to consider pulling out the Titanium.Media.showCamera outside your click listener event function to a separate function - because you will need to have event listeners WITHIN that, to know when you have taken a photo.

    It's not a good idea to nest a listener within a listener, I think ;)
