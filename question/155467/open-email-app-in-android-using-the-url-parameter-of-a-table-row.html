  • Hi Matt,

    To open email client you can use emailDialog also.

    var emailDialog = Ti.UI.createEmailDialog()
emailDialog.subject = "write subject here";
emailDialog.toRecipients = ['hello@mattic-design.com'];
emailDialog.open();
