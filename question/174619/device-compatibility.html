  • Hi

    Sounds like your layout does not take into account resolution independence. Depending on how you have built your app the changes will be, trivial to mildly annoying - but only because you have to tweak values in your positioning properties.

    Check these two answers I have given previously and are usually quoted in this situation, the titles may look like they are only image related, but you will find they provide a significant amount of detail in how to manage layouts;

    • images in Android
    • How to achieve same layout for Android and iPhone using Titanium

    The main key is to use dimensions in device in-dependant pixels by changing a value in tiapp.xml. Then making sure all your values are just numbers when used with properties. Those answers should give more than enough information.
