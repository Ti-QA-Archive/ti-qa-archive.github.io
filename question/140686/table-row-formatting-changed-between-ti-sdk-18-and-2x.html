table row formatting changed between Ti SDK 1.8 and 2.x

I built an iPad app with Ti SDK 1.8.2 back in April. It's heavy on Tables and Rows, and it all looked fine. Now in the latest 2.x SDK, it's as though all the rows lost 100% of their padding. All tight and compressed, and really messed up my UI. Is there some way to easily achieve the old look? I'm trying to avoid having to go back and style hundreds of rows and tables just to make it how it was before. Also, it's not an option to go back to the old SDK.

Any thoughts or advice on this?
