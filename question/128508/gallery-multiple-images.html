  • As far as grabbing images from the gallery to do it, I haven't seen that done without having to import an image one at a time from the photo gallery.

    As far as the effect that is in that link goes, that would be possible. It's a matter of creating an ImageView, set the zIndex of each image and setting the size of each image. You would then make each image change the size and zIndex followed by an animation of the image up front to fade while growing in size. Could be a better way for that, but that's what I quickly envisioned.

    Then again, I could have misunderstood what you meant by "UI of gallery of device".
