  • Wang,

    Its only inconceivable because you have no clue how Titanium actually works.

    Your JavaScript is interpreted by a JS engine that is shipped WITH you app. This is V8 on android and jscore on iOS. This is to ensure a stable base engine that can be trusted to behave the way you expect it to. In the future this may be changed, but this is the reason your binary package seems to be larger than you would expect.

    Mucking around with whats being packaged for you is a fool's errand.
