  • Accepted Answer

    AppStore

    Ti.Platform.openURL('itms-apps://ax.itunes.apple.com/WebObjects/MZStore.woa/wa/viewContentsUserReviews?type=Purple+Software&id=here_goes_the_id_number_of_your_app');

    Google Play

    Ti.Platform.openURL("market://details?id=com.yourapp.identificator");

  • Answers here are outdated (won't work on iOS 7)

    This works on my end (Xcode 5 - iOS 7 - Device!):

    itms-apps://itunes.apple.com/app/idYOUR_APP_ID?at=10l6dK

    For versions lower than iOS 7 use the old one:

    itms-apps://itunes.apple.com/WebObjects/MZStore.woa/wa/viewContentsUserReviews?type=Purple+Software&id=YOUR_APP_ID?at=10l6dK
    ObjectiveC code snippet (you can just copy& paste it):

    #define YOUR_APP_STORE_ID 123456789

static NSString *const iOS7AppStoreURLFormat = @"itms-apps://itunes.apple.com/app/id%@?at=10l6dK";
static NSString *const iOSAppStoreURLFormat = @"itms-apps://itunes.apple.com/WebObjects/MZStore.woa/wa/viewContentsUserReviews?type=Purple+Software&id=%@?at=10l6dK"

[NSURL URLWithString:[NSString stringWithFormat:([[UIDevice currentDevice].systemVersion floatValue] >= 7.0f)? iOS7AppStoreURLFormat: iOSAppStoreURLFormat, YOUR_APP_STORE_ID]]; // Would contain the right link

    There are rumours that iOS 7.1 would also work with old link. I haven't seen anything real that claim it but you shall check it out in time.
