    Please Update your Table View Section, it may help you

    function SacoSabado(){

TablaVisible=1; 
$.Tabla.updateSection( 0, $.Dias, false);
}

    • Hi,

      Please use

      $.Tabla.updateSection( 0, $.Dias, false);

      for every button click function.

      Here

      0 : Is the index of TableViewSection

      $.Dias: Is the Updated TableViewSection

      false: Is the animation property(iOS)
