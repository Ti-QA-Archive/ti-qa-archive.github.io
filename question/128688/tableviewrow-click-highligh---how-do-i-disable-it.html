TableViewRow click highligh - how do I disable it?

Hi,

I've created a table view with some rows in it. everything is great on both android and iPhone. When I click (touch) the row, even when it has not event attached to it, it highlights a blue color that fades in and out quickly on iPhone and a yellow background while pressed on android. How can I make the row to not show these highlights / background color ?

Thanks

  • For iPhone Titanium.UI.iPhone.TableViewCellSelectionStyle but if you want to make it on both platform you can use selectedColor: &#39;transparent&#39;;

    • Add it in your createTableViewRow. I haven't tried selectedColor as well. But you can try this:

      var tableVRow = Ti.UI.createTableViewRow({selectionStyle: Titanium.UI.iPhone.TableViewCellSelectedStyle.NONE;})

      — commented November 25th 2011 by Julius Francisco

      var tableVRow = Ti.UI.createTableViewRow({selectionStyle:Ti.UI.iPhone.TableViewCellSelectionStyle.NONE});
