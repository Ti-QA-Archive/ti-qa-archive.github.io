Splash Screen effects

I have seen splash screens Titanium sample apps(Kitchen Sink, CodeStrong) taking different transition effects when disappearing. How can I achieve these effects in my apps?

    In your app.js when you open your main window write this code (For iPhone).

    // open window with a transition animation
win.open({
    transition:Titanium.UI.iPhone.AnimationStyle.FLIP_FROM_LEFT
});

    So basically it's animation you apply on your first main window. So it looks like your splash screen is animating but actually it's your first window which is animating.
