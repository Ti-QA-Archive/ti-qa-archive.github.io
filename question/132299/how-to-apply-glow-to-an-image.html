  • well right now applying changes to image properties are not available in titanium or i hvnt seen this kind of functionality and module.In another way to can try to put a view over image view to fullfill ur requirements .here's the code:

    var imageview=Ti.UI.createImageView({
height:100,
width:100,
top:10,
image:// specify image url
});
var view =Ti.UI.createView({
height:100,
width:100,
top:10,
backgroundColor:'transparent',
backgroundSelectedColor:'green',
opacity://0.0 to 1.0 as per your need
}):
win.add(imageview);
win.add(view);

    hope this will work.cause this trick worked for me.
