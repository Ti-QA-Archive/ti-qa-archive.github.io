    Looking at the callstack it appears that android does not like a color value you are setting.

    [ERROR][TiApplication( 993)] at android.graphics.Color.parseColor

    Which appears to be happening when you are setting a background color on a tableViewrow

    [ERROR][TiApplication( 993)] at ti.modules.titanium.ui.widget.tableview.TiBase TableViewItem.setBackgroundFromProxy(

    But without a code example is impossible to say what value is wrong
