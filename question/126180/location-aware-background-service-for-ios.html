  • It is possible, but for it to work, you will also have to make sure that you declare location in the background modes in a custom Info.plist 

    
<key>UIBackgroundModes</key>
    <array>
        <string>location</string>
    </array>

    Take a copy of the Info.plist from your build folder and put it in the project root folder.
