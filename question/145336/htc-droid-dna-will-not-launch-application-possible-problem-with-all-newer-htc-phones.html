HTC Droid DNA will not launch application, possible problem with all newer HTC phones.

I would like to know if anyone is having problems with their Titanium apps that are in the play store launching on HTC devices. I recently tested out app on the Droid DNA and the app when launched from the Launcher, app drawer, or the play store will kick the user to the play store after showing the bug2373 dialog that restarts the application. As a test, I replaced the bug 2373 values with the finishfalseroot flag, rebuilt the app for the device and while it no longer shows the restart dialog, it still kicks the user back to the play store everytime I try to launch the app. I have encountered a problem previously on a One X where the icon created on the home screen after installing the app exhibited similar behavior but launching from the app drawer still worked. I have tried installing another launcher like Nova Launcher and the app still will not launch.

Has anyone else experienced this and is there any workaround for this?
