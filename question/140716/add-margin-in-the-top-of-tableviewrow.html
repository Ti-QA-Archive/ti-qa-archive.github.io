    • For the very small empty rows between actual rows, try making a TableViewRow with height:15 (or whatever height you want) and color:&#39;transparent&#39;. If the row turns out taller than you asked for, there is a minRowHeight property of the TableView that may need to be set. I have not tested this exact case, but I have built TableViews with varying row heights mixed into one TableView.
