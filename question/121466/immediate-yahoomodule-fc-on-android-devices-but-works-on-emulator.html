Immediate 'YahooModule' FC on Android devices (but works on Emulator)

Hello community:

For the last few days (I'm currently at SDK 1.7.1 and 1.0.1 of Studio), I'm having problems running Titanium applications on a device. They work fine on emulator.

This is true for Kitchen Sink or a default project (like the Geolocation project or even the 'I am Window 1' project). Sometimes a brand new project will work (sometimes it will crash) but then if I change even one line it will crash.

Crashes immediately upon startup trying to create the initial activity with a NullPointer exception. I don't know much about Ti internals, but it seems (from the stack trace) that the application is dying while trying to bind to a YahooModule.

Yes, I've tried deleting everything in my build directories. I've even deleted and reinstalled Ti studio. I upgraded to Ti Indie. I tried multiple physical devices. I was not having this problem until very recent versions of Studio and SDK.

A representative ddms log is at http://pastie.org/2109661

Any ideas? I'm completely stuck and need to demo on a device tomorrow.

TIA
