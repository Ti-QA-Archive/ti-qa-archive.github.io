Embed video or stream video? Which is better?

I am looking to have numerous 5-8 minute clips (some as large as 5 MB) used throughout my app. Is it better to store them within the app as a local resource or to embed them from a streaming site? Which do users prefer - disk space utilization versus data bandwidth?

  • It depends on your app usage and target audience. I would ask first before you decide:

    • My users have an internet connection available all the time when they need to use my app?
    • If they dont have, can i offer a choice? download the video on demand for instance?
    • I will upgrade the app with more videos or this is it?
    • (8x5mb = 48 mb) so my users will only be able to purchase my app trough wifi. This is an issue or not?

    If you can share more details on your app target audience and expected usage maybe we can help a little bit more. :)
