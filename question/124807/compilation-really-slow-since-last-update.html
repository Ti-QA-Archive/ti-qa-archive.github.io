  • Okay, so I've found THE answer !
    Actually, if you put an accent in the options of the tiapp.xml file, it will be replaced by an odd group of characters, and these characters will be duplicated at EVERY compilation.
    I ended up with a tiapp.xml of 35Mo. Oops. I just cleaned it, removed all the accents, and it's fine, now.
    So, yeah, there /might/ be a bug somewhere :D
