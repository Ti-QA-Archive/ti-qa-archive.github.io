  • You can add items to the Android's calendar, but not iOS'. Fortunately, there is a 3rd party module you can use to gain access.

    Here is the Android calendar;
    http://developer.appcelerator.com/apidoc/mobile/1.7.1/Titanium.Android.Calendar-module

    Here is the module on GitHub;

    https://github.com/stelford/Titanium-Calendar
