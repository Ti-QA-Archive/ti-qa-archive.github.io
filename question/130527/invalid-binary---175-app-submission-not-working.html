    I've had that problem in two different scenarios;

    1. I had added a entitlement to my distribution profile that wasnt being used.

    2. There was a problem with armv6/armv7 settings being built into the app for distribution (circa v 1.7.x) due to the change in minimum IOS version support. I had to manually patch the XCode project to get it to submit.

    Hope that helps…
