showContacts - contact details doesn't show on Android

Hello! I'm displaying the contact list using the following:

function showContacts(e) {
Titanium.Contacts.showContacts({
selectedProperty: function(e){
Ti.API.info("Selected phone #: " + e.value);
Ti.API.info("Type of phone #: "+ e.label);
}
});

}

It shows all contacts but when i click a contact to go to the details nothing happens. This doesn't happen on ios… Does anyone know why this happens on Android?
