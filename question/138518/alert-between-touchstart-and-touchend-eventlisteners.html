Alert between Touchstart and Touchend Eventlisteners

I have a button and I am trying to change the color of button on touchstart and touchend events. It is working fine but when an alert pops up due to some validations then the color of the button remains what it was in touchstart. Can anybody tell me why its behaving like this or what I need to do? Thanks!

  • I suspect that the alert dialog is causing a touchcancel event to be fired. You'll need a listener for that event as well to reset the color.
