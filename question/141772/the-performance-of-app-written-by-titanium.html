  • Accepted Answer

    Assuming that you followed best-practices for each programming language & API, You'll find that apps will perform in this order:

    • Native ObjC or Java: Of course a truly native app will perform the best.
    • Titanium apps: Titanium apps are also native apps, but you will find there is a small performance penalty when you use any framework, even Titanium
    • HTML5 apps (I assume you mean an app using something like PhoneGap): I personally feel that HTML apps are handicapped on iOS. Apple doesn't let apps have access to the same high-performance Javascript engine that Safari has access to. HTML apps on anything earlier than Android 4.1 also, in my opinion, tend to feel sluggish. I believe that between 4.1 "Project Butter" and Chrome, it's gotten better.
    — answered September 2nd 2012 by Shannon Hicks
