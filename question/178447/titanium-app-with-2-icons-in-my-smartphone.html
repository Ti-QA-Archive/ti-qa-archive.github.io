Titanium App with 2 icons in my smartphone

Hi, i'm finish my app and i wish install in my android smartphone. Is a LG E460. I'm working with titanium and android sdk 3.3.0 GA. When i run my app in my android device, Titanium install 2 icons in my smartphone. Then i see in Settings/Apps and there are only one app. I uninstall the app and then the 2 icons disappear. But now reinstall and i have 2 icons again.
What happens?

    <manifest>

    </manifest>

    • Ok, I think you need to remove:

      ...
android:label="@string/app_name"
android:name=".MyappActivity"
...

      Is your app called Myapp or something else? You may need to change this as follows:

      if your app is called "My App" then it becomes ".MyAppActivity"

      if your app is called "MyApp" then it becomes ".MyappActivity"

      Or remove it as Titanium will put it in for you.

      Take a look at the AndroidManifest.xml file in the build\android folder - see what is generated there (and post it here if the above doesn't work)
