    • As I haven't tested this myself, I'm not really sure if the theme definition is correct. I'd say the theme needs to extend a parent Theme like:

      <style name="Theme.NoTitle" parent="@style/Theme.Titanium">
  <item name="android:windowNoTitle">true</item>
  <item name="android:windowActionBar">false</item>
</style>

      and then in tiapp.xml:

      <application android:theme="@style/Theme.NoTitle "/>

      But be aware that - because of windowActionBar:false - the actionBar will not be available anymore (that means win.activity.actionBar.hide(); would make the app crash, theoretically)
