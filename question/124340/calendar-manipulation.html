    • Sadly, there is no iOS equivalent yet in Titanium. It is one thing that I really wish they would work on being as it has been supported since iOS 4 and it's one of our customers most requested features. You can find 3rd party Titanium modules for iOS that support it, if you search these Q&A forums.

      Personally, we reverted to sending an email with an iCal attachment which gets added to the device calendar fairly easily by the user. Though, it looks like Apple has had a bug iOS for awhile that prevents our users from removing our invitations from their calendar once accepted.

      So I am holding out and hoping for iOS calendar support. Even the new Android support leaves a lot to be desired as you cannot delete or edit events you create.
