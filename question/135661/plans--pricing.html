    I really feel as though they have me by the b@lls at the minute and that I should be looking at alternative ways to develop apps - even if it means a whole lot of wasted time learning the API etc.

    The UK sales manager was pretty apologetic about the options available to us Sole Traders and did say that they would be reviewing the options soon, but right now its £xpen$ive and Appcelerator don't seem to care about us small fish - which I feel is huge mistake.
