  • The release notes are drawn from the JIRA bug fixes that have been applied to the release. If you want a deeper understanding of what the changes are you an review the commits for the JIRA. The QA and regression testing on the releases has improved hugely in the last 6 months so I would always say in the case of minor upgrades (version revision changes) just upgrade, but for a major release (like 3.0) then its usually prudent to wait a short while before doing so and monitor the Q&A for any issues people report.
