Why doesn't my app support tablets (specifically Nexus 7)?

Hi, I am wondering if anyone can tell me what I need to do to support tablets and specifically my nexus 7. On local develolpment when installing to my 7 everything looks great. But when I put it up on the Play Store my Nexus 7 is grayed out.
http://imgur.com/Th17A

I dont have any custom manifest and I dont have any specific settings in my tiapp.xml to limit screen sizes. Any idea what I am doing wrong?

part of Tiapp.xml

    <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
        <tool-api-level>8</tool-api-level>
        <manifest android:installLocation="auto" android:versionCode="1" android:versionName="7.16.2012"/>
    </android>

  • <uses-permission android:name="android.permission.CAMERA" />

    This is the problem.

    Create a custom androidManifest or in the appropriate section of your tiapp.xml place:

    <uses-feature android:name="android.hardware.camera" android:required="false"/>
