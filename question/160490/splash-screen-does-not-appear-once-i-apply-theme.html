  • Accepted Answer

    1. create a new style for your custom theme:

      <style name="Theme.Action.Start" parent="Theme.Action">
 <item name="android:windowActionBar">false</item>
 <item name="android:windowNoTitle">true</item>
 <item name="android:windowBackground">@drawable/background</item>
</style>

    2. apply it to your launcher activity:
      ~~~
      <activity android:configChanges="keyboardHidden|orientation|screenSize" 

         android:label="OneView" 
   android:name=".OneviewActivity" 
   android:theme="@style/Theme.Action.Start">

      <intent-filter>

      <action android:name="android.intent.action.MAIN"/>
<category android:name="android.intent.category.LAUNCHER"/>

      </intent-filter>

    </activity>
    ~~~

      2.
