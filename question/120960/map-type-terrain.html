    It's actually not available at all. Apple/Google have yet to include it in the 'MapKit' which the iPhone iOS SDK uses.

    I note that recent SDKs for the iPad do include the TERRAIN type.

    Once Apple have it in their SDKs, it will be very easy to add it for Titanium support.
