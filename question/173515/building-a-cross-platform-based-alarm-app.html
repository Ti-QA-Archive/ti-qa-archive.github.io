    I don't think it is the best idea to use background services as it may time out as you already discovered. Instead triggering a notification is a more solid and battery-saving solution. You have already stumbled upon benCoding.AlarmManager!? Use it! It's great.

    I also recommend you this great tutorial as it explains very well how to implement alarms in both, iOS and Android:

    Building a Ti Mobile App: GrillTime
