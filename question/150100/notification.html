    For iOS take a look here.
    For Android take a look here.

    Here is a quick example of iOS

    var date = new date().getTime+60000;
var notification = Ti.App.iOS.scheduleLocalNotification({
    alertBody:"This is a scheduled alert",
    alertAction:"This a command you can send to your app to do something specific",
    sound:"alert3.mp3",
    date:date,
    repeat:"weekly"
});

    This will notify the user in 60 seconds.
