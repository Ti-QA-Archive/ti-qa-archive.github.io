Error when trying to publish Android app in Studio

Hi folks,

I've built my first Android app using Titanium Studio, after 10+ iOS apps, and having a few issues with the final build to submit to Google Play.

I submitted an APK last week without a problem, but had to redo the key signing process as I'd not put enough years in the valid part.
