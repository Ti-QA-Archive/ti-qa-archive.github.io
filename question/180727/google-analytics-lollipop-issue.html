    1. ti.googleanalytics is a free 3rd party module and you should post your problem/compliments/complaints on the pertinent issue tracker.
    2. ti.googleanalytics is the older/legacy version, you should be updating to ti.ga instead for other reasons
    3. isGooglePlayServicesAvailable() is available in the ti.map module, you could hack out the support, add it to ti.ga if its not there, and well, submit a PR as a thank you to Ben for his hard work and contributions to the community.
