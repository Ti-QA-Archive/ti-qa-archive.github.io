  • I'm not sure the point in putting a label inside of another label. If you've got some dynamic text that you want to change, you can just change the text value of the label and update it at any time. If you've got just a portion of text you want to change and you want to leave the rest of the label untouched, just put 2 labels side by side and just update the text in the one you want to change.

    mylabel.text = "New Text";
