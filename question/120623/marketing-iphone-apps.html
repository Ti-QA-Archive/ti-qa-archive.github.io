Marketing iPhone Apps

I've released one app the App Store and although I didn't expect my first app to be extremely popular, I also didn't expect it to get lost in the App Store as quickly as it did.

I'm working on another app now that I'm hoping will be much more popular, but want to establish a marketing plan beforehand to avoid this one being lost also.

Anyone using Facebook ads or Google Ads to market apps? What types of marketing efforts have worked for you?

  • Facebook ads arent the greatest…the best is to have a website, a blog, a twitter, a 'share with friends' button in your app that people can email links to, etc.

    Also, having an appropriate name for your app works. My initial build of my iPad app was called PlotMe, but unless people were searching for that, they rarely got it. I renamed it to Travel Map for iPad, and now when you're searching, you get so far as "travel m" and it shows up as the #1 search result, the #1 iPad app for that criteria, and its almost all of Google's page 1 search results.
