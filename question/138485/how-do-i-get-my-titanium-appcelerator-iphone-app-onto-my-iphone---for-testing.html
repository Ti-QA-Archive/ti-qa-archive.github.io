    Hi,
    Select the project in Titanium and under run configuration, choose iOS device. when you do that it will ask for you a valid certificate.
    make sure you have a valid certificated you created for your app from your apple account.
    If not you can use the default certificated from Xcode. you can find that certificate in xcode, i.e. launch xcode then from top menu choose window–> organizer then look under provisioning profile.

    select the required certificate, revel that certificate in finder.
    Once you know where that certificate is in the disk, then just locate that certificate to the titanium window asking for certificate.. press finish.

    you will be running your app in iphone now..:)
