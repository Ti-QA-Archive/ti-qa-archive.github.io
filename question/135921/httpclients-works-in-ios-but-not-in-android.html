HttpClients works in iOS but not in Android

My call with HttpClient works finde in iOS and I also get my values back from the server, on Android: nothing happens. I don't know how to debug for Android. Titanium.API.debug(codetodebug); works fine on iOS but its not visible when the Android emulator is running.

Any idea if httpclient expects something special for Android/different settings than IOS?
