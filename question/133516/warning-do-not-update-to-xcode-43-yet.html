Warning: Do not update to Xcode 4.3 yet!

I know that this isn't a question, but this may save people hours of time rolling back to Xcode 4.21.

Titanium Studio does not yet support Xcode 4.3. Xcode has been moved from the developer folder to the applications folder.

I know I've jumped the gun, but an email heads up would have been nice.
"Hi TiStudio users 4.3 is out, but give it a couple of weeks".

If you've already updated Xcode 4.21 is still available here

Let the uninstalling, downloading, reinstalling, back to my workflow, begin.
