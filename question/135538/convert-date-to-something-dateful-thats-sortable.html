Convert Date to something "Dateful" that's sortable.

Hi,

I have a date field in the form of the following string: 

Tue, 17 Apr 2012 09:29:16 +0000

Although it looks good, its obviously impossible to sort by this date format.

Does anyone know how I can convert this date into a date that can be sorted?

A sort function will sort this date by days of the week, it will then sort it by the date of the month, and then by the month… so for example, the 19 of March (Mar) will appear before the 17 Apr.

Actually, (for your own information), this date came from an RSS field - which displays dates in this type. Maybe there is away I can change it from the source?

I would truly appreciate any assistance here.

Thanks

  • var ms = new Date('Tue, 17 Apr 2012 09:29:16 +0000').getTime()

    ms will be 1334654956000 which is sortable now.
