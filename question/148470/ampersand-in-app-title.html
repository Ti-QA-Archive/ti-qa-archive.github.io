Ampersand in app title

I am developing an app for our product "Call&Report", and I need to get the ampersand (&) character into the app (display) name. Titanium does not allow me to simply write the ampersand in the name tag of tiapp.xml, nor does it work when I try with <name>Call&amp;Report</name>. When I google the problem, I mostly find references to internationalization of the app name, e.g. this Appcelerator blog post.

Although that is more than I needed, I tried to follow the instructions there, creating identical xml files for each of the languages we use. For iPhone I get this to work by creating app.xml files in the i18n subdirectories containing 

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<resources>
    <string name="appname">Call&Report</string>
</resources>

but the corresponding approach for Android does not work. In this case you create strings.xml files put in e.g. platform/android/res/values-en and also modify a copy of the AndroidManifest.xml file. To get this to compile, I must put <string name="app_name">Call&amp;Report</string> in the Strings.xml files, but the Android device still shows the name from tiapp.xml.

Any feedback for how to solve this would be much appreciated. Of course, I'd prefer a solution which does not involve creating a lot of indentical files for each language supported by the app (which will be at least 4 in the first version).

Using Titanium SDK 3.0.0.GA on OSX 10.8.2. targetSDKVersion for Android is 14, minSDKVersion is 8.
