Jumping with many pages

I am trying to build a multi window app. From what I have read, this is how you do it.

index.xml

<Alloy>
    <Require src="page1"/>
    <Require src="page2"/>
</Alloy>

page1.xml

<Alloy>
    <Window id="window1">
        <Label id="clickLabel">Go to window 2</Label>
    </Window>
</Alloy>

page2.xml

<Alloy>
    <Window id="window2">
        <Label>You're on page 2</Label>
    </Window>
</Alloy>

page1.js

$.clickLabel.addEventListener('click', function(){
    $.window1.close();
    $.window2.open();
});
$.window1.open();

My window2 won't open after clicking the label. Am I missing examples, or can someone help me?

      Not sure who you are 'reading' but they will never work for me.
