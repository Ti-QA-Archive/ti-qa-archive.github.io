    Working With Media and the File System

    Enhance the TiBountyHunter app by adding the camera function and mechanisms to support taking a picture, saving it, and retrieving it for viewing.

    Integrating Contacts & Social APIs

    Enhance the TiBountyHunter app by accessing contact information and Facebook for status and contact updates.

    Integrating Web Content

    Use a WebView to access an external RSS feed and save to local storage.

    Deploy an app to a physical device for testing and publish the app to the Apple App Store and Android Market.
