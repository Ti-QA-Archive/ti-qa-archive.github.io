Specify the minimum of Android SDK

Hi,

Is there anyway to specify the minimum version of android sdk? In my project, we just want to support from 2.2 and up.

Thanks!

  • In the tiapp.xml you can add the manifest node similar to the below.

    <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
        <tool-api-level>8</tool-api-level>
        <manifest>          
            <uses-sdk android:minSdkVersion="4" 
android:targetSdkVersion="8"/>    
        </manifest>
    </android>
