  • The Android lib of Smaato is a little flaky but there's a beta version of the Ti lib available at http://CrucialDivide.com/mobilesdk-dasher-ads-1.4-linux.zip which is current for the mainline Ti source as of today.

    It's built for Linux and supports Android.

    You'll need to extract & use it as you do for manual installs of the Ti SDK and you can find instructions at the top of Jeff's post here .

    It'll appear as dasher-ads-1.4 sdk in the Ti IDE. An example of it's use with Smaato can be found: http://gist.github.com/457877 and for AdMob: http://gist.github.com/439986

    There's still some work on it but the final version should be available shortly. I'm just waiting back on a bug report with Smaato.

    You can follow the source for this feature here:
    http://github.com/dasher/titanium_mobile/commits/integration-ads
