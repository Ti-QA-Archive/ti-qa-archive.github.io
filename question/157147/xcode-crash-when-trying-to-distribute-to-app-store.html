Xcode Crash when trying to distribute to app store

I am on xcode 4.6.3 and my team member is on xcode 4.6,

we are having problems when we try and distribute the archive to the app store via xcode organiser (initiated by the 'publish'/'distribute to app store' menu in titanium)…if we hit 'validate' nothing happens, if we hit 'distribute' xcode just crashes and quits.

we have been able to publish to the appstore successfully in the past so are familiar with how it is supposed to work. Recently our ios certificates expired and we had to renew. All new certificates and provisioning profiles have been created. They all show up as valid in xcode with green ticks next to them, everything seems to be perfect on paper. Its just that nothing has worked since renewing. We are not getting any valuable error messages back from xcode.

any ideas?, we are going crazy here, nothing seems to work :(

  • We solved this issue.

    After stuffing around with certificates for days we resolved this by stripping things right back to essentials.

    1. compiled basic hello world in pure xcode and it distributed
    2. compiled basic hello world in titanium and it distributed

    so we could rule out the certificates, xcode and titanium, it must have been a problem with the project.

    solution:
    it turned out that there was an extra Info.plist file in our /resources directory that was out of date and overwriting the one in the /build folder, once we deleted it everything was sweet again.
