  • Accepted Answer

    The default background color for pullView is "lightGrayColor".

    For advanced developers: you can replace in TiUIListView.m (line 641):

    _pullViewWrapper.backgroundColor = [UIColor lightGrayColor];

    with

    _pullViewWrapper.backgroundColor = [UIColor clearColor];

    That should give you a transparent pullView background.
