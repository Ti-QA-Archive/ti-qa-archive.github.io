Changes for Apple Maps in iOS6?

Just getting started with Titanium. I have a small iOS/Android application that is using Google Maps for displaying stuff on the map for a few customers. Now it looks like Google Maps will be banned from iOS 6. So I have to make an invest in upgrading to iOS 6 with it's own maps implementation. My thought is to switch over to Titanium to make the multi platform development a bit easier and stick with Google Maps.

But what is the impact of Apple Maps for Titanium? May Google Maps still be used on iOS6 with Titanium even if they are not available from Apple anymore?

Maybe someone can tell me about the future direction of Titanium for applications that rely on map interaction.

Regards
