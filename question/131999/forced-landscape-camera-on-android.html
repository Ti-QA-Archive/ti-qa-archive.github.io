Forced Landscape Camera on Android

Is it normal that an Android phone forces pictures only be taken in Landscape? My personal phone doesn't do this, but the 2nd and 3rd device I tested on seem to only take pictures in landscape made.

  • This behavior is actually part of the native Android behavior for the camera on certain devices. Titanium uses the system intent for camera, this is out of control. A full camera overhaul to implement a custom camera solution is required to support this.
