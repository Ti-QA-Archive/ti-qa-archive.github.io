Why is my window's navigation bar resizing on orientation change?

I have a navigation window with a root window. Everything looks fine when the app loads and works as expected. But, I am running into an issue where the navbar height permanently shrinks when the orientation changes. It seems that the navbar height decreases by about 15px. As far as I know of, there isn't even a property to set the navbar height, so I'm really not sure why this is happening. Does anyone know what might be causing this? It is occurring on iOS 7.

  • Hi

    I think you will find this is by design and has done this since iOS was launched. This is something iOS does and not something that Titanium has any control over.

    Start the "Messages" or "Camera" apps and rotate the device. you will experience the exact same behaviour with those apps and they were created by Apple.

    The reason it shrinks is so there is as much content area left when the device height has reduced due to the landscape orientation.

    If having seen those two apps you feel your example is still different - please provide a screen short of both orientations of your app with a clearly visible navigation bar.
