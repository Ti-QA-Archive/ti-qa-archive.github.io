App freezes after showing splash

I 've developed now for several weeks on my app, builded it, distributed and tested it on the device (iphone 3G) everything worked so far.
The last time I've encoutered some crashes but I could always restart the app.
One sudden time one crash was to much I think. The app will not start anymore, it's just the splash and nothing else. I've deleted, reinstalled, did a clean rebuild (also with 1.5RC). I also can not find anything in the device's logs. (There aren't any but the crashreports of the previous days)

How can I figure it out? Does someone has som tricks to debug?

  • In the app.js (and on the other js files) start putting an alert(something) after each block of instructions (create a window, add a label, etc).

    It's painful (very) but at some point will stop alerting you so you'll know where the issue is.

    I suggest to move the alert and not repeat it as it might crash because of too many alerts.
