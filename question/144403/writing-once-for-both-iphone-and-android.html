  • In 90% of cases, you should only create one App unless they are really different on iOS and Android. The key is how much is 'shared' between the platforms in your App.

    Generally, my 'model' and 'controller' code is 95% the same for both platforms. I'll have the occasion switch statement for android or iOS.

    For the UI, some Apps I create have their own separate 'views' for Android and iOS. Some have shared views and just have switch statements for certain things.

    Kevin Whinnery explains it better than I could at last years CodeStrong: http://vimeopro.com/appcelerator/codestrong-2011/video/29754525
