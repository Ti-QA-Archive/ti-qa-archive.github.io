if gps is turned off, can I turn it on?

if the user has his GPS turned off, how can I prompt the user to turn it on on iPhone and Android? I mean, can I open the settings, so he can immediately turn it on? I understand that I can not turn it on using my own code(is this true on iOS?)

  • That is correct for iOS – you cannot turn it on yourself except using private APIs on a jail-broken device.

    Prior to iOS 5.1, there was a trick to directly open a specific page in Settings by using the Ti.Platform.openURL() method with one of the URL schemes listed here.

    Alas, Apple in their infinite wisdom decided us mere mortals can't be trusted with that much power and took away that ability in iOS 5.1 as talked about here.
