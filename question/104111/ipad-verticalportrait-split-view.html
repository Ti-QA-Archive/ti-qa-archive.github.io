iPad vertical/portrait split view

in the kitchen sink example, turning the iPad to portrait removes the left view and places it in the pop-up (triggered by the "Show List" button), much like the default mail app.

My question is, how do you keep the split view in portrait? What I'm trying to accomplish is something similar to Twitter for iPad. When you turn the iPad to portrait, the twitter app keeps the split view in place, rather than going to single view and a pop-up. I looked at the splitview docs for possibly a property to set this, but I didn't see one.
