    Hi

    The Titanium Compatibility Matrix - is the best place to find out the needs of the platforms you are working with.

    Keep in mind the deployment requirements are;

    • Deploying for iOS 5.1 requires Xcode 4.3.
    • Deploying for iOS 6.0 requires Xcode 4.5.

    Xcode 4.5 minimum OSX version is OS X 10.7.4 or later according to the specifications.

    For my own development I use a mix of OSX 10.7 OSX 10.8 on Mac Minis, this allows the widest mix of target platforms in my experience.
