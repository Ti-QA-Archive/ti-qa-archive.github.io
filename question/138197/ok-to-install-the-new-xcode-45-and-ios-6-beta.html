  • I do think that talking about getting the new Xcode to work falls outside of the NDA, so I'm sharing my solution. I needed to install Xcode 4.5, because I also needed to test Mountain Lion.

    What in the end solved my problem was renaming Xcode45- to Xcode and running

    sudo xcode-select -switch &#x2F;Applications&#x2F;Xcode.app&#x2F;Contents&#x2F;Developer&#x2F;

    The last part may or may not be necessary.
