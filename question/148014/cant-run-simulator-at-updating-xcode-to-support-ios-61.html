  • actually there's a better way that downgrade your xcode… I have the same issue so I tried to figure it out by myself and I find a way…

    open your Xcode - preferences - downloads - iOS 5.1 Simulator

    download this simulator… when this is finished you close your titanium and re-open.
    with your project opened you click in the arrow of build button and go to "Run Configurations…".

    There you will change your iOS SDK to 6.1 that is your xcode sdk… and in simulator you change to 5.1.

    Apply and Run… it should work… works for me so good luck :D
