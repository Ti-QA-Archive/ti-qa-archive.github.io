  • Lucas,

    no matter which option you take, choose JSON as the way to communicate with your mobile app. This way is better because is easier to parse/process, it's smaller (so you don't get a hit on the amount of data to transfer) and it uses less processor poewer in general.

    In addition to that, be careful with the service, try to split as much info as possible (if you are getting records from a db, just get 10 records, 20). In the past I have seen services that will return ALL the results, which is good when you are just having a few dozens. But if you could get thousands records in the future, you should consider that into your webservice.

    For now, I think you can go to a PHP/JSON kind of service. If you need to scale, consider using the cloud, since it makes sense in terms of scale and money.

    Mauro
