Jittery Tables. Can anything be done or are we stuck?

Hi Folks. I have done all the usual recommendations on the forum and in the guides but still stuck with jittery table rows. So quite simply got only 20 table rows each with a view and a couple of labels with a simple light weight graphic but still get jittery scrolling on first flick through. Once you have scrolled through once its as smooth as anything until app is reloaded.

Is this just something we have to put up with in Titanium or has someone come up with the golden solution?

Would appreciate your thoughts or if anyone has got any undocumented tips. Tried all the usual classname and combining everything into a view then adding the view to the row but nothing really helps.

Many thanks in advance.

  • Hi

    Please supply the information requested above if the following does not reveal the issue.

    A common issue for "jittery" tables on iOS at least - has come down to rounded corner effect on a child of the custom tableViewRow.

    Check for borderRadius on any view children on the tableViewRow, remove the property for testing purpose and see if there any improvements. I have this on an iPad app I am working on right now, and removing the borderRadius property fixed the problem.

  • Hi All. Thanks so much for your all your help. On the back of all your pointers I have had a good look at my code and noticed that my images were not quite the correct size(1 pixel too large). I took out the image from the table rows as a test and my rows were smooth. So I resized my images to 48px square (24 width, height) and added the image views back and it was pretty much 100%.

    So rule of thumb here for others is make sure any images you use are correctly sized before using them in your rows. File size is also important.

    The pointers by everyone here were good. Thank you.
