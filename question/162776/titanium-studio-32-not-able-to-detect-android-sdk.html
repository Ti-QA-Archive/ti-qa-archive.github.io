    • The following step is working for me, please let me know if it works for you too!

      In our MAC, search for ManageSoft folder. Give Read-Write permission to all the subfolders inside it. Restart Titanium Studio and update Android SDK and its path (Titanium Studio->Preferences->Studio->Platforms->Android).

      Best of Luck :)
