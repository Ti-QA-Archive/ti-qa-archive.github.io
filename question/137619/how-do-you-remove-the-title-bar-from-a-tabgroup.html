How do you remove the title bar from a tabgroup?

I currently have two tabs in a tabgroup, and now I would like to remove the title bar from this tabgroup so I have more screen real-estate to play with.

How do I achieve this?

  • I think you mean the navBar of window. In this case you can use window.navBarHidden = true;
    window is the name of your window.

    i.E:

    var win = Ti.UI.createWindow({
    navBarHidden: true
});
tab.add(win);
tabgroup.add(tab);
