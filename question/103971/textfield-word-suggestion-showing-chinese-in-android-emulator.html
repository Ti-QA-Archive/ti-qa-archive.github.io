TextField word suggestion showing chinese in Android emulator

Hi. I'm developing an Android app that has several textfields in a form. When I run it in the emulator and type text into a field, an english keyboard shows but then a word suggestion bar appears above it showing chinese characters.
Can anyone tell me how to turn this off or at least force the language to be english?

  • Hi, you must do this:

    • when emulator starts, go to home, go to settings
    • open language & keyboard option
    • turn off - Japanese IME
    • turn off option which is represented by the chinese characters
