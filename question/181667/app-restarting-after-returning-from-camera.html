App restarting after returning from camera

I have a camera button on my app that is used to take pictures and save those pictures in a custom gallery inside the app. After taking some pictures and save them, the app restarts when it returns from camera.
First, I thought that this could be Android killing my app due to low memory, because I was testing on a Galaxy Ace 2. But then this error starts to happen on some newer devices.
Does anyone have a clue about what could be happening?

  • I a somewhat similar problem. I'm showing 10 picture icons plus the current but larger. Had a optionDialog on Alloy that I reused for several onClick text boxes. If I first clicked on a text box and after on a picture it would work like a charm. But if I took a picture first and after clicked on a text box the app would crash. By creating new optionDialog controls in code the problem went away. I suspect the problem was caused by memory rearrangement by Android as it expanded the heap. Could your problem be similar?
