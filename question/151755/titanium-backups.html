  • If you are talking about the source files, the no you won't be able to retrieve the source code files from the Appcelerator site. This list represents the applications you created and their unique identifier that can be used for analytics or even cloud access.

    But other than that, they are not keeping anything else.

    Your only option in this particular case is to dig through your old personal backups in order to retrieve what you need.

    Good luck.
