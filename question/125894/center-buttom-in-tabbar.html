  • Accepted Answer

    Create a tab group with all its tabs. Leave the middle tab in blank. Add styled button to the tab group, positioned over the blank tab, and capture its events. When the button is clicked, selected the middle tab. Something like this

    tabGroup.add(btnTab);

btnTab.addEventListener('click', function(){

    tabGroup.activeTab = middleTab; //the tab behing the button

})
