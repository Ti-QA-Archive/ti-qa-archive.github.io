  • A Mac is required for running the Apple SDKs, however, you can use a service called MacInCloud to basically use a Mac in the cloud. It comes pre-installed with Titanium Studio, so you just need to fire it up and start writing your apps.

  • You can use the Foundry22 service. Basically, you upload your code to their service, they do the build, and issue an over the air update (like a TestFlightApp build) which you could install on your iDevice. You'll still need an Apple developer account.
