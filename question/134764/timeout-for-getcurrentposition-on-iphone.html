Timeout for getCurrentPosition on iPhone

Is there anyway to implement a timeout for Titanium.Geolocation.getCurrentPosition? While I understand it is an asynchronous function, if I currently have a good GPS current signal, the callback is quick to return. However, with a poor signal, it takes at times over 15 seconds for the callback to execute. I have no problems at all on Android, although I understand this is because the radios are not activated. Any help or other suggestions would be greatly appreciated. Thanks.
