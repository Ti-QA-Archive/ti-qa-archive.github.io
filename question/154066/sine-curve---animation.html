  • I'm going to assume you are referring to iOS since you haven't mentioned a platform. The latest Ti build utilizes UIViewAnimationCurve props for animation curves. The ones Ti exposes are the ones available on iOS. For using a sine curve, as a bezier curve, it needs to utilize CALayer animations. Therefore, doing this might be unpractical without a custom implementation.
