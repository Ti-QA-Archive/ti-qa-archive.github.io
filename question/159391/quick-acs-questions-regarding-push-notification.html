Quick ACS questions regarding Push Notification

I am using ACS for the first time in an app, just for the purpose of delivering push notifications.
Everything seems to be working in my development environment. However, I just want to be certain that I only need to login to ACS when I wish to initially subscribe my device to a push notification channel. Subsequently, when the app launches, I do not need to login and I will still receive push notifications. This appears to be what is happening with my testing, but I'd just like a little confirmation before I send my binary off to Apple. Thanks!
