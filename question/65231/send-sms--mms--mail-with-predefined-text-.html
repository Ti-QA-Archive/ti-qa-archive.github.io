  • Accepted Answer

    As of now, it is not possible.

    A way to get around this, however, is to make a text area and tell the user to copy the text, click on a button opening an empty sms (Ti.Platform.openURL("sms:_");), and then pasting.

    That's, as far as I'm concerned, the only way to do such thing.
