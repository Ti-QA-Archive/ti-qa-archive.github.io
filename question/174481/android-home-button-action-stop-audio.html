  • There is no event such as android:home.

    I also recommend you not to override the back-button. Seriously, don't do that!

    A much better solution to your issue is this:

    Get to know the Activity Lifecycle and use Androids pause and stop events:

    • pause
    • stop

    Add them two event-listeners to your Activity and pass the same callback (that one that would stop your player).

    • To be a little bit more clarifying:

      • your webview lives within a Ti.UI.Window
      • that window is attached to an Activity
      • that Activity gets finished by pressing the back button…
      • and paused (sent to background) by pressing the home button

      If you would have read the link I referred to, you would know that pressing the home button on Android fires a "pause" event on your current Activity, as well as pressing the back button fires "pause" and "stop"
