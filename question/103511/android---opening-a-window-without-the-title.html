Android - Opening a window without the title

Hi,

I have the following

                        var win = Titanium.UI.createWindow({
                            url:'call-active.js',
                            title: '',
                        });

                        Titanium.UI.currentTab.open(win,{animated:true});

And what I want is to have NO title bar. But if I leave out the title property it still has one just with a default of my project name.

Is there anyone to have no title bar when opening a new window…?

  • If you want your Android Application to start up with no title bar at all, just edit the tiapp.xml Line 24..

    <navbar-hidden>true</navbar-hidden>

    Clean your project and build. This works as of 1.8.0.1 SDK
