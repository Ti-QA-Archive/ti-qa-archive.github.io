  • I found a solution at the last SDK update:

    var ta1 = Titanium.UI.createTextArea({
    value:'I am a textarea',
    height:70,
    width:300,
    top:20,
    suppressReturn:false

    });

    The SECRET IS >>>>> suppressReturn:false
