iPhone 4 simulator (resolution) as default

Hello,

Can use the iPhone 4 resolution as default in my simulator ?

  • You may open the simulator app (xcode 4.3 path: applications/xcode.app/contents/developer/platforms/iphonesimulator.platform/developer/applications/ios simulator.app) and then go to Hardware -> Device and select iphone (retina).

    Next time you compile and open the simulator from Ti IDE it will open with iphone (retina) resolution.
