    • Hi,

      I know this isn't the perfect answer for your query, but you can give a try and see if it helps you to solve your problem. 

      Ti.UI.backgroundColor = 'gray';
var win = Ti.UI.createWindow({
    statusBarStyle:Titanium.UI.iPhone.StatusBar.DEFAULT,
    top:20,
    backgroundColor:'#fff'
});

win.open();
