How to insert a Button under a ListView with Alloy?

How to insert a Button under a ListView with alloy? The following doesn't work

<Alloy>
  <Window title="Match details">
    <ListView id="list_matches_details">
    <View>
      <Button>My button</Button>
    </View>
  </Window>
</Alloy>

  • Your XML is invalid. You didn't close the <ListView> element:

    <Alloy>
  <Window title="Match details">
    <ListView id="list_matches_details"/>
    <View>
      <Button>My button</Button>
    </View>
  </Window>
</Alloy>

    This also assumes you have styles in place to organize the listview and buttons
