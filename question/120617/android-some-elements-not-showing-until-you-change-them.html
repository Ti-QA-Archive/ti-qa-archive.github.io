Android: Some elements not showing until you change them

This is the same problem I had last week. Then it was with tabs, now it is with regular windows. Some elements just "choose" not to show up. They are defined EXACTLY the same as other elements (in this case labels) and they show on iOS.

However, on Android they will not show up until you change them AFTER the window loads. On the tab one last week, I grabbed the event focus from the tabgroup and then changed something arbitrary and then it magically showed.

I just did a test and did a click event on some other element (that was showing) and changed the text of the label that was not showing… and BOOM then it shows right away.

It seems to me that maybe it's a drawing issue.. and changing the text makes it re-draw.

Anyone dealt with this?? Anything you can do about it but the hackish thing of listening for the window focus event and changing something arbitrary?
