Removing new lines from string

How can i remove all the new line from a string's tail. Suppose, somewhere i'm assigning the string as var str = 'abc' + '\n' + '\n'; and somewhere i want to cut/remove those new line. i tried below. but it works with iphone simulator but does not works in emulator, shows error. How can i solve it ? is there any specific way to do it ?

var s = str.replace(/(\r\n|\n|\r)/gm,"");

    Hi

    Your RegEx is simple enough, and will remove newlines from strings that contain them. It could be even simpler though, the brackets are not required for something like this;

    var s = str.replace(/(\r\n|\n|\r)/gm,"");
// can be
var s = str.replace(/\r\n|\n|\r/gm,"");

    I am assuming you are trying to remove the line breaks and not trying to remove actual lines of text.

    You say iPhone simulator and "emulator" - do you mean Android, as you do not specifically say Android?

    Can you provide a reproducible snippet of code that generates the error so we may try ourselves.
