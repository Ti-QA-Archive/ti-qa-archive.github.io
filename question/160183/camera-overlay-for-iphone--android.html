  • You can pass a Ti.UI.View to overlay

        Titanium.Media.showCamera({
        overlay: myCustomOverlayView,
        success: successHandler,
        error:errorHandler,
        allowEditing:true,
        mediaTypes: [Ti.Media.MEDIA_TYPE_PHOTO]
});
