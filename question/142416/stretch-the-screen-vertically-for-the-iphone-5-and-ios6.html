Stretch the screen vertically for the iphone 5 and ios6?

Has anyone figured out a way to stretch their app window to fill the new iphone 5's larger size?
I'm running xcode 4.5 with the iphone 5 sim, and trying to figure out how to stretch the window.

  • Accepted Answer

    Include a splash screen resource named "default-568h@2x.png" and that will trigger the app to be sized to the iPhone 5 screen.
