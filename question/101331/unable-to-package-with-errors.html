  • Hi, Tan. I had to suffer with that problem for more than 2 weeks when I first built my iphone app.
    I recently found out an answer with help of a great college.

    First go to build/iphone/build and open build.log
    If you go to the way bottom of the file, you will see the specific error message telling you what you messed up.

    For me it was my appicon.png and Default.png.
    If it says something about "cannot find appicon" use "Preview"(Default App for photo in mac) to convert it to png file. If you have any more question please feel free to ask me
