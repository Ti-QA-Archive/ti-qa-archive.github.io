    If you are using android, I think ticket #2361 may be an issue for you. Thus, you must currently use a heavyweight window, by setting the navBarHidden:false property, in order to have separate modal windows and hence titles per "screen".

    Also, see ticket #2786, although this is really a duplication of the above, imo.
