    • Hi Rehman

      Please don't answery your own questions; use the comment facility instead. :)

      About your issue, don't worry about the python or scons "not found" errors - these are only required if you wish to compile Titanium.

      Your system configuration looks pretty good, but there are a couple of things you need to correct:

      You have a duplicate of C:\Program Files\Common Files\Microsoft Shared\Windows Live in your PATH. It should not be before the Windows system paths, so remove the first one of these.

      You need to add the following to your system PATH, immediately after your path to JDK.

      • C:\path\to\android_sdk\tools
      • C:\path\to\android_sdk\platform-tools

      You have not configured your android_sdk environment variable, which should be set to the location of your android sdk (the directory that contains the tools directory).

      You have not configured your JAVA_HOME environment variable. On your system, it should be set to C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_24\

      Have you performed the workaround for the missing adb, as per the end of the section named, Preparing for Android development? You must do this.

      Again, please provide a screenshot of your android installed packages.

      Once you have taken this advice, create another pastebin of your diagnostics output, and I will advise further.

      Cheers
