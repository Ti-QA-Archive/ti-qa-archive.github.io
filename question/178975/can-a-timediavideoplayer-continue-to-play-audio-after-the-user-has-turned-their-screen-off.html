Can a Ti.Media.videoPlayer continue to play audio after the user has turned their screen off?

The native music player, and apps like spotify, continue to play audio even if you turn off your screen / go to the lock screen.

Is this possible with anything from Ti.Media.x ??

  • Your question title says "video", but the rest of your question talks about audio players. You can definitely create an audio player that will play in the background even if you change apps, go to the home screen, lock your phone, turn off the screen:

    Ti.Media.createAudioPlayer ({ allowBackground: true });

    I do not believe you can do this with the video player. It's a bit of an unusual use case that the Appcelerator team probably didn't spend a lot of time working on.

    Maybe there's a way you can deliver an audio-only stream for this use case?
