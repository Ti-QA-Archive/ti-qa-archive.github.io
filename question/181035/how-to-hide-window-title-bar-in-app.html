  • <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
    <manifest>
    <application android:theme="@style/Theme.AppCompat.Translucent.NoTitleBar.Fullscreen">
    </application>
    </manifest>
    </android>

    Add this to TiApp.xml
