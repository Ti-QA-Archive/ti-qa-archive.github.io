How can I access the ScrollView of a WebView on iOS 5?

Since iOS 5, Apple does not support JavaScript in WebViews with a PDF (see here). In place of, UIWebView’s UIScrollView is now exposed and with setContentOffset the contentOffset value could be changed.

In Titanium 1.8.1 this does not work because there is no webview.scrollView property.

Is there any other way to access the ScrollView?
