Recognise my Android SDK

Hi Everyone,

I have been pulling my hair out for the past two days trying to get my Titanium Studio to recognise my Android SDK. I'm on win7 32bit with a Titanium Studio, build: 3.0.1.201212181159 and have had android's sdk on my machine long before Titanium studio. I developed natively on Eclipse with no problems.

I have the latest Android SDK updates (spent most of yesterday updating), correctly configured my environmental path and can push code from Titanium Studio to my device.

However, Titanium Studio doesn't show android in the preferences menu / shortcuts toolbar / or recognise my device in DDMS. However, when I open DDMS on my eclipse, there is my device working perfectly, and adb also lists my device through the cmd line, just nothing in Titanium studio.

I've uninstalled Titanium Studio and reinstalled to no avail.

What am I missing? Do I really need to delete 'android-7' ( http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/121567/could-not-locate-the-android-sdk-at-a-given-path-in-preferences–android-sdk-directory ) or has that issue been fixed?

I tried this yesterday: http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/121567/could-not-locate-the-android-sdk-at-a-given-path-in-preferences–android-sdk-directory and for a brief period there, it WORKED, I could see Android in the preferences menu and my device was found in DDMS! Then I turned my computer back on this morning and android's disappeared again. Arrgghhh…

Please help.

Thanks,
Charles
