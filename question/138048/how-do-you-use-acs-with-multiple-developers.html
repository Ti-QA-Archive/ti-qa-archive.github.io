How do you use ACS with multiple developers?

Looking and excited to utilize ACS services for a mobile app, but stumped a bit at the idea that an ACS-enabled application "lives" under my developer account. How do we have multiple person development teams and eventual client owners interact and get assigned access? Specifically:

  1. A. How do I give my fellow developer team members access to the ACS management website for the new app? Seems essential to peer into the log, users, objects that we're creating in development/testing.

  2. B. Eventually we'd transfer to our "client's account" (which of course they don't yet have). I'm guessing we'd just "start" with a new account and new appkey, and clean DB at that point. Any way to migrate any existing data into that? Or change the "owner" of an existing application once it hits production? Or heck even while still in development, like if the developer-account-owner leaves the team for example?

  3. C. Are there any associated permissions that come into play, assuming more than one person can access the application management console in ACS?

Very curious to hear official and reality stances on this - I can't seem to find this topic being discussed, so pointers to anything would be much appreciated. thanks!
