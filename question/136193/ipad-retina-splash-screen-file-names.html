  • Solved! Turns out for iPad Retina the naming conventions are indeed Default-Landscape@2x.png and Default-Portrait@2x.png. No clue why it wasn't working the first time around… a clean build fixed it I think. No clue as to why just these two scenarios are missing from that otherwise excellent table… working good now!
