Offline Video in app

Hi, I am new to Titanium and building phone apps and I have been searching around but I cannot find a answer for my question.

I am trying to make an app for my new site and basically the main
content of the app would be video, also the app would be likely used
in places with no or very slow mobile internet. So I am trying to work
out, is it possible with Titanium to have the videos hosted offline on
the phone? i.e. when the app is downloaded, so is the video. Or do I
have to learn objective C and Java
Any help is much appreciated.
Thanks,
Matt

    The media play returned by using 

    Titanium.Media.createVideoPlayer

    has a url property that can be a web hosted file or a local filesystem based file
