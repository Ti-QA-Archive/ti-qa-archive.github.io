I am building an app for distribution but it failed on icon for ipad. Strange thing is that I didn't include iPad in my targets. There is no ipad reference in the TiApp.xml. I hae performed clean build but it produced same error.

    Couple of things:

    I wouldn't submit an app to the app store with 1.8.2. A number of submission specific issues were resolved in 1.9.x and 2.0.x.

    You can work around the icon problem by creating a 72x72 icon image, naming it appicon-72.png and dropping it in /Resources/iphone

    You also didn't mention your xCode version. There was a fix to the application loader from Apple that resolved icon issues.

  • You have a universal app, including an appicon72.png in the Info.plist (Project root) will not copy the Info.plist to your distribution build due to an error in the TiSDK. You will have to edit the Info.plist in the build dir and compile again with xcode.
