Titanium 2.0 update crashes studio

Recently I updated my titanium studio to 2.0 but found that the it crashes on startup.
While crashing it gives me the message that see the log for detail.

After analyzing the log i found that in the last I have this message

"Job found still running after platform shutdown. Jobs should be canceled by the plugin that scheduled them during shutdown: com.aptana.editor.common.CommonEditorPlugin$5".

what is this error and how to resolve it.

  • Accepted Answer

    You can resolve this problem by going into your Titanium workspace folder–> .metadata(hidden folder) –>.plugins –>org.eclipse.core.resources.

    delete everything from it. This will remove your projects from the studio. dont worry It will not remove it from your hard disk. When you launch your studio again there will be no folder in your project explorer pane. just import your projects from your Titanium workspace. and it will work again.

    hope that helps, :)
