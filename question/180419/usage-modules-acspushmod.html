  • Hi. Your Titanium App needs to be provisioned for cloud services. You do this at project-creation time. This adds your ACS keys to tiapp.xml. Then, go to my.appcelerator.com, find your app name and add one user as App Administrator. Then on your app replace the uid/pwd placeholders with the real values. Once you do this, run your app, and it'll be registered with ACS. Then from the ACS console you'll be able to see this device and send notifications to it.

    R
