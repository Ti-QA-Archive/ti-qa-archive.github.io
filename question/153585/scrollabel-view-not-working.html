  • You got lots of errors here:

    • You're not closing the <Window> element
    • If you put ImageViews in the XML, they become part of the view hierarchy
    • You can't assign the ScrollableView.views property in an XML attribute like that

    Follow the test app found here: https://github.com/appcelerator/alloy/tree/master/test/apps/ui/scrollableview
