    Hi

    This sentence "The buttons are not when I click and drag." appears to be missing useful words to help explain the property.

    Your colour problem can be resolved by pointing to the correct custom property, you are referencing string in the toggle event but the custom property stores an object.

    e.source.setColor(e.source.fontOn);
// becomes
e.source.setColor(e.source.fontOn.color);
