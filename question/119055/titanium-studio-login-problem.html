  • Hi All,

    We had a server misconfiguration issue that resulted in some users being unable to log in to Titanium Studio. That's been fixed, and we apologize for any issues that may have caused. It should be an extremely rare occurrence, so we hope you do not experience it again, and we're taking steps to prevent that possibility.

    We ask you to login to Titanium Studio because that's how we connect you to various cloud services, such as packaging. However, if there is an issue with these services, that should not prevent you from working with your projects at least locally. We're reviewing those procedures to see how best to fix the problem.
