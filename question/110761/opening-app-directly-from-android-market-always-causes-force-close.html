Opening app directly from Android Market always causes force close

Running from the homescreen works fine as expected. However, when running it straight from the Market, e.g. after it is just downloaded, it glitches. It'll either just hang, or say "An application restart is required," restart itself, and then hang or force close after or on the splash screen. I think this could be considered a pretty serious issue. Any idea what could be going on?

    What titanium SDK level are you using? The hang / force close issue was fixed in ticket 2649 and discussed in threads such as this and this.

    I think you may need to be using any of the 1.6.x continuous builds in order to pick up this fix. Note that even with this fix, you will experience the "An application restart is required" when you launch direct from the install notification instead of the home screen. But the device will not crash or hang.
