  • Accepted Answer

    Our sincerest apologies for this. The emails are authentic and the link is correct. However, the forward slashes are not being escaped correctly when the link redirects from Marketo, which we use to run our promotions, causing CSS not to be applied.

    If you can see a link to the exam in the resulting page, it is perfectly safe for you to proceed. If not, then please be assured that we are working with Marketo to resolve the problem.

    Please subscribe to this thread for updates.

    In the meantime, thank you for your patience.
