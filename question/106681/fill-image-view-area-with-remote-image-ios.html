Fill Image view area with Remote image (iOS)

Is there a way to fill an imageview area (in my case 75x75) with a remote image? I have a tableview of 50 rows. Each row has a thumbnail image on the left. The remote images load in great but they scale the longest side to 75px leaving the other side short not making it to the edge (not filling the 75x75 space with the image).

I tried putting it into a parent view but that didnt work either.

Anyone have any ideas how to make this happen?

  • imageAsThumbnail will convert a blob into a thumbnail of the specified blob

    var blob = imageView.toImage();
var blobThumb = blob.imageAsThumbnail(75);
