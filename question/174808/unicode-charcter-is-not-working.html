  • Not all unicode characters are supported by the Roboto (default Android) font. You could try this app http://www.madore.org/~david/programs/UnicodeMap.html to see which ones are supported. Or, you can use a custom font that does support the characters you want to use. There's a good article on creating a custom Icomoon font on the TiDev.io site you should check out.
