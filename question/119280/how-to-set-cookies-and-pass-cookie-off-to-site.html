  • Hi David,

    What immediately stands out as incorrect is that you're calling client.setRequestHeader after client.open. Try placing client.setRequestHeader just before client.open and see how that works. Hopefully, it'll stop your application from crashing.

    With your second issue, yes, you can use client.onreadystatechange or, alternatively, client.onload.

    In terms of opening the web page in Safari, I'm not sure what you're trying to accomplish, but why not just use a web view for the entire process (i.e. getting/setting the cookie and opening the web page)?

    Hope this helps!
