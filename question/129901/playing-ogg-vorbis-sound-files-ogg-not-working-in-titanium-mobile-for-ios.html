Playing ogg Vorbis sound files (.ogg) not working in Titanium mobile for iOS

I have never had problems playing .wav or .mp3 sound files with Titanium.Media.Sound.
However, I am not able to do the same with .ogg files:

var player = Ti.Media.createSound({
url:"sound.ogg"
});
player.play();

This code works perfectly with mp3 and wav, but it doesn´t with ogg files.
I have tried both with the simulator and a real iPad.
Any clue? Is .ogg not supported?
I am using Ti mobile and Ti studio under iOS (latest builds). I am buiding for iPad.

    Ogg Vorbis is not supported natively on the iPad:

    Audio Playback
Frequency response: 20Hz to 20,000Hz
Audio formats supported: HE-AAC (V1 and V2), AAC (8 to 320 Kbps), Protected AAC (from iTunes Store), MP3 (8 to 320 Kbps), MP3 VBR, Audible (formats 2, 3, and 4, Audible Enhanced Audio, AAX, and AAX+), Apple Lossless, AIFF, and WAV
User-configurable maximum volume limit
Dolby Digital 5.1 surround sound pass-through with Apple Digital AV Adapter (sold separately)

  • AAC (.m4a) gives similar compression rates (in terms of file size and quality) as compared to .ogg, and is also loopable (mp3 is not loopable), so there is not need for .ogg. Thanks.
