How to allow more than 4 Tabs in a Tabbar

Hey

I have 6 windows in my iphone app that are added to a tabgroup. Unfortunately the tab bar limit on the iPhone is set at 4 tabs, with anymore coming under the 'more' tab.

Is there anyway i can override or change this and allow 6 tabs within the tabgroup without a 'more option' automatically being created?

Thanks in advance

    I dont think its supported. However, it is fairly straightforward to simulate a tab group using a container window with a view to hold the tabs and then maintaining an array of windows that open/close within the fake tabgroup when the tab view is clicked.
