Hiding the Keyboard

Yeah, I know, there are a ton of these questions around. I've read them all. The thing is, search.blur() works great when it's included as part of the return event listener. Clears the keyboard, switches to the search window, and when I return no keyboard (yay). But…what about when I drop into the search bar intending to do a search, but then decide not to and want to click something else?

Is there a way to get rid of the keyboard in some other way? Is there a standard/best practice for when/how to do this? Is it possible/sufficient to just clear the key board on window.click or is there a more accepted solution?

Thanks for your input.
