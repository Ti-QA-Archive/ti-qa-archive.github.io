    In one of your comments, you mention the following in your log:

    [ERROR] :  2013-07-28 16:05:05.804 xcodebuild[44215:3c03] Unexpected internal inconcistency: No recorder for buildTask: <Xcode3BuildTask: 0x4021334e0:'CompileC TiDOMPIProxy.m':REfc(8029405157592662016):deps=1:phaseNum=8>

    Are you using Xcode 3?

    As detailed in the Compatibility Matrix; the minimum supported version for Xcode is 4.2..

    If that is the case, simply update your Xcode installation using the App Store.
