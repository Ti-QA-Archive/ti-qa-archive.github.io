How to change status bar style iOS 7 to white?

Now that the solid black status bar problem is solved in Titanium SDK beta 3.1.3 (refrence), does anyone know how to change the style of the status bar from black to white?

I tried to alter my Info.plist by adding 

<key>UIViewControllerBasedStatusBarAppearance</key>
<true/>

I also changed it to

<key>UIViewControllerBasedStatusBarAppearance</key>
<false/>

But no luck so far.
Changing UIStatusBarStyle to UIStatusBarStyleLightContent doesn't work either

before:

<key>UIStatusBarStyle</key>
<string>UIStatusBarStyleDefault</string>

after:

<key>UIStatusBarStyle</key>
<string>UIStatusBarStyleLightContent</string>

Has anyone succesfully changed their status bar style to white?

Thanks.

  • Accepted Answer

    add the following to your tiapp.xml if you want you entire app to have a light content style.

    <ios>
      <plist>
              <dict>
                  <key>UIStatusBarStyle</key>  
                           <string>UIStatusBarStyleLightContent</string>
              </dict>
      </plist>
</ios>

    Or if you want to control this property in a per window basis set the window property to statusBarStyle to Titanium.UI.iPhone.StatusBar.LIGHT_CONTENT (on iOS 7)
