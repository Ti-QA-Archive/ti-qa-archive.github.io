String Manipulation

Hi,

I have a string that contains the following
123.94
The numbers left of the decimal keep on changing, and the numbers right of the decimal keep on changing.

All I need is a routine that puts the numbers on the left hand side of the decimal in a variable, and puts the numbers on the right hand side of the decimal in another variable

So, LHS_value = 123, RHS_value = 94

Remember, that these numbers can be 1 digit to 3 digits long.

Do you have any ideas? Have you done anything like this before? Does this remind you of a problem you once had? If so, we'd love to hear from you.

Thanks,
Happy New Year!

  • var str = '123.45';

var left = str.split('.')[0];
var right = str.split('.')[1];
