App event back to foreground not firing (resume) on Android or iOS

Simple question. I'm trying to get an event fired when my app comes back to the foreground (resumed).
However I can't get it to work on both Android and iOS.

//The below does nothing, no alert or info is shown on either iOS or Android
$.webwin.addEventListener('resume',function(e)
{
    Ti.API.info("HELLO");
    alert("HELLO");
});

//The below gives an error, cannot call 'undefined'
Titanium.Android.Activity.addEventListener('resume', function(){
    alert("hello");
});

On Android what I do is I go back to the home, click on the "recent apps" button, click back into my titanium app, and nothing happens. No alert no nothing.

On iOS what I do is I go back to the home, double tap the home button to bring up recent apps, click on my titanium app, and nothing happens. No alert no nothing.

Is there a fix for this? I've seen other people struggle with this as well. I need a fix that works for both Android and iOS

  • Got it working on android:

        win.addEventListener("open", function(){
        if(this.activity){
            this.activity.onResume = function(){
                alert("test");
            };
        }
    });
