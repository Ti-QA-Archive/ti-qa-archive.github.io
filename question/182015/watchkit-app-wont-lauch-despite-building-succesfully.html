WatchKit app wont lauch despite building succesfully!

After linking it to my Titanium project, it builds succesfully without error.

  • Hi,

    I just came up with this issue also (but not using Titanium), and found the solution having nothing to do with the apple watch but is an issue with your certificate:

    Solution found from and also few posts on stackoverflow:

    https://www.airsignapp.com/ios-apps-using-swift-crash-when-signed-with-inhouse-certificate/

    Your provisioning profile is most likely from before Q4 of 2014 and is missing some crucial information for it to work with Swift.

    You need to revoke your certificate and request it again to solve the problem as the new one will contain the Organisational Unit.

    Best,

    Matthieu
