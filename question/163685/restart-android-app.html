    Hi Abdelrhman,

    there is no need for a module on iOS & Android to restart your app.

    add this line to tiapp.xml

    <property name="ti.android.bug2373.finishfalseroot" type="bool">false</property>

    and then just call this function: 

    Ti.App._restart();
