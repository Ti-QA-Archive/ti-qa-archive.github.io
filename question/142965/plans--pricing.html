  • Hi Filipe. I'm talking from my experience and from what I know from Appcelerator people (starting with employees and ending with Jeff, the CEO )

    1 . No fees, nada, zit, zero - you can develop apps for any purpose without paying a single cent to Appcelerator. I did it myself A LOT and I know A LOT of devs doing the same.

    2 . The explorer pack allows you to do whatever you want for free. If you don't need analytics or don't want to go with Appcelerator just set it to false in tiapp.xml - no logs will be done in this case so no danger to go over the limit.
    ACS - in your case you won't use it as you want to do your own backend. - so again no issues here

    Anyway, if you use ACS there are some additional packs you can find here
    http://www.appcelerator.com/plans-pricing - scroll down a little :)

    3 . Support - I don't know how it goes now, I'm a pro/enterprise user for too long and things changed a little but you always have QA and a lot of blogs around. The QA has a community that is pretty helpful as long as you don't ask for code or don't put "stupid" questions ( by stupid I mean things like : "my app doesn't work, help.").
    I can tell you that I rarely have to reach to the pro support, usually when it happens is for a bug, a feature request or if the things are way to advanced for me or other QA users.

  • As you mentioned, this question has been asked a lot by the community over time. So maybe a good approach would have been to do some searching in the Q&A. But I digress.

    ###Let's put plain and simple:

    • The is absolutely no fee to develop your application using Titanium SDK and Titanium Studio.
    • App explorer would be enough to build any application.
    • As for support, there is always the Q&A for community help.
      • If you require more immediate support and the community can't help you, you will have to subscribe to one of the paid plans provided by Appcelerator. I am not aware of any per incident support plans.
