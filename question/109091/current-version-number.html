  • Titanium Developer is on 1.1.2. Titanium Developer is the program you write the codes in and export them and all.

    Titanium's Mobile SDKs latest stable release was 1.5.1, which should be the latest one it asked you to download as you said. This one is the latest one which should give you the least problems. 1.5.1 fully supports iOS 4, as well as Android all the way up to 2.2 I believe (With 2.3 on it's way).

    I would suggest updating all of your SDKs to the latest, as not many people stay on the lower firmwares and developing for the lowers rather than the newer would just be… Inefficient :)
