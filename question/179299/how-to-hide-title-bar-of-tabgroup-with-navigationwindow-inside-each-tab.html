How to hide title bar of TabGroup with NavigationWindow inside each tab

Hi all,

I'm currently developing an app which has multiple layers of navigation. In this particular case it means that I have a TabGroup with an NavigationWindow linked to each tab. Inside the NavigationWindow is initially one Window.

Thus the structure of the view looks like this (a bit simplified):

<Alloy>
    <TabGroup id="tabGroup" activeTab="infoTab" navBarHidden="true">
        <Tab id="1">
            <NavigationWindow id="1">
                <Window>
                    ...
                </Window>
            </NavigationWindow>
        </Tab>
        <Tab id="2">
            <NavigationWindow id="2">
                <Window>
                    ...
                </Window>
            </NavigationWindow>
        </Tab>
        <Tab id="3">
            <NavigationWindow id="3">
                <Window>
                    ...
                </Window>
            </NavigationWindow>
        </Tab>
    </TabGroup>
</Alloy>

The problem is that I have 2 navigation bars or title bars. One belongs to the TabGroup, while the other belongs to the active NavigationWindow. I'd like to use the bar of the NavigationWindow and hide the one belonging to the TabGroup. But I ran out of ideas how to achieve this…

I tried setting navBarHidden in different Windows, but it doesn't seem to work. Except for hiding the bar of the NavigationWindow, which isn't what I want.

Does anyone have an idea how to do this? If it is at all possible…
