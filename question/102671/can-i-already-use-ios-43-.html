    If an SDK isn't officially supported by Titanium, then it won't be supported in any way. If you choose to use it, you will be leading the way on your own. Although I am sure people would like to hear about your experiences, the first response to any problems you post here about an unsupported version will no doubt be to revert to a supported version.

    Hence, unfortunately, you are on your own. :)
