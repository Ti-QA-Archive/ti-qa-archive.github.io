Tab group animate window from left

Hi all,
I have a tab group with four tabs. I'm looking for an easy way to open a window on the tab entering from the left side of the screen.
I use this code to animate window entrance:

var newWindow = win.mm.createWindow({
    url:'newwindow.js',
    title:'Animate this window'
});
Titanium.UI.currentTab.open(newWindow,{animated:true});

The new window enters the screen from the right side. I want to make it enter from the left side.
Thank you.

  • The reason that the window doesn't enter from the left side is because this would be out of the flow of the page hierarchy. When you drill down, new pages are understood to come from the right. Thats why the default transition comes from the right. If you would like to break out of that flow, you would need to open the new window outside of your current tab group and use either your own animation or a default transition style. ie:

    newWindow.open({transition:Ti.UI.iPhone.AnimationStyle.FLIP_FROM_LEFT});
