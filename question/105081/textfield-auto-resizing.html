TextField Auto-Resizing

Hi,
Is there a way of auto-resizing a TextField if the text entered is longer than the field?
Similar to the way the text field works on an iPhone when you send a SMS.
Many thanks.

  • Alan

    I don't think there is an automated way, but it is quite simple to code. Try something like this:

    textfield.addEventListener('change', function(e){
  if(e.value.length > 50){
    e.source.width = e.source.width + 25;
  }
});

    This hasn't been tested, so it may not be syntactically accurate, but you should be able to adapt the idea to suit your needs.

    Hope it helps

      TextAreas can be tricky, especially if they are positioned near the bottom of the screen and hence easily displaced by the keyboard. Although not the most convenient from the developer's perspective, the best approach is to design screens to accommodate this behavior, or open a modal window containing just the textArea near the top.
