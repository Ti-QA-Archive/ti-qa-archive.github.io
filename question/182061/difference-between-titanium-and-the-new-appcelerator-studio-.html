    Hello Bably,

    The Appcelerator studio which is as of now the enterprise edition of the platform, and The Titanium studio is the free community version. Now, after 1st June 2015, they will make the Appcelerator studio free for everyone.

    It includes:

    Built-in profiler, code analyzer, and LiveView editor to accelerate prototyping and development and measure code quality.

    All features not available in the community "Titanium studio" edition.
