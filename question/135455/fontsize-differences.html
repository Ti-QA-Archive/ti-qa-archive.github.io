Fontsize differences

Hi everyone, i'm a noob at this and didn't know how to google it (I tried,but didn't have a good result). I've been testing my layout in android and iPhone, and noticed that the size of the fonts is not the same (for example, if I give a 14 font size, in iPhone is good, but when i take a look at android, is quite of small), is there a way to set the font size that looks good in both platforms? (I tried with percentages, but it's almost the same, at least in my case).

Thanks everyone!

    Use "dp" values for the font - this will give you device independent font sizes; eg

    "12dp" instead of 12
