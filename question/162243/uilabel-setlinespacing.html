    Hi

    You now have access to attributedString, which amongst other things including; bold, links and more - includes a baseline adjustement;

    • AttributedString more detailed info with examples.

    This is the attribute you can use;

    • ATTRIBUTE_BASELINE_OFFSET

    That is the good news - it is possible - bad news, there are currently issues in iOS7 with support for this (although not tested iOS 7.0.4 and up yet). It clips the top line of the test when changed. iOS 6 works fine, this is not a Titanium issue (I have checked) it does the same in xcode itself.
