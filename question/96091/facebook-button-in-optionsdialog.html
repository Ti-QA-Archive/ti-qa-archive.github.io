Facebook button in optionsDialog?

I'm trying to make a "Share on facebook" function in my application - More specifically i have an optiondialog which has a "Share on facebook" button. When that button is clicked i want the application to ask the user to login(if they aren't already logged in) and then do a simple post on their wall.

I know that i can create a facebook.LoginButton that handles the opening of a login-window but how do i add that button to an optionDialog?

Is there another way to do it?

  • As you know facebook is strict.

    1. You can't force open the Login window
    2. You can't put any "facebook buttons" in the options dialog. You can however have an option called "Share on Facebook" or something. When clicked, call the facebook (post_stream) to post content to their wall or whatever. Must be logged in already though, and the post_stream permissions will pop up automatically.
