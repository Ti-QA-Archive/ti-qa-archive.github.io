    Your markup structure seem to be off. I usually keep one Alloy Controller per Window, and then based on the controller logic open whatever Window I need. Your problem could be easily solved by something like this.

    index.xml

    <Alloy>
    <Window id="first">
        <Require src="login"/>
    </Window>
</Alloy>

    index.js

    if (User.confirmLoginFB()) {
    var second = Alloy.createController('second').getView();
    second.open();
}
else{
    $.first.open();
}

    second.xml

    <Alloy>
    <Window id="second">
         <Label>Second</Label>
    </Window>
</Alloy>
