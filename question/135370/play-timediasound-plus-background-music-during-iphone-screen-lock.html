Play Ti.Media.Sound plus background music during iPhone screen lock

Hi there,

I'm struggling a little with the either/or scenario of AUDIO_SESSION_MODE_AMBIENT and AUDIO_SESSION_MODE_PLAYBACK.

Basically, I want to play sound whilst background music is playing (from the iPhone's built-in iPod player). Setting Ti.Media.audioSessionMode = Ti.Media.AUDIO_SESSION_MODE_AMBIENT achieves this.

I would like this behaviour to continue when the phone is locked. The only way I know to keep my application sound playing during screen lock is by setting Ti.Media.audioSessionMode = Ti.Media.AUDIO_SESSION_MODE_PLAYBACK. Unfortunately, this kills the background music.

Does anyone know of a way to play sound from the app along with background music from the device during screen lock?

In case it helps: the sound I'm playing is looped using setInterval().

Titanium SDK version: 1.8.1 (01/27/12 17:31 a24502a)

Many thanks,

Rob

    Titanium.App.addEventListener('pause', function(e) {
        Titanium.Media.audioSessionMode = Titanium.Media.AUDIO_SESSION_MODE_PLAYBACK;
});
