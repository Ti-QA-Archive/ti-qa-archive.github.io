How to deploy iPad app to another iPad at my company

So I got my Ti iPad app deployed to my personal iPad device. But now I need to deploy it to my client's ipad for testing and approval. How exactly do I do this? I don't think it has to go through the full-blown App Store. I read something about being able to deploy this on up to 100 devices on our dev account. That is more than enough. I know on apple developer site I can register my client's ipad's UDID.

But how do I actually push the app to his ipad? Or how does he download it?

    • These are basically the steps you should follow:

      1. Let the customer register its device in TestFlight so you get its UDID;
      2. Add the UDID to the list of devices in Apples provisioning portal;
      3. Add the device to the provisioning certificate;
      4. Download the certificate;
      5. Build the app in Titanium Studio to a device, select the just downloaded certicate;
      6. Upload the IPA you find in the build folder to TestFlight;
      7. Add the client as a tester in TestFlight

  • Hi, you dont use a development profile you must create an Adhoc provisioning profile, download it, double-click it to install into XCode. Then in Ti Studio compile for "App Store" and select the downloaded Adhoc provisioning profile. That is the one that is allowed to be used against 100 devices per year.

    Send that output .ipa to the client - They must drag/drop it into iTunes to install there, then synch to their device.

  • Once you've added the device to the developer profile and compiled the app for your device. You then go into: <app_root>/build/iphone/build/Debug-iphoneos

    There you will see your <appname>.ipa

    Send that to your client.
