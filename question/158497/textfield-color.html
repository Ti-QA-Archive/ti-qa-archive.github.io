    You can set backgroundColor, borderColor, and (foreground) color on text fields just like you would with a label or view. Looking at the screenshot at your link, it looks to me like the developer used a simple label, set to be full width, with background & foreground colors specified to get those "bars."

    • hi Tim,
      the borderColor, color properties worked but i can not see the foregroundColor effect. I believe the foreground color is the color inside the textfield. 

      var username = Titanium.UI.createTextField({
    borderColor: 'black',
    color:'#336699',
    foregroundColor: 'yellow',
    top:50,
    left:10,
    width:200,
    height:30,
    hintText:'username',
    borderStyle:Titanium.UI.INPUT_BORDERSTYLE_ROUNDED
});
