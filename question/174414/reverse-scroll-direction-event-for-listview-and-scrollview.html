  • Hi

    This question confuses me. The ListView, TableView, WebView, ScrollView all have a scroll direction that matches the location of your finger (as an example).

    If you place your finger in the middle of any of those views and whilst maintains contact move your finger up - the content at the top slides out of view and content currently out of view slides into view from below. The view is at all time connected to the position of the finger.

    This is the normal behaviour. If you repeat the same experiment with a sheet of paper on a desk the same thing happens.

    So if;

    • you experience this - this is the EXPECTED behaviour of the OS and your users
    • you do not experience this then please explain in detail, ideally create a video what you are experiencing
