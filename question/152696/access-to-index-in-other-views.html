Access to $.index in other views

Hi,
I can't access to $.Index in my other views
this is my project structure:

- controllers
   - order
      - home
          Category.coffee
      Home.coffee
   Index.coffee

- styles
   - order
      - home
          Category.coffee
      Home.coffee
   Index.coffee

- views
   - order
      - home
          Category.jade
      Home.jade
   Index.jade

I want to access to $.Index on Category.coffee ( controllers )

    You'll need to hold a reference to it in some location that your Category controller can access. Perhaps do this in your index controller:

    Alloy.Globals.index = $.index;

    Be aware though, that as long as you hold this reference, the index controller and its associated UI will not be freed from memory. From the sounds of it though, it sounds like your index controller is meant to persist as long as your app is running.
