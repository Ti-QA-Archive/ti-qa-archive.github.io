Push Notifications with audio

I've noticed that the new Simpsons game has push notifications with audio. The audio gets played when the message pops on the screen. How cool is that? Is this something you will be supporting? Or does anyone outthere know how to do this, or who can deliver such service?

  • This is native and you should be able to do it out of the box.
    The payload of the push allows you to set a sound to be played when the app receives it.

    "aps" : {
        "alert" : "You got your emails.",
        "badge" : 9,
        "sound" : "bingbong.aiff"
    },

    you will have to have the sound you want to play on the device or else you will get the default one.

    read more here:

    http://developer.apple.com/library/mac/#documentation/NetworkingInternet/Conceptual/RemoteNotificationsPG/ApplePushService/ApplePushService.html
