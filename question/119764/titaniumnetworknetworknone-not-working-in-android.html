  • Seems like for Android the constants Ti.Network.NETWORK_* are all undefined. Tested with SDK 2.0.1

    As a workaround I use Ti.Network.getNetworkTypeName(), result should be NONE, LAN, MOBILE, UNKNOWN or WIFI.
