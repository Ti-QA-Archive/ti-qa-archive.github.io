  • I found out some answers…

    Both the app.js (and your supporting files) along with the Titanium.js include are run through a white space parser, but I don't see any minification being done. Variable names are all the same.

    Nothing appears to strip out unused functionality from the titanium.js

    The titanium.js is 94kb as of 1.7.0.RC1
