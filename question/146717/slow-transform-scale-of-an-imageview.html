Slow transform (scale) of an imageview

Hello,

I'm trying to make a smart scale on my imageviews to be adapted to their dynamic content without breaking my ui.

The following transformation is slow though:

imageView.applyProperties({
transform:Ti.UI.create2DMatrix().scale(Math.max(Number($containerHeight) / Number($sourceHeight), 
                            Number($containerWidth) / Number($sourceWidth))),
                            width:$containerWidth,
                            height:$containerHeight,
                            center: {
                                x:Math.floor(width / 2),
                                y:Math.floor(height / 2)
                            }
                        });

It takes 100-300ms on the simulator which is quite slow…

Any ideas why? It's a very simple calculation..

TiSDK: 3.0.0GA
iOS: 6.0
