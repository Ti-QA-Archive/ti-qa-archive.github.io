go to a tab and to top of page

I'm making an iPhone/Android-app that have a "home button".
To go home (first tab) I use tabGroup.setActiveTab(0), but since it is a scrollable view I also want to go to the top of that window.
How can I do that?

  • Accepted Answer

    You can send an event to the tab/window using fireEvent.

    Titanium also has a "cross-context application event" using Ti.App.fireEvent and Ti.App.addEventListener.

    The events have worked perfectly for me on iOS, but I'm having serious problems on Android with them.
