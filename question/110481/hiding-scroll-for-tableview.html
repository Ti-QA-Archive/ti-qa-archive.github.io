  • I can confirm that, as Sven said, doing the following will hide the scrollView's scroll bar:

    var scrollView = Ti.UI.createScrollView({
    showVerticalScrollIndicator:false
});

    If you have multiple items in your scrollView and one of them is a scrollable view, such as a tableView, make sure that the scrollView is showing up on top by setting zIndexes. It may be that the scroll bars are actually showing on an element other than the scrollView.
