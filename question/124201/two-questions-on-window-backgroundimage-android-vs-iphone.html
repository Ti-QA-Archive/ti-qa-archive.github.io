Two questions on window backgroundimage (android vs iphone)

When I set the background image on a window, it's showing on both platforms, but on iphone it is sitting below the status bar (time, battery etc.) where as on android the top of my background image is being covered by the status bar.

I've deployed a visual technique so I only need one image for both my background and title bar, so on android my title bar (visually) is half blocked.

Is there something I can do to make this consistent. Otherwise I will have to supply special images for android that take into account the height of the status bar and are blank until that point (seems painful over so many android resolution options).

Also… one way to solve it is to make my application (windows) fullscreen and not have the status bar on either platform show at all. Would this be a problem when it comes to app submission? Would my app get rejected because I hide the status bar?
