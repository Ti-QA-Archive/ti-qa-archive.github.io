    • May be you should use navcontroller by kwhinnery. Its great working solution, at least it will give a concept to manage and reference the windows, close and open and directly goto home window.

      a)Define a windowStack array object windowStack = [];
      b)When you open a window, add it to the stack – windowStack.push(win);
      c)When you close the window, remove from the stack – windowStack.pop(win)
      d)When you want to goto home, open(windowstack[0])
