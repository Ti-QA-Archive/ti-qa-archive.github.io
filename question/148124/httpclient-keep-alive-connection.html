HTTPClient keep-alive connection

Hi everybody,

I need to set "Connection:keep-alive" in the HTTP request header.
I tried both
xhr.setEnableKeepAlive(true);
xhr.setRequestHeader('Connection', 'keep-alive');

The sniffer tells me that the HTTP header still contains Connection: close

I do the following:

var xhr = Titanium.Network.createHTTPClient();
xhr.open('PUT',url);
xhr.setRequestHeader('Content-Type', 'application/json');
xhr.setEnableKeepAlive(true);
xhr.send(JSON.stringify(document));

Is this just broken or do I do something wrong?

Target platform is iPhone with iOS6.1, SDK 3.0.0.GA, Developer 3.0.1.201212181159

Thanks for your help!
