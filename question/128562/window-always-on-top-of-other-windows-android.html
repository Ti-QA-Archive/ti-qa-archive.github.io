  • Hi,

    As per what I understand you want to stack windows one on top of the other. For that don't close the current window.

    var win1 = Titanium.UI.currentWindow;

var win2 = Titanium.UI.createWindow({
    fullscreen: false,
    url:'win2.js',
});
win2.open();
