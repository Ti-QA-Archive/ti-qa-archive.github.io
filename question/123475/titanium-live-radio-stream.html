    • I'm not familiar with mms (my guess is it's a protocol Apple doesn't support), but I do know Apple imposes limitations as to what formats and protocols are acceptable, especially now around streaming (to provide users an overall more stable and pleasant experience, etc..). If you are working with your local radio station, you could figure it out by going through the documentation and downloading Apple's tools mentioned in the docs. The general idea is that the stream is broken into small .ts file 'chunks', those files are referenced in a 'playlist' placed on a simple web server, and the audio/videoplayer reads that 'playlist', finds the first chunk file, 'eats' it and then goes back to the list to get the next one, etc. It seems a little daunting at first and does require some planning, testing, etc..
