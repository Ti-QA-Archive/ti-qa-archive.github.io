Hide or Remove one ScrollView and show other Scrollview

I have 2 scrollview (X and Y) in a page. Both have different contents. In one button click I need to hide or remove X scroll view and show other scroll view Y in the same page. and in next click I need to show the hidden X view and hide Y scrollview. How I can handle this situation. I tried with scrollview.hide() and scrollview.remove(). But I can show second one. But first one is going back not hiding.

//Create your scrollViews. Please, add yourself size and location properties top, left...
//Look that one scrollView is visible, but not the other
var scrollX = Ti.UI.createScrollView({visible:true}); 
var scrollY = Ti.UI.createScrollView({visible:false});

//add both scrollViews to window in the same place.
win.add(scrollX);
win.add(scrollY);

button.addEventListener('click', function(){
    //invert visible status on each click
    scrollX.visible = !scrollX.visible;
    scrollY.visible = !scrollY.visible;
})
