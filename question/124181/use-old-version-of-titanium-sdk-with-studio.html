  • You can download SDK's from here back to 1.4 http://builds.appcelerator.com.s3.amazonaws.com/index.html

    Then you just need to put it into the correct directory.

    On Mac that is /Library/Application Support/Titanium/mobilesdk/osx

    When it is there it will appear in Ti Studio and can be selected from the Tiapp.xml file.

    Hope this helps. If your using windows, I am not sure where to put the SDK.
