iPad App: Default to Landscape?

When creating an iPhone app, is it possible to force it into landscape as the app opens? And if so, when you are doing page layout (left, right, top, bottom, etc) can top actually be right, and left be top, etc?

I hope that makes sense.

    you may want to specify behaviour for each window 

    var win = Ti.UI.createWindow(Tools.combine($$.Window,{
        orientationModes: [Titanium.UI.LANDSCAPE_LEFT, Titanium.UI.LANDSCAPE_RIGHT]
    }));

    or globally (this way ensure the launch screen only will be shown in landscape mode). In Tiapp.xml comment or delete the orientations you will not to support

    
        <orientations device="ipad">
            <!--orientation>Ti.UI.PORTRAIT</orientation-->
            <!--orientation>Ti.UI.UPSIDE_PORTRAIT</orientation-->
            <orientation>Ti.UI.LANDSCAPE_LEFT</orientation>
            <orientation>Ti.UI.LANDSCAPE_RIGHT</orientation>
        </orientations>
