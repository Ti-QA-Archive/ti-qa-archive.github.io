iTunes connect validation issues

Hi.

I get: This bundle is invalid. The value for key CFBundleversion , (numbers), in the Info.plist file must be no longer than 18 characters.

The value for shortversionstring must be a period-separated list of the most three non-negative integers.

Help! I dont have a clue what they're saying here…..

I am doing this with Ti.SDK 3.1.0.v20130316201654 iOS SDK 6.1

#cryingandsobbing

this is not an update on an app.

    this means that the version value is too long (longer than 18 chars).

    Open your tiapp.xml and check what you've set in version.
    Then, open the Info.plist under build&#x2F;iphone&#x2F; and check what you have here:

    <key>CFBundleVersion</key>
<string>1.0</string>
<key>CFBundleShortVersionString</key>
<string>1.0</string>

    if it's your first release you should have a 0.x or simply a 1.0, nothing else. And every time you want to update, that version must be higher than the previous.
