Ti Android: A List of Textfields in a Tableview (scrollable:false) in a scrollableView - Textfields hide as you type.

This worked well on the iPhone, though on Android the Textfields that are in the middle, as they focus, they scroll upwards and cannot see them as you type in them.

The Problem:

I have 14 Textfields a TableView which has scrollable:false, and that TableView is in a ScrollView.

When I focus on textfields which are in the middle (Textfields#5 till Textfields#10) the Textfields scrolls out of view as you type. This works well on iOS.

Any workarounds?

    Hi

    Include this property on the window in question;

    windowSoftInputMode: Titanium.UI.Android.SOFT_INPUT_ADJUST_RESIZE,

    This is the right way rather than a work-around. If you think this is a weird thing to need to have to do - you are not alone.
