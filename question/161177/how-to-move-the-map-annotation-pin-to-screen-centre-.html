How to move the Map Annotation Pin to screen centre ?

Hi I had a mapview (using GoogleMap V2) with a number of annotations on it. I had a problem on how to move/display the selected Annotation Pin to the centre of the screen. It will automatically move to centre if I click on any of the Annotation Pin itself. But if the Pin is 'off-screen' then I can't do that.

What i want to achieve is by clicking on a 'Button', it will automatically move and display the Annotation I selected to the screen centre. I used 'selectAnnotation' method, it highlight and display the Annotation only but did not move to centre.

Anyone can help me on this? Is that possible to do that? How? Thanks in advance.

I am using Ti.SDK 3.2.0GA and test on Android device.

    You will need to call the map's setRegion() method, passing in lat/long coordinates of the annotation as the latitude and longitude, and the same latitude & longitude deltas as was originally set for the map.
