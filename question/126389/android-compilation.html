  • If you can't build a default project without getting build errors, then there is likely something wrong with your Android SDK installation, or your Titanium mobile SDK installation. I would suggest trying to reinstall one or both.

    Before you do that, though, in Titanium Studio go to 'preferences -> Titanium Studio -> Titanium' and make sure you have the proper location for your Android SDK configured.
