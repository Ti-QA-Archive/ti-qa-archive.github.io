    • Ok, judging by Brians answer below, there may be some misunderstanding here with your question. When you implement Ti.Barcode within your app, it opens the camera view inside of your app and processes barcodes within the view finder. All this happens within your app, not using the actual Camera app on your iphone, but rather with the camera functionality on your phone.
