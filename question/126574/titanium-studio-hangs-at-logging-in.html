Titanium Studio hangs at "Logging In"

At the title says, I can't get to the Log in screen. I had a previous working version of Titanium Studio, but when I had trouble importing a project, I decided to do a clean install of TS. I physically removed the Titanium Studio folder (Linux), and unpacked the new version, but it does not run, and just hangs before the login screen. And I have no idea if its me or something else. (It can't be my credentials because I can't even get that far). Thx.

    hi, did you edit the proxy settings?
    There's an issue that stops Titanium studio from starting if you edit them, the fix is to remove the hidden ~/.eclipse folder with settings.
