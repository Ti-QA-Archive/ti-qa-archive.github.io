    why not ask 'why do i get this unlicensed error when running MattTuttles module?'

    The answer is… Matt submitted it to the appc marketplace with a GUID. You pull it from matt outside the marketplace (open source) with the same GUID. Appc checks, you did NOT get it from them, so you get an unlicensed error.

    Solution: download from appc marketplace (if still available) OR change the guid in matts and compile it yourself.

    As for alternatives, yeah, I'm happy with Matts…
