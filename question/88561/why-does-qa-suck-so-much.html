Why does Q&A suck so much?

Hi All,

I can't be the only one here who finds Q&A frustrating.

The more I use Q&A the more frustrated I am.

Here are my top frustrations with some suggestions:

1) Comment formatting is a lot smaller than answers, also the comments are not wrapped.

  • Make the formatting larger.
  • wrap the comments to the same width as answers.

2) You can't answer your question.

  • Allow you to mark your own response as 'best answer'

3) Cannot select the number of Questions to be displayed.

  • Make a selection, store it, use it. ie. 10, 20, 50, 100.

4) It is difficult to determine when something was last updated.

  • In addition to the 'Posted' date add a 'Last Updated' date to the summary.
  • Make bold or indicate otherwise since my last visit which questions have been updated.

5) The add comment is not apparent causing people to post as answers.

  • Have one entry and allow the poster or OP to mark as answer.
