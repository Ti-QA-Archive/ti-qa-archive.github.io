Appstore submission: ERROR ITMS-9000

Hi!
I've been trying to submit my app, and it just keeps saying: "ERROR ITMS-9000: "The following URL schemes found in your app are not inte the correct format: carinamansson_se. URL schemes need to begin with an alphabetic character and be comprised of alphanumeric characters, the period, the hyphen or the plus sign only. Please see RFC1738 for more detail"

I have no idea what this means. Everything works fine in the simulator and I've done everything just as usual. Any ideas?

    Look in the info.plist that was created for you in the build/iphone directory.
    Titanium builds a custom URL shcema for you based upon your app name.
    It is possible that while the name of your app is legal when used as a name, it is not when used as a url schema.

    Example:

    You could try to prevent this by declaring your own in your tiapp.xml

    <ios>
        <plist>
            <dict>
                <array>
                    <dict>
                        <key>CFBundleURLName</key>
                        <string>your.app.id.here</string>
                        <key>CFBundleURLSchemes</key>
                        <array>
                            <string>carinamansson-se</string>
                        </array>
                    </dict>
                </array>
            </dict>
        </plist>
    </ios>
