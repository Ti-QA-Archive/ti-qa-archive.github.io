Can i use custom title bar in Android

Hi all,

I am using android 2.3.3 and 1.7.1 titanium studio . I want to use my custom title bar with some color other than grey and title aligned in the center in android .How can i do that?? I couldnt find much about navigation control in the API document FOR android. Just for example if i try this 

var win = Titanium.UI.createWindow({
        title:'Titnium',
        fullscreen :false,
        barColor:'blue'

    });
    win.open();

This opens a window with grey title bar with text aligned left. The bar color also doesnt seems to be working. Can i use my any other color than grey or increase or decrease the height of the title bar??

Thanks

    No, you cannot. All you can do is to fake it.

    var win = Titanium.UI.createWindow({
    fullscreen :false,

    navBarHidden:true

    });

var my_navbar = Ti.UI.createLabel({
    height:50,
    backgroundColor:'#00f',
    color:'#fff',
    text:'My title',
    top:0
});

win.add(my_navbar);
win.open();
