  • Hi Virendra

    Unfortunately the filtering process is designed to find a single entry from the filtered results listed. This is not designed to reduce the list until you turn it off.

    You can however use techniques yourself to achieve the same result, including;

    • Separate the searchbar and tableview and control the rows that are shown yourself, by showing/hiding them
    • Use setData to display the filtered list until a button is pressed then use setData again to show all the results.

    I hope this helps.
