Problem with splash screen on Android

By default, the splash image comes under notification bar and nav bar. I was able to remove the nav bar without any 'side-effects', but if I remove the notification bar by setting either of the two lines in tiapp.xml

<statusbar-hidden>false</statusbar-hidden>
<fullscreen>true</fullscreen>

The splash acts like a window, i.e if I press the back button on the main window of my app, it will display the splash page (instead of closing or minimizing the app). Can anyone help me?

    attach to the window you want to close when u hit the back button this:

    exitOnClose:true

    You need to add it on creation. Setting it after the window is created won't work.

    http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/2.0/index.html#!/api/Titanium.UI.Window-property-exitOnClose
