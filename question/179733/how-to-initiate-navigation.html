how to initiate navigation

I am building my first app (Android only) with titanium and what I am trying to do is start navigation on the phone. I want to place a button on details page and when a user click on Navigate, it should initiate navigation on the user's phone, whether it be Google Maps or Here Maps or whatever it is the user likes. I already have Google Maps for android integrated in my app. On the details page I have the latitude and longitude of the place. I have one button that says View In Map, which when clicked on just shows the user Google maps with an annotation. I want my new button to initiate navigation on their phone, as they maybe in the car and want to navigate there.

Thanks you

  • Ok it looks like I did not do a good job of searching the forum. This question has been asked and answered already.

    http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/176016/how-to-launch-turn-by-turn-gps-navigation

    Basically, it is really simple and all you have to do is:

    Ti.Platform.openURL('http://maps.google.com/maps?daddr=99.9999,-99.9999&directionsmode=driving');`
