IPad camera overlay rotates

    Ticket TIMOB-8619 describes the issue you're having. The ticket got closed, because on iPhone/iPod, the overlay is not supposed to rotate. It looks like the iPad issue is the opposite problem. While that was noted in the comments on the ticket, because it was not the main issue it must have been overlooked when the ticket was closed. (Another ticket should have been created.)

    You should probably create a new ticket to specifically describe what you're seeing. Please provide a simple, sample app demonstrating the problem. In your description, you could add a link to TIMOB-8619 to point out that your issue has been reported previously.

    There are a couple of other orientation-related issues, that are perhaps related too:

    • TIMOB-17648
    • TIMOB-17658
