Tips for submitting apps with iAds in them

Hello,
Here are some good advices to help to submit your iAd-enabled apps for the App Store. Our app was rejected two times because of these.

(1) Hide your ads when an error occurs!

Apple will reject your app, if it does not hide the empty banner in case of any network or server side error. You shall use something similar to

ad.addEventListener('error', function() {
    // code to hide your app and resize your content
    ad.visible = false;
    yourTableView.top = 0; // or something similar
});

to do this.

(2) Don't ever use iAd placeholder ads on your screenshots!

Apple rejected our app second time because we took screenshots froum our app which showed the development version, which featured their placeholder banners (black bg, white 'iAd test' text). You shall either replace them using PhotoShop with real ads from e.g. Apple's own iAd Gallery app, or hide the ads. We choose to PS the screenshots because we have a Lite and a Pro version, and the key feature of the Pro is that it does not have ads, thus we had to show the difference somehow to our users.
