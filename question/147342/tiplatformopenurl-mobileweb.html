  • Internally, Titanium Mobile Web's Ti.Platform.openURL() creates and opens a Ti.UI.Window containing a Ti.UI.WebView. WebViews on MW have many, many limitations.

    One of the limitations is they are subject to frame busting. So, if you try to open http://www.google.com, Google detects it's being opened in an iframe and will redirect the browser to Google, thus leaving your Titanium Mobile Web app.

    Another notable limitation of WebViews on Mobile Web is you cannot scroll a WebView (see TIMOB-8435).
