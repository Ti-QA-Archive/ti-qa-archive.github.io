If you're using Modules from the Titanium Marketplace - you MUST know this!

Titanium just made a breaking change in it's 1.8.0.1.RC3 SDK release, which has been pushed to all Titanium Studio users and automatically installed, so good chance that your installation is affected. Here is the link to the details of that change.

Since Titanium Studio will automatically choose this version of SDK for all newly created apps, developers might be unaware of the reason why some Marketplace modules they are using will do compile anymore!

We, Marketplace vendors, can not communicate to our users about the fact that some of our modules are not compatible with the latest SDK release, because, as insane as it sounds, the whole company left for the 10 days Holiday break after pushing this breakng chage to it's entire customerbase, and updates which vendors are making to their listings are not being published!!!

As a result - we have negative remarks and reviews from angry (and rightfully so) customers:

see the review for my CloudeKit module posted during this time

So, all, please be patient with us, Marketplace vendors, we did nothing wrong!

Hey, Appcelerator, what have you been thinking?!

From the people who created such a brilliant product - we would expect some more evident signs of intelligence…
