  • I think that the problem is with Titanium, I have come across this in my app. I have to submit a post with spaces in to a website, it works fine on android but not on IOS.

    I ran wireshark on the submissions made through a browser, the android emulator and then the IOS emulator, the browser and android emulator encodes "log in" as "log+in", the IOS emulator encodes it as "log%20in", if I try to manually submit the value as "log+in" it gets encoded as "log%2bin".

    I cannot seem to find any way of submitting spaces as + which is what the website expects.
