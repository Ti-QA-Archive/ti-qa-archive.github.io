Camera access authorization (iOS)

In order to check if an app has access to the events in the EventKit we can use the Titanium.Calendar.eventsAuthorization property.

Is there something similar to check if the app has access to the camera?

I am asking because Titanium.Media.showCamera() opens the camera (showing a black screen) even if the app is not authorized to access the camera. I would prefer to not open the camera and instead display an appropriate message.

Also note that Titanium.Media.openPhotoGallery() handles the case of missing authorization properly - this method display a message instead of photos in case the app is not authorized to access the device's photos.

— asked February 18th 2015 by Thomas Wiesen
