Going live with Urban Airship Push and iOS

Hi there
My app is now ready for the app store. I use Urban Airship for push messages. With the test-profile, this works very well. What do I have to take care about when switching to "production" mode?

Do I need to just create a new App in UA with the production-certificate by Apple and update the UA AppKey and SecretKey in my App?

Thanks for any hints…

Jonas

    Yes I would definitely create a separate app in UA for your production app. I would also test your production setup by installing your app with a distribution Ad Hoc provisioning profile so you can test that everything with the production cert is working. And after you have all of that working, re-download your App Store provisioning profile and use that when submitting. I made the mistake before of using an App Store provisioning profile that was created before setting up my Push notification keys and registration of Push just wouldn't happen. Also very hard to debug those type of issues.
