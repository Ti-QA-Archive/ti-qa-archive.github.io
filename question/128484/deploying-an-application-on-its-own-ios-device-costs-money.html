Deploying an application on its own iOS device costs money???

Hi,

I have been developing an application with Titanium Mobile and testing on an Android device.
I finally got a friend's Mac and tested successfully on the iPhone emulator.
Now comes the time to test on an iPhone or iPad device, and… whatever which documentation I read, it seems I need to pay $99 per year to be able to deploy on a device!

Do I have to pay to deploy my own application on my own device?!?!
Or did I miss something??

  • Just to confirm and clarify: the $99 developer account is required to become part of Apple's iOS developer program. Apple requires that you pay the $99 sort of as a sanity check so that people don't keep creating accounts on the fly to distribute apps and circumvent the submission process.
