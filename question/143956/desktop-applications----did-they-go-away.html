    Titanium Desktop was given over to the open source community and is available at TideSDK.org

    • StackOverflow: http://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/tidesdk
    • Documentation: http://tidesdk.multipart.net/docs/user-dev/generated
    • Source Code: https://github.com/TideSDK/TideSDK
    • Tutorials: Get started easily http://tidesdk.multipart.net/docs/user-dev/generated/#!/guide
    • Report a Bug: Help us improve https://github.com/TideSDK/TideSDK/issues.
    • IRC: Chat with us on #tidesdk on freenode.net
    • Twitter: Follow TideSDK @tidesdk.
    • Blog: Read our blog at http://tidesdk.org/blog.
    • Mailing List: Join our mailing list at https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/tidesdk.
    • Wiki: Access additional resources in our wiki https://github.com/TideSDK/TideSDK/wiki

    • I am not sure what you are speaking of when you say it doesn't work with latest version of Appcelerator. Appcelerator is the company, perhaps you mean Titanium Studio.

      TideSDK is of course a Desktop SDK. There was a plugin for Titanium Studio to be able to use it together with the legacy Titanium Desktop 1.2.0.RC4.

      For TideSDK 1.3.1 you can use the TideSDK Developer App with an editor of any flavour to create and package your app. The Developer App can be downloaded from the http://tidesdk.org site.

      You will see services in the near future so stay tuned. Small changes in the Desktop plugin are required by Appcelerator to accomodate TideSDK to allow developers to use and run their apps through Titanium Studio.
