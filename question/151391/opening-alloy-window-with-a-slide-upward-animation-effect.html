Opening Alloy Window with a Slide Upward Animation Effect

Hi there-

Is there an example or documentation which shows how to open a window, where the window slides up from the bottom (similar to how errors are opened), or slide down on close, on press of a close button for the Alloy framework? I looked through the app examples, and have found few transition examples, besides the Navigation group, and have already gotten the flip animation to work within a ScrollableView contorl, but would like a simple window to slide up within my app if possible (and then be destroyed on close).

    Hi Christopher,

    you can use : 

    modal : true

    when opening the window.its opening from bottom to top and close from top to bottom.
