No Statusbar on Android

The only time I see any indication of the Android statusbar is during the app launch / splashscreen phase. After that it is missing from all windows. I have this in tiapp.xml:

<statusbar-style>default</statusbar-style>
<statusbar-hidden>false</statusbar-hidden>

and I have tried creating Windows with fullscreen on/off with no visible effect either way. Creating just a default window, with not options set, does not have a statusbar either. And I can't see where there are other settings that might effect this. I've tried with both 1.7.5 and 1.7.2 just to see if its an SDK kink, but both work the same. I must be missing something, but what???

Thanks.
