  • You can do this without a push server:

    Ti.App.iOS.scheduleLocalNotification({
   alertBody:"Daily reminder",
   alertAction:"Show",
   date: new Date(),
   repeat: 'daily'
});

    More info in the docs: Titanium.App.iOS.LocalNotification
