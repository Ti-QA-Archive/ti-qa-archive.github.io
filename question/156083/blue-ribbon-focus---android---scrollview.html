blue ribbon focus - Android - scrollview

When I scroll a scollview, on Android some blue appears at the top and bottom. This is the equivalent of iOS rubber band. However can I eliminate that blue that appears at the end of the scroll view?

  • Hi

    This can be removed by changing the default property settings to the following;

    • overScrollMode

    Usage example;

    var tbl = Ti.UI.createTableView({
    overScrollMode: Ti.UI.Android.OVER_SCROLL_NEVER, // this
    data:data
});

    Further notes;

    This setting can also be used on the following views;

    • Ti.UI.ScrollableView
    • Ti.UI.ScrollView
    • Ti.UI.WebView

    Plus

    • Ti.UI.TableView
