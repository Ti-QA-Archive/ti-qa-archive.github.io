Auto scale a view while keeping its aspect ratio?

Is it possible in titanium to make a view scale with its parent while keeping its aspect ratio?

For example:

Outer view: 100x200
Inner view: 100x100

Now I want that when the outer view grows, the inner view grows with him, but always keeps its aspect ratio of 1:1 and stays a square.

If the outer view would grow to 300x200 for example, the inner view would have to be 200x200, since this is the max size to what it can grow without losing its aspect ratio.

Is it possible?

  • Accepted Answer

    Yep, using the following:

    var r = Math.min(outerView.height/innerView.height, outerView.width/innerView.width);

innerView.applyProperties({
      width: outerView.width * r
    , height: outerView.height * r
});
