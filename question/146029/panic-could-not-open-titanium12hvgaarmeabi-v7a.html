  • Hi

    If you are having trouble with an emulator AVD (Android Virtual Device), is may be easier to delete it and then let Titanium recreate it for you.

    This assumes you are on a mac, if not the process should be very similar for your OS.

    • Go to Dashboard in Ti Studio
    • Press "Getting Started" (will turn red)
    • Click "Android"
    • Click "Update Android SDK"
    • From the "Android SDK Manager" click the menu "Tools"

    From here you can delete AVDs, just choose the one in question and delete it. It will remove any saved data and settings but the AVD will be auto-created next time you compile. This USUALLY fixes those sort of problems.

    You can also setup some other AVDs if you wish.
