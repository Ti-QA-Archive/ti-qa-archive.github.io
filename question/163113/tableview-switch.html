  • Hey, i got it.
    I was trying to get the value of a rowElement when changing a value of another Element. For future question i will post some codesnips, thanks for reading, and here is my solution:

    switch.addEventListener('change',function(e){
    alert(e.source.parent.children[3].text);
});
