      So in order to assist anyone else who reads this post later this is the reason (iOS focused);

      • A window does not provide any automatic dismiss ability
      • A navigation controller 'Back' buttons are navigation based and not considered a self dismiss
      • Modal dialog's are designed to mask anything below - even if a portion can be seen, no events bubble
      • Pop-over's, alert dialogs, options dialogs are examples of things that do offer dismiss opportunities outside the visual portion of the view itself
      • Research of stock Apple apps will confirm this; mail app in landscape new mail dialog is a form sheet - this cannot be dismissed without use of elements specifically provided by the app developer - buttons for example
