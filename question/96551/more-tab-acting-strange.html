More Tab acting strange

Hello,

after i added the 6th Tab to my app i received the "more" Tab.
First everything seems to work like expected.

As soon i select one of the Tabs in the "More" Table View and navigate back, by clicking the "more" Tab, the title and the Edit button are gone.

Later the "More" Table View took over the complete Navigation Bar of one of the Tabs.

This is happening using iPhone and Titanium 1.5.1.

Whats going on there?
