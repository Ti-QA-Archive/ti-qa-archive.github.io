    A little late, but just to document this….I just ran across this same problem and discovered that I needed a blank picker row in the xml to initiliaze the needed array and prevent the exception.

    index.xml

    <Alloy>
    <Window class="Window">
        <Picker id="team" useSpinner="true">
            <PickerRow title='test'/>
        </Picker>
    </Window>
</Alloy>
