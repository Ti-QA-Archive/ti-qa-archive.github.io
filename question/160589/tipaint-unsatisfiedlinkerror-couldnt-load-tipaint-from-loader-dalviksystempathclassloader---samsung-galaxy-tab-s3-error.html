Ti.Paint UnsatisfiedLinkError: Couldn't load ti.paint from loader dalvik.system.PathClassLoader - Samsung Galaxy Tab S3 error

  • Ti.Paint is not supported by the x86 platform. Even if the app builds successfully (anyway you should find some warnings), the lib will not be included into the apk. To check this, rename .apk to .zip, and give a look into the folder /lib/x86.

    http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/160592/module-tipaint-does-not-support-the-abi-x86
