    Once you subscribe (and I don't see an icon next to your name, so I assume you have not by the time you asked the question) the top frame will include a Helpdesk link. That leads to a page where among other things is a Resources section. One of those links is to the Titanium+ modules, where you can select which one to download. Another link is to the Early Access programs, if you are interested in the Blackberry SDK beta for example.

    You can also search the tickets and knowledge base from that page.

    The videos are listed on that page, so I presume they are under the Training link in the top frame.
