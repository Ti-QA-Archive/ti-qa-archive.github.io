Android on device shows different than in the emulator

I have created a perfectly laid out app the runs in the android emulator. I then decided to test on the android phone device and much to my surprise the font and image and buttons all look very small. How would I code this application so that what I see in the emulator is what I see on the device?

  • Accepted Answer

    If you did not specify the sizes in DP, but in the default pixels, you can try adding the following lines to your TIAPP.xml to see if it looks closer to what you expect.

    <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
        <manifest>
            <supports-screens android:anyDensity="false"/>
        </manifest>
</android>
