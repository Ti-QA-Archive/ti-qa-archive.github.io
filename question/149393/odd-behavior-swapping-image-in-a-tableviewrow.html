  • I have also seen strange TableView issues on Android, and will write a separate Q&A on it. In my case - SDK 3.0.x, android 2.2 and 4.x. Issues I have seen are: wrong images displaying on occasion when the images are from local files. Images flashing white as the tableview is being scrolled - much worse on remote images than local images. And in general - very poor scroll performance, especially for custom rows with an image and a few labels. On iOS these issues don't happen in my case.
