Distributing IOS Apps to Internal Corporate Users w/o iTunes

One of my larger corporate clients is expressing interest in my developing mobile apps to tie into some larger internal systems (that I developed).

In order to distribute such apps to corporate devices (w/o iTunes), would TestFlight be the best solution or are other options available?

    With all iOS distributions you have to generate a certificate. If you using test flight it is designed to allow you to use your developer certificate and distribute to authorised devices.

    What your after is called an apple enterprise account, where you can create the application and then distribute it via a web link to whoever you wish without limitation of devices.

    https://developer.apple.com/programs/ios/enterprise/
