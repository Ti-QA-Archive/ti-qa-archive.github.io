  • Might not be the exact answer that you're looking for but I find this helps.

    You'll need to turn on showing of hidden files for this to work: http://www.mactricksandtips.com/2008/04/show-hidden-files.html

    Go to your Titanium Workspace directory and look for the folder called .metadata and remove it. This will ensure that projects you had open before are now closed.
