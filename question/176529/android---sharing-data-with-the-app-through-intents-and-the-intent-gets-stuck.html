Android - Sharing data with the app through intents and the intent gets "stuck"

Hie.

I'm trying to share data with my app from other apps.
I've added a "SEND" intention to the custom AndroidManifest file like so - 

<activity android:configChanges="keyboardHidden|screenSize" android:label="Lingua.ly" android:name=".LinguaLyActivity" android:screenOrientation="portrait" android:theme="@style/Theme.Titanium">
            <intent-filter>
                <action android:name="android.intent.action.MAIN"/>
                <category android:name="android.intent.category.LAUNCHER"/>
            </intent-filter>
            <intent-filter>
                <action android:name="android.intent.action.SEND"/>
                <category android:name="android.intent.category.DEFAULT"/>
                <data android:mimeType="text/plain"/>
            </intent-filter>
        </activity>

and on my app.js I got this code - 

var currActivity = Titanium.Android.currentActivity;
passedInText = currActivity.getIntent().getStringExtra(Ti.Android.EXTRA_TEXT);
Ti.API.info('passedInText = ' +  passedInText);

On the first time, the intent works just fine. when I try to share another text with the app, it doesn't open the share options menu but goes directly to my app and does nothing.

Is there anywhere to clean the intent after I'm done with it?

I've also tried to launch it as a singleTask or singleInstance but got only greyscreen (my window background color) and nothing more than that.

Any help would be highly appreciated.

Thanks in advance,
