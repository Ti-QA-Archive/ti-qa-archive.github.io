  • just found solution here to change text in french language :

    https://wiki.appcelerator.org/display/guides2/Internationalization#Internationalization-LocalizeSystemButtonsandSubmittoInternationalAppStores

    just add to tiapp.xml :

    <ti:app>
    <ios>
        <plist>
            <dict>
                <key>CFBundleLocalizations</key>
                <array>
                    <string>fr</string>
                </array>
            </dict>
        </plist>
    </ios>
</ti:app>

    works as expeted.
