Titanium Studio clutters my console with 'Analytics' errors

I don't know under which circumstances, but once in a while TitaniumStudio (OSX 10.8.4) starts cluttering the console with these messages:

[ERROR] : … [WARN] Unable to securely connect to api.appcelerator.net with the latest TLS.

and

[ERROR] : Analytics error sending request: A connection failure occurred

What is this? And how can I avoid this?

— asked July 3rd 2013 by Andy Fuchs
  • console,error,login
1 Comment

  • You could disable analytics in the tiapp.xml

    <analytics>false</analytics>

  • These are messages generated from the inbuilt Ti Analytics module that Appcelerator automatically builds into your app. As Manuel suggests, you can disable analytics and these messages will disappear
