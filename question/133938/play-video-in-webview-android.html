  • Our video is hosted on Vimeo. The HTML5 video will not play in the web view as you have discovered. For android and iOS we launch the default browser to a php file that frames the video in from vimeo and it works fine. On android, the back button brings the user right back into our app. On iOS, the "close" button on the php generated html launches a custom url that calls up our app on the mobile and because of saved states, we return the user to the page they launched safari from. Its how we worked around this issue.

    Clients are ok with it as the alternative is streamed .TS files on iOS from S3 which would cost them $$$$ each month.

    • When you create your Ti webview, just pass in the appropriate pluginState.
      For example:

      var webView = Ti.UI.createWebView({
    pluginState:Ti.UI.Android.WEBVIEW_PLUGINS_ON_DEMAND
});
