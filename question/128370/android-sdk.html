    AnnMarie

    Please would you provide all the information about your system? For example, what OS are you using, and what is the exact error you have seen?

    While you do this, check that you have, at a minimum, all the packages shown in the screenshot of the Summary of Installed Android Packages section of the documentation.

    Also install the latest continuous build, by selecting master from the drop down menu, right-clicking and copying the URL of the archive for your OS, and pasting it into Titanium Studio's Help > Install Titanium SDK from URL dialog. Note that you don't have to actually use this version of the SDK for your apps; it just needs to exist on your system. Then restart Studio and try again.

    Thanks
