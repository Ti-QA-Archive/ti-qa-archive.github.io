Sqlite does not handle 64bit integers correctly

Titanium doesn't correctly handle 64bit numbers coming from Sqlite. This is due to two things.

1) A problem in the underlying pldatabase that the use:
http://code.google.com/p/pldatabase/issues/detail?id=25&thanks=25&ts=1377514140

2) A problem with sqlite in Titanium:
https://jira.appcelerator.org/browse/TC-2820

This question is related to a closed question:
http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/132029/sqlite-gives-me-an-other-number-than-there-is-in-the-db.
