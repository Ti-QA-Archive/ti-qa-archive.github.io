  • David

    I know what you mean. When paging through all the Q&As, the date shown on each post is the date it was originally asked.

    When you view the "Most Recent" list, the URL implies that it is in createdAt order, ie http://developer.appcelerator.com/questions/created. Actually, the posts are shown in updatedAt order, so any questions that have received comments or answers the most recently will be listed first.

    I can understand the reason for the confusion, as it is quite counter-intuitive.
