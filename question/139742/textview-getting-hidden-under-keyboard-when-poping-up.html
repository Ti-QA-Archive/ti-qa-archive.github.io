  • var appointmentModifyCreateWin = Titanium.UI.currentWindow;

  • You will have to animate the view to position it above the keyboard when it pops up.

    The animation time for the keyboard is 150ms. So your animation for sliding the text area up will have to be the same.

    Hope this gives you a head start.
