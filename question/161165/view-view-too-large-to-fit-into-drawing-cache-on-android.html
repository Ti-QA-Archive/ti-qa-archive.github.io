View: View too large to fit into drawing cache on Android

Hey, I just switched to titanium 3.2

I have a scrollview which contains several views and those views may contain 1 remote image. For Some remote images i get a warning as "View: View too large to fit into drawing cache, needs 4118356 bytes, only 3932160 available" and that image's view is added to parent view but its not visible. I can still tap on it and get the reaction but its not visible.

I have the problem on my Nexus 4 Kitkat and have no problem with my old samsung android 2.3.
Also when i switch back to titanium 3.1.3 its working fine.

Anyone has idea about how to solve that drawing cache problem?

Thanks.

  • I found a solution which decreased the performance of all parts of app.

    I turned off the hardware acceleration by adding that application part to TiApp.xml

    <manifest android:versionCode="131" android:versionName="1.3.1">
        <application android:hardwareAccelerated="false">

                </application>
</manifest>
