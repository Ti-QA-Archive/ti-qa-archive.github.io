  • Hi

    There is a free module in the market place called Canvas Module, this will give you the ability to draw shapes as you need, including the ability to draw your arrow.

    It acts the same as a normal view - just has the added ability to have your commands add drawings to that canvas.

    It is iOS only though, you said your app was iPad - but then you also mention Android.

    If you do need android - there is paid module (not expensive) that achieves the same goal Android Canvas.
