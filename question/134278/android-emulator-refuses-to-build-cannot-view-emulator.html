  • I'll tell how I solved this problem in my machine.

    On windows, open "Windows Task Manager".
    You should see two "adb.exe" processes.
    Try to kill one of them. If it restarts, kill the other. In the end you should have just one process running.
    Start your application again.

    It worked for me. Hope it helps. ;]
    Cheers .
