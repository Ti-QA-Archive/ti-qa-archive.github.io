  • Akshay

    Please see the Maintaining a Custom AndroidManifest.xml guide for a resolution to the error in the log.

    To allow the app to launch, you probably need to delete your build files and force a full rebuild.

    To do this, delete the contents of the project's build&#x2F;android directory, select Edit Project in Titanium Developer and click the Save Changes button, to touch tiapp.xml which forces a full rebuild, and then launch the app.

    To understand the reason why this needs to be done, and why it isn't such a bad thing, see How does Titanium work?

    If this does not resolve it, please report back.

    Cheers
