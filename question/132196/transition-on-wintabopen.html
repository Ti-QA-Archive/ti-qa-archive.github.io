transition on win.tab.open

(ios)

ive searched all over but trying to way to have transitions with

win.tab.open(cf,{animated:true});

ive tried every combination i can think of placing

{transition:Titanium.UI.iPhone.AnimationStyle.FLIP_FROM_LEFT}

eg

win.tab.open(cf,{animated:true}, {transition:Titanium.UI.iPhone.AnimationStyle.FLIP_FROM_LEFT});

or

win.tab.open(cf,{animated:true,transition:Titanium.UI.iPhone.AnimationStyle.FLIP_FROM_LEFT});

but i always end up with

"Result of expression 'Titanium.UI.iPhone.AnimationStyle' [undefined] is not an object."

on event click - is it at all possible ?

  • Accepted Answer

    if you are Inside a Tab, then you cannot Flip. A Tab has a navigationGroup and calling Tab.open(win) is like calling navGroup.open(win); These do Not accept transitions. However you can use transitions when using win.open(animationConfig); or view.animate();
