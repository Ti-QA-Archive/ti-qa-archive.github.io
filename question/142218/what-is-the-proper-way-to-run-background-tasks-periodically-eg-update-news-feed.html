What is the proper way to run background tasks periodically, e.g. update news feed?

My app has something like a news feed and I want to update it regularly, mainly to set the correct badge for the news tab. It doesn't have to be perfect, i.e. I don't need the update to be executed perfectly every hour for example. However I still want the app to check the news feed from time to time.

Is there some generic/global event that I can bind to? An example of what I am looking for is "app start", "window change", "window focus", etc. (of course without having to go through every window and bind to it).
