  • If you are getting: "The selection cannot be launched and there are no recent launches" OR "builder.py" errors, etc, etc then this will fix them. Read on:

    I just figured this out after a lot of searching and NO real help. Poking in the dark I found out that to run the kitchen sink, you can do this:

    1. Locate the unzipped or untared kitchen sink folder in the "Local Filesystem" dropdown option in the left sidebar.

    2. Right click on the folder and select "Promote to Project . . ."

    3. Now click select the imported Kitchen Sink project (not the folder you imported in the local filesystem browser) and click the little play button that has a circle around it and is between the little bug and 3d box in the toolbar right above the project sidebar.

    Then the kitchen sink will run.
