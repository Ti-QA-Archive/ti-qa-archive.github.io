Creating Android Package

Hi i have creaeted my first android application. I want to give this app to my friends to use. But there is no option in the Titanium.But there is option of Distributing it in Android market. What needs to be done. Please help

    You can use the Distribution option to create an apk and you can give that to your friends for installing - the app will not automatically be published in some market.

    Also, when you build your application you will find an app.apk in your projects build folder:

    <path to titanium project>/build/android/bin/app.apk

    Hope that helps.
