    • It should be noted, that in 1.8.x, the currentPlaybackTime value returned from the player is in milliseconds, and the hack takes an integer. Divide your currentPlaybackTime by 1000, parse it to an int, and add or subtract the seconds you want to FF or REW. (example below shows 15 seconds back)

      MediaPlayer.setCurrentPlaybackTime(parseInt(MediaPlayer.currentPlaybackTime&#x2F;1000,10)-15);
