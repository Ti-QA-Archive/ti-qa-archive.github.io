Programmatically adding to a 'dp' size?

When I define a tableviewrow as eg. 50dp, then doing

tableviewrow.height = tableviewrow.height + 50, it becomes 0.

Do I have to use pixels, which works, but doesn't scale uniformly across devices, or am I doing it wrong?

    IMHO, the "best" approach is to not require the dp in the js source at all by making it the default units. Then you can just use integers or math in your js source as if they were pixels, but they will be interpreted as meaning dp unless you intentionally suffix with px instead. See here for the documention on this.
