How to remove app icon above tabgroup in Android?

Hello,

I'm using a tabGroup in an Android app which I'm testing on 4.4 with a Nexus 7.

Above the tabgroup is displayed my app icon which I don't want here, do you guys know how to remove it?

Thanks.

    You can disable it in your tiapp.xml, using the androidManifest settings <- all available in the docs for your self-taught training regimen.

    android:theme="@android:style/Theme.Holo.Light.NoActionBar.Fullscreen" >

    • <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
  <manifest>
    <application 
        android:debuggable="false"
        android:icon="@drawable/appicon" android:label="AppName"
        android:name="AppNameApplication" android:theme="@android:style/Theme.Holo.Light.NoActionBar.Fullscreen">
  </manifest>
</android>
