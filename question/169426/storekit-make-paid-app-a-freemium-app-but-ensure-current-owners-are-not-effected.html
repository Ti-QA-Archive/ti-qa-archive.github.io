Storekit: Make paid app a freemium app, but ensure current owners are not effected.

I added IAP to a paid app using the standard template from the example app, how do I ensure that current owners of the app are not effected when they upgrade? Do I need to change any of the code?

— asked April 18th 2014 by Michael Zaladonis
  • freemium
  • iap
  • in app purchase
  • storekit
0 Comments

  • It depends on how much content that you want to give the customers that have already bought the app. If you want to give them all or some of the IAP then you will need to change the code to give them the IAP for free. But if you are not going to give them any of the IAP's then you will not have to worry about changing the code, and only worry about complaining customers.
