Compress / Change Size of Images when taken from the camera

According to this Jira issue, Appcelerator are/will be working on a module to support resizing/compressing images taken from the camera.

http://jira.appcelerator.org/browse/TIMOB-4141

I know about the mostly undocumented methods on TiBlob, but looking for a cross platform solution, that will allow images to be resized/compressed.

Anyone know if it's possible to gain insight into when Appcelerator might be working/releasing these things?

  • I have resized images by adding them to a imageView that is smaller than the image and the converting the imageView to a blob. It did not compress the image, but it made it small enough for me to upload the images without a timeout
