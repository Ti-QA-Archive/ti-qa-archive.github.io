  • Your problem is it's behaving exactly as the docs describe. Titanium.UI.Picker

    The controls are different on iOS & Android. On Android, you get a combobox-like control, but on iOS you get the spinner control.

    If you want a combobox, you should look at iOS examples, like MAKING A COMBO-BOX IN TITANIUM
