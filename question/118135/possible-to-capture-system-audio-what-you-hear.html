Possible to capture system audio ("what-you-hear")?

Is it possible using Titanium to capture system audio ("what-you-hear") via the audiorecorder. If not, using some other method?

  • You can play and record at the same time, but the recording will only pick up the sound entering the microphone (which may be picked up from the speaker).

    So, you can't 'capture' the direct audio coming from the system.
