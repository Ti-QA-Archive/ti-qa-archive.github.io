  • Please be more specific when posting your questions about which platforms and devices you're targeting. It usually matters and helps folks answer your questions more quickly and completely.

    The decimal pad type keyboard is not supported on Android; it's an iOS only type. The number pad should work on Android on both phones and tablets. Support for the keyboard types on iPads and tablets varies and they often don't render as clearly different from each other as they do on the phone devices.
