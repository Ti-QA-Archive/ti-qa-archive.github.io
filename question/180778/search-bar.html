Search bar

I have created a menu bar using view.I have placed a search icon on the view.When I click on it, I want searchbar to appear and remain hidden otherwise.Can any one help me with it.
Thanks in advance..

  • You can use setSearchHidden method to hide search bar. When you click on search icon, you can make the search bar visible by sending false to setSearchHidden method.

    
window.addEventListener('focus', function() {
    tableView.setSearchHidden(true);
});

tableView.setSearchHidden(false);

    Hope this helps you.
