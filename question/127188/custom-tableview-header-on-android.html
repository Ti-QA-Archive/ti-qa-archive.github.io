Custom TableView header on Android

I'm trying to make the KitchenSink _table_view_api_customheader.js example work on Android but in vain. The functionality I want is to have the tableview header remain visible during scrolling. It works great on iPhone. The example is currently commented out for Android most likely because there is an issue with tableviewsection's headerview not showing up on Android (there is already a ticket about this bug). headertitle works all right on Android, and for the time being I wouldn't mind headertitle stay visible during scrolling but on Android it beheaves just like the very first row of the table, and scrolls away.

Does anybody have any idea how to solve this? I'm afraid that even when they fix the headerview issue of tableviewsection it will scroll away (?) Of course I can place labels (or a view) just above the table but it doesn't look good.

Thanks.

  • This is the typical behavior for a ListView (the Android equivalent of Titanium TableViews). Most native developers who encounter this problem use a solution very similar to what you mentioned. They typically just create a custom header view that sits above the table in the view hierarchy. You could do the same in Titanium.
