save image from gallery to sql

Ok, I have a sql data base with tons of info, that I am displaying. I want the user to pick a picture from either the gallery, or take a picture and save it to the gallery or save a image from the internet to the gallery. My problem is How do I link a picture from the galley to an ID number in sql. So when the user chooses that ID number they just see the picture they choose? Is it possible, or do I have to think of some other way of doing this? Saving pictures to sql would be stupid. This is for Iphone.

  • You really need to take control of the pictures, not leave them in the gallery. The reason is that the gallery is independent from your app and you can't control if someone deletes the picture. I would suggest you copy the picture to a directory in your app and just store the filename on your SQL, not the photo.
