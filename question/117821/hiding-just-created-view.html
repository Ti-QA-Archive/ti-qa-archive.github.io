Hiding just created View

Hi, I have such a problem on android.
The app has next structure:

tabGroup->tab->window->(inside this window several Views)

The issue is that when I am creating and adding new views. they are appear over the previous. I am trying to hide them without any effect:

    this.galleryInfoView= Ti.UI.createView({
        id:         'galleryInfoView'
    });
    this.parentWindow.add(this.galleryInfoView);
    this.galleryInfoView.hide();

But if I later apply .hide() to the same object on some event it works fine. So how can I hide it initially ?

I suspect that it is somehow connected with threads. May be object is not ready yet when I am trying to hide it at the moment after creating ?
