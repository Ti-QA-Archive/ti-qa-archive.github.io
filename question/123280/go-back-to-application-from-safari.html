Go back to application from Safari

I have a link in my mobile application. If customer clicks the URL, it will bring customer to Safari and load the web page. After customer finishes browsing the webpage how can him or her go back to my application? Go back button in Safari is unclickable. Any solution? Many thanks

  • Accepted Answer

    Here is webview usage :

    var webview = Titanium.UI.createWebView({url:'http://www.google.com'});
    var window = Titanium.UI.createWindow();
    window.add(webview);
    window.open({modal:true});

    You can take a look for more details here
