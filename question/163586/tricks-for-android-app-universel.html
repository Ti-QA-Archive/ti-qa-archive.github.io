    Development time issues

    First, dump the android emulator for genymotion.

    http://www.appcelerator.com/blog/2013/12/using-genymotion-with-titanium-3-2/

    Second, take a look at TiShadow. Even if you use genymotion as your emulator, this is worth having. http://tishadow.yydigital.com/

    Android Sizing Issues

    Use Density specific assets

    http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/3.0/#!/guide/Using_density-specific_resources_on_Android

    Size your views correctly

    http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/latest/#!/guide/Layouts,_Positioning,_and_the_View_Hierarchy

    Post Properly formatted questions

    • I also worry about, when I test with a nexus one everything is ok when I test with samsung s4 emulator, the text is small

      be sure you are using dp for your default on android:

      tiapp.xml

      <property name="ti.ui.defaultunit" type="string">dp</property>

      If you are working from an older sdk's project, this may not have been set, or set to something different.

    • Automatic image generator? Naw, you got to do the work. There is an asset generate for your icons splash screens for titanium though http://ticons.fokkezb.nl/

      To give you a rough idea on sizing:

      android ldpi = ~120dpi = our shop doesn't design for this anymore
      android mdpi = ~160dpi = pretty close to standard iphone images
      android hdpi = ~240dpi
      android xhdpi = ~320dpi = pretty close to retina iphone images

      and there are new xxhdpi devices out/coming.

      For many of your images, you can use a 9patch images. Malcolm has a great starter article http://www.tidev.io/2014/02/12/android-splash-screens-using-9-patch-images/
