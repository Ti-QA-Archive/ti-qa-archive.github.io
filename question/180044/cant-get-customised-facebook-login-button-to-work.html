  • Hi

    So I had a look around and found a couple of answers here:

    • https://developer.appcelerator.com/question/119719/
    • https://developer.appcelerator.com/question/179956/

    From the code I have seen in these answers and also looking at your example, here are a couple of the issues I found.

    Firstly it seems you aren't closing your click event on your custom button, might just be a typo.

    Inside your click event, according to one of the answers, you should call "fb.authorize()".

    Lastly, I always use "===" vs "==" and here is a good thread to explain why: does it matter which equals operator i use in javascript comparisons it's completely your choice, but could cause problems down the line comparing certain statements so its a good habit to form early on.

    Hope this helps.
