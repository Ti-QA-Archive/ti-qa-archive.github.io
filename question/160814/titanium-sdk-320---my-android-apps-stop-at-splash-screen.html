  • I have found the SOLUTION.

    Just go to SDCARD/appID

    Ex: sdcard/com.app.mrt

    And delete this file "DEPLOY.JSON"

    Run again and ENJOY!!!

  • There are people around offering different solutions, but I found out that when I upgraded to 3.2.0 it got rid of my older versions of Android; namely Android 2.3. I noticed that when I cleaned and tried to recompile in 3.1.3 it said it required Android version 2.3 so I ok'd and the project compiled and ran fine as it did. Then when I went back and compiled in 3.2.0 it worked perfectly. So I'm assuming it has something to do with needing an older version of Android included during compilation.
