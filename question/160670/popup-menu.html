  • SubMenus are not supported. You can log a request in Jira.

    I have tested it with no success on Titanium 3.1.3. Here is my test code:

    <Menu id="menu" platform="android">
  <MenuItem id="menu1" class="action" />
  <MenuItem id="menu2" class="overflow" />
  <MenuItem id="menu3" class="overflow" />
  <MenuItem id="menu4" class="overflow" >
    <Menu id="submenu">
       <MenuItem id="menu4a" class="overflow" />
       <MenuItem id="menu4b" class="overflow" />
       <MenuItem id="menu4c" class="overflow" />
    </Menu>
  </MenuItem>
</Menu>

    According to the Android Guide submenus can be created by adding a menu element as a child of an item element. But it seems not to work with the Titanium Menu Proxy.
