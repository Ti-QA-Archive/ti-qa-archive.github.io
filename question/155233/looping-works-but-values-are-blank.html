Looping works, but values are blank.

I am simply trying to loop through my results and I can't seem to figure out this issue. Maybe someone here can shine some light on this problem.

I loop through all my results, yet the data is not showing up correctly. For instance if I have three rows back from my query it will only show 3 pieces of data, if I have two rows come back..only two pieces of data.     You are iterating through those statements within

     for(var i = 0; i < rowCount;i++) {`

    three times and in each iteration you are issuing a `rows.next()' so in total you are iterating through the rows 9 times. The most common way of iterating through the resultset is like this;

    while (rows.isValidRow()){

    /// do your stuff with the current row

  rows.next();
}
