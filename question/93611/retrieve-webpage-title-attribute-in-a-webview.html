Retrieve webPage Title attribute in a WebView

Hi there,

I am building a basic WebBrowser in my iPad app, I would like to display the Title of the webpage being rendered in the WebView.

Is there a way/method to retrieve a page's title from a WebView?

  • With Aaron's help I did what I wanted. To get the Title from the WebView you can use the evalJs method on the WebView load event.

    webView.addEventListener("load", function(e) {

  // Sets the title in a Label
  titleLabel.text = webView.evalJS("document.title");
  // hides the activity animation in the NavBar after the webview is loaded
  actAjax.hide();
});
