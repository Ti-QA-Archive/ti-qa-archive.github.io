iPhone App on iPad

Hello, I'm currently working on a large iPhone app that was created using Titanium 1.7.2. When testing this app on and iPad, it opens as a full iPad app and not as a simulated iPhone app on the iPad. Is there anyway to fix this (turn off universal builds).

— asked October 10th 2011 by Byron Carasco

    Looks like the first setting of universal app sticks even though you uncheck iPad later. Try creating a new project for iPhone only, copy your Resources folder and build.
