  • I would recommend the Gimp. Especially in its new single-window mode. It has everything you need and more for manual image manipulation.

    I would also highly recommend installing ImageMagick for command-line processing of images. If you're trying to support a variety of screen densities on android, it can be helpful to start with a high-res image and use ImageMagick to convert to the various sizes you need.

    I provide some examples of that in the tutorial I'm working on, GrillTime. Part 2 has what you're looking for.
