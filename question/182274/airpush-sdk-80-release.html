Airpush SDK 8.0 Release

Hi Appcelerator Forums,

www.airpush.com/product-release-standard-sdk-8/

Today we are happy to announce one of the largest and most important SDKs upgrades we have ever released. Airpush’s new SDK 8.0 includes one of the most advanced video ad units that the mobile industry has ever seen, new optimizations to our advanced Abstract Banners, removal of our EULA from our Standard SDK and dramatic improvements in ease of integration via a completely new Portal Help Section.

New Ad Unit - HD Video Abstracts
Insert high-definition video ads into your banner placements and make interstitial level CPMs. HD Video Abstracts allow you to add closeable videos to the bottom of your app or mobile web sessions, driving huge earnings and a quality user experience.
New, exciting video ad placement.
Quality ads from premium advertisers.
Huge video earnings in a banner placement.
Easy closeability.

New Abstract Banner Optimizations
You spoke and we listened. Abstract Banners have been revised to reduce their overall size, limit the number of times a single ad is showed to each user and maximize your earnings.
A dramatic reduction in the use of animation at the top of the screen.
Larger, clearer close buttons.
New ‘Maximizer Mode’ to drive even higher earnings.

Improvements to All SDKs
Each of our SDKs - Standard, Bundle 1 and Bundle 2 - have been improved in order to increase earnings and decrease objections to user installation.
Removal of EULA from Standard SDK.
Continued per-install payment of up to $.04 + CPM earnings for Bundle 1.
Continued per-install payment of up to $.05 Worldwide for Bundle 2.

Easier Integrations
At Airpush, we have always had a developer-first focus on driving reliable revenue streams to help you grow your business. We are happy to report dramatic improvements in our help desk support, including an all new searchable FAQ section on the portal.
Quicker Help Desk response times.
New Portal FAQ section.
Searchable database of solutions.
