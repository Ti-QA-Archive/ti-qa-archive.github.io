  • If you were the device, how would you determine which action was to take place on a left to right swipe?

    You could turn off the swipe functionality of the drawer, and only use the button.

    You could try to cheat, and bring the edges of your scrollable view in a few pixels.

    But more likely, read the documentation for the drawer and try setting OpenDrawerGestureMode to either OPEN_MODE_PANNING_NAVBAR or OPEN_MODE_BEZEL_PANNING_CENTERWINDOW. You will need to do the same for CloseDrawerGestureMode
