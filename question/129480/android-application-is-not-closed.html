[Android] Application is not Closed??

Hello folks… i just notice something.. i dont know why is this happening but.. when I close the whole app.. it is still running in application.. and it has RAM.. although i closed my app..in my app.js.. i have exitOnClose.. also i see that RAM grows as I open new win.. but not reduced.. even.. i close the window.. i have this code in every window

win.addEventListener('close',function(e){
    win.close();
});

why is this happening?? is this normal or not?? if not.. how to solve this problem??

    This is normal. If you are interested in the underlying reason, check out this post on StackOverflow regarding the topic of "quitting" apps on Android. It includes a quote from Android engineer Romain Guy: http://stackoverflow.com/questions/2033914/quitting-an-application-is-that-frowned-upon

    The reason RAM grows but does not reduce is that aps on Android will typically take all that is alotted to them and keep it in terms of resources. Android, behind the scenes, will free these resources only when necessary. In other words, Android's app lifecycle takes care of managing resources and actually quitting apps for you.
