  • The early preview beta is only available to paid subscribers, though Indie counts as well as Professional. When it comes out of beta, it will be available to everyone. I think the goal was for that to occur yet in June, so they must be behind schedule. I don't follow the Blackberry SDK progress, so I have no idea if they have a new projected release date. If you really need access now, consider a monthly Indie subscription ($49).
