  • Hi

    The ability to show settings in any way was removed from iOS5 onwards.

    It cannot be done from Titanium. If there is an undocumented API then your app would be liable for exclusion from the App store as that is a violation of the agreement you signed up to with Apple.

    As to how they have seemingly achieved it - unknown.
