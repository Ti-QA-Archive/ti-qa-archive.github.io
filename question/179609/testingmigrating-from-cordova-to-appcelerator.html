Testing/Migrating from Cordova to Appcelerator

We have been developing apps for a client using Cordova. This client wants to move toward Appcelerator for testing, then future development. This leaves us needing to answer two questions.

First, can we use Appcelerator to do functional testing of Cordova apps?

Second, what steps would we need to do to migrate existing Cordova apps to Appcelerator?

Thanks!

  • You can simple put your HTML stuff in a TiUIWebView. But this is an ugly way, because you inherit all problems/issues from phonegap. Additional you must rewrite your phonegap modules.

    Titanium uses a other way to generate apps. Phonegap embeded all stuff in a web container, Titanium uses native elements. You solution depends of seriousity of your client …
