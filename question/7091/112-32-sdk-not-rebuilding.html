  • Hey Jeff,

    I've ran into that too, seems like a few others have as well.

    https://developer.appcelerator.com/question/6831/further-to—sdk-11-not-seeing-source-changes

    http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/6261/suggestion-checkbox-in-developer-ignore-pre-compiled-app-and-force-recompile

    Unless Appcelerator makes a change in the code, the best way I've found to deal with it is to open the Terminal, switch to [myappdir]/build/iphone, and run:

    xcodebuild clean -configuration Debug

    Stinks to do it each time, but does ensure you always get a full rebuild.
