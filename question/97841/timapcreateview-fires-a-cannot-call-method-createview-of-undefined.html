  • Raymond

    • shut down all emulators and Titanium Developer
    • start the android executable, select the "Virtual Devices" menu, and delete all the entries in the list using the "Delete…" button on the right
    • delete the contents of the subdirectories in [PROJECT]/build/[platform]/[subdirectory]. Hence, delete everything in the directories named assets, bin, gen, lib,res,src`, but not the directories themselves.
    • start Titanium Developer
    • on the "Edit" project screen, click the "Save Changes" button to force a full rebuild
    • on the "Run Emulator" screen, ensure that "SDK: APIs 2.2" is selected (the ones that are not prefixed by "APIs" do not support maps)
    • launch your app

    Hope this helps
