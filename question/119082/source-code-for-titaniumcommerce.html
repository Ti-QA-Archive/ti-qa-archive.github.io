  • The source is available for some sample Ti+Plus modules here, but not the source for the Ti+Plus Commerce modules. AFAIK, the source is not available for those and they are only available as compiled modules – and only to those who are on a paid subscription of at least the Small Business Edition.

    Since I'm not a SBE subscriber either, I don't know for sure the source is not available. But my impression is you only get the compiled version. I could be wrong.
