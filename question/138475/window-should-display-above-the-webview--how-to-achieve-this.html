  • I think in your case you do not need a window, a view will do the Job and if using a view you can use zIndex to make sure its on top. The last added view will be on top of others, unless you had a zIndex.
    Using zIndex & modal makes no sense, zIndex will be ignored.

    Also zIndex is only applicable to Views wothin one window but not between Windows.
