Bind a click on a word or phrase inside a label text

  1. Application Type: mobile (ios)
  2. latest 3.4.0GA
  3. Platform: iOS 8.x

I'm finding Titanium UI View's box model very difficult to work with. There are no inline elements, everything's a rectangular block!

I want to be able to highlight some amount of non-continuous words or phrases in the middle of a paragraph…and click on them. I can do the highlighting with attributed strings, but I can't seem to be able to bind a click on each individual attribute string. The paragraph potentially spans multiple lines and I just need to click a few words in it.

Essentially, I want:

<label>Hello, my favorite fruits are <b>apple</b>, <b>pears</b> and <b>bananas</b>. I like them because they are yummy.</label>

while being able to bind tap/touch events on apple, pears and bananas separately.

I'm stumped. Spent a good number of hours trying to figure it out. Any ideas?

PS. Focusing on iOS only for now, but a cross platform one that works on Android would be nice…
