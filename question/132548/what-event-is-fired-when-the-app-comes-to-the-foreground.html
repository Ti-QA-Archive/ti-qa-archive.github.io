What event is fired when the app comes to the foreground?

I'm looking to bind some lines of code to an event that occurs everytime the app is opened (regardless of whether or not the app is already running in the bg on iOS). What event can I listen for?

    There's also a resumed event. In my understanding, the resume event gets triggered only when the app gets resumed from background state, i.e. after pressing the home button and starting again the app. The other is always triggered, either after the resume one, or when the app resumes from standby. There's a presentation from Blain Hamon that briefly depicts this: http://www.slideshare.net/slideshow/embed_code/9479163
