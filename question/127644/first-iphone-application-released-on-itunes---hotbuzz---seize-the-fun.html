First iPhone application released on iTunes - HotBuzz - Seize the fun!

Hello Titans!
Just wanted to tell you that I (as WebFix) just released my first iPhone application on iTunes called HotBuzz. It’s free: http://www.hotbuzzapp.com.
“Find the best party tonight. Discover places ranked as the hottest by people who are actually there. Seize the fun! With HotBuzz you participate in a real-time voting system that gives a new measure of specific events. No log-in necessary.”
Not a single line of Objective-C written, courtesy of Titanium Mobile. Many many thanks, you gods! :)
Features:

  • custom tab bar
  • custom navbar/title bar with logo
  • custom activity indicator
  • push notifications with Urban Airship
  • login(once) and wall posting with Facebook
  • login(once) and checkin with Foursquare
  • middle tier for linking various API-s and the application with PHP: JSON formatting, data stripping, image processing
  • uses Eventful API, Foursquare API, Factual API

Modules used: admob in conjunction with iAd.

I have to admit I did a lot of research and I spent many nights neglecting my lovely wife.
Thank you all for posting great answers and replying with great ideas.

I’m ready for suggestions and critique.

Code strong! Stay hungry, my friends!

P.S.: I also did my own graphic design.
