    • Normally with apple, you supply something like com.myname.myapp as the app id. Apples provisioning adds a group of numbers on the front. The app id in titanium remains com.myname.myapp and this is the appID that itunes/device expects the app to be published under, not BJT63V9DC5.com.mycompany.myappname
