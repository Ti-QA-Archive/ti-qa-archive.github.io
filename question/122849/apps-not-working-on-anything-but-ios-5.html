Apps not working on anything but iOS 5

I've been running into this issue for the last few days now, and I'm stumped. I'm running Titanium mobile sdk 1.7.1, and while running the basic kitchen sink project, it only fully loads on iOS 5. On all others, it shows the kitchen sink loading image, and then closes to the home screen. I'm extremely lost as to why the apps would load on iOS 5, but not any of the earlier iOS sdks.   • It's because Apple doesn't want you building apps for the App Store on anything but Xcode 4.0.2 and iOS 4.3, so they disallow you to build anything but iOS 5 on Xcode 4.2 for the time being.

    I simply uninstalled Xcode 4.2 and re-installed Xcode 4.0.2 from the iOS Developer Center and it worked perfectly on both my iOS 4.3 device and iOS 5 beta 3 device. I could also distribute to the App Store fine.
