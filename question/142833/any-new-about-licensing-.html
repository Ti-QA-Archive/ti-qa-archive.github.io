  • If you don't want paid modules (like rendering shadows , push notifications etc.) or statistics you don't need to pay anything.

    Appcelerator is a great company which knows that a developer can't handle paying licenses for developing and makes it free with no limitations.

    However , If you want to speed the process of development , like free code support or the resource store you must pay.
