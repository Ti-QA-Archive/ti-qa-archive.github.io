Xcode 4 with Titanium

I just installed Titanium on my new iMac. I've also installed Xcode 4

But any project I create or imported projects they all does not work.

The samulator opens but in Titanium gives me

[INFO] Application has exited from Simulator

So does titanium support xcode 4? or what is the problem

  • Titanium only supports XCode 4 with the latest SDK 1.6.1 release. If you are building using anything lower than 1.6.1 (1.6.0, 1.5.0, etc.) you will get errors.
