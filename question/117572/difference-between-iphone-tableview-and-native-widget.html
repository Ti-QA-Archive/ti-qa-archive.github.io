Difference between iphone TableView and native widget?

I think I've found a difference in its behavior, which causes a strange appearance. The Titanium TableView does not request & create remote images in the rows until the table has stopped moving. This contrasts with native apps, such as the App Store app, where images will appear as the table is scrolling.

In Titanium, however, one must scroll the table, and then stop it, in order to see the newly-displayed rows fill in their information.

Anyone else see this?
