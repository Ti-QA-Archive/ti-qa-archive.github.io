Scroll View style in Android

Hi,

Is it possible to show scroll view in android same as iOS. By default it shows arrow and no dots in bottom. For example: We have dots in the bottom of the scroll view of iOs. I want similar thing in Android.

  • Assuming you mean ScrollableView rather than a ScrollView, you can set showPagingControl only on iOS to disable the arrows. They are not shown by default.

    The dots indicator is a iOS specific component. You would have to create your own on Android or use a cross-platform pagingControl. There are Alloy widgets on gittio that you could use: http://gitt.io/search?q=pagingcontrol
