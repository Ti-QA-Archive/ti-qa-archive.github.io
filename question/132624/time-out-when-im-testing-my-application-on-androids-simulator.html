  • I think it's facing problem with creating AVD or some thing related to AVD. are you able to see your emulator started and than after time out occured or emulator is not getting started ?

    If emulator is not getting started just start AVD manager than remove all the available emulator and let Titanium create new AVD when your launch your application.

    If emulator is getting started and then time out is occuring than start AVD manager, start the emulator emplicitly and let emulator get started completely than launch your application as android emualtor takes much more time.

    Hope this helps.
