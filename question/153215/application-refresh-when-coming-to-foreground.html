Application refresh when coming to foreground

If I switch from titanium application's loggedin page to another app and coming back to my app by clicking icon after long time. whether I am able to refresh the page while switching back to my page?
Titanium SDK:3.0.2
platform :ios

  • Hello vivek,

    You can use pause, resume kind of events for Refreshing the page.

    Try below way : 

    //resume event
Ti.App.addEventListener("resume", function(e){
    //refreshing content here
});


//pause event
Ti.App.addEventListener("pause", function(e){
    //refreshing content here
});

    Let me know if it helps.
