Android 4x Supported?

I've enabled the following:

&lt;supports-screens android:anyDensity=&quot;false&quot;&#x2F;&gt;

Most looks good with the exception of android devices running 4.x which seem to squeeze the UI elements and make the UI not look right.

Is Android 4.x not supported for this and must I make special UI adjustments for 4.x?

— asked June 15th 2012 by Lee Jenkins

  • Android 4.x works fine – we're running our app on the Motorola Xoom and Galaxy Nexus.

    We don't use the support-screens tag. But we do a lot of dynamic sizing of elements based on screen density so that things look good on all android devices as well as iOS.

    We don't do anything special for the 4.x devices, but we've found that the Nexus has a very high resolution display (it is designated xhdpi by Android). We have special image resources for that high resolution.
