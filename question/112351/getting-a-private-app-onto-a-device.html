Getting a private app onto a device

We are making an app that is meant for internal use inside a company. Once the app is done, how would we go about getting the app onto the employees iphones and ipads?

Does the app store have a private area or something?

    There's a specific program run by Apple called the Developer Enterprise Program that I think may be what you're looking for.

    Ad-hoc distribution is meant for distribution to quality assurance test teams which explains the max 100 device id's it can be distributed to, the expiration of the provisioning profile, and the need to redistribute provisioning profiles when adding new devices - all of which would be limiting and cumbersome of distributing internally for the purpose of internal use only.
