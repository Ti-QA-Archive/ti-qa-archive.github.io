Just released an update to our app using ti.storekit and had a great experience

We just updated Eyejot to 1.02 after Apple forced us to offer in-app purchasing of our two SaaS service levels. While we don't anticipate our iPhone users will take advantage of the new functionality (since our PRO+ costs $99), it was, nonetheless, required. Thankfully we used Ti.storekit and the experience was awesome! Thanks Titanium.

I was able to add the purchasing functionality in just a few hours and it worked perfectly. I especially liked the ability to run in store sandbox mode from the simulator running iOS5.

Also super happy that some of the other recent module updates brought our app size down from over 20MB to around 10MB! Big, happy change.

If you'd like to try learn more about Eyejot you can find it in the app store.

— asked February 18th 2012 by David Geller
