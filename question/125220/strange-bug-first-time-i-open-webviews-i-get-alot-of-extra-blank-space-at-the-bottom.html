Strange BUG: First time I open webviews, I get alot of extra blank space at the bottom.

This isnt a question as much as a bug-report. It seems the Ti Devs want bug to be posted here first?

I have a scrollview with about 20 webviews contained within it (stacked on top of each other).

The first time I run the app and load the scrollview, some of the webviews (randomly it seems) will be very tall, out of proportion to the content within them (I put a CSS red border on the .body to outline them and make sire its the webview thats adding the extra space).

But then if I back out of the scrollview and re-enter that scrollview or any other scrollview containing webviews they they render properly as they should.

This strange error in calculating height only seems to happen the very first time a scrollview with many webviews is loaded.

  • This is an awful hack, but our solution was to get the content height from the document after the page was rendered, then set the height of the webview after some time has elapsed…

    setTimeout(function(){
    $.myFirstWebview.height = $.myFirstWebview.evalJS("document.height;");
    $.mySecondWebview.height = $.mySecondWebview.evalJS("document.height;");
},250);
