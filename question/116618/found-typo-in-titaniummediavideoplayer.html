Found typo in Titanium.Media.VideoPlayer

Hope I can save a lot of debugging time out there.

In this doc:
http://developer.appcelerator.com/apidoc/mobile/latest/Titanium.Media.VideoPlayer-object

The property: "mediaControlStyle"
Should actually be: "movieControlStyle"

Found this out thanks to this doc:
http://developer.appcelerator.com/apidoc/mobile/latest/Titanium.Media-module.html under the properties VIDEOCONTROL*
