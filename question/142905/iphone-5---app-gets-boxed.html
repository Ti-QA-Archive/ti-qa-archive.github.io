iPhone 5 - App gets boxed

On the iPhone 5, my app is getting, if the right term is, "viewboxed". It centers itself with a height of 460 (excluding the status bar).

My code is as follows:

var tabGroup = Ti.UI.createTabGroup({
    width: Ti.UI.FILL,
    height: Ti.UI.FILL
});

    var win = Ti.UI.createWindow({
        width: Ti.UI.FILL,
        height: Ti.UI.FILL,
        backgroundColor: '#D9DCE8',
        navBarHidden: true,
        tabBarHidden: true,
        title: 'Window'
    });

    tabGroup.addTab(Ti.UI.createTab({
        title: 'Window',
        window: win
    }));

tabGroup.open();

Any clue why this could be happening, since Ti.UI.FILL should fill up the entire viewport, right? Any way of fixing this?

TiSDK: 2.1.3.v20120928203111
