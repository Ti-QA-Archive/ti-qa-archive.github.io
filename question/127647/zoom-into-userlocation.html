  • Here are my settings

    var MVViewMap = Ti.Map.createView
    ({
    mapType:"satellite,
    region: {latitude: currLat,
    longitude: currLong, latitudeDelta: 0.002,
    longitudeDelta:0.002},
    animate: true,
    regionFit: true,
    annotations: annimationArray
    userLocation: true
    });

    The key here is the region and the zoom is controlled by the latitude and longitude deltas.

    Hope this helps
