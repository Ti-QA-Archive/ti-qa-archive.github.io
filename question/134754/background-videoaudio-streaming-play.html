  • Which info.plist are you adding the code you mentioned above?

    In app.js:

    Ti.Media.audioSessionMode = Ti.Media.AUDIO_SESSION_MODE_PLAYBACK;

    In TiApp.xml

    <iphone>
  <backgroundModes>
    <mode>audio</mode>
  </backgroundModes>
</iphone>
