  • Hi Sabari,

    You can use 'type' property of pickerView for defining what type of picker view you want.Like:

    var picker = Ti.UI.createPicker({
  type:PICKER_TYPE_DATE_AND_TIME,
  minDate:new Date(2009,0,1),
  maxDate:new Date(2014,11,31),
  value:new Date(2014,3,12),
  top:50
});

    Other types of pickerView can be : http://docs.appcelerator.com/titanium/latest/#!/api/Titanium.UI.Picker-property-type

    Thanks.
