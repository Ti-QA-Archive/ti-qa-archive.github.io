Android Image issues

Hi all,

I have an app with a tableView with 50 rows.

Each row in the tableView has 6 imageViews, 5kb each.

So a total of 300 images and 1500kb.

iOS had no problem running the app, but android keeps crashing due to memory problems.

I tried testing with 10 rows instead of 50 (on android) - it was slightly better but kept crashing…

Any suggestions on how to resolve this?

  • Because you have not posted the actual error from DDMS, its impossible to give you completely accurate information in return. However, take a look at the Android specific application properties specifically ti.android.threadstacksize as possibly a way to work around your memory problem:

    <property name="ti.android.threadstacksize" type="int">42000</property>

    • for the record, 42k is extremely high and should only be used for testing.
      If you find that it works, methodically bring the number down until you crash again.

      That way you have an idea just what your apps resource draw is.

      The other thing you could do, is in the loop to populate your rows, add

      Ti.API.info('Available Memory: '+Ti.Platform.availableMemory);

      which will output the available memory to the logs.
