FHONK - A Titanium App Approved

Fhonk is the latest, ‘must-have’ social networking accessory. This new iPhone app integrates with Facebook, but gives you functionalities that have never been seen before, taking social networking to a brand new level.

  • Access Facebook and add all your usual likes, comments, posts, uploads, updates, photos etc
  • For the first time ever, you are able to ‘graffiti’ on your own profile or someone else’s wall
  • Chat with your buddies
  • Track or find your Facebook buddies in real time on a map (Great for parents to keep track of their kids too)
  • you can even send personalised audio messages to you fhonk buddies

Don’t just Facebook… it’s time to Fhonk! One app does it all!

We could not have done it with out you Titanium…
