Blurry Text with TableViewRow and Labels iOS

Hi,

I'm having a an issue where label text anti-aliasing is quite blurry in tableViewRows compared to that of just a plain label in the view.

The top set of characters is from the tableView and the bottom the plain label. You can noticeably see the difference with curves and even the sharp edges of the K.

I am curious as to why this might be occurring, if this is a known issue, and possibly how to fix it. I have read around and found posts about auto height and width causing these issues, but I tried fixed widths and heights for both my cells, my table and my labels but with no luck.

I am on Titanium 1.7 and testing on iOs simulator.

Thank you

  • Did you wrap your label with a view? Sometimes the view object makes the text blur, same thing happened to me before. What I did was I removed the view and I use only tableviewrow and it fixed the blurry text. Hope this help you.

  • Thanks Memark,

    I actually fixed this myself after a few hours of head scratching. What I did was first create the label with auto height. Then I grabbed the height of the label added 1px and used that as the row height for the table. Now all text is crisp to how it should be. Not sure the cause however I was thinking the 1px row separator squishes the label's height down and thats why it's causing the blur. But I'm new to Titanium and could be far off :D Thanks for your reply!
