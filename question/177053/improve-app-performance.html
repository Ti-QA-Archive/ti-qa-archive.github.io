  • Hi Kusuma.

    these are nice meaningless general questions that you ask here, expecting clear answers.

    Imagine you were asked these questions and you should answer them!

    Could you give those answers?

    It's as if you were writing a letter and sending it to your car repair shop.

    My car is too slow and rattles, please fix it without seeing the car or knowing the manufacturer or type.

    Even in the previous questions you were posting, you were asked to give more information so that someone can help you effectively.

    So think again about how you ask your questions to get satisfied and the informations needed to achieve this.

    For your current problem, for example, the following information would be helpful:

    • Which mobile OS Versions and Devices you are testing with?
    • Is it a classic or Alloy App?
    • What does your app and how is it designed?
    • What exactly is the problem? The performance, the native look, or both?
    • What features are too slow, or what's the problem with the look?
    • What have you done to identify or fix the problems?
    • Show us the code or layout of these problematic points!

    If you have questions about several topics, consider to ask several questions.

    Have a nice day.

    Greetings,

    Matthias
