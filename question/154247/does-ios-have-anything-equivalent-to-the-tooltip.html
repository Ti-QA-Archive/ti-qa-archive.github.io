  • Well, a tooltip is traditionally something that pops up when you 'hover' over the item. A 'touch' interface has no concept of a 'hover' as you need to make contact with the device. The 'touchstart' and 'touchend' events are the nearest you are going to get. It is relatively each to make a view appear over an item when a 'touch' event is triggered.
