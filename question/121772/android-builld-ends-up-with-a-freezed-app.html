  • i had the same problem, you have to deactivate fastdev in your tiapp.xml by adding the following line to it

    <property name="ti.android.fastdev" type="bool">false</property>

    don't know why, but it stopped working for me all of the sudden, hanging on the splash-screen, seems it's the same issue you have
