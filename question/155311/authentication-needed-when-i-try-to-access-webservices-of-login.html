    Hi

    Your header syntax looks fine - as long as the two values are correct of course.

    Make sure though that you place the setRequestHeader beteen the open and send, like this;

    xhr.open('POST', url);
xhr.setRequestHeader('Authorization', 'Basic ' + Ti.Utils.base64encode($.email.value+':'+$.password.value));
xhr.send(data);
