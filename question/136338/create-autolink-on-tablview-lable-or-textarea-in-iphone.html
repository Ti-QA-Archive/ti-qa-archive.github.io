    • Try this
      Text area is used in IOS to make autolink

      var textarea = Ti.UI.createTextArea({
    top:10,
    left:10,
    right:10,
    bottom:10,
    editable:false,
    value:'some data whith link http://www.google.com',
    font:{fontSize:15},
    autoLink:Ti.UI.AUTODETECT_ALL
});
