  • Accepted Answer

    Ti.Platform.openURL(&#39;tel:999&#39;);

    That will open up the keypad with the number 999 pre-populated. You can concatenate the "tel:" and another phone number to populate the keypad with a different phone number. I'm not sure if it would work without a phone number to call though.
