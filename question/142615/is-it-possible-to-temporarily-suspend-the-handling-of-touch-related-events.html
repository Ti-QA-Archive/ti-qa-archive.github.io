Is it possible to temporarily suspend the handling of touch-related events?

Hi everyone,

is there a Titanium equivalent to the iOS methods beginIgnoringInteractionEvents and endIgnoringInteractionEvents? These methods seem to be quite handy when the app is e.g. busy loading data or doing animation stuff. I can't find anything in the documentation, but maybe someone has a good idea how to imitate these methods. I know I could temporarily add a transparent view that blocks touch-related events, however I believe there must be a better way.

Thank you,
