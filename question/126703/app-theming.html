App Theming

I developed my android app, everything works exactly how I want it to. However I would like to design something in photoshop and make my app look the way I want it to now, so its got a similar look and feel as our website does. For example I am using tabs, which I could change, however I was hoping to be able to make the two tabs smaller right justified and then place a logo in the left corner.

Ultimately Im just confused with theming not sure where to start.

  • To fully customise the interface, especially with tabs, you will have to build your own UI and window/view frame logic. There are a few ways of doing this, but the best place is to just start putting things together to see if the app works the way you need it to - with 'pseudo-tabs' or whatever, then drop the design in later.

  • Hi,

    for elements such as navbar, buttons and labels, you can use background images to give a different look but there's some components which are more strict in terms of customization, like the tabbar. For these, the solution will be to create some custom component or use one made by someone.
    I recommend customizing simpler components and then build up experience for the more difficult ones.
