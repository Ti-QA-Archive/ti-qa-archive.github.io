Updating app on android market

Hello,
I uploaded my first App to the android market and found some bugs I didn't see before. I tried to upload the updated .apk and the form told me

"
The new apk's versionCode (1) in AndroidManifest.xml must be higher than the old apk's versionCode (1).
"

I opened the AndroidManifest.xml and changed the versioncode
to "2"

Then i ran the Distribution and made the new .APK
but after the creation of the new .apk the form said the same thing again
I opened the AndroidManifest.xml file again and the versioncode was back to "1".

I also went into Edit in Titanium Developer and changed the version to 2 but to no avail.

Does anybody know how to change this so i can upload the upgrade?

thank you for your help

    • Go to your YourApp/build/android/ folder. Make a copy of your AndroidManifest.xml. Rename it to AndroidManifest.custom.xml.
      Change following lines: android:versionCode="1" android:versionName="1"
      to: android:versionCode="2" // A number higher than the one above android:versionName="1.1" // Version number to show
      Leave both files and recompile and zipalign. This works with me.
