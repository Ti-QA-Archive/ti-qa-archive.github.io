  • Does it work when using AUDIO_FORMAT_LINEAR_PCM ? Instead of ULAW (which is very low quality)?

    IMHO, the best choice for audio recording on iOS is the combination of AUDIO_FILEFORMAT_MP4A and AUDIO_FORMAT_AAC compression.

    This will give you pretty good quality .m4a files with good compression, instead of massive .wav files (if uncompressed).
