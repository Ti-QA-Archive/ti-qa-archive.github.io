  • As Josh alludes, do you realize it can easily take over a week from the time you submit an application to iTunes Connect for it to get approved and available in the app store? My last one took 9 1/2 days, and a revision to it has been waiting for review for several days now. If it is a new app, even after it becomes officially available, it can take up to a day for it to appear in app store searches for the application name or keywords, as the searches are applied against cached server databases. If you have a link to the iTunes application ID you can download immediately once approved, but new applications have to propagate through the various servers before it will show up in search results – even for the application name itself.
