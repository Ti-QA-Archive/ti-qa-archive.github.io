Gradient Background Android

I am currently writing an app which uses gradient backgrounds on views and windows, and a few other components.

These work great on the iPhone but do not work at all on Android.

Are there any plans to implement this on Android or am I doing something wrong.

T

  • Hi Trevor

    There is a plan that they will be supported, but it has not been scheduled. Watch ticket TIMOB-1124 for updates on this. In the meantime, the workaround is to use background images.

    I'm sorry if this is not the news you were hoping to hear. Hope it helps a little, though.
