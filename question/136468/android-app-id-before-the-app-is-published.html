Android App ID before the app is published

Is it possible to know Android app ID before publishing it?

I know it's possible for iOS by creating the app page, but how to find out the Android app ID to use it in the application before it's published?

  • In Google Play, there are two quick ways to figure out where your app can be found:

    You can list all the apps by a developer:

    https://play.google.com/store/apps/developer?id=MyCompany,+LLC

    You can list the app by its appID:

    https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.stephenfeather.vnstimer
