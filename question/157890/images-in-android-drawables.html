    • The answers I provided you with are not specifically targeted to Alloy.

      As Alloy creates what end up in resources we need to locate the original folders elsewhere.

      {appname}/app/assets/android/images/res-ldpi/
{appname}/app/assets/android/images/res-mdpi/
{appname}/app/assets/android/images/res-hdpi/
{appname}/app/assets/android/images/res-xhdpi/
// please note that ldpi is significantly less required these days

      This is also where you will find the long/notlong folders. This are primarily used for splash screen images.

      In answer to your other question; notlong is for shorter aspect ratios and long is for taller aspect ratio devices.

      If you are only talking about in app graphics then you you only need the folders I mentioned above. You also do not need to use ALL the notlong, long, port, land folders - these are just the maximum combinations, you can use a subset of these.
