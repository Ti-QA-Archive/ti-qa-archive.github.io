  • You asked: "Can I develop an app by myself in Titanium with minimal coding required? Or will I need a lot of coding even for a simple app?"

    I think the problem with the question is the vagueness of "simple app" and "minimal coding". What is "simple" to an experienced developer may be worlds apart from somebody with little experience on other platforms. Ditto for interpreting "minimal coding".

    Titanium is NOT a graphical designer where you drop widgets in an IDE and select some macro or action from a predefined list. There will be coding involved. But is it "simple" and "minimal"? That's in the eye of the beholder.

    YMMV.
