SearchBar in custom ListView

Hello!

I use titanium 3.2.2, for ios and android.

I have created custom listView and I need bind info in labels with id "title" with my searchBar. Here is my code:

<Alloy>
        <Window id="container">
            <ListView id="list" onItemclick="listItemClick" visible="false" defaultItemTemplate="template">
                <SearchBar id="search_bar"/>
                <HeaderView height="Ti.UI.SIZE">
                    <View id="header_view">
                        <Label id="_label1">Label</Label>
                    </View>
                </HeaderView>
                <Templates>
                    <ItemTemplate name="template">
                        <ImageView bindId="arrow_pic" id="icon" />
                        <Label bindId="info" id="title" />
                    </ItemTemplate>
                </Templates>
                <ListSection id="section"/>
            </ListView>
        </Window>
    </Alloy>

Could someone suggest me how can I do that?

  • Hello John,

    You need add the key searchableText in the listitem.

    <Alloy>
    <Window id="container">
        <ListView id="list" onItemclick="listItemClick" visible="false" defaultItemTemplate="template">
            <SearchBar id="search_bar"/>
            <Templates>
                <ItemTemplate name="template">
                    <Label bindId="info" id="title" />
                </ItemTemplate>
            </Templates>
            <ListSection id="section">
                <ListItem info:text="Item 1" searchableText="Item 1" />
            </ListSection>
        </ListView>
    </Window>
</Alloy>

    I hope this helps.
    Thanks.
