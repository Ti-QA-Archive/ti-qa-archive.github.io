  • given 5 labels, label1, label2, label3, label4, label5, with a click on label1

    label1.addEventListener('click',function(e){
    label2.animate({top: label2.top+10});
    label3.animate({top: label2.top+10});
    label4.animate({top: label2.top+10});
    label5.animate({top: label2.top+10});
});

    This would change the top value from its current (arbitrary in this case) and increase it by 10px, eseentially 'dropping' it.
