  • Apple does a couple of things to your icon including adding the light reflection effect (which can be turned off if you want) and rounding the corners. I suspect that your original image has rounded corners - you are now seeing your original rounded corners as well as Apple's automatically added ones. Modify your image to have square corners with no hint of rounding and Apple will round them for you.

  • Also, the image that is in the screenshot you showed is the "Large Icon" that is uploaded into iTunesConnect independent of the app and NOT the one from inside the app bundle. I have several native apps on the App Store but have not yet submitted a Titanium app so I don't know if the process is different but the icon you showed above is handled in iTunesConnect separate from you app bundle - this means that you can change it AFTER your app is reviewed and released in the store so don't worry to much about changing it now.
