  • Alright, we're up and running! Here's the process I followed to make stuff work:

    1. Uninstall Titanium Studio
    2. Remove the Library/Application Support/Titanium/mobilesdk/osx/1.7.2 folder
    3. Update iOS SDK (just open up Xcode and let it do its thing)
    4. Reinstall Titanium Studio
    5. Enjoy

    I'm guessing my iOS SDK wasn't up to date, and Titanium Studio didn't pick up on me updating it until after a reinstall.
