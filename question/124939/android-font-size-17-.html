Android Font Size (1.7 +)

I am in the middle of porting an iPhone App to Android, and I am trying to follow the new guidelines for 1.7. I specified every size value as "dps", and for the most part this has worked great. However, on high density devices, the font size is really small. I have even tried testing for high density and manually setting the font size, but this doesn't seem to fix the problem. What am I doing wrong?

  • Ok - I have tracked down the issue, and it has nothing to do with dips or densities. If a textField height is too small (less than 25, on any density/resolution). One of two things might happen.

    1. The text is shrunk to about 4 pixels, and center aligned.
    2. The text is top aligned and above the text area (so it appears invisible).

    I assume this is a bug, but it is strange that I seem to be the only one that has run into it.

    — commented August 30th 2011 by Jacob Williams

  • Note: In the above comment, my textField is 25 pixels in height, it can be any width and still be problematic.
