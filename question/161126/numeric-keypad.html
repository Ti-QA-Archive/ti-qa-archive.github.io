  • Hi

    I am guessing you are on Android - you have not said.

    The short answer is - there is no keypad that will only show numbers and nothing else. The Android OS does not provide a numeric only keypad option so it is impossible to display something it does not have.

    • Do you have any validation mechanism to validate numeric?

      — commented January 3rd 2014 by Ankita Gadhia

    • Something like;

      var original = txt.getValue();
var cleaned = original.replace(/[^0-9]/g, '')
txt.setValue(cleaned);

      Basically you use Regular Expressions to find what you want and remove the rest - the above example looks for anything that is NOT numeric and replaces it with nothing.
