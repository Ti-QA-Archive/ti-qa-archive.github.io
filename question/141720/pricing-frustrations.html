Pricing frustrations

Hi there,

Despite my successful and happy use of Titanium development tools, I am writting this post to express my frustration regarding the pricing policy :

  • The 'Contact sales' price -> I HATE it so much…. When I create an app, I have a lot of expectations regarding the success but in the end I just don't know what is going to happens. Discussing with somebody any pricing is useless until an app is really successful and I have an idea about the ressources involved.
  • Limits in 'Appcelerator cloud' services -> Again I have no idea how much ressources my users will be using, are they going to update their facebook once or twice or 3 times a day ? Looking and trying to understand the pricing plans drive me away from producing quickly a beta app to test the audience. Of course their is the free plan, but I won't get it because if my app is successfull, I just don't have a clue how to calculate how much it is going to cost me (ie. : Contact sales)

I end up developing using my free account simple applications tied up with web services online. For cloud apps… well I am still looking to better solutions regarding the pricing plans :

  • http://www.tiggzi.com/pricing -> Simple and straight forward
  • http://www.appmobi.com/?page_id=145 -> I LOVE the no-lose guarantee
  • http://www.wakanda.org/faq -> $35 / month, then I just have to find a cloud hosting solution, and there are plenty with great scalable servers which will evolve with the traffic use not the number of time each little feature is used.
  • http://www.applicationcraft.com/pricing-main

I may be wrong but my first concern in app development is to create the app and test the potential success as quick as possible. In a 2nd step I would focus on the statistics to precise my hosting needs. It just feel wrong to focus on the 2nd step first, 9 times on 10 it will just be a waste of time because it is not easy to produce successful apps.

Please, make the pricing schemes simple, devs want to develop. For exemple I would love to see :

  • $50 / month - All included (Up to xxGB of disk space use and xxGB bandwidth use)
  • $50 extra / month to add xxGB disk and xxGB bandwidth

This way I know how much I spend without any calculation, and I just have to wait for an alert that I go over the limit to evaluate how much I need to add each month. In the other side you make people life easier and let's be true not that many users will approach the limits since it's the success of the app which drive the server use. Let's face it, it's not that easy to create a really successfull app so pleaaaaase let us focus on the code.

Best Regards,
Clement Chazarra
