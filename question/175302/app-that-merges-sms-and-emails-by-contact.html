App that merges sms and emails by contact

Hi, just a feasability question here.
Is it technically possible to create an app that would merge both emails and messages, grouped by contact name, so that you can easily follow a thread of past conversations across the board?

    Apple do not provide an interface to access the SMS database (/var/mobile/Library/SMS/sms.db) it is outside the App sandbox to cant be read unless the phone is jailbroken. For accessing someone's mail you would need to find/write a POP3 message client or some such. So, in reality your idea of merging both would not be possible but writing a mail client would be but probably then not accepted by Apple because it duplicates functionality already in the OS
