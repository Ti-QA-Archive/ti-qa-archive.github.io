How close before deadline do I dare updating my app on AppStore?

Hi

Not sure if this is off topic.. but here I go:
I just saw I got an ios-app (created with Titanium) accepted on AppStore. Nice.
But I want to enhance a lot of small stuff which includes work for maybe one day.

How close to a deadline do you dare to upgrade? Does upgrade mean that the app has to go through the long review period again? Does it mean there is a risk of down time, when the app is not accessible on AppStore?
Something else I should know when I (if) I do the first upgrade ever?

Best regards /Tove :-)

  • If your app is currently live, that version stays until the next version is approved. So there is no 'downtime'.

    Yes, your new version has to go through the review process again.

    We assume 2 weeks for review, but tell clients 3 weeks. If folks would quit wasting apple reviewers time with website apps (phonecrap) and torch/fart apps, real apps could get through much faster.
