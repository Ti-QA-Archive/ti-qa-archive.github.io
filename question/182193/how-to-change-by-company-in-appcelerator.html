  • Here are the bits of my tiapp.xml that you are looking for:

    <id>com.archwaypi.mywinecellar</id>
    <name>MyWineCellar</name>
    <version>1.0.2.1</version>
    <publisher>Archway Partnerships, Inc.</publisher>
    <url>http://www.laithwaiteswine.com</url>
    <description>Introducing the My Wine Cellar App for iPhone, the easy-to-use, digital wine assistant from Laithwaite's Wine. Use it to update your Wine Preferences, get our top Recommendations (tailored to you!), add wines to your Favorites list, snap and store pictures of your bottles, Rate and Review what you taste and more!</description>
    <copyright>2015 Archway Partnerships, Inc.</copyright>
    <icon>appicon.png</icon>

    Its very annoying, I contacted AppC support and they said it was my developer profile settings on Apple's site. But I checked that and it looks all correct.

    Also, the connect account is all correctly setup too.
