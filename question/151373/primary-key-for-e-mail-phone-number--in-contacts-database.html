  • Accepted Answer

    Another approach would be to create your own contact database (with your own unique identifier) that would synchronize data between the device's contacts and your application. So if there ever was an update where the email were to be changed, you could still have the old address stored in your own database. Allowing you to retrieve it and then update the information for the contact.

    And for Android, the mechanism would be even simpler since all you would have to worry about id the ID property.
