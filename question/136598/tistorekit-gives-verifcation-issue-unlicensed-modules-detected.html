ti.storekit gives Verifcation Issue (Unlicensed module(s) detected)

Since yesterday I get:

Verification issue

Unlicensed module(s) detected. You must be a subscriber to use one or more of the modules included with this project.

I have narrowed it down to the ti.storkit-module (by removing my modules one by one).

I have a free account.

In Marketplace everything looks good. I subscribe to it and it is free and the correct user is assigned to it. What is wrong here?

Thanks for any ideas on this!

  • Accepted Answer

    For this issue you need to change guid tag in tiapp.xml file.
    <guid>33fdca33-590b-498d-bd4e-1fc3a8be0f37</guid> =====> guid in tiapp.xml file(Original guid)
    guid: 52fdca33-590d-498d-bd4e-1fc3a8be0f37 =====> guid in module manifest file. of ti.storekit

    copy guid from
    ..modules/iphone/ti.storekit/1.5/manifest file and replace tiapp.xml file's guid with manifest file's guid .
    after that clean your project and rebuild it.
