ScrollableView with custom Next and Previous button

I am working on the titanium alloy project. I have successfully implemented ScrollableView with two views(showPagingControl="false").
Now i want to implement custom Previous and next Button out side the ScrollableView. When I click on the next button ScrollableView should move to the next child view if it is available. I tried below methods to achieve the functionality but both are not working. Please help.

$.myscrollableView.setCurrentPage = newIndex;
$.myscrollableView.scrollToView = newIndex;

  • those are functions. You should call them like this: $.myscrollableView.scrollToView(newIndex);
