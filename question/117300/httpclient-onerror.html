HTTPClient onerror

I am trying to use an HTTPClient and it works sometimes, but calls the onerror function more often than the onload. I am not seeing anything that give me an exception message or anything to identify why the request is failing. How can I determine what is happening here? I have turned the timeout up to 20 seconds, so I don't think that it is timing out. Thanks

  • Techniques that I use are:

    1. In the onerror function get the output of the following to Ti.API.info():
      this.status,
      this.responseText or
      e.error assuming you have something like xhr.error = function(e) {...}
    2. Get a packet trace using something like wireshark.

    Also I have found that increasing the timeout doesn't work on iphone (SDK v1.6.0).
