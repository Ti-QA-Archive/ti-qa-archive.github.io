Problem with google play certificate

Hi guys,
I have a problem publishing an app on google play.

This app was created the first time by a company and published on the play store.
The company has been closed and I work for another company: now I have to publish an update of that app (I have the Google account credentials), but when I try to upload the apk it say that the apk is signed with a different certificate.

The problem is that the first company send me a certificate file, I selected this certificate in the titanium build settings but I still receive the error.
What can I do?

  • I'm afraid they sent you the wrong keytool file by mistake. If that is the case, you are in serious trouble, because it is not possible to publish an update if you don't have the original keytool file.
