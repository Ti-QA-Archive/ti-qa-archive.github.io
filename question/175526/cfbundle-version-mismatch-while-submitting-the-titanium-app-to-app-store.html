CFBundle Version Mismatch while submitting the Titanium app to App store

Hi,
In tiapp.xml of my app, under Application section I have specified Version as 1.0. While building the app, it takes CFBundleVersion as 1.0.0 and CFBundleShortVersionString as 1.0.0, but the iTunes Connect version is taken as 1.0.

I am submitting the Titanium app to Appstore. While submitting the app, I get the below warning. How can I resolve this?

"Version Mismatch. Neither CFBundleVersion ['1.0.0'] nor CFBundleShortVersionString ['1.0.0'] in the Info.plist match the version of the app set in iTunes Connect ['1.0']"

Regards,
Chella
