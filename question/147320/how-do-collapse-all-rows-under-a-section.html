how do collapse all rows under a section

i have two TableViewSection and under each of them i have have few rows

how do make all the rows under the specific section to collapse with animation and expand on second tap on section header

  • You'd have to build this out from scratch as sections of a table cannot be hidden and shown like a regular view. You can add and remove entire sections, but once you removed a section, there would be no header left to click on to re-add it.

    You can try swapping sections in and out. A section with just a header for when its closed and a section with the rows that need to be displayed for when its open. Keep in mind that if you try and pull this off, then you'd need to use a headerView on your sections as a headerTitle doesn't accept click events.
