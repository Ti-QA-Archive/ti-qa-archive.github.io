  • After several attempts, I realized this system, which in my case it works well :)
    I create many images, to cover the entire webview, (in my case the height of the webview are always the same, so I just need a loop to get all the images)

    j = 5 //number of times necessary to see the entire webview
for(var i=1, x; i<j; i++){
    var screenShot = myWebView.toImage();
    myWebView.evalJS('window.scrollTo(0,'+(winHeight*i)+')');
    var imageName = "image" + i + ".png"
    var exportImage = Titanium.Filesystem.getFile(Titanium.Filesystem.applicationDataDirectory,imageName);
    exportFile.write(screenShot)
)};
