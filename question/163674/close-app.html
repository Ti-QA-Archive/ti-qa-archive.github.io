  • if it's iOS you can add this to your tiapp:

    <ios>
        <plist>
            <dict>
                <key>UIApplicationExitsOnSuspend</key>
                <true/>
            </dict>
        </plist>
    </ios>

    and the app will close when it's minimized.

    if it's android, add the property exitOnClose to the main window (the last one to be closed, to be clear).
