    • I am not sure what you mean by "didn't want to use a web based program". With either titanium or phonegap, it will run locally on the device unless your web pages are referencing images or other things using external links.

      However, if what you want is to take your HTML code and have that be the cornerstone of the UI, then you will essentially still have a "web application" albeit it one that runs locally.

      In regards to "I am afraid of the application no longer being mine", again it may depend on what you mean. If you mean you don't like the fact that people can see your javascript then a "web app" may not be your cup of tea. You can do a certain amount of obfuscation and minify but a determined user can still reverse to some extent.

      Normally titanium projects do not have any javascript visible in the compiled app resources. But I don't use webviews and I don't know how that would impact the compiled program.

      YMMV.
