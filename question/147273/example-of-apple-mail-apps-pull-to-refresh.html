  • Hi

    The "new" way is built directly into iOS 6 and exposed as a property of of what we see as tableView. This is currently not exposed in Titanium.

    Assuming it will be, you will only be able to use it in iOS 6 - so you will either need to provide a fallback for iOS 4 & 5 or you can only target iOS 6. Obviously this is not available for Android either.

    You have three choices;

    • create a custom module in Xcode and expose the specific feature including all events.
    • create the animation yourself - be aware it is more than a simple stretch as you point out is a teardrop from a circle
    • wait for Titanium to expose it - there is no timeline for this.
