  • Hello Adel,

    Thanks for your inquiry. Actually there is no straight way for apps to support all devices and all resolutions - simply too many - especially when you include everything Android.

    If you are building an app and you care about how it looks on multiple devices you need to spend time working out how you are going to design the app and how it will work across multiple aspect ratios as well as platforms and screen sizes.

    My recommendations are:

    1. Design one set of layouts for 320 x 533 physical pixels and then introduce custom layouts for the other screen sizes.

    2. Use positioning value without the "dp".
