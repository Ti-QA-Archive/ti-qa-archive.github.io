Is there any nice method to implement the Titanium.UI.iOS.TabbedBar for Android?

    If you don't want to reinvent the wheel yourself, you could use the TitanUp utility library.

    It has a component called the SelectBar. It basically behaves like a TabbedBar on iOS and uses Toggle switches on Android.
