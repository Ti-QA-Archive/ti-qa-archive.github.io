  • In the future, you should likely split multiple questions into, well, multiple questions in the Q&A section. Having said that…

    1) Android does not currently support the globalPoint property

    2) Orientation lock is available in the 1.8.0 continuous integration builds

    3) You can test the accelerometer in the android emulator with the help of SensorSimulator. See this link for details: http://stackoverflow.com/questions/3921467/how-can-i-simulate-accelerometer-in-android-emulator
