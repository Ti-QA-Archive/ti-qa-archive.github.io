  • Hi

    Is your module in the correct location?

    It should be located inside a 'modules' folder, same level as '/app' and inside your modules folder you should have the respective folders for Android and iPhone.

    Also the module strings inside you tiapp.xml are missing the version numbers.

    I would removed the references which you have added to tiapp.xml, check that the modules are in the correct folder structure, then open tiapp.xml inside your Titanium Studio. On the overview screen in the top right you will see a modules section where you can add your modules that are available, doing this will amend the relevant strings to you tiapp.xml.
