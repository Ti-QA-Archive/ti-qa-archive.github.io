Unable to change Textfield background color

Hi,

this may be something rather obvious, but I cannot seem to change the background color of Textfields (as in the the color against which the text is set). The backgroundColor property only seems to change the color of the rectangle the Textfield is in. The example in the API shows the use of an undocumented 'color' property, but that does seem to not exist or work, either. (http://developer.appcelerator.com/apidoc/mobile/latest/Titanium.UI.TextField-object)

Am I stuck with white? There seems no option to change the text color, for that matter.

This appears to be related:

http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/681/button-backgroundcolor-ignored

  • Seems that the borderStyle rounded is the one giving the problem.

    var txt = Ti.UI.createTextField({
    top:0, 
    left:0, 
    height:30,
    backgroundColor:'red', 
    backgroundImage:'none'
});
win.add(txt);

    Works fine but 

    var txt = Ti.UI.createTextField({
    top:0, 
    left:0, 
    height:30,
    backgroundColor:'red', 
    backgroundImage:'none',
    borderStyle:Ti.UI.INPUT_BORDERSTYLE_ROUNDED
});
win.add(txt);

    only change the background color of the border..

    Works fine with the two others border style though:

    borderStyle:Ti.UI.INPUT_BORDERSTYLE_LINE,
    borderStyle:Ti.UI.INPUT_BORDERSTYLE_BEZEL,
