  • You will need to have the proper certificate, provisioning profiles and (of course) the necessary credentials in order to package a build for the App Store.

    There will be instances where you will not have complete control over the certificate and profiles creation process. When you are publishing for a client for example. In those cases, the client will have to give you access to the developer portal by inviting you into his team.

    Hope this helps
