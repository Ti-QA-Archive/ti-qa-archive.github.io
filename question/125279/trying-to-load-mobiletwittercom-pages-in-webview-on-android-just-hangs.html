Trying to load mobile.twitter.com pages in webView on Android just hangs

I'm trying to bring up a simple webview showing a page from mobile.twitter.com like so…

var webView = Titanium.UI.createWebView({
    url:'http://mobile.twitter.com/appcelerator',
    scalesPageToFit:false,
    top:0,
    left:0,
    bottom:0,
    right:0,
    width:'100%',
    height:'auto'
    });

The twitter logo is displayed with a loading indicator underneath it and then it just hangs there. The following error is issued in the Console…

E/Web Console(  365): TypeError: Result of expression 'f' [undefined] is not an object. at https://mobile.twitter.com/assets/m5.js?v=7e4345f6721f61b3fa82845306416e1cb041b8b1:60

I understand the error above appears to be complaining about bad javascript code in twitter's m5.js script but the same URL loads fine in the Android browser. This happens on both the emulator and a h/w device.
