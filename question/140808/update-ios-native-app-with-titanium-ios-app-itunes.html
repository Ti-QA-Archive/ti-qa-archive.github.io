  • At the moment in Titanium, BundleVersion and BundleShortString are the same in the info.plist in the .ipa

    When you submit an update, YOU enter the version that the end user sees. It is NOT pulled currently from the bundle you upload.

    • You can create a custom info.plist.
    • You can use the new tiapp.xml options to add info.plist entries in there.
    • You can just make your version in TiStudio what you want the CFBundleVersion to be (higher than your previous one submitted) and then enter the display version you would like to see in iTunes.
