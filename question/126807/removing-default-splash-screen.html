    • PKV, as far as I know, the iPhone does not support automatic selection between portrait and landscape versions of a splash screen at runtime. I think the /iphone/Default-Landscape.png image is only used for iPad. However, for apps that are landscape only, there's nothing that says that /iphone/Default.png (and /iphone/Default@2x.png) can't be landscape-only images. You said you need landscape and portrait…I don't know that you'll be able to accomplish that.

      As far as changing the amount of time the splash screen is shown, all you can do is extend it by not opening your first window until that exetended amount of time has passed, using setTimeout().
