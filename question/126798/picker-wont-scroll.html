Picker won't scroll

Hi!

this is testet on the Android 2.2 emulatur using Titanium 1.7.1.

I have a picker with the spinner style enabled that won't scroll when placed on a view that is placed on a scrollview.

I have tried setting the height of the picker, the view and even the scrollview to some absolute value, but it doesn't make a difference.

The scrollview reacts instead of the picker.

This makes is basically impossible for me to write an app that works both in vertical and horizontal mode since the only workaround I have been able to find is to remove the scrollview.

Any suggestions? I have seen lots of posts regarding this issue but not found a solution that work for me.
