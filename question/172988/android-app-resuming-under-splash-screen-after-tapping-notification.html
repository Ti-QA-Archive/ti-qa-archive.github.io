Android: App Resuming Under Splash Screen After Tapping Notification

I am using the bencoding.AlarmManager module to schedule local notifications in my app. I have a condition in the app that schedules a notification if the app is put into the background at a certain point during processing. The notification fires as expected, but when I tap on the notification, the app resumes to the splash screen. Based on what I see in the log, the active controller/view is running (it is handling the app resume event), but the view itself seems to be under the splash screen.

If I resume the app from the app tray (even after the notification fires), it resumes normally. The launchMode for the app is "singleTop." What might be going on here?

Ti SDK version is 3.2.1.
AlarmManager Module version is 0.11
