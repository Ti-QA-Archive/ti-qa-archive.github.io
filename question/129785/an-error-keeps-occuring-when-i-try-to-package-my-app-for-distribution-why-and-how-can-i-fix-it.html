  • Hi again. I found the solution to the problem; I had checked the wrong seting in the Keychain Access for the certificates… In Keychain Access I had marked the Apple WWDR certificate "Always Trust". It needed to be "System Defaults" - the same with the Development and Distribution certificates, too. That is; in Keychain Access, click on the Apple Worldwide Developer Relations Certificate Authority certificate, select Get Info, expand the Trust settings and select the "System Defaults".
    I thought I would share my newly found solution in case any other semi newbie should experience the same problem ;-) Thanks for your time and help,
    Best Louise
