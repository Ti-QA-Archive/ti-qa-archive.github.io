  • Hi Prakash

    The only advice I can give you about app architecture is to use what you need to.

    Now saying that I use a MVC style architecture for most of my apps, which works quite well, it enables me to construct an app easily and effectively, without others devs having to have a major learning curve on top of titanium.

    I do not use a MVC plugin but architecture the app in a standard way. To see how I implement this take a look at an project for custom menus I open sourced tiCustomMenu. You will see how it all hangs together.

    I hope this helps

    T
