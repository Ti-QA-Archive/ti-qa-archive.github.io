  • Hi

    So from the extra information, I assume you are using a splitWindow? As far as I am aware you can't change the side each view is displayed on. This isn't a limitation of Titanium, it is simply a standard created by Apple and thus isn't available to us.

    The solution to this would be to create you own custom Window and accompanying views to replicate a splitview how you would like to present it.

    Hope this helps.
