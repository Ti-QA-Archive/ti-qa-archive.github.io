  • The ONLY way to change this after many many wasted hours was to reinstall Titanium. I'm guessing the update I had installed was corrupted. I no longer suffered the error, but then had a CLI problem. I had to reinstall node.js, and the 10.X version on the site now got rid of the missing CLI problem… then the project that built last week would not build… After more hunting it turns out that downgrading node.js to 8.22 was the solution. Now I'm back to operating as I did a week ago.
