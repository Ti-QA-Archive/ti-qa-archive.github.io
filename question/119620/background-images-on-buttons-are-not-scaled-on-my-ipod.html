Background images on buttons are not scaled on my iPod.

All the bg images I use for buttons etc. are twice the size, so they will look neat on retina displays and the iPad. I just tested the app on my iPod (2nd gen iOS 4.2) and found out that buttons do not have their bg images scaled. It works for other views, but not for buttons.

Did anyone experience something like this and has a solution? Should I bother solving that problem, or is my iPod too old already and not supported by Titanium?

    • No, you don't need to modify the source. You need to create that 100x25 resolution version of the image, give it a name like image.png, and rename your original 200x50 version image@2x.png. The iOS software will automatically use the correct one based on whether the device is a Retina display or not.

  • Are you naming your images correctly?

    For example, your 100x25 image could be named background.png. Therefore your retina-sized image should be named background@2x.png. You never have to refer to the retina image directly. When you set your button's background, you still use background.png, and iOS will automatically look for and use the @2x image on a retina display if it exists. It's actually a pretty good solution.
