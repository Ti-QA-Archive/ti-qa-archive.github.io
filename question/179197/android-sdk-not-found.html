  • Hello Rafael,

    Thanks for your inquiry, here are some process you can follow.

    First, There may be a problem occurred while upgrading your Ti SDK. It may be that you don't have an avd.py script at the location it's looking.     Second, Try deleting your current virtual device in Genymotion and add new device.
