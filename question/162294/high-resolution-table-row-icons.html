  • Hi

    Have you checked in your tiapp.xml that your system units are set to 'dp' or 'dip'? This could be a cause.

    Other than that, I have seen a couple questions related to this and it seems that the workaround was to use an imageView in replacement of the hasChild/hasCheck. You can then handle the imageView's by providing the correct imagery in the appropriate density folders.

    Here is a link to the documentation on Using_density-specific_resources_on_Android
