This is for an iOS app. I've seen it used in other apps before, as well as the native OS, for example for giving your iTunes login password.

How is this done in Titanium?

    Try creating a window with your desired dimensions, and setting the "modal:true" property. Then add your label, text box and buttons to it, and add eventListeners to the buttons.

    Get as far as you can with the code, and post your results here when you get stuck. I am sure people will guide you to a good solution.
