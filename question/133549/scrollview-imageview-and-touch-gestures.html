ScrollView, ImageView and touch-gestures

So I've been trying all day to make sense of the ScrollView and ImageView (I'm a newbie with mmobile dev and Titanium).

I'm trying to do something (I thought was) simple;

Display an image on the screen, make it zoomable with pinch and double-tap, as well as draggable when zoomed in.

Not so simple it seems.

This is my starting point:

var window = Ti.UI.createWindow({});

var readView = Titanium.UI.createScrollView({
    contentWidth:'auto',
    contentHeight:'auto',
    width:Titanium.Platform.displayCaps.platformWidth,
    height:Titanium.Platform.displayCaps.platformHeight,
    showVerticalScrollIndicator:false,
    showHorizontalScrollIndicator:false,
    minZoomScale:0,
    maxZoomScale:1
});

var image = Ti.UI.createImageView({
    image:"images/image.jpg",
    width:990,
    height:1400
});

readView.add(image);
window.add(readView);

window.open();

From this I get the image displayed on the screen, in it's full size, anchored in the top left corner of the screen. I can pinch in/out, and it zooms in/out - however it seems the content of the ScrollView always positions itself in the top, left corner.

I want the zooming to happen centered - i.e. when I zoom in/out, the page re-positions tthe center of the image to the center of the device.

Anyone ?

  • set the center property of the scroll view to the center of the diplay

    ScrollView.setCenter(point);

    it takes point so you can pass x,y of center postion of your screen

    point.X=Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformWidth / 2,
point.Y=Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformHeight / 2,
