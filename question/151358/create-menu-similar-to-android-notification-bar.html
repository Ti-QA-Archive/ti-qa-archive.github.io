    Christian is trying to explain to you, that on android, you already have this action/event at the top of the device's view. You want to add another one similar in your app. Yes, you could do that. You can capture the swipe event, do the math to see if its a top > bottom swipe and then overlay a new view with your 'menu' in it. However, as Christian has tried to say, your user will have to start that event below the android notification bar, and if you have your start on a header bar or similar just below the notification bar, user will get frustrated if they have fat fingers.
