    • Yes, and you will kick yourself;

      btn.addEventListener("Press", function(e){
...

      You have told the button to listen out for the Press event. The assumption you have is that when a button is 'pressed' this code will get called and the alert will display.

      There are two things wrong here;

      • There is now such thing as a Press event
      • All events are lower - even if there was a Press event.

      The event you are after is click.

      btn.addEventListener("click", function(e){
...

      This will do what you expected it to do.

      The events for the button are shown in the documentation, the main one you will use throughout the app will be click. There are others but they are less common as they control specific things rather than the generic click.

      • Ti.UI.Button-event-click (all other events are listed below this one.
