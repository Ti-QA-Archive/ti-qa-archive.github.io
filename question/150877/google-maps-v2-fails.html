  • It's because "Google play services" is not installed on android.
    Check it installed then if not, redirect to google store.

    For example

    var rc = MapModule.isGooglePlayServicesAvailable();
if(rc != MapModule.SUCCESS){
  Ti.platform.openURL("https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.gms");
}
