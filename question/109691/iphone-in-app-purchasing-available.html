  • Ricardo

    What platform are you referring to?

    The Android OS doesn't currently support in-app purchasing. Although it's available in Honeycomb (version 3), that version is still at the pre-release stage, so Titanium does not support it yet. Also, I think even Honeycomb will only support it for tablets to begin with.

    For iOS, I heard that the module will be released in Titanium Plus which is due for release after Titanium SDK 1.6.0. In the past, I've heard that premium customers get a sneak preview so, if that applies to you, you could make inquiries.
