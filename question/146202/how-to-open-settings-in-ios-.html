how to open settings in iOS ?

can I open the settings to show the Location Serveices is on or off?

[[UIApplication sharedApplication] openURL:[NSURL URLWithString:@"prefs:root=LOCATION_SERVICES"]];
this can work in Xcode, in TitaniumStudio, How to do it?

    Hi

    This is how you do it;

    Ti.Platform.openURL('prefs:root=LOCATION_SERVICES');

    However it WILL NOT work in any iOS 5.1 or higher, Apple removed this ability starting iOS 5.1.

    To confirm this I have just performed the following tests;

    • iOS 5.0 - Works
    • iOS 5.1 - Nothing
    • iOS 6.0 - Nothing

    Sorry :(
