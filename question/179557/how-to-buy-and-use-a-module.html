  • Hello Lin,

    Thanks for your inquiry. If you want to buy a module from Appcelerator marketplace after trial period, Go to "Appcelerator marketplace/Purchased products" In your product list you will see your module is showing "free trial expires". Pointing there will display "Activate Subscription" option. From there activate your product and then rebuild your project with active internet connection. It will verify your module as paid and you are good to go. Don't follow any guide on "changing the module's GUID in the manifest file". That is piracy.

    Hope this helps you. Thanks.
