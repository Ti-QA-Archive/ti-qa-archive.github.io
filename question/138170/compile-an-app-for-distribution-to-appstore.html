  • You can use the

    /mobilesdk/osx/{YOUR_TITANIUM_SDK_VERSION}/iphone/builder.py

    commands to perform a build.

    I've written about it in my blog:

    http://www.gleisarbeiter.de/2012/09/06/building-titanium-mobile-apps-from-the-command-line/

    Hope it helps!
