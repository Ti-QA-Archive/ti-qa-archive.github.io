setLocationServicesAuthorization() not working on iPhone

Title says it all. I am trying to give my users the option to change location settings in the event they accidentally disable location services. I try setting the authorization through the following code, but the alert always display '2':

Titanium.Geolocation.setLocationServicesAuthorization(Ti.Geolocation.AUTHORIZATION_AUTHORIZED);
Titanium.Geolocation.setLocationServicesAuthorization(3);
Titanium.Geolocation.locationServicesAuthorization = 3;
alert(Titanium.Geolocation.getLocationServicesAuthorization());

Is there any way to change it? Or will my users have to reinstall the app to enable location services?

    You cannot programmatically set the location services authorization. If the user denies access to your App to use Location services when you first try to utilise location services, then subsequentially you must prompt them to re-enable themselves from the 'Settings' App
