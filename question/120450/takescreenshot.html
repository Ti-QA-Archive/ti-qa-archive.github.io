Is this possible ? and if so how…. I cannot take a picture and then add the overlay on later.

  • I would assume you're taking the screenshot of the wrong view/window. Also remember if the views are not children of them then they won't show up in a screen shot of that view.

    Perhaps you need to wrap all your objects/views on the window with another view and generate the screen shot off of the wrapper.
