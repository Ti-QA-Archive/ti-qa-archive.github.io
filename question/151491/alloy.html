i have one controler (index.js, and one view index.xml)

i create another controller (categories.js and categories.xml for the view)

what i want to do is when i click on the btnNext in the index to open the categories window..

how can i do this?

    if your categories view is a Window, you can do this :
    categories.js

    <Alloy>
    <Window>

    </Window
</Alloy>

    then you would call it from index like this:

    btnNext.addEventListener('click', function(e){
    var cat = Alloy.createController('categories');
    cat.getView().open();
}
