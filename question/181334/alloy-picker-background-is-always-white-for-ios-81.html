Alloy: Picker background is always White for IOS 8.1

I am almost certain I am doing this right but it never hurts to get a second opinion. Below is an snapshot of the picker in the 5s simulator: Snapshot 5s Picker

Not matter what I have tried, the picker's backgroundColor is always white. I have tried applying backgroundColor directly to the the XML element, creating the picker manually and trying backgroundColor there, and also using styles to attempt to make the picker backgroundColor transparent. I installed the NappUI module to see if I could accomplish this as well with not eval.

SDK 3.5.1 (tried downgrading to 3.5.0 as well)

Anyone have any suggestions what I may be doing wrong ot how to accomplish a transparent background with the picker?

  • Accepted Answer

    Looking into the source code of TiUIPicker.m:

    [picker setBackgroundColor:[UIColor whiteColor]];

    Change it to:

    [picker setBackgroundColor:[UIColor clearColor]];

    Now the background color of your parent view should be visible.

    Search here in Q&A where and how to update the Obj-C source code of the AppC SDK.
