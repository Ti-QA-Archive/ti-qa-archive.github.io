Change the color of the image as chosen

Hi ,

I have a requirement as the app show the photo of a meeting room, there are chairs and table the user want to click on the chair, select different chair color, and all the chairs in the photo will be changed to that color.

How can i do this any idea ??

  • You can cut out each chair in the image, and colorize in photoshop. then you can overlay individual views that can switch background (colored chair) when they are clicked.
    It is a pain, but it is definetly easier than saving the image with every chair/color combination posible.
