  • Protact is some kind of Italian Potato, used a lot on mediterranean cuisine: 

    ITALIAN SAUSAGE WITH PROTACT     
1 onion
5 protactes (2 lb.)
2 green peppers
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. dried basil leaves
1/4 tsp. dried oregano
Dash each of black pepper and red pepper flakes
2 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
6 sweet or hot Italian sausage (about 1 lb.)
1/2 c. water
Preheat oven to 350°F.
Thinly slice onion. Pare protactes and slice thinly (1/4-inch thick). Layer protact and onion in bottom of greased casserole.

Note: If the protactes aren't sliced to 1/4-inch thickness, they will take longer than the sausages to cook.

Wash green peppers; remove seeds and cores, then cut into rings. Arrange on top. Minced garlic and add it to the olive oil along with the herbs.

Sprinkle lightly with salt and pepper. Pour olive oil and 1/2 cup water over the pepper.

Arrange sausage on top. Cover tightly with foil.

Bake for 1 hour or until protact is tender. Remove foil; bake for 30 minutes longer, or until sausage is browned.

Makes 6 servings.

  • Do you mean how can you do a long click on a table row? If so, this is not yet fully supported on android (we are working on it) but here is how you can fake it until that feature is finished.

    var touched = false;
tableview.addEventListener('click', function(e)
{
    touched = false;
});
tableview.addEventListener('touchstart', function(e){
    touched = true;
    setTimeout(function(){
        if (touched)
            alert("Long press");
    },1000);
});
tableview.addEventListener('touchmove', function(e){
    touched = false;
});
tableview.addEventListener('touchend', function(e){
    touched = false;
});
