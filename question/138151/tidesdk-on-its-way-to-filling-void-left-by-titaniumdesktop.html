TideSDK on its way to filling void left by TitaniumDesktop?

TideSDK, the community driven initiative to continue development on the heritage of TitaniumDesktop is very much alive. Recently, the impediments to moving forward have been removed and we have successful builds on all the supported platforms including OSX, Windows and Linux which is a key initial milestone. Our objective is a first release candidate within the next week. You can read about recent developments in our first progress report:

https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/tidesdk
