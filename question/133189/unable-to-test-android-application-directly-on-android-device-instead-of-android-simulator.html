  • Hello! You need to download the drivers for your device so that your operating system knows what it is. Then, once the device is recognised, you can activate USB debugging. Go to setting -> Applications -> Development -> USB Debugging. You might also want to activate Stay awake, and Allow Mock Locations, depending on what your application is doing.
