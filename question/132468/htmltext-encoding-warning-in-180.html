HTML/Text Encoding Warning in 1.8.0

Despite declaring charset=utf-8 in meta tags of html doc, I still get this warning:

[WARN] I have no idea what the appropriate text encoding is for: html/nyc/loveGe

..and i have no idea what to do about this

    Liz, we had this problem, and I think we had to be case sensitive or something in the charset declaration:

    <meta content="text/html; charset=UTF-8" http-equiv="content-type">
