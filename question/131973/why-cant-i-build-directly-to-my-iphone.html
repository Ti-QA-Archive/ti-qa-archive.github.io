    Mark,

    First off this is a community forum, so yelling and shouting is not the best way to go about things…

    Now to answer your question, the reason for this is Titanium uses the Xcode CLI to build your app… the Xcode CLI does not provide the ability to build directly to device.

    That said I have been working on replicating the Xcode commands needed to directly load the app on the device… it's not perfect, and it is still a work in progress… do give it a try, download the work in progress brach here of my MakeTi CLI tool
