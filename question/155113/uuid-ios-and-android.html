  • Hi

    Short answer no.

    Apple restricts the ability to generate a unique value that can track a user on their device across installations. This is a major security protection put in place a couple of iOS versions back. I believe Android has a similiar set of rules.

  • To identify a "unique" device, there are several workarounds:

    • MAC address (iOS & Android, currently often used as UDID replacement, but seems unavailable in iOS 7)
    • OpenUDID (iOS & Android, https://github.com/ylechelle/OpenUDID, deprecated)
    • advertisingIdentifier (iOS, available since iOS 6, related to IDFA)
    • identifierForVendor (iOS, available since iOS 6, related to IDFV)
    • android_id (Android, Secure.ANDROID_ID)
    • device_id (Android, IMEI from TELEPHONYMANAGER)

    Besides MAC address (Ti.Platform.macaddress), you need a custom module to read these values with Titanium Appcelerator. I can provide some snippets if required.
