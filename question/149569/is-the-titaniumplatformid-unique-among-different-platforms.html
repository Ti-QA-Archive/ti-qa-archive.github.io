Is the Titanium.Platform.id unique among different platforms?

I'm willing to use Titanium.Platform.id to save login information, but how cross-platform is this property? Am I guaranteed to get unique values between iPhone and android users, or should I store their platform too just to be sure? And what is it going to return me on web interfaces?

    Hi

    Short answer is yes.

    Ti.Platform.id is a unique value created based on the installation of the app on a device.

    Installing the app on either an iPhone, iPad or Android will give a different answer for each device.

    However I would not suggest you use this as a means to determine a unique user as un-installing and re-installing will generate a different value. Also upgrading from one device to another will also generate another value.

    If you really need a unique value, you should connect to a web service of your own and generate a unique value from that. This would be the only safe way to create a reusable unique value.
