Preloading ImageViews - returned.

We have a series of ImageViews in the rows of a TableView. The user scrolls vertically through these images. Simple enough.

Unfortunately, the images only load once the ImageView is in the viewport. This makes casually scanning the images very unpleasant: you see the ugly default image, then after a lag, the correct image finally appears. Blech! Especially on 3G.

Is there a way to preload the images before the TableView is shown? That way, all the pretty images are waiting for the user.
