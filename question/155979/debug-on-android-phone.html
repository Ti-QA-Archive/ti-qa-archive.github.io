Debug on Android phone

Hi,

Can some one please give me a step by step guide on how I can debug my mobile application which I am building using Titanium Alloy on my Android phone. My Android emulator is very slow and I am stuck for days so I would like to test it on my Android phone.

Thanks in advance,

Bably

  • Accepted Answer

    With your device plugged in, on your command line, go to the location of your Android SDK in the platform_tools folder (looking for an executable called adb and type .&#x2F;adb logcat

    You can also filter out any of the other stuff to focus on your app data with a grep .&#x2F;adb logcat | grep &quot;some_string_in_your_ti_info&quot;
