    Don't worry. I have just checked : the OLD iphone SDK they are referring to was here : https://github.com/facebook/facebook-iphone-sdk

    This was the old version, pre-"graph" methods etc..

    Titanium uses the NEW/current OAuth methods provided by this version : https://github.com/facebook/facebook-ios-sdk

    So.. panic over… everything will be fine ;)

    Happy apps and clients all round!
