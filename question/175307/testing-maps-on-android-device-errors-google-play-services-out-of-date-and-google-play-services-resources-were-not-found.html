    Hi

    My first thoughts would be;

    • If this is a true test device then you may not have setup a Google account on the device yet (think gmail/google id) - if not, then Google Play Services will not work on many versions of Android these days
    • I am assuming this is already enabled as a developer ready device
    • The device may really have out of date Google Play Services, check in the Settings for your installed version, try to update them if possible and very the current version. If out of date and not able to update - Google your version and say "how to update Google Play Service (your version)".
