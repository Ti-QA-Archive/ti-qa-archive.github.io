Lack of incoming call event

The question of detecting incoming calls has come up several times on this forum, the current received wisdom being that as Apple doesn't have one so Appcelerator can't.

The Appcelerator documentation for Streaming Audio at http://wiki.appcelerator.org/display/guides/Audio+APIs suggests we instead use 'pause' and 'resume' events to stop and start the audio while a call is in progress. Surely though this wrecks being able to play audio in the background as 'pause' and 'resume' are also detected when the app goes to the background.

In any case when using 'background modes' in the info.plist the audio automatically fades on an incoming call. The trick is to be able to restart the current stream when app has resumed from a call and not just come back from the background.

Can anybody suggest a workaround/hack .. or shine any light on this at all as it seems quite important to be able to restart a stream that's been paused in this way.

— asked November 16th 2011 by Patrick Mounteney
