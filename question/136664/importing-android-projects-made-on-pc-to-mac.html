Importing Android projects made on PC to Mac

What do we have to do to import an android project made on PC over to a Mac? It never allows us to add 'iPhone/iPad' once we import. Super annoying! I think in earlier versions you could add and 'iphone' folder to the build. That no longer seems to work.

— asked May 8th 2012 by Chris Flowers

  • Chris,

    Create a new empty project targeting the platforms you needs.
    Copy your resources files from the other project into the new project where appropriate.
    Modify the tiapp.xml to match the guid, appid, etc from the other project.

    Run, enjoy.
