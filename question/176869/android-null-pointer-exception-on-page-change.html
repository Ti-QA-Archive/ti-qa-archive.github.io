    First, the answer to your questionâ¦. You don't want to use TableView.add()â¦. you are looking for TableView.appendRow(). iOS lets you nest some views that Android does not (like you can add child views to buttons, etc).

    Second, Android really, really hates input fields in tableviews. That's not going to work out well for you.
