Jumping between XML requests crashes the app constantly?

I have a series of drill down tableviews which are based on XML data from the web.

If i quickly go through the drill down menu the app crashes every time. By quickly I mean that if I choose one of the tableview rows before the entire tableview has loaded from the web (which forces the app to load new XML data for the submenu items) the app will crash, however if I go slowly and let the menu data fully load first then click on a row, everything works just fine.

My presumption here is that if appcelerator has not finished a given XML connection before trying to initiate a new one then this causes a crash.

Does anyone know of a convenient solution to this issue?

Thank you!

    yes, the concurrent httpclients will most likely make your app crash, I've been there. As a workaround, I could suggest to put a check on the second XML request, and if the first connection is still loading, make it wait until it finishes.
