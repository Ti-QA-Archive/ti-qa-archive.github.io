Appcelerator splash screen iPad resizes

When I launch an app for iPad; the red color default appcelerator splash screen loads and then the splash screen moves a little to the left and then the app starts.

Why is this happening ?

    Hi

    When you have an app that includes the status bar, the normal bar at the top of the screen the iPad splash screens should remove that 20 or 40 pixels from the top edge - otherwise this shift occurs.

    • Standard
      • Portrait: 768x1004px
      • Landscape: 1024x748px
    • Retina
      • Portrait: 1536x2008px
      • Landscape: 2048x1496px

    If your app is fill screen (no status bar on the top edge) then you simply use the full resolution for your splash screens;

    • Standard
      • Portrait: 768x1024px
      • Landscape: 1024x768px
    • Retina
      • Portrait: 1536x2048px
      • Landscape: 2048x1536px

    So if you use a size that is incorrect the normal splash screen should be OK but then Titanium shows the bigger screen in the space of the full screen less the status bar - so it slightly reduces in size.
