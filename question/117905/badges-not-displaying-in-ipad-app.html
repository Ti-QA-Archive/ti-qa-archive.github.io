Badges not displaying in iPad app

I've pasted my code below. I can't get a badge to display in my 'Notifications' tab. I don't have an icon assigned for the tab. Could that be the problem? I've also tried using the setBadge method to no avail. I've also tried adding the badge after the tab is added to the tab group to no avail. Any idea why this isn't working? I've tested in the simulator and on my iPad and the badge doesn't display in either. 


var win_notifications = Ti.UI.createWindow({
    title: 'Notifications',
    backgroundColor:'#fff',
    url: 'notifications.js',
    tabBarHidden: false,
    orientationModes: [Titanium.UI.LANDSCAPE_RIGHT]
});

var tab_notifications = Ti.UI.createTab({
    title: 'Notifications',
    window:win_notifications
});

tab_notifications.badge = 3;

tabGroup.addTab(tab_notifications);
