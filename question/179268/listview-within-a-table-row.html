  • Accepted Answer

    It's not just a bad idea, it won't work. Don't consider this as a bug. It's a wrong implementation - you shouldn't do it this way.

    The TableView proxy and the ListView proxy end up in the same components at native level. And you should not nest them. Go by either a TableView or a ListView.
