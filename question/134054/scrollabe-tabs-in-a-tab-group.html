    Hi Vibhor,

    Regarding your question about having a scrollable tab group, unfortunately this is not a native implementation, so it is not available 'out-of-the-box'.

    However you could program it yourself using Titanium. All you really would have to do is create a Ti.UI.ScrollableView, place it at the bottom of the screen, and then add Ti.UI.ImageView objects to it. After that, just add an event listener that listens for 'click' events. When a click it received on a certain Ti.UI.ImageView object, you should open a certain window/view. It will automatically scroll for you left/right, so you don't have to worry about any of that.

    I hope this helps.
