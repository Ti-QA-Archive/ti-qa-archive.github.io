iPhone 6 Plus screen width

I am trying to determine on the fly in my app whether the device is a iPhone 6 Plus. I read somewhere (sry no link) that the height and width are the way to go. iPhone 6 being 375 wide and iPhone 6 Plus being 414.

I am currently using Ti.Platform.displayCaps.platformWidth to detect the width and on emulator everything is fine - on device the width is coming back as 375. At first I thought it was that I had missed a splash screen or app icon to get the phone out of compatability mode so I used this http://ticons.fokkezb.nl/ to recreate them all (only portrait though)

I am still having my problem on device though.

Would anyone be able to help me?

Using Ti 3.4.0 GA

    Solved!

    The iPhone 6 Plus can be setup with a display zoom (thanks apple!!!). This will essentially make it act like an iPhone 6 in terms of display. My device was set to "Zoomed" instead of "Standard".

    Went into Settings->Display & Brightness->Display Zoom and selected Standard as the option.
