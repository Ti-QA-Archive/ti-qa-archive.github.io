  • Accepted Answer

    For iOS/iphone, force a full rebuild by deleting the contents of build&#x2F;iphone, selecting Edit Project in Titanium Developer, clicking the Save Changes button, and then launching the app.

    For android, only delete the contents of the immediate subdirectories of &#x2F;build&#x2F;android, otherwise you may encounter problems. Watch ticket #2852 to be updated by email of a resolution. Then Save Changes and launch the app, as above.
