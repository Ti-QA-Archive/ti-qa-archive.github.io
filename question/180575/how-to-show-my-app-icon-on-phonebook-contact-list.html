  • Hello Husain John Rizvi,

    You can simply show your app icon anywhere in your app using "backgroundImage" or "image" property 

    backgroundImage:"/appicon.png" // for any view

or

image:"/appicon.png" // for imageView

    Thanks.

 </manifest>
