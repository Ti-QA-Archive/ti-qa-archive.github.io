Portrait only on Android?

I can't seem to get my Android app to only support portrait mode. I have the following in my tiapp.xml, but it doesn't seem to do anything. When I put my app in landscape mode the app rotates, which is NOT what I want.

<manifest android:versionCode="1" android:versionName="1">
    <supports-screens android:anyDensity="true" android:screenOrientation="portrait" />
</manifest>

What am I missing?

  • I had fixed this issue by adding 

    android:screenOrientation="portrait"

    in custom android manifest in each activity tag. For eg.

    <activity android:screenOrientation="portrait" android:configChanges="keyboardHidden|orientation" android:launchMode="singleTask" android:name="ti.modules.titanium.map.TiMapActivity"/>
