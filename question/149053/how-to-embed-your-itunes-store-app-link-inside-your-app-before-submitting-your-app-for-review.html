    Hi

    When you add your app to the app store and BEFORE you submit it for review there is a link View in App Store in the App Information page. You do not even need to have uploaded the app at this point.

    This gives you your eventual app store link and you use this to include in your app.

  • You can use this link:

    https://itunes.apple.com/app/id361285480?mt=8&at=10l6dK

    Change the number here "361285480" to the Apple ID you get from iTunes Connect after creating the app there.
    Pay attention that if you want to directly open the App Store/iTunes you must not remove the "at=…".

    Moreover, If you know this link would be open on a Mac only you can replace the "https" with "macappstore" so it won't open safari first and open directly the store (in case that's a mac app). on iOS 7 this problem was solved if you are using this link on older versions of iOS you shall try "itms-apps" instead of the "http" to not open safari before the app store…

    On your case I think "Https" is the best (would work and open the store everywhere). Also, Pay attention that other answer could result in having country code on the link that would make it direct to the specific store (would ask user to change store if he defined a different one there he would probably won't be able to download because his account is for different store).
