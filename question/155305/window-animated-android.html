  • Android Window transitions don't support animations. Except the one when you click the back button.

    You could always create your own animations and apply them to your windows, but you might run into performance issues when doing that. (I tried doing that once and it wasn't smooth enough, so we had to remove them)

    Hope this clears that up for you
