„There was an error saving changes. Please try again later or contact iTunes Connect Support at itunesconnect@apple.com. “

Hi,
it is not a question – but a solved issue.

Since a lot of days this message appears in itunesconnect.
Why comes this message?
Since new SDK for iOS5 the organizer makes screen shots in double size (640x960) and the ajax uploader in itunes connect doesnt accept it.
Solution: resizing of screen shots before uploading.

Best regards
Rainer
