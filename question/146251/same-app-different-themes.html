Hello,
I write an app, to make different versions of the same, the only difference is the data source API request that are going to be different topics.

How is the best way to make this, i have the app ready, what are your suggestions?
Different branches for every topic?
Copy the App for every topic?
or something else.

  • If you are literally going to try and release a completely different app for each topic, then you'll have to copy the app over to a new app because its going to need a new name and appid. So you'll get MyApp Topic1, MyApp Topic2, etc.
    Often times I don't really see the use case for this. And Apple may not allow a bunch of the exact same apps just pointed at different data source (its all relative, depending on the content of course).
    In the end, if you plan on launching separate apps for each topic, then each app will need a unique name and appid.
