How to Show Random Splash Screen out of many Splash Screens?

Hi all!
I want to show the random splash screen whenever my app started.
let's say I have 20 Splash Screens, I want to show random Splash Screen every time when my app started, to make my app attractive.
can anybody tell me about splash screen changing on the basis of random number through javascript code?

    you can't change the native splashscreen, you could start the application with a blank (let's say black) splashscreen, and then, before your app starts, you could show a random image like this:

    var images = ["image1.png", "image2.png", "image3.png"];
var splashScreen = Ti.UI.createImageView({image:images[Math.round(Math.random()*images.length-1)]});
win.add(splashScreen);

setTimeout(function(){
   splashScreen.visible = false;
}, 2000);
