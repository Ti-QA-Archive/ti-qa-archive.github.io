iPad, iPhone in-house app distribution in behalf of a Client with Enterprise distribution license.

How to distribute Titanium iPhone/iPad apps in behalf of a client with a current Enterprise distribution license?

I have created a Titanium app with the help of Titanium Studio for a client who just purchased an Enterprise Distribution License. The problem is that his company does not have either the time or the know-how how to do any of this. In fact they want to give me the log in ID and password of the current Enterprise Agent, so that I could get the Distribution Certificate and do all that necessary to distribute the app to around 60 of their employees. I am not sure, however, that this would be the right way, mostly because all correspondence would go to the currently designated Agent's email address, which I don't have access to. Should I ask the company to either invite me to be the Agent, or, assign me an Agent status before I do anything else? Unfortunately, I cannot find any information on this subject (whether or not a contractor can be the Enterprise Agent for his client).

I would like to hear from anyone who's been in a similar situation, but most of all I would like to hear from somebody who knows what is the best practice in this case.

Thank you in advance,

Alex B.

2) Titanium SDK version: 3.0.2.201302191606
3) IOS 5.0, Xcode 4.3
4) IOS Simulator
5) Host Operating System: Mac OS X version 10.7
6) Ti Studio build: 3.0.2.201302191606, Date: 3/4/2013
