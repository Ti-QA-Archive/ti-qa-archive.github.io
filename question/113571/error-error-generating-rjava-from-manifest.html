  • Hi Eric

    Which version of Windows are you using, and what processor and RAM is installed?

    Ensure you are entering an App ID in the format, com.companyname.appname.

    Install Continuous Builds 1.4.X. Does it happen launching your app using it?

    Try deleting your build files and forcing a full rebuild:

    For android, only delete the contents of the immediate subdirectories of &#x2F;build&#x2F;android, otherwise you may encounter problems. See ticket #2852 for more info about this issue. Then click the Save Changes button to force a full rebuild and launch the app.

    Cheers
