Kindle fire slow keyboard input

The keyboard input on the kindle fire is incredibly slow when entering text into a text field. Its almost unusable. The delay between the button click and the text appearing in the text field is about 10 seconds. It works just fine on iphone, ipad and other android devices. Has anybody seen this behavior?
