Vertical slider for Android

Hi,

I am trying to use a vertical slider in Android using the following code:

var t = Titanium.UI.create2DMatrix();;
t = t.rotate(-90);

slider = Titanium.UI.createSlider({
    min:1.0,
    max:10.0,
    value:1,
    width:270,
    height:'auto',
    top:140,    
    transform:t,
});

view.add(slider);

The vertical slider can be seen in the view, but the performance of the slider is very poor. If I click on the slider, then the slider is not getting updated every time and the singletap event is not getting generated.

Has anyone faced this problem or know any workaround?

Thanks in advance !
