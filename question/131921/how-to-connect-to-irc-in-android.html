  • You will first need to understand IRC. RFC 1459 and RFC 2812 define the IRC client/server protocols and command structures.

    For the javascript, take a look at jisirc. This will help you understand the flow. Using that as your basis (and some of that code can be ported over) you will look at the Ti.Network.Socket-Module for the basic building block of your TCP connections.
