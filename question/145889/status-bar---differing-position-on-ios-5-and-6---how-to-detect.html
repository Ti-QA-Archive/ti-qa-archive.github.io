Status bar - differing position on iOS 5 and 6 - how to detect?

Hi

I have a simple problem - when turning my app to landscape orientation, it resizes to the new width. In iOS 5 the status bar shifts to the top (in landscape). In iOS 6 however, the status bar stays where it was in portrait.
This creates a difference in the width the landscape window should be.

How do I detect where the status bar is appearing? Or can I standardise the behaviour across iOS 5 and 6?

    Are you still seeing the same thing if so please create a simple new empty project and update your tiapp.xml file to include the entries in the first code block. Then report back with your SDK version info and results.
