  • Search for "TiUiTableView.m" (should be located in /Titanium/mobilesdk/osx/[version]/iphone/Classes) and change this line

    tableHeaderPullView.backgroundColor = [UIColor lightGrayColor];

    simply to

    tableHeaderPullView.backgroundColor = [UIColor clearColor];

    Clean the project and after next build, the headerPullView is transparent ;)
