The phone will not be local unfortunately so I can't synch locally to my development machine.

    You can't even deploy to your own device for testing until you have the apple developer's license. (Disclaimer: I suppose it is possible if you jailbreak your phone, but I don't have any experience there.)

    Once you pay the $99 and get a developer's license, apple allows you to create "ad hoc provisioning profiles" that list the UDID of each device you want to use for testing. You are allowed to enter up to 100 unique values in a single year.

    Once you have the license, take a look at this thread.
