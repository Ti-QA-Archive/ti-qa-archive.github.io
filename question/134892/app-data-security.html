App Data Security

Hi,
I've been building my application for about a month now, and now that it's finally finished, we found a major issue - the data saved in SQLite database is completely naked. It's just a matter of unzipping the .ipa or .apk file, and anyone can read our data.
It is a data-driven application, and we don't want people to just get our database of data.

A few weeks ago the Appcelerator Blog posted "Namu" app as the featured app. Turns out that if you take the .apk file and unzip it, you can get access to all of the .json files and the mp3's, as a result, again - easy application data theft.

I am looking for some solution to secure the data. As far as I know SQLchipher could be an option, but there is no support for that…
