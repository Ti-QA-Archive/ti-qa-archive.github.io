    Hi

    It is clear you are unhappy and frustrated, but from what I can tell you had a single question yesterday that that yet to be answered.

    There are a few reasons why that is;

    • You asked a question that very few people will have experienced
    • You asked this question on a Sunday
    • The question is best answered by an Appcelerator staff member
    • Employees are not usually available during the weekends and even those that are, this is a specifically difficult question
    • The Q&A is community driven and almost all the people giving answers are not employees, are not getting paid and are not doing this in their own spare time
    • You have now provided 3 answers to your own question yesterday, to those looking at the list of questions - they see a big 3 in the question box - they figure that question has already had 3 people assisting - so no need to jump in. Use comments next time on your own question.
    • Those answers appear as if someone else wrote them rather than you - so it also confuses things.

    Tone down the negative stance you have as you are going to get no-where with a bad attitude.

    When I started this message you had -1, right now it is -5. Be polite and patient, we all have times when we need assistance - but it is not our fault your system didn't/doesn't work or that the right person with the right knowledge has appeared right away (and on a weekend).
