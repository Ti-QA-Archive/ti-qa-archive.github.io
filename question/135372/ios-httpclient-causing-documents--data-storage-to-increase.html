iOS HttpClient causing "Documents & Data" storage to increase

I have an app that makes HttpClient calls to a server. Everything works fine, however I notice that every time I open the app and close it, then go look at the how much storage my app takes up on the device (Settings/Usage/my app "Documents & Data")…it has gotten bigger.

My app is 10 MB in size and when first installed "Document & Data" is only 10 kb. Now its up to 91 MB! The only way I know to reduce the size is by uninstalling and re-intalling the app.

Is there anyway I can prevent this in code?

One side note…I place the the data from my httpClient calls into Ti.App.DataRetrieved. Is this growing?

      Ti.App.DataRetrieved = parseMyData(httpClient.responseText);

      when it should be like below

      Ti.App.Properties.setString('dataRetrieved', JSON.stringify(parseMyData(httpClient.responseText)));

var myData = JSON.parse(Ti.App.Properties.getString('dataRetrieved'));
