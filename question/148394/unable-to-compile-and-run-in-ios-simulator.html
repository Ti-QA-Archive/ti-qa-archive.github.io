        <Button title="Open Blue Window" onClick="openBlueWindow">
      </Window>
    </NavigationGroup>
 </Window>

    • @Juan, I've seen this error dozens of times and it's a real bugger to track down. What usually works is:

      1. Perform the build again
      2. It will launch the iOS Simulator with a black screen
      3. The CLI will show the error
      4. Go back to the simulator and from the "iOS Simulator" menu at the top, select "Reset content and settings"
      5. After the simulator resets, quit the simulator
      6. Re-build your app again

      That usually fixes it. Give it a shot and let us know how it goes.
