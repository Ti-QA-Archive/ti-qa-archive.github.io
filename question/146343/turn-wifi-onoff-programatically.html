  • Hi
    You can open iPhone settings from application by

    Ti.Platform.openURL('prefs:root=LOCATION_SERVICES');

    However it WILL NOT work in any iOS 5.1 or higher, Apple removed this ability starting iOS 5.1.

    iOS 5.0 - It Works
    iOS 5.1 - Does Not work
    iOS 6.0 - Does Not work

    • For android you may use :

      var intent = Ti.Android.createIntent({action: "android.settings.WIRELESS_SETTINGS"});
Ti.Android.currentActivity.startActivity(intent);
