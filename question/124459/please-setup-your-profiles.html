Please Setup your Profiles

Wanted to remind the new developers (and some of the older ones as well) to go and setup their Developer Profiles.

I'd suggest that you setup at least one way for other devs to reach you off-forum. At minimum, enable DevMail. Your contact information is protected from prying eyes until you respond but allows other devs to follow up on conversations after a thread has been answered, archived, or locked.

Without trying to sound like a creeper, a Twitter or Website URL allows other devs to see what you may be thinking or working on.
