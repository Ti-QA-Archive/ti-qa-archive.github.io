Is it possible to "replace" an App in the App Store with a Titanium built App?

Hey all, I currently have an native iOS app in the App Store, and I want to change it out for my new Titanium built app. Is this possible? From the user point of view, I just want them to see the chhange as simply a version upgrade, but obviously in the background its a whole new app built under titanium.

If this is possible, is it just a matter of using the same App name, and then uploading it to itunes connect?

Thanks!
