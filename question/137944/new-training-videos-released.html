New Training Videos Released!

Hey, fellow Titanium-ers… yea, there's no cool way to say that. :P

Anyhoot (yes, it's a word!), this isn't really a question. I just wanted to let you guys know that Tim Poulsen, Lead Trainer and Curriculum Developer for Appcelerator, just got through releasing an updated version of his video training series, Building Native Mobile Apps with Titanium!

I've looked through it, and it's really great stuff! It's been updated with current best practices, including how to properly use CommonJS, the 'encouraged' way to build best-of-breed apps. There's even a new, in-depth video about the new Appcelerator Cloud Services feature and how to go about registering and using it (big time plus for me!).

Okay, I know this probably sounds like some sort of scripted marketing… er, script (:P). Don't worry though; I'm not being paid for this. I'm just really excited as I've been waiting for this for a good while.

Anyways, I'm gonna shut up now. Hope you guys enjoy it!
Best of luck and code strong! :)
