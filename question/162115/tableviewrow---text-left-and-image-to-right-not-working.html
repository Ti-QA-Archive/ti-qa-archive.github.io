TableViewRow - Text left and image to right not working

Hi,

I've created a TableView with some data but am having issues getting data in.

I want a number to the left, some text in the middle and an arrow image to the right.

At present I have:

<TableView class="leftTable">
            <TableViewRow id="headerRow" title="Objective"></TableViewRow>
            <TableViewRow id="dataRow" title="How long to the xyz" left="1" setRightImage="/rightArrow.png"></TableViewRow>
</TableView>

Any help would be appreciated. Oh and also the text in the middle will be quite long i.e. over multiple lines would \n do the trick with handling that?

Thanks.

    Hi

    I would create your TableViewRow's like so:

    <TableViewRow>
    <View>
        <Label id="number" />
        <Label id="text" />
        <ImageView id="image" />
    </View>
</TableViewRow>

    I am simply passing a containing view to the row containing the components you want. This will allow you to position your number, text and image how you want, giving you more control. By using a label you will be able to wrap your long text over multiple lines also.
