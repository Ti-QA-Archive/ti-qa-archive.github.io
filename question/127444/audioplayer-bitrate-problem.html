  • In my case, when this problem occurred "out of the blue" with an iOS app I'm developing, it was because I'd left my OS X audio device on something that wasn't really valid. I just had to change it back in System Preferences > Sound (Internal Speakers or LED Cinema Display; my "Aggregate Device" was broken).
