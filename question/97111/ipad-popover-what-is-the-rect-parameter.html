    • For the popover widget, the "rect" property defines the size of the popover (via the "height" and "width" properties) and the location of the popover on the screen via the "x" and "y" properties.

      That's what I figured the properties were used for, however, the problem I was having was that the popover wasn't located on the screen where I expected it should be, despite setting the "x" and "y" properties. What prompted my "nevermind" comment was when I finally figured out that I had inadvertently mixed up the "x" and "y" properties. Once I corrected that problem the popover was located where I expected it should be.
