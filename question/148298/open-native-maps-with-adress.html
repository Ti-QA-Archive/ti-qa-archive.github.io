  • This is a really helpful article on developer.apple.com about the different native url schemes in iOS.

    You should be able to do what you want by calling:
    Ti.Platform.openURL(&quot;http:&#x2F;&#x2F;maps.apple.com&#x2F;?q=12345%2C+Example+Street+10%2C+Example+City&quot;);
