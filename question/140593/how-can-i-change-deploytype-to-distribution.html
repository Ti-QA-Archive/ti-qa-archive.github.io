How can I change deployType to distribution?

I used Ti.App.getDeployType() to get deploy type. It always displays development, but it is a read-only property. How can I change it to distribution? I thought it should be modify in "tiapp.xml" but I didn't see any statement on the official wiki.
https://wiki.appcelerator.org/display/guides/tiapp.xml+and+timodule.xml+Reference.
If the app built with development type, all the js sources will be included in ipa or apk?
Thanks a lot.

    Hi Jerry

    It is a read-only property.

    It will tell you if your app is running in the simulator, on a device for testing or has been Distributed by the App Store (or Android Play store) before you put it on the device.

    You cannot change it, you can us it to perform a different job depending on the answer it gives you though as you could use it in an if or switch statement.

    If you are trying to get your app in deployment mode then you need to create difference certificates for the app re-compile using those certificates and then push to the App Store or Android Play store.

    Hope this makes it clearer.
