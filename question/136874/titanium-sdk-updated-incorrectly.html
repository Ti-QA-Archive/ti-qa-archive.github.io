  • Couple of things I've discovered:

    • long filenames and directories with spaces are a bad thing period, and worse on Windows. On OSX I could build apps all day, but when it came time to build custom modules, the Android NDK was upset. Ended up relocating my tiStudio workspace over that one.

    • ONCE in all the time I've been working with Titanium I'v had an SDK update go awry and only partially install. At that time, links to the releases were in the announcements. That has been overlooked with 2.0.1GA release announcement I won't tell you to go the continuous builds area as its difficult to find the actual full releases links there among all the minor releases.
