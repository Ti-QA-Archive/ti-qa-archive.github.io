How to prevent Iphone's keypad from covering textareas?

I guess it's a quite common problem, but I didnt found any proper solution for it. If I have a view with several textarea-fields and click on a field to fill in Text, Android is moving up the field so that the keypad is not covering my input field. BUT IPhone doesn't do that? The keypad just covers 40% of the screen and the textarea-fields on the bottom of my view are not visible why I try to fill them out.

Is there any easy example how to make the highlighted field scroll up so that the keypad is not covering it ? I experimented a little with scroll-views, but they do not seem to work at all. So I need a simple working example somehow..
