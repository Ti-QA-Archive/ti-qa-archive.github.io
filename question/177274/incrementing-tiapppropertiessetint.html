Incrementing Ti.App.Properties.setInt

Can someone give an example of how to increment Ti.App.Propertes.setInt? I would have thought it would have been fairly straightforward but it's not working like I expect. 

Ti.App.Propertes.setInt('myCounter', 0);
Ti.API.info(Ti.App.Properties.getInt('myCounter') + 1));

// Returns 01 instead of 1
