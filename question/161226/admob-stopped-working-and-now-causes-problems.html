  • GOT IT!!!!

    At last!

    I'd tried everything - made a fresh, default app with nothing but the relevant code etc etc etc

    The problem was in the TiApp.xml file!

    I'd followed the instructions for 'locking' screen orientation - which says about copying every activity across from the android manifest after a compile and it was the line pertaining to the AdActivity that was causing the problem! Once I removed the following line, everything worked again :D

    &lt;activity android:configChanges=&quot;keyboard|keyboardHidden|screenLayout|uiMode|screenSize|smallestScreenSize&quot; android:name=&quot;com.google.ads.AdActivity&quot;&#x2F;&gt;
