app freeze on resume

Hello, I have a problem with resume from background.
When I put the app in background and reopen it immediately it is starting normally but if I perform other operations while it is in background, such open and use other applications etc, when I resume my app it has a little freeze time of 1-2 seconds before to become active. In this period, if I try to move the scrollView or press a button all seems to be locked.

Anyone experienced the same problem?
Thank you

  • I'm having the exact same problem, it seems to be common among all Ti apps. One guess I have is that while the app is in the background it's leaking memory somehow. When it resumes, the garbage collector kicks in (and blocks the UI for reasons I don't know). This hugely affects how customers perceive the performance of our app, because they often quickly jump in and out and every time they do so feel like it's "frozen". Has anyone done more testing or could we get an official response beyond "please ask your question better"? IMO this is critical.

  • I had this problem when I had the Titanium Analytics enabled. It seemed to me on Resume it was using a network connection, causing quite a long pause. After disabling the Analtics in my tiapp.xml file everything was pretty much as expected.
