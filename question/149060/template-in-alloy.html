    I'm not in front of the computer right now to try it, but leftNavButton are not actual object, but properties of the Window object, so I think you cannot reference them as <leftNavButton> on your XML. I think you'll need to instance them as buttons, and then assign them as left and right from your controller code.

    Take a look at these examples to see if they help: https://github.com/appcelerator/alloy/tree/master/test/apps/basics/markup_require/views

  • Ok, as you said, this is working (sorry for the french, it is for the idea) :

    agenda.xml

    <Alloy>
    <Window title="Agenda">
        <!--<Require type="view" src="navButtonTemplate" id="navButton"/>-->
        <LeftNavButton>
            <Require type="view" src="navLeftButtonTemplate"/>
        </LeftNavButton>
        <RightNavButton>
            <Require type="view" src="navRightButtonTemplate"/>
        </RightNavButton>
        <!-- Ajouter la pub -->
        <ImageView id="image" image="pubImages/logoNetFenua.jpg" />
        <!-- Ajouter le bandeau de contrôle -->
        <Label>On est dans l'agenda!</Label>
    </Window>
</Alloy>

    navLeftButtonTemplate.xml

    <Alloy>
    <Button title="Retour"/>
</Alloy>
