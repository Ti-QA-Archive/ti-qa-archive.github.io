  • Kyle, not sure if you solved this. But I simply have a view which the webView sits in and is hidden. The view also has an activity indicator and a loading label. When the 'load' event fires on the webView, I hide the indicator and label and show the webView.

    This allows you to style the loading screen how ever you like.
