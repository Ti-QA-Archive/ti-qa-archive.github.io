  • How about;

    var win = Ti.UI.createWindow();

var leftNav = Ti.UI.createView({top:0;left:0, width:'30%', height:'100%');
var contentView = Ti.UI.createView({top:0;left:'30%', width:'70%', height:'100%');

win.add(leftNav);
win.add(contentView);

win.open()

    Now just populate leftNav with your navigation controls, and update contentView when your leftNav selection changes.
