    Hello Mahmudul,

    Thanks for your inquiry, According to official support page the error "Unable to start Genymotion virtual device. Check if your video card supports OpenGL. If no then the virtual device can not start. If yes you should update your drivers. Detailed information: Failed to initialize backend EGL display(error:4) If needed update your video card drivers", occurred because of an outdated video card driver or a very old video chipset.

    Update your video card driver using the latest version provided by your manufacturer (available on their website).

    Hope this helps. Thanks.
