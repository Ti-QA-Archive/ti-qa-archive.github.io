Easy iPad Image/Font Scaling?

I have a completed iPhone app that we just installed on a new iPad3. With the 2x scale button for iPhone apps it looks fantastic. I don't see a need to change the size of any of the images or make any real changes to the app for iPad. I want the thing to look just like it does at 2x.

With that being said, before I go adding tons of lines of 'if ipad' is there a simple way to do one major setting that says if its iPad set everything 2x? Or will I have to edit all sizing information and make the images larger?
