  • Hi Tom,

    There are native android modules available for reading PDFs that you may be able to wrap into a Titanium Android module. Some open sourced 3rd party Android pdf modules are:

    MuPDF (http://www.mupdf.com/)
    apv (https://code.google.com/p/apv/)
    vudroid (https://code.google.com/p/vudroid/)
    apdfviewer (https://code.google.com/p/apdfviewer/)
    droidreader (https://code.google.com/p/droidreader/)

    Thanks.
