Camera capabitlies and licensing

Hey guys,

I'm evaluating Ti for our cross platform app. I've tried to research this but have been unsuccessful - would appreciate if someone experienced can shed some light:

The app will show camera feed (NOT the camera app) with an image overlaid. Autofocus should be available. On tapping the screen, the image within the overlay is uploaded to a server. The app has to work on BOTH iOS and Android.

From my research, these are the points I have been able to understand:

  1. Android does not support overlaying on the camera view
  2. Uploading is possible through XHR

We are a small studio developing this app and the licensing terms are confusing. This app will be create for a client who may or may not charge for it. Will using the Free SDK keep us in the clear?

    Hi

    • Android does allow overlays on the camera
    • You can upload media (image, video, audio or any files) using XHR
    • You can create apps for yourself or your clients which are free or paid apps without paying Appcelerator

    Appcelerator only charge for extras like; Additional Support, Cloud services (outside the free allotments), custom modules (from the market place), Training and Certification.

    You will like the platform - it is fast and flexible and always being updated. Getting from nothing to app is very fast.
