  • The solution for me was:

    • Make a appicon@2x.png (114 x 114)
    • Make a appicon-72.png (72 x 72)
    • Put them under resources/iphone
    • Copy build/iphone/info.plist into yourproject/info.plist
    • Edit info.plist, and be sure to have the icon section like this:
      <key>CFBundleIconFile</key>
  <string>appicon.png</string>
<key>CFBundleIconFiles</key>
<array>
  <string>appicon.png</string>
  <string>appicon@2x.png</string>
  <string>appicon-72.png</string>
</array>

    That worked for me :)
