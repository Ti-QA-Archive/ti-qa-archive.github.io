  • app version must be compatible with HTC drivers, Or you can use DriverAssist. It has a database of over 26 million drivers and can identify HTC devices and almost any other manufacturer's hardware with very high accuracy. DriverAssist performs an in-depth scan of your entire system and all devices attached to it and verifies that you have the latest and most compatible drivers installed. It determines which drivers are missing, corrupt or obsolete.
