  • The screen issue is a common one in Android development. It's not really the fault of Titanium, but moreso the lack of standardized hardware which runs the Android OS. You can't even target a recent release of the OS if you expect to make it to a large number of devices, due to carriers disinterest in providing timely updates. Hopefully, we'll see some improvements to this as the platform progresses.
