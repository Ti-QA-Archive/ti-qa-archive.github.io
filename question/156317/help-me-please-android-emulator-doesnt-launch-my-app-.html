  • I would go into the project in your workspace from the command line and try to enter in the following command: 

    titanium -build

    Allow titanium to build it and then take the apk (it it will build it) and install on the device. You don't even need to plug it in. You could use a service like dropbox and move it into your dropbox but open it up on your phone.
