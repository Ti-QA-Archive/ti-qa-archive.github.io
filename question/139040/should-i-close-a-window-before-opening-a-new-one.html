  • Hi Ryan.

    As Alexander says you can keep the window open, but it does depend on your application structure.

    If you open win 1, then win 2 then win 3 then you want to go back to win 1 you can use navGroup but it has issues across platforms. Or you should consider a bespoke menu system.

    The window stack works that every time you open a window its puts a new instance of that window in the stack so you could have win 1, win 2, win 1, win 3, win 2, win 1 in your stack and the windows are not actually updated. As you close a window it would go back to the previous one.
