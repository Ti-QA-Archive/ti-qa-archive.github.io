    Hi

    There are a couple of techniques you could use;

    • custom fireEvent & addEventListeners pairs to 'ask' for and 'answer' questions. Not ideal but works.
    • extend the returned variable to include customfunctions to act as getters and setters which you access from the step1() from step2()

    However if you are using a multiple step process, the tabgroup is not the best interface choice, you are better off creating a window for step 1, that calls step 2 as a new window and passes any data forward, then step 3 and so on. You should open a new window over your normal work so that the wizard can be closed part way through.

    This would be a simpler process.
