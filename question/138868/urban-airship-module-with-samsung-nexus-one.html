  • Well, I finally manage to get this to work. By modifying my manifest like that:

    Original:

    <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
    <services>
               <service url="VerifyNewCard.js" type="interval"/>
        </services>
        <manifest android:versionCode="1" android:versionName="1.0">
            <property name="ti.android.fastdev" type="bool">false</property>
            <supports-screens android:anyDensity="false"/>
            <activity android:configChanges="orientation|keyboardHidden" android:name="myActivty"/>
        </manifest>
</android>

    New:

     <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
        <manifest android:versionCode="1" android:versionName="1.0">
            <property name="ti.android.fastdev" type="bool">false</property>
            <supports-screens android:anyDensity="false"/>
        </manifest>
</android>

    Now I can see my application in the launcher.
