iPhone app "scales" to iPad

Have an little issue with an app i've developed towards iPhone. I thought that the app would run in "iPhone mode" on the iPad with zoomcontrols like other iPhone apps, but instead it launched in "fullscreen" like it would been an iPad app. How do i get the app to run in iPhone mode on iPad?

  • If you have unchecked iPad on the tiapp.xml screen, clean your project, rebuild.
    You may have to open the tiapp.xml file in the xml editor and make sure it says:

    <target device="ipad">false</target>
