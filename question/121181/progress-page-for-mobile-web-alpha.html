  • Ghaladen

    Thank you for this question and your interest in our Mobile Web SDK, which is currently at the beta stage of its development.

    The general goal is to support all the major browsers, such as those on iOS, Android, WebOS and Blackberry (at least, Blackberry with webkit, which I believe is only in BB 6.0). We will certainly consider adding support for other browsers, as demand from our community warrants.

    We currently track Mobile Web SDK issues internally, while the project is at a pre-release stage. As with all of our major projects, transparency will be crucial. Thus, its management will be moved to Jira around the time of its official release.

    I hope this addresses your concerns.
