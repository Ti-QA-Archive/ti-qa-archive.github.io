My first Titanium iPhone app

Well, I finally finished up my app for iPhone/iPod Touch using the unbelievably amazing Titanium SDK. The name of it is Insane Donkey, and it was approved last night for the App Store. Would love if a few of you could download it and check it out and maybe provide some feedback on whether you believe it has potential, what else could be included that would be beneficial, etc.

It's exciting! Thanks again to the guys at Appcelerator for making a wicked product for us developers who refuse to learn Obj-C. lol

Here is the link to my app:
http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/insane-donkey/id454729496?mt=8&ls=1
