  • Hi Arnold,

    What template you choose really depends on your requirements and how you want your application to look. If you want to replicate what you made with Phonegap, then choose a template closest to that. I can't really make a suggestion without knowing the flow of your app. I'd say a tabbed application is probably the best start in general, just because most apps adopt that format and you'll have a basis in which to add your own custom windows and components.

    If you provide further info about what you'd like to achieve I'd be happy to expand on that.
