  • ok i fixed "Conflicting jar files detected" error:

    copy google-play-services.jar from your android sdk location

    "/Android/SDK/extras/google/google_play_services/libproject/google-play-services_lib/libs"

    and paste/replace google-play-services.jar in

    "/Users/sam/Library/Application Support/Titanium/modules/android/ti.cloudpush/3.2.1/lib"

    and

    "/Users/sam/Library/Application Support/Titanium/modules/android/ti.map/2.1.4/lib"
