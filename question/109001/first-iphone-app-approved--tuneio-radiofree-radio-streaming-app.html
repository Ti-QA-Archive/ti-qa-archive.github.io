First iPhone App Approved ! Tuneio Radio.Free Radio Streaming App.

Just released on iTunes our first iPhone application created with Appcelerator.

http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/tuneio-radio/id416830542?mt=8&ls=1

http://tuneio.com

Using 1.4.3 SDK.

It includes Facebook and Twitter posting from the application and iAd and Admob ad support.

App was submitted on Jan 25th and approved today Feb 4th without any changes being required by apple.

We are now working on an Android version of the same app.
