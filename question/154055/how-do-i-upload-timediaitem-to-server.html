How do I upload Ti.Media.Item to server?

I let the user pick audio from his iOS device, using openMusicLibrary and in the success I get the Ti.Media by writing song = picked.items[0];.

Now I want to upload it via SidsClient. I could upload the image in a similar way by encoding the object to base64 and making it a string, then passing this in my XML header.

So, as the question is, how do I upload the Ti.Media.Item, or is there another way to pick an audio from the iOS's Music Library?

  • If you want to upload your audio blob to server it is simple as you think. By sending of Ti.Blob it will automaticly converted on right format:

    xhr.send({song: mytiblob});  // mytiblob is result of openMusicLibrary
