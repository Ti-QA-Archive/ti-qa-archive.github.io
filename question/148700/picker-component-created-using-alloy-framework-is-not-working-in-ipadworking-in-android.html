  • Accepted Answer

    iOS does not have a combobox. This is not a Titanium limitation, this is an iOS limitation, or choice, depending on who you ask. Android has one, which is why picker works as you expect. For iOS you can create a combobox-like experience using a combination of a couple components. Here's a link to a semi-modal picker which serves much as a combobox would: http://cssgallery.info/making-a-combo-box-in-titanium-appcelerator-code-and-video/
