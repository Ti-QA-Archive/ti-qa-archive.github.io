iOS app run on selected devices

I want my app to run only on iPad 3 and 4 and also iPhone 3 - 5.

How can I specify that in Titanium ?

    Hi

    App compatibility is based on operating system version and not specific devices I am afraid.

    You will find that titanium currently supports iOS 5.0 and above, this is currently iPhone 3GS and higher. This is because the 3GS can run iOS 6.0. All current iPads are supported.

    Here is a current list.

    • iPhones

      • iPhone 1 iOS 3.1.3
      • iPhone 3G iOS 4.2.1
      • iPhone 3GS iOS 6.1.x
      • iPhone 4 iOS 6.1.x
      • iPhone 4S iOS 6.1.x
      • iPhone 5 iOS 6.1.x

    • iPod touches

      • iPod Touch 1 iOS 3.1.3
      • iPod Touch 2 iOS 4.2.1
      • iPod Touch 3 iOS 5.1.1
      • iPod Touch 4 iOS 6.1.x
      • iPod Touch 5 iOS 6.1.x

    • iPad

      • iPad 1 iOS 5.1.1
      • iPad 2 iOS 6.1.x
      • iPad 3 iOS 6.1.x
      • iPad 4 iOS 6.1.x
      • iPad Mini iOS 6.1.x
