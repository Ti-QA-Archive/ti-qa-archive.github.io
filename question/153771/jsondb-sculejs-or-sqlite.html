jsonDB, SculeJS or SQLite?

Hi i read the book "appcelerator Titanium Patterns and Best Prectices" and recomend to use jsonDB instead SQLite, but when i go to download it i read its deprecated and now is SculeJS. Its a good idea use SculeJS or you recommend use SQLite?

    All depends on what you need.
    For a small set of data I'd recommend a js solution.

    But SQLlite has it's place - if you need structured data or you have a big amount of data.

    Other NoSQL solutions might be

    TaffyDB - https://github.com/rborn/TaffyDb4Ti
    A native module for CouchDb - https://github.com/pegli/ti_touchdb (couchdb)

    I use the taffy one, but your needs might be different.
