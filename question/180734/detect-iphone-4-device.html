    • Hi. That's not the correct format of the data for .model

      eg: An iPhone 6 has the value : "iPhone7,1" (confusing, yes. Blame Apple)

      So, try simply running an app with this debug info on an iPhone 4 and 4s device (not simulator) and see wha the output is for : 

      Ti.API.info('device model : '+Ti.Platform.model);

      — commented February 10th 2015 by Kosso

    • OK. I looked it up for you here : http://www.everymac.com/ultimate-mac-lookup/?search_keywords=iphone+4

      You need to match the "Model ID"

      So:
      iPhone 4 (GSM model) = "iPhone3,1"

      iPhone 4 (CDMA) = "iPhone3,3"

      iPhone 4s = "iPhone4,1"
