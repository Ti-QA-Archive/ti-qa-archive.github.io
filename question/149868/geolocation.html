    The simple answer is: No it doesn't

    The more complete answer is: Since there is no data reaching the phone, you won't be able to access any map data (as this is streamed to the phone through the internet) You could technically have GPS information, but having a lock would take between 20 to 30 minutes and the location information would be much less accurate.
