It seems to make trouble under iOS7.
I have putted 

<backgroundModes>
          <mode>location</mode>
</backgroundModes>

in my tiapp.xml (ios section)

    Make sure to use the trackSignificantLocationChange property. This will enable the App to receive location changes longer than the 10 minutes allowed for a background service
