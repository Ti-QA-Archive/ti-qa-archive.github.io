Appcelerator Training Options

On the Custom Training page here: http://www.appcelerator.com/products/training/titanium-training/
the Level200 course which covers (UI Fundamentals, Working with Local and

  • FYI: I went ahead and purchased the $50 working with remote data module. Personally I did not find it very useful, I have been having difficulty with JSON which it claims to cover.

    In reality this is just a walk through of the code for the SnaPost app, the questions that are asked are hard to hear and the information doesn't really go into a lot of detailed explanation of whats happening or how you might adapt or alter the code or functions to serve other purposes.

    I was hoping/expecting that they would take things like xhr and JSON in isolation and then perhaps show them in action, but really, unless you are looking for a straight walkthrough of how Snapost works, I would probably give it a miss.
