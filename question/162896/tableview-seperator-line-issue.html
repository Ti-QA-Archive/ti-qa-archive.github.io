  • Hi kiran, i think what you mean is that the separatorline has not the full width of the tableview. to get a full line, use separatorInsents. 

    "#tbl_InfoList":{
    top:'154',
    height:Ti.UI.SIZE,
    backgroundColor:'transparent',  
    separatorInsets: {
        left:0,
        right:0
    }
}
