  • This is an issue I raised quite a while ago.

    Mainly, because Ti does actually use the 'MGSplitViewController' to deal with splitviews on the iPad. MGSVC does have a LOT of customisation capabilities, such as setting different widths and even making the splitview go horizontal.

    I wish they would expose more of this functionality to us.

    Here's more info on the MGSplitView Controller : http://mattgemmell.com/2010/08/03/mgsplitviewcontroller-updated/
