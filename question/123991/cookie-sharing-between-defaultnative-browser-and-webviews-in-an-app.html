Cookie sharing between default/native Browser and WebViews in an app

I have an app where I execute server-side scripts by using hidden WebViews and I saw that cookies are also set somewhere on my device by these server-side codes (as in any common browser).

My question is: are cookies shared between the app (i.e. Webviews created there) and the native Browser app? If cookies are not shared, what is their lifetime in an app, created via WebViews? I thought about making some tests but maybe an expert has an immediate answer here.

    Cookies are not shared between webviews and the browser. Cookies within the app follow the same rules as a cookie in the browser would.
