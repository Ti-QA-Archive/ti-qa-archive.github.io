  • Flash is only currently supported in android devices natively, and thus is the only place titanium can suport it. That said… all mobile flash, on all mobile platforms has been discontinued by Adobe, and thus you will soon not be able to use an SWF on any mobile platform.

    As recommended, you are probably better off porting your game to HTML5, or native Titanium.
