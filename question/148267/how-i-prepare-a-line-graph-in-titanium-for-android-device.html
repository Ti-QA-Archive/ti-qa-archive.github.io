  • Accepted Answer

    You might want to check out the dygraphs JavaScript Visualization Library. I've had success with it on iOS and Android, although it is a bit of a pain to get the interactive zoom working smoothly.

    For non-interactive charts just make sure you place a transparent view over the webview to prevent it from receiving events.
