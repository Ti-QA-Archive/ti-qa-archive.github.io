LiveView fails with i18n and .svn

I just added i18n folder with strings.xml under en and es folders for English and Spanish.
Everything works fine, until I check in the changes into SVN repo. The checkin creates .svn folder inside i18n directory, and that seems to interfere with LiveView.
Now when I run LiveView, my app shows the splash screen and then stops there with this error:

[LiveView] { [Error: ENOENT, open '/Users/magawa/Workspace/ABC/EnterpriseMobility/trunk/MyGNP-Titanium/i18n/.svn/strings.xml']
errno: 34,
code: 'ENOENT',
path: '/Users/magawa/Workspace/ABC/EnterpriseMobility/trunk/MyGNP-Titanium/i18n/.svn/strings.xml' }

Looks like it treats .svn as a locale folder or something… Is there any way to tell LiveView to ignore certain folders like .svn? Or a different way to handle i18n without getting into this problem?

The app is developed with Titanium SDK 3.2.3 GA in Appcelerator Studio build: 3.2.3.201404181520, targeting iPhone and Android.
Tested with LiveView on a simulator for iPhone Retina (4 inch 64-bit), running under OSX 10.9.3.

I looked around the Internet, including this forum, but could not find anything specific to this situation.
Any hint would be appreciated.
