Resize images right after download

Hi, I download some images to the user device. After the download I want resize the images and change their width to 320 and proportionally their height.

I easily download the images and when each image is downloaded I fire an event onImageDownloaded, passing f.nativePath as value of an object.

Then, in the body of the eventListener 'onImageDownloaded' I try the following:

var file = Titanium.Filesystem.getFile(Titanium.Filesystem.applicationDataDirectory, e.filepath.substring(e.filepath.lastIndexOf('/')+1));

file.write(
   Ti.UI.createImageView({
     image:e.filepath, 
     width:320,
     height:(newWidth/file.width)*file.height 
                    }).toImage());

but I can't get my images resized.

Any help is appreciated. :-)

  • That's an inefficient way to do it. Use Ti.Blob.imageAsResized(w,h) instead. (or imageAsThumbnail, depending on your use case)

    file.write(file.read().imageAsResized(320,heightCalculationHere));
