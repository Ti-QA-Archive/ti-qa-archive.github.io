close TiTabActivity and exit app

hi,

I'm trying to exit an android app when you press the back button on a modal window but there's alway's a black screen that pops out and I have to press back a second time to close it.

I suspect this is because I have a tabgroup that is initialized before I open the modal window and isn't properly closed.

here's my 'close' code for the button:


      @window.addEventListener 'android:back', =>

        if myapp.currentTabGroup?
          Ti.API.warn 'opening tabgroup'
          myapp.currentTabGroup.open()

        Ti.API.warn 'closing window NOW'
        for window in @splash_window.modalStack
          window.close()

        if @splash_window?
          Ti.API.warn 'closing splash window'
          @splash_window.close()

        Ti.API.warn 'closing currentActivity'
        activity=Titanium.Android.currentActivity
        activity.finish()

        Ti.API.warn 'closing TABGROUP'
        if myapp.currentTabGroup?
          myapp.currentTabGroup.close()

I've tried closing the tabgroup before and after the current activity but the TiTabActivity always appears and I don't know what else to do.
Is there a way to call the TiTabActivity and force close it? or force close the app?
