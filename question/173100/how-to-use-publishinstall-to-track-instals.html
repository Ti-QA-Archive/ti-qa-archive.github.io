  • Hi Eduard,

    as of our experience, that module stopped tracking FB activity on March 25th 2014, probably because FB dropped support for the old method of tracking installs by publishInstall in favor of activateApp.

    The module's author, Daniele, is not with FB anymore so we are stuck.
    Apparently, FB itself does not even know the module has been created by their own employees.

    The best thing is to expose activateApp in the Titanium Facebook module, which is now open source under Appcelerator Modules in GitHub, but this would be better managed by Ti team.
