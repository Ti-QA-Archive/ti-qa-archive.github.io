  • Well, reading from your tiapp.xml, you are not using 3.1.0 SDK at all.

    You should change the following line to the latest version:

    <sdk-version>3.0.2.GA</sdk-version>

    Keep in mind that when you upgrade your Titanium SDK, none of your projects will be affected. This means that you will have to manually go and change the sdk-version tag in your project file.
