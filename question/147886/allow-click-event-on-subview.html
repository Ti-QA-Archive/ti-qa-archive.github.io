Allow Click Event on Subview

Hi All,

I have an app that require a view like this

index.xml

<Alloy>
    <Window>
        <NavigationGroup id="navgroup" platform="ios,mobileweb">
            <Window id="win1" navBarHidden="true">
                <TableView id="table" top="0dp" height="100%" separatorStyle="none" >
                    <TableViewRow class="row">
                        <Require id="require" type="view" src="detail" />
                    </TableViewRow>
                </TableView>
            </Window>
        </NavigationGroup>
    </Window>
</Alloy>

detail.xml

<Alloy>
        <View class="developer" id="developer">
            <Label  text="blabla" class="content_title" />
            <ImageView right="20" image="first/hasChild.png" opacity="0.7" />
        </View>
</Alloy>

but i'm unable to obtain a click event on my view "developer"

detail.js

$.developer.addEventListener('click', function() {
    alert('test');
});

Where is my mistake ?

Thanks.

    alert(JSON.stringify(e));
});

    and use e.source to identify what was being clicked ?
