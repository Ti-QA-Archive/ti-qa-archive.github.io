Displaying selection from picker in Alloy

I have a TextField, when selected, the Picker opens. When something in the picker is selected, how can I display the chosen selection in the textfield?

$.output3.addEventListener(&#39;click&#39;, function(e){ $.picker2.show(); }); $.output3.editable = false;

    How about this?

    var selection = $.picker2.getSelectedRow(0).title;
$.output3.value = selected;
