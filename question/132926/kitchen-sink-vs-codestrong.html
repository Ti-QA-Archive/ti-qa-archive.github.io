    To simply answer your question, you should NOT use the kitchen sink as an example of how to code your application. It is designed to show how to use different aspects of the API's and shows examples of how to code things like Maps etc.

    For your application architecture you really need to look at the best practices documentation.

    https://wiki.appcelerator.org/display/guides/Best+Practices
