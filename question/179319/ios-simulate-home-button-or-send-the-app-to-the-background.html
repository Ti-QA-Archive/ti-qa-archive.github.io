iOS: Simulate home button or send the app to the background

Hi,

Is there a way to simulate the home button in iOS? I did it with android using this code 

var intent = Ti.Android.createIntent({
        action : Ti.Android.ACTION_MAIN
    });
intent.addCategory(Ti.Android.CATEGORY_HOME);
Ti.Android.currentActivity.startActivity(intent);

If it's not possible, then how can I send the app to the background?

I wanted to close(not kill/terminate) the app when it triggers a certain event.

Thanks!

    Hi John Chester Isidoro,

    Thanks for your concern, but you cannot change the behavior of the app on iOS like the home button is pressed. All you can do is to catch the Ti.App.pause and resume events like,

    Titanium.App.addEventListener('pause',function(e){
    //do something before pausing
});

Titanium.App.addEventListener('resume',function(e){
    //do something after resuming
});

    Check the appcelerator official doc for those events.

    Hope this will help you to clear things up for you.
