Table view separation line not full width??

Hi,

I have a tableview and have noticed that the separation line (in-between the rows) does not fill the width of the tableview. I'm left with a gap on the left hand side. How can i fix this?

    Short answer: That is the new iOS way.

    Have a look through the settings app. If you want to just run with it, then align with the gap on the side, which is 15.

    If you want to fight the trend, change the separatorColor of the TableView to match the backgroundColor and create your own separator on each row, e.g. using a thin View, image, etc.
