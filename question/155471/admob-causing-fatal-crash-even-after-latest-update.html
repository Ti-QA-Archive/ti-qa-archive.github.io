    Hi,
    you need set api level in your tiapp.xml file..

    set this tag and then test with clean build.

    <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
    <tool-api-level>14</tool-api-level>
</android>

    • Ah… your remark lead me to my solution ;-) You see, I have many API's installed..

      In the run-configurations I changed the Android API for this app to 4.2.2 (armeabi-v7a) and in Ti.xml I changed the tool-api-level to 17. It's working now. Thank you !
