        data.push(row);

  • You forgot to close the actual window in your app2.js file.

    Your Event Handler should look like this:

    closeWin.addEventListener('click', function(){
    win.close();
    win.remove(newView);
    newView = null;
});

    Or maybe I'm missing something here.
