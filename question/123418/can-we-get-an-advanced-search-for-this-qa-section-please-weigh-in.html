    • I think the sort goes something like this:

      • "Best" or accepted answer, if any, is first regardless of votes
      • Then sorted by number of votes, descending
      • Then sorted by date/time, ascending

      The idea is to give the "best" answer first followed by other answers which are voted a higher rating on the theory they may be the most meaningful to a user visiting the thread. When nothing is marked as the accepted answer, and nothing is voted up or down, then they appear chronological.

      Comments always appear under their associated answers.

      So there is some method to the madness. And it works well enough on short threads. But IMHO it is a complete bear on longer threads such as the Tips and tricks topic. When that has a new comment added to an answer, just try and find it fast! (Hint: I use the browser search function and look for "minutes ago" then "hours ago".)
