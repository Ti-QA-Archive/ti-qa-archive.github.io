  • Ok, I found a solution for my project.

    In my tiapp.xml, the sdk-version was reading 3.1.0.GA. I changed it to 3.1.3.GA.

    — commented November 1st 2013 by Reed Abbott

  • Update your Titanium SDK to 3.1.3 GA

    Then set the following properties when you create the window.

    var window = Titanium.UI.createWindow({
   statusBarStyle : Titanium.UI.iPhone.StatusBar.LIGHT_CONTENT,
   fullscreen:true
});
