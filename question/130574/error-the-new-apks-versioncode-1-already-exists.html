Error: The new apk's versionCode (1) already exists.

Hi,

I have created my first android app on titanium studio and was successful in deploying it in Android Market. Now That I have added some more features, I wanted to deploy this new version of the app on Market. I tried uploading the APK in the market it showed me the error

The new apk's versionCode (1) already exists.

Then I went to TiApp.xml and Changed the &lt;version&gt; tag to 0.1.1 and then again I created the build. Even now when I try to upload this new APK it gives the same error. Why could this be happening? Is tere any other place where I am supposed to change the version details?

    Yeah, you indicate these values in your TiApp.xml and titanium generates a valid AndroidManifest.xml automatically. So, you have in your TiApp.xml something like this:

    <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
        <tool-api-level>10</tool-api-level>
        <manifest android:versionCode="2" android:versionName="0.1.1" android:installLocation="preferExternal"/>
        <uses-sdk android:maxSdkVersion="10" android:minSdkVersion="7" android:targetSdkVersion="10"/>
</android>
