App doesn't fill full screen on iPhone5 simulator

I am busy preparing my app for release and testing it on iOS6 with Ti SDK 2.1.3 RC on the iPhone 5 simulator (iPhone 6.0 Simulator Retina 4-inch).

I have noticed that the app does not fill the whole screen. It centres the app in it's iPhone4 resolution so there's more black space at the top and bottom.

Is this a Titanium SDK issue or something I'm doing wrong? I'm not setting window heights, I'm using the platform height from the API. Is this because it's being built with a minimum iOS version of 4.0 (I noticed this in the debug)?

  • Accepted Answer

    You have to create a Splash screeen for iPhone5 in resources/iphone (640x1136).

    Its name must be: Default-568h@2x.png

    That's all!! :)
