center two views inside a view with layout horizontal

Hi,

i have a code like this :

<View layout="horizontal">
   <View id="view1"> </View>
   <View id="view2" left="10"> </View>
</View>

i want to center view1 and view2 inside the parent view..but i have horizontal layout.
i dont know width of view1 and view2 in advance…

is there a simple way ?

thanks

  • Hi

    Yes;

    <View id="outer">
    <View id="inner" layout="horizontal">
        <View id="view1"> </View>
        <View id="view2"> </View>
    </View>
</View>

    Notes;

    • outer has a width of Ti.UI.FILL
    • outer, has no layout
    • inner; has layout of horizontal
    • view1 & view2 have no layout
    • inner, view1 & view2 have width of Ti.UI.SIZE

    Logic

    • view1 & view2 will expand to fit contents
    • inner will expand to fit both view1 & view2 as the use of layout on inner
    • outer will center the child inner as there is no layout or positioning information, no; left, right, top or bottom
