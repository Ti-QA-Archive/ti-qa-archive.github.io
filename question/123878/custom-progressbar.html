Custom progressbar

I'm trying to create a custom progress bar, so all images.
I thought creating a view with a background and adjusting the side would do just fine, but apparently the background image is scaled to fit the view.

Is there a way to stop this? So just keep the image full size, but change the view width?

Or is there another way to create a custom progress bar?

    The easiest way might be to just create a dummy view (with a backgroundcolor) with a higher zIndex that you overlay on-top. Then as the progress happens you make the dummy view smaller and smaller until the image behind it is fully uncovered. If you do it right, that would accomplish your goal.
