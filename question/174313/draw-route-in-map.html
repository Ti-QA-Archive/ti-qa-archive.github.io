    Hi

    Your problem is a very simple one. You cannot expect to return a route of points that encompasses a journey when you do not provide more than the very same starting point.

    If you wanted to return a route from New York to California to San Francisco and back to New York, you HAVE to supply those points of interest. You CANNOT simply say from New York to New York as how would it know you also wanted to include San Francisco in your journey?

    So if you wanted to plot a returning journey you include several point or interest along the way and add the starting point again as the final location.

    • New York
    • California
    • San Francisco
    • New York

    You introduce the extra values as 'waypoints';
