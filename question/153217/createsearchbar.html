createSearchBar

I have an app which works in iPad perfectly, but when I try to use it on a browser I have a problem with the method createSearchBar. I have the error: "Uncaught TypeError: Object # has no method 'createSearchBar' at /index.html"

What can be the problem?

    Createsearchbar is only supported on iOS and android, if you check the documentation, it is not available on the mobile web. That is why you get the error
