  • Accepted Answer

    test.js makes no sense in this context, you don't need a separate controller to open a webview. Do this:

    index.xml

    <Alloy>
    <Window id="win" modal="true">
        <WebView id="webview" url="/test.html" />
    </Window>
</Alloy>

    index.js

    $.win.open();
