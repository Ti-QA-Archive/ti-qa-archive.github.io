  • Hi,
    You have to go to below link:

    https://go.urbanairship.com

    Login with your credentials. Click on your application(If already created otherwise will have to create one).
    Now clic on messages section by clicking "Messages" link from left section.

    From here you can send both Push and Rich Push messages to iOS and Android.
