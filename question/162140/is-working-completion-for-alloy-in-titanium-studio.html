  • Hi

    As far as I am aware (and having just tried - I do not use the studio editor) the code completion feature provides a drill down ability for keyword parents to their children.

    It does not attempt to go inside functions and variables and look for their associated descendants

    So I would not expect to type;

    myBooks.a

    And have any entries appear that are directly related to that variable, but I would expect to see Children of Alloy appear which comes from the dictionaries.
