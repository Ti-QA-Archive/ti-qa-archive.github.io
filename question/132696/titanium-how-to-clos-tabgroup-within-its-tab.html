Titanium: How to clos TabGroup within its Tab?

How can I close the TabGroup within one of its tab ?

I want to close the tabGroup on a button click (Log out button) and want to display a window without the tab group. I know how to display a window but could find how to close tab group ?

I think, to hide tab group is not proper way. Also on another button (Login button) I am creating the tab group so…

Any suggestions ?

  • Hey maulik,

    I think you should not hide or close tab group. Instead of opening new window within current tab open new window just by writing 

    win.open();

    It will cover whole screen instead of opening within current tab so your tab group will not be visible any more.

    And still you want to close your tab group you can call method close

    tabgroupobj.close();

    It will close tab group;

  • Solved : I have stored tabbar's variable like :

    Ti.App.customTabGroup = tabs;

    and in my 4th tab at where I want to close it :

    //code to open new window
Ti.App.customTabGroup.close();
