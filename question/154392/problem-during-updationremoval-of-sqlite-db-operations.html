  • As Mauro stated, Alloy MUST have a unique id in order for the backbone model behavior to work correctly. So if you don't create and designate one, Alloy will do it for you. The field alloy_id will be added to accomplish this. Also, I'm confused. You are calling the above and "UPDATE" operation, yet you are creating a new model and adding it to the collection. It's not clear to me what exactly is supposed to be happening.
