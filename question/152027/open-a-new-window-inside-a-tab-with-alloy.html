Open a new window inside a tab with alloy

Hello,
I need to keep the tabgroup visible at all times on android. On iPhone this is the standard behavior.
I created an simple example(that does not work):

Controller(index.js):

$.index.open();

function openWindowInsideTab(){
    $.firstTab.open($.tabWindow);
}

View(index.xml):

<Alloy>
    <TabGroup>
        <Tab id="firstTab" title="Tab 1" icon="KS_nav_ui.png">
            <Window title="Tab 1">
                <Label>I am Window 1</Label>
                <Button title="Open new window inside tab" onClick="openWindowInsideTab" />
            </Window>
        </Tab>
        <Tab title="Tab 2" icon="KS_nav_views.png">
            <Window title="Tab 2">
                <Label>I am Window 2</Label>
            </Window>
        </Tab>
    </TabGroup>
    <Window id="tabWindow">
        <Label>Hello im supposed to show up inside the tab not over it!</Label>
    </Window>
</Alloy>

  • Hi

    This is by design, Android tabs do not stay on screen at all time, new windows open outside of the tabs.

    It is standard for iOS tabs to be on the screen at all times but not on Android. Google has been moving away from tabs for some time and introduced the ActionBar as a UI element to act as the primary method of navigating through main windows - this was introduced in Android 3.0.

    The ActionBar !== tabs or any sort.

    It is always best to design an Android app to match the common Android expectations, and iOS apps should meet the expectations of that platform. It is more work for us, but the pay off is better apps that users feel comfortable using.
