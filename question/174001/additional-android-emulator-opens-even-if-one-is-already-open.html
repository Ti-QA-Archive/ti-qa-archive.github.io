      • Have you created at least one device in GenyMotion?

        GenyMotion is not just one emulator - you get to create a variety of them and build to whichever you prefer whilst testing your app.

      • Have you started the device running?

        There should be an icon on the Titanium toolbar - click that and it will show any GenyMotion emulators you have configured - select one and "start". You can also start the emulator via the GenyMotion software itself.
