  • Its possible, I have it in one of mine. Its not the easiest. You'll have to first get the person to login via facebook and grab their auth token. Then you'll use another graph call to get their friends. You'll get a set of data back, and then you can format it and have an 'invite' button that you'll have to create, and in that buttons event listener, you'll have to post to their wall.
