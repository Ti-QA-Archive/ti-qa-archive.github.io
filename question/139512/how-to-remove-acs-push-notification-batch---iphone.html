How to remove ACS Push Notification batch - iPhone

I just recently got the ACS Push notification working. The iPhone is receiving the notifications just fine and when I type 1 in the Badge area, it also adds a 1 batch to the application icon.

But how do I remove that batch again, once the apps i relaunched? It just stays there even though I open the app and closes it again.

— asked July 10th 2012 by Nicolai Villadsen

  • You can remove the badge from the App icon using the property Ti.UI.iPhone.appBadge, or on tabs within the app using the property Ti.UI.Tab.badge.
