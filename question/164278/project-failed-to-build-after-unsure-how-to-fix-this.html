    In the past, when I have encountered this, I have done the following which has seemed to work:

    • Close your simulator and reopen from xCode, not the running version launched from Ti. Reset the Sim "IOS Simulator/Reset Content and Settings", from the menu. Close the simulator. Close xCode.
    • Clean your project. "Project/Clean…" select your project from Ti.
    • Reboot your machine. In some instances, I have had issues where something running on my Mac was causing some kind of issue… Can't track it down, but rebooting clears out anything.
    • Reload Studio and retry…

    Good Luck.

    Ray
