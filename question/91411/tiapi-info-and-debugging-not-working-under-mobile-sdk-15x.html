Is this a known issue, or is it just my configuration somewhere??

Similarly, Titanium.Network.createHTTPClient() was not working (was telling me createHTTPClient doesn't work on an undefined object) under 1.5.x, while it works fine under 1.2.0.

Thoughts? Not sure if they are related, but for now, I'm having to stick with 1.2.0…. wasted the whole day trying to figure out what I was doing wrong with the above 2 until I saw that KitchenSink was behaving the same exact way!
