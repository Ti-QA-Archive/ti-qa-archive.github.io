Strings.xml not working in versions 1.7.6 and above

I was getting an error message when compiling localization files:

"substitutions specified in non-positional…"

In my strings.xml I had:

<string name="viewing_x_of_y">Viewing experience %s of %d</string>

All I had to do is add: Formatted=false as an attribute:

<string name="viewing_x_of_y" formatted="false">Viewing experience %s of %d</string>

I thought this might help someone, because I tried searching the forums and couldn't find a single post.
