Ti.API.debug messages no longer displayed on device console (SDK 2.0.1, XCode 4.3.2, iOS 5.1)

I've outfitted my app with lots of Ti.API.debug messages, to help trace/debug its behavior in the simulator and on the device… for the latter, I use the XCode "Organizer" view to access the device's console.

For some reason, I'm no longer seeing those debug messages on the device.

I see them just fine when in the simulator, but not when running on the device.

Has anyone else noticed this?

I'm wondering whether something happened during my upgrades to Titanium SDK 2.0.1, or OS 5.1, or XCode 4.3.2 (or MacOS Lion :)? I upgraded to all of those moving targets over the past few days, and at some point, I lost the on-device debug messages.

I checked the latest documentation, and from what I can tell, it doesn't appear that anything has changed with Ti.API.debug…

Thanks in advance for any help (and apologies in advance if I missed the memo, etc…)
