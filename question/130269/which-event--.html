    You can use the focus event on the TabGroup. There is an example in the app.js in KitchenSink here https://github.com/appcelerator/titanium_mobile/blob/master/demos/KitchenSink/Resources/app.js

    If you are looking to update inactive pages you might want to look at firing a custom event, doing this you should be able to push updates across your app.
