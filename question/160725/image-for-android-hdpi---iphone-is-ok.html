  • Hmm, are you testing on a device that qualifies as hdpi? If you move your mdpi folder out of the Resources/Android/images folder, then I think Android has no choice but load the hdpi version.

    With that said, Titanium SDK 3.1.x and older do have bugs with Android images, especially 9-patch and splash screens. I fixed those issues in 3.2.

    If you're close to being done with your app, it may not be a great idea to upgrade to 3.2, but it would be interesting if what you're seeing is a bug that was fixed in 3.2.
