  • Hi

    It sounds like you are referring to the textField not taking on the Android 4.x UI for some textFields but for others it does look correct.

    • pre Android 4.x; boxed, outlined white backed text field
    • post Android 4.x single underline with far left and right having a small vertical line coming from the base line

    This usually occurs due to the window not being heavyweight. Without knowing when in your app this occurs - try triggering the heavyweight state of Android windows by setting fullscreen or modal to true or false (doesn't matter which).
