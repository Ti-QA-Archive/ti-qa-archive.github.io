  • First, you may refer to our document to see supported tags inner json. In fact, we support not only alert, badge, sound as same as iOS, but also title, vibrate, title for Android. So if you need an icon and badge number, just simply put icon and badge into your json like {"alert":"something", icon: "icon-file-name under res/drawable", "badge" : 500}.

    For your second question, you may use a plain text instead of json. In server side, if we detect that string is not a json, we will pack it with {"alert": "your content"}. But we recommend you to use {"alert" : "content"} to avoid potential problems.
