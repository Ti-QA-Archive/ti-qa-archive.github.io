  • As stated above, log into iTunes Connect using your Apple ID. Then, click the Sales and Trends link to see how many downloads you've received.

    Another useful tool is AppData, where you can put in the name of your app and view graphs that show your application's history in the charts. This information is available in the iTunes store, but it's a bit easier to access here.
