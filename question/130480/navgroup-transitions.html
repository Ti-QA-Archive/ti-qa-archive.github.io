NavGroup transitions

Hi, I have a navigation group that on a specific page of the group I want the users to be able to flip between pages maintaining the navigation back to the parent. E.g. I have a dashboard page, with 6 sub pages, one of which is "menus". Within the menus pages users can swipe through the menus but the "back" button should always go back to the dashboard.

I have this basically working using:

placewin.addEventListener('swipe', function(e){
            Ti.API.info('You swiped to the '+e.direction);
            var nextMid;
            if (e.direction=='left')
                nextMid = getNextMenuLeft(mid,mode);
            else
                nextMid = getNextMenuRight(mid,mode);
            var nextWin = AppUtil.ui.createPlacePage(nextMid,mode);
            AppUtil.navGroup.open(nextWin,{
                 animated:true
            });
             AppUtil.navGroup.close(placewin);


});

My issue is that regardless of which direction the swipe occurs the pages comes in from the right. I've tried to play with the second parameter of the navGroup.open but no luck. Is there a better way to do this?
