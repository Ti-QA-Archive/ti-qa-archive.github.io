  • It's in the docs:

    "In terms of license enforcement, the modules sold in the marketplace are protected by Appcelerator's module verification system. It is designed to make sure that the person who is using the module is the one who purchased it. Buyers are free to use purchased components in their own applications: When the app goes out into the wild, the functionality of that app will continue to work even after the buyer stops paying for the module in question."

    https://wiki.appcelerator.org/display/guides2/Creating+a+New+Titanium+Module#CreatingaNewTitaniumModule-UploadingyourModuletotheMarketplace
