WebView in Android - "Webpage not available"

Hi,
Here's a tiny test app that works fine on iPhone emulator, but crashes on Android (emulator and device).
On Android I get an error that my.html is not available. Help?
Thanks,
Mark

app/views/index.xml:

<Alloy>
    <Window id="webWin" class="container">
        <WebView id="myWebView"/>
    </Window>
</Alloy>

app/styles/index.tss:

".container": {
    backgroundColor:"white"
},
"#myWebView": {
    url:"my.html"
}

app/controllers/index.js:

$.webWin.open();

app/lib/my.html:

<html lang="en">
    <head>
        <title>Title of the document</title>
    </head>
    <body>
        <p>My WebView</p>
    </body>
</html>
