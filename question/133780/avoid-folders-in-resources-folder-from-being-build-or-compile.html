  • Anything you put into your Resources/iphone folder should only be built for iOS. Anything in the Resources/android folder should only be built for Android.

    You can use these folders to separate your resources (images, files etc), so there is no reason why you can't simply put all your iPhone resources in the iPhone folder and Android ones in the Android folder to achieve what you're trying to do.
