  • Here is how I solved this issue:

    1. Go to project properties -> Resource -> Resource Filters;
    2. Click "Add…";
    3. Select: "Exclude All", "Folders", "Name" "matches" ".svn" (in "Filter Type", "Applies To", "Files and Folder Attributes" groups appropriately).
    4. Ok, Apply or whatever to close windows,
