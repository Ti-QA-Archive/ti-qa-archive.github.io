  • The benefits really occur when you adopt the entire MVC approach that Alloy provides. That is to say a clear separation of concerns in your code between your UI, Application Logic and Application Data. Having said that the underlying framework (backbone) used by Alloy is a mature and well respected product with many additional features so is still a fine method for managing your remote data. But if your existing (non-Alloy) app works well then you probably need to weigh up the benefits of potentially rewriting significant parts of your code just to implement a synch adapter
