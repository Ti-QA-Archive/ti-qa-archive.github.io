Hiding the title bar in Android not working

I can't seem to hide the titlebar/navbar in my android app.

I have set the navBarHidden and fullscreen properties in both my tiapp.xml and my app.jst:

tiapp.xml:

<fullscreen>false</fullscreen>
<navbar-hidden>true</navbar-hidden>

app.js:

var mainWindow = Ti.UI.createWindow({
    width: '100%',
    height: '100%',
    backgroundColor: color_primary,
    navBarHidden: true,
    fullscreen: false
});

It is still showing in my app though. I have no idea why?
