App Update Rejected Please Help

I have submitted an update 2 weeks ago that was approved in the app store, but tonight I submitted another update and the binary was automatically rejected.

I only changed 2 lines of code, and the message i get says this:

Dear Developer,

Thank you for your recent binary submission for "NCLEX RN Quiz Cards Lite" to the App Store. Unfortunately we discovered an issue with your binary that you will need to correct in order for your application to proceed to the review stage. The specific issue is outlined below:

Missing Push Notification Entitlement - Your app registers with the Apple Push Notification Service, but the application signature's entitlements do not include the required "aps-environment" entitlement. Make sure you have enabled Push Notification Services for this app, and that you have downloaded a Distribution provisioning profile that includes the "aps-environment" entitlement.

Once you have corrected the issue, please return to the application's version details page in the iTunes Connect Manage Your Applications module and click on the Ready to Submit Binary button. This will take you through the binary submission flow and return your application version status to Waiting for Upload. You can then use Application Loader to upload your new binary. If any other issues are found with your submission you will be contacted.

Thanks,
The iTunes Connect Team

I do not use push notifications, and I never have. I have changed nothing on my computer at all that would have caused this. I have sent many updates in the past with no problems.

Any ideas?!?!
Thanks in advanced

  • Ok All,

    If you dont want to comment any xCode then this seems to fix the issue (App in review after two reject binaries), I have done the following:

    • Go into Apple Developer Program.
    • Click on provisioning portal
    • Click on App ID’s
    • Select configure for the app id that wont submit
    • Tick Enable For Apple Push Notification Service and configure a PRODUCTION certificate
    • follow the steps and upload the certificate Request file
    • Click on provisioning link
    • Select Distribution tab
    • Edit > modify the relevant provisioning profile for your app
    • Click submit (Just refreshing the profile)
    • The profile will now be pending
    • Refresh the page until you can download the new provisioning profile.
    • Once downloaded open Appcelerator
    • click on app then distribute and browse to your download profile
      Package and submit

    Thanks to @jasonkneen for this!
