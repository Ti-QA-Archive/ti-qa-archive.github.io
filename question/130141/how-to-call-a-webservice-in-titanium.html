  • Hi Ajeet,
    The parameter in request.send() is the POST body. You can send parameters in form key1=value1&key2=value2 or json or what you want.

    I good idea is to untrust datas from net and to code:

    req.onload = function(_callback) {
    var res = this.responseText;
    try{ _callback(JSON.parse(res);)
    }
catch(E) {Ti.API.log(E);}

}
