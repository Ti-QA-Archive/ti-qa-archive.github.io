  • Other than Vi, the titanium CLI tools and a stiff bottle of Jack…

    ..ok, all kidding aside…

    I spend most of my editing inside of Studio. Even though I started with Developer, and yes it took some time to move to Studio, it works out ok.

    OSX

    • TextMate - ends up being my default editor for just about everything on the mac.
    • Icons - Chris Fletcher has been screwing with image automation for a while.
    • ShipIt - just got it cheap, growing on me.
    • xScope - Watch for sale bundles, pick it up. great for getting colors, measuring elements.
    • DeltaWalker - visual diff tool - $40 ain't too much money for the time it will save you.
    • TextExpander - think snippets everyplace. You know that annoying little 'please read the QA guidelines' I post when someone says "i have problem, please help" and nothing else? Yeah, I type (dang nab it it inserted it) 3 letters.
    • Liveview - this functionality is now built into the latest xScope
    • Status Barred - sweet little app that strips the status bar from your screenshots.
    • Screenflow - It just works. Its worth the $99, but wait for a bundle deal.

    Web

    • Dynamic Dummy Image Generator - ok, this is great as you are mocking up an app. The query string allows for colors, sizes, text. I use it to show a client what graphic they HAVEN'T DELIVERED YET thus explaining why THEIR project is DELAYED grin
