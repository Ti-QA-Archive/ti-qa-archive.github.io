    The official instructions are here.

    Here's a quick summary:
    If Titanium is running, stop it and any simulators.
    Once you have downloaded the file, unzip it.
    Keep clicking your way up through the folders until you see the folder named 1.6.0, this is the folder you will want to move/copy (the entire folder…..not just the contents).

    Depending on your permissions when you installed Titanium, the SDKs are stored in either
    ~/Library/Application Support/Titanium/mobilesdk/osx/ OR /Library/Application Support/Titanium/mobilesdk/osx/

    Simply copy the folder with the version number (from the extracted folder) to the folder with current SDK version number in place. Start Titanium and you will have to update your project to select the newer version.
