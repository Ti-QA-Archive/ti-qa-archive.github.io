  • auto is not a valid height value. That said, looking at your code, I am going to bet your views are extending beyond the bottom of the screen.

    When you're having scrolling/positioning problems, it helps to put colored borders on your views, so you can see which is what size. I give them each different colors & widths to help with visibility.
