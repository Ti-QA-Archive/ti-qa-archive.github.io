First App approved

Hey guys, we just got our first Titanium app approved.

It's a location based survival game. Check it out here

http://bit.ly/lsxMN0

It was a long journey but we finally got there, the whole thing was a great experience and using Titanium we had many ups and downs, here are a few highlights.

  • Custom Titanium window manager, for super fast responsive windows in the same execution context
  • Integration of the ti.storekit and TiBar modules
  • Custom crypto module for signing data and requests
  • oAuth 2 Integration with services like foursquare and facebook

Please be sure to check it out and let us know what you think, keep in mind this is our very first app and our first attempt at making an app, so this is still a very simple release, we have big things planned for the future though.

Thanks to all of the titanium community for the help in getting this far, the least we can do is help by sharing our experiences.
