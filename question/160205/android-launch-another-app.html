Android Launch Another App

I have an appcelerator based android app, and I'm trying to click a button an launch another app (this is not appcelerator based).

All I have for the other app is the url/package: http://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.other.app

I'm assuming I need to launch an intent: 

var intent = Ti.Android.createIntent({
    action: Ti.Android.ACTION_MAIN,
    category: Ti.Android.CATEGORY_LAUNCHER,
    //url: 'http://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.other.app',
    packageName: 'com.other.app',
});
Ti.Android.currentActivity.startActivity(intent);

However, this just complains that there isn't an activity in my AndroidManifest.xml. I'm not really sure where to go from here. Do I need to get more information about the other app; if so what? From my understanding the other app doesn't support a url scheme, so OpenURL won't work.

  • Hi,

    You can refer this code:

    
var intent = Ti.Android.createIntent({
        action: Ti.Android.ACTION_SENDTO,
        data: 'here go the package name of your activity.java',
        type:'text/plain'
    });


    intent.addCategory(Ti.Android.CATEGORY_DEFAULT);

    Ti.Android.currentActivity.startActivity(intent);

    !!! Rember to write the right action and category in the intent-filter of the manifest of your android app !!!
