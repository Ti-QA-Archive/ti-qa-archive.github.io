slide action bar

is there a way to do that
i am developing for android

    Hi

    Short answer - no.

    If I read your question correctly, you have an android app which has a window set to heavyweight so the actionbar is attached. You then want that window with the actionbar to scroll.

    The problem is that you cannot change the position of a heavyweight window - it takes the full device space.

    You will find that most of the recent Google App (like YouTube) so NOT slide the actionbar - instead they have a view as a container and slide that, this reveals the background view that contains a menu - the actionbar stays at the top of the screen all the time.

    This appears to be the correct UI/UX for new android apps.
