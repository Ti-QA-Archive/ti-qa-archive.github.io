setting min-ios-version to 5.1 in Titanium 3.2

I am trying to use an ipad 1 for development but am having trouble running my app from an alloy project in Titanium 3.2. The error says to lower the version to 5.1 in the tiapp.xml file, which I have done, but I still get the same error saying that the apps minimum version is set to 6.0. Is it possible that that gets overwritten somewhere else? What else can I try?

    ios 5.x support was deprecated in 3.1.x, the 3.2x titanium does not support ios 5 any longer.

    the only way to use your ipad1 will be to use Ti 3.1.3 sdk and install a old version of xcode and a old version of the ios sdk, but you will run into a dozens of problems doing that. It will save you time and headaches if you sell your device and buy a newer one. ios 5.x devices (only ipad1's) have only a market share about 5%, it makes no sense to develop for this 4 year old device today.
