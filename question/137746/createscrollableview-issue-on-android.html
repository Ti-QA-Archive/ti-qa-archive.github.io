  • I can confirm that touchEnabled is ignored.

    But I just made a solution :

    Set you scrollableView with a zIndex:1 Then create a simple view with backgroundColor:&#39;transparent with the exact same dimensions and position as your scrollableView and set zIndex:2

    This will then stop the swipes being passed to the scrollableView, since the transparent view is over the scrollableView.

    I just tested this on Android and it works.
