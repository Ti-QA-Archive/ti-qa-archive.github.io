  • I don't know of any sample code laying around for this, but a cross fade is pretty straight forward. One view fades out while the other fades in. I can think of 2 different ways to do this. Simply start a fade out of one view and then the fade in of the second. Or, even better, if the views are the same size, then you can simply fade out the top view and as it fades out, it will display the view behind it. Its a bit sharper than a crossfade but has the same basic effect.
