Switch between 'DEVELOPMENT' and 'PRODUCTION' in ACS.

I have noticed that there are two different databases for each applications cloud; Development and Production. Is there a way to switch which one you app is accessing? I am speaking for Titanium Integrated cloud apps. Thanks in advance.

  • My experience is that by default, apps running on simulator or built directly to a device are going to point to the development ACS app, and ad hocs and production builds will use the production app. This caught me a bit by surprise, as I had thought that an ad hoc build would still point to development.

    To force your environment to use one ACS app or the other, just open tiapp.xml and copy the 3 acs keys from development to production, or vice versa. Might also want to make a copy of the old keys to easily switch back :)
