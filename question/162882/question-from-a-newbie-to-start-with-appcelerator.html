  • The only marketplace from which people can download apps for Apple devices is from the App Store, which requires you to submit your app for approval. To do this you need to pay the nominal fee to have a registered Apple iOS developer account. To develop your app you do not need the developer account, however you can only test it on the emulator.

  • You can also upload the app on the friend website using Enterprise distribution profile.

    iOS Developers enrolled in the Enterprise program have the opportunity to distribute their application outside of the App Store . Use the “Enterprise” provisioning profile created in the iOS Provisioning Portal to distribute a build of your publication internally.

    From this profile you can share the app to infinite number of users. If you do not want to distribute app on app store, and only want to share from friends website then this is best option.

    In this case you need to upload .ipa file to the friend website.

    I hope this will have answer your question.
