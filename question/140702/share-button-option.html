  • For iOS there is a nice ShareKit Module in the Marketplace.

    For Android, you call an intent with the mime type, and registered apps are then presented.

    • var activity = Ti.Android.currentActivity;
var intent = Ti.Android.createIntent({
            action: Ti.Android.ACTION_SEND,
            type: 'text/plain'
        });

intent.putExtra(Ti.Android.EXTRA_TEXT,'Place the body of your message here');
intent.putExtra(Ti.Android.EXTRA_SUBJECT, 'Place the subject of your message here');
activity.startActivity(Ti.Android.createIntentChooser(intent,'Share'));

      Apps registered to handle text/plain will appear to be selected.
