  • Hi,

    You are showing the status bar snippet from tiapp.xml, but I'll take it that your intention is to change the window title as your text says (and not the status bar). If so, you'd need to use title attributes, like this:

      titleAttributes:{
      color: 'white'
  }

    This will show your window title 'My Title' in white.

    HTH
