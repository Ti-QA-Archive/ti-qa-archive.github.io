    • Works on iOS, but the label doesn't show up on Android. (The same is true of Label and ImageView, among other things, by the way: add works on iOSbut not on Android). As Ivan said, the documentation needs to be corrected here.

      I think it's more or less an accident that it works on iOS, however. You're better off adding the label as a child of the parent view or window.

      If you simply change:

      ta1.add(b1);

      To:

      win.add(b1);

      The label shows up on both platforms.

      Note that you'll have to tweak the position of b1 to get it to show up in its original position over the text area. You can avoid manually tweaking position by wrapping both the TextArea and the Label in a view.
