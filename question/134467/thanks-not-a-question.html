    • thanks for the kind words everyone!

      Stephen - yes there is - I help a friend who runs a venue, so sometimes find myself counting everything from chairs to cables, cash and beer coupons - After an evening of counting ticket stubs I came home in the evening with [this scrap of paper] (http://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=397677666926467&set=a.397677663593134.105253.396561887038045&type=1&theater) and a bet that I couldn't get it working by the performance the next day - and then the idea was born!.

      Version 2 is already underway which has some nice extra features to make managing things you count regularly. Its just a personal project as its something I need, but if it turns out to be useful for other people then thats a great bonus - I already have a couple of friends who work on the door for a local club and they are keen to use it to count people in out, rather than the old fashioned approach of having 2 clickers - one for in and one for out, so seeing it getting some practical use by other people makes the late night geeking worthwhile!
