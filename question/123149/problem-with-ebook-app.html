problem with eBook app

I am new to appcelerator. I am now developing an eBook application for both iPhone and Android. I am now loading the book pages in HTML files in to the webview.

My requirement is:

The application shall allow the user to do some minor personalization for their reading experience such as,

  • selection of font type/ size that the text appears in,
  • selection of page scroll - from up and down, side to side or simulate an actual page turn,
  • selection of the background screen for pages, to brighten the screen etc.
  • The application shall “remember” where the user left off so that they have the option of going either to the front page or open the page where they left off, last time they accessed the application.

Hm, WHether it is possible in appcelerator?

If yes, Please give explanations with sample code..
