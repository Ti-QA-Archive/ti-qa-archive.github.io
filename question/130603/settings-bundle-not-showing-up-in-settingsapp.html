I integrated some advanced settings to my app, but then discovered it could be done more easily using a settings bundle. So, as there were not much documentation on it, I copied the Kitchen Sink bundle at the root of my project, and modified the bundle to act as I wanted.
The problem is that it doesn't even appear in the iPhone settings. I tried to clean the project and rebuild, not working. Also tried copying the unmodified Kitchen Sink bundle… didn't work either.
I don't know what can cause this problem… Everywhere I looked for help it said that I had nothing else to do but create the files in the right place. If you need my sources to help me, just ask.
