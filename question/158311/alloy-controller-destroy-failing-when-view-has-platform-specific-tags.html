(Alloy) Controller .destroy() failing when view has platform-specific tags

I have a controller whose view has 2 platform-specific tags:

<Alloy>
  <View>
    <Picker id="myData" platform="ios" />
    <TableView id="myData" platform="android" />
  </View>
</Alloy>

When I call .destroy() on this controller on the iPhone Simulator, it throws an error.

Removing the Android-specific tag eliminates the error.

Error does not occur on Android.

Also tried using different IDs for each tag, but that doesn't change anything.

Anyone have any ideas on what could be wrong? Or could this be a bug?
