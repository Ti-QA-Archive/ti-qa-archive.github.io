  • Hello everybody,
    as I googled a lot until I found the solution I just summarize the info here:

    Problem: iPhone and Android do have two different module-versions…
    …solution: specify one modules-entry in tiapp.xml:

    <modules>
    <module version="1.1" platform="iphone">ti.admob</module>
    <module version="2.0" platform="android">ti.admob</module>
</modules>

    Problem: Module not found, although I copied everything to /Library/…/modules/iphone/… etc.
    solution: do not copy to /Library/…/ . Just add the modules ZIP you download to your Project root (not within resources! Use the root!) and unzip by double-click. You have to rename the folder (eg. ti.admob-iphone-1.1-android-1.0-2.0) to modules then.

    Your project shall look like:

    -Project Name
  - modules
     -android
        -ti.admob
          -1.0
          -2.0
     -iphone
        -ti.admob
          -1.1
  - Resources

    Hope that helps…
    Cheers,
    Ben

  • I basically just followed the steps in the documentation.

    
INSTALL TiAdMob4iPhone
--------------------

1. Open `Terminal`
2. Run below command

    `python build.py && unzip jp.masuidrive.ti.admob-0.1.zip -d /Library/Application\ Support/Titanium/`


REGISTER TO YOUR PROJECT
---------------------

Register your module with your application by editing `tiapp.xml` and adding your module.
Example:

    <modules>
        <module version="0.1">jp.masuidrive.ti.admob</module>
    </modules>

When you run your project, the compiler will know automatically compile in your module
dependencies and copy appropriate image assets into the application.
