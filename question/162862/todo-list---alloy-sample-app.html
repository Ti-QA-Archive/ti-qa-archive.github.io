Put it beginner-friendly! Thanks!

  • I suggest you take a step back and read the Alloy Concepts documentation.

    The reason you see so many references to 'ToDo' is that Alloy, like many other frameworks uses the notion of Convention Over Configuration. Once you understand the convention, then the code becomes far easier to understand.
