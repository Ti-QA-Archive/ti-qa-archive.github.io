Titanium 2.0: Tableview height Ti.UI.Size does not work!

Set the height of a tableview to Ti.UI.Size should scale the tableview to it's minimum hieght. In Titanium 2.0 this feature does not work! The tableview will be scaled to maximum.

To see the result, take the kitchensink example "tableview_layout_3.js" and set "height : Ti.UI.Size" where tableview variable is declared.

Can anyone help me with this issue?

    • It's my understanding the variables Ti.UI.Size and Ti.UI.Fill are is what is replacing the word 'auto' in these cases. By default Tableviews are Ti.UI.Fill because they should fill in the parent element, in this case your window. I would replace with Ti.UI.Fill unless you plan on specifying at least one of the attributes for height or width (or at least 2 points i.e. top, left, bottom, right, center etc….). I've upgraded my current app to use these variables and my tableviews show fine in iOS and Android (for reference my tableviews do not use either of the variables, I'm spefifying top, bottom, left, right.)
