    Hi

    Not any more, Android tabs are now only positioned at the top of the device.

    However this is in-line with Android UI standards and it the accepted behaviour of Android and the users. Placing tabs at the bottom was usually done to make an app look like an iPhone version (not always though) and it is best to respect the audience and what they expect.

    That said, if you are doing something truly innovative then go right ahead, but it will be harder than you think and also make it harder to target 2.3.x and 4.x at the same time (although no impossible).
