  • Not sure I follow what you mean by paging.

    If you simply want to put WebViews inside a ScrollableView, you can do that, but the way touch events are handled is going to change soon. Right now, there's a bug in Mobile Web where the touch handlers prevent scrolling WebViews. Once we fix that, the WebView would receive the touches and ScrollableView will not receive swipe events to change the selected panel.

    You can work around this by creating a custom nav bar to switch the selected panel.
