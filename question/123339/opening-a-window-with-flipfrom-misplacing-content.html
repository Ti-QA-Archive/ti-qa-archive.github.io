Opening a window with FLIP_FROM_... misplacing content

Hello,

I'm trying to switch between windows with a FLIP_FROM_RIGHT animationStyle on iPhone.
It's working well but when I close the window I opened with the animation, the previous window has its content moved to the left (half the content) and I can't find a reason for this.

Here's my code:

First win:

var contentWin = Ti.UI.createWindow({
    backgroundImage: '../assets/img/bg.jpg',
    url: 'new-win.js'
});

contentWin.open({
    transition: Ti.UI.iPhone.AnimationStyle.FLIP_FROM_RIGHT
});

Second win:

win.close({
    transition: Ti.UI.iPhone.AnimationStyle.FLIP_FROM_LEFT
});

Does someone had this bug before?
