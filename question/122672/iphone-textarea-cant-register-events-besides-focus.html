iPhone textArea can't register events besides 'focus'

Is it known behaviour that every event other than 'focus' won't fire on (iPhone) textareas? Makes it very hard to use a textarea as a read-only block with focus disabled.

Do I have to make a 'label-wrapped-in-scrollView' workaround or is there a less cumbersome fix for this?

  • Hello

    Workaround:

    you can do as follows,

    Add an View where you want to insert a textarea to be use, add the textarea inside the view and assign ownership "TouchEnabled: false" in your textarea.

    So when you touch the textarea, it will pass all touch events to the view, and you can use the "click" of the view.
