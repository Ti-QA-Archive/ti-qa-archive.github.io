  • Accepted Answer

    1. Emulator needs to be off when working with Genymotion.
    2. Physical devices must not be connected
    3. monitor/ddms must not be running.
    4. sometimes a adb kill-server & adb start-server need to be fired for adb/geny to connect

    These are all issues that come from adb. It has issues knowing what to talk to at any one point or another.
