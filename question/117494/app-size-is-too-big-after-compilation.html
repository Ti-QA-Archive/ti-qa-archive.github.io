App size is too big after compilation

Dear Titanium coders, I built an app with some graphics not so many, and hardly a couple thousand lines of code.

The resulting app is 19 meg, way bigger than what it should be. Any of you would know how to streamline that inflated monster?

Are Titanium generated apps, like flash swf files, coming with a huge overhead?

  • Sorry to answer my own question, but this is not documented and maybe someone has the same problem.

    Do not leave source graphics like photoshop files or illustrator files in the resources/images/ folder. They seemingly are getting compiled with the app, even if you are not using them in the app DHU!
