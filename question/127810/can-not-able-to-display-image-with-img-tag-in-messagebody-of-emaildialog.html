  • I solved problem by putting base64encoded string to display image in image tag of emailDialog message body.

    <img src="data:image/png;base64,'+encoded_string+'" width="100" height="100" align="middle" />

    here ,
    encoded_string ==> contains base64encode encodeing of an image.
    here for encodeing i use following module..
    https://github.com/aaronksaunders/base64encodeUtil
