  • Ok, found one solution to the codesign error we were getting after we renewed our developer certificate.

    The packaging error we would get was "Application failed codesign verification. The signature was invalid, or it was not signed with an Apple submission certificate."

    After days of headaches, 93 new certs and profiles, almost no hair on my head left, we yet again recreated the dev and dist csr's in Keychain using the Certificate Assistant. But during this process, we switched ON the option for "Let me specify key pair information". Something we completely forgot to do. This worked!! :)

    This finally allowed us to install to device and package the app successfully. The app has passed validation via xcode and hopefully gets approved without any signing issues.
