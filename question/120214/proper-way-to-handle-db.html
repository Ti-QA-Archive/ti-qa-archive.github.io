Proper way to handle DB

Dear all,
I am developing an app in titanium for iPhone. I need to access the DB many times for updation and selection. I want to know the place where i should create the tables, get data from server, insert into DB. How to open and close the DB, i mean should I open the DB every time and close it even if I am making DB call in a loop ? Please give me some IDEA to handle the DB properly.

Thanks,
OP

  • 1) You should make your database using another software package, then just include it pre-built with your app. Why bother having to create it on each device?

    2) Open the connection once, do your loop, then close it if you're done with it.

  • What I do is just make a global variable with a db connection on launch (in app.js):

    Ti.App.db =  Titanium.Database.open('db');

    This way you can use it throughout your app
