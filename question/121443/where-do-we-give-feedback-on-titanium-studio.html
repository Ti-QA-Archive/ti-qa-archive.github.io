  • You can provide your feedback on Titanium Studio (bug, Feature request, Improvement, etc.) on the JIRA tool.

    http://jira.appcelerator.org/browse/TISTUD

    Titanium Developer will not be supported by Appcelerator in the long run, but the community is stepping up and the product will live on.

    http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/121270/titanium-developer-130

    It's not ready yet, but it's getting there.
