  • First, cover the basics, even the greatest overlook simple things:

    1. Be sure the width and height you are specifying are the exact dimensions of the image.
    2. Be sure the quality of the image is good–open it up in an image viewer.
    3. Is the image being stretched? This will "blur" the image. If this is the case then your height and width specifications aren't being recognized. Post your code and we can take a look.

    If it is neither of these three then perhaps post some of your code for everyone to take a look and maybe something will be found. Hope this helps!
