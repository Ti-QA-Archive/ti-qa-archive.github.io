Any ideas or thoughts / solutions would be appreciated!

    • HI Guellai,

      I ended up using Scrollviews instead of using a Scrollableview, since 1.8 you have access to touchstart / touchend on a scrollview so you can achieve most things provided you know the size of the views contained within a scrollview.

      Personally I needed fullscreen views which was just a case of making the inner views the device with and height.

      It was also for IOS.
