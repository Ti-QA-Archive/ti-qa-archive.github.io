  • App.Properties persist beyond application sessions!

    Just a quick recap:

    Titanium.App.Properties.setString("name","Engjom");

    this will store into the Properties, key 'name' the value Engjom. Should you want to get the 'name' at any point of your app (no matter which window or context) all you have to do is:

    alert(Titanium.App.Properties.getString("name"));
