KitchenSink Alloy?

Hi,
I am looking at the implementation of KitchenSink and I am finding that it does not conform to the MVC methodologies - is this correct?

If so, is there a version of KitchenSink that leverages Alloy/MVC?

    From the FAQ: http://developer.appcelerator.com/question/148610/alloy-kitchen-sink-documentation-samples

    In short, Kitchen Sink is not meant, and was never meant, to be an example of how to build an app. It is simply a collection of all the APIs in tiny, easily-understandable snippets. For this reason it has no Alloy equivalent, but it has plenty of similar resources for learning.
