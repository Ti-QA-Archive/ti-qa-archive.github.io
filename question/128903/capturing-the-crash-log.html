Capturing the crash log

Is there any way to capture the crash log of an application, in the event of a crash, and do something with it?

My objective is to build a log of all events occurred in an application. If it crashes, I want to send as an email. This is an approach similar to generic desktop applications.

Enclosing the entire app in a try-catch-finally block does not seem to do the trick.

  • During beta, we use TestFlight for rolling out our iOS builds. The TestFlight module in the Marketplace is well worth the $10 and the author has been quick to respond. Using the TestFlight API, it captures crash information from the iOS devices of our testers. You can upload your dsym information as well to enhance the information you get from TestFlight.

    We haven't found anything for Android as of yet.
