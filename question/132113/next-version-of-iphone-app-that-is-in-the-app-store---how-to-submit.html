  • Two different questions:

    The error seems as though your provisioning file has disappeared. Re-download your distribution provisioning file from Apple. Re-add it to xCode to make sure its there. Then on your distribute app store dialog, re-add it to Titanium.

    For iOS, simply increasing the version in TIAPP.xml increases the version number of your packaged app. As long as you use the same app id, that is.
