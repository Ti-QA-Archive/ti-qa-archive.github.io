Black Screen on Re-launching App from Notification

My Android app kicks out a notification that looks like this: 

Titanium.Android.NotificationManager.notify(0, 
            Ti.Android.createNotification({
                contentTitle: 'Test Notification',
                contentText: 'Content!',
                tickerText: 'Ticker Text!',
                contentIntent: Titanium.Android.createPendingIntent({
                    intent: Titanium.Android.createIntent({
                        url: 'app.js',
                        flags: Titanium.Android.FLAG_ACTIVITY_CLEAR_TOP | Titanium.Android.FLAG_ACTIVITY_SINGLE_TOP
                    })
                })
            }));

The relevant part of my tiapp.xml looks like this:

<activities>
       <activity url="app.js">
           <intent-filter>
               <action android:name="android.intent.action.VIEW"/>
               <category android:name="android.intent.category.DEFAULT"/>
               <category android:name="android.intent.category.BROWSABLE"/>
           </intent-filter>
       </activity>
   </activities>

When I select the notification on the Win32 Android emulator, I get this log:

[WARN][ActivityManager(   61)] startActivity called from non-Activity context; forcing Intent.FLAG_ACTIVITY_NEW_TASK for: Intent { flg=0x24000000 cmp=com.app.appname/.AppActivity bnds=[0,77][240,125] }

Following that, a black screen comes up with nothing but a titlebar. Any help would be appreciated.

  • I fixed it by calling using the 'package' property in lieu of 'url', and I used different flags. Here's what I ended up with: 

    Titanium.Android.NotificationManager.notify(0, 
            Ti.Android.createNotification({
                contentTitle: 'Test Notification',
                contentText: 'Content!',
                tickerText: 'Ticker Text!',
                contentIntent: Titanium.Android.createPendingIntent({
                    intent: Titanium.Android.createIntent({
                        className: 'org.appcelerator.titanium.TiActivity',
                        flags : Ti.Android.FLAG_ACTIVITY_RESET_TASK_IF_NEEDED | Ti.Android.FLAG_ACTIVITY_SINGLE_TOP
                    })
                })
            }));
