iPhone 6: Wrong app scaling?

Hey there,

i just deployed my current Ti apps on iOS8 on an iPhone 6using the latest 3.4 master and noticed, that the screen appears to be scaled bigger than it should be.
The problem is also noticed in the latest WhatsApp version as the fontSize seems to be bigger than compared to the iMessage app Link.

Am i the only one noticed this, yet? Is there a workaround? Thank you!

    Your app is likely appearing in "compatibility mode." To fix, please follow the 3.4.0 release notes and add the appropriate splash screens for iPhone 6/6+.
