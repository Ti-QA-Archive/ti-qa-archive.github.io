  • As far as I know, Apple will do this automatically and put the links in the "More iPhone app by …" section to the left of your apps.

    As long as it's the same dev account, this should be the case.

    The clickable link to "Bata Construction" in iTunes will also take people to the full list of your previous apps.

  • For the developer apps you can use this:

    https://itunes.apple.com/artist/idYOUR_ARTIST_ID?at=10l6dK

    You can get YOUR_ARTIST_ID from your iTunes Connect (Guess on your case it's 324884634) or from the link on one of your apps (there is a "View more by this developer" and copy the number after the "id" on the link itself).

    The "at=…" would make the App Store opens with your developer account. not using that could open first a web view (safari) then user would have to click there to really open the store. especially if using this link not on iOS.

    Link should be:

    https://itunes.apple.com/artist/id324884634?at=10l6dK

    If you are using iOS older than 7 you shall change "https" to "itms-apps" which would open the store directly without opening safari in the process…
