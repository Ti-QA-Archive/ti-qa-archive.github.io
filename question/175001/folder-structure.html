    Hi

    I can see why that might be confusing, which is why I ended up using my own convention, try this;

    {appname}/platform/android/res/anim

    So you can confirm in your mind, here are some more in the same context.

    {appname}/app/
{appname}/app/assets/
{appname}/platform/
{appname}/i18n/en/
{appname}/resources/
