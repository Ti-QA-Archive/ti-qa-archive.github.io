Titanium apps for the Samsung galaxy i7500 ?

I tried to compile a test app for the samsung galaxy i7500 yesterday.

Windows 7, Titanium, JDK, complete android SDK 1.5, 1.6, 2.0 and 2.1 were installed. I did not succeed to launch either a mobile test app or kitchensink 1.0 in the 1.5 android emulator. I suceeded to lauch both apps in the 1.6, 2.0 and 2.1 emulators on windows 7.

I manually transferred both test apps (app.apk, android version 1.5) to my galaxy phone. However, installation was not possible "you cannot install this application on your device" ….

Has anybody succeeded to
(1) build and launch (emulator) test apps under android 1.5 with titanium ?
(2) build and install apps for the samsung galaxy i7500 ?
