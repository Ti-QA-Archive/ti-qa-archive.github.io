  • Material Theme and tinted action bar and status bar using theme attributes is now working with Titanium SDK 4.0.0RC+

    Example: https://github.com/Hollerweger/Ti.DrawerLayout-Demo-Alloy-App

    tiapp.xml
    <android xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android">
    <manifest>
    <application android:theme="@style/materialTheme"/>
    </manifest>
    </android>

    and look at the custom_theme.xml
