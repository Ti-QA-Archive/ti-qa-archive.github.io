Horizontal layout

I have form with labels and corresponding fields. Labels are to the left of fields in landscape orientation on my phone because there's enough space in row. But I want them to be at the top of fields when in portrait orientation that is when there's no enough space in row. In other words, I need automatic rearrange within the horizontal layout. How to achieve that?
