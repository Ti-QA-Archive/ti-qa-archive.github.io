  • Unfortunately the Android emulator isn't very reliable. I personally would recommend using a device instead, because no matter how perfect your app works on the emulator, it will most likely behave slightly differently on an actual device (even if you select the same properties to emulate).

    This is only what I have observed from my personal experience with Android, I would highly recommend to get a physical device instead. It runs much faster and gives you more accurate results. Sometimes you will have a runtime error on the device, which you won't get on the emulator.

    But to answer your question about the long loading time when starting the emulator, this sometimes happens to me, too. You just to go back into TiStudio and re-run the app, without closing down the emulator itself. You sometimes just have to play around, before the emulator decides to work. I have no idea myself why this happens. I would assume the emulator is waiting on a process that already has been executed, because it took too long for the emulator to boot up, but I can't say that for 100% certainty.. just a guess..

    Hope this helps.
