  • Hi,

    I managed to fix it, I don't have an AndroidManifest.xml but a /platform/android/res/values/theme.xml for the spalsh screen.

    By adding another item with the name "android:windowNoTitle" and setting it to true I got rid of it, I guess this title label was an addition to the Android SDK somewhere between 2.3 and 4.0 since it didn't show on my devices with Android 2.3 and 2.2.

    <?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?>
<resources>
<style name="Theme.Titanium" parent="android:Theme">
    <item name="android:windowBackground">@drawable/splash</item>
    <item name="android:windowNoTitle">true</item>
</style>
</resources>
    — commented December 7th 2012 by Jesper Jonsson

  • And the contents of my theme.xml:

    <?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?>

    <item name="android:windowBackground">@drawable/splash</item>
    <item name="android:windowNoTitle">true</item>
</style>
</resources>
