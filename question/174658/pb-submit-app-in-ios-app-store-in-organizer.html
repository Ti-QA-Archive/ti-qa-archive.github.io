  • The loader is the bane of my existence.

    Bunch of moving parts here.

    1. Try to use the latest version of the application loader instead. https://developer.apple.com/library/ios/documentation/LanguagesUtilities/Conceptual/iTunesConnect_Guide/Chapters/SubmittingTheApp.html
    2. Be sure you have downloaded and installed the Apple Root Certificate Authority
    3. Revoke not just the certs and profiles, remove your signing key and regen it, then create new certs and profiles from it.
    4. We don't use wild card profiles, not sure of you are or not.
